## 🔧 How The Protocol Works

### For Traders
1. **Connect** with your preferred wallet (Keplr, Leap, MetaMask, Phantom)
2. **Browse** auctions for gold, silver, platinum, palladium
3. **Bid** with TESTUSD (10% buyer collateral locked)
4. **Win** the auction, complete payment, receive item
5. **Earn** TRUST reputation and PHNX governance weight

### For Sellers
1. **List** your precious metals (weight, purity, certification)
2. **Post** 10% seller collateral (shows you're serious)
3. **Ship** within timeframe, provide tracking
4. **Get paid** after buyer confirmation
5. **Build** permanent reputation (TRUST tokens)

### For Everyone
- **1.1% fees** → Community Reserve Fund
- **48-hour verification** protects buyers
- **Permanent reputation** follows you forever
- **Governance weight** earned through participation
- **No hidden fees**, no middlemen, no surprises
