### 08 Characteristics of the crypto-asset

The TX Token is the native asset of the TX blockchain, used for transaction fees, staking, delegation, and participation in on-chain governance. It also provides access to embedded compliance functions on the network, including programmable KYC, transfer-rule enforcement, tokenisation modules, and other Smart Token features. The token does not confer ownership, profit participation, equity, or redemption rights against TX Issuer Ltd., TX Labs, or any other entity. All modifications to token functionality or governance parameters follow the network's on-chain decision-making process, requiring a quorum and majority approval. Transfers and usage are subject to protocol-level compliance rules, technical constraints, and upgrade decisions made through the community governance mechanism.

### 10 Key information about the offer to the public or admission to trading

There is **no offer** of TX (TX) Tokens to the public in connection with this disclosure. The token is not being issued through a sale, subscription period, or fundraising process. Instead, the TX Token is created through a **migration** of existing COREUM and SOLO tokens. Following this migration, admission to trading is sought on Payward Europe Solutions Limited (Kraken) to ensure regulated market access and liquidity for eligible users in the European Economic Area. The admission is not linked to any discounted purchase arrangements, pre-sales, private placements, or staged distribution events. No crypto-asset service provider has been appointed to place the token on a firm-commitment or best effort basis, and all trading-related fees are determined independently by Payward Europe Solutions Limited (Kraken).

#### Migration Mechanics

The $TX token is the unified protocol asset resulting from the merger of Coreum and Sologenic. At the Token Generation Event (TGE), the initial circulating supply of $TX will be derived from the conversion of existing Coreum and Sologenic tokens.

A portion of the TGE circulating supply, equal to **6.04%**, will be reserved for Tier 1 Exchanges, including potential support for exchange integrations and listings, subject to applicable terms, conditions, and regulatory considerations.

**All calculations are performed as of February 4, 2026, 00:00:00 UTC**, using on-chain data at Coreum blockchain height 63,741,886. Asset prices are based on 30-day Time-Weighted Average Prices (TWAP) to mitigate short-term volatility and ensure a fair and manipulation-resistant conversion framework.
