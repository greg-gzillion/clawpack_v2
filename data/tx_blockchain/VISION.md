## 🪙 The Three Tokens of PhoenixPME

### 1. PHNX – Governance Weight

**What it is:** Non-transferable voting weight in the Community Reserve Fund.

**How you get it:** For every 1 RLUSD in fees you generate through trading, 1 PHNX voting weight is allocated.

**Why you can't sell it:** Because governance shouldn't be for sale. Your voice should be earned through participation, not purchased with capital.

**What it gives you:** A say in how the Community Reserve Fund (accumulated fees) is used—marketing, development, dispute resolution, grants.

**Founder allocation:** 10% of PHNX voting weight (permanent, non-transferable). I'm stuck with you forever.

### 2. TRUST – Positive Reputation

**What it is:** Non-transferable reputation tokens minted for every successful transaction.

**How you get it:** Complete a trade successfully → 1 TRUST token minted to your wallet.

**Why you can't sell it:** Because reputation isn't merchandise. You can't buy a good name—you have to earn it.

**What it signals:** "This person delivers. They've completed X successful trades."

### 3. DONT TRUST – Negative Reputation

**What it is:** Non-transferable reputation tokens minted for every failed transaction.

**How you get it:** Fail to deliver, scam a buyer, lose a dispute → 1 DONT TRUST token minted to your wallet.

**Why you can't sell it:** You wouldn't want to buy it. And neither would anyone else.

**What it signals:** "Warning: This person has failed to complete X trades."

## 🔐 Why These Tokens Are NOT Securities

Let's be absolutely clear about what these tokens are and aren't:

### The Howey Test (U.S. Supreme Court standard for securities)

| Howey Element | PHNX | TRUST | DONT TRUST |
|---------------|------|-------|------------|
| Investment of money? | ❌ No - earned through fees paid for service | ❌ No - earned through trade completion | ❌ No - earned through failure |
| Common enterprise? | ✅ The platform exists | ✅ The platform exists | ✅ The platform exists |
| Expectation of profits? | ❌ NO - cannot be sold, has no cash value | ❌ NO - cannot be sold, has no cash value | ❌ NO - literally the opposite of profit |
| From efforts of others? | ❌ NO - you earn it yourself | ❌ NO - you earn it yourself | ❌ NO - you earn it yourself |

### The Simple Truth:

| Token | It's Actually... | Like... |
|-------|------------------|---------|
| PHNX | A voting ledger | Frequent flyer status |
| TRUST | A feedback score | eBay feedback, but permanent |
| DONT TRUST | A warning flag | Negative Yelp review |

None of these can be sold. None of these have cash value. None of these are investments. They are simply:

- ✅ Records of participation
- ✅ Reputation scores
- ✅ Governance weight

The SEC regulates securities—investment contracts where people put money in expecting profits from others' efforts.

Nobody is putting money into PHNX, TRUST, or DONT TRUST. You can't buy them. You can't sell them. You earn them by using the platform as intended.

**This is like saying your Reddit karma is a security. It's not. It's just... karma.**
