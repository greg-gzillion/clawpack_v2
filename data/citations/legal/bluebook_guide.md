# Bluebook Citation Guide (21st Edition)

## Legal Citation Standard for US Courts

### Case Citations

#### Supreme Court of the United States
**Format:** `{case_name}, {volume} U.S. {page} ({year})`

**Examples:**
- `Marbury v. Madison, 5 U.S. 137 (1803)`
- `Brown v. Board of Education, 347 U.S. 483 (1954)`
- `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

#### Federal Courts of Appeals
**Format:** `{case_name}, {volume} F.{series} {page} ({circuit} {year})`

**Examples:**
- `Miranda v. Arizona, 384 F.2d 1 (9th Cir. 1967)`
- `United States v. Jones, 565 F.3d 123 (2d Cir. 2009)`

#### Federal District Courts
**Format:** `{case_name}, {volume} F. Supp. {page} ({district} {year})`

**Examples:**
- `Brown v. Board of Education, 98 F. Supp. 797 (D. Kan. 1951)`

#### State Courts
**Format:** `{case_name}, {volume} {state_reporter} {page} ({year})`

**Examples:**
- `People v. Smith, 45 Cal. 3d 123 (1988)`
- `Smith v. Jones, 678 S.W.2d 123 (Tex. 1984)`

### Statutory Citations

#### United States Code
**Format:** `{volume} U.S.C. § {section} ({year})`

**Examples:**
- `42 U.S.C. § 1983 (2018)`
- `18 U.S.C. § 242 (2018)`

#### Public Laws
**Format:** `Pub. L. No. {number}, {volume} Stat. {page} ({year})`

**Example:** `Pub. L. No. 104-191, 110 Stat. 1936 (1996)`

### Regulatory Citations

#### Code of Federal Regulations
**Format:** `{title} C.F.R. § {section} ({year})`

**Example:** `12 C.F.R. § 226.5 (2020)`

#### Federal Register
**Format:** `{volume} Fed. Reg. {page} ({year})`

**Example:** `85 Fed. Reg. 12345 (2020)`

### Secondary Sources

#### Law Review Articles
**Format:** `{author}, {title}, {volume} {journal} {page} ({year})`

**Example:** `Laurence H. Tribe, The Constitution in Cyberspace, 21 Harv. J.L. & Tech. 1 (2007)`

#### Books
**Format:** `{author}, {title} § {section} ({publisher} {year})`

**Example:** `William Blackstone, Commentaries on the Laws of England § 12 (Oxford 1765)`

### Short Forms

| Form | Usage | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| `Id.` | Same source as immediately preceding citation | `Id. at 142.` |
| `Supra` | Previously cited source | `Tribe, supra note 1, at 45.` |
| `Hereinafter` | Long titles | `John Smith, The Law of Contracts (2020) [hereinafter Contracts]` |

### Online Sources
**Format:** `{author}, {title}, {website} ({date}), {url}`

**Example:** `Supreme Court of the United States, About the Court, supremecourt.gov (last visited Apr. 6, 2026), https://www.supremecourt.gov/about/about.aspx`
