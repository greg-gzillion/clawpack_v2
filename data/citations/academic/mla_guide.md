# MLA Citation Guide (9th Edition)

## Modern Language Association Style

### Book Citations
**Format:** `{author}. {title}. {publisher}, {year}.`

**Examples:**
- `Smith, John. Legal Research Methods. Oxford UP, 2020.`
- `Brown, Anne, and Charles Johnson. Understanding the Law. 3rd ed., West Academic, 2019.`

### Journal Articles
**Format:** `{author}. "{title}." {journal}, vol. {volume}, no. {issue}, {year}, pp. {pages}.`

**Examples:**
- `Taylor, Robert. "Artificial Intelligence in Legal Practice." Stanford Law Review, vol. 73, no. 4, 2021, pp. 891-945.`
- `Williams, Sarah. "Court Accessibility in the Digital Age." Yale Law Journal, vol. 129, no. 5, 2020, pp. 1234-1298.`

### Case Law (MLA 9th)
**Format:** `{case_name}. {volume} {reporter} {page} ({court} {year}).`

**Examples:**
- `Brown v. Board of Education. 347 U.S. 483 (1954).`
- `Roe v. Wade. 410 U.S. 113 (1973).`

### Websites
**Format:** `{author}. "{title}." {website}, {date}, {URL}.`

**Example:** `U.S. Courts. "About Federal Courts." United States Courts, 6 Apr. 2026, www.uscourts.gov/about-federal-courts.`

### In-Text Citations
**Format:** `({author} {page})`

**Examples:**
- `(Smith 45)`
- `(Brown and Johnson 123)`
- `(Taylor et al. 89)`

### Works Cited Formatting
- Alphabetical by author last name
- Hanging indent (0.5 inches)
- Double-spaced

### Containers (MLA 9th Edition)
When citing a source within a larger work (container):
- Journal article in a database
- Chapter in a book
- Episode in a TV series
