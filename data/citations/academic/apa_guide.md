# APA Citation Guide (7th Edition)

## American Psychological Association Style

### Book Citations
**Format:** `{author}. ({year}). {title}. {publisher}. {DOI or URL}`

**Examples:**
- `Smith, J. (2020). Legal research methods. Oxford University Press.`
- `Brown, A. B., & Johnson, C. D. (2019). Understanding the law (3rd ed.). West Academic.`

### Journal Articles
**Format:** `{author}. ({year}). {title}. {journal}, {volume}({issue}), {pages}. {DOI}`

**Examples:**
- `Taylor, R. (2021). Artificial intelligence in legal practice. Stanford Law Review, 73(4), 891-945.`
- `Williams, S. (2020). Court accessibility in the digital age. Yale Law Journal, 129(5), 1234-1298.`

### Case Law (APA 7th now includes legal citations)
**Format:** `{case_name}, {volume} {reporter} {page} ({court} {year})`

**Examples:**
- `Brown v. Board of Education, 347 U.S. 483 (1954)`
- `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### Statutes
**Format:** `{name of Act}, {volume} U.S.C. § {section} ({year})`

**Example:** `Civil Rights Act of 1964, 42 U.S.C. § 2000e (1964)`

### Websites
**Format:** `{author}. ({year, month date}). {title}. {website}. Retrieved {date}, from {URL}`

**Example:** `U.S. Courts. (2026, April 6). About federal courts. United States Courts. Retrieved April 6, 2026, from https://www.uscourts.gov/about-federal-courts`

### In-Text Citations

| Type | Format | Example |
|------|--------|---------|
| Parenthetical | (Author, year) | (Smith, 2020) |
| Narrative | Author (year) | Smith (2020) argued that... |
| Multiple authors | (Author1 & Author2, year) | (Smith & Jones, 2020) |
| Three+ authors | (Author et al., year) | (Smith et al., 2020) |

### Reference List Formatting
- Alphabetical by author last name
- Hanging indent (0.5 inches)
- Double-spaced

### DOIs and URLs
- DOI: `https://doi.org/xxxxx`
- No "Retrieved from" before URLs (except for dates)
