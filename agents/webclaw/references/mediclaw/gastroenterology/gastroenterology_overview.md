# Gastroenterology - Overview

## What is Gastroenterology?
Gastroenterology is the medical specialty focused on the digestive system and its disorders, including the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, colon, rectum, pancreas, gallbladder, bile ducts, and liver.

## Gastrointestinal Tract Anatomy
| Segment | Length | Primary Function |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| Esophagus | 25 cm | Transport food to stomach |
| Stomach | Variable | Acid secretion, Mechanical digestion, Protein digestion |
| Duodenum | 25 cm | Iron absorption, Chemical digestion |
| Jejunum | 2.5 m | Nutrient absorption |
| Ileum | 3.5 m | B12 absorption, Bile acid reabsorption |
| Colon | 1.5 m | Water absorption, Electrolyte balance |
| Rectum | 12-15 cm | Storage of feces |

## Accessory Organs
| Organ | Primary Function |
|-------|------------------|
| Liver | Bile production, Metabolism, Detoxification, Synthesis |
| Gallbladder | Bile storage and concentration |
| Pancreas (Exocrine) | Digestive enzyme secretion, Bicarbonate secretion |
| Pancreas (Endocrine) | Insulin, Glucagon secretion |

## Common GI Symptoms and Differential
| Symptom | Differential Diagnosis |
|---------|----------------------|
| Dysphagia | Esophageal stricture, Eosinophilic esophagitis, Achalasia, Esophageal cancer, GERD |
| Odynophagia | Infectious esophagitis, Pill esophagitis, Radiation esophagitis |
| Heartburn | GERD, Esophageal spasm, Eosinophilic esophagitis |
| Nausea/Vomiting | Gastroparesis, Obstruction, Medications, Pregnancy, CNS disorders |
| Abdominal Pain | PUD, Pancreatitis, Cholecystitis, IBS, IBD, Obstruction, Ischemia |
| Diarrhea | Infectious, IBD, IBS, Celiac disease, Microscopic colitis, Malabsorption |
| Constipation | IBS-C, Slow transit constipation, Pelvic floor dysfunction, Medications |
| GI Bleeding | PUD, Varices, Diverticulosis, Angiodysplasia, IBD, Malignancy |
