# Urology - Testicular Cancer

## Testicular Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | **Most common solid tumor in young men** (15-35 years) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/testicular |
| **Risk Factors** | **Cryptorchidism**, **Family history**, **Prior testicular cancer**, **Klinefelter syndrome**, **Infertility** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/testicular |
| **Types** | **Germ cell tumors** (95%): **Seminoma** (50%), **Non-Seminoma** (embryonal, Yolk sac, Choriocarcinoma, Teratoma) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/testicular |
| **Tumor Markers** | **AFP** (non-seminoma only), **hCG**, **LDH** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/testicular |

## Testicular Cancer - Staging and Treatment
| Stage | Seminoma | Non-Seminoma | URL |
|-------|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Stage I** | **Surveillance** (preferred), Carboplatin x1, Radiation | **Surveillance** (preferred), BEP x1, RPLND | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |
| **Stage IIA (Marker Negative)** | Radiation (30 Gy) or BEP x3/EP x4 | **RPLND** or BEP x3/EP x4 | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |
| **Stage IIB (Marker Negative)** | BEP x3 or EP x4 | BEP x3 or EP x4 or **RPLND** (select) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |
| **Stage IIC-III (Good Risk)** | **BEP x3** or **EP x4** | **BEP x3** or **EP x4** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |
| **Stage III (Intermediate/Poor Risk)** | **BEP x4** or **VIP** | **BEP x4** or **VIP** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |

## IGCCCG Risk Classification for Metastatic Germ Cell Tumors
| Risk Group | Seminoma | Non-Seminoma | URL |
|------------|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Good Risk** | **Any primary site**, **No non-pulmonary visceral mets**, **Normal AFP**, Any hCG/LDH | **Testis/retroperitoneal primary**, **No non-pulmonary visceral mets**, **AFP <1000**, hCG <5000, LDH <1.5x ULN | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |
| **Intermediate Risk** | **Any primary site**, **Non-pulmonary visceral mets**, Normal AFP, Any hCG/LDH | **Testis/retroperitoneal primary**, **No non-pulmonary visceral mets**, **AFP 1000-10,000**, hCG 5000-50,000, LDH 1.5-10x ULN | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |
| **Poor Risk** | **No poor risk category** | **Mediastinal primary** OR **Non-pulmonary visceral mets** OR **AFP >10,000** OR **hCG >50,000** OR **LDH >10x ULN** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/testicular |
