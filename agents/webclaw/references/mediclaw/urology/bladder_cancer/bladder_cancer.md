# Urology - Bladder Cancer

## Bladder Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | 4th most common cancer in men | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bladder |
| **Risk Factors** | **Smoking** (50%), **Occupational exposures** (aromatic amines), **Chronic cystitis**, **Schistosomiasis** (SCC) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bladder |
| **Histology** | **Urothelial carcinoma** (90%), Squamous cell (5%), Adenocarcinoma (2%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bladder |

## NMIBC - Risk Stratification and Adjuvant Therapy
| Risk | Criteria | Adjuvant Therapy | URL |
|------|----------|------------------|-----|
| **Low** | Solitary Ta, Low-grade, <3 cm | **Single dose intravesical chemotherapy** (mitomycin C, Gemcitabine) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bladder-cancer |
| **Intermediate** | Multiple/recurrent Ta, Low-grade, >3 cm | **Induction intravesical chemotherapy or BCG** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bladder-cancer |
| **High** | T1, High-grade, CIS, Multifocal | **Induction + Maintenance BCG** (3 years) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bladder-cancer |

## Metastatic Bladder Cancer - Systemic Therapy
| Line | Options | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| **First-Line (Cisplatin-Eligible)** | **Gemcitabine + Cisplatin (GC)** or **MVAC** (dose-dense) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/bladder |
| **First-Line (Cisplatin-Ineligible)** | **Gemcitabine + Carboplatin** or **Pembrolizumab** (PD-L1+) or **Atezolizumab** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/bladder |
| **First-Line (Platinum-Ineligible)** | **Pembrolizumab** or **Atezolizumab** (regardless of PD-L1) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/bladder |
| **Second-Line** | **Pembrolizumab** (preferred), **Enfortumab vedotin**, **Sacituzumab govitecan**, **Erdafitinib** (FGFR3-mutated) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/bladder |
