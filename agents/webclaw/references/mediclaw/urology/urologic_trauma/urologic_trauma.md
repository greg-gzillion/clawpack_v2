# Urology - Urologic Trauma

## Renal Trauma - AAST Grading
| Grade | Description | Management | URL |
|-------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **I** | **Contusion** or **Non-expanding subcapsular hematoma** | **Observation** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/renal-trauma |
| **II** | **Non-expanding perirenal hematoma** or **Laceration <1 cm** | **Observation** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/renal-trauma |
| **III** | **Laceration >1 cm** without collecting system injury | **Observation** (most), **Angioembolization** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/renal-trauma |
| **IV** | **Laceration into collecting system** or **Segmental vascular injury** | **Angioembolization**, **Stenting/Nephrostomy**, **Surgery** (select) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/renal-trauma |
| **V** | **Shattered kidney** or **Renal pedicle avulsion** | **Nephrectomy** (hemodynamically unstable), **Angioembolization** (stable) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/renal-trauma |

## Bladder Trauma
| Type | Mechanism | Diagnosis | Management | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Extraperitoneal Rupture** | **Pelvic fracture** (80-90%) | **CT cystography** | **Foley catheter drainage** x 10-14 days | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bladder-trauma |
| **Intraperitoneal Rupture** | **Blunt trauma** to full bladder | **CT cystography** (contrast in peritoneal cavity) | **Surgical repair** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bladder-trauma |

## Urethral Trauma
| Type | Mechanism | Diagnosis | Management | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Anterior (Bulbar)** | **Straddle injury** | **Retrograde urethrography (RUG)** | **Suprapubic tube (SPT)** ? Delayed repair (3-6 months) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urethral-trauma |
| **Posterior (Membranous)** | **Pelvic fracture** | **RUG** | **SPT** ? Delayed repair (3-6 months); **Primary realignment** (select) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urethral-trauma |

## Penile Trauma
| Type | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Penile Fracture** | **Blunt trauma** to erect penis | **Emergent surgical repair** (evacuate hematoma, Repair tunica albuginea) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/penile-trauma |
| **Amputation** | **Self-inflicted**, **Assault**, **Accidental** | **Microsurgical replantation** (cold ischemia time <24h) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/penile-trauma |

## Testicular Trauma
| Type | Mechanism | Management | URL |
