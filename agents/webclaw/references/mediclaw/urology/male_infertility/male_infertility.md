# Urology - Male Infertility

## Male Infertility - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Inability to achieve pregnancy after 12 months** of regular unprotected intercourse | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/infertility |
| **Epidemiology** | **Male factor** in 50% of infertile couples; Sole cause in 20-30% | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/infertility |

## Male Infertility - Causes and Treatment
| Cause | Diagnosis | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Varicocele** | Physical exam, Scrotal US | **Varicocelectomy** (if palpable and abnormal SA) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Obstructive Azoospermia** | Normal FSH, Normal testicular volume, Absent vas deferens | **Microsurgical reconstruction** (vasovasostomy, Vasoepididymostomy) or **Sperm retrieval + IVF/ICSI** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Non-Obstructive Azoospermia** | **Elevated FSH**, Small testes, Abnormal karyotype/Y microdeletion | **MicroTESE** (microdissection testicular sperm extraction) + IVF/ICSI | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Hypogonadotropic Hypogonadism** | **Low T**, **Low FSH/LH**, Normal prolactin | **hCG + FSH** (or hCG alone) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Ejaculatory Dysfunction** | Retrograde ejaculation, Anejaculation | **Sympathomimetics**, **Electroejaculation**, **Sperm retrieval** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Idiopathic** | Normal evaluation | **Empiric therapy** (clomiphene, Anastrozole), **IUI**, **IVF/ICSI** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
