# Urology - Pediatric Urology

## Common Pediatric Urologic Conditions
| Condition | Presentation | Diagnosis | Management | URL |
|-----------|--------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Cryptorchidism** | **Non-palpable or palpable undescended testis** | Physical exam, US (limited), Laparoscopy | **Orchiopexy** at **6-12 months** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/cryptorchidism |
| **Hypospadias** | **Ventral urethral meatus**, **Chordee**, **Hooded foreskin** | Physical exam | **Surgical repair** at **6-18 months** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hypospadias |
| **Phimosis** | **Non-retractile foreskin** | Physical exam | **Topical steroids**, **Circumcision** (if refractory) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/phimosis |
| **Paraphimosis** | **Retracted foreskin** cannot be reduced ? **Edema**, **Pain** | Physical exam | **Manual reduction** (ice, Compression, Osmotic agents), **Dorsal slit** (if failed) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/phimosis |
| **Vesicoureteral Reflux (VUR)** | **UTI**, **Hydronephrosis** | **VCUG** (grading I-V), **DMSA scan** (scarring) | **Observation** (grades I-II), **Antibiotic prophylaxis**, **Ureteral reimplantation** or **Endoscopic injection** (grades III-V, Breakthrough UTI) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/vesicoureteral-reflux |
| **Posterior Urethral Valves (PUV)** | **Bilateral hydronephrosis**, **Distended bladder**, **Poor stream** | **VCUG**, **Cystoscopy** | **Endoscopic valve ablation**, **Bladder drainage** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/posterior-urethral-valves |
| **UPJ Obstruction** | **Hydronephrosis**, **Flank pain**, **UTI** | **Ultrasound**, **MAG3 Lasix renogram** | **Observation** (asymptomatic, Stable), **Pyeloplasty** (symptomatic, Decreasing function) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/upj-obstruction |

## Circumcision
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Benefits** | **Reduced UTI** (infants), **Reduced STI/HIV**, **Reduced penile cancer**, **Reduced cervical cancer** (female partners) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/circumcision/ |
| **Risks** | **Bleeding**, **Infection**, **Meatal stenosis**, **Inadequate/Excessive skin removal** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/circumcision/ |
| **Contraindications** | **Hypospadias**, **Chordee**, **Buried penis**, **Bleeding diathesis** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/circumcision/ |
| **Analgesia** | **Dorsal penile nerve block**, **Ring block**, **EMLA cream** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/circumcision/ |
