# Urology - Urinary Diversion

## Types of Urinary Diversion
| Type | Description | Indications | URL |
|------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Ileal Conduit** | **Incontinent**; Ureters anastomosed to isolated ileal segment ? Stoma | **Most common**, **Simplest**, **Elderly**, **Comorbidities**, **Poor renal function** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
| **Continent Cutaneous Reservoir** | **Continent**; **Indiana pouch** (right colon), **Kock pouch** (ileum); Self-catheterization of stoma | **Desires continence**, **Unable to perform orthotopic neobladder** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
| **Orthotopic Neobladder** | **Continent**; Ileum/colon shaped into reservoir anastomosed to urethra | **Desires continence**, **Able to perform Valsalva voiding**, **No urethral disease**, **Good renal function**, **Good dexterity** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |

## Urinary Diversion - Complications
| Complication | Timing | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Urine Leak** | Early | **Conservative** (drains, Catheter), **Reoperation** (persistent) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
| **Ureteroenteric Stricture** | Late | **Endoscopic dilation/incision**, **Balloon dilation**, **Open revision** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
| **Metabolic Complications** | Late | **Ileum/colon**: Hyperchloremic metabolic acidosis (HCO3 loss); **Jejunum**: Hyponatremic, Hyperkalemic metabolic acidosis | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
| **Vitamin B12 Deficiency** | Late | **Terminal ileum resection** ? **Monitor B12**, **Supplement** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
| **Stomal Complications** | Variable | **Stenosis** (dilation, Revision), **Prolapse**, **Parastomal hernia**, **Skin irritation** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
| **Neobladder Complications** | Variable | **Incontinence** (day/night), **Hypercontinence** (retention - CIC), **UTI**, **Stones**, **Rupture** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/urinary-diversion |
