# Urology - Prostate Cancer

## Prostate Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | **Most common cancer in men** (excluding skin); 1 in 8 lifetime risk | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate |
| **Risk Factors** | **Age (>65)** , **African American**, **Family history**, **BRCA1/2**, **Lynch syndrome** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate |
| **Screening** | **PSA + DRE**; Shared decision-making starting age 50 (45 for high-risk, 40 for highest-risk) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate |

## Prostate Cancer - Risk Stratification (NCCN)
| Risk Group | Criteria | URL |
|------------|----------|-----|
| **Very Low** | T1c, Grade Group 1, PSA <10, <3 cores positive, =50% in any core, PSA density <0.15 | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **Low** | T1-T2a, Grade Group 1, PSA <10 | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **Favorable Intermediate** | T2b-T2c OR Grade Group 2 OR PSA 10-20; AND <50% cores positive | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **Unfavorable Intermediate** | Grade Group 3 OR =50% cores positive | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **High** | T3a OR Grade Group 4-5 OR PSA >20 | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **Very High** | T3b-T4 OR Primary Gleason pattern 5 | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |

## Prostate Cancer - Gleason Grade Group
| Grade Group | Gleason Score | Description | URL |
|-------------|---------------|-------------|-----|
| **1** | 3+3=6 | Only individual discrete well-formed glands | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **2** | 3+4=7 | Predominantly well-formed + lesser poorly formed/fused | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **3** | 4+3=7 | Predominantly poorly formed/fused + lesser well-formed | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **4** | 4+4=8; 3+5=8; 5+3=8 | Predominantly poorly formed/fused or lacking glands | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **5** | 9-10 | Lack of gland formation or necrosis | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |

## Localized Prostate Cancer - Treatment Options
| Risk Group | Treatment Options | URL |
|------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Very Low/Low** | **Active Surveillance** (preferred), Radical prostatectomy, Radiation therapy | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **Favorable Intermediate** | Active Surveillance (select), Radical prostatectomy, Radiation therapy + ADT (4-6 mo) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **Unfavorable Intermediate** | Radical prostatectomy + PLND, Radiation therapy + ADT (6 mo) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **High/Very High** | Radical prostatectomy + PLND, Radiation therapy + ADT (18-36 mo) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |

## Advanced Prostate Cancer - Systemic Therapy
| Setting | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| **Metastatic Hormone-Sensitive (mHSPC)** | **ADT + ARPI** (abiraterone, Enzalutamide, Apalutamide) or **ADT + Docetaxel** or **Triplet** (ADT + Docetaxel + ARPI) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
| **Metastatic Castration-Resistant (mCRPC)** | **ARPI**, **Docetaxel**, **Cabazitaxel**, **Sipuleucel-T**, **Radium-223** (bone-only), **PARP inhibitors** (BRCA/HRR), **Lu-177-PSMA-617** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/prostate |
