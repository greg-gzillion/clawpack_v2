## Bosniak Classification of Renal Cysts (2019)
| Category | CT/MRI Findings | Malignancy Risk | Management | URL |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Bosniak I** | Simple cyst: Thin wall, No septa, No calcifications | **0%** | No follow-up | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems |
| **Bosniak II** | Minimally complex: Few thin septa, Fine calcifications, <3 cm | **<1%** | No follow-up | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems |
| **Bosniak IIF** | Minimally complex needing follow-up: Multiple thin septa, Thickened calcifications, >3 cm | **5%** | Follow-up imaging | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems |
| **Bosniak III** | Indeterminate: Thick irregular septa/wall, Measurable enhancement | **50-60%** | Surgical excision or Active surveillance | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems |
| **Bosniak IV** | Clearly malignant: Solid enhancing component | **>90%** | Surgical excision | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems |
