# Urology - Hematuria

## Hematuria - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **=3 RBCs per high-power field** on microscopic urinalysis | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hematuria |
| **Gross Hematuria** | **Visible blood** in urine | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hematuria |
| **Microscopic Hematuria** | **Non-visible**, Detected on urinalysis | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hematuria |
| **Significance** | **Urologic malignancy** in 5-10% (gross), 1-3% (microscopic) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hematuria |

## Hematuria - Evaluation (AUA Guidelines)
| Risk Category | Criteria | Evaluation | URL |
|---------------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Low Risk** | **Never smoker**, **Age <50**, **<25 RBCs/HPF**, **No risk factors** | **Repeat UA in 6 months**; If persistent ? **Cystoscopy + Renal US** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hematuria |
| **Intermediate Risk** | **Age 50-60**, **Smoking history 10-30 pack-years**, **25-50 RBCs/HPF** | **Cystoscopy + CT urography** (preferred) or **Renal US + Retrograde pyelography** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hematuria |
| **High Risk** | **Age >60**, **Smoking >30 pack-years**, **Gross hematuria**, **>50 RBCs/HPF** | **Cystoscopy + CT urography** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/hematuria |
