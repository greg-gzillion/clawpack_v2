# Urology - Laparoscopic and Robotic Urology

## Robotic Urologic Procedures
| Procedure | Indications | Advantages | URL |
|-----------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **Robotic Radical Prostatectomy** | **Localized prostate cancer** | **Less blood loss**, **Shorter hospital stay**, **Faster recovery**, **Better visualization** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/prostate-cancer |
| **Robotic Partial Nephrectomy** | **Small renal masses** (<7 cm) | **Nephron-sparing**, **Less blood loss**, **Shorter warm ischemia** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/kidney-cancer |
| **Robotic Radical Cystectomy** | **Muscle-invasive bladder cancer** | **Less blood loss**, **Faster recovery**, **Lower transfusion rate** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bladder-cancer |
| **Robotic Pyeloplasty** | **UPJ obstruction** | **Minimally invasive**, **High success rate** (95%), **Faster recovery** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/upj-obstruction |
| **Robotic Sacrocolpopexy** | **Apical pelvic organ prolapse** | **Durable repair**, **Minimally invasive** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/pelvic-organ-prolapse |
