## Premature Ejaculation (PE)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Ejaculation within 1 minute** of penetration, **Inability to delay**, **Distress** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/premature-ejaculation |
| **Lifelong PE** | Present since first sexual experience | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/premature-ejaculation |
| **Acquired PE** | Developed after period of normal function | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/premature-ejaculation |
| **Treatment** | **SSRIs** (paroxetine, Sertraline, Fluoxetine - daily or PRN), **Clomipramine**, **Topical anesthetics** (lidocaine, Prilocaine), **Behavioral techniques** (stop-start, Squeeze) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/premature-ejaculation |

## Priapism
| Type | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Ischemic (Low-Flow)** | **Venous outflow obstruction**; **Painful**, **Rigid** | **Urologic emergency**: **Aspiration + Phenylephrine injection**, **Distal shunt** (Winter, Ebbehoj, Al-Ghorab), **Proximal shunt** (Quackels, Grayhack) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/priapism |
| **Non-Ischemic (High-Flow)** | **Arteriovenous fistula**; **Painless**, **Partial rigidity** | **Observation**, **Embolization** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/priapism |
| **Stuttering** | Recurrent ischemic episodes | **Treat acute**, **Prevent with PDE5 inhibitors** (daily), **Hormonal therapy** (GnRH agonist, Antiandrogens) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/priapism |
