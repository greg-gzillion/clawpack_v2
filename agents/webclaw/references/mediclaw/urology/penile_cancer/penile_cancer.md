# Urology - Penile Cancer

## Penile Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | Rare (<1% of male cancers in US); Higher in Africa, Asia, South America | https://www.cancer.gov/types/penile |
| **Risk Factors** | **HPV infection** (16, 18), **Phimosis**, **Smoking**, **Lichen sclerosus**, **Poor hygiene**, **HIV** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/penile |
| **Histology** | **Squamous cell carcinoma** (95%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/penile |

## Penile Cancer - Staging and Treatment
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Tis (CIS)** | **Topical 5-FU** or **Imiquimod**, **Laser**, **Glans resurfacing** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **Ta, T1a (Low-Grade)** | **Wide local excision**, **Laser**, **Mohs surgery**, **Glansectomy** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **T1b (High-Grade), T2** | **Partial penectomy**, **Total penectomy** (if inadequate length), **Radiation** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **T3-T4** | **Total penectomy** + **Perineal urethrostomy**, Neoadjuvant chemotherapy + Surgery | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **N+** | **Inguinal lymphadenectomy** (superficial ± deep), **Pelvic lymphadenectomy**, Adjuvant chemotherapy | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **M1** | **Palliative chemotherapy** (TIP: Paclitaxel, Ifosfamide, Cisplatin) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |

## Inguinal Lymph Node Management
| Clinical Nodes | Management | URL |
|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Non-Palpable** | **Dynamic sentinel lymph node biopsy (DSNB)** or **Modified inguinal lymphadenectomy** (if high-risk: T1b, T2, High-grade) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **Palpable Unilateral** | **Bilateral inguinal lymphadenectomy** (superficial ± deep) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **Fixed/Matted** | **Neoadjuvant chemotherapy** ? Bilateral inguinal lymphadenectomy | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
| **Positive Inguinal Nodes** | **Pelvic lymphadenectomy** (ipsilateral) + **Adjuvant chemotherapy** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/penile |
