## Chest X-Ray - Common Findings
| Finding | Description | Differential | URL |
|---------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **Air Bronchograms** | **Air-filled bronchi visible** against opacified lung | **Pneumonia**, **Edema**, **ARDS** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Silhouette Sign** | **Loss of normal border** between structures of similar density | **RML pneumonia** (loss of right heart border), **Lingular pneumonia** (loss of left heart border) | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Kerley B Lines** | **Horizontal lines** at lung bases | **Pulmonary edema** (interstitial) | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Deep Sulcus Sign** | **Deep costophrenic angle** | **Pneumothorax** (supine patient) | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Continuous Diaphragm Sign** | **Air outlines entire diaphragm** | **Pneumomediastinum** | https://radiopaedia.org |

## Chest CT - Patterns
| Pattern | Description | Differential | URL |
|---------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **Ground-Glass Opacity (GGO)** | **Hazy increased attenuation** with visible vessels | **Infection** (PJP, Viral), **ILD** (NSIP, DIP), **Edema**, **Hemorrhage** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Consolidation** | **Opacification obscuring vessels** | **Pneumonia**, **Edema**, **Hemorrhage**, **Malignancy** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Reticular** | **Linear opacities** | **ILD** (UIP, NSIP), **Edema** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Nodular** | **Small rounded opacities** | **Miliary TB**, **Metastases**, **Sarcoidosis**, **HP** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Honeycombing** | **Clustered cystic airspaces** | **UIP** (IPF, CTD-ILD) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Tree-in-Bud** | **Centrilobular nodules + branching** | **Infection** (TB, MAC), **CF**, **ABPA** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Mosaic Attenuation** | **Patchwork of densities** | **Small airways disease**, **Chronic PE**, **HP** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |

## Solitary Pulmonary Nodule (SPN)
| Feature | Benign | Malignant | URL |
|---------|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Size** | **<8 mm** | **>8-10 mm** (or growing) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Margins** | **Smooth**, Well-defined | **Spiculated**, **Lobulated**, **Irregular** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Calcification** | **Central**, **Laminated**, **Popcorn**, **Diffuse** | **Eccentric**, **Stippled**, **Absent** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Density** | **Fat** (hamartoma) | **Solid**, **Part-solid** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Stability** | **Stable =2 years** | **Growing** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
