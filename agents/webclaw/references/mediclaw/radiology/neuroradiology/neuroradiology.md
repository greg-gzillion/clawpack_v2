|-------|----|-----------|-------------|-----------|-----|

| **Meningioma** | Isointense | Variable | **Homogeneous**, **Dural tail** | No restriction | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Metastasis** | Hypointense | **Hyperintense**, Marked edema | **Ring or solid** | Variable | https://radiopaedia.org |
