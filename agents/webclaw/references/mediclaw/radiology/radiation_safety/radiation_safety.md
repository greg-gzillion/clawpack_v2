## Radiation Dose Units
| Unit | Definition | URL |
|------|------------|-----|
| **Absorbed Dose (Gy)** | Energy deposited per unit mass | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/radiation/measurement.html |
| **Equivalent Dose (Sv)** | Absorbed dose x Radiation weighting factor | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/radiation/measurement.html |
| **Effective Dose (Sv)** | Equivalent dose x Tissue weighting factor | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/radiation/measurement.html |
