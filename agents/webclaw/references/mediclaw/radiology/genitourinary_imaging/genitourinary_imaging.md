# Radiology - Genitourinary Imaging

## Renal Masses - Bosniak Classification (2019)
| Category | CT/MRI Findings | Malignancy Risk | Management | URL |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Bosniak I** | **Simple cyst**: Thin wall, No septa, No calcifications, Water density | **0%** | **No follow-up** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Bosniak II** | **Minimally complex**: Few thin septa (<1 mm), Fine calcifications, <3 cm, Non-enhancing | **<1%** | **No follow-up** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Bosniak IIF** | **Minimally complex needing follow-up**: Multiple thin septa, Thickened septa/calcifications, >3 cm | **5%** | **Follow-up imaging** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Bosniak III** | **Indeterminate**: Thick irregular septa/wall, Measurable enhancement | **50-60%** | **Surgical excision** or **Active surveillance** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Bosniak IV** | **Clearly malignant**: Solid enhancing component, Cystic + solid | **>90%** | **Surgical excision** | https://radiopaedia.org |

## Adrenal Incidentaloma
| Feature | Benign | Malignant/Suspicious | URL |
|---------|--------|----------------------|-----|
| **Size** | **<4 cm** | **>4 cm** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Attenuation (Non-Contrast CT)** | **=10 HU** (lipid-rich adenoma) | **>10 HU** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Washout** | **Absolute >60%**, **Relative >40%** (lipid-poor adenoma) | **<60% absolute**, **<40% relative** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Stability** | **Stable =1 year** | **Growing** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Heterogeneity** | **Homogeneous** | **Heterogeneous**, **Necrosis**, **Hemorrhage** | https://radiopaedia.org |
