## Bone Tumors - Key Features
| Tumor | Age | Location | X-Ray Findings | URL |
|-------|-----|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Osteosarcoma** | 10-25 | **Metaphysis** (distal femur, Proximal tibia) | **Codman triangle**, **Sunburst periostitis**, **Mixed lytic/sclerotic** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Ewing Sarcoma** | 5-20 | **Diaphysis** | **Permeative**, **Onion-skin periostitis**, **Soft tissue mass** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Chondrosarcoma** | 40-70 | Pelvis, Femur, Humerus | **Chondroid matrix** (rings and arcs), **Endosteal scalloping** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Giant Cell Tumor** | 20-40 | **Epiphysis** (distal femur, Proximal tibia) | **Lytic**, **Soap-bubble**, **No sclerosis**, **No periostitis** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Osteoid Osteoma** | 5-25 | Femur, Tibia, Spine | **Radiolucent nidus** with **surrounding sclerosis**, **Nocturnal pain** | https://radiopaedia.org |
