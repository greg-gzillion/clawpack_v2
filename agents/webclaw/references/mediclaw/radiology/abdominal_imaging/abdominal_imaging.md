# Radiology - Abdominal Imaging

## Acute Abdomen - Imaging Approach

|-------------------|-------------------|-----|
| **RUQ Pain** | **Ultrasound** (gallbladder, Biliary) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **RLQ Pain** | **CT with IV contrast** (appendicitis), **Ultrasound** (pediatric, Pregnancy) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **LLQ Pain** | **CT with IV contrast** (diverticulitis) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |

## Liver Imaging
| Modality | Indications | Key Findings | URL |
|----------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **Ultrasound** | **Screening**, **Biliary**, **Doppler** | **Steatosis** (hyperechoic), **Cirrhosis** (nodular surface), **Masses** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **CT** | **Characterization**, **Staging** | **Arterial phase** (hypervascular - HCC, Adenoma), **Portal venous** (hypovascular - metastases), **Delayed** (hemangioma, Cholangiocarcinoma) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **MRI** | **Characterization**, **Iron/Fat quantification** | **T1 in/out of phase** (fat), **T2** (cysts, Hemangioma), **Hepatobiliary phase** (FNH) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |

## LI-RADS (Liver Imaging Reporting and Data System)
| Category | Description | Management | URL |
|----------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **LR-1** | **Definitely Benign** | Routine surveillance | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/LI-Rads |
| **LR-2** | **Probably Benign** | Routine surveillance | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/LI-Rads |
| **LR-3** | **Intermediate Probability** | **Repeat imaging in 3-6 months** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/LI-Rads |
| **LR-4** | **Probably HCC** | **Multidisciplinary discussion**, **Biopsy** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/LI-Rads |
| **LR-5** | **Definitely HCC** | **Treat as HCC** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/LI-Rads |
| **LR-M** | **Probably Malignant, Not HCC Specific** | **Biopsy** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/LI-Rads |
| **LR-TIV** | **Tumor in Vein** | **Treat as HCC** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/LI-Rads |
