# Radiology - Overview

## What is Radiology?
Radiology is the medical specialty that uses medical imaging to diagnose and treat diseases within the body. It encompasses a variety of imaging modalities, including X-ray, computed tomography (CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), ultrasound (US), and nuclear medicine.
