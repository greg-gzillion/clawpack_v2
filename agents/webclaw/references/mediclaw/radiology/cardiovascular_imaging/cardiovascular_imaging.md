## Coronary Artery Calcium (CAC) Score
| Score | Interpretation | Cardiovascular Risk | URL |
|-------|----------------|---------------------|-----|
| **0** | **No calcified plaque** | **Very low** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hypertension |
| **1-10** | Minimal plaque | Low | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hypertension |
| **11-100** | Mild plaque | Moderate | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hypertension |
| **101-400** | Moderate plaque | Moderately high | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hypertension |
| **>400** | Extensive plaque | **High** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hypertension |

## Aortic Imaging
| Pathology | Preferred Modality | Key Findings | URL |
|-----------|---------------------|--------------|-----|
| **Aortic Dissection** | **CTA chest/abdomen/pelvis** | **Intimal flap**, **True and false lumens** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Aortic Aneurysm** | **CTA**, **Ultrasound** (screening), **MRI** (surveillance) | **Fusiform or saccular dilation** >3 cm (abdominal), >4 cm (thoracic) | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Aortic Stenosis** | **Echocardiography** (primary), **CTA** (TAVR planning) | **Valve area <1 cmｲ**, **Mean gradient >40 mmHg** | https://www.heart.org |
| **Coarctation of Aorta** | **CTA**, **MRI** | **Focal narrowing** distal to left subclavian, **Collaterals** | https://radiopaedia.org |
