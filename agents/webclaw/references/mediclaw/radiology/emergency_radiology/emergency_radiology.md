| **Acute Diverticulitis** | **CT with IV contrast** | **Colonic diverticula**, **Pericolic stranding**, **Abscess**, **Free air** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Bowel Obstruction** | **CT with IV and oral contrast** | **Dilated bowel loops**, **Transition point**, **Decompressed distal bowel** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Acute Cholecystitis** | **Ultrasound** (first-line), **CT** | **Gallstones**, **Wall thickening (>3 mm)**, **Pericholecystic fluid**, **Sonographic Murphy sign** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Acute Pancreatitis** | **CT with IV contrast** | **Peripancreatic stranding**, **Fluid collections**, **Necrosis** (non-enhancing) | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Nephrolithiasis** | **Non-contrast CT** | **Hyperdense stone** in kidney/ureter, **Hydronephrosis**, **Perinephric stranding** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Testicular Torsion** | **Ultrasound with Doppler** | **Absent flow**, **Enlarged**, **Heterogeneous**, **"Whirlpool sign"** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Ovarian Torsion** | **Ultrasound with Doppler** | **Enlarged ovary**, **Peripheral follicles**, **Absent flow**, **"Whirlpool sign"** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Aortic Dissection** | **CTA chest/abdomen/pelvis** | **Intimal flap**, **True and false lumens** | https://radiopaedia.org |
