## RECIST 1.1 (Response Evaluation Criteria in Solid Tumors)
| Category | Definition | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Complete Response (CR)** | **Disappearance of all target lesions** | https://recist.eortc.org |
| **Partial Response (PR)** | **=30% decrease** in sum of diameters | https://recist.eortc.org |
| **Progressive Disease (PD)** | **=20% increase** in sum of diameters **OR** **new lesions** | https://recist.eortc.org |
| **Stable Disease (SD)** | Neither PR nor PD | https://recist.eortc.org |

## Lugano Classification (Lymphoma Response)
| Category | Definition | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Complete Response (CR)** | **Deauville 1-3** on PET-CT | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Partial Response (PR)** | **Deauville 4-5** with **decreased uptake** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Stable Disease (SD)** | **Deauville 4-5** with **no change** | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **Progressive Disease (PD)** | **New lesions** or **=50% increase** in SPD | https://radiopaedia.org |

## Deauville Criteria (PET-CT in Lymphoma)
| Score | PET-CT Finding | Interpretation | URL |
|-------|----------------|----------------|-----|
| **1** | **No uptake** | Negative | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **2** | **Uptake = mediastinum** | Negative | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **3** | **Uptake > mediastinum but = liver** | **Negative** (end of treatment), **Positive** (interim) | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **4** | **Uptake moderately > liver** | Positive | https://radiopaedia.org |
| **5** | **Uptake markedly > liver** and/or **new lesions** | Positive | https://radiopaedia.org |

## Cancer of Unknown Primary (CUP) - Imaging Approach
| Step | Imaging | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| **1. Initial** | **CT chest/abdomen/pelvis with contrast** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **2. If CT negative** | **PET-CT** (identifies primary in 25-40%) | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **3. Women with axillary adenopathy** | **Mammography + Ultrasound** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **4. Men with blastic bone mets** | **PSA**, **Prostate MRI** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
