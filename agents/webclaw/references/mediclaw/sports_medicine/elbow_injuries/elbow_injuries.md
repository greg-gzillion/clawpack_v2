## Common Elbow Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Lateral Epicondylitis (Tennis Elbow)** | **Repetitive wrist extension** | **Pain over lateral epicondyle**, **Worse with gripping** | **Counterforce brace**, **PT** (eccentric strengthening), **CS injection**, **PRP**, **Surgery** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/tennis-elbow-lateral-epicondylitis/ |
| **Medial Epicondylitis (Golfer's Elbow)** | **Repetitive wrist flexion/pronation** | **Pain over medial epicondyle** | **Counterforce brace**, **PT**, **CS injection**, **Surgery** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/golfers-elbow-medial-epicondylitis/ |
| **UCL Injury** | **Repetitive valgus stress** (throwing) | **Medial elbow pain**, **Valgus instability**, **? Velocity/control** | **PT**, **Platelet-rich plasma**, **UCL reconstruction** (Tommy John) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/ulnar-collateral-ligament-ucl-injuries/ |
| **Olecranon Bursitis** | **Direct trauma**, Repetitive pressure | **Swelling over olecranon**, **± Erythema/warmth** (if infected) | **Rest**, **Padding**, **Aspiration** (if infected or tense), **Antibiotics** (if infected) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/elbow-olecranon-bursitis/ |
| **Distal Biceps Tendon Rupture** | **Eccentric load** with elbow flexed | **Acute pain**, **Ecchymosis**, **Palpable defect**, **Weakness in supination/flexion** | **Surgical repair** (young/active), **Non-operative** (older/low-demand) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/biceps-tendon-tear-at-the-elbow/ |
