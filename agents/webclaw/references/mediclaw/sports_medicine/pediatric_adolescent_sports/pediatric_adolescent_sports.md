# Sports Medicine - Pediatric and Adolescent Sports Medicine

## Unique Considerations
| Consideration | Description | URL |
|---------------|-------------|-----|
| **Growth Plates (Physis)** | **Weakest link** in musculoskeletal chain; **More vulnerable to injury than ligaments/tendons** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/sports-medicine-and-fitness/ |
| **Apophyses** | **Traction apophysitis** common (Osgood-Schlatter, Sever, Little League elbow/shoulder) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/sports-medicine-and-fitness/ |
| **Skeletal Maturity** | **Injury patterns differ** from adults; **Salter-Harris fractures** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/sports-medicine-and-fitness/ |
| **Concussion** | **Longer recovery**, **Return-to-learn before return-to-play** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/concussions/ |

## Common Pediatric Sports Injuries
| Injury | Age | Mechanism | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Osgood-Schlatter Disease** | **10-15 years** | **Traction apophysitis** of tibial tubercle | **Activity modification**, **Stretching**, **Ice**, **Self-limited** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/osgood-schlatter-disease-knee-pain/ |
| **Sinding-Larsen-Johansson** | **10-13 years** | **Traction apophysitis** of inferior patella | **Activity modification**, **Stretching**, **Self-limited** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/sinding-larsen-johansson-syndrome/ |
| **Sever Disease** | **8-14 years** | **Traction apophysitis** of calcaneus | **Heel lifts**, **Stretching**, **Activity modification**, **Self-limited** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/sever-disease/ |
| **Little League Elbow** | **8-15 years** | **Apophysitis** of medial epicondyle | **Rest from throwing**, **PT**, **Pitch count limits** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/little-league-elbow/ |
| **Little League Shoulder** | **11-16 years** | **Apophysitis** of proximal humerus | **Rest from throwing**, **PT**, **Pitch count limits** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/little-league-shoulder/ |
| **Salter-Harris Fractures** | **Skeletally immature** | **Fracture through growth plate** | **Immobilization** (I-II), **Surgical fixation** (III-V) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/growth-plate-fractures/ |
| **ACL Tear (Pediatric)** | **Adolescents** | **Non-contact pivoting** | **Physeal-sparing reconstruction** or **Non-operative** until skeletal maturity | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/anterior-cruciate-ligament-acl-injuries/ |
| **Osteochondritis Dissecans (OCD)** | **10-20 years** | **Idiopathic** or **Repetitive trauma** | **Non-weight bearing** (stable juvenile), **Surgical fixation** (unstable, Adult) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/osteochondritis-dissecans/ |

## Salter-Harris Fracture Classification
| Type | Fracture Pattern | Prognosis | URL |
|------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Type I** | **Through physis only** | **Excellent** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/growth-plate-fractures/ |
| **Type II** | **Through physis + metaphysis** | **Excellent** (most common) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/growth-plate-fractures/ |
| **Type III** | **Through physis + epiphysis** (intraarticular) | **Good** (requires anatomic reduction) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/growth-plate-fractures/ |
| **Type IV** | **Through metaphysis + physis + epiphysis** | **Fair** (growth disturbance risk) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/growth-plate-fractures/ |
| **Type V** | **Crush injury** of physis | **Poor** (growth arrest) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/growth-plate-fractures/ |
