# Sports Medicine - Dermatology in Sports

## Common Skin Conditions in Athletes
| Condition | Description | Treatment | Prevention | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Tinea Corporis (Ringworm)** | **Annular, scaly plaques** with central clearing | **Topical antifungals** (clotrimazole, terbinafine), **Oral terbinafine** (extensive) | **Good hygiene**, **Avoid sharing towels/equipment**, **Dry thoroughly** | https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/index.html |
| **Tinea Pedis (Athlete's Foot)** | **Maceration, scaling, itching** between toes | **Topical antifungals**, **Keep feet dry**, **Change socks frequently** | **Wear shower shoes**, **Dry feet thoroughly** | https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/index.html |
| **Tinea Cruris (Jock Itch)** | **Erythematous, scaly plaques** in groin | **Topical antifungals** | **Loose-fitting clothing**, **Dry thoroughly** | https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/index.html |
| **Herpes Gladiatorum** | **Vesicular rash** on face, neck, arms | **Valacyclovir/Acyclovir**, **Avoid contact sports until lesions crusted** | **Skin checks**, **Avoid sharing equipment** | https://www.cdc.gov/herpes/index.html |
| **Molluscum Contagiosum** | **Umbilicated papules** | **Observation** (self-limited), **Curettage**, **Cryotherapy** | **Cover lesions** (if possible), **Avoid sharing towels** | https://www.cdc.gov/poxvirus/molluscum-contagiosum/index.html |
| **Impetigo** | **Honey-crusted erosions** | **Topical mupirocin** (limited), **Oral cephalexin** (extensive) | **Good hygiene**, **Avoid close contact** | https://www.cdc.gov/groupastrep/diseases-hcp/impetigo.html |
| **Folliculitis/Furunculosis** | **Pustules** around hair follicles | **Warm compresses**, **Mupirocin**, **Oral antibiotics** (MRSA coverage) | **Shower after activity**, **Clean equipment** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/folliculitis |
| **Acne Mechanica** | **Acneiform eruption** from **occlusion/friction** (helmets, pads) | **Topical benzoyl peroxide**, **Topical retinoids**, **Shower after activity** | **Clean equipment**, **Moisture-wicking clothing** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/acne/causes/sports-equipment |
| **Plantar Warts** | **Hyperkeratotic papules** on soles, **Interrupted skin lines**, **Black dots** | **Salicylic acid**, **Cryotherapy** | **Wear shower shoes**, **Keep feet dry** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/warts-overview |

## Return to Play with Skin Infections
| Infection | Return to Play Criteria | URL |
|-----------|------------------------|-----|
| **Tinea** | **After 72 hours of treatment** (lesions covered if possible) | https://www.nata.org/practice-patient-care/health-issues/skin-infections |
| **Herpes Gladiatorum** | **No new lesions for 72 hours**, **All lesions crusted**, **=120 hours of antiviral therapy** | https://www.nata.org/practice-patient-care/health-issues/skin-infections |
| **Molluscum** | **Lesions covered** (if possible) | https://www.nata.org/practice-patient-care/health-issues/skin-infections |
| **Impetigo** | **No new lesions for 48 hours**, **=48 hours of antibiotics** | https://www.nata.org/practice-patient-care/health-issues/skin-infections |
| **MRSA** | **No new lesions for 48 hours**, **=72 hours of antibiotics**, **No drainage** | https://www.nata.org/practice-patient-care/health-issues/skin-infections |
