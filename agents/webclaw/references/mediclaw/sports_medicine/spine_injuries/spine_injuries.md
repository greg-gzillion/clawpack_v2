## Common Spine Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Acute Low Back Pain** | **Lifting**, **Twisting**, **Overuse** | **Non-specific low back pain**, **Muscle spasm** | **NSAIDs**, **Muscle relaxants** (short-term), **Stay active**, **PT** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/low-back-pain/ |
| **Lumbar Radiculopathy** | **Disc herniation**, **Foraminal stenosis** | **Dermatomal pain/weakness/sensory loss**, **+ Straight leg raise** | **NSAIDs**, **PT**, **Epidural steroid injection**, **Microdiscectomy** (refractory, Progressive deficit) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/herniated-disk/ |
| **Lumbar Spondylolysis** | **Repetitive hyperextension** (gymnastics, Football linemen, Weightlifting) | **Focal low back pain**, **Worse with extension** | **Rest**, **PT** (core strengthening, Avoid extension), **Bracing** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/spondylolysis-and-spondylolisthesis/ |
| **Lumbar Spondylolisthesis** | **Bilateral pars defects** ? **Anterior slippage** | **Low back pain**, **Hamstring tightness**, **Radiculopathy** | **PT** (core strengthening, Avoid extension), **Surgical fusion** (refractory, Unstable) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/spondylolysis-and-spondylolisthesis/ |
| **Lumbar Disc Herniation** | **Flexion + rotation** | **Radicular pain** (below knee), **+ Straight leg raise**, **Weakness/Sensory loss** | **NSAIDs**, **PT**, **Epidural steroid**, **Microdiscectomy** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/herniated-disk/ |
