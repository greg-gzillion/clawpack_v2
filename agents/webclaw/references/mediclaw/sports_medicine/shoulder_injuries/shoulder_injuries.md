## Common Shoulder Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Rotator Cuff Tendinopathy** | **Overuse**, Repetitive overhead activity | **Pain with overhead activity**, **Night pain**, **Weakness** | **PT**, **NSAIDs**, **Subacromial CS injection**, **Surgery** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/rotator-cuff-tears/ |
| **Rotator Cuff Tear** | **Acute trauma** or **chronic degeneration** | **Pain**, **Weakness** (especially abduction/external rotation) | **PT** (partial/small), **Surgical repair** (full-thickness, Young/active) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/rotator-cuff-tears/ |
| **Shoulder Impingement** | **Subacromial space narrowing** | **Painful arc** (60-120°), **+ Neer/Hawkins** | **PT** (scapular stabilization, Rotator cuff strengthening), **Subacromial CS injection** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-impingementrotator-cuff-tendinitis/ |
| **Labral Tear (SLAP)** | **Repetitive overhead**, Fall on outstretched arm, **Traction** | **Deep shoulder pain**, **Clicking**, **"Dead arm"** | **PT**, **Surgical repair** (young overhead athletes) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/slap-tears/ |
| **Glenohumeral Instability** | **Traumatic dislocation** (anterior) or **Atraumatic multidirectional** | **Apprehension**, **Recurrent subluxation/dislocation** | **PT** (atraumatic), **Surgical stabilization** (traumatic, Young/active) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-instability/ |
| **AC Joint Separation** | **Direct fall on shoulder** | **Superior shoulder pain**, **Visible deformity**, **Piano key sign** | **Type I-II**: Sling, PT; **Type III**: Controversial; **Type IV-VI**: Surgical | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/ac-joint-injuries/ |
| **Adhesive Capsulitis (Frozen Shoulder)** | **Idiopathic** or **Post-traumatic/Post-op** | **Progressive pain and stiffness** (freezing, frozen, thawing) | **PT**, **Intraarticular CS injection**, **MUA** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/frozen-shoulder/ |
