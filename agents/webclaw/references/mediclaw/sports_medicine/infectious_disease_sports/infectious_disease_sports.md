# Sports Medicine - Infectious Disease in Sports

## Common Infections in Athletes
| Infection | Transmission | Prevention | Return to Play | URL |
|-----------|--------------|------------|----------------|-----|
| **Upper Respiratory Infection** | **Respiratory droplets** | **Hand hygiene**, **Avoid sharing water bottles** | **"Neck check"**: Above neck = OK if mild; Below neck (fever, myalgias) = Rest | https://www.cdc.gov/flu/index.htm |
| **Infectious Mononucleosis** | **Saliva** | **Avoid sharing water bottles**, **Towels** | **No contact sports** for **=3-4 weeks** (splenic rupture risk), **Return when asymptomatic, afebrile** | https://www.cdc.gov/epstein-barr/index.html |
| **Viral Gastroenteritis** | **Fecal-oral** | **Hand hygiene**, **Disinfect surfaces** | **Symptom resolution**, **Adequate hydration** | https://www.cdc.gov/norovirus/index.html |
| **MRSA** | **Skin-to-skin contact**, **Shared equipment** | **Shower after activity**, **Clean equipment**, **Cover wounds** | **No new lesions for 48h**, **=72h antibiotics**, **No drainage** | https://www.cdc.gov/mrsa/community/index.html |
| **COVID-19** | **Respiratory droplets**, **Aerosols** | **Vaccination**, **Hand hygiene**, **Isolate when sick** | **Per CDC guidelines** (symptom-based) | https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/index.html |

## Splenic Rupture Risk in Mononucleosis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Risk Period** | **First 3-4 weeks** after symptom onset | https://www.cdc.gov/epstein-barr/index.html |
| **Splenomegaly** | **Present in 50-100%** of cases | https://www.cdc.gov/epstein-barr/index.html |
| **Rupture Risk** | **0.1-0.5%** (most common in **males**, **Contact sports**) | https://www.cdc.gov/epstein-barr/index.html |
| **Return to Play** | **=3-4 weeks**, **Asymptomatic**, **Afebrile**, **No splenomegaly** (imaging not routinely required) | https://www.cdc.gov/epstein-barr/index.html |
