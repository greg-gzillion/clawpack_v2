# Sports Medicine - Sports Nutrition

## Energy and Macronutrient Needs for Athletes
| Nutrient | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Carbohydrate** | **3-12 g/kg/day** (depending on training intensity/duration) | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **Protein** | **1.2-2.0 g/kg/day** (endurance: 1.2-1.4; strength: 1.6-2.0) | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **Fat** | **20-35%** of total calories | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **Fluid** | **Individualized** based on sweat rate | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |

## Pre-Exercise Nutrition
| Timing | Recommendation | URL |
|--------|----------------|-----|
| **3-4 hours before** | **Full meal**: High carbohydrate, Moderate protein, Low fat/fiber | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **1-2 hours before** | **Light snack**: Easily digestible carbohydrate | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **<1 hour before** | **Small carbohydrate snack** or **carbohydrate mouth rinse** | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |

## During Exercise Nutrition
| Duration | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **<60 minutes** | **Water** sufficient | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **60-90 minutes** | **30-60 g carbohydrate/hour** | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **>90 minutes** | **60-90 g carbohydrate/hour** (multiple transportable carbohydrates: glucose + fructose) | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **Fluid** | **Individualized** (prevent >2% body weight loss) | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **Electrolytes** | **Sodium** (especially in heat, heavy sweaters) | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |

## Post-Exercise Recovery Nutrition
| Timing | Recommendation | URL |
|--------|----------------|-----|
| **0-2 hours after** | **Carbohydrate**: **1.0-1.2 g/kg/hour** for 4 hours (if glycogen depletion); **Protein**: **0.3-0.4 g/kg** (20-40 g) high-quality protein | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
| **Fluid** | **150% of fluid lost** (16-24 oz per pound lost) | https://www.eatright.org/fitness/sports-and-performance |
