## Burners/Stingers
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Transient neurapraxia** of **brachial plexus** or **cervical nerve root** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Burners-and-Stingers |
| **Mechanism** | **Traction** (shoulder depression + neck lateral flexion) or **Compression** (extension + rotation) | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Burners-and-Stingers |
| **Symptoms** | **Unilateral burning/shooting pain** radiating down arm, **Weakness** (deltoid, biceps), **Numbness** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Burners-and-Stingers |
| **Duration** | **Seconds to minutes** (resolves quickly) | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Burners-and-Stingers |
