## Common Knee Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **ACL Tear** | **Non-contact pivoting**, **Hyperextension**, **Direct blow** | **"Pop"**, **Immediate swelling** (hemarthrosis), **Instability** | **Surgical reconstruction** (young/active), **Non-operative** (older/low-demand) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/anterior-cruciate-ligament-acl-injuries/ |
| **PCL Tear** | **Dashboard injury**, **Fall on flexed knee** | **Knee pain**, **Swelling**, **Posterior sag** | **Non-operative** (isolated grade I-II), **Surgical reconstruction** (grade III, Multiligament) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/posterior-cruciate-ligament-pcl-injuries/ |
| **MCL Tear** | **Valgus stress** | **Medial knee pain**, **Tender over MCL** | **Non-operative** (bracing, PT), **Surgical repair** (grade III with instability) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/mcl-injuries/ |
| **LCL/PLC Tear** | **Varus stress**, **Hyperextension** | **Lateral knee pain**, **Varus instability** | **Surgical repair/reconstruction** (often combined) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/lcl-injuries/ |
| **Meniscus Tear** | **Twisting** with planted foot, **Degenerative** | **Joint line pain**, **Clicking**, **Locking**, **Swelling** | **PT** (degenerative), **Arthroscopic partial meniscectomy** or **Meniscus repair** (young, Peripheral) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/meniscus-tears/ |
| **Patellar Dislocation** | **Non-contact twisting**, **Direct blow** | **Acute lateral displacement**, **Apprehension** | **Closed reduction**, **PT** (VMO strengthening), **Surgical stabilization** (recurrent, Osteochondral fracture) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/patellar-dislocation/ |
| **Patellofemoral Pain Syndrome** | **Overuse**, **Malalignment**, **Weak VMO/hip abductors** | **Anterior knee pain**, **Worse with stairs, Prolonged sitting** | **PT** (VMO/hip strengthening), **Activity modification**, **Patellar taping** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/patellofemoral-pain-syndrome/ |
| **Patellar/Quadriceps Tendinopathy** | **Repetitive jumping** (jumper's knee) | **Pain at inferior/superior pole of patella** | **PT** (eccentric strengthening), **Activity modification**, **PRP** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/patellar-tendon-tear/ |
| **Iliotibial Band Syndrome** | **Overuse** (running, cycling) | **Lateral knee pain**, **Worse with activity** | **PT** (stretching, Hip abductor strengthening), **Activity modification** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/iliotibial-band-syndrome/ |

## Knee Dislocation
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Disruption of tibiofemoral articulation** (often reduced by presentation) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-dislocation/ |
| **Mechanism** | **High-energy trauma** (MVC, Fall), **Low-energy** (obese, Sports) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-dislocation/ |
| **Associated Injuries** | **Multiligament**, **Popliteal artery** (5-15%), **Peroneal nerve** (25%) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-dislocation/ |
| **Vascular Evaluation** | **ABI** (<0.9 abnormal), **CT angiography** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-dislocation/ |
| **Neurologic Evaluation** | **Peroneal nerve** (foot drop, Numbness dorsal foot) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-dislocation/ |
| **Treatment** | **Emergent reduction**, **Vascular surgery consultation**, **Delayed ligament reconstruction** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-dislocation/ |
