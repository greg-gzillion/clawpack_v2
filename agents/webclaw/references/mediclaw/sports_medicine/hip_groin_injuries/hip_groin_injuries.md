## Common Hip and Groin Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Femoroacetabular Impingement (FAI)** | **Cam** (femoral head-neck offset) or **Pincer** (acetabular overcoverage) | **Deep anterior hip/groin pain**, **Worse with sitting, pivoting** | **PT** (core/gluteal strengthening, Avoid deep flexion), **Arthroscopic surgery** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/femoroacetabular-impingement/ |
| **Labral Tear** | **FAI**, **Trauma**, **Repetitive pivoting** | **Deep groin pain**, **Clicking**, **Catching**, **Mechanical symptoms** | **PT**, **Intraarticular CS injection**, **Arthroscopic repair** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hip-labral-tear/ |
| **Hip Flexor Strain** | **Eccentric overload** (sprinting, Kicking) | **Anterior hip pain**, **Worse with hip flexion** | **Rest**, **PT** (stretching, Strengthening), **Gradual return** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hip-flexor-strain/ |
| **Adductor Strain** | **Sudden change of direction**, **Kicking** | **Medial thigh/groin pain**, **Tender adductor origin** | **Rest**, **PT** (adductor strengthening), **Gradual return** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/groin-strain/ |
| **Hamstring Strain** | **Eccentric overload** (sprinting) | **Posterior thigh pain**, **Ecchymosis** (severe) | **Rest**, **PT** (eccentric strengthening - Nordic hamstring), **PRP** (controversial) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hamstring-muscle-injuries/ |
| **Proximal Hamstring Tendinopathy** | **Repetitive overload** (running, Cycling) | **Deep buttock pain**, **Worse with sitting, Acceleration** | **PT** (eccentric strengthening), **Shockwave therapy**, **CS injection** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hamstring-muscle-injuries/ |
| **Greater Trochanteric Pain Syndrome** | **Overuse**, **Weak hip abductors** | **Lateral hip pain**, **Tender over greater trochanter**, **Pain lying on side** | **PT** (hip abductor strengthening), **CS injection**, **Shockwave** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/greater-trochanteric-pain-syndrome/ |
| **Sports Hernia (Athletic Pubalgia)** | **Repetitive twisting**, **Cutting** | **Chronic groin pain**, **Worse with activity**, **Tender over pubic tubercle/conjoint tendon** | **PT** (core strengthening), **Surgical repair** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/sports-hernia-athletic-pubalgia/ |

## Hamstring Strain Grading
| Grade | Description | Return to Play | URL |
|-------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| **Grade 1** | **Mild** (few fibers), No loss of strength | **1-3 weeks** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hamstring-muscle-injuries/ |
| **Grade 2** | **Moderate** (partial tear), Weakness, Ecchymosis | **3-8 weeks** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hamstring-muscle-injuries/ |
| **Grade 3** | **Severe** (complete tear), Palpable defect, Severe weakness | **3-6 months** (may require surgery) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hamstring-muscle-injuries/ |
