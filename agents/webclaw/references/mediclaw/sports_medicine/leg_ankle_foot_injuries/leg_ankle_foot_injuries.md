## Common Leg, Ankle, and Foot Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Ankle Sprain (Lateral)** | **Inversion injury** | **Lateral ankle pain/swelling**, **Ecchymosis** | **RICE**, **Functional rehabilitation**, **Brace/tape** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/sprained-ankle/ |
| **High Ankle Sprain (Syndesmosis)** | **External rotation**, **Dorsiflexion** | **Pain with dorsiflexion/external rotation**, **+ Squeeze test** | **Non-weight bearing**, **Longer recovery** (6-12 weeks), **Surgery** (unstable) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/high-ankle-sprain/ |
| **Achilles Tendinopathy** | **Overuse**, **? Training intensity** | **Posterior heel pain**, **Morning stiffness**, **Thickened tendon** | **PT** (eccentric strengthening), **Activity modification**, **Shockwave** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/achilles-tendinitis/ |
| **Achilles Tendon Rupture** | **Eccentric overload**, **Sudden push-off** | **"Pop"**, **Palpable gap**, **Weak plantarflexion**, **+ Thompson** | **Surgical repair** (young/active) or **Functional rehabilitation** (older) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/achilles-tendon-rupture/ |
| **Plantar Fasciitis** | **Overuse**, **Tight Achilles**, **Obesity** | **First-step pain**, **Tender medial calcaneal tubercle** | **Stretching** (Achilles, Plantar fascia), **Night splint**, **CS injection** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/plantar-fasciitis-and-bone-spurs/ |
| **Medial Tibial Stress Syndrome (Shin Splints)** | **Overuse**, **Running on hard surfaces**, **Poor footwear** | **Diffuse posteromedial tibial pain** | **Rest**, **Activity modification**, **PT** (strengthening, Gait analysis) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shin-splints/ |
| **Stress Fracture** | **Repetitive loading**, **? Training intensity**, **Female athlete triad/RED-S** | **Focal bone pain**, **Worse with weight-bearing**, **+ Hop test** | **Rest/Immobilization**, **Address underlying factors** (nutrition, Bone health) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/stress-fractures/ |
| **Exertional Compartment Syndrome** | **Repetitive exercise** | **Aching/cramping leg pain** with exercise, **Relieved by rest** | **Activity modification**, **Gait retraining**, **Fasciotomy** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/compartment-syndrome/ |
| **Acute Compartment Syndrome** | **Trauma**, **Fracture**, **Reperfusion** | **Pain out of proportion**, **Pain with passive stretch**, **Paresthesias**, **Pulselessness** (late) | **Emergent fasciotomy** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/compartment-syndrome/ |
| **Jones Fracture** | **Inversion injury**, **Repetitive stress** | **Pain over base of 5th metatarsal** | **Non-weight bearing cast** (non-displaced) or **Surgical fixation** (athletes, Displaced) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/toe-and-forefoot-fractures/ |

## Ottawa Ankle Rules
| Rule | Indication for X-Ray | URL |
|------|---------------------|-----|
| **Pain in malleolar zone** AND **Bone tenderness at posterior edge of distal 6 cm of lateral malleolus** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in malleolar zone** AND **Bone tenderness at posterior edge of distal 6 cm of medial malleolus** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in malleolar zone** AND **Inability to bear weight immediately and in ED (4 steps)** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in midfoot zone** AND **Bone tenderness at base of 5th metatarsal** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in midfoot zone** AND **Bone tenderness at navicular** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |

|-------|----------------|--------|----------------|-----|
