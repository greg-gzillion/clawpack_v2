# Sports Medicine - Environmental Illness

## Heat Illness
| Condition | Symptoms | Core Temp | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Heat Cramps** | **Painful muscle spasms** (calves, Abdomen) | **Normal** | **Rest**, **Stretching**, **Oral hydration with electrolytes** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |
| **Heat Syncope** | **Dizziness**, **Fainting** after exercise | **Normal** | **Supine with legs elevated**, **Hydration** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |
| **Heat Exhaustion** | **Fatigue**, **Weakness**, **Headache**, **Nausea**, **Dizziness**, **Profuse sweating** | **<40蚓 (104蚌)** | **Move to cool environment**, **Remove excess clothing**, **Cool with fans/cold packs**, **Oral/IV hydration** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |
| **Exertional Heat Stroke** | **CNS dysfunction** (confusion, Agitation, Seizure, Coma), **Hot/dry skin** (may still sweat), **Tachycardia**, **Hypotension** | **>40蚓 (104蚌)** | **MEDICAL EMERGENCY**: **Immediate whole-body cold water immersion**, **IV fluids**, **Monitor ABCs** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |

## Wet Bulb Globe Temperature (WBGT)
| WBGT (蚌) | Risk Level | Recommendations | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|-----|
| **<80** | **Low** | Normal activities, Monitor for symptoms | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/wbgt.html |
| **80-85** | **Moderate** | **? Rest breaks**, **Encourage hydration** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/wbgt.html |
| **85-88** | **High** | **Additional breaks**, **Limit intense activity** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/wbgt.html |
| **>88** | **Extreme** | **Cancel/postpone** outdoor activities | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/wbgt.html |

## Cold Illness
| Condition | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Frostnip** | **Pale/numb skin**, **No tissue damage** | **Rewarm** with body heat/warm water | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |
| **Frostbite** | **Hard, pale, cold skin**, **Numbness**, **Blisters** (severe) | **Rapid rewarming** in warm water (37-39蚓), **Avoid rubbing**, **Seek medical care** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |
| **Hypothermia** | **Shivering**, **Confusion**, **Drowsiness**, **Slurred speech** | **Remove wet clothing**, **Insulate**, **Warm fluids**, **Active rewarming** (severe) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |

## Lightning Safety
| Guideline | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **30-30 Rule** | **Seek shelter** if **thunder =30 seconds after lightning**; **Wait 30 minutes after last thunder** to resume | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/features/lightning-safety/index.html |
| **Safe Shelter** | **Fully enclosed building** or **Hard-topped metal vehicle** | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/features/lightning-safety/index.html |
| **Unsafe Locations** | **Open fields**, **Under trees**, **Near water**, **Open shelters** (dugouts, picnic shelters) | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/features/lightning-safety/index.html |
