# Sports Medicine - Overview

## What is Sports Medicine?
Sports medicine is the medical specialty focused on the prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of injuries and illnesses related to physical activity and sports participation. It encompasses care for athletes of all ages and skill levels, from recreational to elite.
