## Common Hand and Wrist Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Scaphoid Fracture** | **Fall on outstretched hand (FOOSH)** | **Anatomic snuffbox tenderness**, **Pain with wrist extension** | **Thumb spica cast** (non-displaced waist), **Surgical fixation** (displaced, Proximal pole) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/scaphoid-fracture-of-the-wrist/ |
| **Distal Radius Fracture** | **FOOSH** | **Wrist pain**, **Deformity** (dinner fork) | **Closed reduction + Cast** (stable), **Surgical fixation** (unstable, Intraarticular) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/distal-radius-fractures-broken-wrist/ |
| **TFCC Tear** | **FOOSH with rotation**, **Ulnar deviation** | **Ulnar-sided wrist pain**, **Clicking**, **Weak grip** | **Splint/PT**, **Arthroscopic repair** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/tfcc-tears/ |
| **De Quervain Tenosynovitis** | **Repetitive thumb abduction/extension** | **Pain over radial styloid**, **+ Finkelstein** | **Thumb spica splint**, **CS injection**, **Surgical release** (refractory) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/de-quervains-tendinosis/ |
| **Gamekeeper's/Skier's Thumb** | **Forced thumb abduction** (hyperextension) | **Pain/swelling over UCL**, **Laxity with valgus stress** | **Thumb spica splint** (partial tear), **Surgical repair** (complete tear, Stener lesion) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/thumb-sprains/ |
| **Carpal Tunnel Syndrome** | **Repetitive wrist flexion/extension**, **Vibration** | **Nocturnal paresthesias** (thumb, index, middle), **Thenar atrophy** | **Night splint**, **CS injection**, **Carpal tunnel release** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/carpal-tunnel-syndrome/ |
| **Jersey Finger** | **Forced extension of flexed DIP** (grabbing jersey) | **Inability to flex DIP**, **Tenderness over volar aspect** | **Surgical repair** (within 7-10 days) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/jersey-finger/ |
| **Mallet Finger** | **Forced flexion of extended DIP** | **Extensor lag at DIP**, **Flexion deformity** | **Extension splint** (6-8 weeks continuous) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/mallet-finger-baseball-finger/ |
| **Boxer's Fracture** | **Punching** (closed fist) | **Pain/swelling over 5th metacarpal neck**, **Angulation** | **Closed reduction + Splint** (acceptable angulation), **Surgical fixation** (unacceptable angulation) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/boxers-fracture/ |
