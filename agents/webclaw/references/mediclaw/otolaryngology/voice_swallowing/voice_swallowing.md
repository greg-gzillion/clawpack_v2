## Zenker Diverticulum
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Pulsion diverticulum** at **Killian dehiscence** (between cricopharyngeus and inferior constrictor) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/zenker-diverticulum/ |
| **Symptoms** | **Dysphagia**, **Regurgitation of undigested food**, **Halitosis**, **Aspiration** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/zenker-diverticulum/ |
| **Diagnosis** | **Barium swallow** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/zenker-diverticulum/ |
| **Treatment** | **Endoscopic stapling** or **Open diverticulectomy + Cricopharyngeal myotomy** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/zenker-diverticulum/ |
