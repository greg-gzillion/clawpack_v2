# Otolaryngology - Oral Cavity and Pharynx

## Tonsillitis and Pharyngitis
| Condition | Etiology | Diagnosis | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Viral Pharyngitis** | **Rhinovirus**, **Adenovirus**, **EBV**, **HSV** | **Clinical** (cough, Rhinorrhea) | **Supportive** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/pharyngitis/ |
| **Streptococcal Pharyngitis** | **GAS** | **Centor criteria**, **Rapid strep**, **Culture** | **Penicillin** or **Amoxicillin** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/strep-throat/ |
| **Peritonsillar Abscess** | **Polymicrobial** | **Trismus**, **Muffled voice**, **Uvular deviation**, **Bulging tonsil** | **Needle aspiration** or **I&D**, **Antibiotics**, **Tonsillectomy** (recurrent) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/peritonsillar-abscess/ |
| **Deep Neck Space Infection** | **Odontogenic**, **Tonsillar** | **Fever**, **Neck swelling**, **Trismus**, **Airway compromise** | **IV antibiotics**, **Surgical drainage**, **Airway management** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/deep-neck-infections/ |

## Oral Cavity Lesions
| Lesion | Description | Management | URL |
|--------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **Leukoplakia** | **White plaque** (cannot be scraped off) | **Biopsy** (rule out dysplasia/SCC) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/oral-leukoplakia/ |
| **Erythroplakia** | **Red plaque** (higher risk of malignancy) | **Biopsy** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/oral-erythroplakia/ |
| **Oral Lichen Planus** | **Reticular** (Wickham striae), **Erosive** | **Topical corticosteroids**, **Monitor for malignant transformation** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/oral-lichen-planus/ |
| **Aphthous Ulcers** | **Painful**, **Well-circumscribed** | **Topical corticosteroids**, **Amlexanox**, **Treat underlying** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/aphthous-ulcers/ |
