## Thyroid Cancer
| Type | Frequency | Prognosis | Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Papillary** | **80-85%** | **Excellent** | **Lobectomy** or **Total thyroidectomy** ± **Central neck dissection** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Follicular** | **10-15%** | **Good** | **Total thyroidectomy** (cannot diagnose by FNA) | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Medullary** | **3-5%** | **Intermediate** | **Total thyroidectomy + Central + Lateral neck dissection** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Anaplastic** | **1-2%** | **Poor** | **Multimodal** (surgery, Radiation, Chemotherapy) | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |

## Thyroidectomy Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Hypocalcemia** | **Calcium + Calcitriol**, **Monitor** (transient 20-30%, Permanent 1-3%) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/thyroid-surgery/ |
| **Recurrent Laryngeal Nerve Injury** | **Voice therapy**, **Injection laryngoplasty**, **Medialization thyroplasty** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/thyroid-surgery/ |
| **Hematoma** | **Emergent decompression** (bedside if airway compromise) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/thyroid-surgery/ |

## Hyperparathyroidism
| Type | Cause | Treatment | URL |
|------|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Primary** | **Parathyroid adenoma** (80-85%), **Hyperplasia**, **Carcinoma** | **Parathyroidectomy** (minimally invasive or bilateral exploration) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/hyperparathyroidism/ |
| **Secondary** | **CKD** | **Medical management** (calcimimetics, Phosphate binders, Vitamin D), **Parathyroidectomy** (refractory) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/hyperparathyroidism/ |
| **Tertiary** | **Post-renal transplant** | **Parathyroidectomy** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/hyperparathyroidism/ |
