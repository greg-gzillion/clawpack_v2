| **Symptoms** | **Pain/swelling with meals** (mealtime syndrome) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/sialolithiasis/ |

## Salivary Gland Neoplasms
| Tumor | Frequency | Gland | Behavior | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Pleomorphic Adenoma** | **Most common** | **Parotid** (80%) | **Benign** (risk of malignant transformation) | **Superficial or Total parotidectomy** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/salivary-gland-tumors/ |
| **Warthin Tumor** | **Second most common** | **Parotid** (tail) | **Benign**, **Smoking-associated** | **Superficial parotidectomy** or **Observation** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/salivary-gland-tumors/ |
| **Mucoepidermoid Carcinoma** | **Most common malignant** | **Parotid**, **Minor glands** | **Low to high grade** | **Surgical resection** ± **Neck dissection** ± **Radiation** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/salivary-gland-tumors/ |
| **Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma** | **Second most common malignant** | **Minor glands** | **Perineural invasion** | **Surgical resection** + **Radiation** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/salivary-gland-tumors/ |
