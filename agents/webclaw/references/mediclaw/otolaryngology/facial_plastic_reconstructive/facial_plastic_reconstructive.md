## Otoplasty
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indications** | **Prominent ears**, **Constricted ears**, **Cryptotia**, **Stahl ear** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/otoplasty/ |
| **Timing** | **Age 5-6** (ear growth nearly complete) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/otoplasty/ |
| **Techniques** | **Mustarde sutures** (conchal setback), **Furnas sutures** (concha-mastoid), **Cartilage scoring** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/otoplasty/ |

## Facial Trauma
| Fracture | Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Nasal Fracture** | **Deformity**, **Epistaxis**, **Septal hematoma** | **Closed reduction** (<2 weeks), **Septal hematoma drainage** (emergency) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/facial-trauma/ |
| **Orbital Floor Fracture** | **Enophthalmos**, **Diplopia**, **Infraorbital hypesthesia** | **Orbital floor reconstruction** (if enophthalmos >2mm or entrapment) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/facial-trauma/ |
| **Zygomaticomaxillary Complex (ZMC)** | **Malar flattening**, **Trismus**, **Diplopia** | **ORIF** (1-3 point fixation) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/facial-trauma/ |
| **Mandible Fracture** | **Malocclusion**, **Pain**, **Trismus** | **ORIF** or **MMF** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/facial-trauma/ |

## Facial Reanimation
| Condition | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Acute Facial Paralysis** (Bell palsy, Trauma) | **Corticosteroids**, **Antivirals** (Bell), **Eye protection**, **Gold weight**, **Tarsorrhaphy** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/facial-nerve-paralysis/ |
| **Chronic Facial Paralysis** | **Nerve transfers** (hypoglossal, masseteric), **Muscle transfers** (temporalis, Gracilis free flap), **Static slings** | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/facial-reanimation/ |
