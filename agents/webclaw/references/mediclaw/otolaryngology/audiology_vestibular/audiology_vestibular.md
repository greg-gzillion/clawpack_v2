# Otolaryngology - Audiology and Vestibular

## Audiometry
| Test | Measures | Interpretation | URL |
|------|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Pure Tone Audiometry** | **Hearing thresholds** (250-8000 Hz) | **Degree** (mild to profound), **Configuration** (flat, sloping, rising) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/audiology/ |
| **Speech Audiometry** | **Speech Reception Threshold (SRT)** , **Word Recognition Score (WRS)** | **SRT should match PTA**; **WRS** assesses clarity | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/audiology/ |
| **Tympanometry** | **Middle ear function** | **Type A**: Normal; **Type B**: Effusion/TM perf; **Type C**: Eustachian tube dysfunction; **Type As**: Otosclerosis; **Type Ad**: Ossicular discontinuity | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/audiology/ |
| **Acoustic Reflex** | **Stapedius muscle contraction** | **Absent** in conductive loss, CN VIII lesion; **Present** in cochlear loss | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/audiology/ |
| **Otoacoustic Emissions (OAEs)** | **Cochlear outer hair cell function** | **Present** = Normal cochlea; **Absent** = Cochlear hearing loss (>30 dB) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/audiology/ |
| **Auditory Brainstem Response (ABR)** | **Retrocochlear function** (CN VIII, Brainstem) | **Prolonged I-V interpeak latency** = Acoustic neuroma | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/audiology/ |

## Vestibular Testing
| Test | Measures | Interpretation | URL |
|------|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Videonystagmography (VNG)** | **Eye movements** (nystagmus) | **Peripheral vs Central vertigo**, **Unilateral weakness** (calorics) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/vestibular-testing/ |
| **Video Head Impulse Test (vHIT)** | **VOR function** of each semicircular canal | **Decreased gain + catch-up saccades** = Peripheral vestibular loss | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/vestibular-testing/ |
| **Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potentials (VEMP)** | **Otolith function** (saccule/utricle) | **cVEMP** (saccule/inferior vestibular nerve), **oVEMP** (utricle/superior vestibular nerve) | https://www.entnet.org/conditions/vestibular-testing/ |
