# Mental Health - Obsessive-Compulsive and Related Disorders

## Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Obsessions** (recurrent intrusive thoughts) AND/OR **Compulsions** (repetitive behaviors/mental acts) that are **time-consuming (>1 hour/day)** or cause **significant distress/impairment** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Common Obsessions** | **Contamination**, **Symmetry/Order**, **Harm**, **Forbidden thoughts** (sexual, religious), **Doubting** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Common Compulsions** | **Washing/Cleaning**, **Checking**, **Counting**, **Ordering/Arranging**, **Repeating**, **Mental rituals** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Prevalence** | Lifetime: 2-3% | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Insight Specifiers** | **Good or fair**, **Poor**, **Absent/delusional** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## OCD Treatment (APA Guidelines)
| Treatment | Recommendation | Dosing | URL |
|-----------|----------------|--------|-----|
| **CBT with Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP)** | **First-line** (strong evidence) | N/A | https://iocdf.org/about-ocd/treatment/ |
| **Sertraline** | First-line pharmacotherapy | 50-200 mg | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/sertraline |
| **Fluoxetine** | First-line pharmacotherapy | 20-80 mg | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/fluoxetine |
| **Fluvoxamine** | First-line pharmacotherapy | 100-300 mg | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/fluvoxamine |
| **Paroxetine** | First-line pharmacotherapy | 20-60 mg | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/paroxetine |
| **Clomipramine** | **Most effective**, but more side effects | 75-250 mg | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/clomipramine |
| **Augmentation with Atypical Antipsychotic** | **Risperidone**, **Aripiprazole** (for partial response) | Low dose | https://www.apa.org/ocd-guideline |

## Body Dysmorphic Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Preoccupation with perceived defect or flaw in physical appearance**; **Repetitive behaviors** (checking mirror, grooming) or **mental acts** (comparing) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Insight Specifiers** | **Good or fair**, **Poor**, **Absent/delusional** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Prevalence** | 2-3% (higher in cosmetic surgery/dermatology settings) | https://iocdf.org/related-disorders/body-dysmorphic-disorder/ |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (specialized for BDD), **High-dose SSRI** | https://iocdf.org/related-disorders/body-dysmorphic-disorder/ |

## Hoarding Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Persistent difficulty discarding possessions** regardless of actual value; **Accumulation of possessions** that congest living areas; **Significant distress/impairment** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Prevalence** | 2-6% | https://iocdf.org/related-disorders/hoarding-disorder/ |
| **Treatment** | **CBT specialized for hoarding**, **SSRI** (limited evidence) | https://iocdf.org/related-disorders/hoarding-disorder/ |

## Trichotillomania (Hair-Pulling Disorder)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Recurrent pulling out of one's hair** resulting in **hair loss**; Repeated attempts to decrease or stop | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Prevalence** | 1-2% | https://www.bfrb.org |
| **Treatment** | **Habit Reversal Training (HRT)** , **CBT**, **N-Acetylcysteine (NAC)** , **Clomipramine** | https://www.bfrb.org/learn-about-bfrbs/treatment |
