# Mental Health - Trauma and Stressor-Related Disorders

## Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Exposure to actual or threatened death, serious injury, or sexual violence** + Symptoms from 4 clusters for **>1 month** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Cluster B: Intrusion** | **Flashbacks**, **Nightmares**, **Intrusive memories**, **Psychological/physiological distress to cues** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Cluster C: Avoidance** | **Avoiding thoughts/feelings** or **external reminders** of trauma | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Cluster D: Negative Alterations in Cognition/Mood** | **Amnesia**, **Negative beliefs**, **Distorted blame**, **Negative emotions**, **Diminished interest**, **Detachment**, **Inability to experience positive emotions** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Cluster E: Alterations in Arousal/Reactivity** | **Irritability/Anger**, **Reckless behavior**, **Hypervigilance**, **Exaggerated startle**, **Poor concentration**, **Sleep disturbance** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Prevalence** | Lifetime: 7-8% | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |

## Acute Stress Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | Similar to PTSD but **duration 3 days - 1 month** after trauma | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Symptoms** | **Intrusion**, **Negative mood**, **Dissociation**, **Avoidance**, **Arousal** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Treatment** | **Trauma-focused CBT** (prevent progression to PTSD) | https://www.ptsd.va.gov/professional/treat/essentials/acute_stress_disorder.asp |

## Adjustment Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Emotional or behavioral symptoms** in response to **identifiable stressor** within **3 months**; **Clinically significant distress/impairment** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Specifiers** | With depressed mood, With anxiety, With mixed anxiety and depressed mood, With disturbance of conduct, With mixed disturbance of emotions and conduct, Unspecified | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Treatment** | **Supportive psychotherapy**, **CBT**, **Resolve stressor**; Pharmacotherapy for symptom relief | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560704/ |

## Prolonged Grief Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Persistent grief response** (=12 months adults, =6 months children) with **intense yearning/longing** and **preoccupation with deceased** + =3 additional symptoms | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Treatment** | **Grief-focused CBT**, **Complicated Grief Treatment (CGT)** | https://complicatedgrief.columbia.edu |
