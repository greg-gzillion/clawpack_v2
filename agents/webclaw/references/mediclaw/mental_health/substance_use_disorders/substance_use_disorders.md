# Mental Health - Substance Use Disorders

## DSM-5-TR Criteria for Substance Use Disorder
| Criterion | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Impaired Control** | **1**. Taking larger amounts/longer than intended; **2**. Persistent desire/unsuccessful efforts to cut down; **3**. Great deal of time spent obtaining/using/recovering; **4**. **Craving** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Social Impairment** | **5**. Failure to fulfill major role obligations; **6**. Continued use despite social/interpersonal problems; **7**. Activities given up/reduced | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Risky Use** | **8**. Recurrent use in physically hazardous situations; **9**. Continued use despite physical/psychological problems | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Pharmacologic** | **10**. **Tolerance**; **11**. **Withdrawal** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Severity** | **Mild**: 2-3 criteria; **Moderate**: 4-5 criteria; **Severe**: =6 criteria | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## Alcohol Use Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Screening** | **Single question**: "How many times in the past year have you had 5+ (men) or 4+ (women) drinks in a day?"; **AUDIT-C** | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/alcohol-health/screening-and-assessment-tools |
| **Unhealthy Use** | Men: >14 drinks/week or >4/day; Women: >7 drinks/week or >3/day | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |
| **Withdrawal** | **Tremulousness** (6-12h), **Seizures** (12-48h), **Delirium Tremens** (48-96h) | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |
| **CIWA-Ar Protocol** | **Benzodiazepines** (diazepam, lorazepam, chlordiazepoxide) based on CIWA-Ar score | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/10433/ciwa-ar-alcohol-withdrawal |

## Medications for Alcohol Use Disorder
| Medication | Dose | Mechanism | Considerations | URL |
|------------|------|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Naltrexone** | 50 mg daily (PO) or 380 mg monthly (IM) | **Opioid antagonist**, ? Craving | **First-line**; Requires abstinence from opioids; **Hepatotoxicity** (rare) | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Acamprosate** | 666 mg TID | **Glutamate modulator** | **First-line**; Safe in liver disease; **Renal dosing** (CrCl <50) | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Disulfiram** | 250-500 mg daily | **Inhibits ALDH** ? Aversive reaction | Requires commitment; **Contraindicated in severe CAD** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Topiramate** | 50-300 mg daily | **Anticonvulsant** | Off-label; **Cognitive effects**, **Weight loss** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Gabapentin** | 300-1200 mg TID | **GABA analog** | Off-label; **Sedation**, **Dizziness** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |

## Opioid Use Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | Same 11 criteria applied to opioids | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Overdose** | **Miosis**, **Respiratory depression**, **Altered mental status** | https://www.cdc.gov/stop-overdose/caring/naloxone.html |
| **Withdrawal** | **Mydriasis**, **Rhinorrhea**, **Piloerection**, **Yawning**, **Diarrhea**, **Insomnia**, **Dysphoria** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **COWS** | **Clinical Opiate Withdrawal Scale** (guides buprenorphine initiation) | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/10434/cows-score-opiate-withdrawal |

## Medications for Opioid Use Disorder (MOUD)
| Medication | Setting | Dose | Considerations | URL |
|------------|---------|------|----------------|-----|
| **Buprenorphine** | **Office-based** (waivered) | 8-24 mg SL daily | **First-line**; **Precipitates withdrawal** if given too soon (must have COWS =8-12) | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Methadone** | **OTP only** | 80-120 mg daily | **Most effective** for retention; **QTc monitoring**; **Respiratory depression risk** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Naltrexone** | Office-based | 380 mg IM monthly | Requires **7-14 days opioid-free**; **Not first-line**; **High attrition** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |

## Tobacco Use Disorder
