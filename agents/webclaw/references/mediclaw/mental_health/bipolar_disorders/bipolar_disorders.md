# Mental Health - Bipolar and Related Disorders

## Bipolar I Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **=1 manic episode** (=7 days or requiring hospitalization) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Manic Episode** | **DIGFAST**: **D**istractibility, **I**mpulsivity/Insomnia, **G**randiosity, **F**light of ideas, **A**ctivity increased, **S**peech pressured, **T**houghtlessness | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/bipolar-disorder |
| **Prevalence** | Lifetime: 1% | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics/bipolar-disorder |
| **Age of Onset** | Peak: 18-25 years | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics/bipolar-disorder |

## Bipolar II Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **=1 hypomanic episode** (=4 days) + **=1 major depressive episode** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Hypomania vs Mania** | **Similar symptoms** but **less severe**, **shorter duration**, **No psychosis or hospitalization** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/bipolar-disorder |
| **Prevalence** | Lifetime: 1-2% | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics/bipolar-disorder |

## Cyclothymic Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **=2 years** of **subthreshold hypomanic and depressive symptoms** (never meeting full criteria) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Prevalence** | Lifetime: 0.4-1% | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics/bipolar-disorder |

## Mood Stabilizers for Bipolar Disorder
| Medication | Indication | Starting Dose | Monitoring | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|------------|------------|---------------|------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Lithium** | **Gold standard** (acute mania, maintenance, Suicide prevention) | 300 mg BID-TID | **Level** (0.6-1.2), **TSH**, **Cr**, **Ca** | **Hypothyroidism**, **Nephrogenic DI**, Tremor, Weight gain | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **Valproate** | Acute mania, Maintenance | 250-500 mg BID | **Level** (50-100), **LFTs**, **CBC**, Ammonia | **Teratogenicity**, Weight gain, Tremor, Thrombocytopenia | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/valproate |
| **Carbamazepine** | Acute mania, Maintenance | 200 mg BID | **Level** (4-12), **CBC**, **LFTs** | **Agranulocytosis**, **SJS/TEN** (HLA-B*15:02), CYP inducer | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/carbamazepine |
| **Lamotrigine** | **Bipolar depression**, Maintenance | 25 mg daily (slow titration) | None routinely | **SJS/TEN** (slow titration), Well tolerated | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lamotrigine |

## Lithium Monitoring and Toxicity
| Level (mEq/L) | Interpretation | Symptoms | Management | URL |
|---------------|----------------|----------|------------|-----|
| **<0.6** | Subtherapeutic | Depressive/manic symptoms | Increase dose | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **0.6-1.2** | **Therapeutic** (maintenance) | Well-controlled | Continue | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **1.0-1.5** | Therapeutic (acute mania) | May have mild tremor | Monitor closely | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **1.5-2.5** | **Mild-Moderate Toxicity** | Nausea, Vomiting, Diarrhea, Coarse tremor, Ataxia, Slurred speech | **Hold lithium**, IVF, Consider dialysis if renal impairment | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **2.5-3.5** | **Severe Toxicity** | Confusion, Myoclonus, Seizures, Coma | **Emergent hemodialysis** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **>3.5** | **Life-Threatening** | Coma, Seizures, Cardiovascular collapse | **Emergent hemodialysis** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
