# Mental Health - Neurodevelopmental Disorders

## Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Persistent pattern of inattention and/or hyperactivity-impulsivity** for =6 months; **Several symptoms before age 12**; **Present in =2 settings** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Inattentive Symptoms** | Careless mistakes, Difficulty sustaining attention, Does not listen, Fails to finish tasks, Disorganized, Avoids mental effort, Loses things, Easily distracted, Forgetful | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/attention-deficit-hyperactivity-disorder-adhd |
| **Hyperactive-Impulsive Symptoms** | Fidgets, Leaves seat, Runs/climbs inappropriately, Unable to play quietly, "On the go," Talks excessively, Blurts out, Cannot wait turn, Interrupts | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/attention-deficit-hyperactivity-disorder-adhd |
| **Prevalence** | Children: 5-10%; Adults: 2-5% | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics/attention-deficit-hyperactivity-disorder-adhd |

## Stimulant Adverse Effects and Monitoring
| Adverse Effect | Management | Monitoring | URL |
|----------------|------------|------------|-----|
| **Appetite suppression** | **Dose after meals**, **High-calorie snacks** | **Weight**, **Growth** (children) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/adhd/ |
| **Insomnia** | **Dose early in day**, **Sleep hygiene** | Sleep patterns | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/adhd/ |
| **Cardiovascular** | **Baseline cardiac history**, **Family history of sudden death** | **BP**, **HR** at each visit | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/adhd/ |
| **Abuse/Diversion** | **Monitor for misuse** | **Prescription drug monitoring program** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |

## Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. Persistent deficits in social communication/interaction**; **B. Restricted, repetitive patterns of behavior/interests/activities**; **C. Symptoms present in early development** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Social Communication Deficits** | **Social-emotional reciprocity**, **Nonverbal communication**, **Developing/maintaining relationships** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/autism-spectrum-disorders-asd |
| **Restricted/Repetitive Behaviors** | **Stereotyped movements**, **Insistence on sameness**, **Fixated interests**, **Sensory reactivity** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/autism-spectrum-disorders-asd |
| **Prevalence** | 1 in 36 children (CDC 2023) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/autism/data.html |
| **Levels of Support** | **Level 1**: Requiring support; **Level 2**: Requiring substantial support; **Level 3**: Requiring very substantial support | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## Intellectual Disability (Intellectual Developmental Disorder)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. Deficits in intellectual functions**; **B. Deficits in adaptive functioning**; **C. Onset during developmental period** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Severity Levels** | **Mild** (85%): Can live independently with support; **Moderate** (10%): Requires supervision; **Severe** (3-4%): Requires extensive support; **Profound** (1-2%): Requires total care | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Common Etiologies** | **Genetic** (Down, Fragile X, Prader-Willi), **Prenatal** (alcohol, Infection), **Perinatal** (hypoxia), **Postnatal** (trauma, Infection) | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/idds |
