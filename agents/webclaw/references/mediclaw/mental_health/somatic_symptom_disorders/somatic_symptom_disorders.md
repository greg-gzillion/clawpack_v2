# Mental Health - Somatic Symptom and Related Disorders

## Somatic Symptom Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. =1 somatic symptom** that is distressing or disruptive; **B. Excessive thoughts, feelings, or behaviors** related to symptoms (=1 of: Disproportionate thoughts, High anxiety, Excessive time/energy) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Treatment** | **CBT**, **SSRI/SNRI**, **Regular scheduled visits** (avoid PRN), **Avoid unnecessary testing/procedures** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/clinical-practice-guidelines |

## Illness Anxiety Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. Preoccupation with having/acquiring serious illness**; **B. Somatic symptoms are mild or absent**; **C. High health anxiety**; **D. Excessive health-related behaviors or maladaptive avoidance** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Treatment** | **CBT**, **SSRI/SNRI**, **Regular scheduled visits** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/clinical-practice-guidelines |

## Conversion Disorder (Functional Neurological Symptom Disorder)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. =1 symptom of altered voluntary motor or sensory function**; **B. Incompatibility with recognized neurological/medical conditions** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Common Symptoms** | **Weakness/Paralysis**, **Abnormal movements**, **Gait disturbance**, **Speech disturbance**, **Seizure-like episodes**, **Sensory loss**, **Visual disturbance** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/functional-neurologic-disorder |
| **Positive Signs** | **Hoover sign** (functional weakness), **Tremor entrainment**, **Distractibility** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/functional-neurologic-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **Education**, **CBT**, **Physical therapy**, **Occupational therapy**, **Speech therapy** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/functional-neurologic-disorder |

## Factitious Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. Falsification of physical or psychological signs/symptoms**; **B. Deceptive behavior is evident in absence of external rewards**; **C. Not better explained by another disorder** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Munchausen Syndrome** | Severe, chronic form with **peregrination** (traveling to multiple hospitals) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560547/ |
| **Munchausen by Proxy** | **Imposed on another** (usually child) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560547/ |
