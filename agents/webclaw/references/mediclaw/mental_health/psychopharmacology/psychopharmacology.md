# Mental Health - Psychopharmacology

## Antidepressants
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **SSRI** | Sertraline, Escitalopram, Fluoxetine, Paroxetine, Citalopram | **5-HT reuptake inhibition** | **Sexual dysfunction**, **GI upset**, **QTc prolongation** (citalopram >40 mg) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ssris |
| **SNRI** | Venlafaxine, Duloxetine, Desvenlafaxine | **5-HT + NE reuptake inhibition** | **Hypertension** (venlafaxine), Sexual dysfunction, **Discontinuation syndrome** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/snris |
| **NDRI** | Bupropion | **NE + DA reuptake inhibition** | **Seizures** (dose-dependent), **No sexual dysfunction**, **Contraindicated in eating disorders** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/bupropion |
| **NaSSA** | Mirtazapine | **a2 antagonist** (? NE/5-HT) | **Sedation**, **Weight gain**, **Increased appetite** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/mirtazapine |
| **SARI** | Trazodone | **5-HT2A antagonist**, Weak SRI | **Sedation**, **Orthostasis**, **Priapism** (rare) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/trazodone |
| **TCA** | Amitriptyline, Nortriptyline | **5-HT + NE reuptake inhibition** | **Anticholinergic**, **Cardiotoxic in overdose**, **QTc prolongation** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/tricyclic-antidepressants |
| **MAOI** | Phenelzine, Tranylcypromine | **MAO-A/B inhibition** | **Hypertensive crisis** (tyramine), **Serotonin syndrome** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/maois |

## Antipsychotics

|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|

## Serotonin Syndrome
| Severity | Symptoms | Management | URL |
|----------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Mild** | Tremor, Hyperreflexia, Diaphoresis | **Discontinue serotonergic agents**, **Benzodiazepines** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/serotonin-syndrome |
| **Moderate** | Clonus, Agitation, Hyperthermia, Rigidity | Discontinue agents, Benzodiazepines, **Cyproheptadine** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/serotonin-syndrome |
| **Severe** | Severe hyperthermia, Autonomic instability, Delirium | **ICU**, Cyproheptadine, Cooling, Paralysis | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/serotonin-syndrome |
