# Mental Health - Child and Adolescent Psychiatry

## Pediatric-Specific Considerations
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Developmental Context** | Symptoms must be evaluated in context of normal development | https://www.aacap.org |
| **Family Involvement** | **Essential** for assessment and treatment | https://www.aacap.org |
| **School Collaboration** | Coordinate with teachers, School counselors, IEP/504 plans | https://sites.ed.gov/idea |
| **Off-Label Prescribing** | Many medications are off-label in children (limited pediatric trials) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/development-resources/pediatric-drug-development |

## Common Pediatric Psychiatric Disorders
| Disorder | Prevalence | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|------------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **ADHD** | 5-10% | Inattention, Hyperactivity, Impulsivity | **Stimulants** (first-line), **Behavioral therapy** | https://www.aacap.org/AACAP/Families_and_Youth/Resource_Centers/ADHD_Resource_Center/Home.aspx |
| **Anxiety Disorders** | 7-15% | Separation anxiety, Social anxiety, GAD | **CBT**, **SSRI** | https://www.aacap.org/AACAP/Families_and_Youth/Resource_Centers/Anxiety_Disorder_Resource_Center/Home.aspx |
| **Major Depressive Disorder** | 2-8% (adolescents) | Irritability, Anhedonia, Somatic complaints | **CBT**, **IPT**, **SSRI** (fluoxetine, escitalopram) | https://www.aacap.org/AACAP/Families_and_Youth/Resource_Centers/Depression_Resource_Center/Home.aspx |
| **Autism Spectrum Disorder** | 1 in 36 | Social communication deficits, Restricted/repetitive behaviors | **ABA**, **Speech/OT**, **Risperidone/Aripiprazole** (irritability) | https://www.aacap.org/AACAP/Families_and_Youth/Resource_Centers/Autism_Resource_Center/Home.aspx |
| **Oppositional Defiant Disorder** | 3-5% | Angry/irritable mood, Argumentative/defiant behavior, Vindictiveness | **Parent Management Training**, **CBT** | https://www.aacap.org/AACAP/Families_and_Youth/Resource_Centers/Oppositional_Defiant_Disorder_Resource_Center/Home.aspx |
| **Conduct Disorder** | 2-5% | Aggression, Destruction, Deceitfulness, Serious rule violations | **Multisystemic Therapy**, **Parent training** | https://www.aacap.org/AACAP/Families_and_Youth/Resource_Centers/Conduct_Disorder_Resource_Center/Home.aspx |
| **Tourette Syndrome** | 1% | Multiple motor and =1 vocal tic | **CBIT**, **Alpha-2 agonists**, **Antipsychotics** | https://www.aacap.org/AACAP/Families_and_Youth/Resource_Centers/Tourette_Disorder_Resource_Center/Home.aspx |

|------------|---------|-----|
