# Mental Health - Personality Disorders

## Overview of Personality Disorders
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR General Criteria** | **Enduring pattern of inner experience and behavior** that deviates markedly from cultural expectations in =2 areas: **Cognition**, **Affectivity**, **Interpersonal functioning**, **Impulse control** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Pattern** | **Inflexible and pervasive**, **Stable over time**, **Onset in adolescence/early adulthood**, **Causes significant distress/impairment** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## Cluster A (Odd/Eccentric)
| Disorder | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Paranoid** | **Pervasive distrust and suspiciousness**; Reads hidden meanings; Bears grudges | **CBT**, **Low-dose antipsychotics** (limited evidence) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Schizoid** | **Detachment from social relationships**, **Restricted emotional expression**; Prefers solitary activities | **Supportive therapy**, **Social skills training** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Schizotypal** | **Eccentric behavior**, **Odd beliefs/magical thinking**, **Perceptual distortions**, **Social anxiety** | **Low-dose antipsychotics**, **CBT**, **Social skills training** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## Cluster B (Dramatic/Emotional/Erratic)
| Disorder | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Antisocial** | **Disregard for others' rights**, **Deceitfulness**, **Impulsivity**, **Irritability/Aggression**, **Lack of remorse**; **Must be =18 with conduct disorder before 15** | **CBT** (limited efficacy), **Treat comorbidities** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Borderline** | **Unstable relationships**, **Identity disturbance**, **Impulsivity**, **Self-harm/Suicidality**, **Affective instability**, **Chronic emptiness**, **Anger**, **Dissociation** | **DBT** (gold standard), **MBT**, **TFP**, **Schema therapy** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Histrionic** | **Attention-seeking**, **Inappropriately seductive**, **Shallow emotions**, **Theatrical** | **Psychodynamic therapy**, **CBT** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Narcissistic** | **Grandiosity**, **Need for admiration**, **Lack of empathy**, **Entitlement**, **Arrogance** | **Psychodynamic therapy**, **CBT** (limited evidence) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## Cluster C (Anxious/Fearful)
| Disorder | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Avoidant** | **Social inhibition**, **Inadequacy**, **Hypersensitivity to criticism**; Desires relationships | **CBT**, **Social skills training**, **SSRI** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Dependent** | **Excessive need to be taken care of**, **Submissive behavior**, **Fear of separation** | **CBT**, **Assertiveness training** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Obsessive-Compulsive** | **Preoccupation with orderliness**, **Perfectionism**, **Control** at expense of flexibility/efficiency | **CBT**, **SSRI** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD) - Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Four Modules** | **Mindfulness**, **Distress Tolerance**, **Emotion Regulation**, **Interpersonal Effectiveness** | https://behavioraltech.org/resources/faqs/dialectical-behavior-therapy-dbt/ |
| **Structure** | **Individual therapy** (weekly), **Group skills training** (weekly), **Phone coaching**, **Consultation team** | https://behavioraltech.org/resources/faqs/dialectical-behavior-therapy-dbt/ |
