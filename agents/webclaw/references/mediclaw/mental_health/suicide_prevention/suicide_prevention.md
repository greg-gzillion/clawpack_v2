## Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale (C-SSRS)
| Category | Description | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Wish to be Dead** | Endorses thoughts about wanting to be dead | https://cssrs.columbia.edu |
| **Non-Specific Active Suicidal Thoughts** | Thoughts of killing self without specific plan | https://cssrs.columbia.edu |
| **Active Suicidal Ideation with Method** | Thoughts of killing self with specific method but no intent | https://cssrs.columbia.edu |
| **Active Suicidal Ideation with Intent** | Thoughts of killing self with specific method and intent | https://cssrs.columbia.edu |
| **Suicidal Behavior** | Actual, Aborted, or Interrupted attempt; Preparatory acts | https://cssrs.columbia.edu |

## Means Restriction
| Means | Intervention | Evidence | URL |
|-------|--------------|----------|-----|
| **Firearms** | **Remove from home**, Safe storage (locked, unloaded, Ammunition separate) | **Strong** (reduces suicide by firearm) | https://www.hsph.harvard.edu/means-matter/ |
| **Medications** | **Limit quantity**, **Lock up**, **Dispose of unused** | **Moderate** | https://www.hsph.harvard.edu/means-matter/ |
| **Jumping** | **Barriers on bridges** | **Strong** | https://www.hsph.harvard.edu/means-matter/ |
| **Carbon Monoxide** | **Catalytic converters**, **Detectors** | **Moderate** | https://www.hsph.harvard.edu/means-matter/ |

## Medications for Suicide Prevention
| Medication | Evidence | Indication | URL |
|------------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Lithium** | **Strongest evidence** for suicide prevention | **Bipolar disorder**, **MDD** (augmentation) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **Clozapine** | **Strong evidence** | **Schizophrenia**, **Schizoaffective disorder** | https://www.clozapinerems.com |
| **Ketamine/Esketamine** | **Rapid reduction** of suicidal ideation | **MDD** (acute suicidal ideation) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/esketamine |
| **SSRI** | **Moderate evidence** | **MDD**, **Anxiety disorders** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ssris |
