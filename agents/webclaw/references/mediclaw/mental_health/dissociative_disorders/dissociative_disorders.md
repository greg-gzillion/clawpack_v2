# Mental Health - Dissociative Disorders

## Dissociative Identity Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. Disruption of identity characterized by =2 distinct personality states**; **B. Recurrent gaps in recall** of everyday events, personal information, or traumatic events | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Prevalence** | Lifetime: 1-2% | https://www.nami.org/About-Mental-Illness/Mental-Health-Conditions/Dissociative-Disorders |
| **Etiology** | **Severe childhood trauma** (abuse, neglect) | https://www.nami.org/About-Mental-Illness/Mental-Health-Conditions/Dissociative-Disorders |
| **Treatment** | **Trauma-focused psychotherapy**, **Phase-oriented treatment** (Stabilization ? Trauma processing ? Integration) | https://www.isst-d.org |

## Dissociative Amnesia
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **A. Inability to recall important autobiographical information**, usually of traumatic/stressful nature; **B. Not attributable to ordinary forgetfulness** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Specifiers** | **With dissociative fugue** (purposeful travel or bewildered wandering) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Treatment** | **Psychotherapy**, **Hypnosis**, **Medication-assisted interview** | https://www.nami.org/About-Mental-Illness/Mental-Health-Conditions/Dissociative-Disorders |

## Depersonalization/Derealization Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Depersonalization** | **Feeling detached from one's own mind, body, or self** (observing from outside) | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Derealization** | **Feeling that the external world is unreal, dreamlike, or distorted** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **DSM-5-TR Criteria** | **Persistent or recurrent experiences of depersonalization and/or derealization**; **Reality testing remains intact** | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |
| **Treatment** | **CBT**, **Grounding techniques**, **SSRI**, **Lamotrigine**, **Naltrexone** | https://www.nami.org/About-Mental-Illness/Mental-Health-Conditions/Dissociative-Disorders |
