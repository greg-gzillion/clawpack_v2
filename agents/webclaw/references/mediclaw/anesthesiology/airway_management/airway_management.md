## Rapid Sequence Induction (RSI)
| Step | Action | Rationale | URL |
|------|--------|-----------|-----|
| 1. Pre-oxygenate | 100% O2 for 3-5 min or 8 vital capacity breaths | Denitrogenation; apnea time | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| 2. Prepare | Equipment; suction; backup plan | Readiness | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| 3. Induction Agent | Propofol, etomidate, ketamine | Rapid unconsciousness | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| 4. Cricoid Pressure | Sellick maneuver | Prevent aspiration | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| 5. Paralysis | Succinylcholine or rocuronium | Rapid intubation conditions | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| 6. Intubation | Direct or video laryngoscopy | Secure airway | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| 7. Confirm | ETCO2; auscultation | Verify tracheal placement | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| 8. Release Cricoid | After confirmation | Decrease airway pressure | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
