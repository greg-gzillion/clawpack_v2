## Perioperative Medication Management
| Medication | Recommendation | URL |
|------------|----------------|-----|
| Beta-Blockers | Continue; start only if indicated (avoid initiation day of surgery) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| ACE-I/ARB | Hold morning of surgery (hypotension risk) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Calcium Channel Blockers | Continue | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Statins | Continue (pleiotropic benefits) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Diuretics | Hold morning of surgery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Antiplatelet Agents | ASA: continue; Clopidogrel: hold 5-7 days (unless coronary stent) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Warfarin | Hold 5 days; bridge if high risk | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| DOACs | Hold 1-2 days (normal renal function) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Oral Hypoglycemics | Hold morning of surgery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Insulin | Reduced dose of long-acting; hold short-acting | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| SGLT2 Inhibitors | Hold 3-4 days pre-op (euglycemic DKA risk) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Thyroid Medications | Continue | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Steroids | Continue; stress dose if indicated | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
| Herbal Supplements | Hold 1-2 weeks (variable) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482367/ |
