## Local Anesthetic Toxicity
| System | Signs/Symptoms | URL |
|--------|----------------|-----|
| CNS (Early) | Perioral numbness, metallic taste, tinnitus, lightheadedness | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK499881/ |
| CNS (Late) | Seizures, coma, respiratory arrest | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK499881/ |
| Cardiovascular | Hypotension, bradycardia, arrhythmias, cardiac arrest | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK499881/ |
| Bupivacaine Cardiotoxicity | Refractory arrhythmias; difficult resuscitation | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK499881/ |
| Methemoglobinemia | Prilocaine, benzocaine; treat with methylene blue | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK499881/ |
| Allergic Reaction | Rare with amides; more common with esters | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK499881/ |
