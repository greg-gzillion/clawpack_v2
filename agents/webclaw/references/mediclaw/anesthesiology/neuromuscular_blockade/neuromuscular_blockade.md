## Monitoring Neuromuscular Blockade
| Method | Stimulus | Response | Clinical Correlation | URL |
|--------|----------|----------|---------------------|-----|
| Train-of-Four (TOF) | 4 stimuli at 2 Hz | Ratio of T4:T1 | TOF >0.9 = adequate recovery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| TOF Count | 4 stimuli | Number of twitches | 0=deep block; 4=recovery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Post-Tetanic Count (PTC) | 50 Hz tetanus then single twitch | Number of twitches | Deep block assessment | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Double Burst Stimulation (DBS) | Two 50 Hz bursts | Fade detection | Clinical fade assessment | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Tetanus | 50-100 Hz sustained | Fade | 5 sec sustained = adequate recovery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
