## Constipation - Stepwise Management
| Step | Intervention | URL |
|------|--------------|-----|
| **Step 1 (Prevention)** | **Scheduled stimulant laxative** (senna 2-4 tablets daily) + **Stool softener** (docusate) for all patients on opioids | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Step 2** | **Add osmotic laxative**: **Polyethylene glycol (PEG)** 17g daily or **Lactulose** 15-30 mL daily | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Step 3** | **Add suppository**: **Bisacodyl** or **Glycerin** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Step 4** | **Add enema**: **Mineral oil**, **Tap water**, **Fleet** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Step 5** | **Manual disimpaction** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Refractory** | **Methylnaltrexone** 8-12 mg SC qod, **Naloxegol** 12.5-25 mg daily, **Naldemedine** 0.2 mg daily | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |

## Malignant Bowel Obstruction
| Component | Management | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Goals** | **Relieve symptoms** (pain, Nausea, Vomiting), **Avoid surgery** in most cases | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/bowel-obstruction/ |
| **NPO** | Initially; May allow clear liquids if partial obstruction | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/bowel-obstruction/ |
| **NGT** | **Avoid if possible** (uncomfortable); Consider for severe vomiting | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/bowel-obstruction/ |
| **Pharmacologic** | **Dexamethasone** 4-8 mg daily (anti-inflammatory), **Haloperidol** 0.5-2 mg q6-8h (antiemetic), **Octreotide** 100-600 mcg/day SC (? secretions), **Hyoscyamine** 0.125-0.25 mg SL q4h (anticholinergic) | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/bowel-obstruction/ |
| **Venting PEG** | For refractory vomiting; Allows oral intake for pleasure | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/bowel-obstruction/ |

## Diarrhea
| Cause | Management | URL |
|-------|------------|-----|
| **Laxative-Induced** | **Stop laxatives** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/diarrhea/ |
| **Infectious** | **Stool studies**, **Antibiotics** if indicated | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/diarrhea/ |
| **Chemotherapy/Radiation** | **Loperamide** 4 mg initial, Then 2 mg after each loose stool (max 16 mg/day) | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/diarrhea/ |
| **C. difficile** | **Oral vancomycin** or **Fidaxomicin** | https://www.cdc.gov/cdiff/index.html |
| **Fecal Impaction** | **Overflow diarrhea**; **Disimpaction** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Non-Specific** | **Loperamide**, **Diphenoxylate/Atropine**, **Bismuth subsalicylate**, **Octreotide** (refractory) | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/diarrhea/ |
