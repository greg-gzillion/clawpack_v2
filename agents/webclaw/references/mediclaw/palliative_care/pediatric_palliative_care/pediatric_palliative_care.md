# Palliative Care - Pediatric Palliative Care

## Pediatric Palliative Care - Unique Aspects
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Conditions** | **Congenital anomalies**, **Genetic disorders**, **Neurologic impairment**, **Cancer**, **Metabolic disorders** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
| **Trajectory** | Often **longer**, **Unpredictable** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
| **Communication** | **Developmentally appropriate**; **Include child** (when appropriate) | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
| **Family Unit** | **Parents**, **Siblings**, **Grandparents** all affected | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
| **Concurrent Care** | **Palliative care alongside curative treatment** (mandated by ACA for children) | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |

## Pediatric Symptom Management
| Symptom | Pediatric Considerations | URL |
|---------|------------------------|-----|
| **Pain** | **Weight-based dosing**; **Non-pharmacologic** (distraction, Play, Music); **Sucrose** for neonates | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/pediatric-palliative-care/ |
| **Dyspnea** | **Fan**, **Positioning**, **Opioids** (weight-based) | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/pediatric-palliative-care/ |
| **Nausea/Vomiting** | **Ondansetron** (weight-based), **Metoclopramide** (caution: EPS) | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/pediatric-palliative-care/ |
| **Constipation** | **PEG**, **Lactulose**, **Senna** (weight-based) | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/pediatric-palliative-care/ |
| **Seizures** | **Lorazepam**, **Midazolam**, **Levetiracetam** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/pediatric-palliative-care/ |
| **Agitation/Delirium** | **Haloperidol** (caution: EPS), **Lorazepam** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/pediatric-palliative-care/ |

## Communication with Children
| Age | Approach | URL |
|-----|----------|-----|
| **Infant/Toddler (0-2)** | **Comfort**, **Routine**, **Parental presence** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
| **Preschool (3-5)** | **Simple language**, **Magical thinking**, **Reassurance it's not their fault** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
| **School-Age (6-12)** | **Concrete explanations**, **Answer questions honestly**, **Respect privacy** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
| **Adolescent (13+)** | **Adult-like understanding**, **Include in decision-making**, **Respect autonomy** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/pediatric-palliative-care |
