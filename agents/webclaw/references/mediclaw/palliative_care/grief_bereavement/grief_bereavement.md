## Normal Grief vs Complicated Grief
| Feature | Normal Grief | Complicated Grief | URL |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Duration** | Improves over months-years | **Persistent**, **Prolonged** (>12 months) | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/grief-and-mourning |
| **Intensity** | Gradual decrease | **Remains intense** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/grief-and-mourning |
| **Function** | Able to function | **Significant impairment** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/grief-and-mourning |
| **Identity** | Preserved sense of self | **Loss of identity**, **Feeling empty** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/grief-and-mourning |
| **Yearning** | Decreases over time | **Intense persistent yearning** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/grief-and-mourning |

## Anticipatory Grief
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Grief occurring before an impending loss** | https://www.nhpco.org/anticipatory-grief |
| **Symptoms** | Similar to post-death grief: Sadness, Anger, Anxiety, Withdrawal | https://www.nhpco.org/anticipatory-grief |
| **Benefits** | **Preparation**, **Opportunity to say goodbye**, **Complete unfinished business** | https://www.nhpco.org/anticipatory-grief |
| **Risks** | **Emotional withdrawal** from patient, **Complicated grief** after death | https://www.nhpco.org/anticipatory-grief |
| **Support** | **Acknowledge**, **Normalize**, **Encourage connection** with patient | https://www.nhpco.org/anticipatory-grief |
