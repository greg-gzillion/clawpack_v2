# Palliative Care - Spiritual Care

## Spiritual Assessment
| Tool | Questions | URL |
|------|-----------|-----|
| **FICA** | **F**aith: "Do you consider yourself spiritual or religious?" **I**mportance: "How important is this in your life?" **C**ommunity: "Are you part of a spiritual community?" **A**ddress: "How would you like me to address these issues?" | https://www.nhpco.org/spiritual-care |
| **HOPE** | **H**ope: "What are your sources of hope?" **O**rganized religion: "Are you part of a religious community?" **P**ersonal spirituality: "What personal spiritual practices are important?" **E**ffects: "How do your beliefs affect your medical decisions?" | https://www.nhpco.org/spiritual-care |

|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Dignity Therapy
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Brief psychotherapy** to address **existential distress** and **preserve dignity** | https://dignityincare.ca |
| **Process** | **Guided interview** about life story, Accomplishments, Lessons, Hopes for loved ones | https://dignityincare.ca |
| **Outcome** | **Generativity document** (edited transcript) to share with family | https://dignityincare.ca |
| **Benefits** | **Increased sense of dignity**, **Purpose**, **Meaning**, **Reduced existential distress** | https://dignityincare.ca |
