# Palliative Care - Advance Care Planning

## Advance Care Planning - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Process of **discussing and documenting** patient's values, goals, and preferences for future medical care | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/advance-care-planning |
| **Timing** | **Early** in disease trajectory, **Before crisis**, **Revisit with changes in condition** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/advance-care-planning |
| **Key Documents** | **Living Will**, **Healthcare Proxy** (Durable Power of Attorney for Healthcare), **POLST/MOLST** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/advance-care-planning |

## Advance Directive Components
| Document | Purpose | URL |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Living Will** | Specifies **treatment preferences** (ventilator, CPR, Artificial nutrition/hydration) | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/advance-care-planning |
| **Healthcare Proxy** | Designates **surrogate decision-maker** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/advance-care-planning |
| **POLST/MOLST** | **Portable medical orders** (signed by provider); **Honored across settings** | https://polst.org |

## POLST/MOLST Components
| Section | Options | URL |
|---------|---------|-----|
| **CPR** | **Attempt CPR** vs **Do Not Attempt (DNR)** | https://polst.org |
| **Medical Interventions** | **Comfort measures only**, **Limited interventions**, **Full treatment** | https://polst.org |
| **Antibiotics** | **No antibiotics**, **Determine use based on goals**, **Full use** | https://polst.org |
| **Artificial Nutrition** | **No artificial nutrition**, **Trial period**, **Long-term** | https://polst.org |

## Goals of Care Conversations - Serious Illness Conversation Guide
| Step | Question | URL |
|------|----------|-----|
| **1. Set up** | "I'd like to talk about what's ahead with your illness and do some planning." | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |
| **2. Assess understanding** | "What is your understanding of where you are with your illness?" | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |
| **3. Share prognosis** | "I want to share with you my understanding of where things are with your illness." | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |
| **4. Explore goals** | "What are your most important goals if your health situation worsens?" | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |
| **5. Explore fears** | "What are your biggest fears and worries about the future?" | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |
| **6. Explore trade-offs** | "What abilities are so critical to your life that you can't imagine living without them?" | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |
| **7. Explore family** | "How much does your family know about your priorities and wishes?" | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |
| **8. Close** | "I will do everything I can to help you through this." | https://www.ariadnelabs.org/serious-illness-care-program |

## Surrogate Decision-Making
| Standard | Description | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Substituted Judgment** | What would the **patient have wanted**? (preferred) | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/surrogate-decision-making |
| **Best Interest** | What is in the **patient's best interest**? (if patient's wishes unknown) | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/surrogate-decision-making |
| **Hierarchy of Surrogates** | **Spouse** ? **Adult children** ? **Parents** ? **Siblings** ? **Other relatives** | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/surrogate-decision-making |
