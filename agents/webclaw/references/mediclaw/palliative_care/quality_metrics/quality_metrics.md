# Palliative Care - Quality Metrics

## National Quality Forum (NQF) Palliative Care Measures
| Measure | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Pain Assessment** | % of patients with **documented pain assessment** | https://www.qualityforum.org/Palliative_Care |
| **Pain Treatment** | % of patients with pain receiving **appropriate treatment** | https://www.qualityforum.org/Palliative_Care |
| **Dyspnea Assessment** | % of patients with **documented dyspnea assessment** | https://www.qualityforum.org/Palliative_Care |
| **Dyspnea Treatment** | % of patients with dyspnea receiving **appropriate treatment** | https://www.qualityforum.org/Palliative_Care |
| **Advance Care Planning** | % of patients with **documented advance care planning discussion** | https://www.qualityforum.org/Palliative_Care |
| **Spiritual Assessment** | % of patients with **documented spiritual assessment** | https://www.qualityforum.org/Palliative_Care |
| **Bereavement Support** | % of families offered **bereavement support** | https://www.qualityforum.org/Palliative_Care |
