## Palliative Performance Scale (PPS)
| PPS | Description | Survival Estimate | URL |
|-----|-------------|-------------------|-----|
| **100%** | Full ambulation, Normal activity, No disease | Years | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
| **70%** | Full ambulation, Reduced activity, Some disease | Months-Years | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
| **60%** | Full ambulation, Reduced activity, Significant disease | Months | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
| **50%** | Mainly sit/lie, Self-care intact, Significant disease | Weeks-Months | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
| **40%** | Mainly in bed, Extensive assistance | Weeks | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
| **30%** | Totally bedbound, Total care | Days-Weeks | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
| **20%** | Totally bedbound, Minimal intake | Days | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
| **10%** | Totally bedbound, Unresponsive | Hours-Days | https://www.nhpco.org/palliative-performance-scale |
