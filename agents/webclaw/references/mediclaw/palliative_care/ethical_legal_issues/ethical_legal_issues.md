## Withholding and Withdrawing Life-Sustaining Treatment
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Ethical Equivalence** | **No ethical difference** between withholding and withdrawing | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/withholding-withdrawing-life-sustaining-treatment |
| **Patient Autonomy** | Patient/surrogate has right to **refuse or discontinue** treatment | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/withholding-withdrawing-life-sustaining-treatment |
| **Futility** | **No obligation** to provide medically **futile** treatment | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/medical-futility |
| **Palliative Sedation** | **Intent is symptom relief**, **Not hastening death** (doctrine of double effect) | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/palliative-sedation |

## Physician-Assisted Death (PAD) and Euthanasia
| Term | Definition | Legal Status | URL |
|------|------------|--------------|-----|
| **Physician-Assisted Death (PAD)** | Physician provides **lethal prescription**; Patient **self-administers** | **Legal** in OR, WA, CA, CO, HI, VT, ME, NJ, NM, DC | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/physician-assisted-suicide |
| **Euthanasia** | Physician **administers** lethal medication | **Illegal** in US; Legal in Canada, Netherlands, Belgium | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/euthanasia |
| **AMA Position** | **Opposes** both PAD and euthanasia | | https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/ethics/physician-assisted-suicide |
