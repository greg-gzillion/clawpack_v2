# Palliative Care - Dyspnea Management

## Dyspnea - Assessment and Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Subjective experience of breathing discomfort** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Assessment** | **Numerical rating scale** (0-10), **Modified Borg Scale**, **Respiratory Distress Observation Scale (RDOS)** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Non-Pharmacologic** | **Fan** (cool air on face), **Positioning** (upright, Leaning forward), **Pursed-lip breathing**, **Relaxation techniques**, **Energy conservation** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |

## Pharmacologic Management of Dyspnea
| Drug | Dose (Opioid-Naive) | Dose (Opioid-Tolerant) | Considerations | URL |
|------|---------------------|------------------------|----------------|-----|
| **Morphine** | 2.5-5 mg PO/SC q4h PRN | 10-20% of total daily opioid dose | **First-line**; Monitor for respiratory depression | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Hydromorphone** | 0.5-1 mg PO/SC q4h PRN | 10-20% of total daily dose | Alternative to morphine | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Oxycodone** | 2.5-5 mg PO q4h PRN | 10-20% of total daily dose | Alternative to morphine | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Lorazepam** | 0.5-1 mg PO/SL q4-6h PRN | Same | **Adjunctive** for anxiety-associated dyspnea; **Avoid as monotherapy** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Dexamethasone** | 4-8 mg daily | Same | **Lymphangitic carcinomatosis**, **Tumor obstruction**, **COPD exacerbation**, **SVC syndrome** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Furosemide** | 20-40 mg IV/PO | Same | **CHF**, **Volume overload** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |

## Oxygen Therapy
| Indication | Recommendation | URL |
|------------|----------------|-----|
| **Hypoxia (SpO2 <88%)** | **Oxygen therapy** to maintain SpO2 >90% | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Normoxia (SpO2 =88%)** | **No benefit** from oxygen; Focus on non-pharmacologic and opioids | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Placebo Effect** | Fan may be as effective as oxygen for subjective relief | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |

## Disease-Specific Dyspnea Management
| Disease | Specific Interventions | URL |
|---------|----------------------|-----|
| **COPD** | **Bronchodilators** (SABA, LABA, SAMA, LAMA), **Corticosteroids**, **Pulmonary rehabilitation** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/copd |
| **CHF** | **Diuretics**, **ACE-I/ARB**, **Beta-blockers**, **Nitrates** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-failure |
| **Pleural Effusion** | **Thoracentesis**, **Pleurodesis**, **Indwelling pleural catheter** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Lymphangitic Carcinomatosis** | **Dexamethasone**, **Opioids** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **SVC Syndrome** | **Dexamethasone**, **Radiation**, **Stenting** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Anemia** | **Transfusion** (if symptomatic), **Iron supplementation** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |

## Terminal Dyspnea
| Component | Management | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Goals** | **Relieve suffering**, **Not prolong life** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Opioids** | **Morphine** 2.5-5 mg SC q15-30min until comfort | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Benzodiazepines** | **Lorazepam** 0.5-1 mg SL/SC for associated anxiety | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Non-Pharmacologic** | **Fan**, **Positioning**, **Family presence**, **Reassurance** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Discontinue Non-Beneficial Interventions** | **Stop oxygen** (if no benefit), **Stop monitoring**, **Stop IV fluids** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
