# Palliative Care - Non-Pain Symptom Management

## Dyspnea
| Component | Management | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Non-Pharmacologic** | **Fan**, **Positioning** (upright, Leaning forward), **Pursed-lip breathing**, **Relaxation** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Opioids** | **Morphine** 2.5-5 mg PO/SC q4h PRN (opioid-naive); **First-line** for dyspnea | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Benzodiazepines** | Lorazepam 0.5-1 mg PO/SL q4-6h PRN for **anxiety-associated dyspnea** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Oxygen** | Only if **hypoxic** (SpO2 <88%); No benefit if normoxic | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Corticosteroids** | Dexamethasone for **lymphangitic carcinomatosis**, **Tumor obstruction**, **COPD exacerbation** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |
| **Diuretics** | For **CHF**, **Volume overload** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/dyspnea/ |

| **Increased ICP** | **Dexamethasone** | Anti-inflammatory | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/nausea-vomiting/ |
| **Opioid-Induced** | **Haloperidol**, **Metoclopramide**, **Ondansetron** | D2 antagonist, Prokinetic, 5-HT3 antagonist | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/nausea-vomiting/ |

## Constipation
| Component | Management | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Prevention** | **Scheduled stimulant laxative** (senna) + Stool softener (docusate) for **all patients on opioids** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Osmotic Laxatives** | **Polyethylene glycol (PEG)** , **Lactulose** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Refractory Constipation** | **Methylnaltrexone** (peripheral opioid antagonist), **Naloxegol**, **Naldemedine** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |
| **Impaction** | **Manual disimpaction**, **Enemas**, **Suppositories** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/constipation/ |

## Fatigue
| Component | Management | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Non-Pharmacologic** | **Energy conservation**, **Exercise** (as tolerated), **Sleep hygiene** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/fatigue/ |
| **Treat Underlying Causes** | **Anemia**, **Depression**, **Hypothyroidism**, **Medication side effects** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/fatigue/ |
| **Pharmacologic** | **Methylphenidate** 2.5-5 mg AM and noon, **Modafinil** | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/fatigue/ |
| **Corticosteroids** | **Dexamethasone** 2-4 mg daily for short-term energy boost | https://www.capc.org/toolkits/fatigue/ |

## Anorexia and Cachexia
