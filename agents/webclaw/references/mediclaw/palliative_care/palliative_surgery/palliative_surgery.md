# Palliative Care - Palliative Surgery

## Indications for Palliative Surgery
| Indication | Examples | URL |
|------------|----------|-----|
| **Obstruction** | **Bowel obstruction** (colostomy, Ileostomy), **Biliary obstruction** (stent, Bypass), **Airway obstruction** (tracheostomy, Stent) | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Bleeding** | **Tumor debulking**, **Vessel ligation/embolization** | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Pain** | **Spinal stabilization** (cord compression), **Nerve blocks**, **Tumor debulking** | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Wound Care** | **Debridement**, **Amputation** (fungating tumor) | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Pathologic Fracture** | **Internal fixation** (impending or actual fracture) | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |

## Decision-Making for Palliative Surgery
| Factor | Consideration | URL |
|--------|---------------|-----|
| **Goals of Care** | Align with patient's values and preferences | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Performance Status** | **ECOG 0-2**: Likely to benefit; **ECOG 3-4**: High surgical risk | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Life Expectancy** | **Weeks**: Non-operative management preferred; **Months**: Surgery may be appropriate | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Symptom Burden** | **Severe, Refractory symptoms** may justify surgery | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Alternatives** | **Interventional radiology**, **Radiation**, **Medical management** | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |

## Palliative Surgery Outcomes
| Outcome | Consideration | URL |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| **Symptom Relief** | **60-90%** achieve symptom relief | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Morbidity** | **20-40%** complication rate | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Mortality** | **5-10%** 30-day mortality | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
| **Quality of Life** | **Variable**; Important to set realistic expectations | https://www.facs.org/for-medical-professionals/education/programs/palliative-surgery/ |
