## Subcutaneous ICD (S-ICD)
| Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|-----------|---------------|-----|
| No vascular access | No pacing capability (except post-shock) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Reduced lead complications | No ATP | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Avoids transvenous lead extraction | Larger device | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Young patients; Infection risk | Cannot treat bradycardia | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
