# Cardiology - Pulmonary Hypertension

## Hemodynamic Definition
| Parameter | Definition | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| mPAP | >20 mmHg at rest | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| PAWP | =15 mmHg (Pre-capillary); >15 mmHg (Post-capillary) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| PVR | =3 WU (Pre-capillary); <3 WU (Post-capillary) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |

## WHO Functional Class
| Class | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Class I | No limitation of physical activity | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Class II | Slight limitation; comfortable at rest; ordinary activity causes dyspnea/fatigue | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Class III | Marked limitation; comfortable at rest; less than ordinary activity causes symptoms | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Class IV | Unable to carry on any physical activity; symptoms at rest; signs of right heart failure | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |

## Diagnostic Evaluation
| Test | Purpose | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| Echocardiography | Estimate RVSP/PASP; RV size/function; Left heart disease | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Right Heart Catheterization | Confirm diagnosis; Hemodynamic classification; Vasoreactivity testing | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| V/Q Scan | Rule out CTEPH | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| PFTs | Rule out Group 3 | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Overnight Oximetry/PSG | Rule out OSA/hypoventilation | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| CTD Serologies | ANA; RF; Anti-Scl-70; Anti-centromere; Anti-U1 RNP | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| HIV; LFTs; TSH | Rule out other causes | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |

## Vasoreactivity Testing
| Response | Definition | Management | URL |
|----------|------------|------------|-----|
| Positive | Decrease in mPAP =10 mmHg to =40 mmHg with preserved CO | High-dose CCB | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Negative | Does not meet criteria | PAH-specific therapy | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Note | Only performed in IPAH, HPAH, or drug-induced PAH | | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |

## PAH Risk Assessment
| Determinant | Low Risk | Intermediate Risk | High Risk | URL |
|-------------|----------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| WHO FC | I, II | III | IV | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| 6MWD | >440 m | 165-440 m | <165 m | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| BNP/NT-proBNP | Normal/minimally elevated | Moderately elevated | Markedly elevated | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| RAP | <8 mmHg | 8-14 mmHg | >14 mmHg | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| CI | =2.5 L/min/m2 | 2.0-2.4 L/min/m2 | <2.0 L/min/m2 | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| SvO2 | >65% | 60-65% | <60% | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Echo | Normal/near-normal RV | Some RV dysfunction | Significant RV dysfunction; Pericardial effusion | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |

## PAH Treatment Algorithm
| Risk | Initial Therapy | URL |
|------|-----------------|-----|
| Low Risk | Oral combination (ERA + PDE5i) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Intermediate Risk | Oral combination (ERA + PDE5i); Consider prostacyclin | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| High Risk | Parenteral prostacyclin + oral combination | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |

## CTEPH (Chronic Thromboembolic Pulmonary Hypertension)
| Feature | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Definition | PH due to chronic thromboembolic obstruction of pulmonary arteries | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Diagnosis | V/Q scan (screening); Pulmonary angiography; RHC | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Treatment | PTE (Pulmonary Thromboendarterectomy) - curative; Riociguat (inoperable or residual); Balloon pulmonary angioplasty | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Anticoagulation | Lifelong | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
