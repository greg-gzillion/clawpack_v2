## PCI Complications
| Complication | Incidence | Management | URL |
|--------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| Coronary Dissection | 1-2% | Stent; Prolonged balloon inflation | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Coronary Perforation | 0.2-0.5% | Balloon tamponade; Covered stent; Pericardiocentesis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| No-Reflow | 1-2% | Intracoronary vasodilators (Adenosine; Verapamil; Nitroprusside) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Stent Thrombosis | 0.5-1% (first year) | Emergency PCI; Optimize antiplatelet therapy | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Contrast-Induced Nephropathy | 2-5% | Hydration; Minimize contrast; Avoid nephrotoxins | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Vascular Access Complications | 2-5% | Manual compression; Vascular closure devices; Surgery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
