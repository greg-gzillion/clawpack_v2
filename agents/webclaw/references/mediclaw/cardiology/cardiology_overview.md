# Cardiology - Overview

## What is Cardiology?
Cardiology is the medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the heart and circulatory system. It encompasses coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, valvular heart disease, congenital heart defects, and vascular diseases.

## Major Subspecialties
| Subspecialty | Focus |
|--------------|-------|
| Interventional Cardiology | Catheter-based treatment of structural heart disease |
| Electrophysiology | Diagnosis and treatment of heart rhythm disorders |
| Heart Failure and Transplant | Advanced heart failure management |
| Adult Congenital Heart Disease | Care of adults with congenital heart defects |
| Cardiac Imaging | Echocardiography, nuclear, CT, MRI |
| Preventive Cardiology | Risk factor modification and prevention |
| Vascular Medicine | Peripheral arterial and venous disease |
