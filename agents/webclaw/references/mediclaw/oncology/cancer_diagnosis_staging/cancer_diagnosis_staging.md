# Oncology - Cancer Diagnosis and Staging

## Diagnostic Modalities
| Modality | Indications | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Biopsy** | **Gold standard** for cancer diagnosis; **Core needle** (preferred) or **FNA** or **Excisional** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/diagnosis |
| **Immunohistochemistry (IHC)** | Protein expression; Tumor typing, Lineage, Prognostic markers | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/diagnosis |
| **Flow Cytometry** | Cell surface markers; Hematologic malignancies | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/diagnosis |
| **Cytogenetics/FISH** | Chromosomal abnormalities, Translocations, Amplifications | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/diagnosis |
| **Molecular Testing** | **NGS panels**, PCR; Mutations, Fusions, MSI, TMB | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/diagnosis |
| **Tumor Markers** | PSA (prostate), CA-125 (ovarian), CEA (colorectal), AFP (liver/germ cell) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/diagnosis |

## TNM Staging System (AJCC 8th Edition)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **T (Tumor)** | Size and extent of primary tumor; **T0** (no evidence), **Tis** (in situ), **T1-T4** (increasing size/invasion) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |
| **N (Node)** | Regional lymph node involvement; **N0** (none), **N1-N3** (increasing number/extent) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |
| **M (Metastasis)** | Distant metastasis; **M0** (none), **M1** (present) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |

## AJCC Prognostic Stage Groups
| Stage | Definition | URL |
|-------|------------|-----|
| **Stage 0** | Carcinoma in situ (Tis, N0, M0) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |
| **Stage I** | Localized, Small tumor, No nodal involvement (T1-2, N0, M0) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |
| **Stage II** | Locally advanced, Larger tumor or limited nodal involvement | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |
| **Stage III** | Extensive local/regional disease, Significant nodal involvement | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |
| **Stage IV** | Distant metastasis (Any T, Any N, M1) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/diagnosis-staging/staging |

## Response Evaluation Criteria in Solid Tumors (RECIST 1.1)
| Category | Definition | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Complete Response (CR)** | Disappearance of all target lesions | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/clinical-trials/recist |
| **Partial Response (PR)** | =30% decrease in sum of diameters of target lesions | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/clinical-trials/recist |
| **Progressive Disease (PD)** | =20% increase in sum of diameters OR new lesions | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/clinical-trials/recist |
| **Stable Disease (SD)** | Neither PR nor PD | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/clinical-trials/recist |
