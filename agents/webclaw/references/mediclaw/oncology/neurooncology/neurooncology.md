## Glioblastoma - Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Standard of Care** | **Maximal safe resection** ? **Radiation** (60 Gy) + **Concurrent Temozolomide** ? **Adjuvant Temozolomide** x 6-12 cycles | https://www.cancer.gov/types/brain/hp/adult-brain-treatment-pdq |
| **Tumor Treating Fields (TTFields)** | **Optune** (alternating electric fields); Improves survival | https://www.cancer.gov/types/brain/hp/adult-brain-treatment-pdq |
| **Recurrent Disease** | **Bevacizumab**, **Lomustine**, **Re-irradiation**, Clinical trials | https://www.cancer.gov/types/brain/hp/adult-brain-treatment-pdq |
