## Immune-Related Adverse Events (irAEs) - Management
| irAE | Grade 2 | Grade 3-4 | URL |
|------|---------|-----------|-----|
| **Colitis/Diarrhea** | **Hold ICI**, **Corticosteroids** (0.5-1 mg/kg) | **High-dose corticosteroids** (1-2 mg/kg), **Infliximab** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/immunotherapy |
| **Hepatitis** | **Hold ICI**, **Corticosteroids** (0.5-1 mg/kg) | **High-dose corticosteroids** (1-2 mg/kg), **Mycophenolate** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/immunotherapy |
| **Pneumonitis** | **Hold ICI**, **Corticosteroids** (0.5-1 mg/kg) | **High-dose corticosteroids** (1-2 mg/kg), **Infliximab**, **Cyclophosphamide** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/immunotherapy |
| **Hypophysitis** | **Hormone replacement** | **Hormone replacement**, High-dose steroids if mass effect | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/immunotherapy |
| **Thyroiditis** | **Levothyroxine** (hypothyroid) | **Beta-blockers** (hyperthyroid), Levothyroxine | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/immunotherapy |
| **Rash** | **Topical steroids**, Antihistamines | **Systemic steroids**, **Hold ICI** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/immunotherapy |

## Cytokine Release Syndrome (CRS)
| Grade | Symptoms | Management | URL |
|-------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Grade 1** | Fever | **Supportive care**, **Acetaminophen**, **IV fluids** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/crs |
| **Grade 2** | Hypotension (no pressors), Hypoxia (low-flow O2) | **Tocilizumab** ｱ **Corticosteroids** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/crs |
| **Grade 3** | Hypotension (pressors), Hypoxia (high-flow O2) | **Tocilizumab + Corticosteroids** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/crs |
