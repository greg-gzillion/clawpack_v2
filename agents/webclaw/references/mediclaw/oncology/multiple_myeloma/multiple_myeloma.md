## Multiple Myeloma - Risk Stratification (R-ISS)
| Stage | Criteria | 5-Year OS | URL |
|-------|----------|----------|-----|
| **R-ISS I** | ß2M <3.5, Albumin =3.5, **Standard-risk cytogenetics**, Normal LDH | 82% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/myeloma/hp/myeloma-treatment-pdq |
| **R-ISS II** | Neither I nor III | 62% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/myeloma/hp/myeloma-treatment-pdq |
| **R-ISS III** | ß2M =5.5 + **High-risk cytogenetics** OR **Elevated LDH** | 40% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/myeloma/hp/myeloma-treatment-pdq |
| **High-Risk Cytogenetics** | **del(17p)**, **t(4;14)**, **t(14;16)**, **t(14;20)**, **1q gain/amplification** | | https://www.cancer.gov/types/myeloma/hp/myeloma-treatment-pdq |
