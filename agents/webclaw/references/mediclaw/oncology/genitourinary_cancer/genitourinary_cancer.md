|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Bladder Cancer - Treatment by Stage
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Ta, T1 (NMIBC)** | **TURBT** ? **Intravesical BCG** (high-risk) or **Intravesical chemotherapy** (intermediate-risk) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bladder/hp/bladder-treatment-pdq |
| **T2-T4a (MIBC)** | **Neoadjuvant cisplatin-based chemotherapy** ? **Radical cystectomy + PLND** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bladder/hp/bladder-treatment-pdq |
| **Metastatic** | **Cisplatin-based chemotherapy** (GC or MVAC) if cisplatin-eligible; **Enfortumab vedotin + Pembrolizumab**; **Checkpoint inhibitors** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bladder/hp/bladder-treatment-pdq |

## Renal Cell Carcinoma (RCC)
| Subtype | Frequency | Genetics | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Clear Cell RCC** | 75% | **VHL** (3p25) | **ICI + TKI** (IO/IO or IO/TKI) for advanced | https://www.cancer.gov/types/kidney/hp/kidney-treatment-pdq |
| **Papillary RCC** | 15% | MET, Hereditary papillary RCC | ICI + TKI or TKI alone | https://www.cancer.gov/types/kidney/hp/kidney-treatment-pdq |
| **Chromophobe RCC** | 5% | Multiple chromosomal losses | ICI + TKI or TKI alone | https://www.cancer.gov/types/kidney/hp/kidney-treatment-pdq |

| Risk Group | First-Line Therapy | URL |
