|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Epithelial Ovarian Cancer - Treatment
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I** | **Surgical staging** (TH/BSO, Omentectomy, Lymphadenectomy); Adjuvant chemotherapy for high-risk | https://www.cancer.gov/types/ovarian/hp/ovarian-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage II-IV** | **Primary debulking surgery** ? **Platinum-based chemotherapy** (Carboplatin + Paclitaxel) OR **Neoadjuvant chemotherapy** ? Interval debulking | https://www.cancer.gov/types/ovarian/hp/ovarian-treatment-pdq |
| **Maintenance** | **PARP inhibitor** (olaparib, Niraparib, Rucaparib) for BRCA-mutated or HRD-positive; **Bevacizumab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/ovarian/hp/ovarian-treatment-pdq |
| **Recurrent (Platinum-Sensitive)** | **Platinum doublet** ｱ Bevacizumab ? PARP inhibitor maintenance | https://www.cancer.gov/types/ovarian/hp/ovarian-treatment-pdq |
| **Recurrent (Platinum-Resistant)** | **Non-platinum agents**: Pegylated liposomal doxorubicin, Gemcitabine, Topotecan, Paclitaxel (weekly) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/ovarian/hp/ovarian-treatment-pdq |
