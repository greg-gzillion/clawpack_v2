# Oncology - Breast Cancer

## Breast Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | **Most common cancer in women** (excluding skin); 1 in 8 lifetime risk | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast |
| **Risk Factors** | Age, **BRCA1/2**, Family history, Early menarche, Late menopause, Nulliparity, Obesity, Alcohol, HRT | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-prevention-pdq |
| **Subtypes** | **HR+/HER2-** (70%), **HER2+** (15-20%), **Triple-Negative** (10-15%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |

## Breast Cancer - Treatment by Stage
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I-II (HR+/HER2-)** | **Surgery** (lumpectomy + radiation or mastectomy) + **Endocrine therapy** (tamoxifen or AI) ｱ Chemotherapy (genomic testing) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage I-II (HER2+)** | **Neoadjuvant chemotherapy + Anti-HER2** ? Surgery ? Adjuvant anti-HER2; **T-DM1** if residual disease | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage I-II (Triple-Negative)** | **Neoadjuvant chemotherapy + Pembrolizumab** (PD-L1+) ? Surgery ? Adjuvant pembrolizumab ｱ Capecitabine (if residual) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage III** | **Neoadjuvant systemic therapy** ? Surgery ? Radiation ? Adjuvant therapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV (HR+/HER2-)** | **Endocrine therapy + CDK4/6 inhibitor** (first-line); Chemotherapy (visceral crisis, Endocrine-refractory) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV (HER2+)** | **Chemotherapy + Dual anti-HER2** (trastuzumab + pertuzumab); **T-DXd** (second-line) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV (Triple-Negative)** | **Chemotherapy** ｱ Pembrolizumab (PD-L1+) ｱ PARP inhibitor (BRCA-mutated); **Sacituzumab govitecan** (later lines) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/breast/hp/breast-treatment-pdq |
