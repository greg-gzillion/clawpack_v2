# Oncology - Colorectal Cancer

## Colorectal Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | Third most common cancer; Second leading cause of cancer death | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal |
| **Risk Factors** | Age >50, **Family history**, **IBD**, **Lynch syndrome**, **FAP**, Obesity, Red/processed meat, Smoking, Alcohol | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colorectal-prevention-pdq |
| **Pathogenesis** | **Adenoma-carcinoma sequence** (APC ? KRAS ? TP53); **Serrated pathway** (BRAF) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colorectal-treatment-pdq |

## Colorectal Cancer - Molecular Subtypes
| Subtype | Frequency | Key Alterations | Prognosis | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **MSS (Microsatellite Stable)** | 85% | APC, KRAS, TP53 | Standard | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colorectal-treatment-pdq |
| **MSI-H (Microsatellite Instability-High)** | 15% | **MMR deficiency** (MLH1, MSH2, MSH6, PMS2), **BRAF V600E** (sporadic) | **Better prognosis**, **Responds to immunotherapy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colorectal-treatment-pdq |

## Colorectal Cancer - Treatment by Stage
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I** | **Surgical resection alone** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage II (Low-Risk)** | Surgery alone; Observation | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage II (High-Risk)** | Surgery + **Adjuvant chemotherapy** (FOLFOX or CAPOX) for T4, Perforation, Obstruction, LVI, PNI, <12 nodes, Poor differentiation | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage III** | Surgery + **Adjuvant chemotherapy** (FOLFOX or CAPOX) x 3-6 months | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV (Resectable)** | **Neoadjuvant chemotherapy** ? Surgery ? Adjuvant chemotherapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV (Unresectable)** | **Systemic therapy** (chemotherapy + targeted therapy ｱ immunotherapy for MSI-H) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |

## Metastatic Colorectal Cancer - Systemic Therapy
| Molecular Profile | First-Line Therapy | URL |
|-------------------|-------------------|-----|
| **RAS/BRAF Wild-Type (Left-Sided)** | **FOLFOX/FOLFIRI + Anti-EGFR** (cetuximab or panitumumab) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **RAS/BRAF Wild-Type (Right-Sided)** | **FOLFOX/FOLFIRI + Bevacizumab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **RAS Mutant** | **FOLFOX/FOLFIRI + Bevacizumab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **BRAF V600E Mutant** | **FOLFOXIRI + Bevacizumab**; Encorafenib + Cetuximab (second-line) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **MSI-H/dMMR** | **Pembrolizumab** (first-line, regardless of RAS/BRAF) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |
| **HER2 Amplified** | Trastuzumab + Pertuzumab or T-DXd (later lines) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/colon-treatment-pdq |

## Rectal Cancer - Special Considerations
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage II-III** | **Total neoadjuvant therapy (TNT)** : Chemoradiation + Chemotherapy ? **TME surgery** ? Adjuvant chemotherapy OR **Non-operative management** (watch and wait) if clinical complete response | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/rectal-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV** | Similar to colon cancer; Consider **pelvic radiation** for symptomatic primary | https://www.cancer.gov/types/colorectal/hp/rectal-treatment-pdq |

## Hereditary Colorectal Cancer Syndromes
| Syndrome | Gene(s) | Lifetime CRC Risk | Management | URL |
|----------|---------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Lynch Syndrome** | MLH1, MSH2, MSH6, PMS2, EPCAM | 30-70% | **Colonoscopy q1-2 years** starting age 20-25 | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/genetics/lynch-syndrome |
| **Familial Adenomatous Polyposis (FAP)** | APC | 100% | **Annual sigmoidoscopy** starting age 10-12; **Prophylactic colectomy** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/genetics/fap |
| **Attenuated FAP (AFAP)** | APC | 70% | Colonoscopy q1-2 years starting age 20-25 | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/genetics/fap |
| **MUTYH-Associated Polyposis (MAP)** | MUTYH (biallelic) | 80% | Colonoscopy q1-2 years starting age 20-25 | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/genetics/map |
| **Peutz-Jeghers Syndrome** | STK11 | 40% | EGD + Colonoscopy q2-3 years starting age 8 | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/genetics/peutz-jeghers |
| **Juvenile Polyposis Syndrome** | SMAD4, BMPR1A | 40-50% | Colonoscopy + EGD q1-3 years starting age 12-15 | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/genetics/juvenile-polyposis |
