## Late Effects of Cancer Treatment
| Treatment | Late Effect | Monitoring | URL |
|-----------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **Anthracyclines** | **Cardiomyopathy** | **Echocardiogram** (periodic) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Chest Radiation** | **CAD**, **Valvular disease**, **Pericarditis**, **Secondary breast/lung cancer** | Cardiac risk factor management, Echocardiogram, Mammography/Lung screening | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Platinum Agents** | **Peripheral neuropathy**, **Ototoxicity**, **Nephrotoxicity** | Symptom assessment, Audiometry, Renal function | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Alkylating Agents** | **Infertility**, **Secondary MDS/AML** | Fertility counseling, CBC monitoring | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Endocrine Therapy** | **Hot flashes**, **Arthralgias**, **Osteoporosis**, **Vaginal dryness**, **Sexual dysfunction** | Bone density, Symptom management | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Pelvic Radiation** | **Infertility**, **Premature menopause**, **Bladder/bowel dysfunction**, **Secondary malignancy** | Gynecologic/Colorectal surveillance | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Cranial Radiation** | **Cognitive impairment**, **Hypopituitarism**, **Secondary brain tumor** | Neurocognitive assessment, Endocrine evaluation | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |

## Secondary Malignancy Screening
| Primary Cancer | Secondary Malignancy Risk | Screening | URL |
|----------------|--------------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Hodgkin Lymphoma** | **Breast** (chest radiation), **Lung**, **MDS/AML**, **Thyroid** | **Mammography/MRI** (age 25 or 8 years post-RT), Lung screening | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Breast Cancer** | **Contralateral breast**, **Ovarian** (BRCA), **Endometrial** (tamoxifen), **MDS/AML** | Annual mammography, Gynecologic surveillance | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship/late-effects |
| **Childhood Cancer** | Variable depending on treatment | **Long-term follow-up guidelines** (COG) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/childhood-cancers/late-effects-pdq |
