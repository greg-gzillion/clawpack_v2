# Oncology - Hepatobiliary Cancer

## Hepatocellular Carcinoma (HCC)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | Most common primary liver cancer; Rising incidence in US | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver |
| **Risk Factors** | **Cirrhosis** (any cause), **HBV**, **HCV**, **NAFLD/NASH**, **Alcohol**, **Aflatoxin**, **Hemochromatosis** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-prevention-pdq |
| **Screening** | **Ultrasound ± AFP q6 months** for high-risk patients (cirrhosis, HBV) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-screening-pdq |

## HCC - Staging and Treatment (BCLC)
| Stage | Criteria | Treatment | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **0 (Very Early)** | Single =2 cm, Child-Pugh A, ECOG 0 | **Resection**, Ablation | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-treatment-pdq |
| **A (Early)** | Single or =3 nodules =3 cm, Child-Pugh A-B, ECOG 0 | **Resection**, Ablation, **Liver transplant** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-treatment-pdq |
| **B (Intermediate)** | Multinodular, Child-Pugh A-B, ECOG 0 | **TACE** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-treatment-pdq |
| **C (Advanced)** | Portal invasion or extrahepatic spread, Child-Pugh A-B, ECOG 1-2 | **Systemic therapy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-treatment-pdq |
| **D (Terminal)** | Child-Pugh C, ECOG >2 | **Best supportive care** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-treatment-pdq |

## Advanced HCC - Systemic Therapy
| Line | Options | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| **First-Line** | **Atezolizumab + Bevacizumab** (preferred), **Tremelimumab + Durvalumab**, **Lenvatinib**, **Sorafenib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-treatment-pdq |
| **Second-Line** | **Cabozantinib**, **Regorafenib**, **Ramucirumab** (AFP =400), **Pembrolizumab**, **Nivolumab + Ipilimumab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/liver-treatment-pdq |

## Cholangiocarcinoma
| Type | Location | Risk Factors | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Intrahepatic** | Within liver | Cirrhosis, HBV/HCV, NAFLD, Liver flukes | Resection, TACE/TARE, Systemic therapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
| **Perihilar (Klatskin)** | Bifurcation | **PSC**, Choledochal cysts, Liver flukes | Resection ± Liver transplant, Palliative stenting | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
| **Distal** | Common bile duct | PSC, Choledochal cysts | **Pancreaticoduodenectomy (Whipple)** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |

## Advanced Cholangiocarcinoma - Systemic Therapy
| Molecular Profile | Therapy | URL |
|-------------------|---------|-----|
| **All Comers** | **Gemcitabine + Cisplatin + Durvalumab** or **Pembrolizumab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
| **FGFR2 Fusion/Rearrangement** | **Pemigatinib**, **Futibatinib**, **Infigratinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
| **IDH1 Mutation** | **Ivosidenib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
| **BRAF V600E** | **Dabrafenib + Trametinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
| **MSI-H/dMMR** | **Pembrolizumab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
| **HER2 Amplification** | **Trastuzumab + Pertuzumab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/liver/hp/bile-duct-treatment-pdq |
