# Oncology - Lung Cancer

## Lung Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | **Leading cause of cancer death**; 85% due to smoking | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung |
| **Histologic Types** | **Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC)** - 85%; **Small Cell Lung Cancer (SCLC)** - 15% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **NSCLC Subtypes** | **Adenocarcinoma** (40%), **Squamous cell** (25-30%), **Large cell** (5-10%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |

## NSCLC - Molecular Drivers and Targeted Therapy
| Alteration | Frequency | Targeted Therapy | URL |
|------------|-----------|------------------|-----|
| **EGFR mutation** | 10-15% (higher in Asian, Never-smokers) | **Osimertinib** (preferred), Erlotinib, Gefitinib, Afatinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **ALK fusion** | 3-5% | **Alectinib** (preferred), Brigatinib, Lorlatinib, Crizotinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **ROS1 fusion** | 1-2% | **Crizotinib**, Entrectinib, Repotrectinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **BRAF V600E** | 1-2% | **Dabrafenib + Trametinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **MET exon 14 skipping** | 3% | **Capmatinib**, Tepotinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **RET fusion** | 1-2% | **Selpercatinib**, Pralsetinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **NTRK fusion** | <1% | **Larotrectinib**, Entrectinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **KRAS G12C** | 13% | **Sotorasib**, Adagrasib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |

## NSCLC - Treatment by Stage
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I-II** | **Surgical resection** (lobectomy); **SBRT** for inoperable; Adjuvant chemotherapy for Stage II, Adjuvant osimertinib (EGFR+), Adjuvant atezolizumab (PD-L1 =1%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage III** | **Chemoradiation** ? **Durvalumab** (consolidation) for unresectable; Neoadjuvant chemoimmunotherapy ? Surgery for resectable | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV (Driver Mutation+)** | **Targeted therapy** (first-line); Chemotherapy if progression | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV (No Driver Mutation)** | **Chemoimmunotherapy** (PD-L1 <50%) or **Immunotherapy alone** (PD-L1 =50%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |

## Small Cell Lung Cancer (SCLC) - Treatment
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Limited Stage** | **Chemotherapy (Cisplatin + Etoposide) + Concurrent radiation** ? **Prophylactic cranial irradiation (PCI)** if response | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/small-cell-lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Extensive Stage** | **Chemoimmunotherapy** (Carboplatin + Etoposide + Atezolizumab or Durvalumab) ? Maintenance immunotherapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/small-cell-lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Relapsed** | **Lurbinectedin**, Topotecan, Re-challenge platinum if platinum-sensitive | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/small-cell-lung-treatment-pdq |

## Immune Checkpoint Inhibitors in NSCLC
| Drug | Target | PD-L1 Threshold | URL |
|------|--------|-----------------|-----|
| **Pembrolizumab** | PD-1 | =1% (combination), =50% (monotherapy) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Nivolumab** | PD-1 | Not required (combination) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Atezolizumab** | PD-L1 | Not required (combination) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |
| **Durvalumab** | PD-L1 | Stage III consolidation (regardless of PD-L1) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp/lung-treatment-pdq |

## Lung Cancer Screening and Prevention
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Screening** | **Annual low-dose CT** for age 50-80, =20 pack-years, Current or quit <15 years | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Smoking Cessation** | **Most effective prevention**; Reduces risk by 50% after 10 years | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/tobacco |
| **Radon Testing** | Second leading cause; Test homes | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/substances/radon |
