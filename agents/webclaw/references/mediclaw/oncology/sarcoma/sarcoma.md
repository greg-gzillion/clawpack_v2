## Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumor (GIST)
| Mutation | Frequency | Sensitivity | URL |
|----------|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **KIT exon 11** | 65% | **Most sensitive to imatinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/gist/hp/gist-treatment-pdq |
| **KIT exon 9** | 10% | Requires higher imatinib dose (800 mg) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/gist/hp/gist-treatment-pdq |
| **PDGFRA exon 18 (D842V)** | 5% | **Resistant to imatinib**; **Avapritinib** sensitive | https://www.cancer.gov/types/gist/hp/gist-treatment-pdq |
| **Wild-Type** | 10-15% | SDH-deficient, NF1, BRAF | https://www.cancer.gov/types/gist/hp/gist-treatment-pdq |
