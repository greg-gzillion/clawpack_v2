# Oncology - Thyroid Cancer

## Thyroid Cancer - Types
| Type | Frequency | Prognosis | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Papillary** | 80-85% | **Excellent** (98% 5-year survival) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Follicular** | 10-15% | Good (95% 5-year survival) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Medullary** | 5% | Intermediate; **Calcitonin** marker; **RET** mutation (MEN2) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/medullary-thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Anaplastic** | <2% | **Poor** (1-year survival 20%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/anaplastic-thyroid-treatment-pdq |

## Differentiated Thyroid Cancer (Papillary/Follicular) - Risk Stratification (ATA)
| Risk Level | Criteria | Management | URL |
|------------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Low Risk** | No local invasion, No nodal mets or =5 micro-metastases, No distant mets | **Lobectomy** or **Total thyroidectomy**; Consider **RAI remnant ablation**; **TSH suppression 0.5-2.0** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Intermediate Risk** | Minor extrathyroidal extension, Central neck mets, Vascular invasion | **Total thyroidectomy + Central neck dissection**; **RAI**; **TSH suppression 0.1-0.5** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **High Risk** | Gross extrathyroidal extension, Incomplete resection, Distant mets | **Total thyroidectomy + Comprehensive neck dissection**; **RAI**; **TSH suppression <0.1** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/thyroid-treatment-pdq |

## Medullary Thyroid Cancer (MTC)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Sporadic** | 75% of MTC | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/medullary-thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Hereditary** | **MEN2A**, **MEN2B**, **Familial MTC**; **RET** mutation | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/medullary-thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Tumor Marker** | **Calcitonin**, **CEA** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/medullary-thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Treatment (Localized)** | **Total thyroidectomy + Central neck dissection** + Lateral neck dissection (if clinically involved) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/medullary-thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Treatment (Metastatic)** | **Selpercatinib**, **Pralsetinib** (RET-mutated), **Vandetanib**, **Cabozantinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/medullary-thyroid-treatment-pdq |

## Anaplastic Thyroid Cancer (ATC)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **Staging** | **Stage IVA** (confined to thyroid), **IVB** (extrathyroidal), **IVC** (metastatic) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/anaplastic-thyroid-treatment-pdq |
| **Treatment (IVA/IVB)** | **Chemoradiation** or **Neoadjuvant therapy** ? Surgery if possible | https://www.cancer.gov/types/thyroid/hp/anaplastic-thyroid-treatment-pdq |
