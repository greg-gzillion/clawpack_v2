| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | 4% of all cancers in US | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck |

| **Subsites** | Oral cavity, Oropharynx, Nasopharynx, Hypopharynx, Larynx | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |

## HNSCC - Treatment by Stage
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I-II** | **Surgery** or **Definitive radiation** (single modality) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage III-IVA (Resectable)** | **Surgery + Adjuvant radiation** ｱ Chemotherapy (if high-risk) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage III-IVB (Unresectable)** | **Definitive chemoradiation** (Cisplatin + Radiation) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IVC (Metastatic)** | **Systemic therapy** (Chemotherapy + Immunotherapy) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |

## Metastatic HNSCC - Systemic Therapy
| PD-L1 CPS | First-Line Therapy | URL |
|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **CPS =1** | **Pembrolizumab + Platinum + 5-FU** or **Pembrolizumab alone** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |
| **CPS <1** | **Platinum + 5-FU + Cetuximab** or **Pembrolizumab + Platinum + 5-FU** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |
| **Second-Line** | **Pembrolizumab**, Nivolumab, Cetuximab, Chemotherapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/head-and-neck/hp/head-neck-treatment-pdq |
