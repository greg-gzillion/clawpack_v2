# Oncology - Cancer of Unknown Primary (CUP)

## CUP - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Metastatic cancer** with **no identifiable primary** after standard evaluation | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Epidemiology** | 2-5% of all cancers | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Favorable Subsets** | 20% of CUP; Treatable with site-specific therapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Unfavorable Subsets** | 80% of CUP; **Empiric chemotherapy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |

## CUP - Diagnostic Evaluation
| Test | Purpose | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| **History and Physical** | Identify clues (smoking, Family history, Occupational) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **CT Chest/Abdomen/Pelvis** | Identify primary site, Disease extent | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **PET-CT** | May identify primary in 25-40% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Biopsy** | **Core needle** preferred; **IHC panel** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Molecular Profiling** | **NGS**, **Gene expression profiling** (tissue of origin) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |

## CUP - Favorable Subsets
| Subset | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Women with Peritoneal Adenocarcinoma** | Treat as **ovarian cancer** (surgery + platinum-based chemotherapy) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Women with Axillary Adenocarcinoma** | Treat as **breast cancer** (surgery + endocrine/chemotherapy ｱ radiation) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Squamous Cell Carcinoma in Cervical Nodes** | Treat as **head and neck cancer** (chemoradiation) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Squamous Cell Carcinoma in Inguinal Nodes** | Treat as **anal/genital cancer** (chemoradiation) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Poorly Differentiated Carcinoma with Midline Distribution** | Treat as **germ cell tumor** (platinum-based chemotherapy) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Neuroendocrine Carcinoma** | Treat as **small cell lung cancer** or **well-differentiated NET** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Men with Blastic Bone Metastases + Elevated PSA** | Treat as **prostate cancer** (ADT ｱ chemotherapy) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |

## CUP - Unfavorable Subsets
| Subset | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Adenocarcinoma (Non-Specific)** | **Empiric chemotherapy** (platinum + taxane or gemcitabine) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Molecularly-Guided Therapy** | **Targeted therapy** based on NGS findings (if actionable) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
| **Immunotherapy** | **Pembrolizumab** for MSI-H, TMB-H, or PD-L1+ | https://www.cancer.gov/types/unknown-primary/hp/unknown-primary-treatment-pdq |
