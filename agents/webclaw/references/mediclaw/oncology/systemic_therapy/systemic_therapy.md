# Oncology - Systemic Therapy

## Chemotherapy - Classes
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Toxicities | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Alkylating Agents** | Cyclophosphamide, Ifosfamide, Cisplatin, Carboplatin | **DNA crosslinking** | **Myelosuppression**, **Hemorrhagic cystitis** (cyclophosphamide/ifosfamide - give Mesna), **Nephrotoxicity** (cisplatin) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/chemotherapy |
| **Antimetabolites** | 5-FU, Capecitabine, Gemcitabine, Methotrexate | Inhibit nucleotide synthesis | **Myelosuppression**, **Mucositis**, **Hand-foot syndrome** (capecitabine) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/chemotherapy |
| **Anthracyclines** | Doxorubicin, Daunorubicin, Epirubicin | **Topoisomerase II inhibition**, DNA intercalation | **Cardiomyopathy** (cumulative dose), **Myelosuppression**, **Alopecia** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/chemotherapy |
| **Taxanes** | Paclitaxel, Docetaxel, Cabazitaxel | **Microtubule stabilization** | **Peripheral neuropathy**, **Myelosuppression**, **Hypersensitivity** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/chemotherapy |
| **Vinca Alkaloids** | Vincristine, Vinblastine, Vinorelbine | **Microtubule destabilization** | **Peripheral neuropathy** (vincristine), **Myelosuppression** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/chemotherapy |
| **Topoisomerase I Inhibitors** | Irinotecan, Topotecan | Inhibit topoisomerase I | **Diarrhea** (irinotecan), **Myelosuppression** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/chemotherapy |
| **Bleomycin** | Bleomycin | DNA strand breaks | **Pulmonary fibrosis**, **Skin toxicity** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/chemotherapy |

## Chemotherapy - Key Toxicities and Management
| Toxicity | Management | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Chemotherapy-Induced Nausea/Vomiting (CINV)** | **NK1 antagonist** (aprepitant), **5-HT3 antagonist** (ondansetron), **Dexamethasone**, **Olanzapine** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/nausea |
| **Febrile Neutropenia** | **G-CSF** prophylaxis for high-risk regimens | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/neutropenia |
| **Mucositis** | **Oral care**, **Palifermin** (HSCT), **Pain management** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/mouth-throat |
| **Peripheral Neuropathy** | **Dose reduction**, **Duloxetine**, **Gabapentinoids** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/nerve-problems |
| **Hand-Foot Syndrome** | **Urea cream**, **Avoid heat/friction**, **Dose reduction** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/skin-nail-changes |
