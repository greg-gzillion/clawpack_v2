# Oncology - Melanoma and Skin Cancer

## Melanoma - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | 5th most common cancer; Incidence rising | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin |
| **Risk Factors** | **UV exposure**, Fair skin, Dysplastic nevi, Family history, **CDKN2A**, **CDK4**, **MC1R** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-prevention-pdq |
| **Types** | **Superficial spreading** (70%), **Nodular** (15%), **Lentigo maligna**, **Acral lentiginous** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |

## Melanoma - Staging and Prognosis
| Stage | 5-Year Survival | URL |
|-------|----------------|-----|
| **Stage I** (=2 mm, No ulceration) | 98-99% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage II** (>2 mm or ulceration) | 80-94% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage III** (Nodal involvement) | 40-77% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV** (Distant metastasis) | 10-35% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |

## Melanoma - Treatment by Stage
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I-II** | **Wide local excision**; **Sentinel lymph node biopsy** for =T1b | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage III** | **Wide local excision + Completion lymphadenectomy** (or observation for SLN+); **Adjuvant immunotherapy** (pembrolizumab, Nivolumab) or **Targeted therapy** (dabrafenib + trametinib for BRAF+) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Stage IV** | **Immunotherapy** (pembrolizumab, Nivolumab, Nivolumab + Ipilimumab) or **Targeted therapy** (BRAF/MEK inhibitors for BRAF+) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |

## Melanoma - Molecular Drivers and Targeted Therapy
| Alteration | Frequency | Targeted Therapy | URL |
|------------|-----------|------------------|-----|
| **BRAF V600E/K** | 40-50% | **Dabrafenib + Trametinib**, Vemurafenib + Cobimetinib, Encorafenib + Binimetinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **NRAS** | 15-20% | MEK inhibitors (limited efficacy) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **c-KIT** | <5% (acral, Mucosal) | Imatinib, Nilotinib | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |

## Non-Melanoma Skin Cancer

|------|-----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)** | 80% | **UV exposure**, Fair skin, Immunosuppression | **Surgical excision**, Mohs surgery, Topical therapy, Radiation, **Hedgehog inhibitors** (advanced) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/basal-cell-treatment-pdq |
