# Oncology - Radiation Oncology

## Radiation Therapy - Types
| Type | Description | Indications | URL |
|------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **External Beam Radiation (EBRT)** | Radiation delivered from outside body | Most solid tumors | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |
| **Intensity-Modulated Radiation (IMRT)** | Modulated beam intensity to conform to tumor shape | Head/neck, Prostate, CNS | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |
| **Stereotactic Radiosurgery (SRS)** | High-dose, Precise radiation (1-5 fractions) | Brain mets, Early NSCLC, Spine mets | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |
| **Stereotactic Body Radiation (SBRT)** | Same as SRS for body | Early NSCLC, Liver mets, Spine mets | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |
| **Brachytherapy** | Radioactive source placed inside body | Cervical, Endometrial, Prostate, Breast | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |
| **Proton Therapy** | Charged particle radiation; **Bragg peak** | Pediatric, Skull base, Ocular | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |

## Radiation Therapy - Fractionation
| Fractionation | Dose per Fraction | Total Dose | Indications | URL |
|---------------|-------------------|------------|-------------|-----|
| **Conventional** | 1.8-2.0 Gy | 50-70 Gy | Most curative-intent EBRT | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |
| **Hypofractionation** | >2.5 Gy | Lower total dose | Breast, Prostate, Palliation | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |
| **SBRT/SRS** | 6-30 Gy | 1-5 fractions | Early NSCLC, Brain mets, Spine mets | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/types/radiation-therapy |

## Radiation Therapy - Toxicity
| Timing | Toxicity | Management | URL |
|--------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Acute (Days-Weeks)** | **Fatigue**, **Skin reaction** (erythema, Desquamation), **Mucositis**, **Esophagitis**, **Diarrhea** | Supportive care, Topical agents, Pain management | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/radiation |
| **Late (Months-Years)** | **Fibrosis**, **Lymphedema**, **Secondary malignancy**, **Cognitive decline** (brain), **Cardiac toxicity** (chest), **Pneumonitis** (lung) | Surveillance, Symptom management | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/radiation |
