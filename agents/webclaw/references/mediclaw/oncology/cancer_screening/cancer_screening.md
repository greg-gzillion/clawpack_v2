## Lung Cancer Screening

## Cervical Cancer Screening
| Age | USPSTF Recommendation | URL |
|-----|----------------------|-----|
| **<21** | **Do NOT screen** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/cervical-cancer-screening |
| **21-29** | **Pap q3 years** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/cervical-cancer-screening |
| **30-65** | **HPV primary q5 years** (preferred) OR **HPV/Pap co-test q5 years** OR **Pap q3 years** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/cervical-cancer-screening |
| **>65** | **Stop** if adequate negative screening and no CIN2+ in past 25 years | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/cervical-cancer-screening |
| **Post-Hysterectomy** | **Stop** if total hysterectomy for benign disease and no CIN2+ | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/cervical-cancer-screening |
