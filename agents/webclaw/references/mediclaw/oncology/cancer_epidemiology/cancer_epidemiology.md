## Cancer Disparities
| Population | Disparity | URL |
|------------|-----------|-----|
| **African Americans** | Highest overall cancer mortality; Higher rates of prostate, Breast (triple-negative), Colorectal | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/understanding/disparities |
| **Hispanic/Latino** | Higher rates of liver, Stomach, Cervical; Lower screening rates | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/understanding/disparities |
| **American Indian/Alaska Native** | Higher rates of lung, Colorectal, Kidney, Liver | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/understanding/disparities |
| **Rural Populations** | Higher cancer mortality; Lower screening rates; Limited access to care | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/understanding/disparities |
| **LGBTQ+** | Higher rates of anal, Lung, Cervical; Lower screening rates | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/understanding/disparities |
