## Neuroblastoma
| Risk Group | Features | Treatment | URL |
|------------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Low Risk** | Localized, Favorable histology, No MYCN amplification | **Observation** or **Surgery alone** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/neuroblastoma/hp/neuroblastoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Intermediate Risk** | Localized, Unfavorable histology, No MYCN amplification | **Chemotherapy + Surgery** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/neuroblastoma/hp/neuroblastoma-treatment-pdq |
| **High Risk** | Metastatic (>18 months), **MYCN amplified**, Unfavorable histology | **Induction chemotherapy** ? Surgery ? **Consolidation** (high-dose chemotherapy + Auto-HSCT) ? **Radiation** ? **Maintenance** (Dinutuximab + Cytokines + Isotretinoin) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/neuroblastoma/hp/neuroblastoma-treatment-pdq |
