## Hodgkin Lymphoma - Treatment
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Early Stage Favorable** | **ABVD x 2 + ISRT** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-hodgkin-treatment-pdq |
| **Early Stage Unfavorable** | **ABVD x 4 + ISRT** or **ABVD x 2 + AVD x 4** (PET-2 negative) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-hodgkin-treatment-pdq |
| **Advanced Stage** | **ABVD x 6** or **A+AVD x 6** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-hodgkin-treatment-pdq |
| **Relapsed/Refractory** | Salvage chemotherapy ? **High-dose chemotherapy + Auto-HSCT**; **Brentuximab vedotin**, **Checkpoint inhibitors** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-hodgkin-treatment-pdq |

## Diffuse Large B-Cell Lymphoma (DLBCL)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | Most common NHL (30-40%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |
| **Cell of Origin** | **GCB** (better), **ABC** (worse) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |
| **Double-Hit Lymphoma** | **MYC + BCL2 and/or BCL6**; **DA-EPOCH-R** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |

## DLBCL - Treatment
| Setting | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| **First-Line** | **R-CHOP** x 6 cycles | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |
| **Relapsed/Refractory** | Salvage chemotherapy ? **Auto-HSCT**; **CAR-T** (Axi-cel, Liso-cel), **Bispecific antibodies** (Epcoritamab, Glofitamab) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |

## Follicular Lymphoma (FL)
| Tumor Burden | Treatment | URL |
|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Low** | **Observation** or **Rituximab** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |
| **High** | **Bendamustine + Rituximab (BR)** or **R-CHOP** or **Obinutuzumab + Chemotherapy** ? Rituximab maintenance | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |

## Mantle Cell Lymphoma (MCL)
| Setting | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| **First-Line (Transplant-Eligible)** | **R-DHAP alternating R-CHOP** ? **Auto-HSCT** ? Rituximab maintenance | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/adult-nhl-treatment-pdq |
