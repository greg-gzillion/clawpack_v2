# Oncology - Prostate Cancer

## Prostate Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | **Most common cancer in men** (excluding skin); 1 in 8 lifetime risk | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate |
| **Risk Factors** | Age (>65), **African American**, Family history, **BRCA1/2**, **Lynch syndrome** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-prevention-pdq |
| **Screening** | **PSA** + **DRE**; Shared decision-making starting age 50 (45 for high-risk, 40 for highest-risk) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-screening-pdq |

## Prostate Cancer - Risk Stratification (NCCN)
| Risk Group | Criteria | URL |
|------------|----------|-----|
| **Very Low** | T1c, Grade Group 1, PSA <10, <3 cores positive, =50% in any core, PSA density <0.15 | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Low** | T1-T2a, Grade Group 1, PSA <10 | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Favorable Intermediate** | T2b-T2c OR Grade Group 2 OR PSA 10-20; AND <50% cores positive | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Unfavorable Intermediate** | Grade Group 3 OR =50% cores positive | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **High** | T3a OR Grade Group 4-5 OR PSA >20 | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Very High** | T3b-T4 OR Primary Gleason pattern 5 | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Metastatic** | N1 or M1 | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |

## Localized Prostate Cancer - Treatment Options
| Risk Group | Treatment Options | URL |
|------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Very Low/Low** | **Active Surveillance** (preferred), Radical prostatectomy, Radiation therapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Favorable Intermediate** | Active Surveillance (select), Radical prostatectomy, Radiation therapy + ADT (4-6 months) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Unfavorable Intermediate** | Radical prostatectomy + PLND, Radiation therapy + ADT (6 months) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **High/Very High** | Radical prostatectomy + PLND, Radiation therapy + ADT (18-36 months) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |

## Metastatic Prostate Cancer - Systemic Therapy
| Setting | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| **Metastatic Hormone-Sensitive (mHSPC)** | **ADT + ARPI** (abiraterone, Enzalutamide, Apalutamide) or **ADT + Docetaxel** or **Triplet** (ADT + Docetaxel + ARPI) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |
| **Metastatic Castration-Resistant (mCRPC)** | **ARPI** (abiraterone, Enzalutamide), **Docetaxel**, **Cabazitaxel**, **Sipuleucel-T**, **Radium-223** (bone-only), **PARP inhibitors** (BRCA/HRR-mutated), **Lutetium-177-PSMA-617** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/hp/prostate-treatment-pdq |

## Prostate Cancer - Key Trials
| Trial | Population | Intervention | Outcome | URL |
|-------|------------|--------------|---------|-----|
| **PROTECT** | Localized | Active Monitoring vs. Surgery vs. Radiation | No difference in 15-year mortality | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/research/protect-trial |
| **STAMPEDE** | mHSPC | ADT + Docetaxel or Abiraterone | Improved OS | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/research/stampede-trial |
| **CHAARTED** | mHSPC | ADT + Docetaxel | Improved OS in high-volume disease | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/research/chaarted-trial |
| **VISION** | mCRPC (post-ARPI and taxane) | Lu-177-PSMA-617 | Improved OS and rPFS | https://www.cancer.gov/types/prostate/research/vision-trial |
