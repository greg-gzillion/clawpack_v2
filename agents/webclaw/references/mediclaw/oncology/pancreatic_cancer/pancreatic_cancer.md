# Oncology - Pancreatic Cancer

## Pancreatic Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | 4th leading cause of cancer death; **5-year survival 13%** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/pancreatic |
| **Risk Factors** | Age >60, Smoking, Obesity, Diabetes, Chronic pancreatitis, Family history, **BRCA2**, **PALB2**, **ATM**, **Lynch syndrome**, **Peutz-Jeghers**, **FAMMM** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/pancreatic/hp/pancreatic-prevention-pdq |
| **Types** | **Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma (PDAC)** - 90%; Neuroendocrine tumors (PNETs) - 5-10% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/pancreatic/hp/pancreatic-treatment-pdq |

## Pancreatic Neuroendocrine Tumors (PNETs)
| Type | Grade | Ki-67 | Mitotic Rate | Treatment | URL |
|------|-------|-------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Well-Differentiated NET (Grade 1)** | Low | <3% | <2/10 HPF | **Observation** (small, Non-functional), **Surgery** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/pancreatic/hp/gastroenteropancreatic-neuroendocrine-treatment-pdq |
| **Well-Differentiated NET (Grade 2)** | Intermediate | 3-20% | 2-20/10 HPF | Surgery; **Somatostatin analogs** (octreotide, Lanreotide), **Everolimus**, **Sunitinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/pancreatic/hp/gastroenteropancreatic-neuroendocrine-treatment-pdq |
| **Well-Differentiated NET (Grade 3)** | High | >20% | >20/10 HPF | Chemotherapy (CAPTEM, FOLFOX), PRRT (Lu-177-DOTATATE) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/pancreatic/hp/gastroenteropancreatic-neuroendocrine-treatment-pdq |
| **Poorly Differentiated NEC** | High | >20% | >20/10 HPF | **Chemotherapy** (Cisplatin/Carboplatin + Etoposide) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/pancreatic/hp/gastroenteropancreatic-neuroendocrine-treatment-pdq |
