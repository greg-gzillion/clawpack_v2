# Psychiatry - Somatic Symptom and Related Disorders

## Somatic Symptom Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **=1 somatic symptom** that is **distressing or disruptive** + **Excessive thoughts, Feelings, or behaviors** related to the symptom (=1 of: Disproportionate thoughts, Persistently high anxiety, Excessive time/energy) | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/somatic-symptom-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **CBT**, **Regular scheduled visits** with single provider, **Avoid unnecessary testing**, **Treat comorbid anxiety/depression** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/somatic-symptom-disorder |

## Illness Anxiety Disorder (Hypochondriasis)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Preoccupation with having or acquiring serious illness**; **Somatic symptoms absent or mild**; **High health anxiety**, **Excessive health-related behaviors or maladaptive avoidance** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/illness-anxiety-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **CBT**, **SSRIs** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/illness-anxiety-disorder |

## Conversion Disorder (Functional Neurologic Symptom Disorder)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **=1 symptom of altered voluntary motor or sensory function**; **Incompatibility between symptom and recognized neurologic/medical conditions**; **Distress or impairment** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/functional-neurologic-disorder |
| **Common Symptoms** | **Weakness/Paralysis**, **Abnormal movements** (tremor, Gait), **Swallowing symptoms**, **Speech symptoms**, **Seizures** (psychogenic non-epileptic seizures), **Sensory loss**, **Visual symptoms** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/functional-neurologic-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **Education**, **CBT**, **Physical therapy**, **Treat comorbid psychiatric disorders** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/functional-neurologic-disorder |

## Factitious Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Falsification of physical or psychological signs/symptoms** or **induction of injury/disease**; **Deceptive behavior evident even in absence of external rewards** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/factitious-disorder |
| **Factitious Disorder Imposed on Another** | **Falsification of signs/symptoms in another person** (usually child - Munchausen by proxy) | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/factitious-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **Non-confrontational approach**, **Psychotherapy**, **Treat underlying psychiatric disorders** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/factitious-disorder |
