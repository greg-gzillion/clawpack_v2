# Psychiatry - Child and Adolescent Psychiatry

## Pediatric Mood and Anxiety Disorders
| Disorder | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Pediatric Depression** | **Irritability** (often more prominent than sadness), **Somatic complaints**, **Social withdrawal**, **Academic decline** | **CBT**, **IPT**, **SSRIs** (fluoxetine, Escitalopram) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/child-and-adolescent-mental-health |
| **Pediatric Anxiety** | **Excessive worry**, **Separation anxiety**, **School refusal**, **Somatic complaints** | **CBT**, **SSRIs** (sertraline, Fluoxetine) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/child-and-adolescent-mental-health |
| **Pediatric Bipolar Disorder** | **Chronic irritability**, **Mixed states**, **Rapid cycling** | **Atypical antipsychotics**, **Mood stabilizers**, **Family therapy** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/child-and-adolescent-mental-health |

## Disruptive, Impulse-Control, and Conduct Disorders
| Disorder | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD)** | **Angry/irritable mood**, **Argumentative/defiant behavior**, **Vindictiveness** | **Parent Management Training (PMT)** , **CBT**, **Treat comorbid ADHD** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/disruptive-behavior-disorders |
| **Conduct Disorder** | **Aggression to people/animals**, **Destruction of property**, **Deceitfulness/Theft**, **Serious rule violations** | **Multisystemic Therapy (MST)** , **PMT**, **Treat comorbid ADHD**, **Mood stabilizers/Antipsychotics** for aggression | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/disruptive-behavior-disorders |
