# Psychiatry - Personality Disorders

## Personality Disorders - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **General Criteria** | **Enduring pattern** of inner experience and behavior that deviates from cultural expectations, Is **pervasive and inflexible**, Has **onset in adolescence/early adulthood**, Leads to **distress or impairment** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |
| **Clusters** | **Cluster A** (odd/eccentric), **Cluster B** (dramatic/emotional/erratic), **Cluster C** (anxious/fearful) | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |

## Cluster A (Odd/Eccentric)
| Disorder | Key Features | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Paranoid** | **Pervasive distrust and suspiciousness**; Interprets others' motives as malevolent | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |
| **Schizoid** | **Detachment from social relationships**; **Restricted range of emotional expression** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |
| **Schizotypal** | **Acute discomfort with close relationships**, **Cognitive/perceptual distortions**, **Eccentricities of behavior** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |

## Cluster B (Dramatic/Emotional/Erratic)
| Disorder | Key Features | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Antisocial** | **Disregard for and violation of rights of others**; Conduct disorder before age 15 | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |
| **Borderline** | **Instability of interpersonal relationships, Self-image, and affects**; **Marked impulsivity**; **Chronic emptiness**, **Frantic efforts to avoid abandonment**, **Self-harm**, **Identity disturbance** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/borderline-personality-disorder |
| **Histrionic** | **Excessive emotionality and attention-seeking**; **Uncomfortable when not center of attention** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |
| **Narcissistic** | **Grandiosity**, **Need for admiration**, **Lack of empathy** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |

## Cluster C (Anxious/Fearful)
| Disorder | Key Features | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Avoidant** | **Social inhibition**, **Feelings of inadequacy**, **Hypersensitivity to negative evaluation** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |
| **Dependent** | **Excessive need to be taken care of** leading to **submissive and clinging behavior**, **Fear of separation** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |
| **Obsessive-Compulsive** | **Preoccupation with orderliness**, **Perfectionism**, **Mental and interpersonal control** at expense of flexibility and efficiency | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/personality-disorders |

## Borderline Personality Disorder - Treatment
| Modality | Options | URL |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Psychotherapy (First-Line)** | **Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)** (gold standard), **Mentalization-Based Treatment (MBT)** , **Transference-Focused Psychotherapy (TFP)** , **Schema Therapy** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/borderline-personality-disorder |
| **Pharmacotherapy** | **Target symptoms**: Mood lability (mood stabilizers), Impulsivity (SSRIs, Mood stabilizers), Transient psychosis (low-dose antipsychotics) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/borderline-personality-disorder |
