# Psychiatry - Anxiety Disorders

## Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Excessive anxiety and worry** occurring more days than not for **=6 months** + =3 of: Restlessness, Fatigue, Difficulty concentrating, Irritability, Muscle tension, Sleep disturbance | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (first-line), **SSRIs** (escitalopram, Paroxetine, Sertraline), **SNRIs** (venlafaxine, Duloxetine), **Buspirone**, **Pregabalin** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |

## Panic Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Recurrent unexpected panic attacks** + **=1 month of persistent concern about additional attacks** or **maladaptive behavioral changes** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/panic-disorder |
| **Panic Attack Symptoms** | **=4 of**: Palpitations, Sweating, Trembling, Shortness of breath, Choking, Chest pain, Nausea, Dizziness, Chills/heat, Paresthesias, Derealization/Depersonalization, Fear of losing control/dying | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/panic-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (exposure, Cognitive restructuring), **SSRIs** (first-line), **SNRIs**, **TCAs**, **Benzodiazepines** (short-term, PRN) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/panic-disorder |

## Social Anxiety Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Marked fear/anxiety about social situations** where scrutiny possible; Fear of negative evaluation; **Avoidance or endured with intense distress**; Duration =6 months | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/social-anxiety-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (exposure, Social skills training), **SSRIs** (sertraline, Paroxetine), **SNRIs** (venlafaxine), **Beta-blockers** (performance anxiety) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/social-anxiety-disorder |

## Specific Phobia
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Marked fear/anxiety about specific object/situation**; **Immediate fear response**; **Avoidance or endured with intense distress**; Duration =6 months | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/specific-phobias |
| **Treatment** | **Exposure therapy** (first-line), **CBT** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/specific-phobias |

## Agoraphobia
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Marked fear/anxiety about =2 of**: Public transportation, Open spaces, Enclosed spaces, Crowds/lines, Being outside home alone; **Avoidance** due to fear of escape being difficult | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (exposure), **SSRIs**, **SNRIs** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |

## Separation Anxiety Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Developmentally inappropriate excessive fear/anxiety concerning separation from attachment figures**; Duration =4 weeks (children) or =6 months (adults) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (parent-child interaction therapy), **SSRIs** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |
