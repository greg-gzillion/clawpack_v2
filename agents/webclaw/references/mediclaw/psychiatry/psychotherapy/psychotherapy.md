# Psychiatry - Psychotherapy

## Major Psychotherapy Modalities
| Modality | Description | Indications | URL |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** | **Identify and modify maladaptive thoughts and behaviors**; Time-limited (12-20 sessions), Structured, Goal-oriented | **Depression**, **Anxiety**, **PTSD**, **OCD**, **Eating disorders**, **Insomnia** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)** | **CBT + Mindfulness + Distress tolerance + Emotion regulation + Interpersonal effectiveness**; Individual therapy + Group skills training + Phone coaching | **Borderline personality disorder**, **Chronic suicidality**, **Self-harm**, **Eating disorders** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Interpersonal Therapy (IPT)** | **Focus on interpersonal relationships and role transitions**; Time-limited (12-16 sessions) | **Depression**, **Eating disorders**, **Peripartum depression** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Psychodynamic Psychotherapy** | **Explore unconscious conflicts and early life experiences**; Can be brief or long-term | **Depression**, **Anxiety**, **Personality disorders**, **Relationship problems** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)** | **Accept difficult thoughts/feelings, Commit to values-based action** | **Chronic pain**, **Depression**, **Anxiety**, **PTSD** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Motivational Interviewing (MI)** | **Enhance intrinsic motivation for change**; Collaborative, Evocative, Respects autonomy | **Substance use disorders**, **Medication adherence**, **Health behavior change** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Family Therapy** | **Engage family members in treatment** | **Child/adolescent disorders**, **Eating disorders**, **Schizophrenia**, **Bipolar disorder** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Group Therapy** | **Therapeutic interactions among multiple patients** | **Substance use disorders**, **PTSD**, **Eating disorders**, **Social skills training** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
