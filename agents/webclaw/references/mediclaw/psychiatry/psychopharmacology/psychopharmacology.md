# Psychiatry - Psychopharmacology

## Antidepressants
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **SSRIs** | Sertraline, Escitalopram, Fluoxetine, Paroxetine, Citalopram, Fluvoxamine | **Inhibit serotonin reuptake** | **GI upset**, **Sexual dysfunction**, **Insomnia/Activation**, **Hyponatremia** (elderly), **QTc prolongation** (citalopram >40 mg) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **SNRIs** | Venlafaxine, Duloxetine, Desvenlafaxine, Levomilnacipran | **Inhibit serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake** | **Hypertension** (venlafaxine >150 mg), **Nausea**, **Sexual dysfunction** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Bupropion** | Bupropion (NDRI) | **Inhibits norepinephrine and dopamine reuptake** | **Seizures** (dose-dependent), **Insomnia**, **Anxiety**, **No sexual dysfunction** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Mirtazapine** | Mirtazapine (NaSSA) | **Antagonist at a2, 5-HT2, 5-HT3, H1** | **Sedation**, **Weight gain**, **Increased appetite**, **No sexual dysfunction** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Trazodone** | Trazodone (SARI) | **5-HT2 antagonist, Weak SRI** | **Sedation**, **Priapism** (rare), **Orthostasis** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **TCAs** | Amitriptyline, Nortriptyline, Clomipramine, Imipramine | **Inhibit serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake** | **Anticholinergic** (dry mouth, Constipation, Blurred vision, Urinary retention), **Orthostasis**, **QTc prolongation**, **Weight gain**, **Lethal in overdose** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **MAOIs** | Phenelzine, Tranylcypromine, Selegiline (transdermal), Isocarboxazid | **Inhibit monoamine oxidase** | **Hypertensive crisis** with tyramine, **Serotonin syndrome**, **Orthostasis**, **Dietary restrictions** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
