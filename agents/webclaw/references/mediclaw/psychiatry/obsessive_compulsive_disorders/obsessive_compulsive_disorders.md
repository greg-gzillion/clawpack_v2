# Psychiatry - Obsessive-Compulsive and Related Disorders

## Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Obsessions** (recurrent intrusive thoughts/urges) and/or **Compulsions** (repetitive behaviors/mental acts) that are **time-consuming** (>1 hour/day) or cause **distress/impairment** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Common Obsessions** | **Contamination**, **Symmetry/Order**, **Harm** (to self or others), **Religious/Moral** (scrupulosity), **Sexual**, **Somatic** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Common Compulsions** | **Washing/Cleaning**, **Checking**, **Counting**, **Ordering/Arranging**, **Repeating**, **Mental rituals**, **Reassurance-seeking** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |

## OCD - Treatment
| Modality | Options | URL |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Psychotherapy (First-Line)** | **Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP)** , **CBT** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Pharmacotherapy** | **SSRIs** (high-dose: fluoxetine 40-80 mg, Fluvoxamine 200-300 mg, Sertraline 200 mg, Paroxetine 40-60 mg), **Clomipramine** (TCA) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Augmentation** | **Atypical antipsychotics** (risperidone, Aripiprazole), **Memantine**, **NAC** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/obsessive-compulsive-disorder-ocd |
| **Neuromodulation** | **Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS)** for severe, Treatment-refractory | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/brain-stimulation-therapies |

## Body Dysmorphic Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Preoccupation with perceived defect or flaw in physical appearance**; **Repetitive behaviors** (mirror checking, Grooming, Comparing) or **mental acts** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/body-dysmorphic-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (ERP, Cognitive restructuring), **SSRIs** (high-dose) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/body-dysmorphic-disorder |

## Hoarding Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Persistent difficulty discarding possessions** regardless of value; **Accumulation of possessions** that congest living areas; **Distress or impairment** | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/hoarding-disorder |
| **Treatment** | **CBT** (specialized for hoarding), **SSRIs** (modest benefit) | https://www.psychiatry.org/patients-families/hoarding-disorder |

## Trichotillomania (Hair-Pulling Disorder)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Recurrent pulling out of one's hair** resulting in **hair loss**; **Repeated attempts to decrease or stop** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/trichotillomania |
| **Treatment** | **Habit Reversal Training (HRT)** , **CBT**, **NAC**, **Clomipramine**, **SSRIs** (limited evidence) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/trichotillomania |

## Excoriation (Skin-Picking) Disorder
