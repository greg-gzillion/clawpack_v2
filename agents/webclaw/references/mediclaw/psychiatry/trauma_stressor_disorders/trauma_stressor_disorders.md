# Psychiatry - Trauma and Stressor-Related Disorders

## Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Exposure to actual or threatened death, Serious injury, or sexual violence** + **=1 month** of: **Intrusion** (=1), **Avoidance** (=1), **Negative alterations in cognition/mood** (=2), **Alterations in arousal/reactivity** (=2) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Intrusion Symptoms** | **Recurrent, Involuntary distressing memories**, **Nightmares**, **Flashbacks**, **Intense distress at reminders**, **Physiologic reactivity** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Avoidance** | Avoidance of **memories/thoughts/feelings** or **external reminders** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Negative Alterations** | **Amnesia** for trauma, **Negative beliefs**, **Distorted blame**, **Negative emotions**, **Diminished interest**, **Detachment**, **Inability to experience positive emotions** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Arousal/Reactivity** | **Irritability/Anger**, **Reckless behavior**, **Hypervigilance**, **Exaggerated startle**, **Concentration problems**, **Sleep disturbance** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |

## PTSD - Treatment
| Modality | Options | URL |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Psychotherapy (First-Line)** | **Trauma-focused CBT**, **Prolonged Exposure (PE)** , **Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT)** , **Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Pharmacotherapy** | **SSRIs** (sertraline, Paroxetine, Fluoxetine), **SNRIs** (venlafaxine), **Prazosin** (nightmares) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |

## Acute Stress Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | Similar to PTSD but **duration 3 days - 1 month**; **=9 symptoms** across intrusion, Negative mood, Dissociative, Avoidance, Arousal | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |
| **Treatment** | **Trauma-focused CBT**, **Psychological first aid** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder-ptsd |

## Adjustment Disorder
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Emotional or behavioral symptoms** in response to **identifiable stressor** within **3 months**; **Clinically significant distress or impairment**; **Does not meet criteria for another disorder** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/adjustment-disorders |
| **Treatment** | **Supportive psychotherapy**, **CBT**, **Brief pharmacotherapy** for symptoms | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/adjustment-disorders |
