## Neuromodulation Therapies
| Therapy | Mechanism | Indications | URL |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)** | **Magnetic pulses** to dorsolateral prefrontal cortex | **Treatment-resistant depression**, **OCD**, **Smoking cessation** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/brain-stimulation-therapies |
| **Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS)** | **Electrical stimulation** of left vagus nerve | **Treatment-resistant depression** (chronic) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/brain-stimulation-therapies |
| **Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS)** | **Implanted electrodes** in specific brain regions | **Severe, Treatment-refractory OCD**, **Essential tremor**, **Parkinson disease** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/brain-stimulation-therapies |
| **Ketamine/Esketamine** | **NMDA antagonist** | **Treatment-resistant depression**, **Acute suicidality** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/brain-stimulation-therapies |
