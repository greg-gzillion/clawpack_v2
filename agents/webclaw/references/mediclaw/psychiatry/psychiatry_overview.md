# Psychiatry - Overview

## What is Psychiatry?
Psychiatry is the medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders. It encompasses biological, psychological, and social approaches to patient care (the biopsychosocial model).
