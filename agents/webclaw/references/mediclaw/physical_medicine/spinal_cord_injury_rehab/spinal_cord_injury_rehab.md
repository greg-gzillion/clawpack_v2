## Neurologic Level and Functional Outcomes
| Level | Key Muscle | Functional Outcome | URL |
|-------|------------|-------------------|-----|
| **C1-C4** | **Diaphragm** (C3-5) | **Ventilator dependent** (complete above C3), **Power wheelchair** with chin/head control | https://www.nscia.org/rehabilitation |
| **C5** | **Deltoid**, **Biceps** | **Elbow flexion**, Power wheelchair with hand controls, **Requires assistance for ADLs** | https://www.nscia.org/rehabilitation |
| **C6** | **Wrist extension** | **Tenodesis grasp**, Manual wheelchair, **Independent feeding/grooming** | https://www.nscia.org/rehabilitation |
| **C7** | **Triceps**, **Wrist flexion**, **Finger extension** | **Transfers**, **Independent ADLs**, Manual wheelchair | https://www.nscia.org/rehabilitation |
| **C8** | **Finger flexion** | Improved hand function | https://www.nscia.org/rehabilitation |
| **T1-T12** | Intercostals, Abdominals | **Independent with manual wheelchair**, Standing frame, Brace walking (T10-L2) | https://www.nscia.org/rehabilitation |
| **L1-L5** | Hip flexors, Knee extensors, Ankle dorsiflexion | **Community ambulation** with braces/crutches | https://www.nscia.org/rehabilitation |

## SCI Complications and Management
| Complication | Prevention/Management | URL |
|--------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Pressure Injury** | **Pressure relief q15-30min**, **Specialized mattress/cushion**, **Skin inspection**, **Nutrition** | https://www.nscia.org/pressure-injuries |
| **Autonomic Dysreflexia** | **Sit upright**, **Remove trigger** (bladder, bowel), **Antihypertensives** (nifedipine, nitroglycerin paste) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/autonomic-dysreflexia |
| **Orthostatic Hypotension** | **Compression stockings**, **Abdominal binder**, **Slow position changes**, **Midodrine**, **Fludrocortisone** | https://www.nscia.org/orthostatic-hypotension |
| **Spasticity** | **Stretching**, **Oral medications** (baclofen, tizanidine), **Botulinum toxin**, **Intrathecal baclofen** | https://www.aapmr.org/about-physiatry/conditions-treatments/rehabilitation/spinal-cord-injury |
| **Heterotopic Ossification (HO)** | **Early ROM**, **Etidronate**, **NSAIDs**, **Surgical excision** | https://www.nscia.org/heterotopic-ossification |
| **Osteoporosis** | **Weight-bearing** (standing frame), **Vitamin D/Calcium**, **Bisphosphonates** | https://www.nscia.org/osteoporosis |
| **Neuropathic Pain** | **Gabapentinoids**, **SNRIs**, **TCAs**, **Topicals** | https://www.nscia.org/pain-management |
