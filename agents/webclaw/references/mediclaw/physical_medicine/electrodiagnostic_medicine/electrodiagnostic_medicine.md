## Nerve Conduction Studies (NCS)
| Parameter | Definition | Interpretation | URL |
|-----------|------------|----------------|-----|
| **Distal Latency** | **Time from stimulus to response** | **?** = Demyelination or distal conduction block | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Amplitude** | **Magnitude of response** | **?** = **Axonal loss** or conduction block | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |

## Electromyography (EMG)
| Finding | Description | Interpretation | URL |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| **Insertional Activity** | **Electrical activity** with needle insertion | **?** = Denervation, Myopathy; **?** = Fibrosis, Atrophy | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Spontaneous Activity** | **Activity at rest** | **Fibrillations**, **Positive sharp waves** = **Denervation**; **Fasciculations** = Motor neuron disease; **Myotonic discharges** = Myotonic dystrophy | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Motor Unit Action Potentials (MUAPs)** | **Waveform of motor unit** | **Large, polyphasic** = **Neurogenic** (reinnervation); **Small, polyphasic** = **Myopathic** | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Recruitment** | **Number of MUAPs firing** | **?** = Neurogenic; **Early/rapid** = Myopathic | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |

| **Polyneuropathy** | **? Amplitudes** (axonal) or **? Velocities** (demyelinating) | **Denervation** in **distal symmetric** distribution | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Myopathy** | **Normal** (unless severe) | **Small, polyphasic MUAPs**, **Early recruitment** | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Motor Neuron Disease (ALS)** | **Normal sensory**, **? Motor amplitudes** | **Active + Chronic denervation** in **=3 regions** | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
