## Phases of Return to Sport
| Phase | Goal | Activities | URL |
|-------|------|------------|-----|
| **1. Acute** | **Protect**, **Control inflammation** | **RICE**, **Modalities**, **Gentle ROM** | https://www.amssm.org |
| **2. Recovery** | **Restore ROM**, **Strength**, **Endurance** | **Therapeutic exercise**, **Proprioception**, **Cardio** | https://www.amssm.org |
| **3. Functional** | **Sport-specific skills** | **Agility**, **Plyometrics**, **Non-contact drills** | https://www.amssm.org |
| **4. Return to Play** | **Safe return to competition** | **Full contact practice**, **Functional testing** | https://www.amssm.org |

## Return to Play Criteria
| Criterion | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pain-Free** | **No pain** with sport-specific activities | https://www.amssm.org |
| **Full ROM** | **Full pain-free range of motion** | https://www.amssm.org |
| **Strength** | **=90% of contralateral strength** | https://www.amssm.org |
| **Functional Testing** | **Successful completion of sport-specific drills** | https://www.amssm.org |
| **Psychological Readiness** | **Confidence**, **No fear of reinjury** | https://www.amssm.org |
