# Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation - Overview

## What is Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation (PM&R)?
Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation (PM&R), also known as physiatry, is the medical specialty concerned with the prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation management of people with disabling medical conditions. Physiatrists focus on restoring function, reducing pain, and improving quality of life.

| Core Principle | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Function-Oriented** | **Restore and maximize function**, not just treat disease | https://www.aapmr.org/about-physiatry/what-is-physiatry |
| **Team-Based** | **Interdisciplinary approach** (PT, OT, SLP, Rehab nursing, Psychologist, Social work) | https://www.aapmr.org/about-physiatry/what-is-physiatry |
| **Whole Person** | **Address physical, psychological, social, and vocational needs** | https://www.aapmr.org/about-physiatry/what-is-physiatry |
| **Patient-Centered Goals** | **Collaborative goal setting** based on patient's values and priorities | https://www.aapmr.org/about-physiatry/what-is-physiatry |
