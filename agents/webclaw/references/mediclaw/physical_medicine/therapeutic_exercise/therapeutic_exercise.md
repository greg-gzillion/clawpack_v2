## Types of Therapeutic Exercise
| Type | Description | Examples | URL |
|------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Range of Motion (ROM)** | **Passive**, **Active-Assisted**, **Active** | **PROM** (prevent contracture), **AAROM** (weakness), **AROM** (maintain mobility) | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions/therapeutic-exercise |
| **Strengthening** | **Increase muscle force production** | **Isometric**, **Isotonic** (concentric, eccentric), **Isokinetic** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions/therapeutic-exercise |
| **Endurance** | **Sustain activity over time** | **Aerobic exercise**, **High-repetition strengthening** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions/therapeutic-exercise |
| **Stretching** | **Increase muscle/tendon length** | **Static**, **Dynamic**, **PNF** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions/therapeutic-exercise |
| **Balance/Proprioception** | **Improve stability and body awareness** | **Single-leg stance**, **Unstable surfaces**, **Tai Chi** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions/therapeutic-exercise |
| **Coordination/Agility** | **Improve motor control** | **Agility ladder**, **Cone drills**, **Sport-specific** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions/therapeutic-exercise |
