# Genetics - Multifactorial Inheritance

## Multifactorial Inheritance Model
| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Polygenic | Multiple genes contribute to phenotype |
| Environmental | Non-genetic factors influence expression |
| Threshold Model | Disease manifests when liability exceeds threshold |
| Heritability | Proportion of phenotypic variance due to genetics |

## Recurrence Risk in Multifactorial Disorders
| Relationship | Approximate Risk |
|--------------|------------------|
| First-degree relative | Square root of population prevalence |
| Multiple affected siblings | Higher risk |
| Severe phenotype | Higher risk |
| Sex bias | Higher if affected sex is less commonly affected |
| Consanguinity | Slightly increased risk |

## Genome-Wide Association Studies (GWAS)
| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| SNPs | Single nucleotide polymorphisms |
| Odds Ratio | Strength of association (usually small for complex traits) |
| Polygenic Risk Score (PRS) | Aggregate effect of multiple variants |
| Limitations | Missing heritability, Population stratification, Limited predictive value |

## Gene-Environment Interactions
| Disorder | Genetic Factor | Environmental Factor |
|----------|----------------|---------------------|
| Type 2 Diabetes | TCF7L2 variants | Obesity, Diet |
| Coronary Artery Disease | 9p21 locus | Smoking, Cholesterol |
| Alzheimer Disease | **APOE e4** | Head trauma, Education |
| Parkinson Disease | GBA, LRRK2 | Pesticide exposure |
| Lung Cancer | CYP1A1, GST | Smoking |
| Spina Bifida | MTHFR variants | **Folate deficiency** |
| Alcoholism | ALDH2 | Alcohol consumption |
| Celiac Disease | HLA-DQ2/DQ8 | Gluten ingestion |

## Prevention and Screening in Multifactorial Disorders
| Intervention | Example |
|--------------|---------|
| Folic Acid Supplementation | **400-800 mcg/day** prevents neural tube defects |
| Lifestyle Modification | Diet and exercise for diabetes prevention |
| Newborn Screening | Congenital hypothyroidism, PKU |
| Cascade Screening | Familial hypercholesterolemia |
| Prenatal Screening | Ultrasound for structural defects |
