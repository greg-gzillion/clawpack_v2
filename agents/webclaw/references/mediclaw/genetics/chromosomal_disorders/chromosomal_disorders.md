# Genetics - Chromosomal Disorders

## Types of Chromosomal Abnormalities
| Type | Mechanism | Examples |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Aneuploidy | Abnormal chromosome number | Trisomy 21, Monosomy X |
| Polyploidy | Extra set(s) of chromosomes | Triploidy (69 chromosomes) |
| Deletion | Loss of chromosomal segment | 22q11.2 deletion, Cri-du-chat (5p-) |
| Duplication | Extra copy of segment | Charcot-Marie-Tooth type 1A (PMP22 dup) |
| Translocation | Exchange between chromosomes | Robertsonian, Reciprocal |
| Inversion | Segment reversed | Pericentric, Paracentric |
| Ring Chromosome | Ends fuse forming ring | Ring chromosome 20 |
| Isochromosome | Two identical arms | Isochromosome Xq |

## Aneuploidy Syndromes - Autosomes
| Syndrome | Karyotype | Key Features | Life Expectancy |
|----------|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| Down Syndrome | 47,XX,+21 or 47,XY,+21 | Intellectual disability, Flat facies, Single palmar crease, CHD, Duodenal atresia | 60+ years |
| Edwards Syndrome | 47,XX,+18 or 47,XY,+18 | Severe ID, Clenched hands, Rocker-bottom feet, CHD | <1 year (90%) |
| Patau Syndrome | 47,XX,+13 or 47,XY,+13 | Severe ID, Holoprosencephaly, Cleft lip/palate, Polydactyly | <1 year (80%) |

| Williams Syndrome | 7q11.23 | **ELN** | Supravalvular aortic stenosis, Hypercalcemia, "Cocktail party" personality, Elfin facies |

| Angelman Syndrome | 15q11-q13 (maternal) | **UBE3A** | Severe ID, Seizures, Ataxia, Happy demeanor, Absent speech |
| Smith-Magenis Syndrome | 17p11.2 | **RAI1** | ID, Sleep disturbance, Self-injurious behavior, Hoarse voice |
| Cri-du-Chat Syndrome | 5p15.2 | **CTNND2** | High-pitched cry, Microcephaly, ID, Hypertelorism |

| Rubinstein-Taybi Syndrome | 16p13.3 | **CREBBP** | Broad thumbs/halluces, ID, CHD, Increased tumor risk |
| WAGR Syndrome | 11p13 | **WT1, PAX6** | Wilms tumor, Aniridia, Genitourinary anomalies, ID |

## Cytogenetic Testing
| Method | Resolution | Detects |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Karyotype | 5-10 Mb | Aneuploidy, Large structural variants |
| FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) | 100-200 kb | Specific loci (microdeletions) |
| Chromosomal Microarray (CMA) | 50-100 kb | **Copy number variants (CNVs)** |
| Karyomapping | Whole genome | SNP-based inheritance mapping |
