## Altitude Illness
| Condition | Altitude | Symptoms | Management | URL |
|-----------|----------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS)** | >2500m | Headache, Nausea, Fatigue | Rest, Acetazolamide, Descend | https://www.cdc.gov/altitude-illness/index.html |
| **High Altitude Pulmonary Edema (HAPE)** | >3000m | Dyspnea, Pink frothy sputum | Immediate descent, Oxygen, Nifedipine | https://www.cdc.gov/altitude-illness/index.html |
