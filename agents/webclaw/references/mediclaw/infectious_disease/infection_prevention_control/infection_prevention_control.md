# Infectious Disease - Infection Prevention and Control

## Standard Precautions
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Hand Hygiene** | Alcohol-based hand rub or Soap and water | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/hand-hygiene/index.html |
| **Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)** | Gloves, Gowns, Masks/Eye protection | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/ppe/index.html |
| **Safe Injection Practices** | One needle, One syringe, One time | https://www.cdc.gov/injectionsafety/index.html |
| **Environmental Cleaning** | Routine cleaning and disinfection | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/environmental/index.html |

## Transmission-Based Precautions
| Precaution | Indications | Requirements | URL |
|------------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **Contact** | MRSA, VRE, C. difficile, ESBL, CRE | Gloves + Gown; Private room | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/transmission/index.html |
| **Droplet** | Influenza, Pertussis, Meningococcus | Surgical mask; Private room | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/transmission/index.html |
| **Airborne** | TB, Measles, Varicella | N95 respirator; Negative pressure room | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/transmission/index.html |

## Isolation for Specific Pathogens
| Pathogen | Precaution Type | Duration | URL |
|----------|-----------------|----------|-----|
| **C. difficile** | Contact | Duration of illness | https://www.cdc.gov/cdiff/index.html |
| **MRSA** | Contact | Duration of hospitalization | https://www.cdc.gov/mrsa/index.html |
| **Influenza** | Droplet | 7 days or 24h after fever resolves | https://www.cdc.gov/flu/index.html |
| **TB (Active Pulmonary)** | Airborne | 2 weeks of effective therapy + 3 negative AFB smears | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/index.html |
| **Measles** | Airborne | 4 days after rash onset | https://www.cdc.gov/measles/index.html |
