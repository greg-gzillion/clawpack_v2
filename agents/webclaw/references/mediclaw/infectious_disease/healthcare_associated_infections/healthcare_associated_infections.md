# Infectious Disease - Healthcare-Associated Infections (HAIs)

## Common Healthcare-Associated Infections
| Infection | Definition | Common Pathogens | URL |
|-----------|------------|------------------|-----|
| **Central Line-Associated Bloodstream Infection (CLABSI)** | Bacteremia in patient with central line >2 days | CoNS, S. aureus, Enterococci, Candida, Gram-negatives | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/bsi/index.html |
| **Catheter-Associated UTI (CAUTI)** | UTI in patient with indwelling catheter >2 days | E. coli, Klebsiella, Pseudomonas, Enterococcus, Candida | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/cauti/index.html |
| **Ventilator-Associated Pneumonia (VAP)** | Pneumonia occurring >48h after intubation | S. aureus (MRSA), P. aeruginosa, Enterobacteriaceae, Acinetobacter | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/vap/index.html |
| **Surgical Site Infection (SSI)** | Infection within 30-90 days of surgery | S. aureus (MRSA), CoNS, Gram-negatives, Anaerobes | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/ssi/index.html |
| **Clostridioides difficile Infection (CDI)** | Antibiotic-associated diarrhea/colitis | C. difficile | https://www.cdc.gov/cdiff/index.html |

## CLABSI - Prevention and Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Prevention Bundle** | Hand hygiene, Maximal sterile barriers, Chlorhexidine skin prep, Avoid femoral site, Remove unnecessary lines | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/bsi/index.html |
| **Diagnosis** | Blood cultures (peripheral + catheter), Catheter tip culture | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/bsi/index.html |
| **Management** | Remove catheter, Empiric: Vancomycin + Anti-Pseudomonal beta-lactam | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/bsi/index.html |

## CAUTI - Prevention and Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Prevention** | Avoid unnecessary catheters, Remove as soon as possible, Aseptic insertion, Maintain closed system | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/cauti/index.html |
| **Diagnosis** | Only culture if symptomatic; Do NOT treat asymptomatic bacteriuria | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/cauti/index.html |
| **Management** | Remove/replace catheter + Antibiotics | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/cauti/index.html |

## VAP - Prevention and Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Prevention Bundle** | Elevate head of bed 30-45°, Daily sedation vacation, Oral care with chlorhexidine, Subglottic secretion drainage | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/vap/index.html |
| **Empiric Treatment** | Anti-MRSA + Anti-Pseudomonal | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/vap/index.html |
| **Duration** | 7 days (if clinically improving) | https://www.cdc.gov/hai/vap/index.html |

## Multidrug-Resistant Organisms (MDROs)
| Organism | Resistance Mechanism | Key Antibiotics | Infection Control | URL |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|-------------------|-----|
| **MRSA** | mecA (altered PBP2a) | Vancomycin, Daptomycin, Linezolid, Ceftaroline | Contact precautions, CHG bathing | https://www.cdc.gov/mrsa/index.html |
| **VRE** | vanA, vanB | Linezolid, Daptomycin, Tigecycline | Contact precautions | https://www.cdc.gov/vre/index.html |
| **ESBL-Producing Enterobacteriaceae** | Hydrolyze penicillins, Cephalosporins | Carbapenems, Ceftazidime-avibactam | Contact precautions | https://www.cdc.gov/esbl/index.html |
| **CRE** | KPC, NDM, OXA-48 | Ceftazidime-avibactam, Meropenem-vaborbactam, Cefiderocol | Contact precautions, Active surveillance | https://www.cdc.gov/cre/index.html |
| **MDR Acinetobacter** | Carbapenemases, Efflux pumps | Ampicillin-sulbactam, Polymyxins, Cefiderocol | Contact precautions, Environmental cleaning | https://www.cdc.gov/acinetobacter/index.html |
| **C. difficile** | Toxin production | Oral vancomycin, Fidaxomicin | Contact precautions (soap and water), Sporicidal cleaning | https://www.cdc.gov/cdiff/index.html |
