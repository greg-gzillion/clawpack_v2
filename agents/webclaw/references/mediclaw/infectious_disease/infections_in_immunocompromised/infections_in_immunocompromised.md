# Infectious Disease - Infections in Immunocompromised Hosts

## Immunocompromised Host - Categories
| Category | Examples | Characteristic Pathogens | URL |
|----------|----------|-------------------------|-----|
| **Neutropenia** | Chemotherapy, HSCT, Aplastic anemia | Bacteria (S. aureus, Pseudomonas), Fungi (Aspergillus, Candida) | https://www.cdc.gov/cancer/preventinfections/index.htm |
| **Cell-Mediated Immunity Defect** | HIV, Solid organ transplant, Corticosteroids | Intracellular pathogens, Viruses, Fungi, Parasites | https://www.cdc.gov/cancer/preventinfections/index.htm |
| **Humoral Immunity Defect** | CVID, XLA, Multiple myeloma, Splenectomy | Encapsulated bacteria, Giardia, Enteroviruses | https://www.cdc.gov/cancer/preventinfections/index.htm |
| **Complement Deficiency** | C5-C9, Properdin | Encapsulated bacteria (especially Neisseria) | https://www.cdc.gov/cancer/preventinfections/index.htm |
