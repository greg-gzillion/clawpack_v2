# Infectious Disease - DNA Viral Infections

## Herpesviruses
| Virus | Primary Infection | Reactivation Syndrome | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-------------------|----------------------|-----------|-----|
| **HSV-1** | Gingivostomatitis | Herpes labialis, Keratitis, Encephalitis | Acyclovir, Valacyclovir | https://www.cdc.gov/herpes/index.html |
| **HSV-2** | Genital herpes | Genital herpes, Mollaret meningitis | Acyclovir, Valacyclovir | https://www.cdc.gov/std/herpes/index.htm |
| **VZV** | Varicella (chickenpox) | Herpes zoster (shingles) | Acyclovir, Valacyclovir | https://www.cdc.gov/chickenpox/index.html |
| **EBV** | Infectious mononucleosis | Lymphoproliferative disease | Supportive | https://www.cdc.gov/epstein-barr/index.html |
| **CMV** | Mononucleosis-like | Retinitis, Colitis, Pneumonitis | Valganciclovir, Ganciclovir | https://www.cdc.gov/cmv/index.html |
| **HHV-6** | Roseola infantum | Encephalitis (transplant) | Supportive; Ganciclovir | https://www.cdc.gov/hhv-6/index.html |
| **HHV-8 (KSHV)** | Often asymptomatic | Kaposi sarcoma, Castleman disease | Chemotherapy, Immunosuppression reduction | https://www.cdc.gov/kaposi-sarcoma/index.html |

## Herpes Simplex Virus (HSV)
| Syndrome | Treatment | URL |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Orolabial Herpes** | Valacyclovir 2g BID x 1 day | https://www.cdc.gov/herpes/index.html |
| **Genital Herpes (Primary)** | Acyclovir 400 mg TID x 7-10 days | https://www.cdc.gov/std/herpes/index.htm |
| **HSV Encephalitis** | Acyclovir 10 mg/kg IV q8h x 14-21 days | https://www.cdc.gov/herpes/index.html |
| **Neonatal HSV** | Acyclovir 20 mg/kg IV q8h x 14-21 days | https://www.cdc.gov/herpes/index.html |

## Human Papillomavirus (HPV)
| Type | Clinical Syndrome | Treatment | URL |
|------|-------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Low-Risk (6, 11)** | Anogenital warts | Imiquimod, Cryotherapy | https://www.cdc.gov/std/hpv/index.htm |
| **High-Risk (16, 18)** | Cervical, Anal, Oropharyngeal cancers | Treat precancerous lesions | https://www.cdc.gov/std/hpv/index.htm |
| **Prevention** | HPV vaccine (Gardasil 9) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hpv/index.html |
