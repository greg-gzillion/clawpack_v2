# Infectious Disease - Sexually Transmitted Infections

## CDC STI Treatment Guidelines - Summary
| Infection | Pathogen | First-Line Treatment | URL |
|-----------|----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Chlamydia** | C. trachomatis | Doxycycline 100 mg BID x 7 days | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/chlamydia.htm |
| **Gonorrhea** | N. gonorrhoeae | Ceftriaxone 500 mg IM x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/gonorrhea.htm |
| **Trichomoniasis** | T. vaginalis | Metronidazole 500 mg BID x 7 days | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/trichomoniasis.htm |
| **Bacterial Vaginosis** | Polymicrobial | Metronidazole 500 mg BID x 7 days | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/bv.htm |
| **Vulvovaginal Candidiasis** | C. albicans | Fluconazole 150 mg x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/candidiasis.htm |
| **Syphilis (Primary/Secondary)** | T. pallidum | Penicillin G benzathine 2.4 million units IM x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/syphilis.htm |
| **Genital Herpes (Initial)** | HSV-1, HSV-2 | Acyclovir 400 mg TID x 7-10 days | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/herpes.htm |
| **Chancroid** | H. ducreyi | Ceftriaxone 250 mg IM x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/chancroid.htm |
| **LGV** | C. trachomatis (L1-L3) | Doxycycline 100 mg BID x 21 days | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/lgv.htm |
| **Mycoplasma genitalium** | M. genitalium | Doxycycline x 7d followed by Moxifloxacin x 7d | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/mycoplasmagenitalium.htm |

## Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pathogens** | N. gonorrhoeae, C. trachomatis, Anaerobes | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/pid.htm |
| **Outpatient Treatment** | Ceftriaxone 500 mg IM + Doxycycline x 14d + Metronidazole x 14d | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/pid.htm |
| **Inpatient Treatment** | IV antibiotics; Transition to oral after 24-48h | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/pid.htm |

## STI Screening Recommendations
| Population | Screening | Frequency | URL |
|------------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **All sexually active women <25** | Chlamydia, Gonorrhea | Annual | https://www.cdc.gov/std/screening/index.htm |
| **All pregnant women** | Chlamydia, Gonorrhea, Syphilis, HIV, HBV | Initial prenatal visit | https://www.cdc.gov/std/screening/index.htm |
| **MSM** | HIV, Syphilis, Chlamydia, Gonorrhea | q3-6 months if high risk | https://www.cdc.gov/std/screening/index.htm |
