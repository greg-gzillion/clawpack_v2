# Infectious Disease - Vector-Borne Diseases

## Tick-Borne Diseases

|---------|----------|--------|-------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Lyme Disease** | Borrelia burgdorferi | Ixodes tick | Erythema migrans, CN VII palsy, Arthritis | Doxycycline | https://www.cdc.gov/lyme/index.html |
| **Rocky Mountain Spotted Fever** | Rickettsia rickettsii | Dermacentor tick | Fever, Headache, Rash (wrists/ankles) | Doxycycline | https://www.cdc.gov/rmsf/index.html |
| **Ehrlichiosis** | Ehrlichia chaffeensis | Amblyomma tick | Fever, Leukopenia, Thrombocytopenia | Doxycycline | https://www.cdc.gov/ehrlichiosis/index.html |
| **Anaplasmosis** | Anaplasma phagocytophilum | Ixodes tick | Fever, Leukopenia, Thrombocytopenia | Doxycycline | https://www.cdc.gov/anaplasmosis/index.html |
| **Babesiosis** | Babesia microti | Ixodes tick | Fever, Hemolytic anemia | Atovaquone + Azithromycin | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/babesiosis/index.html |
