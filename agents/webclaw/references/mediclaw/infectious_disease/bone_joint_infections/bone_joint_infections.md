## Prosthetic Joint Infection (PJI)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Classification** | Early (<3 mo): S. aureus, Gram-negatives; Delayed (3-12 mo): CoNS; Late (>12 mo): Hematogenous | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448131/ |
| **Clinical Features** | Pain, Swelling, Erythema, Sinus tract, Loosening on X-ray | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448131/ |
| **Diagnosis** | Synovial fluid (WBC >3000, PMN >80%), Serum CRP/ESR, Tissue culture | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448131/ |
| **Surgical Management** | DAIR, One-stage exchange, Two-stage exchange | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448131/ |
| **Antibiotic Duration** | 6 weeks IV/PO + Chronic suppression | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448131/ |

## Septic Arthritis (Native Joint)

|-----------|-------------|-----|
