## HIV Prevention
| Strategy | Intervention | Efficacy | URL |
|----------|--------------|----------|-----|
| **PrEP** | Daily oral TDF/FTC or TAF/FTC; Cabotegravir IM q2 months | >99% with adherence | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/prevention/index.html |
| **PEP** | TDF/FTC + Raltegravir or Dolutegravir x 28 days | >80% if started within 72h | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/prevention/index.html |
| **Treatment as Prevention (U=U)** | Undetectable = Untransmittable | 100% | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/prevention/index.html |
