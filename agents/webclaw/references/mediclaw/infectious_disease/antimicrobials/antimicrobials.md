| Class | Examples | Spectrum | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Penicillins** | Penicillin G, Amoxicillin, Ampicillin | Streptococci, Oral anaerobes, Listeria, Treponema | Inhibit cell wall synthesis (bind PBPs) | Hypersensitivity (rash, Anaphylaxis), GI upset | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK548127/ |
| **Anti-Staphylococcal Penicillins** | Nafcillin, Oxacillin, Dicloxacillin | MSSA | Same | Same; Interstitial nephritis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557829/ |
| **Aminopenicillins** | Ampicillin, Amoxicillin | Enterococci, Listeria, Some Gram-negatives | Same | Non-allergic rash (EBV, CLL) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482448/ |
| **Anti-Pseudomonal Penicillins** | Piperacillin, Ticarcillin | Pseudomonas, Gram-negatives | Same | Same; Platelet dysfunction | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK545281/ |
| **Beta-Lactamase Inhibitor Combinations** | Amoxicillin-clavulanate, Ampicillin-sulbactam, Piperacillin-tazobactam | MSSA, Anaerobes, Gram-negatives | Same + Inhibit beta-lactamases | Same as parent drug | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557829/ |
| **Cephalosporins (1st Gen)** | Cefazolin, Cephalexin | MSSA, Streptococci, E. coli, Klebsiella, Proteus | Inhibit cell wall synthesis | Hypersensitivity (5-10% cross-reactivity with penicillin), GI | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK551517/ |
| **Cephalosporins (2nd Gen)** | Cefuroxime, Cefoxitin, Cefotetan | Same + Anaerobes (cefoxitin, Cefotetan), H. influenzae | Same | Same; Disulfiram-like reaction (cefotetan) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557505/ |
| **Cephalosporins (3rd Gen)** | Ceftriaxone, Cefotaxime, Ceftazidime | Broad Gram-negative, Streptococci; Ceftazidime: Pseudomonas | Same | Biliary sludging (ceftriaxone) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK459163/ |
| **Cephalosporins (4th Gen)** | Cefepime | Broad Gram-positive and Gram-negative, Pseudomonas | Same | Neurotoxicity (seizures, Encephalopathy - especially renal impairment) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK459162/ |
| **Cephalosporins (5th Gen)** | Ceftaroline, Ceftobiprole | MRSA, Broad spectrum | Same | Same as other cephalosporins | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK548127/ |
| **Carbapenems** | Meropenem, Imipenem-cilastatin, Ertapenem | Broadest spectrum (Gram-positive, Gram-negative, Anaerobes, ESBL); Ertapenem: No Pseudomonas | Same | Seizures (imipenem), Hypersensitivity | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK545281/ |
| **Monobactams** | Aztreonam | Aerobic Gram-negatives only (including Pseudomonas); No Gram-positive or anaerobic activity | Same | Safe in penicillin-allergic patients | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK538313/ |

## Non-Beta-Lactam Antibiotics
| Class | Examples | Spectrum | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Glycopeptides** | Vancomycin, Teicoplanin | Gram-positive only (MRSA, MRSE, Enterococci) | Inhibit cell wall synthesis (bind D-Ala-D-Ala) | Red Man Syndrome, Nephrotoxicity, Ototoxicity | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK459263/ |
| **Lipopeptides** | Daptomycin | Gram-positive only (MRSA, VRE); Inactivated by pulmonary surfactant | Depolarizes cell membrane | CPK elevation, Myopathy, Eosinophilic pneumonia | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470407/ |
| **Oxazolidinones** | Linezolid, Tedizolid | Gram-positive (MRSA, VRE) | Inhibit protein synthesis (50S) | Myelosuppression, Serotonin syndrome, Peripheral/optic neuropathy | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539793/ |
| **Macrolides** | Azithromycin, Clarithromycin | Atypicals, Streptococci | Inhibit protein synthesis (50S) | QTc prolongation, GI intolerance, Drug interactions | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK551495/ |
| **Clindamycin** | Clindamycin | Anaerobes (above diaphragm), Streptococci, MSSA | Inhibit protein synthesis (50S) | C. difficile infection, Rash | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK519574/ |
| **Aminoglycosides** | Gentamicin, Tobramycin, Amikacin | Aerobic Gram-negatives (including Pseudomonas) | Inhibit protein synthesis (30S) | Nephrotoxicity, Ototoxicity, Neuromuscular blockade | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK541105/ |
| **Tetracyclines** | Doxycycline, Minocycline | Atypicals, Rickettsia, Borrelia, MRSA | Inhibit protein synthesis (30S) | Photosensitivity, Teeth discoloration, Vestibular toxicity | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK555905/ |
| **Fluoroquinolones** | Ciprofloxacin, Levofloxacin, Moxifloxacin | Gram-negatives (cipro), Respiratory (levo, Moxi) | Inhibit DNA gyrase/topoisomerase IV | Tendinopathy, QTc prolongation, Aortic dissection, Peripheral neuropathy | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-drug-class/fluoroquinolone-antimicrobial-drugs |
| **Sulfonamides** | TMP-SMX | Gram-negatives, Pneumocystis, Nocardia, MRSA | Inhibit folate synthesis | Hypersensitivity (SJS/TEN, DRESS), Hyperkalemia, Hemolysis (G6PD) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557758/ |
| **Metronidazole** | Metronidazole | Anaerobes (below diaphragm), Protozoa | DNA damage | Disulfiram-like reaction, Metallic taste, Peripheral neuropathy | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539728/ |
