# Infectious Disease - RNA Viral Infections

## Respiratory RNA Viruses
| Virus | Clinical Syndrome | Treatment | Prevention | URL |
|-------|-------------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Influenza A/B** | Fever, Cough, Myalgias | Oseltamivir, Baloxavir | Annual vaccine | https://www.cdc.gov/flu/index.html |
| **SARS-CoV-2 (COVID-19)** | Fever, Cough, Anosmia | Nirmatrelvir-ritonavir, Remdesivir | mRNA vaccines | https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/index.html |
| **RSV** | Bronchiolitis, Pneumonia | Ribavirin (severe); Supportive | RSV vaccine, Nirsevimab | https://www.cdc.gov/rsv/index.html |
| **Human Metapneumovirus** | Bronchiolitis, Pneumonia | Supportive | None | https://www.cdc.gov/human-metapneumovirus/index.html |
| **Parainfluenza** | Croup, Bronchiolitis | Dexamethasone (croup) | None | https://www.cdc.gov/parainfluenza/index.html |
| **Rhinovirus/Enterovirus** | Common cold | Supportive | None | https://www.cdc.gov/non-polio-enterovirus/index.html |
