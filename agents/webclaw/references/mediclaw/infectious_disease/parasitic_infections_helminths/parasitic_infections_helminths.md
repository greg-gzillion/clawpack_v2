# Infectious Disease - Parasitic Infections (Helminths)

## Nematodes (Roundworms)
| Parasite | Transmission | Clinical Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Enterobius vermicularis (Pinworm)** | Fecal-oral | Perianal pruritus | Albendazole 400 mg x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/pinworm/index.html |
| **Ascaris lumbricoides** | Ingestion of eggs | Loeffler syndrome, Obstruction | Albendazole 400 mg x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/ascariasis/index.html |
| **Trichuris trichiura (Whipworm)** | Ingestion of eggs | Dysentery, Rectal prolapse | Albendazole 400 mg daily x 3 days | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/whipworm/index.html |
| **Hookworm** | Skin penetration | Iron deficiency anemia | Albendazole 400 mg x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/hookworm/index.html |
| **Strongyloides stercoralis** | Skin penetration, Autoinfection | Larva currens, Hyperinfection | Ivermectin 200 mcg/kg daily x 1-2 days | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/strongyloides/index.html |
| **Toxocara canis/cati** | Ingestion of eggs | Visceral larva migrans, Ocular larva migrans | Albendazole ± Corticosteroids | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/toxocariasis/index.html |
| **Trichinella spiralis** | Undercooked pork | Myalgias, Periorbital edema | Albendazole + Corticosteroids | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/trichinellosis/index.html |
| **Wuchereria bancrofti** | Mosquito | Lymphedema, Elephantiasis | Diethylcarbamazine (DEC) | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/lymphaticfilariasis/index.html |
| **Onchocerca volvulus** | Blackfly | Subcutaneous nodules, Blindness | Ivermectin | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/onchocerciasis/index.html |

## Cestodes (Tapeworms)
| Parasite | Transmission | Clinical Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Taenia saginata (Beef)** | Undercooked beef | Proglottids in stool | Praziquantel 5-10 mg/kg x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/taeniasis/index.html |
| **Taenia solium (Pork)** | Undercooked pork | Cysticercosis (neurocysticercosis) | Albendazole + Corticosteroids | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/cysticercosis/index.html |
| **Diphyllobothrium latum** | Undercooked freshwater fish | Vitamin B12 deficiency | Praziquantel 5-10 mg/kg x 1 | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/diphyllobothrium/index.html |
| **Echinococcus granulosus** | Dog feces | Hydatid cysts (liver/lung) | Albendazole + PAIR or Surgery | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/echinococcosis/index.html |
