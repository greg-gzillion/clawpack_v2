# Endocrinology - Diagnostic Testing

## Dynamic Endocrine Testing
| Test | Indication | Protocol | Interpretation |
|------|------------|----------|----------------|
| ACTH Stimulation (Cosyntropin) | Adrenal insufficiency | 250 mcg ACTH IV; Cortisol at 0, 30, 60 min | Peak cortisol <18-20 mcg/dL = Adrenal insufficiency |
| Dexamethasone Suppression (1 mg) | Cushing's screening | 1 mg dexamethasone at 11 PM; Cortisol at 8 AM | Cortisol >1.8 mcg/dL = Abnormal |
| Dexamethasone Suppression (Low-Dose 2-day) | Confirm Cushing's | 0.5 mg q6h x 48h; 24h urine cortisol | Failure to suppress = Cushing's |
| Dexamethasone Suppression (High-Dose) | Differentiate Cushing's | 2 mg q6h x 48h; 24h urine cortisol | Suppression >50% = Pituitary (Cushing's disease) |
| CRH Stimulation | Cushing's, Adrenal insufficiency | 100 mcg CRH IV; ACTH and cortisol | Pituitary: ACTH rises; Ectopic: No rise |
| Glucose Suppression (Acromegaly) | Acromegaly | 75g glucose PO; GH at 0, 30, 60, 120 min | GH >1 ng/mL = Acromegaly |
| Water Deprivation | Diabetes insipidus | Fluid restriction; Urine osmolality, Sodium | Urine osmolality <300 + Hypernatremia = DI; Response to DDAVP = Central DI |
| Saline Infusion | Primary aldosteronism | 2L 0.9% NaCl over 4h; Aldosterone | Aldosterone >10 ng/dL = Primary aldosteronism |
| Mixed Meal Test | Postprandial hypoglycemia | Standardized meal; Glucose q30min x 5h | Glucose <60 mg/dL = Hypoglycemia |
| 72-Hour Fast | Insulinoma | Supervised fast; Glucose, Insulin, C-peptide | Glucose <55 + Insulin >=3 + C-peptide >=0.6 = Insulinoma |

## Pituitary Function Testing
| Axis | Screening Test | Confirmatory Test |
|------|----------------|-------------------|
| GH-IGF-1 | IGF-1 | GH stimulation (Insulin tolerance, Glucagon, Macimorelin) |
| ACTH-Cortisol | AM Cortisol | ACTH stimulation (Cosyntropin); Insulin tolerance |
| TSH-Thyroid | TSH, Free T4 | TRH stimulation (rarely needed) |
| LH/FSH-Gonadal | LH, FSH, Testosterone/Estradiol | GnRH stimulation |
| Prolactin | Prolactin | MRI if elevated |
| ADH | Serum sodium, Urine osmolality | Water deprivation test |

## Thyroid Function Testing
| Pattern | TSH | Free T4 | Interpretation |
|---------|-----|---------|----------------|
| Primary Hyperthyroidism | Low | High | Graves, Toxic nodule |
| Subclinical Hyperthyroidism | Low | Normal | Mild thyroid autonomy |
| Primary Hypothyroidism | High | Low | Hashimoto's, Iatrogenic |
| Subclinical Hypothyroidism | High | Normal | Mild thyroid failure |
| Central Hypothyroidism | Low/Normal | Low | Pituitary/Hypothalamic disease |
| Euthyroid Sick Syndrome | Variable | Low | Non-thyroidal illness |
| TSH-secreting Adenoma | High/Normal | High | Pituitary tumor |

## Adrenal Function Testing
| Test | Indication | Interpretation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| AM Cortisol | Adrenal insufficiency screening | <3 mcg/dL = AI; 3-15 = Indeterminate; >15 = Normal |
| ACTH | Differentiate primary vs secondary AI | High = Primary (Addison's); Low = Secondary/Tertiary |
| Aldosterone:Renin Ratio (ARR) | Primary aldosteronism screening | >20-30 + Aldosterone >15 ng/dL = Positive |
| Plasma Metanephrines | Pheochromocytoma screening | Elevated = Positive (most sensitive) |
| 24h Urine Metanephrines | Pheochromocytoma confirmation | Elevated = Positive |
| 24h Urine Free Cortisol | Cushing's screening | >3x upper limit = Cushing's |
| Late-Night Salivary Cortisol | Cushing's screening | Elevated = Loss of diurnal rhythm |
| DHEA-S | Adrenal androgen source | Elevated in PCOS, Adrenal tumors |

## Gonadal Function Testing
| Test | Indication | Interpretation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Testosterone (Total and Free) | Male hypogonadism | Low total T (<300 ng/dL) on 2 occasions = Hypogonadism |
| LH, FSH | Differentiate primary vs secondary | High = Primary; Low/Normal = Secondary |
| SHBG | Calculate free testosterone | High SHBG lowers free T (obesity, aging, liver disease) |
| Estradiol | Female hypogonadism, Menopause | Low in menopause, POI |
| Prolactin | Galactorrhea, Hypogonadism | Elevated = Prolactinoma, Medications, Hypothyroidism |
| hCG | Pregnancy, Tumor marker | Elevated in pregnancy, Germ cell tumors |
| Inhibin B | Ovarian reserve, Sertoli cell function | Low = Decreased reserve |

## Bone and Mineral Testing
| Test | Indication | Interpretation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Calcium (Total and Ionized) | Calcium disorders | Correct for albumin |
| PTH | Hyper/Hypocalcemia | Differentiates PTH-mediated vs non-PTH-mediated |
| 25(OH)Vitamin D | Vitamin D status | <20 = Deficient; 20-30 = Insufficient; >30 = Sufficient |
| 1,25(OH)2Vitamin D | Granulomatous disease, CKD | Elevated in sarcoidosis, lymphoma |
| Phosphate | Bone/mineral disorders | Low in hyperparathyroidism, XLH; High in CKD, Hypoparathyroidism |
| Magnesium | Hypocalcemia, Arrhythmias | Low in malabsorption, Alcoholism, PPI use |
| Alkaline Phosphatase | Bone turnover, Liver disease | Elevated in Paget's, Metastases, Hyperparathyroidism |
| Bone-Specific Alk Phos | Differentiate bone vs liver ALP | Elevated in high bone turnover |
| 24h Urine Calcium | Hypercalcemia, Kidney stones | Low in FHH; High in primary hyperparathyroidism |
| FGF23 | Hypophosphatemic disorders | Elevated in tumor-induced osteomalacia, XLH |

## Diabetes Testing
| Test | Indication | Interpretation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| HbA1c | Diabetes diagnosis, Monitoring | <5.7% Normal; 5.7-6.4% Prediabetes; >=6.5% Diabetes |
| Fasting Glucose | Diabetes diagnosis | <100 Normal; 100-125 IFG; >=126 Diabetes |
| OGTT | GDM, Prediabetes, Diabetes | 2h <140 Normal; 140-199 IGT; >=200 Diabetes |
| C-peptide | Differentiate type 1 vs 2; Insulinoma | Low in type 1; High in type 2, Insulinoma |
| Insulin | Insulin resistance, Insulinoma | High in insulin resistance, Insulinoma |
| GAD65, IA-2, ZnT8 Antibodies | Type 1 diabetes | Positive = Autoimmune diabetes |
| Urine Albumin:Creatinine | Diabetic nephropathy | >30 mg/g = Microalbuminuria |
