# Endocrinology - Obesity and Lipid Disorders

## Obesity Classification
| Class | BMI (kg/m2) | Obesity-Related Disease Risk |
|-------|-------------|------------------------------|
| Underweight | <18.5 | Increased risk of other problems |
| Normal | 18.5-24.9 | Average |
| Overweight | 25-29.9 | Increased |
| Class I Obesity | 30-34.9 | High |
| Class II Obesity | 35-39.9 | Very High |
| Class III Obesity | >=40 | Extremely High |

## Lipid Panel - Normal Values
| Lipid | Optimal | Borderline | High |
|-------|---------|------------|------|
| Total Cholesterol | <200 mg/dL | 200-239 mg/dL | >=240 mg/dL |
| LDL Cholesterol | <100 mg/dL | 130-159 mg/dL | >=160 mg/dL |
| HDL Cholesterol | >60 mg/dL | 40-59 mg/dL (men), 50-59 (women) | <40 (men), <50 (women) |
| Triglycerides | <150 mg/dL | 150-199 mg/dL | >=200 mg/dL |
| Non-HDL Cholesterol | <130 mg/dL | 130-159 mg/dL | >=160 mg/dL |

## LDL Goals by Risk Category
| Risk Category | LDL Goal | Statin Intensity |
|---------------|----------|------------------|
| Clinical ASCVD | <70 mg/dL | High-intensity |
| LDL >=190 mg/dL | <100 mg/dL | High-intensity |
| Diabetes (age 40-75) | <70 mg/dL (or 50% reduction) | Moderate to High-intensity |
| Intermediate Risk (7.5-20% 10-year ASCVD) | Consider <100 mg/dL | Moderate-intensity |
| Low Risk (<7.5% 10-year ASCVD) | <160 mg/dL | Lifestyle; Consider statin |

## Statin Therapy
| Intensity | LDL Reduction | Examples |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| High-Intensity | >=50% | Atorvastatin 40-80 mg, Rosuvastatin 20-40 mg |
| Moderate-Intensity | 30-49% | Atorvastatin 10-20 mg, Rosuvastatin 5-10 mg, Simvastatin 20-40 mg, Pravastatin 40-80 mg |
| Low-Intensity | <30% | Simvastatin 10 mg, Pravastatin 10-20 mg, Lovastatin 20 mg |

## Statin Side Effects
| Side Effect | Management |
|-------------|------------|
| Myalgias/Myopathy | Reduce dose, Switch statin, Alternate day dosing, CoQ10 |
| Hepatotoxicity | Monitor LFTs; Discontinue if >3x ULN |
| New-Onset Diabetes | Benefits outweigh risks; Continue statin |
| Drug Interactions | Avoid gemfibrozil + statin; Use fenofibrate instead |

## Non-Statin Lipid-Lowering Agents
| Drug Class | Examples | LDL Reduction | Key Features |
|------------|----------|---------------|--------------|
| Ezetimibe | Zetia | 15-20% | Inhibits cholesterol absorption; Add-on to statin |
| PCSK9 Inhibitors | Alirocumab, Evolocumab | 50-60% | Injectable; For FH or statin-intolerant ASCVD |
| Bempedoic Acid | Nexletol | 15-20% | Oral; No muscle side effects |
| Inclisiran | Leqvio | 50% | siRNA; Twice-yearly injection |
| Bile Acid Sequestrants | Cholestyramine, Colesevelam | 10-15% | GI side effects; Multiple drug interactions |
| Fibrates | Fenofibrate, Gemfibrozil | Minimal LDL | **Lowers TG**; Risk of myopathy with statins |
| Omega-3 Fatty Acids | Icosapent Ethyl (Vascepa) | Minimal LDL | **Lowers TG**; Reduces CV events |
| Niacin | Niaspan | 10-15% | Flushing; No CV benefit in trials |

## Hypertriglyceridemia
| Severity | Triglycerides | Management |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| Mild-Moderate | 150-500 mg/dL | Lifestyle; Statin if ASCVD risk; Fibrates/Omega-3 if needed |
| Severe | 500-1000 mg/dL | Lifestyle; Fibrates/Omega-3; Treat secondary causes |
| Very Severe | >1000 mg/dL | **Risk of pancreatitis**; Aggressive TG lowering; Insulin if uncontrolled DM |

## Familial Hypercholesterolemia (FH)
| Component | Management |
|-----------|------------|
| Diagnostic Criteria | LDL >190 mg/dL (untreated) + Family history + Physical findings (xanthomas, corneal arcus) |
| Genetic Testing | LDLR, APOB, PCSK9 mutations |
| Heterozygous FH | 1 in 250; High-intensity statin + Ezetimibe ｱ PCSK9 inhibitor |
| Homozygous FH | 1 in 300,000; LDL apheresis; Lomitapide; Evinacumab |
| Cascade Screening | Test all first-degree relatives |

## Metabolic Syndrome
| Criteria | Cutoffs (3 of 5 required) |
|----------|---------------------------|
| Waist Circumference | >=40 inches (men), >=35 inches (women) |
| Triglycerides | >=150 mg/dL or on treatment |
| HDL Cholesterol | <40 mg/dL (men), <50 mg/dL (women) or on treatment |
| Blood Pressure | >=130/85 mmHg or on treatment |
| Fasting Glucose | >=100 mg/dL or on treatment |
