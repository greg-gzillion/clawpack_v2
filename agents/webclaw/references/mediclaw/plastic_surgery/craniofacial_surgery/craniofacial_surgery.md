## Craniofacial Syndromes

|----------|------|----------------------|----------------|-----|

| **Hemifacial Microsomia** | Sporadic | **Asymmetric mandibular hypoplasia**, **Ear anomalies**, **Facial nerve weakness** | **Goldenhar syndrome** (epibulbar dermoids, Vertebral anomalies) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/hemifacial-microsomia |
