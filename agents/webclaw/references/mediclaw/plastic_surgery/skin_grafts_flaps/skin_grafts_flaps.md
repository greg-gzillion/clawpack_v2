## STSG vs FTSG
| Feature | STSG | FTSG | URL |
|---------|------|------|-----|
| **Take Rate** | **Higher** | Lower | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/skin-grafts |
| **Contraction** | **Significant** (30-50%) | **Minimal** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/skin-grafts |
| **Color Match** | Poor | **Better** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/skin-grafts |
| **Texture** | Thin, Shiny | More natural | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/skin-grafts |
| **Donor Site** | Heals by re-epithelialization | Requires primary closure | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/skin-grafts |
