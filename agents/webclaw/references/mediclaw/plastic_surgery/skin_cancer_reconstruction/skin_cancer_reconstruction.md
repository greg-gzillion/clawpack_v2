# Plastic Surgery - Skin Cancer Reconstruction

## Skin Cancer - Overview
| Type | Frequency | Metastatic Potential | Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------|----------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)** | 80% | **Low** | **Mohs surgery**, **Surgical excision**, **Topical** (superficial) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/basal-cell-treatment-pdq |
| **Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC)** | 20% | **Moderate** | **Mohs surgery**, **Surgical excision**, **Radiation** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/squamous-cell-treatment-pdq |
| **Melanoma** | <5% | **High** | **Wide local excision**, **Sentinel lymph node biopsy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |

## Mohs Micrographic Surgery
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indications** | **High-risk BCC/SCC** (face, Recurrent, Aggressive histology), **Tissue-sparing** | https://www.skincancer.org/treatment-resources/mohs-surgery/ |
| **Process** | **Staged excision** with **complete margin assessment** | https://www.skincancer.org/treatment-resources/mohs-surgery/ |
| **Cure Rate** | **99%** for primary BCC, **97%** for primary SCC | https://www.skincancer.org/treatment-resources/mohs-surgery/ |
| **Reconstruction** | **Immediate** or **Delayed** after clear margins | https://www.skincancer.org/treatment-resources/mohs-surgery/ |
