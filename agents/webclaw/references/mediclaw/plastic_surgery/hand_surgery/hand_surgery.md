## Common Hand Injuries
| Injury | Description | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Mallet Finger** | **Extensor tendon avulsion** at DIP | **Splint DIP in extension** x 6-8 weeks | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/mallet-finger |
| **Boutonniere Deformity** | **Central slip disruption** at PIP | **Splint PIP in extension** x 6 weeks | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/boutonniere-deformity |
| **Jersey Finger** | **FDP avulsion** (ring finger most common) | **Surgical repair** within 7-10 days | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/jersey-finger |
| **Gamekeeper's Thumb** | **UCL tear** at MCP | **Splinting** (partial), **Surgery** (complete, Stener lesion) | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/gamekeepers-thumb |
