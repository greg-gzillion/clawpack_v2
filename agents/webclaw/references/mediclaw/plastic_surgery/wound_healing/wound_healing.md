|-------|-----------|------------|------------|-----|

## Types of Wound Healing
| Type | Description | Examples | URL |
|------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Primary Intention** | **Direct approximation** of wound edges | **Surgical incisions**, **Lacerations** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/wound-care |
| **Secondary Intention** | Wound left open to heal by **granulation** and **epithelialization** | **Contaminated wounds**, **Pressure injuries** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/wound-care |
| **Tertiary Intention (Delayed Primary)** | Wound left open initially, then **closed surgically** after 3-5 days | **Contaminated wounds**, **Questionable viability** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/wound-care |
