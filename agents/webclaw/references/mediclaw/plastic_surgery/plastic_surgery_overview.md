# Plastic Surgery - Overview

## What is Plastic Surgery?
Plastic surgery is the surgical specialty concerned with the restoration, reconstruction, and alteration of the human body. It encompasses both reconstructive surgery (restoring form and function after trauma, cancer, or congenital conditions) and aesthetic (cosmetic) surgery (enhancing appearance).
