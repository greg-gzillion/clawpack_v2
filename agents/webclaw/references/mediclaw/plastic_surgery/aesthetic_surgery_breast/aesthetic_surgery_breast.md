## Breast Augmentation - Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Capsular Contracture** | **Observation**, **Capsulotomy**, **Capsulectomy**, **Implant exchange**, **Site change** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/cosmetic-procedures/breast-augmentation/complications |
| **Implant Rupture** | **MRI** (silicone), **Implant exchange** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/cosmetic-procedures/breast-augmentation/complications |
| **Infection** | **Antibiotics**, **Salvage**, **Explantation** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/cosmetic-procedures/breast-augmentation/complications |
| **Breast Implant-Associated Anaplastic Large Cell Lymphoma (BIA-ALCL)** | **Textured implants**; **Seroma**, **Mass** ? **CD30+**, **ALK-** | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/breast-implants/breast-implant-associated-anaplastic-large-cell-lymphoma-bia-alcl |
| **Breast Implant Illness (BII)** | **Systemic symptoms**; **Consider explantation** | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/breast-implants/breast-implant-illness |
