## Midface Fractures (Le Fort Classification)
| Type | Fracture Pattern | Clinical Features | URL |
|------|-----------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Le Fort I** | **Transverse** above maxillary teeth | **Floating palate**, **Malocclusion** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/midface-fracture |
| **Le Fort II** | **Pyramidal** through nasal bones, Medial orbits, Maxilla | **Midface mobility**, **Periorbital edema/ecchymosis**, **Epistaxis**, **CSF rhinorrhea** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/midface-fracture |
| **Le Fort III** | **Craniofacial disjunction** (zygomaticofrontal, Zygomaticotemporal, Nasofrontal sutures) | **Complete midface mobility**, **"Dish face" deformity**, **CSF rhinorrhea** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/midface-fracture |

## Orbital Floor Fracture
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Mechanism** | **Blunt trauma** ? Increased intraorbital pressure ? **Blowout fracture** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/orbital-fracture |
| **Indications for Surgery** | **Enophthalmos >2 mm**, **Diplopia** with positive forced duction, **Large defect (>50% floor)**, **Entrapment** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/orbital-fracture |
| **Timing** | **1-2 weeks** (after edema resolves) | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/orbital-fracture |
| **Approaches** | **Transconjunctival** (preferred), **Subciliary**, **Transantral** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/orbital-fracture |
| **Reconstruction** | **Titanium mesh**, **Porous polyethylene** (Medpor), **Bone graft**, **Resorbable plate** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/orbital-fracture |
