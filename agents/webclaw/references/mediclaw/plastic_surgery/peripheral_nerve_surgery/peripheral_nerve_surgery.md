# Plastic Surgery - Peripheral Nerve Surgery

## Nerve Injury Classification (Seddon and Sunderland)
| Seddon | Sunderland | Pathology | Recovery | URL |
|--------|------------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Neurapraxia** | **Grade I** | **Conduction block**, Axon intact | **Complete** (days-weeks) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |
| **Axonotmesis** | **Grade II** | **Axon disruption**, Endoneurium intact | **Good** (months) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |
| **Neurotmesis** | **Grade III** | **Axon + Endoneurium disruption** | **Variable** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |
| **Neurotmesis** | **Grade IV** | **Axon + Endoneurium + Perineurium disruption** | **Poor** (requires surgery) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |
| **Neurotmesis** | **Grade V** | **Complete nerve transection** | **None** (requires surgery) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |

## Nerve Repair Techniques
| Technique | Description | Indications | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Primary Neurorrhaphy** | **Direct end-to-end suture** | **Clean laceration**, **No tension** | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/nerve-injury |
| **Nerve Graft** | **Autograft** (sural, Medial antebrachial) | **Gap**, **Tension** | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/nerve-injury |
| **Nerve Transfer** | **Redundant nerve transferred to distal target** | **Proximal injury**, **Long regeneration distance** | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/nerve-injury |
| **Nerve Allograft** | **Processed human nerve** | **Gap**, **Avoid donor site** | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/nerve-injury |
| **Conduit** | **Synthetic or biologic tube** | **Small gaps** (<3 cm) | https://www.assh.org/handcare/condition/nerve-injury |
