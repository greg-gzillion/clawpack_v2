|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Autologous Breast Reconstruction
| Flap | Blood Supply | Donor Site | Indications | URL |
|------|--------------|------------|-------------|-----|
| **DIEP (Deep Inferior Epigastric Perforator)** | **Deep inferior epigastric artery** | **Lower abdomen** | **Most common autologous**; Abdominal tissue available | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/autologous |
| **Pedicled TRAM** | **Superior epigastric artery** | **Lower abdomen** | Older technique; Higher abdominal morbidity | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/autologous |
| **Latissimus Dorsi** | **Thoracodorsal artery** | **Back** | **Implant coverage**, **Partial breast defects**, **Salvage** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/autologous |
| **TUG (Transverse Upper Gracilis)** | **Medial circumflex femoral artery** | **Inner thigh** | **Small-moderate breasts**, **No abdominal tissue** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/autologous |
| **PAP (Profunda Artery Perforator)** | **Profunda femoris artery** | **Posterior thigh** | **Small-moderate breasts**, **No abdominal tissue** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/autologous |
| **SGAP/IGAP** | **Superior/Inferior gluteal artery** | **Buttock** | **Alternative when other sites unavailable** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/autologous |

## Breast Reconstruction - Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Implant Infection** | **Antibiotics**, **Salvage** (washout, Implant exchange), **Explantation** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/complications |
| **Capsular Contracture** | **Observation**, **Capsulotomy**, **Capsulectomy**, **Implant exchange**, **ADM** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/complications |
| **Implant Rupture** | **MRI** (silent rupture), **Implant exchange** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/complications |
| **Flap Failure** | **Re-exploration**, **Leech therapy**, **Salvage**, **Alternative reconstruction** | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/complications |
| **Fat Necrosis** | **Observation**, **Excision** if symptomatic | https://www.plasticsurgery.org/reconstructive-procedures/breast-reconstruction/complications |
