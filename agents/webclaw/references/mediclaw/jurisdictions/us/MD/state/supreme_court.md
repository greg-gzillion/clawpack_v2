# Maryland Supreme Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of Maryland
- **Website**: https://www.mdcourts.gov/supremecourt
- **Phone**: (410) 260-1500
- **Fax**: (410) 260-1590
- **Address**: 361 Rowe Boulevard, Annapolis, MD 21401
- **Clerk Email**: supremecourtclerk@mdcourts.gov
- **Chief Justice**: Matthew J. Fader
- **Number of Justices**: 7
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Formerly known as the Maryland Court of Appeals (name changed in 2022)
