# Chatham County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Chatham
- **Court Name**: Chatham County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (912) 652-7000
- **Address**: 133 Montgomery Street, Savannah, GA 31401
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
