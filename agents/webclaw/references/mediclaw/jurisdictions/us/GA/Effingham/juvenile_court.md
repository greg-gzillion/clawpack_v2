# Effingham County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Effingham
- **Court Name**: Effingham County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (912) 754-2112
- **Address**: 701 N Laurel Street, Springfield, GA 31329
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
