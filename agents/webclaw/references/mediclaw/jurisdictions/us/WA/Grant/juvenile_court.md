# Grant County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Grant
- **Court Name**: Grant County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (509) 754-2011
- **Address**: 35 C Street NW, Ephrata, WA 98823
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 8-17)
- At-risk youth (ARY)
- Child in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Dependency (abuse/neglect)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
