# Stevens County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Stevens
- **Court Name**: Stevens County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (509) 684-6501
- **Address**: 215 S Oak Street, Colville, WA 99114
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 8-17)
- At-risk youth (ARY)
- Child in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Dependency (abuse/neglect)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
