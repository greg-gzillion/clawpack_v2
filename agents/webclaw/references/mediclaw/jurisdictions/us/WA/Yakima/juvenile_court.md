# Yakima County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Yakima
- **Court Name**: Yakima County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (509) 574-1400
- **Address**: 128 N 2nd Street, Yakima, WA 98901
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 8-17)
- At-risk youth (ARY)
- Child in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Dependency (abuse/neglect)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
