# Oregon Tax Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Oregon Tax Court
- **Website**: https://www.courts.oregon.gov/tax
- **Phone**: (503) 986-5640
- **Fax**: (503) 986-5880
- **Address**: 1163 State Street, Salem, OR 97301
- **Clerk Email**: taxcourtclerk@ojd.state.or.us
- **Magistrate Division**: Handles smaller tax disputes
- **Regular Division**: Handles larger tax disputes with appeals to Supreme Court
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Jurisdiction
- State tax appeals
- Property tax assessments
- Corporate excise tax
- Personal income tax
