# Lowndes County Chancery Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Lowndes
- **Court Name**: Lowndes County Chancery Court
- **Phone**: (662) 329-5800
- **Address**: 505 2nd Avenue N, Columbus, MS 39701
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Domestic relations (divorce, custody, support)
- Probate and estates
- Guardianships and conservatorships
- Land title disputes
- Contract disputes
- Injunctions and equity matters
