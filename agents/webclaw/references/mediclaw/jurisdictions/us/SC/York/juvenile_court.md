# York County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: York
- **Court Name**: York County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (803) 628-3020
- **Address**: 2 S Congress Street, York, SC 29745
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 18)
- Status offenses (truancy, running away)
- Children in Need of Supervision
- Abuse and neglect
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
