## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction for criminal cases
- Death penalty appeals
- Post-conviction relief
- Habeas corpus in criminal matters

## Note: Oklahoma has a bifurcated court system:
- Court of Criminal Appeals handles criminal appeals
- Supreme Court handles civil appeals
