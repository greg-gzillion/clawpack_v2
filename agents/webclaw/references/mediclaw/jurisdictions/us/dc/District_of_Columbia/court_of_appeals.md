# District of Columbia Court of Appeals

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: District of Columbia Court of Appeals
- **Website**: https://www.dccourts.gov/court-of-appeals
- **Phone**: (202) 879-2700
- **Address**: 430 E Street NW, Washington, DC 20001
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Highest court for DC (equivalent of state supreme court)
- Appeals from DC Superior Court
- Administrative agency appeals
- Attorney discipline and bar admissions

## Judges
- Chief Judge and 8 Associate Judges
- Appointed by President, confirmed by Senate
- 15-year terms
