# North Dakota Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- North Dakota Council on the Arts — https://www.arts.nd.gov/
- AIA North Dakota — https://www.aiand.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Extreme cold climate design (-40F to 100F)
- Heavy snow loads (45-60 psf)
- Prairie and Badlands landscape
- Energy industry design (oil, wind)
