# Arizona Court of Appeals

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Arizona Court of Appeals
- **Website**: https://www.azcourts.gov/appeals
- **Phone**: (602) 452-3300
- **Address**: 1501 W Washington Street, Phoenix, AZ 85007
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM MST

## Districts/Divisions
2 Divisions: Division 1 (Phoenix), Division 2 (Tucson)

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals from trial courts
- Review of administrative agency decisions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
