# Supreme Court of Virginia

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of Virginia
- **Website**: https://www.vacourts.gov/supreme

- **Address**: 100 N 9th Street, Richmond, VA 23219
- **Clerk Email**: supremecourtclerk@vacourts.gov
- **Chief Justice**: S. Bernard Goodwyn
- **Number of Justices**: 7
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:15 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Administrative supervision over all Virginia courts
- Oldest continuously active judicial body in the Western Hemisphere (established 1779)
