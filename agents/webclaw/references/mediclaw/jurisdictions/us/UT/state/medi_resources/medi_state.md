## Latter-day Saint (LDS/Mormon) Healthcare Context
Utah has the highest concentration of Latter-day Saint (Mormon) population in the world. While there are no official "Mormon hospitals" today, the following systems have historical LDS roots and continue to serve the community:
- Intermountain Health ó founded by LDS Church in 1975 (originally Intermountain Health Care, donated to community in 1975) ó https://www.intermountainhealthcare.org/
- The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints Welfare Services ó https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/
- LDS Family Services ó counseling/mental health ó https://www.providentliving.org/
- Deseret Industries ó vocational rehab/health ó https://www.deseretindustries.org/
- The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints Missionary Health Services ó medical care for missionaries ó Salt Lake City ó https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/
