# Hardy County Magistrate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Hardy
- **Court Name**: Hardy County Magistrate Court
- **Phone**: (304) 530-0230
- **Address**: 204 Washington St, Moorefield, WV 26836
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM ET
- **Website**: http://www.courtswv.gov

## Jurisdiction
- Misdemeanors
- Traffic violations
- Small claims (up to $10,000)
- Civil cases (up to $10,000)
- Preliminary hearings
- Issue warrants and emergency orders
