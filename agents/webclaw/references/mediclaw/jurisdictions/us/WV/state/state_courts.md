# West Virginia Court System

## Court Structure
- **Supreme Court of Appeals** — Final appellate court (5 justices)
- **Circuit Courts** — 31 judicial circuits serving 55 counties
  - Felonies, civil cases over $10,000, appeals from lower courts
- **Magistrate Courts** — County-level limited jurisdiction
  - Misdemeanors, civil under $10,000, small claims, warrants
- **Family Courts** — Domestic relations in each judicial circuit
- **Juvenile Courts** — Juvenile matters handled by circuit courts
