# Pathology - Clinical Chemistry

## Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP)
| Analyte | Reference Range | Clinical Significance | URL |
|---------|-----------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Sodium** | 135-145 mEq/L | **Hyponatremia**: SIADH, Volume depletion; **Hypernatremia**: Dehydration, DI | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/hyponatremia |
| **Potassium** | 3.5-5.0 mEq/L | **Hypokalemia**: Diuretics, GI loss; **Hyperkalemia**: CKD, ACEi, Pseudohyperkalemia | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/what-hyperkalemia |
| **Chloride** | 98-107 mEq/L | Metabolic acidosis (normal AG vs high AG) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/acid-base-disorders |
| **Bicarbonate** | 22-29 mEq/L | **? Metabolic acidosis**; **? Metabolic alkalosis** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/acid-base-disorders |
| **BUN** | 7-20 mg/dL | ? Renal failure, Dehydration, GI bleed, High protein | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/acute-kidney-injury |
| **Creatinine** | 0.6-1.2 mg/dL | ? Renal dysfunction; **eGFR** calculation | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/acute-kidney-injury |
| **Glucose** | 70-100 mg/dL (fasting) | **Diabetes** (=126 fasting), Hypoglycemia (<70) | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |

## Liver Function Tests (LFTs)
| Analyte | Pattern | Interpretation | URL |
|---------|---------|----------------|-----|
| **ALT** | **Hepatocellular** (?? ALT, ? AST) | Viral hepatitis, DILI, Ischemia | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **AST** | Hepatocellular or Muscle | ALT > AST = Viral; AST > ALT = Alcohol, Cirrhosis | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **ALP** | **Cholestatic** (?? ALP, ? GGT) | Biliary obstruction, Infiltrative disease | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **GGT** | Cholestatic, Alcohol use | Confirms ALP is hepatic (not bone) | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **Total Bilirubin** | ? Unconjugated vs Conjugated | **Unconjugated**: Hemolysis, Gilbert; **Conjugated**: Cholestasis, Hepatitis | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
