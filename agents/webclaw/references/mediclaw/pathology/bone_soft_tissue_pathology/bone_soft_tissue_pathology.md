# Pathology - Bone and Soft Tissue Pathology

## Sarcoma Classification
| Tumor | Cell of Origin | Histology | IHC | Molecular | URL |
|-------|---------------|-----------|-----|-----------|-----|
| **Osteosarcoma** | Osteoblast | **Malignant osteoid**, **Lace-like**, Pleomorphic cells | **SATB2+**, Osteocalcin+, ALP+ | **TP53**, **RB1**, **MDM2** amplification | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Chondrosarcoma** | Chondrocyte | **Lobules of cartilage**, **Myxoid matrix**, Atypia | **S100+**, **SOX9+** | **IDH1/2** (conventional), **COL2A1** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Ewing Sarcoma** | Primitive neuroectodermal | **Small round blue cells**, **Homer-Wright rosettes** | **CD99+** (strong membranous), **NKX2.2+**, **FLI1+** | **EWSR1-FLI1** (85%), **EWSR1-ERG** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Liposarcoma (Well-differentiated)** | Adipocyte | **Mature fat**, **Atypical stromal cells**, **Lipoblasts** | **MDM2+**, **CDK4+**, **S100+** (fat cells) | **MDM2 amplification** (12q13-15) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Liposarcoma (Myxoid/Round Cell)** | Adipocyte | **Myxoid stroma**, **"Chicken-wire" vasculature**, **Lipoblasts** | **S100+** (variable), **NY-ESO-1+** | **FUS-DDIT3** (>95%), **EWSR1-DDIT3** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Leiomyosarcoma** | Smooth muscle | **Fascicles of spindle cells**, **Cigar-shaped nuclei**, **Eosinophilic cytoplasm** | **Desmin+**, **SMA+**, **Caldesmon+**, **h-Calponin+** | Complex karyotype | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Synovial Sarcoma** | Unknown | **Monophasic** (spindle) or **Biphasic** (spindle + epithelial) | **TLE1+**, **CK+** (epithelial), **EMA+**, **BCL2+**, CD99+ | **SS18-SSX1/SSX2/SSX4** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumor (GIST)** | Interstitial cells of Cajal | **Spindle** (70%), **Epithelioid** (20%), Mixed | **CD117 (c-KIT)+**, **DOG1+**, CD34+ (70%) | **KIT** (80%), **PDGFRA** (5-10%) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1447 |
