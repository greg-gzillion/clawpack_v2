# Pathology - Hematopathology

## Overview
Hematopathology is the study of diseases of blood, bone marrow, lymph nodes, and spleen.

| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Peripheral Blood Smear** | Examination of RBC, WBC, and platelet morphology | https://www.hematology.org/education/trainees/blood-smear |
| **Bone Marrow Examination** | Aspirate (cytology) and Core biopsy (architecture) | https://www.hematology.org/education/trainees/bone-marrow |
| **Flow Cytometry** | Immunophenotyping of hematopoietic cells | https://www.amp.org/education/flow-cytometry-resources/ |
| **Cytogenetics/FISH** | Chromosomal abnormalities in hematologic malignancies | https://www.genome.gov/genetics-glossary/Fluorescence-In-Situ-Hybridization |
