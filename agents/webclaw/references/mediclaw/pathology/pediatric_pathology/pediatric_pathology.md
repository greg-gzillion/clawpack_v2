# Pathology - Pediatric Pathology

## Common Pediatric Tumors
| Tumor | Age | Site | Histology | Molecular | URL |
|-------|-----|------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Neuroblastoma** | <5 years | **Adrenal**, Sympathetic chain | **Small round blue cells**, **Neuropil**, **Homer-Wright rosettes**, **Ganglionic differentiation** | **MYCN amplification** (poor prognosis), **ALK**, **ATRX** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1470 |
| **Wilms Tumor (Nephroblastoma)** | 2-5 years | Kidney | **Triphasic**: **Blastema**, **Epithelial** (tubules), **Stroma** | **WT1**, **CTNNB1**, **WTX**, **IGF2** LOH (11p15) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1469 |
| **Retinoblastoma** | <2 years | Retina | **Small round blue cells**, **Flexner-Wintersteiner rosettes**, **Fleurettes** | **RB1** (germline or somatic) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/retinoblastoma |
| **Hepatoblastoma** | <3 years | Liver | **Fetal**, **Embryonal**, **Mesenchymal**, **Teratoid** components | **CTNNB1**, **APC**, **IGF2** LOH (11p15) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1444 |
| **Rhabdomyosarcoma (Embryonal)** | <10 years | Head/neck, GU | **Small round/spindle cells**, **Rhabdomyoblasts**, **Cambium layer** | **PAX3/PAX7-FOXO1 negative**, **RAS** mutations | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |
| **Rhabdomyosarcoma (Alveolar)** | 10-25 years | Extremities, Trunk | **Alveolar pattern**, **Small round blue cells**, **Multinucleated giant cells** | **PAX3-FOXO1** (60%), **PAX7-FOXO1** (20%) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1471 |

## Sudden Infant Death Syndrome (SIDS)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Sudden unexpected death of infant <1 year that remains unexplained after complete autopsy, death scene investigation, and review of clinical history | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/sids |
| **Risk Factors** | **Prone sleeping**, **Maternal smoking**, **Soft bedding**, **Prematurity** | https://www.cdc.gov/sids/index.htm |
| **Autopsy Findings** | **Intrathoracic petechiae** (thymus, pleura, epicardium), Pulmonary congestion/edema, **No anatomic cause of death** | https://www.cdc.gov/sids/index.htm |
| **Triple Risk Model** | **Vulnerable infant** (genetic, prematurity) + **Critical developmental period** (2-4 months) + **Exogenous stressor** (prone sleep, infection) | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/sids |
