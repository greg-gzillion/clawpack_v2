# Pathology - Endocrine Pathology

## Thyroid Carcinoma
| Type | Frequency | Histology | IHC | Molecular | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|-----|-----------|-----|
| **Papillary** | 80-85% | **Papillae**, **Nuclear clearing** (Orphan Annie), **Nuclear grooves**, **Pseudoinclusions**, **Psammoma bodies** | **Tg+**, **TTF-1+**, **PAX8+**, CK19+, HBME-1+ | **BRAF V600E** (60%), **RET/PTC**, **RAS** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Follicular** | 10-15% | **Follicles**, **Capsular/vascular invasion** | **Tg+**, **TTF-1+**, **PAX8+** | **RAS**, **PAX8-PPARG** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Medullary** | 3-5% | **Nested**, **Spindle cells**, **Amyloid** | **Calcitonin+**, **CEA+**, **Chromogranin+**, **Synaptophysin+**, TTF-1+, Tg- | **RET** (germline or somatic) | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Anaplastic** | 1-2% | **Spindle**, **Giant cells**, **High mitotic rate** | **PAX8+** (often), **Tg-**, **TTF-1-**, **p53 mutant** | **TP53**, **BRAF**, **PIK3CA**, **TERT** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
