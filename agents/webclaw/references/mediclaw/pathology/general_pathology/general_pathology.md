## Types of Necrosis
| Type | Gross Appearance | Microscopic | Examples | URL |
|------|------------------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Coagulative** | Firm, Pale | **Preserved tissue architecture**, Loss of nuclei | Ischemic injury (heart, kidney, spleen) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK53449/ |
| **Liquefactive** | Soft, Liquid | **Digestion of tissue**, Cavitation | **Brain infarction**, Abscess | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK53449/ |
| **Caseous** | **"Cottage cheese"** | Amorphous granular debris, **Granulomatous inflammation** | **Tuberculosis**, Fungal infections | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK53449/ |
| **Fat Necrosis** | Chalky white | **Saponification** (calcium soaps), Ghost adipocytes | **Pancreatitis**, Trauma to breast | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK53449/ |
| **Fibrinoid Necrosis** | Bright eosinophilic | **Deposition of immune complexes + fibrin** | **Vasculitis**, Malignant hypertension | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK53449/ |
| **Gangrenous** | Black, Dry or wet | Coagulative necrosis + secondary infection | **Ischemic limb**, Bowel ischemia | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK53449/ |
