## Transfusion Reactions
| Reaction | Timing | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|----------|--------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Acute Hemolytic** | **Immediate** | **ABO incompatibility** | **STOP transfusion**, IVF, Diuretics, Supportive care | https://www.cdc.gov/nhsn/dialysis/transfusion-reaction/index.html |
| **Febrile Non-Hemolytic** | <4h | Cytokines from leukocytes | Acetaminophen, Rule out hemolysis | https://www.cdc.gov/nhsn/dialysis/transfusion-reaction/index.html |
| **Allergic (Urticarial)** | <4h | Plasma proteins | Antihistamines, Resume if mild | https://www.cdc.gov/nhsn/dialysis/transfusion-reaction/index.html |
| **Anaphylactic** | **Immediate** | **IgA deficiency** with anti-IgA | **STOP transfusion**, Epinephrine, IVF | https://www.cdc.gov/nhsn/dialysis/transfusion-reaction/index.html |
| **TRALI** | <6h | Donor anti-leukocyte antibodies | **STOP transfusion**, Oxygen, Supportive care | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/trali |
| **TACO** | <6h | **Volume overload** | **STOP transfusion**, Diuretics, Oxygen | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/taco |
| **Delayed Hemolytic** | 3-14 days | Anamnestic antibody response | Monitor, Supportive care | https://www.cdc.gov/nhsn/dialysis/transfusion-reaction/index.html |
