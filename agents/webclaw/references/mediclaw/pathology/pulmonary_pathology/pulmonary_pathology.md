# Pathology - Pulmonary Pathology

## Diffuse Alveolar Damage (DAD) / ARDS
| Phase | Timing | Microscopic | URL |
|-------|--------|-------------|-----|
| **Exudative** | 0-7 days | **Hyaline membranes**, Edema, Hemorrhage | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Proliferative** | 1-3 weeks | **Type II pneumocyte hyperplasia**, Fibroblast proliferation | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Fibrotic** | >3 weeks | **Interstitial fibrosis**, Honeycombing | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |

## Interstitial Lung Disease Patterns
| Pattern | Description | Associated Diseases | URL |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|-----|
| **UIP (Usual Interstitial Pneumonia)** | **Patchy fibrosis**, **Fibroblastic foci**, **Honeycombing**, Subpleural predominance | **IPF**, Connective tissue disease, Asbestosis | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/idiopathic-pulmonary-fibrosis |
| **NSIP (Nonspecific Interstitial Pneumonia)** | **Uniform fibrosis**, Lymphocytic inflammation | Connective tissue disease, Drug-induced, Idiopathic | https://www.lung.org/lung-health-diseases/lung-disease-lookup/nsip |
| **OP (Organizing Pneumonia)** | **Masson bodies** (fibroblastic plugs in alveoli) | Idiopathic (COP), Infection, Drug, CTD | https://www.lung.org/lung-health-diseases/lung-disease-lookup/cryptogenic-organizing-pneumonia |
| **DIP (Desquamative Interstitial Pneumonia)** | **Pigmented macrophages** filling alveoli | **Smoking-related** | https://www.lung.org/lung-health-diseases/lung-disease-lookup/dip |

## Lung Carcinoma
| Type | Frequency | Histology | IHC | Molecular | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|-----|-----------|-----|
| **Adenocarcinoma** | 40% | Glandular differentiation, **Lepidic**, Acinar, Papillary, Micropapillary, Solid | **TTF-1+**, **Napsin A+**, CK7+ | **EGFR**, **KRAS**, **ALK**, **ROS1**, **BRAF**, **MET**, **RET**, **NTRK** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1450 |
| **Squamous Cell Carcinoma** | 25-30% | **Keratinization**, **Intercellular bridges** | **p40+**, **CK5/6+**, TTF-1- | **PIK3CA**, **FGFR1**, **DDR2** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1450 |
| **Small Cell Carcinoma** | 15% | **Small blue cells**, **Nuclear molding**, **Crush artifact**, High mitotic rate | **Synaptophysin+**, **Chromogranin+**, **TTF-1+** (often), **Ki-67 >80%** | **TP53**, **RB1** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1462 |
| **Large Cell Neuroendocrine Carcinoma** | 3% | Large cells, Neuroendocrine morphology | Synaptophysin+, Chromogranin+ | **TP53**, **RB1** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1450 |
| **Carcinoid** | 1-2% | **Uniform cells**, **Salt-and-pepper chromatin**, No necrosis | Synaptophysin+, Chromogranin+, **Ki-67 <20%** | **MEN1** mutations | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1449 |
