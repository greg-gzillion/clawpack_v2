# Pathology - Microbiology

## Diagnostic Methods in Microbiology
| Method | Principle | Applications | URL |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-----|
| **Gram Stain** | Crystal violet, Iodine, Alcohol, Safranin | **Rapid identification** of bacteria (Gram-positive vs Gram-negative) | https://www.cdc.gov/labtraining/training-courses/basic-microbiology/gram-stain.html |
| **Culture** | Growth on selective/differential media | Definitive identification, Antimicrobial susceptibility | https://www.cdc.gov/labtraining/training-courses/basic-microbiology/culture-media.html |
| **MALDI-TOF MS** | Matrix-assisted laser desorption/ionization time-of-flight | **Rapid identification** of bacteria and fungi | https://www.asm.org/Articles/2021/February/MALDI-TOF-MS-in-the-Clinical-Microbiology-Lab |
| **PCR/NAAT** | Nucleic acid amplification | **Rapid detection** of pathogens (respiratory, STI, C. diff) | https://www.cdc.gov/labtraining/training-courses/basic-molecular-biology/index.html |
| **Antigen Detection** | Antibody-based detection | Rapid strep, Influenza, Legionella urine antigen | https://www.cdc.gov/labtraining/training-courses/basic-immunology/index.html |
| **Serology** | Detection of host antibodies | HIV, Hepatitis, Syphilis, Lyme | https://www.cdc.gov/labtraining/training-courses/basic-immunology/index.html |

|--------|-------------|-----|

## CLSI Breakpoints
| Term | Definition | URL |
|------|------------|-----|
| **Susceptible (S)** | High likelihood of therapeutic success | https://clsi.org/meetings/ast/ |
| **Intermediate (I)** | Uncertain therapeutic effect; May respond to higher doses | https://clsi.org/meetings/ast/ |
| **Resistant (R)** | High likelihood of therapeutic failure | https://clsi.org/meetings/ast/ |
| **MIC** | Minimum Inhibitory Concentration | https://clsi.org/meetings/ast/ |
