# Pathology - Renal Pathology

## Glomerular Disease Patterns
| Pattern | Light Microscopy | Immunofluorescence | EM | Associated Diseases | URL |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|----|---------------------|-----|
| **Minimal Change Disease** | Normal | Negative | **Foot process effacement** | Nephrotic syndrome (children) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/glomerular-disease |
| **FSGS** | **Segmental sclerosis** | IgM, C3 in sclerotic areas | Foot process effacement | Primary, Secondary (HIV, obesity, reflux) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/glomerular-disease |
| **Membranous Nephropathy** | **Thickened GBM**, **Spikes** (silver stain) | **Granular IgG**, C3 along GBM | **Subepithelial deposits** | Primary (anti-PLA2R), Secondary (SLE, drugs, cancer) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/glomerular-disease |
| **IgA Nephropathy** | **Mesangial expansion** | **Mesangial IgA**, C3 | Mesangial deposits | Most common GN worldwide; Synpharyngitic hematuria | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/iga-nephropathy |
| **Post-Streptococcal GN** | **Diffuse proliferation**, Neutrophils | **Granular IgG**, C3 | **Subepithelial "humps"** | 1-3 weeks after GAS infection | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/glomerular-disease |
| **Anti-GBM Disease** | **Crescents** | **Linear IgG** along GBM | No deposits | **Goodpasture syndrome** (renal + pulmonary) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/glomerular-disease |
| **ANCA Vasculitis** | **Crescents**, **Necrosis** | **Pauci-immune** (minimal or no deposits) | No deposits | GPA (c-ANCA/PR3), MPA/EGPA (p-ANCA/MPO) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/glomerular-disease |
| **Lupus Nephritis** | Variable (mesangial, proliferative, membranous) | **"Full house"** (IgG, IgA, IgM, C3, C1q) | Variable (subepithelial, subendothelial, mesangial) | **SLE** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/lupus-nephritis |
| **Diabetic Nephropathy** | **Mesangial expansion**, **Kimmelstiel-Wilson nodules**, **Hyaline arteriolosclerosis** | Linear IgG (non-specific) | **Thickened GBM** | Diabetes mellitus | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/diabetic-kidney-disease |
| **Amyloidosis** | **Amorphous eosinophilic material**, **Congo Red positive** (apple-green birefringence) | Light chains (AL) or SAA (AA) | **Random fibrils** (8-12 nm) | AL (primary), AA (secondary) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/amyloidosis |

## Renal Cell Carcinoma (RCC)
| Subtype | Histology | IHC | Genetics | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----|----------|-----|
| **Clear Cell RCC** | **Clear cytoplasm**, Nested growth, Delicate vasculature | **PAX8+**, **CAIX+** (diffuse), **CD10+**, CK7- | **VHL** (3p25) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1440 |
| **Papillary RCC** | **Papillary architecture**, Foam cells, Psammoma bodies | **PAX8+**, **CK7+**, **AMACR+**, CD10+ | **MET**, Trisomy 7/17 | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1440 |
| **Chromophobe RCC** | **Pale eosinophilic cytoplasm**, **Perinuclear halos**, **"Raisinoid" nuclei** | **PAX8+**, **CK7+**, **CD117+**, CAIX- | Multiple chromosomal losses | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1440 |
| **Oncocytoma** | **Dense eosinophilic cytoplasm**, **Round uniform nuclei**, Central scar | **PAX8+**, **CK7-**, **CD117+**, Hale colloidal iron+ | No significant alterations | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1440 |
