# Pathology - Breast Pathology

## Invasive Breast Carcinoma
| Subtype | Frequency | Histology | IHC | Molecular | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----|-----------|-----|
| **Invasive Ductal Carcinoma, NOS** | 70-80% | **Glandular**, Cords, Sheets | **ER/PR** (70%+), **HER2** (15-20%) | **PIK3CA**, **TP53**, **GATA3** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1419 |
| **Invasive Lobular Carcinoma** | 10-15% | **Single-file**, **Targetoid**, **Signet ring cells** | **ER/PR+** (95%), **HER2-**, **E-cadherin-** | **CDH1** (E-cadherin) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1419 |
| **Mucinous Carcinoma** | 2% | **Mucin pools**, Floating tumor cells | **ER/PR+**, HER2- | **PIK3CA** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1419 |
| **Tubular Carcinoma** | 2% | **Well-formed tubules**, **Low-grade nuclei** | **ER/PR+**, HER2- | **PIK3CA** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1419 |
| **Medullary Carcinoma** | <1% | **Syncytial growth**, **Lymphocytic infiltrate**, **High-grade nuclei** | **ER/PR-**, **HER2-**, **Basal-like** | **TP53**, **BRCA1** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1419 |

## Breast Biomarker Reporting (CAP/ASCO Guidelines)
| Biomarker | Testing Method | Reporting | Clinical Implication | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **ER** | IHC | **Positive**: =1% nuclei | **Endocrine therapy** (tamoxifen, AIs) | https://www.cap.org/protocols-and-guidelines/cap-guidelines/current-cap-guidelines/recommendations-for-ihc-testing-of-estrogen-and-progesterone-receptors |
| **PR** | IHC | **Positive**: =1% nuclei | Endocrine therapy (less predictive than ER) | https://www.cap.org/protocols-and-guidelines/cap-guidelines/current-cap-guidelines/recommendations-for-ihc-testing-of-estrogen-and-progesterone-receptors |
| **HER2** | IHC, ISH | **Positive**: IHC 3+ OR IHC 2+ with ISH amplified | **Anti-HER2 therapy** (trastuzumab, pertuzumab) | https://www.cap.org/protocols-and-guidelines/cap-guidelines/current-cap-guidelines/recommendations-for-human-epidermal-growth-factor-2-testing-in-breast-cancer |
| **Ki-67** | IHC | **High**: >20-30% | Prognostic; Guides chemotherapy decisions | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1419 |
