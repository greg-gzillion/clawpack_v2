# Pathology - Hepatobiliary Pathology

## Patterns of Liver Injury
| Pattern | Histology | Common Causes | URL |
|---------|-----------|---------------|-----|
| **Acute Hepatitis** | Lobular inflammation, Hepatocyte injury, Councilman bodies | **Viral hepatitis**, DILI, AIH | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **Chronic Hepatitis** | **Portal inflammation**, **Interface hepatitis** (piecemeal necrosis), Fibrosis | **Viral hepatitis B/C**, AIH, NAFLD | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **Steatosis** | **Macrovesicular** (large droplet, nucleus displaced) or **Microvesicular** (small droplets) | **NAFLD**, Alcohol, Drugs (amiodarone) | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/nafld |
| **Steatohepatitis (NASH)** | Steatosis + **Ballooning degeneration** + **Mallory-Denk bodies** + Inflammation + Fibrosis | **Metabolic syndrome**, Alcohol | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/nafld |
| **Cholestasis** | **Bile plugs** in canaliculi, **Feathery degeneration** | Biliary obstruction, DILI, Sepsis | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **Cirrhosis** | **Diffuse fibrosis + Regenerative nodules** | Any chronic liver disease | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |

## Alcoholic Liver Disease
| Stage | Histology | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Alcoholic Fatty Liver** | **Macrovesicular steatosis** (zone 3), Minimal inflammation | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |
| **Alcoholic Hepatitis** | Steatosis + **Ballooning degeneration**, **Mallory-Denk bodies**, **Satellitosis** (neutrophils around hepatocytes), **Cholestasis** | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/alcoholic-liver-disease |
| **Alcoholic Cirrhosis** | **Micronodular cirrhosis**, Eventually macronodular | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/alcoholic-liver-disease |
