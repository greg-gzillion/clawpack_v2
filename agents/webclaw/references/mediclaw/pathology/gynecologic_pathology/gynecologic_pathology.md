## Endometrial Carcinoma
| Type | Frequency | Histology | IHC | Molecular | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|-----|-----------|-----|
| **Endometrioid** | 80% | **Glandular**, Squamous differentiation | **ER/PR+**, **p53 wild-type**, PTEN loss | **PTEN**, **PIK3CA**, **ARID1A**, **MSI** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1473 |
| **Serous** | 10% | **Papillary**, **Psammoma bodies**, High-grade nuclei | **p53 mutant** (overexpression or null), **p16+**, ER/PR- | **TP53**, **PIK3CA**, **PPP2R1A** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1473 |
| **Clear Cell** | 2-4% | **Clear cells**, **Hobnail cells**, Hyaline globules | **Napsin A+**, **HNF1ﾟ+**, ER/PR- | **PIK3CA**, **ARID1A** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1473 |
| **Carcinosarcoma** | <5% | **Malignant epithelial + Mesenchymal components** | Variable (epithelial component determines) | **TP53** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1473 |
