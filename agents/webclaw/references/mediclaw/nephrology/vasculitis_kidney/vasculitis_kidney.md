# Nephrology - Vasculitis and Kidney

## ANCA-Associated Vasculitis (AAV)
| Disease | ANCA Pattern | Target Antigen | Renal Manifestations | Extrarenal Manifestations | URL |
|---------|--------------|----------------|----------------------|--------------------------|-----|
| **Granulomatosis with Polyangiitis (GPA)** | **c-ANCA** (90%) | **PR3** | **Necrotizing crescentic GN**, **Pauci-immune** | **Upper/lower respiratory tract** (sinusitis, Pulmonary nodules/cavities), **Nasal crusting/saddle nose**, **Otitis media** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Microscopic Polyangiitis (MPA)** | **p-ANCA** (70%) | **MPO** | **Necrotizing crescentic GN**, **Pauci-immune** | **Pulmonary hemorrhage** (alveolar hemorrhage), **Skin** (purpura), **Neuropathy** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Eosinophilic Granulomatosis with Polyangiitis (EGPA)** | **p-ANCA** (40-50%) | **MPO** | **Necrotizing crescentic GN** (less common than GPA/MPA) | **Asthma**, **Eosinophilia**, **Mononeuritis multiplex**, **Cardiac involvement**, **Skin** (purpura) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |

## ANCA-Associated Vasculitis - Diagnosis
| Test | Finding | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Urinalysis** | **Dysmorphic RBCs**, **RBC casts**, **Proteinuria** (usually subnephrotic) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Serology** | **c-ANCA/PR3** (GPA), **p-ANCA/MPO** (MPA, EGPA) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Kidney Biopsy** | **Pauci-immune necrotizing crescentic GN** (minimal or no immune deposits on IF) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Chest Imaging** | **Nodules/Cavities** (GPA), **Diffuse alveolar hemorrhage** (MPA, GPA) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |

## ANCA-Associated Vasculitis - Treatment
| Phase | Regimen | URL |
|-------|---------|-----|
| **Induction (First 3-6 months)** | **Cyclophosphamide** (IV pulse or oral) + **High-dose corticosteroids** OR **Rituximab** + **Corticosteroids** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Plasmapheresis** | **Severe RPGN** (Cr >5.7 or dialysis-dependent), **Diffuse alveolar hemorrhage** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Maintenance** | **Rituximab** (q6 months) or **Azathioprine** or **Methotrexate** + **Low-dose corticosteroids** (2-4 years) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |
| **Relapse** | Re-induce with **Rituximab** or **Cyclophosphamide** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/vasculitis |

## Anti-GBM Disease (Goodpasture Syndrome)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pathophysiology** | **Antibodies against type IV collagen** (a3 chain of GBM) ? **Linear IgG deposition** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/anti-gbm-disease |
| **Clinical Features** | **Rapidly progressive GN** + **Diffuse alveolar hemorrhage** (60-80%) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/anti-gbm-disease |
| **Diagnosis** | **Anti-GBM antibody** (serum), **Kidney biopsy** (linear IgG along GBM) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/anti-gbm-disease |
| **Treatment** | **Plasmapheresis** + **Cyclophosphamide** + **Corticosteroids** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/anti-gbm-disease |
| **Prognosis** | **Excellent if treated early**; **Poor if dialysis-dependent at presentation** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/anti-gbm-disease |

## IgA Vasculitis (Henoch-Sch霵lein Purpura)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pathophysiology** | **IgA immune complex deposition** in skin, GI, Kidney | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/iga-vasculitis |
| **Clinical Features** | **Palpable purpura** (lower extremities, Buttocks), **Arthralgias/arthritis**, **Abdominal pain**, **Hematuria** (synpharyngitic) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/iga-vasculitis |
| **Renal Manifestations** | **IgA nephropathy** (mesangial IgA deposits), Hematuria, Mild proteinuria | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/iga-vasculitis |
| **Treatment** | **Supportive** (most); **Corticosteroids** for severe GI or renal involvement | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/iga-vasculitis |

## Lupus Nephritis
| Class | Pathology | Clinical Features | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Class I** | Minimal mesangial | Normal UA, Normal GFR | No specific therapy | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/lupus-nephritis |
| **Class II** | Mesangial proliferative | Mild hematuria/proteinuria | RAAS blockade | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/lupus-nephritis |
| **Class III** | Focal proliferative (<50% glomeruli) | Hematuria, Proteinuria, 崇PGN | **Mycophenolate** or **Cyclophosphamide** + Corticosteroids | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/lupus-nephritis |
| **Class IV** | Diffuse proliferative (=50% glomeruli) | **Most common and severe**, RPGN, Nephritic syndrome | **Mycophenolate** or **Cyclophosphamide** + Corticosteroids | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/lupus-nephritis |
| **Class V** | Membranous | **Nephrotic syndrome** | RAAS blockade, **Mycophenolate** or **Calcineurin inhibitors** + Corticosteroids | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/lupus-nephritis |
| **Class VI** | Advanced sclerosing | **CKD/ESKD** | Prepare for RRT | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/lupus-nephritis |
