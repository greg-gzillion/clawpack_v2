# Nephrology - Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)

## AKI - Definition and Staging (KDIGO)
| Stage | Serum Creatinine | Urine Output | URL |
|-------|------------------|--------------|-----|
| **Stage 1** | 1.5-1.9x baseline OR =0.3 mg/dL increase | <0.5 mL/kg/h for 6-12 hours | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |
| **Stage 2** | 2.0-2.9x baseline | <0.5 mL/kg/h for =12 hours | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |
| **Stage 3** | =3.0x baseline OR =4.0 mg/dL OR Initiation of RRT | <0.3 mL/kg/h for =24 hours OR Anuria =12 hours | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |

## AKI - Classification by Etiology
| Category | Mechanism | Common Causes | URL |
|----------|-----------|---------------|-----|
| **Prerenal (Functional)** | Decreased renal perfusion | Hypovolemia, Hypotension, CHF, Cirrhosis, Renal artery stenosis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK553163/ |
| **Intrinsic (Structural)** | Damage to renal parenchyma | ATN, AIN, Glomerulonephritis, Vasculitis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK553163/ |
| **Postrenal (Obstructive)** | Urinary tract obstruction | BPH, Stones, Malignancy, Neurogenic bladder | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK553163/ |

## Prerenal AKI vs Acute Tubular Necrosis (ATN)
| Parameter | Prerenal AKI | ATN | URL |
|-----------|--------------|-----|-----|
| **BUN/Cr Ratio** | >20:1 | 10-15:1 | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK507815/ |
| **Urine Sodium (UNa)** | <20 mEq/L | >40 mEq/L | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK507815/ |
| **Fractional Excretion of Sodium (FeNa)** | <1% | >2% | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK507815/ |
| **Fractional Excretion of Urea (FeUrea)** | <35% | >50% (useful on diuretics) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK507815/ |
| **Urine Osmolality** | >500 mOsm/kg | <350 mOsm/kg | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK507815/ |
| **Urinalysis** | Bland or hyaline casts | **Muddy brown granular casts**, Tubular epithelial cells | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK507815/ |

## Intrinsic AKI - Causes
| Cause | Mechanism | Diagnostic Clues | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Acute Tubular Necrosis (ATN)** | Ischemic or nephrotoxic injury | Muddy brown casts, FeNa >2% | Supportive, Avoid nephrotoxins | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK507815/ |
| **Acute Interstitial Nephritis (AIN)** | Drug-induced hypersensitivity (NSAIDs, PCN, PPI) | Fever, Rash, Eosinophilia, **WBC casts**, Eosinophiluria | Stop offending drug, Corticosteroids | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK559182/ |
| **Glomerulonephritis** | Immune-mediated glomerular injury | **Dysmorphic RBCs**, **RBC casts**, Proteinuria | Immunosuppression, Plasmapheresis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Vasculitis** | Vessel wall inflammation | Systemic symptoms, **c-ANCA/PR3** (GPA), **p-ANCA/MPO** (MPA) | Immunosuppression, Plasmapheresis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Atheroembolic Disease** | Cholesterol emboli after vascular procedures | **Livedo reticularis**, **Blue toes**, **Eosinophilia**, **Hypocomplementemia** | Supportive, Avoid anticoagulation | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Thrombotic Microangiopathy** | TTP, HUS, aHUS | **Schistocytes**, Thrombocytopenia, Fever, Neurologic symptoms | PLEX (TTP), Eculizumab (aHUS) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |

## Postrenal AKI - Causes and Management
| Cause | Diagnosis | Management | URL |
|-------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH)** | Renal ultrasound (bilateral hydronephrosis), Post-void residual | Bladder catheterization, Alpha-blockers, 5-ARIs | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK558920/ |
| **Nephrolithiasis** | Non-contrast CT, Ultrasound | Medical expulsive therapy, Ureteroscopy, ESWL | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK559227/ |
| **Malignancy** | CT abdomen/pelvis | Ureteral stenting, Nephrostomy tube | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Retroperitoneal Fibrosis** | CT/MRI (periaortic soft tissue) | Corticosteroids, Ureterolysis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Neurogenic Bladder** | Urodynamics | Intermittent catheterization, Anticholinergics | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560617/ |

## AKI - Complications and Management
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Hyperkalemia** | Calcium gluconate (ECG changes), Insulin/glucose, Beta-agonists, Kayexalate, Dialysis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470284/ |
| **Metabolic Acidosis** | Sodium bicarbonate (if pH <7.2), Dialysis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482146/ |
| **Fluid Overload** | Diuretics, Fluid restriction, Dialysis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK553163/ |
| **Uremia** | Dialysis (pericarditis, Encephalopathy, Bleeding) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK553163/ |

## Contrast-Induced AKI
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Risk Factors** | CKD (eGFR <45), Diabetes, CHF, Hypotension, High contrast volume | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Contrast-Manual |
| **Prevention** | **IV fluids** (isotonic crystalloid), **Hold nephrotoxins**, **Minimize contrast volume** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Contrast-Manual |
