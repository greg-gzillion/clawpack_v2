## Acid-Base Disorder - Stepwise Approach
| Step | Assessment | Interpretation | URL |
|------|------------|----------------|-----|
| **1. pH** | <7.35 = Acidemia; >7.45 = Alkalemia | Primary disorder direction | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482146/ |
| **2. PaCO2** | High = Respiratory acidosis; Low = Respiratory alkalosis | Identify respiratory component | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482146/ |
| **3. HCO3** | Low = Metabolic acidosis; High = Metabolic alkalosis | Identify metabolic component | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482146/ |
| **4. Anion Gap** | >12 = High AG metabolic acidosis | **MUDPILES** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482146/ |
| **5. Delta Ratio** | <0.4 = Normal AG acidosis only; 0.4-0.8 = Mixed; 0.8-1.2 = High AG acidosis only; >2.0 = High AG + Metabolic alkalosis | Identify mixed disorders | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482146/ |
| **6. Compensation** | Compare expected vs. actual | Identify mixed disorders | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482146/ |

## Renal Tubular Acidosis (RTA)
| Type | Defect | Urine pH | Serum K+ | Causes | Treatment | URL |
|------|--------|----------|---------|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Type 1 (Distal)** | Impaired distal acidification | **>5.5** | **Low** or normal | **Sjren**, **SLE**, **Amphotericin B**, **Lithium** | **Alkali therapy** (1-2 mEq/kg/day) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK519044/ |
| **Type 2 (Proximal)** | Impaired HCO3 reabsorption | **<5.5** (can acidify), **>5.5** (when HCO3 > threshold) | **Low** | **Fanconi syndrome**, **Multiple myeloma**, **Acetazolamide**, **Tenofovir** | **Alkali therapy** (high doses, 10-15 mEq/kg/day) + **Thiazides** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK519044/ |
| **Type 4 (Hyperkalemic)** | **Hypoaldosteronism** or aldosterone resistance | **<5.5** | **High** | **Diabetes**, **ACE-I/ARB**, **NSAIDs**, **Trimethoprim**, **Calcineurin inhibitors** | **Treat hyperkalemia** (diuretics, K+ binders), **Fludrocortisone** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK519044/ |
