# Nephrology - Glomerular Diseases

## Glomerular Disease - Clinical Syndromes
| Syndrome | Key Features | Common Causes | URL |
|----------|--------------|---------------|-----|
| **Nephrotic Syndrome** | Proteinuria >3.5 g/day, Hypoalbuminemia, Edema, Hyperlipidemia | Minimal change disease, FSGS, Membranous nephropathy, Diabetic nephropathy, Amyloidosis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470444/ |
| **Nephritic Syndrome** | Hematuria, Hypertension, Oliguria, Azotemia, Mild-moderate proteinuria | Post-streptococcal GN, IgA nephropathy, Lupus nephritis, Anti-GBM disease, ANCA vasculitis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK562240/ |
| **Rapidly Progressive GN (RPGN)** | Crescent formation, Rapid decline in GFR (weeks-months) | Anti-GBM disease, ANCA vasculitis, Immune complex-mediated | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Asymptomatic Urinary Abnormalities** | Isolated hematuria or proteinuria | IgA nephropathy, Thin basement membrane disease, Alport syndrome | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |

## Nephrotic Syndrome - Primary Causes
| Disease | Pathology | Clinical Features | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Minimal Change Disease** | **Foot process effacement on EM**; Normal light microscopy | **Most common in children**, Abrupt onset, Often post-viral | **Corticosteroids** (high response rate); Calcineurin inhibitors, Rituximab for steroid-resistant | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560639/ |
| **Focal Segmental Glomerulosclerosis (FSGS)** | Segmental sclerosis in some glomeruli | **More common in Black patients**, Progressive, Poor response to steroids | Corticosteroids, Calcineurin inhibitors, **Consider genetic testing** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK532272/ |
| **Membranous Nephropathy** | **Thickened GBM**, **Subepithelial deposits**, **Spike and dome** on silver stain | **Most common cause in White adults**, Associated with malignancy, HBV, SLE, Drugs | **Anti-PLA2R antibody** (diagnostic/prognostic), **Rituximab**, Cyclophosphamide + steroids, CNIs | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK559241/ |
| **Membranoproliferative GN (MPGN)** | **Mesangial proliferation**, **Double contour of GBM** (tram-tracking) | Associated with HCV, Cryoglobulinemia, Complement dysregulation | Treat underlying cause; Immunosuppression for idiopathic | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **C3 Glomerulopathy** | **Dominant C3 deposition** on IF | Complement dysregulation (genetic or acquired) | Eculizumab (if C5 convertase dysregulation), Immunosuppression | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |

## Nephrotic Syndrome - Secondary Causes
| Cause | Key Features | Diagnosis | Treatment | URL |
|-------|--------------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Diabetic Nephropathy** | **Nodular glomerulosclerosis (Kimmelstiel-Wilson)**, Thickened GBM | Clinical (long-standing diabetes, Retinopathy) | **RAAS blockade**, SGLT2 inhibitors, Glycemic/BP control | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK534200/ |
| **Amyloidosis** | **Congo red stain** (apple-green birefringence), **AL** (lambda>kappa) or **AA** | Serum/urine immunofixation, Fat pad/bone marrow biopsy | **Treat underlying plasma cell dyscrasia** (AL) or inflammation (AA) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470285/ |
| **Lupus Nephritis** | **"Full house"** IF (IgG, IgA, IgM, C3, C1q) | ANA, Anti-dsDNA, Low complement | **Mycophenolate** or **Cyclophosphamide** + Corticosteroids | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |

## Nephritic Syndrome - Causes
| Disease | Pathology | Clinical Features | Serology | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Post-Streptococcal GN** | **Subepithelial "humps"**, Diffuse proliferation | 1-3 weeks after GAS pharyngitis/skin infection, **Low C3** (normalizes in 8 weeks) | **ASO**, Anti-DNase B | **Supportive**; No role for immunosuppression | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482255/ |
| **IgA Nephropathy** | **Mesangial IgA deposits** on IF | **Synpharyngitic hematuria** (1-2 days after URI), Most common GN worldwide | None | **RAAS blockade**, Corticosteroids (high-risk), SGLT2 inhibitors | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK538215/ |
| **IgA Vasculitis (HSP)** | IgA deposition in skin, GI, Kidney | **Palpable purpura**, Arthralgias, Abdominal pain, Hematuria | None | Supportive; Corticosteroids for severe GI/renal | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482410/ |
| **Anti-GBM Disease (Goodpasture)** | **Linear IgG along GBM** on IF | **RPGN + Diffuse alveolar hemorrhage** | **Anti-GBM antibody** | **Plasmapheresis** + **Cyclophosphamide** + **Corticosteroids** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482255/ |
| **ANCA-Associated Vasculitis** | **Pauci-immune** GN (minimal IF) | GPA (c-ANCA/PR3), MPA (p-ANCA/MPO), EGPA (p-ANCA/MPO, Asthma, Eosinophilia) | **c-ANCA/PR3**, **p-ANCA/MPO** | **Cyclophosphamide** or **Rituximab** + Corticosteroids | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |

## Glomerular Disease - Urinalysis Patterns
| Finding | Interpretation | URL |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Dysmorphic RBCs** | **Glomerular bleeding** (nephritic syndrome) | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urinalysis-urine-test |
| **RBC Casts** | **Glomerulonephritis** (pathognomonic) | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urinalysis-urine-test |
| **Waxy Casts** | **CKD**, Advanced renal failure | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urinalysis-urine-test |
| **Fatty Casts / Oval Fat Bodies** | **Nephrotic syndrome** ("Maltese cross" under polarized light) | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urinalysis-urine-test |
| **WBC Casts** | **Acute interstitial nephritis**, Pyelonephritis | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urinalysis-urine-test |
