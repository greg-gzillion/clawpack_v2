# Nephrology - Overview

## What is Nephrology?
Nephrology is the medical specialty focused on the diagnosis and management of kidney diseases, including disorders of kidney structure and function, fluid and electrolyte balance, acid-base homeostasis, and hypertension. It encompasses acute kidney injury, chronic kidney disease, glomerular diseases, tubular disorders, and end-stage kidney disease requiring dialysis or transplantation.

## Assessment of Kidney Function
| Test | Normal Range | Clinical Significance | URL |
|------|--------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Serum Creatinine** | 0.6-1.2 mg/dL (varies by muscle mass) | Marker of kidney function; Used to estimate GFR | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/serum-creatinine |
| **Estimated GFR (eGFR)** | =90 mL/min/1.73mｲ | Best overall index of kidney function | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/estimated-glomerular-filtration-rate-egfr |
| **Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN)** | 7-20 mg/dL | Affected by protein intake, GI bleeding, Volume status | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Urinalysis** | No protein, No blood, No casts | Screening for kidney disease | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urinalysis-urine-test |
| **Urine Protein/Creatinine Ratio** | <0.2 g/g | Quantifies proteinuria | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urine-protein-creatinine-ratio |
| **Urine Albumin/Creatinine Ratio** | <30 mg/g | Screening for diabetic nephropathy | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/urine-albumin-creatinine-ratio-uacr |
| **Renal Ultrasound** | Kidney size 9-12 cm, Normal echogenicity | Assesses structure, Obstruction, Cysts | https://www.radiologyinfo.org/en/info/us-renal |
