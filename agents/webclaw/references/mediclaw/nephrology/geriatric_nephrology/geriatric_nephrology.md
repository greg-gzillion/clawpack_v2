# Nephrology - Geriatric Nephrology

## Renal Aging
| Change | Clinical Implication | URL |
|--------|---------------------|-----|
| **Decreased GFR** | ~1 mL/min/year decline after age 40 | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Decreased renal blood flow** | Increased ischemic injury risk | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Impaired sodium conservation** | Hypovolemia risk with salt restriction, Diuretics | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Impaired free water excretion** | Hyponatremia risk | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Impaired potassium excretion** | Hyperkalemia risk with ACE-I, ARB, MRA | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Decreased renin and aldosterone** | Type IV RTA (hyperkalemic, Normal AG metabolic acidosis) | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Decreased vitamin D activation** | Osteoporosis, Secondary hyperparathyroidism | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |

## Estimating GFR in Older Adults
| Equation | Considerations | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **CKD-EPI (2021)** | **Race-free**; More accurate at higher GFR | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/estimated-glomerular-filtration-rate-egfr |
| **Cockcroft-Gault** | **Used for drug dosing**; Overestimates in frail/elderly | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/estimated-glomerular-filtration-rate-egfr |
| **Cystatin C** | **Less influenced by muscle mass**; Preferred in sarcopenia | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/estimated-glomerular-filtration-rate-egfr |

## CKD in Older Adults
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Prevalence** | 30-40% of adults =65 years | https://www.cdc.gov/kidneydisease/publications-resources/annual-report.html |
| **eGFR Decline** | May represent normal aging vs. true CKD; **Monitor for progression** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Albuminuria** | **Stronger predictor of outcomes** than eGFR alone | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/kidney-disease |
| **Blood Pressure Target** | **Individualize**; <140/90 mmHg for most; Avoid aggressive lowering in frail | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/high-blood-pressure |
| **Medication Management** | **Avoid nephrotoxins** (NSAIDs), **Dose adjust for GFR**, **Deprescribe when appropriate** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/safe-use-medicines-older-adults |

## Dialysis in Older Adults
| Component | Consideration | URL |
|-----------|---------------|-----|
| **Decision-Making** | **Shared decision-making**, **Consider quality of life**, **Functional status**, **Frailty**, **Comorbidities** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/dialysis-older-adults |
| **Conservative Kidney Management** | **Active management without dialysis**; Focus on symptom control, Quality of life | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/conservative-kidney-management |
| **Hemodialysis** | **AV fistula may not mature** in frail elderly; **Catheter use higher** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/dialysis-older-adults |
| **Peritoneal Dialysis** | **Home-based**, **Gentler**, **May be better tolerated** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/dialysis-older-adults |
| **Prognosis** | **1-year mortality 20-40%** after dialysis initiation in =75 years | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/dialysis-older-adults |

## Common Geriatric Nephrology Issues
| Issue | Management | URL |
|-------|------------|-----|
| **Polypharmacy** | **Review medications regularly**, **Deprescribe NSAIDs**, **Adjust doses for GFR** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/safe-use-medicines-older-adults |
| **Falls** | **Assess fall risk**, **Avoid medications causing orthostasis/dizziness** | https://www.cdc.gov/falls/index.html |
| **Cognitive Impairment** | **Affects medication adherence**, **Dietary compliance**, **Dialysis decision-making** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/memory-loss-and-forgetfulness |
| **Frailty** | **Assess frailty** (Fried criteria, Clinical Frailty Scale), **Individualize care** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/frailty |
| **Advance Care Planning** | **Discuss goals of care early**, **Complete advance directives** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/advance-care-planning |
