## HLA Matching and Crossmatch
| Test | Purpose | Interpretation | URL |
|------|---------|----------------|-----|
| **HLA Typing** | Identify HLA-A, B, C, DR, DQ antigens | **Better match = Improved graft survival** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |
| **CDC Crossmatch** | Detect preformed anti-donor HLA antibodies | **Positive = Contraindication** (hyperacute rejection risk) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |
| **Flow Cytometry Crossmatch** | More sensitive than CDC | **Positive = Increased rejection risk** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |
| **Virtual Crossmatch** | Compare recipient antibodies to donor HLA | **Predict compatibility** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |
| **Panel Reactive Antibody (PRA)** | % of population to which recipient has antibodies | **High PRA = Harder to find compatible donor** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |

## Kidney Transplant Rejection
| Type | Timing | Mechanism | Histology | Treatment | URL |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Hyperacute** | Minutes-hours | **Preformed anti-donor antibodies** | Hemorrhage, Thrombosis | **Irreversible** ? Graft removal | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |
| **Acute Cellular** | Days-weeks | **T cell-mediated** | **Lymphocytic infiltrate**, **Tubulitis** | **Corticosteroids**, **Thymoglobulin** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |
| **Acute Antibody-Mediated** | Days-weeks | **Donor-specific antibodies (DSA)** | **C4d deposition**, **Microvascular inflammation** | **Plasmapheresis**, **IVIG**, **Rituximab** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |
| **Chronic** | Months-years | Multifactorial | **Fibrosis**, **Graft vasculopathy** | **No effective treatment** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |

## Kidney Transplant - Complications
| Complication | Timing | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Delayed Graft Function (DGF)** | Immediate post-op | **Dialysis until recovery**, **Biopsy to rule out rejection** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |
| **Surgical Complications** | Early | **Bleeding**, **Thrombosis**, **Ureteral leak/obstruction**, **Lymphocele** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557641/ |
| **Infection** | 1-6 months | **CMV**, **BK virus**, **PJP**, **UTI**, **Wound infection** | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/index.html |
| **Cardiovascular Disease** | Long-term | **Leading cause of death with functioning graft**; **Aggressive risk factor management** | https://www.heart.org |
| **Post-Transplant Diabetes Mellitus (PTDM)** | Variable | **Tacrolimus > cyclosporine**, **Corticosteroids**; **Screen and treat** | https://www.diabetes.org |
| **Malignancy** | Long-term | **Skin cancer** (most common), **PTLD** (EBV-driven), **Kaposi sarcoma**; **Cancer screening** | https://www.cancer.gov |
