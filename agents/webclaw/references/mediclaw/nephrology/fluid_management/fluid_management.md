## Crystalloids - Clinical Use
| Fluid | Indications | Cautions | URL |
|-------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **0.9% NaCl** | Hypovolemia, Hyponatremia, Metabolic alkalosis | **Hyperchloremic metabolic acidosis**, Hyperkalemia risk | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Lactated Ringer's** | **Resuscitation** (sepsis, Trauma, Surgery), Burns | **Hyperkalemia** (caution in K+ >5), **Liver failure** (impaired lactate clearance) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Plasma-Lyte** | Resuscitation, Liver failure, DKA | More expensive | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **0.45% NaCl** | Maintenance fluid, Hypernatremia | **Hypotonic** (do NOT use for resuscitation) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **D5W** | Free water replacement (hypernatremia), Hypoglycemia | **Hypotonic**, Not for resuscitation | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
