## Hemodialysis (HD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Mechanism** | **Diffusion** (solute removal across semipermeable membrane), **Ultrafiltration** (fluid removal by hydrostatic pressure) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/kidney-failure/hemodialysis |
| **Vascular Access** | **Arteriovenous (AV) fistula** (preferred), **AV graft**, **Central venous catheter** (tunneled or non-tunneled) | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/hemodialysis-access |
| **Frequency** | **3 times weekly** (conventional), **4-6 times weekly** (short daily), **Nocturnal** (6-8 hours, 3-6 times weekly) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/kidney-failure/hemodialysis |
| **Dialysate Composition** | Na+ (135-145 mEq/L), K+ (0-4 mEq/L), Ca2+ (2.5-3.5 mEq/L), HCO3- (30-40 mEq/L) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Anticoagulation** | **Heparin** (most common), **Citrate** (regional), **No heparin** (high bleeding risk) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |

## Hemodialysis Access
| Access Type | Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|-------------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **AV Fistula** | **Lowest infection rate**, **Longest patency**, **Best outcomes** | **Long maturation time** (2-6 months), **Primary failure rate 20-30%** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/hemodialysis-access |
| **AV Graft** | **Shorter maturation** (2-4 weeks), **Easier cannulation** | **Higher infection and thrombosis rates**, **Shorter patency** (2-3 years) | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/hemodialysis-access |
| **Tunneled CVC** | **Immediate use**, **No need for venipuncture** | **Highest infection rate**, **Catheter-related thrombosis**, **Central vein stenosis** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/hemodialysis-access |
| **Non-Tunneled CVC** | **Emergency use** | **Short-term only** (<1-2 weeks), **High infection risk** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/hemodialysis-access |

## Hemodialysis Complications
| Complication | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|--------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Intradialytic Hypotension** | Rapid fluid removal, Autonomic dysfunction | **Decrease ultrafiltration rate**, **Cool dialysate**, **Midodrine**, **Increase dialysate Na+** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/dialysis-complications |
| **Muscle Cramps** | Volume contraction, Electrolyte shifts | **Reduce ultrafiltration**, **Stretching**, **Quinine** (limited), **Vitamin E** | https://www.kidney.org/kidney-topics/dialysis-complications |
| **Disequilibrium Syndrome** | Rapid osmolar shifts ? Cerebral edema | **Slow initial dialysis**, **Mannitol**, **Hypertonic saline** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Air Embolism** | Air entering venous circulation | **Clamp line**, **Left lateral Trendelenburg**, **100% O2**, **Hyperbaric oxygen** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Hemolysis** | Hypotonic dialysate, Overheated dialysate, Kinked line | **Stop dialysis**, **Supportive care**, **Check dialysate** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Anaphylaxis** | Ethylene oxide, AN69 membrane (ACE-I) | **Stop dialysis**, **Epinephrine**, **Antihistamines**, **Corticosteroids** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |

## Peritoneal Dialysis (PD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Mechanism** | **Diffusion** and **osmosis** across peritoneal membrane | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/kidney-failure/peritoneal-dialysis |
| **Types** | **Continuous Ambulatory PD (CAPD)** - manual exchanges 4-5x daily; **Automated PD (APD)** - cycler at night | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/kidney-failure/peritoneal-dialysis |
| **Catheter** | **Tenckhoff catheter** (tunneled, Dacron cuffs) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/kidney-failure/peritoneal-dialysis |
| **Dialysate** | Dextrose concentrations (1.5%, 2.5%, 4.25%), Icodextrin (glucose polymer) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Advantages** | **Preserves residual renal function**, **Home-based**, **Less hemodynamic stress**, **Fewer dietary restrictions** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/kidney-failure/peritoneal-dialysis |
| **Contraindications** | **Extensive abdominal adhesions**, **Ostomies**, **Abdominal wall hernias**, **Severe obesity**, **Poor personal hygiene** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/kidney-disease/kidney-failure/peritoneal-dialysis |

## Peritoneal Dialysis Complications
| Complication | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|--------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Peritonitis** | Catheter contamination, Exit-site infection | **Intraperitoneal antibiotics** (cefazolin + ceftazidime or gentamicin), **Catheter removal if refractory** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Exit-Site/Tunnel Infection** | Skin flora (S. aureus, Pseudomonas) | **Oral antibiotics**, **Catheter removal if refractory** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Catheter Malfunction** | **Outflow failure** (constipation, Catheter migration, Omental wrapping) | **Treat constipation**, **Catheter manipulation**, **Catheter replacement** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Encapsulating Peritoneal Sclerosis** | Long-term PD, Recurrent peritonitis | **Bowel obstruction**, **Weight loss**, **Stop PD**, **Tamoxifen**, **Surgery** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
| **Metabolic Complications** | **Glucose absorption** (hyperglycemia, Weight gain, Hypertriglyceridemia), **Hypokalemia**, **Protein loss** | **Dietary modification**, **Icodextrin**, **Lipid management** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482327/ |
