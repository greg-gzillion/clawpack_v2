## Nephron - Functional Unit
| Segment | Function | Key Transporters | URL |
|---------|----------|------------------|-----|
| **Glomerulus** | Filtration of plasma (180 L/day) | Fenestrated endothelium, GBM, Podocytes | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Proximal Tubule** | Reabsorption of 65% Na+, 65% H2O, 90% HCO3-, 100% glucose/AAs | NHE3, SGLT2, Aquaporin-1 | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Thin Descending Limb** | Water reabsorption (passive) | Aquaporin-1 | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Thin Ascending Limb** | NaCl reabsorption (passive) | CLC-K1 | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Thick Ascending Limb** | Reabsorption of 25% NaCl, Ca2+, Mg2+; Impermeable to water | **NKCC2** (target of loop diuretics), ROMK | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Distal Convoluted Tubule** | Reabsorption of 5% NaCl; Ca2+ reabsorption | **NCC** (target of thiazides), TRPM6 | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Connecting Tubule/Collecting Duct** | Na+ reabsorption, K+ secretion, Water reabsorption (ADH), Acid secretion | **ENaC** (target of amiloride), Aquaporin-2, H+-ATPase | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |

## Determinants of GFR
| Factor | Effect on GFR | Mechanism | URL |
|--------|---------------|-----------|-----|
| **Renal Plasma Flow** | Increased RPF ? Increased GFR | Increased glomerular capillary pressure | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Afferent Arteriolar Tone** | Afferent dilation ? Increased GFR | Prostaglandins, NO, ANP | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Efferent Arteriolar Tone** | Efferent constriction ? Increased GFR (initially) | **Angiotensin II** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Glomerular Capillary Pressure** | Increased Pgc ? Increased GFR | Determined by RPF and arteriolar tone | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Plasma Oncotic Pressure** | Increased pgc ? Decreased GFR | Opposes filtration | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
| **Bowman's Space Pressure** | Increased PBS ? Decreased GFR | Urinary obstruction | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK554544/ |
