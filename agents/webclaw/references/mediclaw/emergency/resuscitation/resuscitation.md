# Emergency Medicine - Resuscitation

## Cardiac Arrest Algorithms (AHA 2020)
| Rhythm | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| VF/pVT | Defibrillation (120-200J) -> CPR 2 min -> Epinephrine 1 mg q3-5 min -> Amiodarone 300 mg | https://cpr.heart.org |
| Asystole/PEA | CPR 2 min -> Epinephrine 1 mg q3-5 min -> Treat reversible causes (H's and T's) | https://cpr.heart.org |

## High-Quality CPR Components
| Component | Adult | Pediatric |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Rate | 100-120/min | 100-120/min |
| Depth | 2-2.4 inches (5-6 cm) | 1/3 AP diameter (~2 inches) |
| Compression:Ventilation | 30:2 | 30:2 (single); 15:2 (two rescuer) |
| Chest Recoil | Full recoil | Full recoil |
| Interruptions | <10 seconds | <10 seconds |

## Reversible Causes of Cardiac Arrest (H's and T's)
| H's | T's |
|-----|-----|
| Hypovolemia | Tension Pneumothorax |
| Hypoxia | Tamponade (Cardiac) |
| Hydrogen ion (Acidosis) | Toxins |
| Hyper/Hypokalemia | Thrombosis (Pulmonary) |
| Hypothermia | Thrombosis (Coronary) |
| Hypoglycemia | Trauma |

## Post-Cardiac Arrest Care
| Component | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Targeted Temperature Management (TTM) | 32-36C for 24 hours |
| MAP Goal | >65 mmHg |
| SpO2 Goal | 92-98% |
| PaCO2 Goal | 35-45 mmHg |
| Glucose Goal | 144-180 mg/dL |

## Shock Types and Management
| Shock Type | Hemodynamics | Initial Management |
|------------|--------------|---------------------|
| Hypovolemic | Low CVP, Low CO, High SVR | Fluid resuscitation; blood products |
| Cardiogenic | High CVP, Low CO, High SVR | Inotropes; revascularization |
| Distributive (Septic) | Low CVP, High CO, Low SVR | Antibiotics; vasopressors; fluids |
| Obstructive | High CVP, Low CO, High SVR | Treat cause (pericardiocentesis, thrombolytics) |

## Vasopressor Dosing in Shock
| Agent | Dose | Primary Effect |
|-------|------|----------------|
| Norepinephrine | 0.05-2 mcg/kg/min | Vasoconstriction |
| Epinephrine | 0.05-2 mcg/kg/min | Inotropy + Vasoconstriction |
| Vasopressin | 0.03-0.04 units/min | Vasoconstriction |
| Dopamine | 5-20 mcg/kg/min | Dose-dependent |
| Dobutamine | 2-20 mcg/kg/min | Inotropy |

## Massive Transfusion Protocol (MTP)
| Ratio | Components | Trigger |
|-------|------------|---------|
| 1:1:1 | PRBC : FFP : Platelets | Shock + active bleeding |
| Adjuncts | Tranexamic acid (TXA) 1g IV over 10 min then 1g over 8 hrs | Within 3 hours of injury |
| Calcium | Calcium gluconate 1-2g or Calcium chloride 1g | Citrate toxicity |
