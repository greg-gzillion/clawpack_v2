# Emergency Medicine - Scoring Systems and Clinical Decision Rules

## Cardiovascular Scores
| Score | Purpose | Components |
|-------|---------|------------|
| HEART Score | Chest pain risk stratification | History, ECG, Age, Risk factors, Troponin |
| TIMI Score | ACS risk | Age, Risk factors, ASA use, Angina, ST changes, Markers |
| GRACE Score | ACS mortality | Age, HR, SBP, Cr, CHF, ST changes, Markers, Arrest |
| Wells Score (PE) | PE probability | DVT signs, Alternative dx less likely, HR >100, Immobilization, Prior DVT/PE, Hemoptysis, Malignancy |
| Wells Score (DVT) | DVT probability | Cancer, Immobilization, Bedridden, Tenderness, Swelling, Collaterals, Prior DVT, Alt dx |
| CHADS2-VASc | Stroke risk in AFib | CHF, HTN, Age (65, 75), DM, Stroke/TIA, Vascular disease, Sex |
| HAS-BLED | Bleeding risk on anticoagulation | HTN, Renal/Liver disease, Stroke, Bleeding, Labile INR, Elderly, Drugs/Alcohol |

## HEART Score Interpretation
| Score | Risk | Management |
|-------|------|------------|
| 0-3 | Low (0.9-1.7% MACE) | Discharge |
| 4-6 | Intermediate (12-20% MACE) | Observation |
| 7-10 | High (50-65% MACE) | Admission |

## Wells Score for PE Interpretation
| Score | Risk | Management |
|-------|------|------------|
| 0-1 | Low | PERC rule; Consider d-dimer |
| 2-6 | Moderate | d-dimer; If positive -> CTA |
| >=7 | High | CTA |

## PERC Rule (Pulmonary Embolism Rule-out Criteria)
| Criterion (ALL must be met to rule out PE without d-dimer) |
|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| Age <50 |
| HR <100 |
| SpO2 >=95% |
| No hemoptysis |
| No estrogen use |
| No prior DVT/PE |
| No unilateral leg swelling |
| No surgery/trauma requiring admission in past 4 weeks |

## CURB-65 Interpretation
| Score | Mortality | Management |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| 0-1 | Low (<3%) | Outpatient |
| 2 | Intermediate (9%) | Inpatient |
| >=3 | High (15-40%) | ICU consideration |

## PECARN Head Trauma Rules (Pediatric)
| Age | Low Risk (No CT) |
|-----|------------------|
| <2 years | Normal mental status, No scalp hematoma, No LOC >5 sec, Non-severe mechanism, No palpable skull fracture, Acting normally per parent |
| >=2 years | Normal mental status, No LOC, No vomiting, Non-severe mechanism, No signs of basilar skull fracture, No severe headache |

## PECARN for Abdominal Trauma
| Low Risk (No CT) | High Risk (Consider CT) |
|------------------|------------------------|
| Normal GCS | GCS <14 |
| No abdominal tenderness | Abdominal tenderness |
| No thoracic wall trauma | Thoracic wall trauma |
| No vomiting | Vomiting |
| No decreased breath sounds | Decreased breath sounds |
