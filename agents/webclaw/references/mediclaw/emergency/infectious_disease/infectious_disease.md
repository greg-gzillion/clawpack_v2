# Emergency Medicine - Infectious Disease Emergencies

## Sepsis and Septic Shock
| Bundle | Timeframe | Components |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| 1-Hour Bundle | Within 1 hour | Measure lactate; Blood cultures; Antibiotics; 30 mL/kg crystalloid; Vasopressors |
| qSOFA Score | RR >=22, AMS, SBP <=100 | Screen for sepsis |

## Empiric Antibiotics by Source
| Source | Empiric Regimen |
|--------|-----------------|
| Community-Acquired Pneumonia | Ceftriaxone + Azithromycin |
| Healthcare-Associated Pneumonia | Piperacillin-tazobactam + Vancomycin |
| Intra-abdominal Infection | Piperacillin-tazobactam or Ceftriaxone + Metronidazole |
| UTI/Pyelonephritis | Ceftriaxone or Ciprofloxacin |
| Meningitis | Ceftriaxone 2g + Vancomycin + Dexamethasone |
| Neutropenic Fever | Cefepime or Piperacillin-tazobactam |
| Necrotizing Fasciitis | Piperacillin-tazobactam + Clindamycin + Vancomycin |

## Febrile Neutropenia
| Risk | Criteria | Management |
|------|----------|------------|
| Low Risk | MASCC Score >=21 | Oral antibiotics |
| High Risk | MASCC Score <21 | IV antibiotics |

## Tick-Borne Illnesses
| Disease | Treatment |
|---------|-----------|
| Lyme Disease | Doxycycline 100 mg BID x 10-21 days |
| Rocky Mountain Spotted Fever | **Doxycycline** (even in children) |
| Ehrlichiosis/Anaplasmosis | Doxycycline |
| Babesiosis | Atovaquone + Azithromycin |

## Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs)
| Condition | Treatment |
|-----------|-----------|
| Urethritis/Cervicitis | Ceftriaxone 500 mg IM + Doxycycline 100 mg BID x 7 days |
| Pelvic Inflammatory Disease | Ceftriaxone 1g IM + Doxycycline x 14 days + Metronidazole x 14 days |
| Syphilis | Penicillin G benzathine 2.4 million units IM x 1 |
| Genital Herpes | Acyclovir 400 mg TID x 7-10 days |

## HIV Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (PEP)
| Component | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Indication | Exposure within 72 hours |
| Regimen | Tenofovir DF + Emtricitabine + Raltegravir |
| Duration | 28 days |

## COVID-19
| Severity | Management |
|----------|------------|
| Mild | Supportive; Isolation |
| Moderate | Dexamethasone 6 mg daily; Remdesivir |
| Severe | Dexamethasone; Consider Tocilizumab |

## Influenza
| Presentation | Treatment |
|--------------|-----------|
| Uncomplicated | Supportive |
| High-Risk or Severe | Oseltamivir 75 mg BID x 5 days |
