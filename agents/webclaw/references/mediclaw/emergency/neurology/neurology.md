# Emergency Medicine - Neurologic Emergencies

## Acute Ischemic Stroke
| Time Window | Management |
|-------------|------------|
| <4.5 hours | IV tPA (alteplase) 0.9 mg/kg |
| 4.5-24 hours | CT Perfusion -> Mechanical thrombectomy if LVO |
| >24 hours | Antiplatelet therapy |

## tPA Eligibility Criteria
| Inclusion | Exclusion |
|-----------|-----------|
| Age >=18 | Intracranial hemorrhage on CT |
| Onset <4.5 hours | Recent surgery (<3 months) |
| | Platelets <100,000; INR >1.7 |
| | SBP >185 or DBP >110 |

## Large Vessel Occlusion (LVO) Screening
| Scale | Score Range | LVO Threshold |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| NIHSS | 0-42 | >=6 |
| RACE | 0-9 | >=5 |
| CPSS | 0-3 | >=2 |

## NIHSS Interpretation
| Score | Stroke Severity |
|-------|-----------------|
| 0 | No stroke symptoms |
| 1-4 | Minor stroke |
| 5-15 | Moderate stroke |
| 16-20 | Moderate to severe stroke |
| 21-42 | Severe stroke |

## Intracerebral Hemorrhage (ICH)
| Component | Management |
|-----------|------------|
| BP Control | Reduce SBP to 140-160 mmHg |
| Coagulopathy Reversal | Warfarin: 4-factor PCC + Vitamin K |
| Seizure Prophylaxis | Not recommended routinely |
| ICP Management | Mannitol/Hypertonic saline; EVD |

## Subarachnoid Hemorrhage (SAH)
| Component | Management |
|-----------|------------|
| Diagnosis | Non-contrast CT head; LP if CT negative |
| Nimodipine | 60 mg PO q4h x 21 days |
| Aneurysm Securement | Coiling vs clipping within 24-72 hrs |

## Seizures and Status Epilepticus
| Timeframe | Management |
|-----------|------------|
| 0-5 min | Lorazepam 4 mg IV or Midazolam 10 mg IM |
| 5-20 min | Levetiracetam 60 mg/kg or Fosphenytoin 20 PE/kg |
| 20-40 min | Second antiepileptic agent |
| >40 min | Propofol or Midazolam infusion; Continuous EEG |

## Meningitis
| Finding | Management |
|---------|------------|
| Suspected Bacterial | Ceftriaxone 2g + Vancomycin + Dexamethasone 10 mg IV |
| LP Contraindications | Coagulopathy; Mass effect; Infection at site |

## CSF Interpretation
| Condition | WBC | Glucose | Protein |
|-----------|-----|---------|---------|
| Normal | <5 | 2/3 serum | 15-45 |
| Bacterial | >1000 (PMN) | Low | High |
| Viral | 10-500 (Lymph) | Normal | Normal/Slight high |

## HINTS Exam for Vertigo
| Finding | Peripheral | Central (Stroke) |
|---------|------------|------------------|
| Head Impulse | Abnormal | Normal |
| Nystagmus | Unidirectional | Bidirectional |
| Test of Skew | No skew | Skew present |
