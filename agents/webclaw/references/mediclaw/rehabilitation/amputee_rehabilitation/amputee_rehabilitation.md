## Amputee Gait Deviations
| Deviation | Cause | Management | URL |
|-----------|-------|------------|-----|
| **Vaulting** | **Prosthesis too long**, **Weak hip flexors** | **Adjust prosthesis**, **Strengthen hip flexors** | https://www.amputee-coalition.org |
| **Circumduction** | **Prosthesis too long**, **Weak hip/knee flexors** | **Adjust prosthesis**, **Strengthen** | https://www.amputee-coalition.org |
| **Lateral Trunk Bending** | **Short prosthesis**, **Weak abductors**, **Pain** | **Adjust prosthesis**, **Strengthen abductors**, **Pain management** | https://www.amputee-coalition.org |
| **Terminal Impact** | **Inadequate knee flexion**, **Weak quadriceps** | **Gait training**, **Adjust knee unit** | https://www.amputee-coalition.org |
| **Foot Slap** | **Weak dorsiflexors**, **Inadequate plantarflexion resistance** | **Adjust foot**, **Strengthen** | https://www.amputee-coalition.org |
