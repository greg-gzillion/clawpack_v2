## Post-Operative Rehabilitation Protocols
| Procedure | Weight-Bearing | ROM Progression | Return to Sport | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|-----|
| **ACL Reconstruction** | **WBAT** with brace | Early full extension | **9-12 months** | https://www.aaos.org |
| **Rotator Cuff Repair** | - | **Passive only** x 4-6 weeks | **6-9 months** | https://www.aaos.org |
| **Total Hip Arthroplasty** | **WBAT** (posterior precautions) | No hip flexion >90° | **3-6 months** | https://www.aaos.org |
| **Total Knee Arthroplasty** | **WBAT** | Emphasis on extension | **3-6 months** | https://www.aaos.org |

## Common Orthopedic Conditions - Rehabilitation
| Condition | Key Interventions | URL |
|-----------|------------------|-----|
| **Low Back Pain** | **Core strengthening**, **Stretching** (hamstrings, Hip flexors), **Posture/body mechanics**, **Avoid bed rest** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/low-back-pain |
| **Rotator Cuff Tendinopathy** | **Eccentric strengthening**, **Scapular stabilization**, **Avoid overhead/painful activities** | https://www.aaos.org |
| **Lateral Epicondylitis** | **Eccentric wrist extension**, **Counterforce brace**, **Activity modification** | https://www.aaos.org |
| **Patellofemoral Pain** | **Quadriceps/hamstring strengthening**, **IT band stretching**, **Patellar taping** | https://www.aaos.org |
| **Achilles Tendinopathy** | **Eccentric heel drops**, **Activity modification** | https://www.aaos.org |
| **Ankle Sprain** | **RICE** (acute), **ROM**, **Strengthening** (peroneals), **Proprioception** (balance) | https://www.aaos.org |
