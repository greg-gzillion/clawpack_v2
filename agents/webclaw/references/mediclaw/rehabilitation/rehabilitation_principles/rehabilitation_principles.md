## Functional Assessment Tools
| Tool | Domain | Description | URL |
|------|--------|-------------|-----|
| **Functional Independence Measure (FIM)** | **ADLs**, **Mobility**, **Cognition** | 18 items, 7-point scale (1=total assist, 7=independent) | https://www.va.gov |
| **Barthel Index** | **ADLs**, **Mobility** | 10 items, 0-100 scale | https://www.sralab.org/rehabilitation-measures |
| **Modified Rankin Scale (mRS)** | **Global disability** | 0-6 scale (0=no symptoms, 6=dead) | https://www.stroke.org |
| **Berg Balance Scale** | **Balance** | 14 items, 0-56 scale (=45 = fall risk) | https://www.sralab.org/rehabilitation-measures |
| **6-Minute Walk Test** | **Functional capacity** | Distance walked in 6 minutes | https://www.thoracic.org |
| **Timed Up and Go (TUG)** | **Mobility**, **Fall risk** | Time to rise, Walk 3m, Return, Sit | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
