# Rehabilitation Medicine - Electrodiagnostic Medicine

## Electrodiagnostic Studies - Overview
| Study | Assesses | URL |
|-------|----------|-----|
| **Nerve Conduction Studies (NCS)** | **Peripheral nerve function** (amplitude, Latency, Conduction velocity) | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Electromyography (EMG)** | **Muscle electrical activity** (insertional, Spontaneous, Voluntary motor unit potentials) | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |
| **Repetitive Nerve Stimulation (RNS)** | **Neuromuscular junction** (decrement in MG, Increment in LEMS) | https://www.aanem.org/Patients/What-is-Electrodiagnostic-Medicine |

## Nerve Conduction Studies - Parameters
| Parameter | Definition | Abnormalities | URL |
|-----------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **Compound Muscle Action Potential (CMAP) Amplitude** | **Sum of muscle fiber action potentials** | **Axonal loss** (low) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Sensory Nerve Action Potential (SNAP) Amplitude** | **Sum of sensory nerve fiber action potentials** | **Axonal loss** (low) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Distal Latency** | **Time from stimulus to CMAP onset** | **Demyelination** (prolonged) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Conduction Velocity** | **Speed of nerve conduction** | **Demyelination** (slowed) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **F-Wave** | **Late response** assessing proximal nerve segments | **Demyelination** (prolonged/absent) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **H-Reflex** | **Electrical correlate of ankle reflex** (S1) | **S1 radiculopathy**, **Peripheral neuropathy** | https://www.aanem.org |

## Electromyography (EMG) - Findings
| Finding | Description | Associated Conditions | URL |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Fibrillation Potentials** | **Spontaneous single muscle fiber discharges** | **Active denervation** (acute neuropathy, Active radiculopathy, Myositis) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Positive Sharp Waves** | **Spontaneous single muscle fiber discharges** | **Active denervation** | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Fasciculation Potentials** | **Spontaneous motor unit discharges** | **Motor neuron disease** (ALS), **Chronic radiculopathy** | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Myokymic Discharges** | **Rhythmic bursts of motor units** | **Radiation plexopathy**, **Facial nerve** (MS, Brainstem glioma) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Myotonic Discharges** | **Waxing and waning** high-frequency discharges | **Myotonic dystrophy**, **Myotonia congenita** | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Complex Repetitive Discharges** | **Abrupt onset/offset**, **Uniform frequency** | **Chronic neurogenic or myopathic** conditions | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Large, Polyphasic MUPs** | **Increased amplitude and duration** | **Chronic denervation/reinnervation** (chronic radiculopathy, Motor neuron disease) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Small, Polyphasic MUPs** | **Decreased amplitude and duration** | **Myopathy** | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Early Recruitment** | **Increased number of MUPs for force generated** | **Myopathy** | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Decreased Recruitment** | **Decreased number of MUPs for force generated** | **Neurogenic** conditions | https://www.aanem.org |

## Common Electrodiagnostic Patterns
| Condition | NCS | EMG | URL |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| **Carpal Tunnel Syndrome** | **Prolonged median distal latency**, **Slowed median sensory CV** across wrist | **Neurogenic changes** in median-innervated muscles (thenar) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Ulnar Neuropathy at Elbow** | **Slowed ulnar motor CV** across elbow, **Conduction block** | **Neurogenic changes** in ulnar-innervated muscles | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Radiculopathy** | **Normal** (lesion proximal to DRG) | **Neurogenic changes** in myotomal distribution (paraspinals + limb) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Diabetic Neuropathy** | **Low SNAP/CNAP amplitudes**, Mild slowing | **Distal > proximal neurogenic changes** | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Guillain-Barré Syndrome** | **Demyelinating features** (prolonged latencies, Slowed CV, Conduction block, Temporal dispersion) | **Decreased recruitment**, **Fibrillations** (after 2-3 weeks) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Myasthenia Gravis** | **Normal** (single stimulus), **Decrement** on **RNS** (3 Hz) | **Normal** (may see variation in MUPs) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Lambert-Eaton Syndrome** | **Low CMAP amplitudes**, **Increment** on **RNS** (50 Hz) or **post-exercise** | **Small, Polyphasic MUPs** with **early recruitment** | https://www.aanem.org |
| **ALS** | **Low CMAP amplitudes** (late), Normal sensory | **Active and chronic denervation** in =3 regions (bulbar, Cervical, Thoracic, Lumbosacral) | https://www.aanem.org |
| **Myopathy** | **Normal or low CMAP amplitudes**, Normal sensory | **Small, Polyphasic MUPs**, **Early recruitment**, **Fibrillations** (inflammatory) | https://www.aanem.org |
