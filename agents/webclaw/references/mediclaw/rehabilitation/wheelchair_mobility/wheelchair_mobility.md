| Component | Considerations | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Frame** | **Folding** vs **Rigid** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
| **Weight** | **Standard** vs **Lightweight** vs **Ultralight** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
| **Seat Width** | **2 inches wider than hips** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
| **Seat Depth** | **2 inches less than popliteal fossa to posterior buttock** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
| **Seat Height** | **Allow 2 inches clearance under footrests** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
| **Back Height** | **Lower** (increased mobility) vs **Higher** (increased support) | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
| **Cushion** | **Pressure relief** (air, Gel, Foam), **Positioning** | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
| **Wheels/Tires** | **Mag wheels** (durable) vs **Spoke** (lighter), **Pneumatic** (shock absorption) vs **Solid** (maintenance-free) | https://www.apta.org/patient-care/interventions |
