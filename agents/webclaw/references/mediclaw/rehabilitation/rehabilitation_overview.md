# Rehabilitation Medicine - Overview

## What is Rehabilitation Medicine?
Rehabilitation Medicine, also known as Physiatry or Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation (PM&R), is the medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis, evaluation, and management of persons with disabling medical conditions. It focuses on restoring and maximizing functional ability and quality of life.
