## Upper Extremity Rehabilitation - Key Interventions
| Intervention | Description | Indications | URL |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Constraint-Induced Movement Therapy (CIMT)** | **Restraint of unaffected arm** + **Intensive training** of affected arm | **Chronic stroke** (some active movement) | https://www.aota.org/practice/domain-and-process |
| **Mirror Therapy** | **Mirror reflection of unaffected hand** creates visual illusion of affected hand movement | **Stroke**, **CRPS**, **Phantom limb pain** | https://www.aota.org/practice/domain-and-process |
| **Sensory Re-Education** | **Graded sensory stimulation** to improve sensory processing | **Peripheral nerve injury**, **Stroke**, **CRPS** | https://www.aota.org/practice/domain-and-process |
| **Graded Motor Imagery** | **Laterality training** ? **Imagined movements** ? **Mirror therapy** | **CRPS**, **Phantom limb pain**, **Chronic pain** | https://www.aota.org/practice/domain-and-process |
