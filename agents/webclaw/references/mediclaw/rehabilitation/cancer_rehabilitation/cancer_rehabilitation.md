# Rehabilitation Medicine - Cancer Rehabilitation

## Cancer Rehabilitation - Impairments and Interventions
| Impairment | Causes | Interventions | URL |
|------------|--------|---------------|-----|
| **Fatigue** | **Cancer**, **Treatment** (chemotherapy, Radiation) | **Energy conservation**, **Graded exercise**, **Psychostimulants** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship |
| **Pain** | **Tumor**, **Treatment**, **Post-surgical** | **PT/OT**, **Modalities**, **Medications**, **Interventional procedures** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/pain |
| **Weakness/Deconditioning** | **Cancer cachexia**, **Steroid myopathy**, **Immobility** | **Therapeutic exercise**, **Nutritional support** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/coping/survivorship |
| **Lymphedema** | **Lymph node dissection**, **Radiation** | **Complete decongestive therapy (CDT)** , **Compression**, **Exercise**, **Surgery** (LVA, VLNT) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/lymphedema |
| **Chemotherapy-Induced Peripheral Neuropathy (CIPN)** | **Platinum**, **Taxanes**, **Vinca alkaloids** | **Balance training**, **Gait training**, **Desensitization**, **Pain management** (gabapentinoids, Duloxetine) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/nerve-problems |
| **Cognitive Impairment ("Chemo Brain")** | **Chemotherapy**, **Radiation**, **Hormonal therapy** | **Cognitive rehabilitation**, **Compensatory strategies**, **Exercise** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/memory |
| **Radiation Fibrosis** | **Radiation therapy** | **PT** (ROM, Stretching), **Manual therapy**, **Modalities** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/radiation |
