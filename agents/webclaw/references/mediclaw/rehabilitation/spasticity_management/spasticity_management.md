# Rehabilitation Medicine - Spasticity Management

## Spasticity - Definition and Assessment
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Velocity-dependent increase in muscle tone** due to **UMN lesion** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spasticity |
| **Modified Ashworth Scale (MAS)** | 0 (no increase) to 4 (rigid in flexion/extension) | https://www.sralab.org/rehabilitation-measures |
| **Tardieu Scale** | Assesses **spasticity** at different velocities | https://www.sralab.org/rehabilitation-measures |

## Spasticity - Management Ladder
| Step | Intervention | Indications | URL |
|------|--------------|-------------|-----|
| **1. Non-Pharmacologic** | **Stretching**, **Splinting/Casting**, **Positioning** | **Mild spasticity** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spasticity |
| **2. Oral Medications** | **Baclofen**, **Tizanidine**, **Dantrolene**, **Gabapentin**, **Benzodiazepines** | **Generalized spasticity** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spasticity |
| **3. Focal Injections** | **Botulinum toxin**, **Phenol**, **Alcohol** | **Focal spasticity** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spasticity |
| **4. Intrathecal Baclofen** | **Implanted pump** delivers baclofen intrathecally | **Severe generalized spasticity** (SCI, MS, CP) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spasticity |
| **5. Surgical** | **Selective dorsal rhizotomy (SDR)** , **Tendon lengthening/transfer** | **Refractory spasticity** (CP) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spasticity |
