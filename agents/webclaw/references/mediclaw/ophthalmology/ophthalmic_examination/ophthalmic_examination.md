## Fundoscopic Examination
| Structure | Normal Appearance | Abnormal Findings | URL |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Optic Disc** | Sharp margins, Yellow-orange, Cup-to-disc ratio <0.5 | **Papilledema** (blurred margins, Elevated), **Glaucomatous cupping** (>0.7), **Pallor** (optic atrophy) | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/fundoscopic-examination |
| **Vessels** | Arterioles lighter, Narrower than veins | **AV nicking**, **Copper/silver wiring**, **Hemorrhages**, **Cotton-wool spots**, **Emboli** | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/fundoscopic-examination |
| **Macula** | Foveal light reflex, Avascular | **Drusen**, **Pigment changes**, **Hemorrhage**, **Edema**, **Exudates** | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/fundoscopic-examination |
| **Peripheral Retina** | Uniform orange-red | **Hemorrhages**, **Exudates**, **Retinal tears**, **Lattice degeneration** | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/fundoscopic-examination |

## Pupillary Examination
| Test | Technique | Normal | Abnormal | URL |
|------|-----------|--------|----------|-----|
| **Direct Response** | Light in eye ? Ipsilateral constriction | Brisk constriction | Sluggish or absent | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/pupil-examination |
| **Consensual Response** | Light in one eye ? Contralateral constriction | Brisk constriction | Sluggish or absent | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/pupil-examination |
| **Swinging Flashlight Test** | Alternate light between eyes | Both pupils remain constricted | **Afferent pupillary defect (APD)** : Pupil dilates when light swung to affected eye | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/pupil-examination |
| **Near Response** | Convergence on near target | **Miosis**, Accommodation, Convergence | Light-near dissociation (Argyll Robertson) | https://www.aao.org/eyenet/article/pupil-examination |
