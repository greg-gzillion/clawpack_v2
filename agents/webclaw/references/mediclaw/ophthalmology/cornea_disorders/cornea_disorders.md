# Ophthalmology - Cornea Disorders

## Corneal Infections (Keratitis)
| Type | Pathogens | Risk Factors | Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Bacterial Keratitis** | **S. aureus**, **P. aeruginosa** (contact lens), **S. pneumoniae**, **Moraxella** | **Contact lens wear**, Trauma, Ocular surface disease | **Fluoroquinolone** (ciprofloxacin, Moxifloxacin) or **Fortified antibiotics** (cefazolin + tobramycin) hourly | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/bacterial-keratitis |
| **Fungal Keratitis** | **Fusarium**, **Aspergillus** (filamentous); **Candida** (yeast) | **Trauma with vegetative matter**, Contact lens, Topical steroids | **Natamycin** (filamentous), **Voriconazole**, **Amphotericin B** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/fungal-keratitis |
| **Herpes Simplex Keratitis** | **HSV-1** (most), HSV-2 | Prior orolabial herpes, Immunosuppression | **Epithelial**: **Topical ganciclovir** or **Oral acyclovir/valacyclovir**; **Stromal**: **Topical steroids + Oral antiviral** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/herpes-keratitis |
| **Herpes Zoster Ophthalmicus** | **VZV** | Age >50, Immunosuppression | **Oral valacyclovir** (1g TID x 7-10 days); **Topical steroids** for keratitis/uveitis | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/herpes-zoster-ophthalmicus |
| **Acanthamoeba Keratitis** | **Acanthamoeba** | **Contact lens wear + Tap water exposure** | **Polyhexamethylene biguanide (PHMB)** + **Propamidine** or **Chlorhexidine** | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/acanthamoeba/index.html |

## Corneal Dystrophies
| Dystrophy | Layer | Pathology | Clinical Features | URL |
|-----------|-------|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| **Epithelial Basement Membrane Dystrophy (EBMD)** | Epithelium | Abnormal basement membrane | **Map-dot-fingerprint**, **Recurrent corneal erosions** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/corneal-dystrophies |
| **Reis-B𡡷klers Dystrophy** | Bowman layer | **TGFBI** mutation | **Reticular opacities**, **Recurrent erosions** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/corneal-dystrophies |
| **Granular Dystrophy** | Stroma | **TGFBI** mutation | **Discrete**, **Breadcrumb-like opacities** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/corneal-dystrophies |
| **Lattice Dystrophy** | Stroma | **TGFBI** mutation | **Branching**, **Refractile lines**; **Amyloid deposits** (Congo red+) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/corneal-dystrophies |
| **Macular Dystrophy** | Stroma | **CHST6** mutation | **Diffuse**, **Gray-white opacities**; **Thinnest cornea** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/corneal-dystrophies |
| **Fuchs Endothelial Dystrophy** | Endothelium | **Loss of endothelial cells**, **Guttae** | **Corneal edema**, **Decreased vision** (worse in morning), **Pain** from bullae | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/fuchs-dystrophy |

## Keratoconus
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Progressive, Bilateral, Asymmetric **corneal thinning and ectasia** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/keratoconus |
| **Risk Factors** | **Eye rubbing**, **Atopy**, **Down syndrome**, **Connective tissue disorders** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/keratoconus |
| **Clinical Features** | **Progressive myopia and astigmatism**, **Munson sign** (V-shaped bulging of lower lid on downgaze), **Fleischer ring** (iron deposition), **Vogt striae** (vertical stress lines) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/keratoconus |
| **Treatment (Mild-Moderate)** | **Glasses**, **Rigid gas permeable (RGP) contact lenses**, **Scleral contact lenses** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/keratoconus |
| **Treatment (Progressive)** | **Corneal cross-linking (CXL)** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/keratoconus |
| **Treatment (Advanced)** | **Deep anterior lamellar keratoplasty (DALK)** , **Penetrating keratoplasty (PKP)** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/keratoconus |

## Corneal Transplantation
| Procedure | Layers Replaced | Indications | URL |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Penetrating Keratoplasty (PKP)** | **Full-thickness** | **Full-thickness scars**, **Ectasias**, **Failed DALK/DSEK** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/corneal-transplantation |
| **Deep Anterior Lamellar Keratoplasty (DALK)** | **Stroma** (spares Descemet/endothelium) | **Keratoconus**, **Anterior stromal scars** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/corneal-transplantation |
| **Descemet Stripping Endothelial Keratoplasty (DSEK/DSAEK)** | **Endothelium + Descemet + Thin stroma** | **Fuchs dystrophy**, **Pseudophakic bullous keratopathy** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/corneal-transplantation |
| **Descemet Membrane Endothelial Keratoplasty (DMEK)** | **Endothelium + Descemet only** | **Fuchs dystrophy**, **Pseudophakic bullous keratopathy** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/corneal-transplantation |
