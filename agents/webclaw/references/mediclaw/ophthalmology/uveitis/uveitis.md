## Anterior Uveitis - Clinical Features
| Finding | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Symptoms** | **Pain**, **Photophobia**, **Redness**, **Blurred vision** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/anterior-uveitis |
| **Signs** | **Ciliary flush**, **Keratic precipitates** (mutton-fat = granulomatous), **Cell and flare** in anterior chamber, **Hypopyon** (severe), **Posterior synechiae**, **Miosis** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/anterior-uveitis |
| **Treatment** | **Topical corticosteroids** (prednisolone acetate 1%), **Cycloplegics** (cyclopentolate, Atropine) for pain and synechiae prevention | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/anterior-uveitis |

## Uveitis - Systemic Associations (Workup)
| Disease | Key Features | Laboratory/Imaging | URL |
|---------|--------------|---------------------|-----|
| **HLA-B27 Associated** | **Ankylosing spondylitis**, **Reactive arthritis**, **IBD**, **Psoriatic arthritis** | **HLA-B27**, Sacroiliac X-ray/MRI | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/uveitis-systemic-associations |
| **Sarcoidosis** | **Granulomatous uveitis** (mutton-fat KP, Iris nodules), **Elevated ACE/Lysozyme** | **Chest X-ray/CT**, **ACE**, **Lysozyme**, **Biopsy** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/uveitis-systemic-associations |
| **Beh蔒t Disease** | **Recurrent oral/genital ulcers**, **Hypopyon uveitis**, **Retinal vasculitis** | **Clinical criteria**, **HLA-B51** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/uveitis-systemic-associations |
| **Vogt-Koyanagi-Harada (VKH)** | **Bilateral granulomatous panuveitis**, **Exudative retinal detachments**, **Vitiligo**, **Poliosis**, **Alopecia**, **Hearing loss**, **Meningismus** | **Clinical**, **CSF pleocytosis**, **FA** (pinpoint leaks) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/uveitis-systemic-associations |
| **Toxoplasmosis** | **Focal necrotizing retinochoroiditis**, **"Headlight in fog"** on FA | **Serum anti-Toxoplasma IgG** | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/toxoplasmosis/index.html |
| **Syphilis** | **Can mimic any uveitis**; **Placoid chorioretinitis** (acute syphilitic posterior placoid chorioretinitis) | **RPR**, **Treponemal antibody** (FTA-ABS, TPPA) | https://www.cdc.gov/std/syphilis/index.htm |
| **Tuberculosis** | **Granulomatous uveitis**, **Choroidal tubercles**, **Serpiginous-like choroiditis** | **TST** or **IGRA**, **Chest X-ray** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/index.html |
| **Lyme Disease** | **Intermediate uveitis**, **Keratitis**, **Optic neuritis** | **Lyme serology** (ELISA + Western blot) | https://www.cdc.gov/lyme/index.html |

## Infectious Uveitis
| Pathogen | Clinical Features | Diagnosis | Treatment | URL |
|----------|-------------------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **HSV/VZV** | **Unilateral**, **Elevated IOP**, **Iris atrophy**, **Keratitis** | **PCR** (aqueous), Clinical | **Oral valacyclovir** (1g TID), **Topical steroids** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/herpetic-uveitis |
| **CMV** | **Immunocompromised** (HIV, Transplant); **Retinitis** (pizza-pie), **Anterior uveitis** (immunocompetent) | **PCR** (aqueous), **CMV PCR** (blood) | **Valganciclovir** (oral), **Ganciclovir** (IV/intravitreal) | https://www.cdc.gov/cmv/index.html |
| **Toxoplasmosis** | **Focal necrotizing retinochoroiditis**, **Reactivation** | **Serum IgG**, **Clinical** | **Pyrimethamine + Sulfadiazine + Leucovorin** + **Corticosteroids** (if vision-threatening) | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/toxoplasmosis/index.html |
