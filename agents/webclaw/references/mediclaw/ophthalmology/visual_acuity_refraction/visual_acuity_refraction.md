## Refractive Errors
| Type | Pathophysiology | Correction | URL |
|------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Myopia (Nearsightedness)** | Eye too long or cornea too steep; Image focused **in front of** retina | **Minus (-) lens** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/refractive-errors |
| **Hyperopia (Farsightedness)** | Eye too short or cornea too flat; Image focused **behind** retina | **Plus (+) lens** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/refractive-errors |
| **Astigmatism** | Irregular corneal or lens curvature; Multiple focal points | **Cylinder correction** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/refractive-errors |
| **Presbyopia** | Age-related loss of accommodation (lens stiffening) | **Plus (+) add** for near | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/refractive-errors |

## Refractive Surgery
