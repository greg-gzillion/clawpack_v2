# Ophthalmology - Glaucoma

## Glaucoma - Types
| Type | Mechanism | Angle | Clinical Features | URL |
|------|-----------|-------|-------------------|-----|
| **Primary Open-Angle Glaucoma (POAG)** | Impaired aqueous outflow through **trabecular meshwork** | **Open** | **Insidious**, **Asymptomatic**, **Progressive optic neuropathy**, **Arcuate scotomas** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |
| **Primary Angle-Closure Glaucoma (PACG)** | **Iris blocks trabecular meshwork** | **Narrow/Closed** | **Acute**: Pain, Headache, N/V, Halos, Mid-dilated fixed pupil, Cloudy cornea, Elevated IOP (>40-50); **Chronic**: Asymptomatic, PAS | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |
| **Normal-Tension Glaucoma** | Optic neuropathy with **IOP <21 mmHg** | Open | **Vasospastic** (migraine, Raynaud), **Disc hemorrhages**, **Nocturnal hypotension** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |
| **Pseudoexfoliation Glaucoma** | **Fibrillar material** on lens/anterior segment | Open | **Elevated IOP**, **Phacodonesis**, **Zonular weakness** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |
| **Pigmentary Glaucoma** | **Pigment dispersion** from iris | Open | **Young myopic males**, **Krukenberg spindle**, **Transillumination defects**, IOP spikes after exercise/dilation | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |
| **Uveitic Glaucoma** | Inflammation ? Trabecular meshwork damage, PAS | Open or Closed | **Elevated IOP**, **Keratic precipitates**, **Synechiae** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |
| **Neovascular Glaucoma** | **Retinal ischemia** ? **NVI/NVA** ? **Fibrovascular membrane** ? **Angle closure** | Closed | **Pain**, **Elevated IOP**, **NVI**, **NVA** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |
| **Steroid-Induced Glaucoma** | **Increased trabecular meshwork resistance** | Open | Elevated IOP weeks-years after steroid use (topical > systemic) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma |

## Acute Angle-Closure Glaucoma - Management
| Phase | Intervention | URL |
|-------|--------------|-----|
| **Immediate** | **Lower IOP**: **Timolol 0.5%**, **Apraclonidine 1%**, **Brimonidine 0.2%**, **Pilocarpine 2%** (after IOP starts to decrease), **Acetazolamide 500 mg IV/PO**, **Mannitol 1-2 g/kg IV** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/acute-angle-closure-glaucoma |
| **Definitive** | **Laser peripheral iridotomy (LPI)** or **Laser iridoplasty** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/acute-angle-closure-glaucoma |
| **Prophylaxis** | **Prophylactic LPI** in fellow eye | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/acute-angle-closure-glaucoma |

## Glaucoma - Medical Therapy
| Drug Class | Examples | Mechanism | Adverse Effects | URL |
|------------|----------|-----------|-----------------|-----|
| **Prostaglandin Analogs** | **Latanoprost**, Bimatoprost, Travoprost, Tafluprost | **Increase uveoscleral outflow** | **Hyperemia**, **Iris darkening**, **Eyelash growth**, **Periorbital fat atrophy** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma/treatment |
| **Beta-Blockers** | **Timolol**, Betaxolol, Levobunolol | **Decrease aqueous production** | **Bradycardia**, **Hypotension**, **Bronchospasm**, **Depression**, **Impotence** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma/treatment |
| **Alpha-2 Agonists** | **Brimonidine**, Apraclonidine | **Decrease aqueous production**, **Increase uveoscleral outflow** | **Allergy** (brimonidine), **Fatigue**, **Dry mouth** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma/treatment |
| **Carbonic Anhydrase Inhibitors** | **Dorzolamide**, Brinzolamide (topical); **Acetazolamide**, Methazolamide (oral) | **Decrease aqueous production** | **Bitter taste**, **Blurred vision**; Oral: **Paresthesias**, **Metabolic acidosis**, **Kidney stones**, **Aplastic anemia** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma/treatment |
| **Rho Kinase Inhibitors** | **Netarsudil** | **Increase trabecular outflow**, **Decrease episcleral venous pressure** | **Hyperemia**, **Corneal verticillata** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma/treatment |
| **Miotics** | **Pilocarpine** | **Increase trabecular outflow** (contraction of ciliary muscle) | **Miosis**, **Brow ache**, **Retinal detachment** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/glaucoma/treatment |

## Glaucoma - Laser and Surgical Therapy
| Procedure | Mechanism | Indications | URL |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT)** | **Increases trabecular outflow** | **POAG**, **Pseudoexfoliation**, **Pigmentary glaucoma** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/selective-laser-trabeculoplasty |
| **Laser Peripheral Iridotomy (LPI)** | **Relieves pupillary block** | **Angle-closure glaucoma**, **Anatomic narrow angles** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/laser-peripheral-iridotomy |
| **Trabeculectomy** | **Filtration surgery** (subconjunctival bleb) | **Medically uncontrolled glaucoma** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/trabeculectomy |
| **Glaucoma Drainage Device (Tube Shunt)** | **Aqueous drainage to equatorial plate** | **Refractory glaucoma**, **Failed trabeculectomy**, **Neovascular glaucoma**, **Uveitic glaucoma** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/glaucoma-drainage-implants |
| **Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgery (MIGS)** | **Bypass trabecular meshwork**, **Increase outflow**, **Decrease aqueous production** | **Mild-moderate glaucoma** undergoing cataract surgery | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/migs |
| **Cyclophotocoagulation** | **Laser ablation of ciliary body** ? **Decreased aqueous production** | **Refractory glaucoma**, **Poor vision**, **Painful blind eye** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/treatments/cyclophotocoagulation |
