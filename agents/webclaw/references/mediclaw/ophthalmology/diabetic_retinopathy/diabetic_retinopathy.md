# Ophthalmology - Diabetic Retinopathy (DR)

## DR - Classification
| Stage | Clinical Features | Management | URL |
|-------|-------------------|------------|-----|
| **No DR** | Normal fundus | **Annual screening** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy |
| **Mild NPDR** | **Microaneurysms only** | **Annual screening**, **Glycemic control**, **BP control** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy |
| **Moderate NPDR** | Microaneurysms + **Dot-blot hemorrhages**, **Hard exudates**, **Cotton-wool spots** (but less than severe) | **6-12 month follow-up**, **Glycemic/BP control** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy |
| **Severe NPDR** | **4-2-1 Rule**: Hemorrhages in **4 quadrants** OR **Venous beading** in **2 quadrants** OR **IRMA** in **1 quadrant** | **3-6 month follow-up**, **Consider PRP** (if poor follow-up or high-risk) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy |
| **Proliferative DR (PDR)** | **Neovascularization** of disc (NVD) or elsewhere (NVE), **Vitreous hemorrhage**, **Tractional RD** | **Panretinal photocoagulation (PRP)** , **Anti-VEGF injections**, **Vitrectomy** (if VH or TRD) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy |

## Diabetic Macular Edema (DME)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Retinal thickening** within **1 disc diameter of fovea** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy |
| **Diagnosis** | **OCT** (retinal thickening, Cystoid spaces), **FA** (leakage) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy |
| **Treatment (Center-Involving)** | **Anti-VEGF injections** (aflibercept, Ranibizumab, Bevacizumab) - first-line; **Focal laser** (non-center involving); **Intravitreal steroids** (dexamethasone implant, Fluocinolone implant) - second-line | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/diabetic-retinopathy/treatment |

## DR - Systemic Management
| Component | Target | URL |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **Glycemic Control** | **HbA1c <7.0%** (individualize) | https://www.diabetes.org/diabetes/complications/eye-complications |
| **Blood Pressure** | **<130/80 mmHg** | https://www.diabetes.org/diabetes/complications/eye-complications |
| **Lipids** | **Statin therapy** for ASCVD risk | https://www.diabetes.org/diabetes/complications/eye-complications |
| **Smoking Cessation** | **Strongly recommended** | https://www.diabetes.org/diabetes/complications/eye-complications |
| **Pregnancy** | **Worsening of DR common**; **Monthly exams** during pregnancy | https://www.diabetes.org/diabetes/complications/eye-complications |
