# Ophthalmology - Retinal Vascular Diseases

## Retinal Artery Occlusion
| Type | Clinical Features | Systemic Workup | Management | URL |
|------|-------------------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Central Retinal Artery Occlusion (CRAO)** | **Sudden**, **Painless**, **Severe monocular vision loss**, **Afferent pupillary defect**, **Cherry-red spot**, **Boxcarring** of vessels | **Stroke workup** (carotid ultrasound, Echo, ECG/Holter, ESR/CRP for GCA if >50) | **Ocular massage**, **Lower IOP** (timolol, Acetazolamide, Paracentesis), **Hyperbaric oxygen**, **Treat underlying cause** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/retinal-artery-occlusion |
| **Branch Retinal Artery Occlusion (BRAO)** | **Sudden**, **Sectoral vision loss**, **Retinal whitening** along artery | **Stroke workup** (carotid, Cardiac) | Same as CRAO (less urgent) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/retinal-artery-occlusion |

## Retinal Vein Occlusion
| Type | Clinical Features | Complications | Management | URL |
|------|-------------------|---------------|------------|-----|
| **Central Retinal Vein Occlusion (CRVO)** | **Sudden**, **Painless vision loss**, **"Blood and thunder"** fundus (diffuse hemorrhages, Tortuous veins, Cotton-wool spots) | **Macular edema**, **Neovascularization** (iris, Angle), **Neovascular glaucoma** | **Anti-VEGF** for macular edema, **PRP** if neovascularization, **Treat risk factors** (HTN, DM, Hyperlipidemia) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/retinal-vein-occlusion |
| **Branch Retinal Vein Occlusion (BRVO)** | **Sectoral vision loss**, **Hemorrhages in wedge-shaped distribution** along vein | **Macular edema**, **Neovascularization** | **Anti-VEGF** for macular edema, **Focal laser**, **Treat risk factors** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/retinal-vein-occlusion |

## Ocular Ischemic Syndrome
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Chronic ocular hypoperfusion** due to **severe carotid artery stenosis** (>90%) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/ocular-ischemic-syndrome |
| **Clinical Features** | **Gradual vision loss**, **Prolonged retinal arterial filling**, **Mid-peripheral hemorrhages**, **Neovascularization** (iris, Angle), **Neovascular glaucoma**, **Ocular pain** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/ocular-ischemic-syndrome |
| **Diagnosis** | **Carotid ultrasound**, **CTA/MRA**, **Fluorescein angiography** (delayed filling, Prolonged AV transit) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/ocular-ischemic-syndrome |
| **Management** | **Carotid endarterectomy/stenting**, **PRP** if neovascularization, **Anti-VEGF** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/ocular-ischemic-syndrome |
