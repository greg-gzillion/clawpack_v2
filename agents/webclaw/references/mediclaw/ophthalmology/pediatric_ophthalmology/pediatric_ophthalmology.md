# Ophthalmology - Pediatric Ophthalmology

## Amblyopia
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Unilateral or bilateral decreased vision** due to abnormal visual development | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/amblyopia |
| **Types** | **Strabismic** (misalignment), **Refractive** (anisometropia, High bilateral refractive error), **Deprivation** (cataract, Ptosis, Corneal opacity) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/amblyopia |
| **Treatment** | **Correct refractive error** (glasses), **Patching** (occlusion therapy), **Atropine penalization**, **Treat underlying cause** (cataract surgery, Strabismus surgery) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/amblyopia/treatment |

## Retinopathy of Prematurity (ROP)
| Stage | Findings | Management | URL |
|-------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Stage 1** | **Demarcation line** | **Observation** (weekly exams) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/retinopathy-prematurity |
| **Stage 2** | **Ridge** | Observation | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/retinopathy-prematurity |
| **Stage 3** | **Ridge with extraretinal fibrovascular proliferation** | **Laser photocoagulation** or **Anti-VEGF** if threshold disease | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/retinopathy-prematurity |
| **Stage 4** | **Partial retinal detachment** | **Vitrectomy** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/retinopathy-prematurity |
| **Stage 5** | **Total retinal detachment** | Vitrectomy (poor prognosis) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/retinopathy-prematurity |
| **Plus Disease** | **Tortuosity and dilation** of posterior pole vessels | **Laser or Anti-VEGF** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/retinopathy-prematurity |

## Congenital Glaucoma
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Triad** | **Epiphora** (tearing), **Photophobia**, **Blepharospasm** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/congenital-glaucoma |
| **Signs** | **Buphthalmos** (enlarged eye), **Corneal edema**, **Haab striae** (breaks in Descemet membrane), **Elevated IOP**, **Optic nerve cupping** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/congenital-glaucoma |
| **Treatment** | **Surgery** (goniotomy, Trabeculotomy, Trabeculectomy, Tube shunt); **Medical therapy** as temporizing measure | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/congenital-glaucoma |
