# Ophthalmology - Sclera Disorders

## Episcleritis vs Scleritis
| Feature | Episcleritis | Scleritis | URL |
|---------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Pain** | **Mild**, Gritty | **Severe**, Boring, Radiates to face, Wakes from sleep | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Tenderness** | Minimal | **Marked** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Injection** | **Sectoral**, Blanches with phenylephrine | **Deep**, Violaceous, Does NOT blanch | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Vision** | Normal | **May be decreased** (keratitis, Uveitis, Glaucoma) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Systemic Association** | 30% (usually mild) | **50%** (often severe connective tissue disease) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Treatment** | **Topical NSAIDs**, **Artificial tears**; Self-limited | **Oral NSAIDs**, **Oral corticosteroids**, **Immunosuppressants** (methotrexate, Mycophenolate, Biologics) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |

## Scleritis - Types
| Type | Clinical Features | Systemic Associations | URL |
|------|-------------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Diffuse Anterior** | **Most common** (60%); Widespread inflammation | **Rheumatoid arthritis** (most common), ANCA vasculitis | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Nodular Anterior** | Tender, Immobile nodule | **Rheumatoid arthritis**, ANCA vasculitis | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Necrotizing Anterior with Inflammation** | **Most severe**; Avascular patches, Scleral thinning, **High risk of perforation** | **Rheumatoid arthritis**, ANCA vasculitis, Relapsing polychondritis | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Necrotizing Anterior without Inflammation (Scleromalacia Perforans)** | **Painless**, Progressive scleral thinning, Exposure of uvea | **Long-standing rheumatoid arthritis** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
| **Posterior Scleritis** | **Pain**, **Decreased vision**, **Serous retinal detachment**, **Choroidal folds**, **Proptosis** | **ANCA vasculitis**, Rheumatoid arthritis | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/episcleritis-scleritis |
