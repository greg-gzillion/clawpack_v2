# Ophthalmology - Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD)

## AMD - Types
| Type | Pathophysiology | Clinical Features | URL |
|------|-----------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Dry AMD (Non-Exudative)** | **Drusen**, **RPE changes**, **Geographic atrophy** (advanced) | **Gradual central vision loss**, **Metamorphopsia**, **Scotoma** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration |
| **Wet AMD (Exudative/Neovascular)** | **Choroidal neovascularization (CNV)** ? **Subretinal fluid/hemorrhage** | **Sudden/rapid central vision loss**, **Metamorphopsia**, **Scotoma** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration |

## Dry AMD - Management
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **AREDS2 Vitamins** | **Vitamin C** (500 mg), **Vitamin E** (400 IU), **Zinc** (80 mg), **Copper** (2 mg), **Lutein** (10 mg), **Zeaxanthin** (2 mg) for **intermediate AMD** or **advanced AMD in one eye** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/research/clinical-trials/areds2 |
| **Smoking Cessation** | **Strongly recommended** (2-4x increased risk of progression) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration |
| **Monitoring** | **Amsler grid** home monitoring, **Regular dilated eye exams** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration |
| **Geographic Atrophy** | **Pegcetacoplan** (C3 inhibitor) - intravitreal injection | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |

## Wet AMD - Treatment
| Therapy | Mechanism | Regimen | URL |
|---------|-----------|---------|-----|
| **Ranibizumab (Lucentis)** | **Anti-VEGF-A** (antibody fragment) | Monthly or Treat-and-Extend | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration/treatment |
| **Aflibercept (Eylea)** | **VEGF Trap** (binds VEGF-A, VEGF-B, PlGF) | q8 weeks after loading | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration/treatment |
| **Bevacizumab (Avastin)** | **Anti-VEGF-A** (full antibody) | Monthly or Treat-and-Extend (off-label) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration/treatment |
| **Faricimab (Vabysmo)** | **Anti-VEGF-A + Anti-Ang-2** | q8-16 weeks after loading | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Brolucizumab (Beovu)** | **Anti-VEGF-A** (single-chain antibody fragment) | q8-12 weeks after loading | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Photodynamic Therapy (PDT)** | **Verteporfin** + **Laser** | For **classic CNV** not responding to anti-VEGF | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/age-related-macular-degeneration/treatment |

## AMD - Imaging
| Modality | Findings | URL |
|----------|----------|-----|
| **Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)** | **Drusen**, **RPE changes**, **Subretinal fluid**, **Intraretinal fluid**, **CNV**, **Geographic atrophy** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/amd-diagnosis |
| **Fluorescein Angiography (FA)** | **Classic CNV** (early hyperfluorescence, Late leakage), **Occult CNV** (stippled hyperfluorescence, Late leakage), **RPE tear** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/amd-diagnosis |
