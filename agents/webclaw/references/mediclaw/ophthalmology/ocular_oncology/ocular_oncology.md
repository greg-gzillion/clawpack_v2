# Ophthalmology - Ocular Oncology

## Intraocular Tumors
| Tumor | Location | Malignant Potential | Treatment | URL |
|-------|----------|---------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Choroidal Nevus** | Choroid | **Benign**; <1% transformation to melanoma | **Observation** (photo documentation, OCT, Autofluorescence) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/choroidal-nevus |
| **Choroidal Melanoma** | Choroid | **Malignant**; Metastatic potential | **Plaque brachytherapy** (I-125, Ru-106), **Proton beam**, **Enucleation** (large tumors), **Transpupillary thermotherapy (TTT)** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/eye/hp/intraocular-melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Iris Melanoma** | Iris | **Malignant**; Lower metastatic potential | **Observation** (small, No growth), **Plaque brachytherapy**, **Iridectomy/Iridocyclectomy**, **Enucleation** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/eye/hp/intraocular-melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Retinoblastoma** | Retina | **Malignant**; **Most common intraocular malignancy in children** | **Chemoreduction** + **Focal therapy** (laser, Cryotherapy), **Plaque brachytherapy**, **Enucleation** (advanced) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/retinoblastoma/hp/retinoblastoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Choroidal Metastasis** | Choroid | **Metastatic** from systemic cancer | **External beam radiation**, **Systemic therapy** (treat primary) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/eye/hp/intraocular-melanoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Choroidal Hemangioma** | Choroid | **Benign** (can cause vision loss from SRF) | **Observation**, **Photodynamic therapy (PDT)** , **Laser**, **Anti-VEGF** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/choroidal-hemangioma |
| **Retinal Capillary Hemangioma** | Retina | **Benign** (associated with **von Hippel-Lindau**) | **Laser**, **Cryotherapy**, **Anti-VEGF** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/kidney/hp/vhl-syndrome |

## Choroidal Nevus vs Melanoma - Differentiation
| Feature | Nevus | Melanoma | URL |
|---------|-------|----------|-----|
| **Thickness** | <2 mm | **>2 mm** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/choroidal-nevus-melanoma |
| **Subretinal Fluid** | Absent | **Present** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/choroidal-nevus-melanoma |
| **Orange Pigment (Lipofuscin)** | Absent | **Present** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/choroidal-nevus-melanoma |
| **Drusen** | **Present** | Absent | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/choroidal-nevus-melanoma |
| **Documented Growth** | None | **Present** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/choroidal-nevus-melanoma |

## Orbital Tumors
| Tumor | Age Group | Characteristics | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Capillary Hemangioma** | **Infants** | **Bluish/red mass**, **Grows in first year**, **Involutes by age 5-7** | **Observation** (if no amblyopia), **Oral propranolol**, **Intralesional steroids**, **Surgery** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/capillary-hemangioma |
| **Cavernous Hemangioma** | **Adults** (middle-aged) | **Slowly progressive proptosis**, **Well-circumscribed**, **Enhances on CT/MRI** | **Observation**, **Surgical excision** (if symptomatic) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/cavernous-hemangioma |
| **Lymphangioma** | **Children/Young adults** | **Sudden proptosis** (hemorrhage - "chocolate cyst"), **Infiltrative** | **Observation**, **Surgical debulking**, **Sclerotherapy** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/lymphangioma |
| **Optic Nerve Glioma** | **Children** (associated with **NF1**) | **Progressive vision loss**, **Proptosis**, **Optic nerve enlargement** | **Observation**, **Chemotherapy**, **Radiation** (older children) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/brain/hp/child-optic-pathway-glioma-treatment-pdq |
| **Optic Nerve Sheath Meningioma** | **Adults** (middle-aged women) | **Progressive vision loss**, **Optic disc swelling**, **Optociliary shunt vessels** | **Observation**, **Fractionated stereotactic radiotherapy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/brain/hp/adult-meningioma-treatment-pdq |
| **Lacrimal Gland Tumors** | Adults | **Pleomorphic adenoma** (benign), **Adenoid cystic carcinoma** (malignant) | **Complete surgical excision** (without biopsy for suspected pleomorphic adenoma) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/eye/hp/lacrimal-gland-treatment-pdq |
| **Lymphoma** | Adults (50-70) | **Salmon-patch** conjunctival lesion, **Orbital mass**, **Mucosa-associated lymphoid tissue (MALT)** | **Biopsy**, **External beam radiation**, **Systemic chemotherapy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lymphoma/hp/ocular-adnexal-lymphoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Rhabdomyosarcoma** | **Children** (<10 years) | **Rapidly progressive proptosis**, **Palpable mass** | **Biopsy**, **Chemotherapy + Radiation** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/soft-tissue-sarcoma/hp/rhabdomyosarcoma-treatment-pdq |
