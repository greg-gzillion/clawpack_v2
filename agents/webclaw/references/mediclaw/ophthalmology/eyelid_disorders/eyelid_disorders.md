# Ophthalmology - Eyelid Disorders

## Eyelid Malpositions
| Disorder | Description | Etiology | Treatment | URL |
|----------|-------------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Ptosis** | Drooping of upper eyelid | **Congenital** (levator dysgenesis), **Aponeurotic** (aging, Trauma), **Neurogenic** (CN III palsy, Horner), **Myogenic** (myasthenia gravis, CPEO) | Treat underlying cause; **Surgery** (levator advancement, M黮lerectomy, Frontalis sling) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/ptosis |
| **Ectropion** | Outward turning of eyelid margin | **Involutional** (aging), **Cicatricial**, **Paralytic** (CN VII), **Mechanical** | **Lubrication**, **Surgery** (lateral tarsal strip) | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/ectropion |
| **Entropion** | Inward turning of eyelid margin | **Involutional**, **Cicatricial** (trachoma, Chemical burns), **Spastic** | **Lubrication**, **Taping**, **Botulinum toxin** (temporary), **Surgery** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/entropion |
| **Trichiasis** | Misdirected lashes rubbing cornea | **Idiopathic**, **Cicatricial**, **Blepharitis** | **Epilation**, **Electrolysis**, **Cryotherapy**, **Surgery** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/trichiasis |

## Eyelid Inflammation
| Disorder | Description | Treatment | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Blepharitis (Anterior)** | Inflammation of eyelid margin (Staphylococcal, Seborrheic) | **Warm compresses**, **Lid hygiene** (baby shampoo), **Topical antibiotic** (erythromycin, Bacitracin), **Oral doxycycline** (refractory) | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/blepharitis |
| **Blepharitis (Posterior)** | **Meibomian gland dysfunction (MGD)** | **Warm compresses**, **Lid massage**, **Omega-3 supplements**, **Oral doxycycline**, **Intense pulsed light (IPL)** | https://www.nei.nih.gov/learn-about-eye-health/eye-conditions-and-diseases/blepharitis |
| **Hordeolum (Stye)** | Acute infection of eyelid gland (**External**: Zeis/Moll; **Internal**: Meibomian) | **Warm compresses**, **Topical antibiotic**, **Incision and drainage** if pointing | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/what-is-chalazion-hordeolum |
| **Chalazion** | Chronic granulomatous inflammation of meibomian gland | **Warm compresses**, **Intralesional steroid**, **Incision and curettage** | https://www.aao.org/eye-health/diseases/what-is-chalazion-hordeolum |

## Eyelid Tumors
| Tumor | Characteristics | Malignant Potential | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------------|---------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Basal Cell Carcinoma** | **Most common** eyelid malignancy; Pearly nodule, Telangiectasias, Ulceration | **Locally invasive**, Rarely metastasizes | **Mohs micrographic surgery**, Excision with margin control | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/basal-cell-treatment-pdq |
| **Squamous Cell Carcinoma** | Erythematous nodule, Scaling, Ulceration | **Metastatic potential** (higher than BCC) | **Mohs surgery**, Excision with margin control | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/squamous-cell-treatment-pdq |
| **Sebaceous Carcinoma** | Yellowish nodule, Mimics chalazion | **Aggressive**, **High metastatic potential** | **Wide local excision** with margin control, **Sentinel lymph node biopsy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/sebaceous-carcinoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Melanoma** | Pigmented lesion, Irregular borders | **High metastatic potential** | **Wide local excision**, **Sentinel lymph node biopsy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/skin/hp/melanoma-treatment-pdq |
