# Vascular Surgery - Carotid Artery Disease

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Atherosclerotic narrowing** of carotid artery | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Significance** | **Major cause of ischemic stroke** (10-20% of all strokes) | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Risk Factors** | Same as PAD: **Smoking**, **Diabetes**, **HTN**, **HLD**, **Age** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/carotid-artery-disease |

## Symptomatic vs Asymptomatic
| Classification | Definition | URL |
|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Symptomatic** | **TIA or stroke** within **6 months** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Asymptomatic** | **No symptoms** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |

## Landmark Trials
| Trial | Population | Intervention | Outcome | URL |
|-------|------------|--------------|---------|-----|
| **NASCET** | **Symptomatic** 50-69%, =70% | CEA vs Medical | **CEA superior** for =70% (absolute risk reduction 17%), Moderate benefit for 50-69% | https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJM199108153250701 |
| **ACAS** | **Asymptomatic** =60% | CEA vs Medical | **CEA superior** (5-year stroke risk 5.1% vs 11.0%) | https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jama/fullarticle/388344 |
| **ACST** | **Asymptomatic** =60% | CEA vs Medical | **CEA superior** (5-year stroke risk 6.4% vs 11.8%) | https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lancet/article/PIIS0140-6736(04)16146-1/fulltext |
| **CREST** | **Symptomatic + Asymptomatic** | CEA vs CAS | **No difference** in composite outcome; **CEA**: More MI, CN injury; **CAS**: More stroke (especially in elderly) | https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa0912321 |

## Carotid Endarterectomy (CEA) vs Carotid Artery Stenting (CAS)
| Feature | CEA | CAS | URL |
|---------|-----|-----|-----|
| **Stroke Risk** | **Lower** (especially symptomatic, Elderly) | **Higher** (especially elderly >70) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **MI Risk** | **Higher** | **Lower** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Cranial Nerve Injury** | **Risk** (7-10%) | **None** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Restenosis** | **Lower** | **Higher** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Preferred in** | **Elderly**, **Symptomatic**, **Unfavorable arch anatomy** | **Hostile neck** (radiation, Prior surgery), **High carotid bifurcation**, **Contralateral CN palsy**, **Severe CAD/CHF** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |

## SVS Guidelines for Carotid Revascularization
| Scenario | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Symptomatic =50%** | **CEA** (preferred) or **CAS** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Asymptomatic =70%** | **CEA** (if low surgical risk, Life expectancy =3-5 years) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Asymptomatic 60-69%** | **Consider CEA** in select patients (low risk, Life expectancy =5 years) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Asymptomatic <60%** | **Medical therapy alone** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/carotid-artery-disease |

## Best Medical Therapy for Carotid Disease
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Antiplatelet** | **Aspirin** (75-325 mg) or **Clopidogrel** 75 mg | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Statin** | **High-intensity** (atorvastatin 40-80 mg, rosuvastatin 20-40 mg) | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Blood Pressure** | **<130/80 mmHg** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/carotid-artery-disease |
| **Lifestyle** | **Smoking cessation**, **Healthy diet**, **Exercise** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/carotid-artery-disease |
