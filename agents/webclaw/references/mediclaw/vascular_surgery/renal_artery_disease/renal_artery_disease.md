## Overview

| **Etiology** | **Atherosclerosis** (90%, Ostial/proximal), **Fibromuscular dysplasia** (10%, Distal, Young women) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |

## Clinical Presentation
| Feature | Atherosclerotic RAS | Fibromuscular Dysplasia | URL |
|---------|---------------------|------------------------|-----|
| **Demographics** | **Age >50**, **Atherosclerosis risk factors** | **Young women** (15-50 years) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Hypertension** | **Resistant HTN**, **New-onset after 55** | **New-onset in young** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Renal Function** | **AKI with ACEi/ARB**, **Unexplained CKD** | **Normal** (usually) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Other** | **Flash pulmonary edema**, **Asymmetric kidney size** | **Epigastric bruit** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |

## Diagnostic Imaging
| Modality | Sensitivity/Specificity | Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|----------|------------------------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **Duplex Ultrasound** | **85-90% / 90-95%** | **No contrast**, **No radiation**, **Physiologic** (PSV >180 cm/s, RAR >3.5) | **Operator-dependent**, **Difficult with obesity/bowel gas** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **CTA** | **>95% / >95%** | **Excellent anatomy**, **Incidental findings** | **Contrast**, **Radiation** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **MRA** | **>95% / >95%** | **No radiation** | **Contrast** (gadolinium - NSF risk in CKD), **Overestimates stenosis** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Catheter Angiography** | **Gold standard** | **Allows intervention**, **Pressure gradient** | **Invasive**, **Contrast**, **Radiation** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |

## Indications for Revascularization (SVS Guidelines)
| Indication | Evidence | URL |
|------------|----------|-----|
| **Fibromuscular Dysplasia with HTN** | **Strong** (angioplasty curative) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Hemodynamically Significant RAS + Cardiac Disturbance Syndrome** (flash pulmonary edema, Unstable angina) | **Strong** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Hemodynamically Significant RAS + Resistant HTN** (=3 drugs) | **Moderate** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Hemodynamically Significant RAS + Ischemic Nephropathy** (CKD, Bilateral stenosis, Solitary kidney) | **Moderate** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
| **Asymptomatic RAS** | **NOT recommended** (CORAL, ASTRAL trials showed no benefit) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/renal-artery-disease |
