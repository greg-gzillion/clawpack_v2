| Procedure | Indications | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **Open AAA Repair** | **AAA** (not anatomically suitable for EVAR, Young patients) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Aortobifemoral Bypass** | **Aortoiliac occlusive disease** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Femoral-Popliteal Bypass** | **Femoropopliteal disease** (not amenable to endovascular) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Femoral-Tibial Bypass** | **Tibial disease** (CLI) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Extra-Anatomic Bypass** | **Axillofemoral**, **Femorofemoral** (high-risk, Infected fields) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |

## Bypass Graft Conduits
| Conduit | Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|---------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **Autogenous Vein (Great Saphenous)** | **Best patency**, **Resistant to infection** | **Limited availability**, **Vein harvest morbidity** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Prosthetic (PTFE, Dacron)** | **Readily available**, **No harvest morbidity** | **Lower patency**, **Infection risk** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Cryopreserved Vein/Artery** | **Infected fields** | **Lower patency**, **Cost**, **Availability** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Bovine Carotid Artery** | **Alternative to prosthetic** | **Cost** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |

## Graft Infection
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Incidence** | **1-5%** (higher in groin) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Presentation** | **Draining sinus**, **Pseudoaneurysm**, **Graft thrombosis**, **Sepsis** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Microbiology** | **S. aureus** (early), **S. epidermidis** (late) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Imaging** | **CTA** (perigraft fluid, gas), **WBC scan**, **PET-CT** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
| **Treatment** | **Graft excision** + **Extra-anatomic bypass** or **In-situ reconstruction** (cryopreserved, Rifampin-soaked, Autogenous vein) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/open-vascular-surgery |
