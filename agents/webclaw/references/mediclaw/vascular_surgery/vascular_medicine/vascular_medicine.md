# Vascular Surgery - Vascular Medicine

## Antiplatelet Therapy
| Drug | Mechanism | Dose | Indications | URL |
|------|-----------|------|-------------|-----|
| **Aspirin** | **Irreversible COX-1 inhibition** | 81-325 mg daily | **PAD**, **CAD**, **Stroke/TIA** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/peripheral-artery-disease |
| **Clopidogrel** | **Irreversible P2Y12 inhibition** | 75 mg daily | **PAD** (alternative to aspirin), **ACS**, **Post-stenting** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/peripheral-artery-disease |
| **Dual Antiplatelet (DAPT)** | **Aspirin + Clopidogrel** | - | **Post-carotid stenting**, **Post-peripheral stenting** (1-3 months) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/peripheral-arterial-disease |

## Statin Therapy
| Intensity | Drugs | LDL Reduction | URL |
|-----------|-------|---------------|-----|
| **High** | **Atorvastatin 40-80 mg**, **Rosuvastatin 20-40 mg** | **=50%** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/cholesterol |
| **Moderate** | Atorvastatin 10-20 mg, Rosuvastatin 5-10 mg, Simvastatin 20-40 mg | **30-49%** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/cholesterol |
| **Low** | Simvastatin 10 mg, Pravastatin 10-20 mg | **<30%** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/cholesterol |

## Antihypertensive Therapy
| Class | Preferred in Vascular Patients | URL |
|-------|------------------------------|-----|
| **ACE Inhibitors/ARBs** | **First-line** (especially with DM, CKD, CAD) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/high-blood-pressure |
| **Beta-Blockers** | **First-line** in aortic dissection, **Preferred** in CAD | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/high-blood-pressure |
| **Calcium Channel Blockers** | **Effective**, **Safe in PAD** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/high-blood-pressure |
| **Thiazide Diuretics** | **Effective** (caution with gout) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/high-blood-pressure |
