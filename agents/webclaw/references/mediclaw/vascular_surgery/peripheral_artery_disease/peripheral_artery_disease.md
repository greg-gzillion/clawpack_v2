# Vascular Surgery - Peripheral Artery Disease (PAD)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Atherosclerotic narrowing** of arteries supplying lower extremities | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/peripheral-arterial-disease |
| **Prevalence** | **>8.5 million** US adults; **20-30%** of diabetics >50 years | https://www.cdc.gov/heartdisease/peripheral_artery_disease.htm |
| **Risk Factors** | **Smoking** (strongest), **Diabetes**, **Hypertension**, **Hyperlipidemia**, **Age >65**, **CKD** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/peripheral-artery-disease |

## Management of PAD (SVS/ESVS/AHA Guidelines)
| Intervention | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| **Lifestyle** | **Smoking cessation** (single most important), **Supervised exercise therapy** (30-45 min, =3x/week) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/peripheral-arterial-disease |
| **Pharmacotherapy** | **Antiplatelet** (aspirin or clopidogrel), **High-intensity statin** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/peripheral-artery-disease |
| **Cilostazol** | **Improves claudication** (contraindicated in HF) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/peripheral-arterial-disease |
| **Revascularization** | **Lifestyle-limiting claudication** despite medical therapy, **Critical limb ischemia (CLI)** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/peripheral-arterial-disease |

| **Open Surgical Bypass** | **Extensive/multilevel disease**, **Failed endovascular**, **Good surgical candidate** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/peripheral-arterial-disease |

## Critical Limb Ischemia (CLI) / Chronic Limb-Threatening Ischemia (CLTI)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Rest pain**, **Tissue loss** (ulcer, gangrene) >2 weeks with **ABI <0.50** or **TBI <0.30** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/critical-limb-ischemia |
| **WIfI Classification** | **W**ound, **I**schemia, **F**oot **I**nfection (predicts amputation risk) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/critical-limb-ischemia |
| **Management** | **Urgent revascularization**, **Wound care**, **Infection control** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/critical-limb-ischemia |

## Acute Limb Ischemia (ALI)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Sudden decrease in limb perfusion** (<14 days) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/acute-limb-ischemia |
| **6 P's** | **P**ain, **P**allor, **P**ulselessness, **P**aresthesias, **P**aralysis, **P**oikilothermia | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/acute-limb-ischemia |
| **Etiology** | **Embolism** (cardiac - AF), **Thrombosis** (native artery or bypass graft), **Trauma** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/acute-limb-ischemia |
| **Rutherford Classification** | **I**: Viable; **IIa**: Marginally threatened; **IIb**: Immediately threatened; **III**: Irreversible | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/acute-limb-ischemia |
| **Management** | **I-IIa**: Anticoagulation ± Revascularization; **IIb**: **Emergent revascularization**; **III**: **Amputation** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/acute-limb-ischemia |
