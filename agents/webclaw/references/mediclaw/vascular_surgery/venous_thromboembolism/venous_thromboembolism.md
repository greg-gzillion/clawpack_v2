# Vascular Surgery - Venous Thromboembolism (VTE)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Deep vein thrombosis (DVT)** and/or **Pulmonary embolism (PE)** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/deep-vein-thrombosis |
| **Incidence** | **1-2 per 1,000** annually | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/dvt/data.html |
| **Mortality** | **10-30%** of PE cases (many undiagnosed) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/dvt/data.html |

## VTE Treatment (CHEST Guidelines 2021)
| Phase | Duration | Anticoagulant | URL |
|-------|----------|---------------|-----|
| **Initial (3-6 months)** | **3-6 months** | **DOAC** (apixaban, rivaroxaban) preferred over warfarin | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Provoked VTE** | **3 months** | Discontinue after 3 months (if transient risk factor resolved) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Unprovoked VTE** | **Indefinite** | Continue anticoagulation (reassess annually) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Cancer-Associated VTE** | **Indefinite** | **LMWH** or **DOAC** (apixaban, rivaroxaban, edoxaban) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |

## Post-Thrombotic Syndrome (PTS)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Chronic venous insufficiency** following DVT | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/post-thrombotic-syndrome |
| **Prevalence** | **20-50%** after proximal DVT | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/post-thrombotic-syndrome |
| **Symptoms** | **Leg pain**, **Swelling**, **Heaviness**, **Skin changes**, **Ulceration** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/post-thrombotic-syndrome |
| **Prevention** | **Compression stockings** (controversial), **Early mobilization**, **Anticoagulation** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/post-thrombotic-syndrome |

## Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis (CDT) and Mechanical Thrombectomy
| Indication | Recommendation | URL |
|------------|----------------|-----|
| **Iliofemoral DVT** | **Consider CDT** in young, active patients with low bleeding risk (ATTRACT trial: reduced PTS severity) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/deep-vein-thrombosis |
| **Phlegmasia Cerulea Dolens** | **Urgent CDT or thrombectomy** (limb-threatening) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/deep-vein-thrombosis |
| **Massive PE** | **Systemic thrombolytics** or **Catheter-directed thrombolysis** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/pulmonary-embolism |
| **Submassive PE** | **Consider CDT** if deteriorating (controversial) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/pulmonary-embolism |
