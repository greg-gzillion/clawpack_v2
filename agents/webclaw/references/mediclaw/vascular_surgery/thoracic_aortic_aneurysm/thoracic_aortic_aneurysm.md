# Vascular Surgery - Thoracic Aortic Aneurysm (TAA)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Focal dilation of thoracic aorta =1.5x normal diameter** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |
| **Locations** | **Ascending** (most common), **Arch**, **Descending**, **Thoracoabdominal** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |
| **Etiology** | **Degenerative** (most common), **Genetic** (Marfan, Loeys-Dietz, Ehlers-Danlos, Bicuspid aortic valve), **Post-dissection**, **Infectious** (mycotic), **Inflammatory** (Takayasu, Giant cell) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |

## Normal Aortic Diameters and Repair Thresholds
| Segment | Normal Diameter | Repair Threshold | URL |
|---------|-----------------|------------------|-----|
| **Ascending** | **<3.5 cm** | **=5.5 cm** (=5.0 cm if BAV, =4.5 cm if Marfan) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |
| **Descending** | **<3.0 cm** | **=6.0 cm** (=5.5 cm if Marfan) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |
| **Thoracoabdominal** | Variable | **=6.0 cm** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |

## Indications for Repair (ACC/AHA)
| Indication | Threshold | URL |
|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Ascending TAA** | **=5.5 cm** (or =5.0 cm with BAV, =4.5 cm with Marfan/Loeys-Dietz) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/aortic-disease |
| **Descending TAA** | **=6.0 cm** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/aortic-disease |
| **Rapid Expansion** | **=0.5 cm in 6 months** or **=1.0 cm in 1 year** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/aortic-disease |
| **Symptomatic** | **Any size** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/aortic-disease |

## Treatment Options
| Location | Open Repair | Endovascular (TEVAR) | URL |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Ascending** | **Gold standard** (requires CPB) | **Not routinely** (investigational) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |
| **Descending** | Thoracotomy (high morbidity) | **TEVAR** (preferred if anatomically suitable) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |
| **Thoracoabdominal** | **Open** (Crawford classification) or **Hybrid** or **Fenestrated/Branched EVAR** | | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/thoracic-aortic-aneurysm |

## Genetic Syndromes Associated with TAA
| Syndrome | Gene | Aortic Involvement | Repair Threshold | URL |
|----------|------|-------------------|------------------|-----|
| **Marfan** | **FBN1** | **Ascending** (root) | **=5.0 cm** (or =4.5 cm with risk factors) | https://www.marfan.org |
| **Loeys-Dietz** | **TGFBR1/2**, **SMAD3**, **TGFB2** | **Aggressive**, **Dissection at smaller sizes** | **=4.2-4.5 cm** | https://www.loeysdietz.org |
| **Ehlers-Danlos (Vascular Type)** | **COL3A1** | **Fragile vessels**, **High rupture risk** | **Individualize** (high surgical risk) | https://www.ehlers-danlos.com |
| **Bicuspid Aortic Valve** | **NOTCH1**, **GATA5** | **Ascending** (often associated) | **=5.0 cm** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/congenital-heart-defects |
