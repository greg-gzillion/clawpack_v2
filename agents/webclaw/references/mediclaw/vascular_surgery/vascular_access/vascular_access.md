# Vascular Surgery - Vascular Access

## Hemodialysis Access
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Goal** | **Reliable, high-flow access** for hemodialysis | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **KDOQI Guidelines** | **"Fistula First"** (AVF > AVG > CVC) | https://www.kidney.org/professionals/guidelines/guidelines_commentaries |
| **AVF** | **Autogenous arteriovenous fistula** (lowest infection/thrombosis risk) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **AVG** | **Prosthetic arteriovenous graft** (higher infection/thrombosis risk, Faster maturation) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **CVC** | **Central venous catheter** (highest infection/thrombosis risk, Immediate use) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |

## Arteriovenous Fistula (AVF)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Preferred Configurations** | **Radiocephalic** (wrist - Brescia-Cimino), **Brachiocephalic** (elbow), **Brachiobasilic** (transposition) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Preoperative Evaluation** | **Duplex ultrasound** (arterial and venous mapping) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Maturation** | **6-12 weeks** (requires adequate inflow, Outflow, Diameter =6 mm, Depth =6 mm, Flow =600 mL/min) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Patency** | **60-70% at 1 year**, **50-60% at 2 years** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |

## AVF Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Thrombosis** | **Thrombectomy** (surgical or endovascular), **Angioplasty** of underlying stenosis | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Stenosis** | **Angioplasty** (first-line), **Stenting** (refractory, Cephalic arch, Central veins) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Infection** | **Antibiotics** (AVF rare), **AVG**: Excision if infected | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Steal Syndrome** | **Distal revascularization-interval ligation (DRIL)** , **Proximalization of arterial inflow (PAI)** , **Banding** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Venous Hypertension** | **Central venography**, **Angioplasty/Stenting** of central stenosis | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Aneurysm/Pseudoaneurysm** | **Observation** (if stable), **Surgical repair** (if enlarging, Painful, Skin compromise) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |

## AV Fistula vs AV Graft
| Feature | AVF | AVG | URL |
|---------|-----|-----|-----|
| **Infection Risk** | **Lowest** | **Higher** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Thrombosis Risk** | **Lower** | **Higher** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Maturation Time** | **Longer** (6-12 weeks) | **Shorter** (2-4 weeks) | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Primary Patency** | **60-70% at 1 year** | **50-60% at 1 year** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
| **Secondary Patency** | **70-80% at 1 year** | **70-80% at 1 year** | https://vascular.org/patients/vascular-conditions/hemodialysis-access |
