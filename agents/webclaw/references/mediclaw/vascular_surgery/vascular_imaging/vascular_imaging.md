# Vascular Surgery - Vascular Imaging

## Modalities
| Modality | Indications | Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|----------|-------------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **Duplex Ultrasound** | **PAD**, **Carotid stenosis**, **DVT**, **Venous reflux**, **AVF surveillance** | **No contrast**, **No radiation**, **Portable**, **Physiologic** | **Operator-dependent**, **Limited by obesity/bowel gas/calcification** | https://www.svunet.org/clinical-practice/guidelines |
| **CTA** | **Aneurysm**, **Dissection**, **PAD**, **Trauma**, **Pre-operative planning** | **Excellent spatial resolution**, **Rapid**, **Widely available** | **Contrast** (nephropathy, Allergy), **Radiation**, **Artifact from calcium/stents** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **MRA** | **PAD**, **Renal artery stenosis**, **Aneurysm** | **No radiation**, **Excellent soft tissue contrast** | **Contrast** (gadolinium - NSF risk), **Overestimates stenosis**, **Claustrophobia**, **Pacemakers** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/ACR-Appropriateness-Criteria |
| **Catheter Angiography** | **Diagnostic and therapeutic** | **Gold standard**, **Allows intervention**, **Pressure gradients** | **Invasive**, **Contrast**, **Radiation**, **Access complications** | https://www.radiologyinfo.org/en/info/angiocath |
