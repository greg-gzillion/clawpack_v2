# First Aid - Choking (Foreign Body Airway Obstruction)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Partial or complete obstruction of airway** by foreign body | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Signs of Mild Obstruction** | **Can speak**, **Can cough forcefully**, **Can breathe**, **May wheeze** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Signs of Severe Obstruction** | **Cannot speak**, **Weak/ineffective cough**, **High-pitched noise or silent**, **Cyanosis**, **Universal choking sign** (hands clutching throat) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |

## Adult and Child (>1 year) Choking Management
| Severity | Action | URL |
|----------|--------|-----|
| **Mild Obstruction** | **Encourage coughing**, **Do NOT interfere** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Severe Obstruction** | **Ask "Are you choking?"**, **If nods yes ? Act** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Step 1** | **5 Back Blows**: Stand behind, Support chest, Lean forward, Strike between shoulder blades with heel of hand | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Step 2** | **5 Abdominal Thrusts (Heimlich Maneuver)** : Stand behind, Wrap arms around waist, Make fist above navel, Grasp with other hand, Thrust inward and upward | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Step 3** | **Alternate 5 back blows and 5 abdominal thrusts** until object expelled or victim unresponsive | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **If Unresponsive** | **Lower to ground**, **Start CPR**, **Look in mouth before breaths** (remove object if visible) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |

## Infant (<1 year) Choking Management
| Severity | Action | URL |
|----------|--------|-----|
| **Mild Obstruction** | **Encourage coughing**, **Do NOT interfere** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Severe Obstruction** | **Cannot cry/cough**, **Cyanosis**, **Weak/ineffective sounds** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Step 1** | **5 Back Blows**: Hold infant face down on forearm, Support head/jaw, Give 5 firm back blows between shoulder blades with heel of hand | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Step 2** | **5 Chest Thrusts**: Turn infant face up, Support head, Place 2 fingers on sternum (just below nipple line), Give 5 chest thrusts (compress 1.5 inches) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Step 3** | **Alternate 5 back blows and 5 chest thrusts** until object expelled or infant unresponsive | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **If Unresponsive** | **Start CPR**, **Look in mouth before breaths** (remove object if visible) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |

## Special Considerations
| Situation | Action | URL |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **Pregnant or Obese Victim** | **Chest thrusts** instead of abdominal thrusts (place hands on sternum) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Self-Choking (Alone)** | **Self-administer abdominal thrusts** using chair back or counter edge, OR **Call 911** (leave phone on) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
| **Wheelchair-Bound** | **Chest thrusts** from behind, OR **Abdominal thrusts** if able to stand briefly | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/choking |
