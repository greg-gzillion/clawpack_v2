# First Aid - Burns

## Burn Classification
| Degree | Depth | Appearance | Sensation | Healing | URL |
|--------|-------|------------|-----------|---------|-----|
| **First-Degree (Superficial)** | **Epidermis only** | **Red**, **Dry**, **Painful**, **No blisters** | **Painful** | **3-6 days** (no scar) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |
| **Second-Degree (Partial Thickness)** | **Epidermis + Dermis** | **Red**, **Blistered**, **Moist**, **Painful** | **Very painful** | **1-3 weeks** (minimal scar) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |
| **Third-Degree (Full Thickness)** | **All skin layers** | **White**, **Waxy**, **Leathery**, **Charred**, **Painless** | **Painless** (nerve destruction) | **Requires grafting** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |

## Burn First Aid
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **Stop the burning process**: **Remove from source**, **Remove clothing/jewelry** (unless stuck) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |
| **2** | **Cool the burn**: **Cool running water** for **10-20 minutes** (NOT ice - causes further damage) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |
| **3** | **Cover loosely** with **sterile, non-stick dressing** or **clean cloth** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |
| **4** | **Do NOT apply**: **Ice**, **Butter**, **Ointments**, **Toothpaste**, **Egg whites** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |
| **5** | **Do NOT break blisters** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |

## Specific Burn Types
| Burn Type | Special Considerations | URL |
|-----------|----------------------|-----|
| **Chemical Burn** | **Brush off dry powder**, **Irrigate with water x20+ min**, **Remove contaminated clothing**, **Do NOT neutralize** (heat reaction) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/chemical-burns |
| **Electrical Burn** | **Ensure power source is OFF**, **Check for entry/exit wounds**, **Monitor for cardiac arrhythmias**, **Immobilize spine** (blast injury) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/electrical-burns |
| **Tar/Plastic Burn** | **Cool with water**, **Do NOT remove** (leave for medical personnel) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/burns |
