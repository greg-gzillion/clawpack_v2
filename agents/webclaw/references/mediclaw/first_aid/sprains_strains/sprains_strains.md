# First Aid - Sprains and Strains

## Overview
| Injury | Definition | URL |
|--------|------------|-----|
| **Sprain** | **Stretching or tearing of ligaments** (connects bone to bone) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **Strain** | **Stretching or tearing of muscle or tendon** (connects muscle to bone) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |

## Signs and Symptoms
| Sign/Symptom | Sprain | Strain | URL |
|--------------|--------|--------|-----|
| **Pain** | **Yes** (localized to joint) | **Yes** (localized to muscle) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **Swelling** | **Rapid** | **Gradual** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **Bruising** | **Often** | **Sometimes** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **Instability** | **Yes** (severe sprain) | **No** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **Muscle Spasm** | **Sometimes** | **Often** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |

## RICE Protocol
| Component | Action | URL |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **R**est | **Stop activity**, **Avoid weight-bearing** if lower extremity | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **I**ce | **Apply ice/cold pack** for **20 min q2-3h** (first 48-72h) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **C**ompression | **Elastic bandage** (wrap from distal to proximal) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **E**levation | **Elevate above heart level** to reduce swelling | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |

## When to Seek Medical Care
| Criteria | URL |
|----------|-----|
| **Severe pain** or **Inability to bear weight** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **Visible deformity** or **Joint instability** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **Numbness/Tingling** distal to injury | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **No improvement in 48-72 hours** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
| **"Pop" sound** at time of injury (possible fracture or ligament rupture) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/sprains-strains |
