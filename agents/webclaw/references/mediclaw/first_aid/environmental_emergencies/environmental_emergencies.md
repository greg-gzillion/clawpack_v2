# First Aid - Environmental Emergencies

## Heat-Related Emergencies
| Condition | Symptoms | Core Temp | First Aid | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Heat Cramps** | **Painful muscle spasms** (calves, abdomen) | **Normal** | **Rest**, **Hydrate with electrolytes**, **Stretch** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |
| **Heat Exhaustion** | **Fatigue**, **Headache**, **Nausea**, **Dizziness**, **Profuse sweating** | **<104°F** | **Move to cool area**, **Remove excess clothing**, **Cool with fans/cold packs**, **Hydrate** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |
| **Heat Stroke** | **Confusion**, **Seizure**, **Hot/dry skin** (may still sweat), **Throbbing headache** | **>104°F** | **CALL 911**, **Rapid cooling** (cold water immersion, Ice packs to groin/axillae/neck), **Do NOT give fluids** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |

## Cold-Related Emergencies
| Condition | Symptoms | First Aid | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Frostnip** | **Pale/numb skin**, **No tissue damage** | **Rewarm** with body heat or warm water, **Do NOT rub** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |
| **Frostbite** | **Hard, pale, cold skin**, **Numbness**, **Blisters** (severe) | **Move to warm area**, **Remove wet clothing**, **Rewarm in warm water** (99-104°F) x20-30 min, **Do NOT rub**, **Do NOT use dry heat** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |
| **Hypothermia** | **Shivering**, **Confusion**, **Drowsiness**, **Slurred speech**, **Slow breathing** | **CALL 911**, **Move to warm area**, **Remove wet clothing**, **Insulate** (blankets), **Warm fluids** if alert, **Handle gently** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |

## Lightning Safety
| Guideline | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **30-30 Rule** | **Seek shelter** if **thunder =30 seconds after lightning**; **Wait 30 minutes after last thunder** to resume | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/features/lightning-safety/index.html |
| **Safe Shelter** | **Fully enclosed building** or **Hard-topped metal vehicle** | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/features/lightning-safety/index.html |
| **If Caught Outside** | **Avoid tall objects**, **Do NOT lie flat** (crouch on balls of feet), **Avoid water**, **Avoid metal objects** | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/features/lightning-safety/index.html |
| **Lightning Strike First Aid** | **Victim does NOT carry charge**, **Start CPR if unresponsive/pulseless**, **Treat burns** | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/features/lightning-safety/index.html |
