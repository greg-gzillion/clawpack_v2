# First Aid - Water Emergencies

## Drowning
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Respiratory impairment from submersion/immersion in liquid** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/drowning |
| **"Reach, Throw, Row, Go"** | **Reach** with pole/branch, **Throw** flotation device, **Row** boat, **Go** (only if trained) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/swimming/water-safety |

## Drowning First Aid
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **Ensure scene safety** (do NOT become second victim) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/drowning |
| **2** | **Remove victim from water** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/drowning |
| **3** | **Check responsiveness and breathing** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/drowning |
| **4** | **If not breathing**: **Start CPR** with **2 rescue breaths first** ? **30 compressions** ? **2 breaths** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/drowning |
| **5** | **Use AED if available** (dry chest before pad placement) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/drowning |
| **6** | **All drowning victims require medical evaluation** (even if asymptomatic) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/drowning |

## Spinal Injury in Water
| Situation | Action | URL |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **Suspected spinal injury** (diving, Surfing, Water slide) | **Immobilize spine in water**, **Use backboard**, **Maintain airway** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/spinal-injuries |
