# First Aid - Chest Pain and Heart Attack

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Heart Attack (MI)** | **Blockage of coronary artery** ? **Myocardial ischemia/infarction** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack |
| **Angina** | **Temporary chest pain** due to reduced blood flow (usually resolves with rest/nitroglycerin) | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/angina-chest-pain |

## Signs and Symptoms of Heart Attack
| Symptom | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Chest Pain** | **Pressure**, **Squeezing**, **Fullness**, **Pain** in center of chest lasting >few minutes or comes and goes | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/warning-signs-of-a-heart-attack |
| **Radiation** | **Pain radiating to arm(s), back, neck, jaw, stomach** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/warning-signs-of-a-heart-attack |
| **Other Symptoms** | **Shortness of breath**, **Nausea/Vomiting**, **Lightheadedness**, **Cold sweat** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/warning-signs-of-a-heart-attack |
| **Atypical Presentation** | **Women**, **Diabetics**, **Elderly** may have less typical chest pain | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/warning-signs-of-a-heart-attack |

## First Aid for Suspected Heart Attack
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **CALL 911 immediately** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/heart-attack |
| **2** | **Position**: **Semi-sitting** (most comfortable), **Loosen tight clothing** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/heart-attack |
| **3** | **If victim has prescribed nitroglycerin**: **Assist** (do NOT give your own) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/heart-attack |
| **4** | **If not allergic and no contraindication**: **Chewable aspirin 325 mg** or **4 baby aspirin (81 mg each)** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/heart-attack |
| **5** | **Stay with victim**, **Monitor ABCs**, **Be prepared to start CPR** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/heart-attack |
| **6** | **Do NOT let victim eat/drink** (except to take aspirin) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/heart-attack |

## Aspirin for Heart Attack
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Dose** | **325 mg** (chewable) or **4 x 81 mg** (baby aspirin) | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/treatment-of-a-heart-attack |
| **Contraindications** | **Allergy**, **Active bleeding**, **Recent GI bleed**, **Bleeding disorder** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/treatment-of-a-heart-attack |
| **Do NOT give if** | **Stroke symptoms** (may be hemorrhagic) | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/treatment-of-a-heart-attack |
