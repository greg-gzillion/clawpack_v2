## Common Pediatric Emergencies
| Emergency | First Aid | URL |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Fever** | **Acetaminophen/Ibuprofen** (weight-based), **Light clothing**, **Hydration** | https://www.healthychildren.org/English/health-issues/conditions/fever/Pages/Fever-Without-Fear.aspx |
| **Febrile Seizure** | **Protect from injury**, **Do NOT put in water**, **Seek medical evaluation** | https://www.healthychildren.org/English/health-issues/conditions/head-neck-nervous-system/Pages/Febrile-Seizures.aspx |
| **Croup** | **Cool mist humidifier**, **Steamy bathroom**, **Upright position**, **Seek care if stridor at rest** | https://www.healthychildren.org/English/health-issues/conditions/chest-lungs/Pages/Croup-Treatment.aspx |
| **Foreign Body Ingestion** | **If asymptomatic**, **Observe**; **If drooling/choking**, **Seek emergency care** | https://www.poison.org/articles/battery |
