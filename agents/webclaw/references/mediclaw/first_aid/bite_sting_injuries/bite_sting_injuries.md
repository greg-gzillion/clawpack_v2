# First Aid - Bite and Sting Injuries

## Animal and Human Bites
| Injury | First Aid | URL |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Minor Bite** | **Wash with soap and water**, **Apply antibiotic ointment**, **Cover with sterile dressing** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/bites-stings |
| **Deep/Puncture Bite** | **Control bleeding**, **Seek medical care** (risk of infection, Rabies, Tetanus) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/bites-stings |
| **Report Animal Bite** | **Contact animal control** or **local health department** | https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/index.html |

## Snake Bites
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **Move away from snake** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/snake-bites |
| **2** | **Keep victim calm**, **Immobilize extremity** at/below heart level | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/snake-bites |
| **3** | **Remove jewelry/tight clothing** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/snake-bites |
| **4** | **CALL 911** or **Seek immediate medical care** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/snake-bites |
| **5** | **Do NOT**: **Apply tourniquet**, **Cut wound**, **Suck venom**, **Apply ice**, **Apply electric shock** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/snake-bites |
| **6** | **If possible, note snake appearance** (do NOT attempt to capture/kill) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/snake-bites |

## Spider Bites
| Spider | First Aid | URL |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Black Widow** | **Wash area**, **Apply cold compress**, **Seek medical care** (muscle cramps, pain) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/spider-bites |
| **Brown Recluse** | **Wash area**, **Apply cold compress**, **Elevate**, **Seek medical care** (necrotic wound) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/spider-bites |

## Insect Stings (Bee, Wasp, Hornet)
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **Remove stinger** (scrape with credit card, do NOT pinch) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/bites-stings |
| **2** | **Wash with soap and water** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/bites-stings |
| **3** | **Apply cold compress** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/bites-stings |
| **4** | **Monitor for allergic reaction** (anaphylaxis) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/bites-stings |
| **5** | **If sting in mouth/throat**: **Give ice to suck**, **Seek immediate care** (airway edema) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/bites-stings |

## Tick Bites
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **Remove tick promptly**: **Use fine-tipped tweezers**, **Grasp close to skin**, **Pull straight up** (do NOT twist/jerk) | https://www.cdc.gov/ticks/removing_a_tick.html |
| **2** | **Clean area** with soap and water or rubbing alcohol | https://www.cdc.gov/ticks/removing_a_tick.html |
| **3** | **Do NOT**: **Paint with nail polish**, **Apply petroleum jelly**, **Apply heat** | https://www.cdc.gov/ticks/removing_a_tick.html |
| **4** | **Save tick** in sealed bag for identification | https://www.cdc.gov/ticks/removing_a_tick.html |
| **5** | **Monitor for Lyme disease symptoms** (erythema migrans rash, Fever, Arthralgias) | https://www.cdc.gov/lyme/index.html |

## Jellyfish Stings
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **Rinse with vinegar** (inactivates nematocysts) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/jellyfish-stings |
| **2** | **Remove tentacles** with tweezers/gloved hand | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/jellyfish-stings |
| **3** | **Soak in hot water** (110-113°F) x20-45 min | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/jellyfish-stings |
| **4** | **Do NOT**: **Rinse with fresh water**, **Apply pressure bandage**, **Rub area** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/jellyfish-stings |
