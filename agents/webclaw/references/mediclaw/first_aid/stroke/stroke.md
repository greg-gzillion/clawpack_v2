# First Aid - Stroke

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **"Time is Brain"** | **Every minute**, **1.9 million neurons die** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke |

## FAST Assessment
| Letter | Assessment | Positive Finding | URL |
|-------|------------|------------------|-----|
| **F**ace | **Ask to smile** | **One side droops** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/stroke-symptoms |
| **A**rm | **Ask to raise both arms** | **One arm drifts down** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/stroke-symptoms |
| **S**peech | **Ask to repeat simple phrase** | **Slurred**, **Inappropriate words**, **Unable to speak** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/stroke-symptoms |
| **T**ime | **Note time symptoms started** | **CALL 911 immediately** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/stroke-symptoms |

## First Aid for Suspected Stroke
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **CALL 911 immediately** (do NOT drive to hospital) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/stroke |
| **2** | **Note time of symptom onset** (critical for tPA eligibility) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/stroke |
| **3** | **Position**: **Lying on side** with head slightly elevated (if conscious), **Recovery position** (if unconscious) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/stroke |
| **4** | **Loosen tight clothing** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/stroke |
| **5** | **Do NOT give anything to eat/drink** (risk of aspiration) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/stroke |
| **6** | **Stay with victim**, **Reassure** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/stroke |
