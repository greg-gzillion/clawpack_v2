# First Aid - Eye Injuries

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **General Principle** | **Do NOT rub eye**, **Do NOT attempt to remove embedded object**, **Seek medical care** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/eye-injuries |

## Specific Eye Injuries
| Injury | First Aid | URL |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Foreign Body** | **Irrigate with water/saline**, **Blink repeatedly**, **Pull upper lid over lower lid** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/eye-injuries |
| **Embedded Object** | **Do NOT remove**, **Stabilize object** (cover with paper cup), **Cover both eyes** (reduces movement), **Seek immediate care** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/eye-injuries |
| **Chemical Burn** | **Irrigate with copious water** x15-20 min (aim away from unaffected eye), **Remove contact lenses**, **Seek immediate care** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/eye-injuries |
| **Blow to Eye** | **Apply cold compress** (do NOT apply pressure), **Seek medical care** (rule out orbital fracture, hyphema) | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/eye-injuries |
| **Eyelid Cut** | **Apply gentle pressure** (if not on eyeball), **Seek medical care** | https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/first-aid/eye-injuries |
