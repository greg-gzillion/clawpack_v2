# Obstetrics - Prenatal Screening and Diagnostic Testing

## Aneuploidy Screening Options
| Test | Timing | Detection Rate (T21) | URL |
|------|--------|---------------------|-----|
| **NIPT (cfDNA)** | =10 weeks | **>99%** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/08/screening-for-fetal-chromosomal-abnormalities |
| **First Trimester Screen** | 10-13 weeks | **82-87%** (combined with NT, PAPP-A, free ß-hCG) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/08/screening-for-fetal-chromosomal-abnormalities |
| **Quad Screen** | 15-22 weeks | **81%** (AFP, hCG, uE3, Inhibin A) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/08/screening-for-fetal-chromosomal-abnormalities |
| **Integrated Screen** | First + Second trimester | **94-96%** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/08/screening-for-fetal-chromosomal-abnormalities |
| **Sequential Screen** | First + Second trimester | **95%** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/08/screening-for-fetal-chromosomal-abnormalities |

## Diagnostic Testing
| Test | Timing | Indications | Risk of Pregnancy Loss | URL |
|------|--------|-------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Chorionic Villus Sampling (CVS)** | **10-13 weeks** | Abnormal screening, Ultrasound anomaly, Prior aneuploidy, Parental translocation | **1 in 300-500** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/05/invasive-diagnostic-testing-for-aneuploidy |
| **Amniocentesis** | **=15 weeks** | Same as CVS | **1 in 500-1,000** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/05/invasive-diagnostic-testing-for-aneuploidy |

## Neural Tube Defect Screening
| Test | Timing | Interpretation | URL |
|------|--------|----------------|-----|
| **Maternal Serum AFP (MSAFP)** | **15-20 weeks** | **=2.5 MoM** = Increased risk (requires ultrasound) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2017/07/neural-tube-defects |
| **Anatomy Ultrasound** | **18-22 weeks** | Direct visualization of spine and cranium | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2017/07/neural-tube-defects |
| **Amniotic Fluid AFP + Acetylcholinesterase** | =15 weeks | Confirmatory for open NTD | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2017/07/neural-tube-defects |
