# Obstetrics - Lactation and Breastfeeding

## Breastfeeding Recommendations
| Organization | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| **AAP** | **Exclusive breastfeeding for 6 months**, Continued with complementary foods for **=1 year** (or as long as mutually desired) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/breastfeeding/ |
| **ACOG** | **Exclusive breastfeeding for 6 months**, Continued for **=1 year** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/02/breastfeeding |
| **WHO** | **Exclusive breastfeeding for 6 months**, Continued for **=2 years** | https://www.who.int/health-topics/breastfeeding |

## Lactation Support
| Issue | Management | URL |
|-------|------------|-----|
| **Engorgement** | **Frequent feeding/pumping**, **Warm compresses** before feeding, **Cold compresses** after, **Reverse pressure softening** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/maternal-or-infant-illnesses/engorgement.html |
| **Sore Nipples** | **Correct latch**, **Nipple cream** (lanolin), **Hydrogel pads**, **Nipple shields** (temporary) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/maternal-or-infant-illnesses/sore-nipples.html |
| **Low Milk Supply** | **Increase frequency**, **Ensure adequate latch**, **Galactagogues** (domperidone, Metoclopramide - limited evidence) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/maternal-or-infant-illnesses/low-milk-supply.html |
| **Plugged Duct** | **Frequent feeding/pumping**, **Massage**, **Warm compresses**, **Sunflower lecithin** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/maternal-or-infant-illnesses/plugged-ducts.html |
| **Mastitis** | **Antibiotics** (dicloxacillin, cephalexin), **Continue breastfeeding**, **Analgesics** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/maternal-or-infant-illnesses/mastitis.html |

## Contraindications to Breastfeeding
| Condition | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **HIV** | **Avoid breastfeeding** in United States (safe infant formula available) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| **HTLV-1/2** | **Avoid breastfeeding** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| **Active TB** | **Avoid breastfeeding until treated** (can resume after 2 weeks of treatment) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| **Active HSV on breast** | **Avoid breastfeeding from affected breast** until lesions healed | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| **Chemotherapy** | **Avoid breastfeeding** (most agents) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| **Radioactive Iodine** | **Avoid breastfeeding** (permanently) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| **Substance Use** | **Avoid breastfeeding** if actively using illicit drugs (except stable methadone/buprenorphine) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
