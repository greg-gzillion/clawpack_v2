| Stage | Definition | Duration (Nulliparous) | Duration (Multiparous) | URL |
|-------|------------|----------------------|----------------------|-----|
| **First Stage** | **Onset of labor to complete cervical dilation** | | | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/03/management-of-labor |

| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **Bishop Score** | **Cervical dilation**, **Effacement**, **Station**, **Consistency**, **Position** (=8 = favorable) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/08/induction-of-labor |

## Labor Dystocia
| Type | Definition | Management | URL |
|------|------------|------------|-----|
| **Protraction Disorder** | **Cervical dilation <1.2 cm/h** (nulliparous) or **<1.5 cm/h** (multiparous) | **Amniotomy**, **Oxytocin**, **Supportive care** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/03/management-of-labor |
| **Arrest Disorder** | **No cervical change for =4h** with adequate contractions (=200 MVU) OR **=6h** with inadequate contractions | **Cesarean delivery** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/03/management-of-labor |
| **Second Stage Arrest** | **No progress after =3h** (nulliparous) or **=2h** (multiparous) | **Operative vaginal delivery** or **Cesarean** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/03/management-of-labor |

## Operative Vaginal Delivery
| Type | Indications | Prerequisites | URL |
|------|-------------|---------------|-----|
| **Vacuum** | **Maternal exhaustion**, **Non-reassuring fetal status**, **Prolonged second stage** | **Vertex**, **Fully dilated**, **Engaged** (=+2 station), **Empty bladder**, **Adequate analgesia**, **Informed consent** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/11/operative-vaginal-delivery |
| **Forceps** | Same as vacuum | Same as vacuum + **Exact position known**, **No suspected CPD** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/11/operative-vaginal-delivery |
| **Contraindications** | **Unengaged head**, **Unknown position**, **Cephalopelvic disproportion**, **Fetal bleeding disorder** (relative) | | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/11/operative-vaginal-delivery |
