# Obstetrics - Obstetric Ultrasound

## First Trimester Ultrasound
| Parameter | Timing | Purpose | URL |
|-----------|--------|---------|-----|
| **Confirm Viability** | 5-7 weeks | **Gestational sac**, **Yolk sac**, **Fetal pole**, **Cardiac activity** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **Dating** | **=13 6/7 weeks** | **Crown-rump length (CRL)** most accurate | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **Nuchal Translucency (NT)** | 11-13 6/7 weeks | **Aneuploidy screening** (CRL 45-84 mm) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **Chorionicity (Twins)** | **=13 6/7 weeks** | **Lambda sign** (dichorionic), **T sign** (monochorionic) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |

## Amniotic Fluid Assessment
| Method | Normal | Oligohydramnios | Polyhydramnios | URL |
|--------|--------|-----------------|----------------|-----|
| **Amniotic Fluid Index (AFI)** | **5-25 cm** | **=5 cm** | **=24-25 cm** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **Single Deepest Pocket (SDP)** | **2-8 cm** | **<2 cm** | **=8 cm** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **SDP Preferred** | **Reduces unnecessary interventions** | Use SDP for diagnosis | Use SDP for diagnosis | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |

## Placental Location
| Finding | Significance | Management | URL |
|---------|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Placenta Previa** | Placenta covers **internal cervical os** | **Cesarean delivery**; Serial ultrasounds to assess resolution | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **Low-Lying Placenta** | Placental edge **<2 cm from internal os** | Repeat at 32 weeks; Cesarean if persists | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **Vasa Previa** | Fetal vessels overlying internal os | **Cesarean delivery at 34-35 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/10/ultrasound-in-pregnancy |
| **Placenta Accreta Spectrum** | Abnormal placental invasion | **Multidisciplinary planning**, **Cesarean hysterectomy** often needed | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/obstetric-care-consensus/articles/2018/12/placenta-accreta-spectrum |

## Cervical Length Screening
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indication** | **Prior spontaneous preterm birth** (universal screening controversial) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Timing** | **16-24 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Short Cervix** | **=25 mm** (or =20 mm in some protocols) before 24 weeks | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Management** | **Vaginal progesterone** (singleton), **Cerclage** (if prior PTB and CL <25 mm), **Arabin pessary** (controversial) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
