# Obstetrics - Diabetes in Pregnancy

## Classification
| Type | Definition | URL |
|------|------------|-----|
| **Pregestational Diabetes (Type 1 or Type 2)** | **Diabetes diagnosed before pregnancy** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/pregestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Gestational Diabetes (GDM)** | **Diabetes diagnosed during pregnancy** (usually 24-28 weeks) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |

## Gestational Diabetes Screening
| Approach | Timing | Diagnostic Criteria | URL |
|----------|--------|---------------------|-----|
| **One-Step (75g OGTT)** | **24-28 weeks** | **Fasting =92**, **1h =180**, **2h =153** (=1 abnormal = GDM) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Two-Step** | **24-28 weeks** | **1. 50g GLT** (non-fasting): **=130-140 ? 2. 100g OGTT** (fasting) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **100g OGTT (Carpenter-Coustan)** | After abnormal GLT | **Fasting =95**, **1h =180**, **2h =155**, **3h =140** (=2 abnormal = GDM) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Early Screening** | **First trimester** if high-risk (obesity, Prior GDM, Family history) | Use standard diagnostic criteria | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |

## Glycemic Targets in Pregnancy
| Parameter | Target | URL |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **Fasting** | **<95 mg/dL** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **1-hour Postprandial** | **<140 mg/dL** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **2-hour Postprandial** | **<120 mg/dL** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Mean Glucose** | **<100 mg/dL** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **HbA1c** | **<6.0-6.5%** (pregestational); **Not recommended** for GDM | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |

## GDM Management
| Intervention | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| **Medical Nutrition Therapy** | **Individualized carbohydrate distribution** (3 meals + 2-3 snacks) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Physical Activity** | **=30 min/day moderate activity** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Glucose Monitoring** | **Fasting + 1h or 2h postprandial** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Pharmacotherapy** | **Insulin** (first-line); **Metformin** or **Glyburide** (alternative) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Antenatal Testing** | **Start at 32 weeks** (or earlier if poorly controlled or comorbidities) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |

## Intrapartum Glycemic Management
| Glucose Level | Action | URL |
|---------------|--------|-----|
| **<70 mg/dL** | **D5W or D10W** infusion; Recheck in 15-30 min | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/pregestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **70-100 mg/dL** | **D5W** or **D5 1/2 NS** at 75-100 mL/h | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/pregestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **100-140 mg/dL** | **NS** or **LR** (no dextrose) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/pregestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **>140 mg/dL** | **Insulin infusion** (regular insulin) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/pregestational-diabetes-mellitus |

## Postpartum Diabetes Screening
| Test | Timing | Diagnostic Criteria | URL |
|------|--------|---------------------|-----|
| **75g OGTT** | **4-12 weeks postpartum** | **Fasting =126** or **2h =200** = Diabetes; **Fasting 100-125** or **2h 140-199** = Prediabetes | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
| **Lifelong Screening** | **Every 1-3 years** | HbA1c, Fasting glucose, or OGTT | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2018/02/gestational-diabetes-mellitus |
