# Obstetrics - Preterm Labor and Birth

## Definitions
| Term | Definition | URL |
|------|------------|-----|
| **Preterm Birth** | Delivery at **<37 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Late Preterm** | **34 0/7 - 36 6/7 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Moderate Preterm** | **32 0/7 - 33 6/7 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Very Preterm** | **28 0/7 - 31 6/7 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Extremely Preterm** | **<28 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |

## Risk Factors for Preterm Birth
| Category | Risk Factors | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Obstetric History** | **Prior spontaneous PTB** (strongest), Prior cervical surgery | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Uterine/Cervical** | **Short cervix** (<25 mm), Uterine anomalies, Fibroids | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Maternal** | **African American race**, Age <18 or >35, Low BMI, Periodontal disease | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Pregnancy-Related** | **Multiple gestation**, **Infection**, **Bleeding**, **Polyhydramnios/Oligohydramnios** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Lifestyle** | **Smoking**, **Substance use**, Stress | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |

## Prevention of Preterm Birth
| Intervention | Indication | Evidence | URL |
|--------------|------------|----------|-----|
| **Progesterone (17-OHPC)** | **Prior spontaneous PTB** (singleton) | **Strong** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Vaginal Progesterone** | **Short cervix =20 mm** (singleton, No prior PTB) | **Strong** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Cerclage** | **Prior spontaneous PTB + Short cervix <25 mm** | **Strong** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |
| **Smoking Cessation** | All pregnant women | **Strong** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/08/prediction-and-prevention-of-spontaneous-preterm-birth |

## Diagnosis of Preterm Labor
| Criteria | URL |
|----------|-----|
| **Gestational age 20-36 6/7 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |
| **Regular contractions** (=4 in 20 min or =8 in 60 min) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |
| **Cervical change** (dilation =3 cm or effacement =80%) OR **Cervical length <20 mm** with contractions | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |

## Management of Acute Preterm Labor (24-34 weeks)
| Intervention | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| **Corticosteroids** | **Betamethasone 12 mg IM q24h x2** OR **Dexamethasone 6 mg IM q12h x4** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |
| **Magnesium Sulfate** | **Fetal neuroprotection** if <32 weeks and delivery anticipated within 24h | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |
| **Tocolysis** | **Short-term** (48h) to allow corticosteroids and transfer | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |
| **GBS Prophylaxis** | If unknown GBS status or positive | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |
| **Antibiotics** | **NOT for routine tocolysis**; Only for GBS prophylaxis or PPROM | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2016/08/management-of-preterm-labor |
