# Obstetrics - Overview

## What is Obstetrics?
Obstetrics is the medical specialty dealing with pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period. It encompasses the care of the pregnant person and fetus from preconception through delivery and the immediate postpartum period.

| Core Concept | Description | URL |
|--------------|-------------|-----|
| **Gravidity (G)** | **Total number of pregnancies** (including current) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Parity (P)** | **Number of pregnancies reaching =20 weeks** (or viable gestational age) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **TPAL System** | **T**erm (=37 weeks), **P**reterm (20-36 weeks), **A**bortions (<20 weeks), **L**iving children | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Gestational Age** | Age of pregnancy calculated from **first day of last menstrual period (LMP)** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Estimated Date of Delivery (EDD)** | **Naegele's Rule**: LMP + 280 days (LMP - 3 months + 7 days + 1 year) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Trimesters** | **First**: 0-13 weeks; **Second**: 14-26 weeks; **Third**: 27 weeks to delivery | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Term Definitions** | **Early Term**: 37 0/7 - 38 6/7; **Full Term**: 39 0/7 - 40 6/7; **Late Term**: 41 0/7 - 41 6/7; **Postterm**: =42 0/7 | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
