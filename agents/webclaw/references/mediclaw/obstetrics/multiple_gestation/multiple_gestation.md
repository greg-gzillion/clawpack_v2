| Type | Timing of Division | Chorionicity | Amnionicity | URL |
|------|-------------------|--------------|-------------|-----|
| **Dichorionic Diamniotic (Di-Di)** | **=3 days** | **2 chorions** | **2 amnions** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Monochorionic Diamniotic (Mono-Di)** | **4-8 days** | **1 chorion** | **2 amnions** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |

| **Conjoined Twins** | **>13 days** | 1 chorion | 1 amnion | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |

## Twin-Specific Complications
| Complication | Chorionicity | Monitoring | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------------|------------|------------|-----|
| **Twin-Twin Transfusion Syndrome (TTTS)** | **Monochorionic** | **q2 weeks** ultrasound (discordant fluid, Bladders) | **Fetoscopic laser ablation** (Quintero stages) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Selective Fetal Growth Restriction (sFGR)** | **Monochorionic** | **q2 weeks** ultrasound, **Umbilical artery Doppler** | **Expectant**, **Selective reduction**, **Delivery** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Twin Anemia-Polycythemia Sequence (TAPS)** | **Monochorionic** | **MCA-PSV Doppler** | **Laser**, **IUT**, **Delivery** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Twin Reversed Arterial Perfusion (TRAP)** | **Monochorionic** | Acardiac twin | **Radiofrequency ablation** of acardiac twin | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Discordant Growth** | Any | Serial growth ultrasounds | Delivery based on **growth-restricted twin** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Single Fetal Demise** | **Monochorionic** | **? Risk of co-twin demise or neurologic injury** | **MRI of co-twin** at 32 weeks; Deliver at 34-36 weeks | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |

## Antenatal Surveillance in Twins
| Chorionicity | Surveillance | Timing | URL |
|--------------|--------------|--------|-----|
| **Dichorionic** | **Growth ultrasounds q4 weeks**, **Antenatal testing** if indicated | Start 28-32 weeks | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Monochorionic Diamniotic** | **Growth ultrasounds q2 weeks**, **Fluid/Bladder assessment q2 weeks** (TTTS surveillance) | Start 16 weeks | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
| **Monochorionic Monoamniotic** | **Inpatient surveillance** vs **Intensive outpatient** (q1-2 weeks) | Start 24-26 weeks | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/multiple-gestation |
