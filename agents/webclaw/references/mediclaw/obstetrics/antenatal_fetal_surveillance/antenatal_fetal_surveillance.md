# Obstetrics - Antenatal Fetal Surveillance

| Category | Examples | URL |
|----------|----------|-----|
| **Maternal Conditions** | **Diabetes** (pregestational, Gestational on medication), **Hypertensive disorders**, **CKD**, **SLE**, **Antiphospholipid syndrome**, **Cyanotic heart disease** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Fetal Conditions** | **Fetal growth restriction (FGR)** , **Oligohydramnios**, **Polyhydramnios**, **Decreased fetal movement**, **Multiple gestation** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Obstetric Conditions** | **Postterm pregnancy**, **Prior stillbirth**, **Alloimmunization** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |

## Methods of Antenatal Testing
| Test | Description | Reactive/Normal | Nonreactive/Abnormal | URL |
|------|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Nonstress Test (NST)** | Fetal heart rate monitoring without contractions | **=2 accelerations** (=15 bpm x 15 sec) in 20 min | **<2 accelerations** in 40 min | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Biophysical Profile (BPP)** | NST + Ultrasound parameters | **8-10/10** (or 8/8 without NST) | **=6/10** (consider delivery) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Modified BPP** | NST + **Amniotic Fluid Index (AFI)** | Reactive NST + AFI >5 cm | Nonreactive NST or AFI =5 cm | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Contraction Stress Test (CST)** | Assess fetal response to contractions | **No late decelerations** | **=50% of contractions have late decelerations** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Umbilical Artery Doppler** | Assess placental resistance | **S/D ratio normal**, **Absent/reversed end-diastolic flow absent** | **? S/D ratio**, **Absent/reversed end-diastolic flow** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |

## Biophysical Profile (BPP) Scoring
| Parameter | 2 Points | 0 Points | URL |
|-----------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Fetal Breathing Movements** | **=1 episode =30 sec** in 30 min | **<30 sec** in 30 min | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Gross Body Movements** | **=3 discrete movements** in 30 min | **<3 movements** in 30 min | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Fetal Tone** | **=1 episode of extension/flexion** | Slow/absent extension/flexion | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Amniotic Fluid Volume** | **=1 pocket =2 cm** in vertical axis | No pocket =2 cm | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **Nonstress Test (NST)** | **Reactive** | **Nonreactive** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |

## BPP Interpretation and Management
| Score | Interpretation | Management | URL |
|-------|----------------|------------|-----|
| **8-10/10** | **Normal** | Repeat per indication | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **6/10** | **Equivocal** | Consider delivery if =36-37 weeks; If <36 weeks, repeat in 24h | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **4/10** | **Abnormal** | **Delivery** (if =32 weeks or with oligohydramnios) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |
| **0-2/10** | **Severely abnormal** | **Delivery** (usually by cesarean) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/06/antepartum-fetal-surveillance |

## Umbilical Artery Doppler in FGR
| Waveform | Interpretation | Management | URL |
|----------|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Normal** | S/D ratio <95th percentile | Weekly Doppler | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Elevated S/D Ratio** | **Increased placental resistance** | Twice weekly surveillance | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Absent End-Diastolic Flow (AEDF)** | **Severe placental insufficiency** | **Delivery at 34 weeks** (or earlier with steroids) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Reversed End-Diastolic Flow (REDF)** | **Critical placental insufficiency** | **Delivery at 32 weeks** (or earlier with steroids) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
