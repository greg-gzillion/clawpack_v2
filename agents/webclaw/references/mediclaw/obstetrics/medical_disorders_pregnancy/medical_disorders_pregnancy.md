| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|

## Seizure Disorders in Pregnancy
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|

| **Preferred AEDs** | **Lamotrigine**, **Levetiracetam** (lowest teratogenic risk) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/04/seizure-disorders-in-pregnancy |
| **Avoid** | **Valproate** (highest teratogenic risk - neural tube defects, Cognitive impairment) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/04/seizure-disorders-in-pregnancy |

| **Vitamin K** | **10 mg/day** starting at **36 weeks** (controversial) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/04/seizure-disorders-in-pregnancy |

## Cardiac Disease in Pregnancy
| Condition | Risk | Management | URL |
|-----------|------|------------|-----|
| **Pulmonary Hypertension** | **mWHO Class IV** (extremely high risk, Pregnancy contraindicated) | **Termination offered**; Multidisciplinary care | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/05/pregnancy-and-heart-disease |
| **Mechanical Heart Valve** | **High risk** (thrombosis, Bleeding) | **Warfarin** throughout (or LMWH with close monitoring) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/05/pregnancy-and-heart-disease |
| **Marfan Syndrome** | **? Risk of aortic dissection** (especially if aortic root >4.0-4.5 cm) | **Beta-blockers**, **Multidisciplinary care**, **Cesarean** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/05/pregnancy-and-heart-disease |
| **Peripartum Cardiomyopathy** | **LVEF <45%** in late pregnancy or postpartum | **Standard HF therapy** (avoid ACEi/ARBs during pregnancy) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/05/pregnancy-and-heart-disease |
