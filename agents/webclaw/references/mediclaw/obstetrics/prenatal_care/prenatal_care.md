# Obstetrics - Prenatal Care

## Preconception Care
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Folic Acid** | **400-800 mcg/day** starting at least 1 month before conception | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/folicacid/recommendations.html |
| **Immunizations** | Ensure **MMR**, **Varicella**, **Tdap**, **Hepatitis B**, **Influenza**, **COVID-19** up to date | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/pregnancy/index.html |
| **Chronic Disease Management** | Optimize control of **diabetes**, **hypertension**, **thyroid disease**, **seizures**, **mental health** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Medication Review** | Avoid **teratogens** (ACEi, ARBs, Valproate, Warfarin, Isotretinoin, Methotrexate) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Substance Use** | **Stop tobacco**, **Alcohol**, **Illicit drugs** | https://www.cdc.gov/pregnancy/features/substance-use.html |
| **Genetic Carrier Screening** | Offer **CF**, **Hemoglobinopathies**, **Tay-Sachs**, **SMA**, **Fragile X** based on ancestry/family history | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2017/03/carrier-screening-in-the-age-of-genomic-medicine |

## Routine Prenatal Visit Schedule (ACOG)
| Gestational Age | Visits | Key Components | URL |
|-----------------|--------|----------------|-----|
| **6-12 weeks** | Initial visit | **Confirm pregnancy**, **Establish EDD**, **Prenatal labs**, **Genetic screening options**, **Ultrasound** (dating/viability) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **16 weeks** | Visit | Fundal height, Fetal heart tones, **Quad screen** (if elected) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **18-22 weeks** | Visit | **Anatomy ultrasound**, Fundal height, Fetal heart tones | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **24-28 weeks** | Visit | **Diabetes screening** (1-hour glucose challenge), **Rhogam** (if Rh-negative), Fundal height, Fetal heart tones | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **28-36 weeks** | q2-3 weeks | Fundal height, Fetal heart tones, **Tdap** (27-36 weeks), **RSV vaccine** (32-36 weeks seasonal), **GBS screening** (36-37 weeks) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **36 weeks to delivery** | Weekly | Fundal height, Fetal heart tones, **Cervical exam** (if indicated), **Discuss labor/delivery plan** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |

## Initial Prenatal Labs
| Test | Purpose | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| **CBC** | Screen for **anemia**, **Thrombocytopenia** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Blood Type, Rh, Antibody Screen** | Identify **Rh-negative** status, **Atypical antibodies** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Rubella Titer** | Identify non-immune women (vaccinate postpartum) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Varicella Titer** | Identify non-immune women (vaccinate postpartum) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **HBsAg** | Screen for **Hepatitis B** (prevent perinatal transmission) | https://www.cdc.gov/hepatitis/hbv/perinatalxmtn.htm |
| **RPR/VDRL** | Screen for **Syphilis** (universal) | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/syphilis-pregnancy.htm |
| **HIV** | Screen for **HIV** (opt-out) | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/testing/index.html |
| **Urinalysis/Urine Culture** | Screen for **Asymptomatic bacteriuria** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Chlamydia/Gonorrhea** | Screen for **STIs** | https://www.cdc.gov/std/treatment-guidelines/default.htm |
| **Cervical Cytology** | If due for screening (per ASCCP guidelines) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Hemoglobin Electrophoresis** | If at risk for **Hemoglobinopathy** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **TSH** | If history of thyroid disease or symptoms | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |

## Prenatal Weight Gain Recommendations (IOM)
| Pre-pregnancy BMI | Recommended Weight Gain | URL |
|-------------------|------------------------|-----|
| **Underweight (<18.5)** | **28-40 lbs** (12.5-18 kg) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2013/01/weight-gain-during-pregnancy |
| **Normal Weight (18.5-24.9)** | **25-35 lbs** (11.5-16 kg) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2013/01/weight-gain-during-pregnancy |
| **Overweight (25-29.9)** | **15-25 lbs** (7-11.5 kg) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2013/01/weight-gain-during-pregnancy |
| **Obese (=30)** | **11-20 lbs** (5-9 kg) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2013/01/weight-gain-during-pregnancy |
