# Obstetrics - Fetal Growth Disorders

## Fetal Growth Restriction (FGR)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Estimated fetal weight (EFW) <10th percentile** for gestational age | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Early-Onset FGR** | Diagnosed **<32 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Late-Onset FGR** | Diagnosed **=32 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |

## Etiology of FGR
| Category | Causes | URL |
|----------|--------|-----|
| **Placental** | **Uteroplacental insufficiency** (most common), Abnormal placentation, Infarction | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Maternal** | **Hypertensive disorders**, **CKD**, **SLE**, **Thrombophilia**, **Malnutrition**, **Substance use** (tobacco, alcohol, cocaine) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Fetal** | **Aneuploidy** (triploidy, Trisomy 18), **Congenital infection** (CMV, Toxoplasmosis), **Structural anomalies** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |

| Modality | Frequency | Abnormal Findings | URL |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| **Umbilical Artery Doppler** | **Weekly** (if early-onset) | **? S/D ratio**, **AEDF**, **REDF** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **NST/BPP** | **1-2x/week** | **Nonreactive NST**, **BPP =6** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **Growth Ultrasounds** | **q3-4 weeks** | **Arrested growth**, **Crossing percentiles** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |
| **MCA Doppler** | If anemia suspected or AEDF/REDF | **? PSV** (anemia) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |

| **FGR + Oligohydramnios** | **36-37 weeks** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2021/02/fetal-growth-restriction |

## Large for Gestational Age (LGA) and Macrosomia
| Term | Definition | URL |
|------|------------|-----|
| **LGA** | **EFW or Birth weight >90th percentile** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/01/macrosomia |
| **Macrosomia** | **Birth weight =4,000 g** (regardless of gestational age) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/01/macrosomia |
| **Risk Factors** | **Diabetes** (pregestational or GDM), **Obesity**, **Excessive weight gain**, **Postterm**, **Prior macrosomic infant** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/01/macrosomia |

## Macrosomia Management
| Consideration | Recommendation | URL |
|---------------|----------------|-----|
| **Suspected Macrosomia (EFW =4,500g with Diabetes)** | **Offer cesarean delivery** (reduces shoulder dystocia) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/01/macrosomia |
| **Suspected Macrosomia (EFW =5,000g without Diabetes)** | **Offer cesarean delivery** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/01/macrosomia |
| **Induction for Suspected Macrosomia** | **Not recommended** (does not improve outcomes) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2020/01/macrosomia |
