# Obstetrics - Postpartum Care

## Postpartum Visit Schedule (ACOG)
| Timing | Components | URL |
|--------|------------|-----|
| **Initial Contact (1-3 weeks)** | **Phone or in-person**: Assess **bleeding**, **Pain**, **Breastfeeding**, **Mood**, **Contraception** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2018/05/optimizing-postpartum-care |
| **Comprehensive Visit (4-12 weeks)** | **In-person**: Full assessment, **Depression screening** (PHQ-9), **Contraception**, **Chronic disease follow-up**, **Pelvic exam** (if indicated) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2018/05/optimizing-postpartum-care |
| **Ongoing Care** | **Transition to primary care**; **Cardiovascular risk assessment** (especially after preeclampsia, GDM) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2018/05/optimizing-postpartum-care |

## Postpartum Depression Screening
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Screening Tool** | **PHQ-9** or **Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (EPDS)** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2018/11/screening-for-perinatal-depression |
| **Timing** | **At least once** during perinatal period; Comprehensive postpartum visit | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2018/11/screening-for-perinatal-depression |
| **Positive Screen** | **Refer for mental health evaluation** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2018/11/screening-for-perinatal-depression |
| **Treatment** | **Psychotherapy** (CBT, IPT), **SSRI** (sertraline preferred for breastfeeding) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/committee-opinion/articles/2018/11/screening-for-perinatal-depression |

## Postpartum Contraception
| Method | Timing | Breastfeeding | URL |
|--------|--------|---------------|-----|
| **LARC (IUD, Implant)** | **Immediate postpartum** (before discharge) or **Interval** (4-6 weeks) | **Safe** (progestin-only) | https://www.cdc.gov/reproductivehealth/contraception/mmwr/mec/summary.html |
| **Progestin-Only Pill** | **Immediate** | **Safe** | https://www.cdc.gov/reproductivehealth/contraception/mmwr/mec/summary.html |
| **Combined Hormonal Contraception** | **=3 weeks postpartum** (if no VTE risk factors) OR **=6 weeks** (if breastfeeding) | **May reduce milk supply** (delay until 6 weeks) | https://www.cdc.gov/reproductivehealth/contraception/mmwr/mec/summary.html |
| **DMPA (Depo-Provera)** | **Immediate** | **Safe** | https://www.cdc.gov/reproductivehealth/contraception/mmwr/mec/summary.html |
| **Tubal Ligation** | **Postpartum** (at cesarean or postpartum) or **Interval** | **Permanent** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2019/01/sterilization |

## Rhogam Postpartum
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Indication** | **Rh-negative mother** with **Rh-positive infant** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2017/06/management-of-alloimmunization-during-pregnancy |
| **Dose** | **300 mcg IM** within **72 hours of delivery** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2017/06/management-of-alloimmunization-during-pregnancy |
| **Kleihauer-Betke Test** | Quantify **fetomaternal hemorrhage**; **Additional Rhogam** if >30 mL | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/practice-bulletin/articles/2017/06/management-of-alloimmunization-during-pregnancy |
