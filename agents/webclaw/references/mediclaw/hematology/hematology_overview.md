# Hematology - Overview

## What is Hematology?
Hematology is the medical specialty concerned with the study, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the blood and bone marrow, including disorders of red blood cells, white blood cells, platelets, and the coagulation system.

## Hematopoiesis
| Lineage | Mature Cells | Primary Function |
|---------|--------------|------------------|
| **Erythroid** | Red Blood Cells (Erythrocytes) | Oxygen transport |
| **Myeloid** | Neutrophils, Eosinophils, Basophils, Monocytes/Macrophages | Innate immunity, Phagocytosis |
| **Lymphoid** | B cells, T cells, NK cells | Adaptive immunity |
| **Megakaryocytic** | Platelets | Hemostasis |
