# Addiction Medicine - Cannabis Use Disorder

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Problematic pattern of cannabis use** leading to clinically significant impairment or distress | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/marijuana |
| **Prevalence** | **~5-6%** of US adults (past year CUD); **~30%** of users develop CUD | https://www.samhsa.gov/data/report/2021-nsduh-annual-national-report |
| **Potency** | **THC content has increased** from ~4% (1995) to **>15-30%** (current) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/marijuana |
