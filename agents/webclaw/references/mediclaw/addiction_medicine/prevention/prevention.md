# Addiction Medicine - Prevention

## Prevention Framework (IOM)
| Level | Description | Examples | URL |
|-------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Universal** | **Entire population** | **School-based education**, **Media campaigns**, **Policies** (taxation, Age limits) | https://www.samhsa.gov/prevention |
| **Selective** | **At-risk subgroups** | **Children of parents with SUD**, **Youth in foster care** | https://www.samhsa.gov/prevention |
| **Indicated** | **Early signs of problematic use** | **SBIRT**, **Brief intervention** | https://www.samhsa.gov/prevention |
