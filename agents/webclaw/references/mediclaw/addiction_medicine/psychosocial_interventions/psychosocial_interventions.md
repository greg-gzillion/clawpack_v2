# Addiction Medicine - Psychosocial Interventions

## Evidence-Based Psychosocial Interventions
| Intervention | Description | Evidence | URL |
|--------------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** | **Identify and change maladaptive thoughts/behaviors** | **Strong** (all SUDs) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/behavioral-therapies |
| **Motivational Interviewing (MI)** | **Enhance intrinsic motivation** | **Strong** (engagement) | https://motivationalinterviewing.org |
| **Motivational Enhancement Therapy (MET)** | **Structured MI + Feedback** | **Strong** (alcohol, cannabis) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/behavioral-therapies |
| **Contingency Management (CM)** | **Voucher-based reinforcement** for abstinence | **Strong** (stimulants, opioids) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/behavioral-therapies |
| **Community Reinforcement Approach (CRA)** | **Reinforce non-drug activities** | **Strong** (alcohol, stimulants) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/behavioral-therapies |
| **Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)** | **Emotion regulation**, **Distress tolerance** | **Moderate** (co-occurring) | https://behavioraltech.org |
| **12-Step Facilitation (TSF)** | **Engage in mutual-help groups** | **Strong** (alcohol, opioids) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/behavioral-therapies |
| **Family Behavior Therapy (FBT)** | **Engage family in treatment** | **Strong** (adolescents) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/behavioral-therapies |
