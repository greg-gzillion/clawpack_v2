# Addiction Medicine - Acute Intoxication and Withdrawal Management

## Acute Intoxication Management
| Substance | Management | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Alcohol** | **Supportive care**, **Thiamine**, **Monitor for withdrawal** | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |
| **Opioids** | **Naloxone** for overdose, **Supportive care** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Stimulants** | **Benzodiazepines** (agitation), **Active cooling**, **IV fluids** | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/stimulants |
| **Benzodiazepines** | **Supportive care**, **Flumazenil** (rarely - seizures) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/prescription-drugs |
| **Cannabis** | **Supportive care**, **Benzodiazepines** (severe anxiety) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/marijuana |
| **Hallucinogens** | **Supportive care**, **Benzodiazepines** (agitation), **"Talk down"** | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/psychedelic-dissociative-drugs |

## Withdrawal Management by Substance
| Substance | Management | Level of Care | URL |
|-----------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **Alcohol** | **Benzodiazepines** (CIWA-Ar protocol), **Thiamine** | **Outpatient** (mild-moderate), **Inpatient** (severe, Comorbidities) | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |
| **Opioids** | **Buprenorphine** or **Methadone** (taper), **Clonidine**, **Symptomatic** | **Outpatient** (motivated), **Inpatient** (unstable) | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Benzodiazepines** | **Slow taper** (weeks-months), **Convert to long-acting** | **Outpatient** (most), **Inpatient** (severe, Unstable) | https://www.benzo.org.uk/manual/ |
| **Stimulants** | **Supportive care** (no FDA-approved medications) | **Outpatient** (most) | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/stimulants |
