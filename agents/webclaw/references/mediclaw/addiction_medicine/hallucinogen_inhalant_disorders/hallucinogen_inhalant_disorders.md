## Hallucinogen Persisting Perception Disorder (HPPD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Persistent visual disturbances** (visual snow, Trails, Halos) after hallucinogen use | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/psychedelic-dissociative-drugs |
| **Treatment** | **Limited evidence**; **Clonazepam**, **Lamotrigine**, **Abstinence** | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/psychedelic-dissociative-drugs |
