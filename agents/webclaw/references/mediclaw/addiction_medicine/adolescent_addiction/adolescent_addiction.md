# Addiction Medicine - Adolescent Addiction

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Unique Vulnerabilities** | **Developing brain** (prefrontal cortex), **Impulsivity**, **Peer influence**, **Academic stress** | https://www.nida.nih.gov/research-topics/adolescent-brain |
| **Prevalence** | **~8%** of adolescents have SUD | https://www.samhsa.gov/data/report/2021-nsduh-annual-national-report |
| **Screening** | **CRAFFT** (6-item validated tool) | https://crafft.org |

## CRAFFT Screening Tool
| Question | URL |
|----------|-----|
| **C** - Have you ever ridden in a **C**ar driven by someone who was high? | https://crafft.org |
| **R** - Do you ever use alcohol or drugs to **R**elax? | https://crafft.org |
| **A** - Do you ever use alcohol or drugs while **A**lone? | https://crafft.org |
| **F** - Do you ever **F**orget things you did while using? | https://crafft.org |
| **F** - Do your **F**amily or friends tell you to cut down? | https://crafft.org |
| **T** - Have you ever gotten into **T**rouble while using? | https://crafft.org |
| **Scoring** | **=2 "Yes" = High risk** | https://crafft.org |
