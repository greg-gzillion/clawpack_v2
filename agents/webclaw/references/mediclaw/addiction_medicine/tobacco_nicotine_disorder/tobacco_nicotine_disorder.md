# Addiction Medicine - Tobacco and Nicotine Use Disorder

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Problematic pattern of tobacco/nicotine use** leading to clinically significant impairment or distress | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/index.htm |
| **Prevalence** | **~12-14%** of US adults smoke cigarettes | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/data_statistics/fact_sheets/adult_data/cig_smoking/index.htm |
| **Health Consequences** | **Leading cause of preventable death** (cardiovascular, pulmonary, Cancer) | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/health_effects/index.htm |

## Pharmacotherapy for Tobacco Cessation
| Medication | Dose | Duration | Considerations | URL |
|------------|------|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Nicotine Patch** | **21 mg/day** (if >10 cig/day) | **8-12 weeks** (taper) | **Start on quit date**; Rotate sites | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Nicotine Gum** | **2-4 mg q1-2h PRN** | **Up to 12 weeks** | **Chew and park**; Avoid acidic drinks | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Nicotine Lozenge** | **2-4 mg q1-2h PRN** | **Up to 12 weeks** | **Dissolve slowly** | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Varenicline** | **1 mg BID** | **12-24 weeks** | **Start 1 week before quit date**; Nausea, Vivid dreams | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/varenicline |
| **Bupropion SR** | **150 mg BID** | **12-24 weeks** | **Start 1-2 weeks before quit date**; Seizure risk | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/bupropion |
| **Combination NRT** | **Patch + PRN gum/lozenge** | **As above** | **More effective than single NRT** | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |

## E-Cigarettes and Vaping
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **For Cessation** | **Not FDA-approved**; May help some adults quit but evidence limited | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/index.htm |
| **Dual Use** | **Avoid** (continuing both cigarettes and e-cigarettes) | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/index.htm |
| **Youth** | **Strongly discourage** (nicotine harms developing brain) | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/index.htm |
| **EVALI** | **E-cigarette or Vaping Associated Lung Injury** (associated with THC products, Vitamin E acetate) | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/severe-lung-disease.html |
