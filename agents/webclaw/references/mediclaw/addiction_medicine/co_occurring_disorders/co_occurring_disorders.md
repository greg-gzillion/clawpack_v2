# Addiction Medicine - Co-Occurring Disorders

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Co-occurrence of SUD and mental health disorder** | https://www.samhsa.gov/co-occurring-disorders |
| **Prevalence** | **~50%** of individuals with SUD have co-occurring mental health disorder | https://www.samhsa.gov/co-occurring-disorders |
| **Integrated Treatment** | **Simultaneous treatment** of both disorders (preferred over sequential or parallel) | https://www.samhsa.gov/co-occurring-disorders |
