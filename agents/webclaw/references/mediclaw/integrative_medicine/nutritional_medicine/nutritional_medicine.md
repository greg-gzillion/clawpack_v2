# Integrative Medicine - Nutritional Medicine

## Therapeutic Diets
| Diet | Description | Evidence-Based Indications | URL |
|------|-------------|---------------------------|-----|
| **Mediterranean Diet** | High in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, olive oil, fish | **Strong evidence**: CVD prevention, Diabetes, Cognitive health | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/heart-healthy-eating-plans/mediterranean-diet |
| **DASH Diet** | Low sodium, High potassium/magnesium/calcium | **Strong evidence**: Hypertension | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/education/dash-eating-plan |
| **Anti-Inflammatory Diet** | Emphasis on omega-3s, phytonutrients, fiber | **Moderate evidence**: Chronic inflammation, Autoimmune | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/antioxidants-and-health |
| **Low FODMAP Diet** | Restriction of fermentable carbohydrates | **Strong evidence**: IBS | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases/irritable-bowel-syndrome/treatment |
| **Gluten-Free Diet** | Elimination of gluten-containing grains | **Required**: Celiac disease; **Limited**: Non-celiac gluten sensitivity | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases/celiac-disease |

## Clinical Applications of Nutritional Medicine
| Condition | Dietary Approach | Evidence | URL |
|-----------|------------------|----------|-----|
| **Cardiovascular Disease** | Mediterranean, DASH, Portfolio Diet | **Strong** | https://www.acc.org/latest-in-cardiology/articles/2021/03/22/14/29/diet-and-cardiovascular-disease |
| **Type 2 Diabetes** | Mediterranean, Low-carb, Plant-based | **Strong** | https://professional.diabetes.org/sites/default/files/media/nutrition_consensus_report.pdf |
| **Obesity** | Caloric restriction, Mediterranean, Low-carb | **Strong** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/weight-management |
| **Inflammatory Bowel Disease** | Exclusive enteral nutrition (Crohn), Specific carbohydrate diet | **Moderate** | https://www.crohnscolitisfoundation.org/diet-and-nutrition |
| **Cognitive Decline** | Mediterranean, MIND diet | **Moderate** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/what-do-we-know-about-diet-and-prevention-alzheimers-disease |
