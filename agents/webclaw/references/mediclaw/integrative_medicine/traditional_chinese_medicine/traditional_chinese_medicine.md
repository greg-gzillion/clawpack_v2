# Integrative Medicine - Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)

## Overview
Traditional Chinese Medicine is a complete medical system that has been used for over 2,500 years. It includes acupuncture, herbal medicine, dietary therapy, massage (Tui Na), and exercise (Tai Chi, Qigong).

## Acupuncture
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Mechanism** | Neurohumoral effects (endorphins, adenosine), Connective tissue stimulation | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/acupuncture-what-you-need-to-know |
| **Evidence-Based Indications** | **Strong**: Chronic pain (low back, neck, OA, headache); **Moderate**: Nausea, Allergic rhinitis | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/acupuncture-effectiveness-and-safety |
| **Meta-Analysis Findings** | Acupuncture superior to sham and usual care for chronic pain | https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/29198932/ |
| **Safety** | Generally safe with trained practitioner; Rare adverse events (pneumothorax, infection) | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/acupuncture-in-depth |
