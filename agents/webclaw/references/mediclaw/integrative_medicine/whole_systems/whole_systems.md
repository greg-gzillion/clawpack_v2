# Integrative Medicine - Whole Systems Approaches

## Overview
Whole medical systems are complete systems of theory and practice that have evolved independently from conventional Western medicine.

| System | Origin | Core Principles | URL |
|--------|--------|-----------------|-----|
| **Traditional Chinese Medicine** | China | Qi, Yin-Yang, Five Elements, Meridians | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/traditional-chinese-medicine-what-you-need-to-know |
| **Ayurveda** | India | Doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), Agni, Prakriti | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/ayurvedic-medicine-in-depth |
| **Naturopathy** | Europe/North America | Healing power of nature, Identify and treat cause | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/naturopathy |
| **Homeopathy** | Germany | "Like cures like," Law of minimum dose | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/homeopathy |
| **Functional Medicine** | United States | Systems biology approach, Root cause analysis | https://www.ifm.org |
