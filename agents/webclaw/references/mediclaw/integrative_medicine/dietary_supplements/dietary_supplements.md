| **Vitamin B12** | RDA: 2.4 mcg/day; Deficiency common in elderly, vegans | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/VitaminB12-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Magnesium** | RDA: 310-420 mg/day; Forms: citrate, glycinate (better absorbed) | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Magnesium-HealthProfessional/ |
