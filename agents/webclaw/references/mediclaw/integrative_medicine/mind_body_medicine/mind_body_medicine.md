# Integrative Medicine - Mind-Body Medicine

## Overview
Mind-body medicine focuses on the interactions between the brain, mind, body, and behavior, and the powerful ways in which emotional, mental, social, spiritual, and behavioral factors can directly affect health.

| Practice | Description | Evidence Base | URL |
|----------|-------------|---------------|-----|
| **Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR)** | 8-week program teaching mindfulness meditation | **Strong evidence** for anxiety, depression, chronic pain | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/meditation-and-mindfulness-what-you-need-to-know |
| **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** | Evidence-based psychotherapy | **Gold standard** for anxiety, depression, insomnia | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/psychotherapies |
| **Biofeedback** | Real-time monitoring of physiologic functions | **Moderate evidence** for headache, hypertension, incontinence | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/biofeedback |
| **Clinical Hypnosis** | Focused attention and heightened suggestibility | **Moderate evidence** for pain, IBS, procedure anxiety | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/hypnosis |
| **Guided Imagery** | Using mental images to promote relaxation | **Limited-moderate evidence** for anxiety, pain | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/guided-imagery |
| **Progressive Muscle Relaxation** | Systematic tensing and relaxing of muscle groups | **Moderate evidence** for anxiety, insomnia | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/relaxation-techniques-what-you-need-to-know |
