# Integrative Medicine - Overview

## What is Integrative Medicine?
Integrative medicine combines conventional medical treatments with evidence-based complementary therapies to address the whole person𤪳ody, mind, and spirit. It emphasizes the therapeutic relationship and makes use of all appropriate therapeutic approaches, healthcare professionals, and disciplines to achieve optimal health and healing.

| Core Principle | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Patient-Centered Care** | Treating the whole person, not just the disease | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/integrative-health |
| **Evidence-Informed** | Combining conventional medicine with proven complementary approaches | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/complementary-alternative-or-integrative-health-whats-in-a-name |
| **Prevention Focus** | Emphasis on lifestyle, nutrition, and wellness | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/wellness-and-well-being |
| **Partnership** | Collaborative relationship between practitioner and patient | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/integrative-health |
