# Integrative Medicine - Energy Medicine

## Overview
Energy medicine involves therapies that are based on the belief that there are subtle energy fields in and around the body. These include veritable energy fields (measurable, like light and sound) and putative energy fields (not yet scientifically measured, like "life force").

| Therapy | Type | Description | URL |
|---------|------|-------------|-----|
| **Acupuncture** | Putative | Stimulation of specific points to balance qi | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/acupuncture-what-you-need-to-know |
| **Reiki** | Putative | Channeling universal life force energy through hands | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/reiki |
| **Therapeutic Touch** | Putative | Manipulation of human energy field | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/therapeutic-touch |
| **Healing Touch** | Putative | Energy-based approach to health and healing | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/healing-touch |
| **Pulsed Electromagnetic Field (PEMF)** | Veritable | FDA-approved for bone healing, depression (TMS) | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices |

## Reiki
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/reiki |
| **Method** | Practitioner places hands lightly on or just above patient | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/reiki |
| **Evidence** | **Limited**; Some benefit for pain, anxiety, depression | https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25703086/ |
| **Safety** | Generally considered safe; Not a substitute for conventional care | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/reiki |

## Therapeutic Touch
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
