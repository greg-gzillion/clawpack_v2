## IVF Cycle Steps
| Step | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **1. Ovarian Stimulation** | **Gonadotropins** to stimulate multiple follicle development | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **2. Monitoring** | **Ultrasound** and **Estradiol** levels | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **3. Trigger** | **hCG** or **GnRH agonist** to induce final oocyte maturation | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **4. Oocyte Retrieval** | **Transvaginal ultrasound-guided aspiration** (~36h after trigger) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **5. Fertilization** | **Conventional IVF** or **ICSI** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **6. Embryo Culture** | **Culture to blastocyst** (day 5-6) or **cleavage stage** (day 3) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **7. Embryo Transfer** | **Fresh transfer** or **Freeze-all** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **8. Luteal Support** | **Progesterone** supplementation | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **9. Pregnancy Test** | **hCG** ~14 days after retrieval | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |

## IVF Stimulation Protocols
| Protocol | Description | Indications | URL |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **GnRH Antagonist** | **GnRH antagonist** added mid-follicular to prevent premature LH surge | **Most patients**, **Lower OHSS risk** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **GnRH Agonist Long** | **GnRH agonist** started in luteal phase ? **Pituitary downregulation** | **Endometriosis**, **Fibroids** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **GnRH Agonist Flare** | **GnRH agonist** started in follicular phase ? **Initial flare** of FSH/LH | **Poor responders** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **Minimal Stimulation** | **Low-dose gonadotropins** or **Clomiphene/Letrozole** | **Poor responders**, **Cost constraints** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
