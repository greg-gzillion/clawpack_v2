## Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome (OHSS)
| Grade | Symptoms | Management | URL |
|-------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Mild** | **Abdominal bloating**, **Mild pain**, **Ovaries <8 cm** | **Outpatient**, **Hydration**, **Analgesics** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ohss/ |
| **Moderate** | **Nausea/Vomiting**, **Ascites on US**, **Ovaries 8-12 cm** | **Outpatient or Inpatient**, **IV fluids**, **Monitor** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ohss/ |
| **Severe** | **Tense ascites**, **Oliguria**, **Hemoconcentration**, **Thromboembolism**, **Respiratory distress** | **Inpatient**, **IV fluids**, **Paracentesis**, **Anticoagulation**, **ICU** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ohss/ |
| **Critical** | **Renal failure**, **ARDS**, **Thromboembolism** | **ICU** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ohss/ |
