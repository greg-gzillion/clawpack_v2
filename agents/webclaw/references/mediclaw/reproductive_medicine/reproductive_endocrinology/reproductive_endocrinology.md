# Reproductive Medicine - Reproductive Endocrinology

## Hypothalamic-Pituitary-Ovarian (HPO) Axis
| Component | Hormone | Function | URL |
|-----------|---------|----------|-----|
| **Hypothalamus** | **GnRH** (pulsatile) | **Stimulates pituitary** to release FSH and LH | https://www.asrm.org/topics/reproductive-endocrinology/ |
| **Anterior Pituitary** | **FSH** | **Follicular development**, **Estrogen production**, **Spermatogenesis** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/reproductive-endocrinology/ |
| **Anterior Pituitary** | **LH** | **Ovulation**, **Luteinization**, **Testosterone production** (Leydig cells) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/reproductive-endocrinology/ |
| **Ovary** | **Estradiol (E2)** | **Endometrial proliferation**, **Secondary sexual characteristics**, **Negative feedback** (late follicular positive feedback) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/reproductive-endocrinology/ |
| **Ovary** | **Progesterone (P4)** | **Endometrial secretory transformation**, **Supports early pregnancy** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/reproductive-endocrinology/ |
| **Ovary** | **AMH** | **Produced by granulosa cells** of preantral/antral follicles; **Marker of ovarian reserve** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/reproductive-endocrinology/ |
| **Ovary** | **Inhibin B** | **Negative feedback on FSH**; Produced by developing follicles | https://www.asrm.org/topics/reproductive-endocrinology/ |

## Ovarian Reserve Testing
| Test | Normal | Diminished Reserve | URL |
|------|--------|---------------------|-----|
| **AMH** | **>1.0 ng/mL** | **<1.0 ng/mL** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ovarian-reserve/ |
| **AFC (Antral Follicle Count)** | **>10** (total) | **<5-7** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ovarian-reserve/ |
