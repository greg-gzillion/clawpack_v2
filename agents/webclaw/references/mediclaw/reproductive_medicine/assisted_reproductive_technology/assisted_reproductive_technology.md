## Overview

|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **Procedures** | **IVF**, **ICSI**, **Embryo transfer**, **Cryopreservation**, **Gamete intrafallopian transfer (GIFT)** , **Zygote intrafallopian transfer (ZIFT)** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/art/ |

## ART Procedures
| Procedure | Description | Indications | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **IVF** | **Fertilization of oocytes by sperm in vitro** | **Tubal factor**, **Endometriosis**, **Unexplained**, **Male factor** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ivf/ |
| **ICSI** | **Injection of single sperm into oocyte** | **Severe male factor**, **Prior fertilization failure**, **PGT**, **Cryopreserved oocytes** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/icsi/ |
| **IUI** | **Placement of washed sperm into uterine cavity** | **Unexplained**, **Cervical factor**, **Mild male factor**, **Donor sperm** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/iui/ |
| **GIFT** | **Placement of oocytes and sperm into fallopian tube** | **Unexplained** (requires laparoscopy) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/art/ |
| **ZIFT** | **Placement of zygote into fallopian tube** | **Unexplained** (requires laparoscopy) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/art/ |
