# Reproductive Medicine - Fertility Preservation

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Preservation of reproductive potential** for future use | https://www.asrm.org/topics/fertility-preservation/ |
| **Indications** | **Medical** (cancer treatment), **Elective** (age-related fertility decline), **Transgender** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/fertility-preservation/ |

## Fertility Preservation Options for Females
| Option | Description | Indications | URL |
|--------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Oocyte Cryopreservation** | **Freezing unfertilized oocytes** | **Postpubertal**, **No male partner**, **Elective** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/oocyte-cryopreservation/ |
| **Embryo Cryopreservation** | **Freezing fertilized embryos** | **Postpubertal**, **Male partner or donor sperm** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/embryo-cryopreservation/ |
| **Ovarian Tissue Cryopreservation** | **Freezing ovarian cortical tissue** | **Prepubertal**, **Cannot delay cancer treatment** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ovarian-tissue-cryopreservation/ |
| **Ovarian Transposition** | **Moving ovaries out of radiation field** | **Pelvic radiation** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/ovarian-transposition/ |
| **GnRH Agonist** | **Ovarian suppression** during chemotherapy | **Breast cancer** (controversial) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/gnrh-agonist/ |

## Oocyte Cryopreservation Outcomes
| Age at Freezing | Live Birth per Oocyte | Recommended Number to Freeze | URL |
|-----------------|----------------------|----------------------------|-----|
| **<35** | **~5-10%** | **10-15 oocytes** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/oocyte-cryopreservation/ |
| **35-37** | **~3-7%** | **15-20 oocytes** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/oocyte-cryopreservation/ |
| **38-40** | **~2-5%** | **20-30 oocytes** | https://www.asrm.org/topics/oocyte-cryopreservation/ |
| **>40** | **<2%** | **>30 oocytes** (consider donor oocyte) | https://www.asrm.org/topics/oocyte-cryopreservation/ |
