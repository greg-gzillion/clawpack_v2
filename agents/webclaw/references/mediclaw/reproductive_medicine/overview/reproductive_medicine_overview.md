# Reproductive Medicine - Overview

## What is Reproductive Medicine?
Reproductive medicine is the branch of medicine concerned with the prevention, diagnosis, and management of reproductive problems. It encompasses the evaluation and treatment of infertility, reproductive endocrinology, assisted reproductive technology (ART), and fertility preservation.

## Infertility Definition (ASRM)
| Definition | Criteria | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Infertility** | **Failure to achieve pregnancy after 12 months** of regular unprotected intercourse (or **6 months** if age =35) | https://www.asrm.org/practice-guidance/practice-committee-documents/definitions-of-infertility/ |
| **Primary Infertility** | **Never achieved pregnancy** | https://www.asrm.org/practice-guidance/practice-committee-documents/definitions-of-infertility/ |
| **Secondary Infertility** | **Prior pregnancy** but unable to conceive again | https://www.asrm.org/practice-guidance/practice-committee-documents/definitions-of-infertility/ |
| **Fecundability** | **Probability of achieving pregnancy** in one menstrual cycle (~20-25% in healthy couples) | https://www.asrm.org/practice-guidance/practice-committee-documents/definitions-of-infertility/ |
