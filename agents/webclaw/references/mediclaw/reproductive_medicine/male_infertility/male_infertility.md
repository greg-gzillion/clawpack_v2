## Treatment of Male Infertility
| Condition | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Varicocele** | **Varicocelectomy** (if palpable and abnormal SA) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Obstructive Azoospermia** | **Microsurgical reconstruction** or **Sperm retrieval + IVF/ICSI** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Non-Obstructive Azoospermia** | **MicroTESE** + IVF/ICSI | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Hypogonadotropic Hypogonadism** | **hCG + FSH** (or hCG alone) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
| **Idiopathic** | **Empiric therapy** (clomiphene, Anastrozole), **IUI**, **IVF/ICSI** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/male-infertility |
