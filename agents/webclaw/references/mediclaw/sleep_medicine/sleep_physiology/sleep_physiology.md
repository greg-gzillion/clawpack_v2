# Sleep Medicine - Sleep Physiology

## Sleep Stages (AASM Scoring Manual)
| Stage | EEG Characteristics | EOG | EMG | % of Total Sleep | URL |
|-------|---------------------|-----|-----|------------------|-----|
| **Wake** | **Alpha** (8-13 Hz) with eyes closed, Eye blinks | Rapid eye movements | High tonic activity | - | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **N1** | **Theta** (4-7 Hz), **Vertex sharp waves**, Slow rolling eye movements | Slow eye movements | Decreased from wake | **5%** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **N2** | **Sleep spindles** (12-14 Hz), **K-complexes** | No eye movements | Low | **45-55%** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **N3 (Slow Wave)** | **Delta** (0.5-4 Hz, >20% of epoch) | No eye movements | Low | **15-25%** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **REM** | **Low-voltage mixed frequency**, **Sawtooth waves** | **Rapid eye movements** | **Atonia** (lowest) | **20-25%** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
