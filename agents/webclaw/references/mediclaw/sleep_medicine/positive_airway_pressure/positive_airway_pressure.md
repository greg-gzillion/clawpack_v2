# Sleep Medicine - Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) Therapy

## PAP Modalities
| Modality | Description | Indications | URL |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **CPAP** | **Continuous positive airway pressure** (fixed pressure) | **OSA** (first-line) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Auto-PAP (APAP)** | **Auto-titrating** pressure within set range | **OSA** (alternative to CPAP), **Positional OSA**, **REM-predominant OSA** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Bilevel PAP (S Mode)** | **Separate IPAP and EPAP** (spontaneous trigger) | **OSA with high pressure intolerance**, **Hypoventilation** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Bilevel PAP (S/T Mode)** | **Spontaneous/Timed** (backup rate) | **CSA**, **Hypoventilation** (OHS, Neuromuscular), **Opioid-induced CSA** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Adaptive Servo-Ventilation (ASV)** | **Variable pressure support** to stabilize ventilation | **CSA** (except HFrEF LVEF =45%), **Treatment-emergent CSA**, **Opioid-induced CSA** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## CPAP Titration (AASM Guidelines)
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Goal** | **Eliminate obstructive events** (apneas, hypopneas, RERAs) and **snoring** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Starting Pressure** | **4-8 cm H2O** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Pressure Adjustment** | **? 1-2 cm H2O q5-10 min** for persistent events | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Optimal Pressure** | **Residual AHI <5/hour** with **SpO2 >90%** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Maximum Pressure** | **20 cm H2O** (consider bilevel if higher needed) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## PAP Adherence
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition of Adherence** | **=4 hours/night** on **=70% of nights** (Medicare criteria) | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Adherence Rate** | **~50-70%** long-term | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Barriers** | **Mask discomfort**, **Claustrophobia**, **Nasal congestion**, **Dry mouth**, **Pressure intolerance**, **Lack of perceived benefit** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Interventions** | **Patient education**, **Mask fitting**, **Heated humidification**, **Pressure relief** (EPR, C-Flex), **Cognitive behavioral therapy**, **Telemonitoring** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## Common PAP Side Effects and Solutions
| Side Effect | Solution | URL |
|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Mask Leak** | **Refit mask**, **Try different mask type** (nasal, full face, nasal pillows), **Chinstrap** (mouth leak) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Nasal Congestion/Rhinorrhea** | **Heated humidification**, **Saline spray**, **Intranasal corticosteroids**, **Heated tubing** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Dry Mouth** | **Heated humidification**, **Full face mask**, **Chinstrap** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Aerophagia** | **? Pressure**, **Bilevel PAP**, **Avoid supine sleep** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Claustrophobia** | **Gradual desensitization**, **Practice during wake**, **Different mask** (nasal pillows) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Pressure Intolerance** | **Ramp feature**, **Pressure relief** (EPR), **Auto-PAP**, **Bilevel PAP** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## Medicare PAP Coverage Criteria
| Criteria | Requirement | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **OSA Diagnosis** | **PSG or HSAT** showing **AHI =15** OR **AHI 5-14 + symptoms/comorbidities** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Face-to-Face Visit** | **Prior to sleep study** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Adherence Documentation** | **=4 hours/night** on **=70% of nights** during **31-90 day trial period** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Follow-Up** | **Face-to-face visit** between 31-91 days | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
