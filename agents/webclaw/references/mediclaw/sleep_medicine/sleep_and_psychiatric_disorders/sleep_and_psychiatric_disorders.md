## Bipolar Disorder and Sleep
| Sleep Disturbance | Description | Management | URL |
|-------------------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **Mania/Hypomania** | **? Need for sleep** (cardinal feature) | **Treat mood episode**, **Sedating antipsychotics** (quetiapine, olanzapine) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/bipolar-disorder |

## Anxiety Disorders and Sleep
| Disorder | Sleep Disturbance | Management | URL |
|----------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Generalized Anxiety Disorder** | **Sleep-onset insomnia** (racing thoughts) | **CBT-I**, **Treat anxiety**, **Avoid benzodiazepines** long-term | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |
| **Panic Disorder** | **Nocturnal panic attacks** (NREM, not REM) | **CBT**, **SSRI** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders |
| **PTSD** | **Insomnia**, **Nightmares**, **Hyperarousal** | **CBT-I**, **Imagery Rehearsal Therapy** (nightmares), **Prazosin** (nightmares), **Treat PTSD** | https://www.ptsd.va.gov/professional/treat/txessentials/sleep_ptsd.asp |

## Other Psychiatric Disorders and Sleep

|----------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Schizophrenia** | **Insomnia**, **Circadian disruption**, **OSA** (high prevalence) | **Treat psychosis**, **Screen for OSA**, **CBT-I** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/schizophrenia |
| **ADHD** | **Insomnia**, **Delayed sleep phase**, **RLS** | **Sleep hygiene**, **Melatonin**, **Treat ADHD** (stimulants may worsen or improve sleep) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/attention-deficit-hyperactivity-disorder-adhd |
