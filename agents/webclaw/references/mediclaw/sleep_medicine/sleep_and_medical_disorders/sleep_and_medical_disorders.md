# Sleep Medicine - Sleep and Medical Disorders

## Cardiovascular Disease and Sleep
| Disorder | Sleep Disturbance | Management | URL |
|----------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Heart Failure** | **OSA** (50-70%), **CSA-CSR** (30-40%), **Insomnia** | **Treat OSA** (CPAP, ASV if LVEF >45%), **Optimize HF therapy** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/sleep-disorders/sleep-apnea-and-heart-disease-stroke |
| **Atrial Fibrillation** | **OSA** (independent risk factor, ? Recurrence) | **CPAP** (improves rhythm control) | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/atrial-fibrillation |
| **Hypertension** | **OSA** (especially resistant HTN) | **CPAP** (modest BP reduction) | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/high-blood-pressure |
| **Nocturnal Angina** | **OSA** (hypoxemia triggers ischemia) | **Treat OSA** | https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/sleep-disorders/sleep-apnea-and-heart-disease-stroke |

## Pulmonary Disease and Sleep
| Disorder | Sleep Disturbance | Management | URL |
|----------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **COPD** | **Insomnia**, **Hypoxemia**, **OSA** (Overlap Syndrome) | **Oxygen**, **PAP therapy**, **Optimize bronchodilators** | https://www.thoracic.org/patients/patient-resources/resources/copd-and-sleep.pdf |
| **Asthma** | **Nocturnal asthma** (worse 2-4 AM), **OSA** | **Optimize controller therapy**, **Treat OSA** | https://www.thoracic.org/patients/patient-resources/resources/asthma-and-sleep.pdf |
| **ILD** | **Insomnia**, **Hypoxemia**, **OSA** | **Oxygen**, **PAP therapy** | https://www.thoracic.org/patients/patient-resources/resources/ild-and-sleep.pdf |

## Neurologic Disease and Sleep
| Disorder | Sleep Disturbance | Management | URL |
|----------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Parkinson Disease** | **Insomnia**, **RBD**, **RLS**, **EDS**, **OSA** | **Treat specific disorder**, **Avoid sedating medications** | https://www.parkinson.org/understanding-parkinsons/non-movement-symptoms/sleep-disorders |
| **Alzheimer Disease** | **Irregular sleep-wake rhythm**, **Sundowning**, **OSA** | **Light therapy**, **Structured routine**, **Treat OSA** | https://www.alz.org/help-support/caregiving/stages-behaviors/sleep-issues-sundowning |
| **Dementia with Lewy Bodies** | **RBD** (often precedes diagnosis), **Insomnia**, **EDS** | **Environmental safety**, **Melatonin**, **Clonazepam** (caution) | https://www.lbda.org |
| **Stroke** | **OSA** (50-70%), **CSA**, **Insomnia**, **Hypersomnia** | **Treat OSA** (improves outcomes), **Stimulants** (post-stroke fatigue) | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/effects-of-stroke/physical-effects-of-stroke/sleep-disorders |

## Other Medical Disorders and Sleep
| Disorder | Sleep Disturbance | Management | URL |
|----------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Chronic Kidney Disease** | **RLS** (30-50%), **OSA**, **Insomnia** | **Treat RLS** (gabapentinoids, Avoid dopamine agonists), **Treat OSA** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/sleep-disorders |
| **Gastroesophageal Reflux** | **Nocturnal reflux**, **Insomnia** | **Elevate HOB**, **Avoid meals before bed**, **PPI** | https://gi.org/topics/gastroesophageal-reflux-disease-gerd/ |
| **Chronic Pain** | **Insomnia** (bidirectional relationship) | **CBT-I + Pain CBT**, **Treat underlying pain** | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health/sleep-disorders-and-complementary-health-approaches |
| **Menopause** | **Insomnia** (vasomotor symptoms), **OSA** | **Treat hot flashes**, **CBT-I**, **Hormone therapy** | https://www.menopause.org/for-women/menopauseflashes/sleep-disorders |
