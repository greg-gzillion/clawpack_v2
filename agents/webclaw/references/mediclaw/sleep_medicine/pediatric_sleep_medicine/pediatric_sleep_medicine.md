|-----------|------------------|-----|

| **Toddlers (1-2 years)** | **11-14 hours** (including naps) | https://aasm.org/resources/pdf/pediatricsleepdurationconsensus.pdf |
| **Preschool (3-5 years)** | **10-13 hours** (including naps) | https://aasm.org/resources/pdf/pediatricsleepdurationconsensus.pdf |
| **School Age (6-12 years)** | **9-12 hours** | https://aasm.org/resources/pdf/pediatricsleepdurationconsensus.pdf |
| **Adolescents (13-18 years)** | **8-10 hours** | https://aasm.org/resources/pdf/pediatricsleepdurationconsensus.pdf |

## Common Pediatric Sleep Disorders
| Disorder | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Behavioral Insomnia of Childhood** | **Sleep-onset association type** (requires parent presence) OR **Limit-setting type** (refusal to go to bed) | **Extinction** (cry it out), **Graduated extinction**, **Bedtime fading**, **Positive routines** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Pediatric OSA** | **Snoring**, **Mouth breathing**, **Enuresis**, **Hyperactivity**, **Poor school performance** | **Adenotonsillectomy** (first-line), **CPAP**, **Weight loss** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Delayed Sleep-Wake Phase Disorder** | **Difficulty falling asleep** at appropriate time, **Difficulty waking** for school | **Bright light therapy**, **Melatonin**, **Strict schedule** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Parasomnias** | **Sleepwalking**, **Sleep terrors**, **Confusional arousals** (common in children) | **Reassurance**, **Safety measures**, **Treat triggers** (OSA, Sleep deprivation) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Narcolepsy** | **EDS**, **Cataplexy** (may be subtle - facial hypotonia, Tongue protrusion) | **Modafinil**, **Sodium oxybate** (=7 years) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
