## Medications to Avoid in Insomnia (Beers Criteria)
| Medication | Rationale | URL |
|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Benzodiazepines** | **Falls**, **Cognitive impairment**, **Dependence** | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Nonbenzodiazepine Hypnotics** | **Falls**, **Delirium**, **Complex sleep behaviors** | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Anticholinergics** (diphenhydramine, doxylamine) | **Confusion**, **Constipation**, **Urinary retention** | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Barbiturates** | **High abuse potential**, **Respiratory depression** | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
