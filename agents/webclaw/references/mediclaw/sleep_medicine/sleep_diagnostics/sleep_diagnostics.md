# Sleep Medicine - Sleep Diagnostics

## Polysomnography (PSG)
| Component | Parameters Monitored | URL |
|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Sleep Staging** | **EEG** (frontal, central, occipital), **EOG** (eye movements), **Chin EMG** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Respiratory** | **Nasal/oral airflow** (thermistor, pressure transducer), **Respiratory effort** (chest/abdomen belts), **Oxygen saturation** (pulse oximetry) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Cardiac** | **ECG** (single lead) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Movement** | **Leg EMG** (anterior tibialis), **Body position** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Additional** | **Snore sensor**, **ETCO2** (if hypoventilation suspected), **Esophageal pressure** (if central vs obstructive unclear) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## Home Sleep Apnea Test (HSAT)
| Component | Parameters Monitored | URL |
|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Type 3** | **Airflow**, **Respiratory effort**, **Oxygen saturation**, **Heart rate** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Type 4** | **Oxygen saturation** + **Heart rate** (or 1-2 channels) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Indications** | **High pretest probability of moderate-severe OSA** (without significant comorbidities) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Contraindications** | **Suspected CSA**, **Hypoventilation**, **Significant cardiopulmonary disease**, **Neuromuscular disease**, **Other sleep disorders** (narcolepsy, Parasomnias) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## PSG vs HSAT
| Feature | PSG | HSAT | URL |
|---------|-----|------|-----|
| **Setting** | **Attended** (lab) | **Unattended** (home) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Channels** | **Comprehensive** (=16 channels) | **Limited** (4-7 channels) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Sleep Staging** | **Yes** (EEG, EOG, EMG) | **No** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **AHI Denominator** | **Total Sleep Time (TST)** | **Recording Time** (underestimates AHI) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Cost** | **Higher** | **Lower** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Technical Failure** | **Low** | **Higher** (3-20%) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## Respiratory Event Definitions (AASM)
| Event | Definition | URL |
|-------|------------|-----|
| **Apnea** | **=90% reduction** in airflow for **=10 seconds** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **Hypopnea** | **=30% reduction** in airflow for **=10 seconds** + **=3% oxygen desaturation** OR **arousal** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **Obstructive Event** | Apnea/hypopnea with **continued or increased respiratory effort** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **Central Event** | Apnea/hypopnea with **absent respiratory effort** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **Mixed Event** | **Central followed by obstructive** effort | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **Respiratory Effort-Related Arousal (RERA)** | **Sequence of breaths** with **increasing respiratory effort** or **flattening** leading to **arousal** (not meeting apnea/hypopnea criteria) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |

## Key PSG Metrics
| Metric | Definition | URL |
|--------|------------|-----|
| **AHI (Apnea-Hypopnea Index)** | **Apneas + Hypopneas / TST** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **RDI (Respiratory Disturbance Index)** | **AHI + RERAs / TST** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **ODI (Oxygen Desaturation Index)** | **Number of =3% or =4% desaturations / TST** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **Sleep Efficiency** | **TST / Time in Bed × 100%** (normal >85%) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **Sleep Latency** | **Time from lights out to first epoch of sleep** (normal <30 min) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **REM Latency** | **Time from sleep onset to first REM epoch** (normal 70-120 min) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |
| **PLMI (Periodic Limb Movement Index)** | **PLMS / TST** (normal <5-15/hour) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/scoring-manual/ |

## Multiple Sleep Latency Test (MSLT) and Maintenance of Wakefulness Test (MWT)
| Test | Protocol | Indication | URL |
|------|----------|------------|-----|
| **MSLT** | **5 nap opportunities** at 2h intervals following overnight PSG | **Narcolepsy**, **Idiopathic hypersomnia** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **MWT** | **4 wake trials** (40 min each) at 2h intervals | **Assess ability to remain awake** (occupational safety, Treatment response) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
