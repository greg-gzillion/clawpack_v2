## Types of Sleep-Related Hypoventilation
| Type | Key Features | URL |
|------|--------------|-----|
| **Obesity Hypoventilation Syndrome (OHS)** | **BMI =30** + **Awake PaCO2 >45 mmHg** + **No other cause** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Congenital Central Alveolar Hypoventilation Syndrome (CCHS)** | **PHOX2B mutation**, **Hypoventilation from birth**, **Blunted CO2 response** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Late-Onset Central Hypoventilation with Hypothalamic Dysfunction** | **Rapid-onset obesity**, **Hypothalamic dysfunction**, **Hypoventilation**, **Autonomic dysregulation** (ROHHAD) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Idiopathic Central Alveolar Hypoventilation** | **Blunted CO2 response** without identifiable cause | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Sleep-Related Hypoventilation Due to Medication** | **Opioids**, **Benzodiazepines**, **Alcohol** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |
| **Sleep-Related Hypoventilation Due to Medical Disorder** | **COPD**, **Neuromuscular disease** (ALS, Myasthenia), **Chest wall restriction** (kyphoscoliosis) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/international-classification-sleep-disorders/ |

## Obesity Hypoventilation Syndrome (OHS)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **BMI =30** + **Awake PaCO2 >45 mmHg** + **Sleep-disordered breathing** (90% have OSA) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Prevalence** | **~10-20%** of obese patients with OSA | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/obesity-hypoventilation-syndrome |
| **Clinical Features** | **Daytime hypercapnia**, **Hypoxemia**, **Polycythemia**, **Cor pulmonale**, **Pulmonary hypertension** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/obesity-hypoventilation-syndrome |
| **Diagnosis** | **ABG** (PaCO2 >45), **Serum bicarbonate** (>27 mEq/L suggests chronic hypercapnia), **PSG** (OSA + Hypoventilation) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Treatment** | **PAP therapy** (CPAP or Bilevel), **Weight loss**, **Bariatric surgery**, **Treat comorbidities** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |

## Treatment of Sleep-Related Hypoventilation
| Type | Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------|-----|
| **OHS with OSA** | **CPAP** (first-line); **Bilevel PAP** if persistent hypoventilation | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **OHS without OSA** | **Bilevel PAP** (S/T mode with backup rate) | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **Neuromuscular Disease** | **Bilevel PAP** (S/T mode), **Noninvasive ventilation (NIV)** , **Tracheostomy with mechanical ventilation** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
| **CCHS** | **Phrenic nerve pacing**, **Diaphragm pacing**, **Mechanical ventilation** | https://aasm.org/clinical-resources/practice-standards/practice-guidelines/ |
