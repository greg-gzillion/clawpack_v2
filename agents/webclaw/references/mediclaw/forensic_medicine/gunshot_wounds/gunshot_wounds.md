## Entrance vs Exit Wounds
| Feature | Entrance Wound | Exit Wound | URL |
|---------|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Size** | **Smaller** (often caliber size) | **Larger**, **Irregular** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
| **Margins** | **Inverted**, **Abrasion collar** | **Everted** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
| **Shape** | **Round/oval** (unless tangential) | **Irregular**, **Stellate** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
| **Soot** | **Present** (close range) | **Absent** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
| **Stippling** | **Present** (intermediate range) | **Absent** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |

## Range of Fire Determination
| Range | Distance | Findings | URL |
|-------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Contact** | **Muzzle against skin** | **Muzzle imprint**, **Soot in wound**, **Stellate laceration** (skull) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
| **Close (Near Contact)** | **<6 inches** | **Soot**, **Singeing of hair** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
| **Intermediate** | **6 inches - 2-3 feet** | **Stippling** (tattooing), **No soot** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
| **Distant** | **>2-3 feet** | **No soot**, **No stippling** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK556119/ |
