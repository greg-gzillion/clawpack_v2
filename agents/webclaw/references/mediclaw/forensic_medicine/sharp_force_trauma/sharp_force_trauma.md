## Types of Sharp Force Injuries
| Injury | Description | Characteristics | URL |
|--------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| **Incised Wound (Cut)** | **Length > Depth** | **Clean margins**, **No bridging tissue**, **Tapered ends** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Stab Wound** | **Depth > Length** | **Track**, **May have hilt mark** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Chop Wound** | **Heavy sharp object** (axe, machete) | **Combination of sharp and blunt**, **Underlying bone fracture** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Defense Wounds** | **Injuries to hands/arms** while defending | **Incised or stab wounds** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Hesitation Marks** | **Superficial cuts** near fatal wound | **Suggests suicide** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
