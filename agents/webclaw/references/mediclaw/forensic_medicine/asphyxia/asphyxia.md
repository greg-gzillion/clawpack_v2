## Types of Asphyxia
| Type | Mechanism | Findings | URL |
|------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Hanging** | **Compression of neck by ligature** with **body weight** | **Inverted V-shaped furrow**, **Highest point opposite knot**, **Fracture of hyoid/thyroid cartilage** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Strangulation (Ligature)** | **Compression of neck by ligature** with **force other than body weight** | **Horizontal furrow**, **Uniform depth**, **± Hyoid/thyroid fracture** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Manual Strangulation** | **Compression of neck by hands** | **Fingertip bruises**, **Crescentic abrasions**, **Hyoid/thyroid fracture** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Suffocation** | **Occlusion of airway or deprivation of oxygen** | **May have no findings**, **Petechiae** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Choking** | **Foreign body in airway** | **Foreign body**, **Café coronary** (food bolus) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Positional Asphyxia** | **Body position impairs breathing** | **Alcohol/drug intoxication**, **Restraint** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Traumatic Asphyxia** | **Chest compression** | **Cervicofacial cyanosis**, **Petechiae above compression** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
