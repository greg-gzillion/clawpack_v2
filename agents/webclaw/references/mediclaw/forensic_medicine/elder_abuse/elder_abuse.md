# Forensic Medicine - Elder Abuse

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Intentional act or failure to act** by caregiver causing harm or risk to older adult | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **Prevalence** | **1 in 10** older adults experience abuse annually | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **Types** | **Physical**, **Sexual**, **Emotional/Psychological**, **Neglect**, **Financial** | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |

## Physical Abuse Findings
| Finding | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Bruises** | **Large (>5 cm)**, **Unusual locations** (medial arm, Back), **Patterned** | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **Fractures** | **Rib**, **Vertebral**, **Femur** (especially spiral) | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **Pressure Injuries** | **Indicates neglect** (unless unavoidable) | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **Head Trauma** | **Subdural hematoma**, **Facial injuries** | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |

## Differentiating Accidental vs Inflicted Injury
| Feature | Accidental | Inflicted | URL |
|---------|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Location** | **Bony prominences**, **Extensor surfaces** | **Protected areas** (medial arm, Back, Buttocks) | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **Pattern** | **Irregular** | **Patterned** (hand, belt, cord) | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **Stage** | **Uniform** | **Multiple stages of healing** | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
| **History** | **Consistent with injury** | **Inconsistent**, **Changing**, **Implausible** | https://www.cdc.gov/elder-abuse/about/index.html |
