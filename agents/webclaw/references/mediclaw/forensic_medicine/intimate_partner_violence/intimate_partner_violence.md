# Forensic Medicine - Intimate Partner Violence (IPV)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Physical, sexual, or psychological harm** by current or former intimate partner | https://www.cdc.gov/intimate-partner-violence/about/index.html |
| **Prevalence** | **1 in 4 women**, **1 in 10 men** experience IPV | https://www.cdc.gov/intimate-partner-violence/about/index.html |
| **Screening** | **USPSTF Grade B** for women of reproductive age | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/intimate-partner-violence-and-abuse-of-elderly-and-vulnerable-adults-screening |

|---------|-------------|-----|

## Strangulation in IPV
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Significance** | **Strong predictor of future homicide** | https://www.familyjusticecenter.org/resources/strangulation/ |
| **Symptoms** | **Voice changes**, **Swallowing difficulty**, **Breathing difficulty**, **Loss of consciousness**, **Incontinence** | https://www.familyjusticecenter.org/resources/strangulation/ |
| **Physical Findings** | **Often minimal or absent** (50% have no visible injury) | https://www.familyjusticecenter.org/resources/strangulation/ |
| **Evaluation** | **CT/CTA neck** (carotid dissection, Laryngeal fracture), **Laryngoscopy** | https://www.familyjusticecenter.org/resources/strangulation/ |
