# Forensic Medicine - Overview

## What is Forensic Medicine?
Forensic medicine is the branch of medicine that applies medical knowledge to legal issues. It encompasses death investigation, injury interpretation, and the evaluation of living individuals involved in legal proceedings.

| Core Competency | Description | URL |
|-----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Death Investigation** | **Determine cause and manner of death** | https://www.thename.org/what-is-a-medical-examiner |
| **Injury Interpretation** | **Analyze wounds and injuries** to determine mechanism | https://www.aafs.org/forensic-science-forensic-medicine |
| **Forensic Toxicology** | **Detect and interpret drugs and poisons** in biological specimens | https://www.aafs.org/forensic-science-forensic-toxicology |
| **Forensic Anthropology** | **Identify human remains** and analyze skeletal trauma | https://www.aafs.org/forensic-science-forensic-anthropology |
| **Forensic Odontology** | **Identify remains via dental records**, **Analyze bite marks** | https://www.aafs.org/forensic-science-forensic-odontology |
| **Clinical Forensic Medicine** | **Evaluate living victims** of assault, abuse, sexual assault | https://www.aafs.org/forensic-science-forensic-medicine |
