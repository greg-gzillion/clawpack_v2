## Competency to Stand Trial
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Standard (Dusky)** | **Sufficient present ability to consult with lawyer** with **reasonable degree of rational understanding** AND **rational and factual understanding of proceedings** | https://www.aapl.org/competency |
| **NOT Retrospective** | **Current mental state**, NOT mental state at time of offense | https://www.aapl.org/competency |
| **Restoration** | **Treatment** (often medication) to restore competency | https://www.aapl.org/competency |

## Criminal Responsibility (Insanity Defense)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **M'Naghten Rule** | **Did not know nature/quality of act** OR **Did not know act was wrong** (due to mental disease/defect) | https://www.aapl.org/criminal-responsibility |
| **Irresistible Impulse** | **Unable to control conduct** despite knowing wrongfulness | https://www.aapl.org/criminal-responsibility |
| **Durham Rule** | **Act was product of mental disease/defect** | https://www.aapl.org/criminal-responsibility |
| **ALI Standard** | **Lacks substantial capacity** to appreciate criminality or conform conduct | https://www.aapl.org/criminal-responsibility |
| **Guilty But Mentally Ill (GBMI)** | **Alternative verdict** in some jurisdictions | https://www.aapl.org/criminal-responsibility |
