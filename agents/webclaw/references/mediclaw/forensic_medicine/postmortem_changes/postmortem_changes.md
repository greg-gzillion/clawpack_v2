# Forensic Medicine - Postmortem Changes

## Early Postmortem Changes
| Change | Timing | Description | Forensic Significance | URL |
|--------|--------|-------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Algor Mortis** | **Immediate** | **Cooling of body** (0.5-1°C/h initially, then slower) | **Estimates time since death** (Henssge nomogram) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Rigor Mortis** | **2-6h onset**, **12h peak**, **24-36h resolution** | **Stiffening of muscles** (ATP depletion) | **Estimates time since death** (variable) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Livor Mortis (Lividity)** | **30 min - 2h onset**, **Fixes 8-12h** | **Purple discoloration** of dependent areas | **Indicates body position**, **Can be blanched before fixation** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Corneal Clouding** | **2-4h** (open eyes), **24h** (closed eyes) | **Opacification of cornea** | **Rough estimate of time since death** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |

## Late Postmortem Changes
| Change | Timing | Description | URL |
|--------|--------|-------------|-----|
| **Decomposition (Putrefaction)** | **24-72h** | **Green discoloration** (RLQ), **Marbling**, **Bloating**, **Skin slippage** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Adipocere** | **Weeks-months** | **Waxy transformation** of fat (moist environment) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Mummification** | **Weeks-months** | **Desiccation** (dry environment) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Skeletonization** | **Months-years** | **Exposure of bone** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |

## Time Since Death Estimation
| Method | Principle | Limitations | URL |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Henssge Nomogram** | **Rectal temperature** + **Ambient temperature** + **Body weight** | **First 24-48h only** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Vitreous Potassium** | **? K+ concentration** after death | **Variable**, **Requires lab** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Entomology** | **Insect succession** on body | **Requires expert**, **Highly accurate** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
| **Gastric Emptying** | **Stomach contents** | **Highly variable** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539741/ |
