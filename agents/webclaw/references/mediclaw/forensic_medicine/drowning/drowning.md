## Freshwater vs Saltwater Drowning
| Feature | Freshwater | Saltwater | URL |
|---------|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Hemodilution** | **Yes** | **No** (hemoconcentration) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Electrolyte Shifts** | **Hyponatremia**, **Hyperkalemia** | **Hypernatremia**, **Hyperchloremia** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
| **Clinical Significance** | **Minimal** (volume aspirated small) | **Minimal** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470336/ |
