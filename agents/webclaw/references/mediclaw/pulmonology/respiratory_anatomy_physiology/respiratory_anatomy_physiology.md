## Lung Volumes and Capacities
| Parameter | Definition | Normal Value | URL |
|-----------|------------|--------------|-----|
| **Tidal Volume (TV)** | Volume inspired/expired per breath | **500 mL** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lungs/respiratory-system |
| **Inspiratory Reserve Volume (IRV)** | Maximum volume inspired beyond TV | **3000 mL** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lungs/respiratory-system |
| **Expiratory Reserve Volume (ERV)** | Maximum volume expired beyond TV | **1200 mL** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lungs/respiratory-system |
| **Residual Volume (RV)** | Volume remaining after maximal expiration | **1200 mL** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lungs/respiratory-system |
| **Vital Capacity (VC)** | TV + IRV + ERV | **4700 mL** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lungs/respiratory-system |
| **Total Lung Capacity (TLC)** | VC + RV | **5900 mL** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lungs/respiratory-system |
| **Functional Residual Capacity (FRC)** | ERV + RV | **2400 mL** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lungs/respiratory-system |
