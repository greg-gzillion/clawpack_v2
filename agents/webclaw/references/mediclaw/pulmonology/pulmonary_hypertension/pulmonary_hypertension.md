## PAH - Risk Stratification and Treatment Approach
| Risk | 1-Year Mortality | Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Low** | <5% | **Oral combination** (ERA + PDE5i) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-hypertension |
| **Intermediate-Low** | 5-10% | **Oral combination** or **Add prostacyclin** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-hypertension |
| **Intermediate-High** | 10-20% | **Triple therapy** (ERA + PDE5i + Prostacyclin) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-hypertension |
| **High** | >20% | **Parenteral prostacyclin** + **ERA + PDE5i** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-hypertension |

## Group 4 - Chronic Thromboembolic Pulmonary Hypertension (CTEPH)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **Diagnosis** | **V/Q scan** (screening), **CT pulmonary angiography**, **Pulmonary angiography** (gold standard) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-hypertension |
| **Treatment** | **Pulmonary thromboendarterectomy (PTE)** (curative), **Balloon pulmonary angioplasty (BPA)** , **Riociguat** (medical) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-hypertension |
