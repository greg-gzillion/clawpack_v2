# Pulmonology - Asthma

## Asthma - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Chronic inflammatory airway disease** characterized by **reversible airflow obstruction**, **Airway hyperresponsiveness**, and **respiratory symptoms** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/asthma |
| **Pathophysiology** | **Type 2 inflammation** (IL-4, IL-5, IL-13, IgE, Eosinophils) or **Non-Type 2 inflammation** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/asthma |
| **Diagnosis** | **Spirometry**: **FEV1/FVC <0.70** with **=12% and =200 mL improvement** after bronchodilator; **Bronchoprovocation** (methacholine); **Peak flow variability** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/asthma |

## Asthma Exacerbation - Management
| Severity | PEF/FEV1 | Management | URL |
|----------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Mild-Moderate** | =50% predicted | **SABA** (nebulized or MDI with spacer), **Oral corticosteroids** (prednisone 40-60 mg x 5-7 days) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/asthma |
| **Severe** | <50% predicted | **SABA + Ipratropium**, **IV corticosteroids**, **Consider IV magnesium**, **NIV** or **Intubation** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/asthma |
| **Life-Threatening** | <25% predicted, Silent chest, Cyanosis, Bradycardia, Hypotension | **Emergent intubation**, **IV epinephrine**, **Heliox** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/asthma |
