# Pulmonology - Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

## COPD - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Heterogeneous lung condition** characterized by **chronic respiratory symptoms** due to **airway and/or alveolar abnormalities** causing **persistent, Often progressive airflow obstruction** | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Risk Factors** | **Tobacco smoke** (primary), **Biomass fuel**, **Occupational exposures**, **Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency**, **Asthma**, **Respiratory infections** | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Diagnosis** | **Spirometry**: **FEV1/FVC <0.70** post-bronchodilator | https://goldcopd.org |

## COPD - Pharmacologic Treatment (GOLD 2024)
| Group | Initial Therapy | Follow-Up Therapy | URL |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|-----|
| **A** | **Bronchodilator** (LAMA or LABA) | Continue or switch | https://goldcopd.org |
| **B** | **LAMA + LABA** | Continue; Consider **ICS** if eosinophils =300 | https://goldcopd.org |
| **E** | **LAMA + LABA** (eosinophils <300) or **ICS + LABA + LAMA** (eosinophils =300) | Continue triple therapy; Consider **Roflumilast** (FEV1 <50%, Chronic bronchitis), **Azithromycin** (exacerbations) | https://goldcopd.org |

## COPD - Non-Pharmacologic Treatment
| Intervention | Indications | URL |
|--------------|-------------|-----|
| **Smoking Cessation** | **All smokers** | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Vaccination** | **Influenza** (annual), **Pneumococcal** (PCV20 or PCV15 + PPSV23), **RSV**, **COVID-19**, **Tdap**, **Zoster** | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Pulmonary Rehabilitation** | **Symptomatic patients** (mMRC =2) | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Long-Term Oxygen Therapy (LTOT)** | **PaO2 =55 mmHg** or **SpO2 =88%**; **PaO2 56-59** + **Cor pulmonale** or **Hct >55%** | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Non-Invasive Ventilation (NIV)** | **Chronic hypercapnic respiratory failure** (PaCO2 =52 mmHg) | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Lung Volume Reduction Surgery (LVRS)** | **Upper lobe-predominant emphysema**, **Low exercise capacity** | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Bronchoscopic Lung Volume Reduction** | **Endobronchial valves** for severe emphysema | https://goldcopd.org |

## COPD Exacerbation - Management
| Severity | Management | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Mild** | **SABA** ± SAMA (increase dose/frequency), **Oral corticosteroids** (prednisone 40 mg x 5 days) | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Moderate** | **SABA + SAMA**, **Oral corticosteroids**, **Antibiotics** (if increased sputum purulence) | https://goldcopd.org |
| **Severe (Hospitalized)** | **Nebulized bronchodilators**, **IV corticosteroids**, **Antibiotics**, **Oxygen** (target SpO2 88-92%), **NIV** (if acute hypercapnic respiratory failure) | https://goldcopd.org |

## Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Genetics** | **SERPINA1** gene; **ZZ** (most severe), **SZ**, **MZ** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency |
| **Clinical Features** | **COPD** (panacinar emphysema, Basilar predominant), **Liver disease** (cirrhosis, HCC), **Panniculitis** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency |
| **Diagnosis** | **Low serum AAT level** (<11 µmol/L or <57 mg/dL), **Phenotype** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency |
| **Treatment** | **Augmentation therapy** (weekly IV AAT) for **ZZ**, **SZ**, **Z-null** with **FEV1 30-65%** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency |
