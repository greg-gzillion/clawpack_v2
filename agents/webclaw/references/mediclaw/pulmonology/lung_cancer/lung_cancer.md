# Pulmonology - Lung Cancer

## Lung Cancer - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | **Leading cause of cancer death**; 85% due to smoking | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung |
| **Histologic Types** | **Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC)** - 85%; **Small Cell Lung Cancer (SCLC)** - 15% | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **NSCLC Subtypes** | **Adenocarcinoma** (40%), **Squamous cell** (25-30%), **Large cell** (5-10%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |

## Lung Cancer Screening
| Criteria | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Age** | 50-80 years | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Smoking History** | **=20 pack-years** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Smoking Status** | **Current smoker** or **quit within 15 years** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Modality** | **Annual low-dose CT chest** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |

## NSCLC - Molecular Drivers and Targeted Therapy
| Alteration | Frequency | Targeted Therapy | URL |
|------------|-----------|------------------|-----|
| **EGFR mutation** | 10-15% | **Osimertinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **ALK fusion** | 3-5% | **Alectinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **ROS1 fusion** | 1-2% | **Crizotinib**, **Entrectinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **BRAF V600E** | 1-2% | **Dabrafenib + Trametinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **MET exon 14 skipping** | 3% | **Capmatinib**, **Tepotinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **RET fusion** | 1-2% | **Selpercatinib**, **Pralsetinib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **KRAS G12C** | 13% | **Sotorasib**, **Adagrasib** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |

## NSCLC - Treatment by Stage
| Stage | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I-II** | **Surgical resection**; **SBRT** for inoperable; Adjuvant chemotherapy for Stage II, Adjuvant osimertinib (EGFR+) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **Stage III** | **Chemoradiation** ? **Durvalumab** (unresectable); Neoadjuvant chemoimmunotherapy ? Surgery (resectable) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **Stage IV (Driver Mutation+)** | **Targeted therapy** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
| **Stage IV (No Driver)** | **Chemoimmunotherapy** (PD-L1 <50%) or **Immunotherapy** (PD-L1 =50%) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/lung/hp |
