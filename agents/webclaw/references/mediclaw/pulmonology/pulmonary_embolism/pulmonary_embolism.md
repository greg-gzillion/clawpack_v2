# Pulmonology - Pulmonary Embolism (PE)

## PE - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Obstruction of pulmonary artery** or its branches by **thrombus** (usually from DVT) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |
| **Risk Factors** | **Virchow's Triad**: **Stasis**, **Hypercoagulability**, **Endothelial injury** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |

| Alternative diagnosis less likely | 3 | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |

## PE - Diagnostic Algorithm
| Step | Action | URL |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1. Assess Pretest Probability** | **Wells score** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |
| **2. D-Dimer** | If **PE unlikely** (Wells =4) ? **Normal D-dimer excludes PE**; If **PE likely** (Wells >4) ? Skip D-dimer, Proceed to imaging | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |
| **3. Imaging** | **CT Pulmonary Angiography (CTPA)** (preferred), **V/Q scan** (if CTPA contraindicated) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |

## PE - Risk Stratification
| Category | Criteria | Management | URL |
|----------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Low-Risk** | **sPESI 0**, No RV dysfunction, Normal biomarkers | **Early discharge**, **DOAC** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |
| **Intermediate-Low** | sPESI =1, RV dysfunction **OR** elevated biomarkers (not both) | **Inpatient**, **Anticoagulation** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |
| **Intermediate-High** | sPESI =1, RV dysfunction **AND** elevated biomarkers | **Inpatient**, **Anticoagulation**, **Consider advanced therapies** if deteriorates | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |
| **High-Risk (Massive)** | **Hypotension** (SBP <90 for >15 min) or **Shock** | **Thrombolytics** (tPA) or **Embolectomy** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pulmonary-embolism |
