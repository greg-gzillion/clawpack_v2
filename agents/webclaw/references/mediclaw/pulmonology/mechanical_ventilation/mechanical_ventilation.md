# Pulmonology - Mechanical Ventilation

## Mechanical Ventilation - Modes
| Mode | Description | Indications | URL |
|------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Assist-Control (AC)** | **Set rate and tidal volume**; Patient triggers additional breaths at set volume | **Full ventilatory support** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Synchronized Intermittent Mandatory Ventilation (SIMV)** | **Set rate** with volume/pressure support for spontaneous breaths | **Weaning** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Pressure Support (PS)** | **Patient-triggered**, **Pressure-limited**, **Flow-cycled** | **Weaning**, **Spontaneous breathing trials** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Pressure Control (PC)** | **Time-cycled**, **Pressure-limited** | **ARDS**, **High airway pressures** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |

## Ventilator Settings
| Parameter | Typical Initial Settings | URL |
|-----------|-------------------------|-----|
| **Mode** | **Assist-Control (AC)** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Tidal Volume** | **6-8 mL/kg PBW** (ARDS: 6 mL/kg) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Respiratory Rate** | **12-16 breaths/min** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **FiO2** | **100%** initially, Titrate to SpO2 92-96% | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **PEEP** | **5 cm H2O** initially | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Flow Rate** | **60 L/min** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **I:E Ratio** | **1:2** to **1:3** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |

## Ventilator Alarms
| Alarm | Causes | Management | URL |
|-------|--------|------------|-----|
| **High Pressure** | **Airway obstruction** (secretions, Biting, Bronchospasm), **Decreased compliance** (pneumothorax, Pulmonary edema, Atelectasis), **Patient-ventilator dyssynchrony** | **Suction**, **Bronchodilators**, **Check for pneumothorax**, **Sedation** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Low Pressure** | **Circuit disconnect**, **Cuff leak**, **ETT displacement** | **Check circuit**, **Check cuff pressure**, **Confirm ETT position** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Low Minute Ventilation** | **Apnea**, **Hypoventilation**, **Circuit leak** | **Check patient**, **Check circuit** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **High Minute Ventilation** | **Tachypnea**, **Anxiety**, **Pain**, **Hypoxia**, **Metabolic acidosis** | **Treat underlying cause** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |

## Spontaneous Breathing Trial (SBT)
| Criteria | Parameters | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Readiness** | **Resolved underlying cause**, **PaO2/FiO2 =150**, **FiO2 =0.4**, **PEEP =8**, **Hemodynamically stable**, **Spontaneous breathing efforts** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Method** | **T-piece** or **Pressure Support** (5-7 cm H2O) or **CPAP** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Duration** | **30-120 minutes** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |
| **Failure Criteria** | **RR >35**, **SpO2 <90%**, **HR >140 or <60**, **SBP >180 or <90**, **Anxiety/Diaphoresis**, **Respiratory acidosis** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/mechanical-ventilation |

| Parameter | Criteria | URL |
