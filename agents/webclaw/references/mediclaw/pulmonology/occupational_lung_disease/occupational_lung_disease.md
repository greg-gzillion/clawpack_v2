# Pulmonology - Occupational Lung Disease

## Pneumoconioses
| Disease | Exposure | Imaging Findings | URL |
|---------|----------|------------------|-----|
| **Silicosis** | **Silica dust** (mining, Sandblasting, Stone cutting) | **Upper lobe predominant**, **Eggshell calcification** of hilar nodes | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/silica/default.html |
| **Asbestosis** | **Asbestos** (construction, Shipbuilding, Insulation) | **Lower lobe predominant**, **Subpleural lines**, **Pleural plaques** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/asbestos/default.html |
| **Coal Worker's Pneumoconiosis** | **Coal dust** | **Upper lobe predominant**, **Small rounded opacities** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coalworkerspneumoconiosis/default.html |
| **Berylliosis** | **Beryllium** (aerospace, Electronics) | **Granulomatous inflammation** (similar to sarcoidosis) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/beryllium/default.html |
