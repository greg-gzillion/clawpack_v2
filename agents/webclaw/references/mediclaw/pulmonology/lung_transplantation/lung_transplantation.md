## Lung Transplant - Listing Criteria
| Parameter | Criteria | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| **General** | **High risk of death** (>50% in 2 years) without transplant, **High likelihood of survival** (>80% at 90 days, >50% at 5 years) with transplant | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |
| **COPD** | **BODE index =7**, **FEV1 <20%**, **Frequent exacerbations**, **Hypercapnia** (PaCO2 >50) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |
| **IPF** | **FVC decline >10% in 6 months**, **Desaturation <88% on 6MWT**, **Hospitalization** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |
| **CF** | **FEV1 <30%**, **Rapid decline**, **Frequent exacerbations**, **Pneumothorax**, **Hemoptysis** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |
| **PAH** | **NYHA III-IV despite maximal therapy**, **Low cardiac index**, **High RAP** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov |

## Lung Transplant - Complications
| Timing | Complication | Management | URL |
|--------|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Early (<30 days)** | **Primary graft dysfunction (PGD)** | **Supportive**, **ECMO** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lung-transplant |
| **Early** | **Airway complications** (dehiscence, Stenosis) | **Bronchoscopy**, **Stenting**, **Surgery** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lung-transplant |
| **Early** | **Infection** (bacterial, Fungal, Viral) | **Antibiotics/Antifungals/Antivirals** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lung-transplant |
| **Intermediate (1-12 months)** | **Acute rejection** | **Corticosteroids**, **Augment immunosuppression** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lung-transplant |
| **Intermediate** | **CMV**, **EBV**, **PJP** | **Prophylaxis**, **Treatment** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lung-transplant |
| **Late (>12 months)** | **Chronic lung allograft dysfunction (CLAD)** : **BOS** (obliterative bronchiolitis), **RAS** (restrictive allograft syndrome) | **Azithromycin**, **Montelukast**, **Photopheresis**, **Re-transplant** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lung-transplant |
| **Late** | **Malignancy** (skin, PTLD) | **Screening**, **Treatment** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/lung-transplant |
