# Pulmonology - Tuberculosis (TB)

## TB - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pathogen** | **Mycobacterium tuberculosis** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/index.html |
| **Transmission** | **Airborne** (inhalation of droplet nuclei) | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/index.html |
| **Latent TB Infection (LTBI)** | **Asymptomatic**, **Positive TST/IGRA**, **Normal CXR** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/ltbi.htm |
| **Active TB** | **Symptomatic**, **Positive TST/IGRA**, **Abnormal CXR** (cavitation, Infiltrates) | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/tbdisease.htm |

## LTBI - Screening and Treatment
| Test | Interpretation | URL |
|------|----------------|-----|
| **TST (PPD)** | **=5 mm**: HIV, Immunocompromised, Close contact; **=10 mm**: High-risk; **=15 mm**: Low-risk | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/testing/tbtesttypes.htm |
| **IGRA** (QuantiFERON-TB Gold) | **Positive** = TB infection | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/testing/igra.htm |
| **LTBI Treatment** | **Isoniazid x 9 months**, **Rifampin x 4 months**, **Isoniazid + Rifapentine weekly x 12 weeks (3HP)** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/ltbi.htm |

## Active TB - Treatment (RIPE Therapy)
| Phase | Drugs | Duration | URL |
|-------|-------|----------|-----|
| **Intensive Phase** | **R**ifampin + **I**soniazid + **P**yrazinamide + **E**thambutol | **2 months** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/tbdisease.htm |
| **Continuation Phase** | **Rifampin + Isoniazid** | **4 months** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/tbdisease.htm |
| **Total** | | **6 months** (9 months for cavitary disease with positive culture at 2 months) | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/tbdisease.htm |

## Antituberculous Drugs - Adverse Effects
| Drug | Adverse Effects | Monitoring | URL |
|------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Isoniazid (INH)** | **Hepatotoxicity**, **Peripheral neuropathy** (give pyridoxine) | Baseline and monthly LFTs | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/adverseevents.htm |
| **Rifampin** | **Orange body fluids**, **Hepatotoxicity**, **CYP3A4 induction** | Baseline and monthly LFTs | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/adverseevents.htm |
| **Pyrazinamide (PZA)** | **Hepatotoxicity**, **Hyperuricemia** | Baseline and monthly LFTs, Uric acid | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/adverseevents.htm |
| **Ethambutol (EMB)** | **Optic neuritis** (red-green color blindness) | **Monthly visual acuity and color vision** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/adverseevents.htm |

## Drug-Resistant TB
| Type | Definition | Treatment | URL |
|------|------------|-----------|-----|
| **MDR-TB** | Resistant to **INH + Rifampin** | **Bedaquiline**, **Pretomanid**, **Linezolid**, **Moxifloxacin**, **Clofazimine** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/drtb/default.htm |
| **XDR-TB** | MDR-TB + Resistant to **fluoroquinolone** + **one injectable** | Individualized | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/drtb/default.htm |
