## CAP - Empiric Treatment (ATS/IDSA)
| Setting | Preferred | Alternative | URL |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Outpatient (No comorbidities)** | **Amoxicillin** or **Doxycycline** or **Macrolide** (if resistance <25%) | Respiratory fluoroquinolone | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
| **Outpatient (Comorbidities)** | **Amoxicillin-clavulanate** + **Macrolide** or **Cephalosporin** + **Macrolide** | Respiratory fluoroquinolone | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
| **Inpatient (Non-ICU)** | **Ceftriaxone + Azithromycin** or **Respiratory fluoroquinolone** | - | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
| **Inpatient (ICU)** | **Ceftriaxone + Azithromycin** or **Ceftriaxone + Respiratory fluoroquinolone** | **Add Vancomycin** (MRSA) or **Anti-Pseudomonal** (if risk) | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
| **Duration** | **5 days** (if clinically stable) | | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |

## Hospital-Acquired/Ventilator-Associated Pneumonia (HAP/VAP)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Pneumonia occurring **=48 hours** after admission (HAP) or intubation (VAP) | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
| **Pathogens** | **S. aureus** (MRSA, MSSA), **P. aeruginosa**, **Enterobacteriaceae**, **Acinetobacter** | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
| **Empiric Treatment** | **Anti-MRSA** (Vancomycin or Linezolid) + **Anti-Pseudomonal** (Cefepime, Piperacillin-tazobactam, Meropenem) | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
| **Duration** | **7 days** | https://www.thoracic.org/statements/pneumonia.php |
