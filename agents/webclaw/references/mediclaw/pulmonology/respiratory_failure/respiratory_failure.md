## Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome (ARDS) - Berlin Definition
| Criteria | Definition | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Timing** | **Within 1 week** of known clinical insult | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Imaging** | **Bilateral opacities** on CXR/CT | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Origin of Edema** | **Not fully explained by cardiac failure or fluid overload** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Oxygenation** | **Mild**: PaO2/FiO2 200-300 (PEEP/CPAP =5); **Moderate**: 100-200; **Severe**: <100 | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |

## ARDS - Management
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Lung-Protective Ventilation** | **Tidal volume 6 mL/kg PBW**, **Plateau pressure =30 cm H2O** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **PEEP** | **Higher PEEP** for moderate-severe ARDS (ARDSNet PEEP/FiO2 table) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Prone Positioning** | **=16 hours/day** for **PaO2/FiO2 <150** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Neuromuscular Blockade** | **Cisatracurium** for 48 hours for **PaO2/FiO2 <150** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Conservative Fluid Strategy** | **Avoid positive fluid balance** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **Corticosteroids** | **Dexamethasone** or **Methylprednisolone** for moderate-severe ARDS | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
| **ECMO** | **Refractory hypoxemia** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/ards |
