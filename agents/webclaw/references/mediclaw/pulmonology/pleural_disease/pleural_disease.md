# Pulmonology - Pleural Disease

## Pleural Effusion
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Accumulation of fluid in pleural space** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pleural-disorders |
| **Transudate vs Exudate** | **Light's Criteria** (=1 = exudate): **Pleural protein/serum protein >0.5**, **Pleural LDH/serum LDH >0.6**, **Pleural LDH >2/3 upper limit of normal** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pleural-disorders |

| Cause | Mechanism | URL |

## Exudative Effusion - Causes
| Cause | Diagnostic Findings | URL |
|-------|---------------------|-----|
| **Parapneumonic/empyema** | **pH <7.2**, **Glucose <60**, **LDH >1000**, **Positive Gram stain/culture** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pleural-disorders |
| **Malignancy** | **Positive cytology**, **Bloody**, **Lymphocytic** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pleural-disorders |
| **Tuberculosis** | **Lymphocytic**, **ADA >40**, **Positive culture/PCR** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pleural-disorders |
| **Pulmonary Embolism** | **Bloody**, **Exudative or transudative** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pleural-disorders |
| **Collagen Vascular Disease** | **RA** (low glucose, Low pH), **SLE** (ANA, Anti-dsDNA) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pleural-disorders |

## Pneumothorax
| Type | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Primary Spontaneous** | **Rupture of subpleural bleb** (tall, Thin young men) | **Observation** (<2 cm), **Aspiration**, **Chest tube** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pneumothorax |
| **Secondary Spontaneous** | Underlying lung disease (**COPD**, **CF**, **ILD**) | **Chest tube**, **Pleurodesis** (recurrent) | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pneumothorax |
| **Traumatic** | **Blunt** or **Penetrating trauma**, **Iatrogenic** | **Chest tube** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pneumothorax |
| **Tension** | **One-way valve** ? **Progressive air accumulation** ? **Hemodynamic compromise** | **Needle decompression** ? **Chest tube** | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pneumothorax |
