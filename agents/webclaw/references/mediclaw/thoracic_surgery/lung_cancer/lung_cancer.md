## Surgical Resection for Lung Cancer
| Procedure | Indication | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Wedge Resection** | **Small peripheral nodules**, **Poor pulmonary reserve** | https://www.sts.org/patients/lung-cancer-surgery |
| **Segmentectomy** | **=2 cm tumor**, **AIS/MIA**, **Poor reserve** | https://www.sts.org/patients/lung-cancer-surgery |
| **Lobectomy** | **Gold standard** for operable NSCLC | https://www.sts.org/patients/lung-cancer-surgery |
| **Bilobectomy** | **Tumor involving bronchus intermedius** or **fissure** | https://www.sts.org/patients/lung-cancer-surgery |
| **Pneumonectomy** | **Central tumors** not amenable to lesser resection | https://www.sts.org/patients/lung-cancer-surgery |
| **Sleeve Resection** | **Central tumors** (bronchial or vascular sleeve) | https://www.sts.org/patients/lung-cancer-surgery |
