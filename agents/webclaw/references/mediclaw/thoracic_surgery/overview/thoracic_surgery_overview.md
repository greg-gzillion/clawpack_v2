# Thoracic Surgery - Overview

## What is Thoracic Surgery?
Thoracic surgery is the surgical specialty concerned with the operative, perioperative, and critical care of patients with pathologic conditions within the chest, including the lungs, esophagus, trachea, mediastinum, chest wall, and diaphragm.
