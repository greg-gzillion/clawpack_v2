# Thoracic Surgery - Lung Transplantation

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indications** | **End-stage lung disease** (COPD, ILD, CF, Pulmonary hypertension, Bronchiectasis) | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Contraindications** | **Active malignancy** (<5 years), **Untreatable infection**, **Severe comorbidities**, **Non-adherence**, **Substance abuse** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |

## Lung Allocation Score (LAS)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Purpose** | **Prioritize candidates** based on **urgency** (waitlist mortality) and **benefit** (post-transplant survival) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/data/allocation-calculators/las-calculator/ |
| **Range** | **0-100** (higher = more urgent) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/data/allocation-calculators/las-calculator/ |

## Transplant Procedures
| Procedure | Indications | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Single Lung Transplant** | **COPD**, **IPF** (older recipients) | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Bilateral Lung Transplant** | **CF**, **Bronchiectasis**, **Pulmonary hypertension**, **Younger recipients** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Heart-Lung Transplant** | **Eisenmenger syndrome**, **Severe biventricular failure with lung disease** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Lobar Transplant** | **Size mismatch**, **Pediatric recipients** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |

## Post-Transplant Complications
| Complication | Timing | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Primary Graft Dysfunction (PGD)** | **<72h** | **Supportive care**, **Low tidal volume ventilation**, **ECMO** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Acute Rejection** | **Weeks-months** | **Corticosteroids**, **Augment immunosuppression** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Chronic Lung Allograft Dysfunction (CLAD)** | **Months-years** | **Azithromycin**, **Augment immunosuppression**, **Retransplantation** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Infection** | **Any** | **Antimicrobials**, **Prophylaxis** (PJP, CMV, Fungal) | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Airway Complications** | **Weeks-months** | **Bronchoscopic dilation**, **Stenting**, **Surgery** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |

## Immunosuppression
| Phase | Regimen | URL |
|-------|---------|-----|
| **Induction** | **Basiliximab** (IL-2R antagonist) or **Alemtuzumab** (anti-CD52) or **ATG** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Maintenance** | **Tacrolimus + Mycophenolate + Prednisone** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
