# Thoracic Surgery - Perioperative Care

## Preoperative Risk Assessment
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pulmonary** | **PFTs**, **DLCO**, **6-Minute Walk Test**, **CPET** (see PFT section) | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Cardiac** | **RCRI**, **ACC/AHA Guidelines**, **Stress testing** if indicated | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Smoking Cessation** | **=4 weeks before surgery** reduces complications | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Nutrition** | **Albumin <3.5 g/dL** = Increased risk | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |

## Enhanced Recovery After Surgery (ERAS) for Thoracic Surgery
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Preoperative** | **Carbohydrate loading**, **Avoid prolonged fasting**, **Patient education** | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Intraoperative** | **Opioid-sparing analgesia**, **Goal-directed fluid therapy**, **Normothermia**, **Regional anesthesia** | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Postoperative** | **Early mobilization**, **Early oral intake**, **Multimodal analgesia**, **Chest tube management** | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |

## Chest Tube Management
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Size** | **Small-bore tubes** (=19 Fr) preferred for air/fluid | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Suction** | **Water seal** preferred over suction (unless large air leak or expanding pneumothorax) | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Removal Criteria** | **<200-400 mL/day**, **No air leak**, **CXR shows lung expansion** | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Digital Drainage Systems** | **Objective measurement**, **Earlier removal** | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |

## Postoperative Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Prolonged Air Leak (>5 days)** | **Chest tube to water seal**, **Heimlich valve**, **Blood patch**, **Reoperation** | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Atrial Fibrillation** | **Rate control** (beta-blocker, CCB), **Anticoagulation**, **Cardioversion** (unstable) | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Pneumonia** | **Antibiotics**, **Pulmonary toilet**, **Bronchoscopy** | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
| **Chylothorax** | **Low-fat diet/MCT**, **TPN**, **Thoracic duct ligation** (if >1L/day) | https://www.sts.org/education/thoracic-surgery-perioperative |
