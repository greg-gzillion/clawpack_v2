# Thoracic Surgery - Pleural Diseases

## Pneumothorax
| Type | Etiology | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Primary Spontaneous** | **Rupture of subpleural bleb** (tall, thin, young male, Smoker) | **Observation** (<2 cm, asymptomatic), **Aspiration**, **Chest tube**, **VATS blebectomy + Pleurodesis** (recurrent) | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Secondary Spontaneous** | **COPD**, **ILD**, **TB**, **Cystic fibrosis**, **Catamenial** | **Chest tube**, **VATS pleurodesis** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Traumatic** | **Blunt or penetrating trauma** | **Chest tube**, **Surgery** (persistent air leak) | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Tension** | **One-way valve** ? **? Intrathoracic pressure** ? **Hemodynamic compromise** | **Emergent needle decompression** (2nd ICS, midclavicular line) ? **Chest tube** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |

## Pleural Effusion
| Type | Etiology | Light Criteria | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Transudate** | **CHF**, **Cirrhosis**, **Nephrotic syndrome** | **Pleural/Serum Protein <0.5**, **Pleural/Serum LDH <0.6** | **Treat underlying** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Exudate** | **Malignancy**, **Infection** (parapneumonic, TB), **PE**, **Autoimmune** | **Pleural/Serum Protein >0.5**, **Pleural/Serum LDH >0.6**, **Pleural LDH >2/3 ULN** | **Treat underlying**, **Thoracentesis**, **Chest tube**, **Pleurodesis**, **Indwelling pleural catheter** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |

## Empyema
| Stage | Description | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Stage I (Exudative)** | **Free-flowing**, **Sterile** | **Antibiotics**, **Thoracentesis** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Stage II (Fibrinopurulent)** | **Loculated**, **Infected** | **Chest tube**, **Intrapleural fibrinolytics** (tPA/DNase) | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Stage III (Organizing)** | **Thick peel**, **Trapped lung** | **VATS decortication** or **Open decortication** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |

## Malignant Pleural Effusion
| Treatment | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Thoracentesis** | **Diagnostic and therapeutic**, **Rapid reaccumulation** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Indwelling Pleural Catheter (IPC)** | **Tunneled catheter** for **intermittent drainage**, **Spontaneous pleurodesis** in 50% | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **Chemical Pleurodesis** | **Talc** (most effective), **Doxycycline**, **Bleomycin** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
| **VATS Pleurodesis** | **Mechanical** or **Talc insufflation** | https://www.sts.org/patients/pleural-diseases |
