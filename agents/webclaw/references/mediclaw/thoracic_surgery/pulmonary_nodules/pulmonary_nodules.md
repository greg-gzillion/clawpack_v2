# Thoracic Surgery - Pulmonary Nodules

## Overview

|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Focal opacity =3 cm** surrounded by lung parenchyma | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |

| **Subsolid Nodule** | **Ground-glass opacity** (GGO) or **Part-solid** | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |

## Fleischner Society Guidelines (2017) - Solid Nodules
| Nodule Size | Low Risk | High Risk | URL |
|-------------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **<6 mm** | **No routine follow-up** | **Optional CT at 12 months** | https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2017161659 |
| **6-8 mm** | **CT at 6-12 months**, then consider CT at 18-24 months | **CT at 6-12 months**, then CT at 18-24 months | https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2017161659 |
| **>8 mm** | **Consider CT at 3 months**, PET-CT, or Tissue sampling | **Consider CT at 3 months**, PET-CT, or Tissue sampling | https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2017161659 |

## Fleischner Society Guidelines (2017) - Subsolid Nodules
| Nodule Type | Size | Management | URL |
|-------------|------|------------|-----|
| **Pure GGO** | **<6 mm** | **No routine follow-up** | https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2017161659 |
| **Pure GGO** | **=6 mm** | **CT at 6-12 months**, then q2 years x5 years | https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2017161659 |
| **Part-Solid** | **<6 mm** | **No routine follow-up** | https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2017161659 |
| **Part-Solid** | **=6 mm** | **CT at 3-6 months**, then annual x5 years (if solid component =6 mm, consider PET-CT or biopsy) | https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2017161659 |
