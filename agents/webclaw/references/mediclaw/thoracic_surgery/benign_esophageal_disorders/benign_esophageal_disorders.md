## Hiatal Hernia
| Type | Definition | Treatment | URL |
|------|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Type I (Sliding)** | **GE junction above diaphragm** | **Repair if symptomatic** | https://www.sages.org/publications/guidelines/ |
| **Type II (Paraesophageal)** | **GE junction in position**, **Fundus herniates** | **Repair** (risk of gastric volvulus) | https://www.sages.org/publications/guidelines/ |
| **Type III (Mixed)** | **Both GE junction and fundus herniate** | **Repair** | https://www.sages.org/publications/guidelines/ |
| **Type IV** | **Other organs** (colon, spleen) in chest | **Repair** | https://www.sages.org/publications/guidelines/ |

## Achalasia
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pathophysiology** | **Loss of inhibitory neurons** ? **Failed LES relaxation** + **Absent peristalsis** | https://www.sages.org/publications/guidelines/ |
| **Diagnosis** | **Esophageal manometry** (gold standard), **Barium swallow** ("bird beak") | https://www.sages.org/publications/guidelines/ |
| **Treatment** | **Heller myotomy + Partial fundoplication**, **POEM**, **Pneumatic dilation** | https://www.sages.org/publications/guidelines/ |
