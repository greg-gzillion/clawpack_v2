## 2010 ACR/EULAR Classification Criteria
| Domain | Points | URL |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Joint Involvement** | **1 large joint** (0) to **>10 joints** (at least 1 small joint) (5) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/2010-ACR-EULAR-RA-Criteria.pdf |
| **Serology** | **RF negative + Anti-CCP negative** (0); **Low-positive RF or anti-CCP** (2); **High-positive RF or anti-CCP** (3) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/2010-ACR-EULAR-RA-Criteria.pdf |
| **Acute Phase Reactants** | **Normal CRP and ESR** (0); **Abnormal CRP or ESR** (1) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/2010-ACR-EULAR-RA-Criteria.pdf |
| **Duration** | **<6 weeks** (0); **=6 weeks** (1) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/2010-ACR-EULAR-RA-Criteria.pdf |
| **Diagnosis** | **=6/10 points** = Definite RA | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/2010-ACR-EULAR-RA-Criteria.pdf |

## Pharmacotherapy for RA (ACR 2021)
| Disease Activity | Initial Therapy | URL |
|------------------|-----------------|-----|
| **Treatment-Naive, Low Disease Activity** | **Methotrexate** (preferred) OR **Hydroxychloroquine**, **Sulfasalazine** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **Treatment-Naive, Moderate-High Disease Activity** | **Methotrexate** (strong recommendation) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **Inadequate Response to MTX** | **Add TNF inhibitor** OR **Non-TNF biologic** (IL-6i, JAKi, Abatacept) OR **Triple therapy** (MTX + HCQ + SSZ) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |

## DMARDs (Disease-Modifying Antirheumatic Drugs)
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **csDMARDs** | **Methotrexate**, Leflunomide, Sulfasalazine, Hydroxychloroquine | Broad immunosuppression | **MTX**: Hepatotoxicity, Myelosuppression, Pneumonitis; **HCQ**: **Retinopathy** (annual eye exam) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **TNF Inhibitors** | Adalimumab, Etanercept, Infliximab, Certolizumab, Golimumab | Block TNF-a | **Infection** (TB screening), **Injection site reactions**, **Demyelinating disease** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **IL-6 Inhibitors** | Tocilizumab, Sarilumab | Block IL-6 receptor | **? LFTs**, **? Lipids**, **Neutropenia**, **GI perforation** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **JAK Inhibitors** | Tofacitinib, Baricitinib, Upadacitinib | Inhibit JAK-STAT pathway | **Herpes zoster**, **? Lipids**, **VTE**, **Boxed warning**: Malignancy, MACE, Thrombosis | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **T-cell Costimulation Blocker** | Abatacept | Blocks CD80/86-CD28 | **COPD exacerbation** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **Anti-CD20** | Rituximab | B-cell depletion | **Infusion reactions**, **Hypogammaglobulinemia**, **PML** (rare) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/RA-Guideline-2021.pdf |
