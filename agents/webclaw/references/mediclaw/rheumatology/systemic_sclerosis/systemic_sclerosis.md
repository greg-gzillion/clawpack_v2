# Rheumatology - Systemic Sclerosis (Scleroderma)

## Classification
| Type | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **Limited Cutaneous SSc** | **Skin thickening limited to distal extremities and face**; **CREST syndrome**: **C**alcinosis, **R**aynaud, **E**sophageal dysmotility, **S**clerodactyly, **T**elangiectasias | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Scleroderma |
| **Diffuse Cutaneous SSc** | **Skin thickening proximal to elbows/knees** and/or **trunk**; **Rapid progression**, **Higher risk of internal organ involvement** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Scleroderma |
| **Systemic Sclerosis Sine Scleroderma** | **Internal organ involvement without skin thickening** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Scleroderma |

## Autoantibody Profiles in SSc
| Antibody | Clinical Association | URL |
|----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Anti-Centromere (ACA)** | **Limited cutaneous SSc**, **PAH**, **Lower risk of ILD** | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_whatis_antibodies |
| **Anti-Scl-70 (Topoisomerase I)** | **Diffuse cutaneous SSc**, **ILD**, **Higher risk of renal crisis** | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_whatis_antibodies |
| **Anti-RNA Polymerase III** | **Diffuse cutaneous SSc**, **Renal crisis** (highest risk), **Malignancy-associated** | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_whatis_antibodies |

## Organ System Involvement
| System | Manifestations | Screening | Treatment | URL |
|--------|----------------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Vascular** | **Raynaud phenomenon**, **Digital ulcers**, **PAH** | **Echocardiogram** annual, **Right heart cath** if suspected PAH | **CCB** (nifedipine), **PDE5i** (sildenafil), **Epoprostenol** (PAH), **Bosentan** (digital ulcers) | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_organ |
| **Pulmonary** | **ILD** (NSIP pattern), **PAH** | **PFTs** q6-12 months, **HRCT** baseline | **Mycophenolate**, **Cyclophosphamide**, **Nintedanib**, **Tocilizumab** | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_organ |
| **Gastrointestinal** | **GERD**, **Dysphagia**, **Gastroparesis**, **SIBO**, **Constipation** | **EGD**, **Esophageal manometry** | **PPI**, **Prokinetics**, **Antibiotics** (SIBO) | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_organ |
| **Renal** | **Scleroderma Renal Crisis** (hypertension, AKI, Microangiopathic hemolytic anemia) | **BP monitoring**, **Cr** | **ACE inhibitor** (captopril) - **DO NOT STOP** | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_organ |
| **Cardiac** | **Myocardial fibrosis**, **Pericarditis**, **Arrhythmias** | **ECG**, **Echocardiogram**, **Cardiac MRI** | **CCB**, **Immunosuppression** | https://www.scleroderma.org/site/PageServer?pagename=patients_organ |

## Scleroderma Renal Crisis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
