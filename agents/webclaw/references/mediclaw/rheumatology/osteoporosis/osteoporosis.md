# Rheumatology - Osteoporosis

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Skeletal disorder characterized by low bone mass and microarchitectural deterioration** leading to **increased fracture risk** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Osteoporosis |
| **DXA Criteria (WHO)** | **T-score = -1.0**: Normal; **-1.0 to -2.5**: Osteopenia; **= -2.5**: Osteoporosis; **= -2.5 + fragility fracture**: Severe osteoporosis | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |
| **Prevalence** | **>10 million US adults** have osteoporosis; **>43 million** have osteopenia | https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/fastats/osteoporosis.htm |
| **Risk Factors** | **Age**, **Female sex**, **Menopause**, **Low BMI**, **Family history**, **Smoking**, **Alcohol**, **Corticosteroids**, **CKD**, **Malabsorption** | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |

## Screening Recommendations
| Population | Recommendation | URL |
|------------|----------------|-----|
| **Women =65** | **DXA scan** (USPSTF Grade B) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/osteoporosis-screening |
| **Postmenopausal women <65** | **DXA scan** if **=1 risk factor** (USPSTF Grade B) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/osteoporosis-screening |
| **Men =70** | **DXA scan** (Endocrine Society) | https://www.endocrine.org/clinical-practice-guidelines/osteoporosis |
| **Men 50-69** | **DXA scan** if risk factors | https://www.endocrine.org/clinical-practice-guidelines/osteoporosis |
| **Secondary Causes** | **Screen for secondary osteoporosis** in all patients with osteoporosis | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |

## FRAX (Fracture Risk Assessment Tool)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Purpose** | Estimates **10-year probability of hip fracture** and **major osteoporotic fracture** | https://frax.shef.ac.uk/FRAX/ |
| **Inputs** | **Age**, **Sex**, **BMI**, **Prior fracture**, **Parental hip fracture**, **Smoking**, **Corticosteroids**, **RA**, **Secondary osteoporosis**, **Alcohol**, **Femoral neck BMD** (optional) | https://frax.shef.ac.uk/FRAX/ |
| **Treatment Threshold (US)** | **Hip fracture risk =3%** OR **Major fracture risk =20%** | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |

| Drug | When to Consider | Duration | URL |
