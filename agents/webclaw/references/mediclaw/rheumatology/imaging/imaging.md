# Rheumatology - Imaging in Rheumatology

## Modalities
| Modality | Indications | Findings | URL |
|----------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **X-ray** | **Initial evaluation** of arthritis | **Erosions**, **Joint space narrowing**, **Osteophytes**, **Periarticular osteopenia**, **Chondrocalcinosis** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Imaging |
| **Ultrasound** | **Synovitis**, **Tenosynovitis**, **Enthesitis**, **Crystal deposition**, **Guidance for injections** | **Power Doppler** (hyperemia), **Double contour sign** (gout), **Erosions** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Imaging |
| **MRI** | **Early inflammatory arthritis**, **Sacroliitis**, **Myositis**, **Osteonecrosis**, **Spinal cord compression** | **Bone marrow edema** (osteitis), **Synovitis**, **Erosions**, **Enthesitis** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Imaging |
| **DECT** | **Gout** (urate deposition) | **Color-coded urate deposits** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Imaging |
| **PET-CT** | **Large vessel vasculitis** (GCA, Takayasu), **Malignancy screening**, **Fever of unknown origin** | **Increased FDG uptake** in vessel walls | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Imaging |

## Characteristic Imaging Findings
| Disease | X-ray | MRI/Ultrasound | URL |
|---------|-------|----------------|-----|
| **Rheumatoid Arthritis** | **Marginal erosions** (MCP, MTP), **Joint space narrowing**, **Periarticular osteopenia** | **Synovitis**, **Bone marrow edema**, **Erosions** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Rheumatoid-Arthritis |
| **Ankylosing Spondylitis** | **Sacroliitis** (erosions, sclerosis, ankylosis), **Syndesmophytes**, **Bamboo spine** | **Bone marrow edema** at sacroiliac joints (MRI) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Spondyloarthritis |
| **Psoriatic Arthritis** | **Pencil-in-cup deformity**, **Fluffy periostitis**, **DIP predominant**, **Asymmetric sacroliitis** | **Enthesitis**, **Dactylitis** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Psoriatic-Arthritis |
| **Gout** | **Well-marginated erosions** with **overhanging edges**, **Tophi**, **Joint space preserved until late** | **Double contour sign** (ultrasound), **Urate deposits** (DECT) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Gout |
| **Osteoarthritis** | **Joint space narrowing**, **Osteophytes**, **Subchondral sclerosis**, **Subchondral cysts** | **Meniscal extrusion**, **Effusion** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Osteoarthritis |
| **Calcium Pyrophosphate Deposition** | **Chondrocalcinosis** (menisci, Triangular fibrocartilage) | **Crystal deposition** in cartilage | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Calcium-Pyrophosphate-Deposition-CPPD |
