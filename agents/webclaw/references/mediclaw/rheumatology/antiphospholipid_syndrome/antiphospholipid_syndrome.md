# Rheumatology - Antiphospholipid Syndrome (APS)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Autoantibodies against phospholipid-binding proteins** causing **thrombosis** and/or **pregnancy morbidity** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Primary APS** | No associated autoimmune disease | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Secondary APS** | Associated with **SLE** (most common) or other autoimmune disease | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Catastrophic APS (CAPS)** | **Multiple organ thromboses** over **<1 week** (mortality ~50%) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |

## Revised Sapporo (Sydney) Classification Criteria (2006)
| Category | Criteria | URL |
|----------|----------|-----|
| **Clinical Criteria** | | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Vascular Thrombosis** | **=1 clinical episode** of arterial, venous, or small vessel thrombosis | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Pregnancy Morbidity** | **=1 unexplained death** of morphologically normal fetus =10 weeks; OR **=1 premature birth** <34 weeks due to severe preeclampsia/insufficiency; OR **=3 consecutive spontaneous abortions** <10 weeks | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Laboratory Criteria** | (Positive on **=2 occasions =12 weeks apart**) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Lupus Anticoagulant (LAC)** | **Positive** per ISTH guidelines | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Anticardiolipin Antibody (aCL)** | **IgG and/or IgM =40 GPL/MPL** (or >99th percentile) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Anti-ﾟ2-Glycoprotein I (anti-ﾟ2GPI)** | **IgG and/or IgM =99th percentile** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Diagnosis** | **=1 Clinical + =1 Laboratory criterion** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Antiphospholipid-Syndrome.pdf |

## Clinical Manifestations
| System | Manifestations | URL |
|--------|----------------|-----|
| **Vascular** | **DVT** (most common), **PE**, **Stroke/TIA** (especially young), **Myocardial infarction**, **Renal artery thrombosis** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Obstetric** | **Recurrent early miscarriage**, **Late fetal loss**, **Preeclampsia**, **HELLP syndrome**, **IUGR** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Hematologic** | **Thrombocytopenia**, **Autoimmune hemolytic anemia** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Neurologic** | **Livedo reticularis**, **Cognitive dysfunction**, **Chorea**, **Seizures** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Cardiac** | **Valvular heart disease** (Libman-Sacks), **Intracardiac thrombus** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |
| **Renal** | **Renal artery stenosis**, **Thrombotic microangiopathy** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Antiphospholipid-Syndrome |

## Management of APS (EULAR 2019)
| Scenario | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Primary Thromboprophylaxis (aPL+, No Thrombosis)** | **Low-dose Aspirin** (consider in high-risk profile) | https://www.eular.org/recommendations |
| **Secondary Prevention (Venous Thrombosis)** | **Warfarin** (target INR **2-3**) indefinitely; **DOACs not recommended** (especially triple-positive or arterial) | https://www.eular.org/recommendations |
| **Secondary Prevention (Arterial Thrombosis)** | **Warfarin** (target INR **2-3** or **3-4**) + **Aspirin** | https://www.eular.org/recommendations |
| **Obstetric APS** | **Low-dose Aspirin + Prophylactic LMWH** (antepartum) ? **6 weeks postpartum** | https://www.eular.org/recommendations |
| **Catastrophic APS** | **Anticoagulation + High-dose CS + Plasmapheresis/IVIG ｱ Rituximab/Eculizumab** | https://www.eular.org/recommendations |
