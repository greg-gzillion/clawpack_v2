## Ankylosing Spondylitis (Modified New York Criteria)
| Criterion | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Clinical** | **Low back pain =3 months** improved by exercise, **Limitation of lumbar spine motion**, **Limitation of chest expansion** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Spondyloarthritis |
| **Radiographic** | **Sacroliitis =Grade 2 bilaterally** or **Grade 3-4 unilaterally** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Spondyloarthritis |
| **Diagnosis** | **=1 Clinical + Radiographic criterion** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Spondyloarthritis |

## Clinical Features of axSpA
| Feature | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Inflammatory Back Pain** | **Insidious onset**, **Improves with activity**, **Worsens with rest**, **Morning stiffness**, **Night pain** (second half of night) | https://spondylitis.org/about-spondylitis/symptoms/ |
| **Enthesitis** | Inflammation at **tendon/ligament insertion** (Achilles, Plantar fascia) | https://spondylitis.org/about-spondylitis/symptoms/ |
| **Peripheral Arthritis** | **Asymmetric**, **Lower extremity predominant** | https://spondylitis.org/about-spondylitis/symptoms/ |
| **Dactylitis** | **"Sausage digit"** (inflammation of entire digit) | https://spondylitis.org/about-spondylitis/symptoms/ |
| **Extra-Articular** | **Anterior uveitis** (25-30%), **IBD** (5-10%), **Psoriasis** (10%) | https://spondylitis.org/about-spondylitis/symptoms/ |
| **HLA-B27** | **Positive in 80-90%** of AS patients | https://spondylitis.org/about-spondylitis/symptoms/ |

## Treatment of axSpA (ACR/SAA/SPARTAN 2019)
| Disease Activity | Recommendation | URL |
|------------------|----------------|-----|
| **Active axSpA** | **NSAIDs** (first-line), **Physical therapy** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/AS-SPA-Clinical-Practice-Guideline-2019.pdf |
| **Inadequate Response to NSAIDs** | **TNF inhibitor** (adalimumab, etanercept, infliximab, certolizumab, golimumab) OR **IL-17 inhibitor** (secukinumab, ixekizumab) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/AS-SPA-Clinical-Practice-Guideline-2019.pdf |
| **Contraindication to TNFi/IL-17i** | **JAK inhibitor** (tofacitinib, upadacitinib) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/AS-SPA-Clinical-Practice-Guideline-2019.pdf |
| **Stable Disease** | **Taper biologics** (do not abruptly stop) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/AS-SPA-Clinical-Practice-Guideline-2019.pdf |
