## ANCA-Associated Vasculitis (AAV)
| Disease | ANCA Pattern | Target Antigen | Key Features | URL |
|---------|--------------|----------------|--------------|-----|
| **Granulomatosis with Polyangiitis (GPA)** | **c-ANCA** (90%) | **PR3** | **Upper/lower respiratory tract** (sinusitis, Pulmonary nodules/cavities), **Renal** (necrotizing GN) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Granulomatosis-with-Polyangiitis-Wegeners |
| **Microscopic Polyangiitis (MPA)** | **p-ANCA** (50-75%) | **MPO** | **Renal** (necrotizing GN), **Pulmonary hemorrhage**, **No granulomas** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Microscopic-Polyangiitis |
| **Eosinophilic Granulomatosis with Polyangiitis (EGPA/Churg-Strauss)** | **p-ANCA** (40-50%) | **MPO** | **Asthma**, **Eosinophilia**, **Neuropathy** (mononeuritis multiplex), **Cardiac involvement** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Eosinophilic-Granulomatosis-with-Polyangiitis |

## Treatment of ANCA-Associated Vasculitis (ACR/VF 2021)
| Phase | Recommendation | URL |
|-------|----------------|-----|
| **Induction (Severe)** | **Corticosteroids + Rituximab** OR **Corticosteroids + Cyclophosphamide** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/ANCA-Associated-Vasculitis-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **Induction (Non-Severe)** | **Corticosteroids + Methotrexate** or **Mycophenolate** | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/ANCA-Associated-Vasculitis-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **Maintenance** | **Rituximab** (preferred) OR **Methotrexate** OR **Azathioprine**; **Continue for =18-24 months** after remission | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/ANCA-Associated-Vasculitis-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **Relapse** | **Rituximab** (preferred over cyclophosphamide) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/ANCA-Associated-Vasculitis-Guideline-2021.pdf |
| **Avacopan** | **Oral C5a receptor antagonist** as glucocorticoid-sparing agent | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/ANCA-Associated-Vasculitis-Guideline-2021.pdf |
