# Rheumatology - Sjögren Syndrome

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Autoimmune exocrinopathy** causing **sicca symptoms** (dry eyes, dry mouth) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Sjogrens-Syndrome |
| **Primary Sjögren** | No associated autoimmune disease | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Sjogrens-Syndrome |
| **Secondary Sjögren** | Associated with **RA**, **SLE**, **SSc** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Sjogrens-Syndrome |
| **Prevalence** | **~0.5-1%** of population; **Women > Men (9:1)** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/sjogrens-syndrome |
| **Age of Onset** | Peak: **40-60 years** | https://www.sjogrens.org/understanding-sjogrens/symptoms |

## 2016 ACR/EULAR Classification Criteria
| Item | Weight/Score | URL |
|------|--------------|-----|
| **Labial Salivary Gland Biopsy** | **Focal lymphocytic sialadenitis with focus score =1** (3) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Sjogrens-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Anti-SSA/Ro Antibody** | **Positive** (3) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Sjogrens-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Ocular Staining Score** | **=5** (1) OR **=5 + positive Schirmer** (2) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Sjogrens-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Schirmer Test** | **=5 mm/5 min** (1) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Sjogrens-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Unstimulated Whole Saliva Flow Rate** | **=0.1 mL/min** (1) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Sjogrens-Syndrome.pdf |
| **Diagnosis** | **=4 points** (in setting of sicca symptoms) | https://www.rheumatology.org/Portals/0/Files/Classification-Criteria-Sjogrens-Syndrome.pdf |

## Clinical Manifestations
| System | Manifestations | URL |
|--------|----------------|-----|
| **Glandular** | **Keratoconjunctivitis sicca** (dry eyes), **Xerostomia** (dry mouth), **Parotid enlargement**, **Dental caries**, **Oral candidiasis** | https://www.sjogrens.org/understanding-sjogrens/symptoms |
| **Extraglandular** | **Fatigue**, **Arthralgias/Arthritis**, **Raynaud phenomenon**, **Interstitial lung disease**, **Renal tubular acidosis**, **Peripheral neuropathy**, **Purpura**, **Lymphoma** (5-10% lifetime risk) | https://www.sjogrens.org/understanding-sjogrens/symptoms |
| **Laboratory** | **ANA** (70-90%), **Anti-SSA/Ro** (60-70%), **Anti-SSB/La** (40-50%), **Rheumatoid Factor** (50-60%), **Hypergammaglobulinemia** | https://www.sjogrens.org/understanding-sjogrens/diagnosis |

## Treatment of Sjögren Syndrome
| Manifestation | Treatment | URL |
|---------------|-----------|-----|
| **Dry Eyes** | **Artificial tears** (preservative-free), **Cyclosporine 0.05%** (Restasis), **Lifitegrast 5%** (Xiidra), **Punctal plugs**, **Autologous serum tears** | https://www.sjogrens.org/understanding-sjogrens/treatment |
| **Dry Mouth** | **Sugar-free lozenges/gum**, **Pilocarpine** (Salagen), **Cevimeline** (Evoxac), **Fluoride**, **Treat oral candidiasis** | https://www.sjogrens.org/understanding-sjogrens/treatment |
| **Systemic Disease** | **Hydroxychloroquine** (first-line), **Methotrexate**, **Azathioprine**, **Mycophenolate**, **Rituximab** | https://www.sjogrens.org/understanding-sjogrens/treatment |
