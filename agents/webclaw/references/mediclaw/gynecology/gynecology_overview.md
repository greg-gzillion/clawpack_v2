# Gynecology - Overview

## What is Gynecology?
Gynecology is the medical specialty focused on the health of the female reproductive system, including the uterus, ovaries, fallopian tubes, cervix, vagina, and vulva, as well as breast health. It encompasses preventive care, diagnosis and management of reproductive disorders, family planning, and menopausal care.

## Core Areas of Gynecology
| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Preventive Care | Well-woman exams, Cancer screening, Vaccinations |
| Menstrual Disorders | Abnormal uterine bleeding, Amenorrhea, Dysmenorrhea |
| Contraception | Family planning, Birth control methods |
| Reproductive Endocrinology | PCOS, Infertility, Menopause |
| Urogynecology | Pelvic organ prolapse, Urinary incontinence |
| Gynecologic Oncology | Cervical, Uterine, Ovarian, Vulvar cancers |
| Pediatric/Adolescent Gynecology | Pubertal disorders, Congenital anomalies |
| Minimally Invasive Surgery | Laparoscopy, Hysteroscopy, Robotic surgery |
