## Enteral Formula Selection
| Formula Type | Description | Indications | URL |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Standard/Polymeric** | **Intact protein**, Complex carbs, LCT | **Most patients** with normal GI function | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **High-Protein** | **=20% protein** | **Wound healing**, **Critical illness**, **Sarcopenia** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Fiber-Containing** | Added fiber (soluble/insoluble) | **Diarrhea**, **Constipation**, **Stable patients** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Semi-Elemental/Elemental** | **Hydrolyzed protein** or **Free amino acids**, MCT | **Malabsorption**, **Pancreatic insufficiency**, **Short bowel syndrome** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Disease-Specific** | Modified macronutrients/electrolytes | **Renal** (low K/PO4), **Hepatic** (BCAA), **Pulmonary** (low CHO), **Diabetes** (low CHO, fiber) | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

## Enteral Feeding Administration
| Method | Description | Indications | URL |
|--------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Continuous** | **24-hour infusion** via pump | **Critically ill**, **Hemodynamically unstable**, **Jejunal feeds**, **Poor tolerance** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Cyclic** | **8-16 hour infusion** (often overnight) | **Transitioning from continuous**, **Home EN** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Intermittent/Bolus** | **Gravity or syringe** 4-6 times/day | **Stable patients** with gastric access | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

## EN Complications and Management
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **High Gastric Residual Volume (GRV)** | **Do NOT routinely check**; If >500 mL with symptoms, **Prokinetic agent** (metoclopramide, erythromycin), **Post-pyloric feeding** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Diarrhea** | **Rule out infection** (C. diff), **Medications** (sorbitol-containing elixirs, Antibiotics), **Consider fiber-containing or semi-elemental formula** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Constipation** | **Hydration**, **Fiber-containing formula**, **Bowel regimen** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Aspiration** | **Elevate HOB 30-45ｰ**, **Post-pyloric feeding**, **Prokinetic agent** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Refeeding Syndrome** | **Start low, go slow** (10-20 kcal/kg/day), **Monitor/replace electrolytes**, **Thiamin supplementation** | https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng69 |
| **Tube Occlusion** | **Flush q4h with 30 mL water**, **Pancreatic enzymes** or **Coca-Cola** to unclog | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

## PN Components
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Carbohydrate** | **Dextrose** (3.4 kcal/g); Max infusion rate **4-5 mg/kg/min** (prevent hyperglycemia, Hepatic steatosis) | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Protein** | **Amino acids** (4 kcal/g); 0.8-2.5 g/kg/day depending on needs | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Lipid** | **IV lipid emulsion** (10 kcal/g); **SOLE** (soybean), **MCT/LCT**, **FOLE** (fish oil), **SMOFlipid** (mixed); **Provide =2x/week to prevent EFAD** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Electrolytes** | Sodium, Potassium, Calcium, Magnesium, Phosphorus, Chloride, Acetate | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Vitamins** | **Multivitamin (MVI)** added daily | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Trace Elements** | Zinc, Copper, Manganese, Chromium, Selenium | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Fluid** | Individualized (typically 25-40 mL/kg/day) | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

## PN Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Catheter-Related Bloodstream Infection (CRBSI)** | **Strict aseptic technique**, **Dedicated lumen**, **Antibiotic lock therapy** or **Catheter removal** | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/bsi/index.html |
| **Hyperglycemia** | **Insulin** (added to PN or separate infusion); Target glucose **140-180 mg/dL** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Hypoglycemia** | **Taper PN** before stopping (avoid abrupt discontinuation) | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Hypertriglyceridemia** | **Reduce or hold IV lipids** if TG >400 mg/dL | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Refeeding Syndrome** | **Start low, go slow** (=20 kcal/kg/day or 50% estimated needs), **Monitor/replace K, PO4, Mg**, **Thiamin** | https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng69 |
| **Intestinal Failure-Associated Liver Disease (IFALD)** | **Cycle PN**, **Prevent overfeeding**, **Use mixed lipid emulsions**, **Consider enteral stimulation** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Metabolic Bone Disease** | **Monitor Ca, PO4, Vitamin D**, **Encourage weight-bearing activity** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
