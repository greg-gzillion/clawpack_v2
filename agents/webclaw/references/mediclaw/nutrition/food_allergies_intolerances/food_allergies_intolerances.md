# Nutrition - Food Allergies and Intolerances

## Food Allergy vs Intolerance
| Feature | Food Allergy | Food Intolerance | URL |
|---------|--------------|------------------|-----|
| **Mechanism** | **Immune-mediated** (IgE or non-IgE) | **Non-immune** (enzyme deficiency, Chemical sensitivity) | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/diseases-conditions/food-allergy |
| **Onset** | **Minutes-2 hours** (IgE) | Hours-days | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/diseases-conditions/food-allergy |
| **Amount** | **Trace amounts** can trigger | Usually **dose-dependent** | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/diseases-conditions/food-allergy |
| **Severity** | **Anaphylaxis possible** | Not life-threatening | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/diseases-conditions/food-allergy |
| **Diagnosis** | **Skin prick test**, **Serum IgE**, **Oral food challenge** | **Elimination diet**, **Breath test** (lactose) | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/diseases-conditions/food-allergy |

## Major Food Allergens (FDA)
| Allergen | Prevalence | Common Sources | URL |
|----------|------------|----------------|-----|
| **Milk** | Most common in children | Dairy products, Baked goods, Processed foods | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Egg** | Common in children | Baked goods, Mayonnaise, Pasta, Vaccines (some) | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Peanut** | **Most common cause of fatal anaphylaxis** | Peanut butter, Asian cuisine, Baked goods | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Tree Nuts** | Often lifelong | Almonds, Walnuts, Cashews, Pistachios, Pecans | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Soy** | Common in children | Tofu, Edamame, Soy sauce, Processed foods | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Wheat** | Often outgrown | Bread, Pasta, Baked goods (distinct from celiac) | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Fish** | Often lifelong | Salmon, Tuna, Cod, Anchovies | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Shellfish** | **Most common adult-onset allergy** | Shrimp, Crab, Lobster, Scallops | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Sesame** | **Added as 9th major allergen (2023)** | Tahini, Hummus, Baked goods, Asian cuisine | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |

## Management of Food Allergy
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Strict Avoidance** | Eliminate trigger food and cross-contamination | https://www.foodallergy.org/living-food-allergies/food-allergy-essentials/avoiding-cross-contact |
| **Emergency Plan** | **Epinephrine autoinjector** prescription, **Anaphylaxis action plan** | https://www.foodallergy.org/living-food-allergies/food-allergy-essentials/anaphylaxis-action-plan |
| **Label Reading** | **FALCPA** requires clear labeling of major allergens | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |
| **Oral Immunotherapy** | **Palforzia** (peanut OIT) for ages 4-17 | https://www.fda.gov/vaccines-blood-biologics/allergenics/palforzia |

## Common Food Intolerances
| Intolerance | Mechanism | Symptoms | Management | URL |
|-------------|-----------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Lactose Intolerance** | **Lactase deficiency** | Bloating, Gas, Diarrhea after dairy | **Lactose-free dairy**, **Lactase enzyme supplements**, **Limit intake** | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases/lactose-intolerance |
| **Fructose Malabsorption** | Impaired fructose absorption | Bloating, Gas, Diarrhea after high-fructose foods | **Low FODMAP diet**, Limit excess fructose | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases |
| **Histamine Intolerance** | **Impaired histamine degradation** (DAO deficiency) | Headache, Flushing, Hives, GI symptoms | **Low-histamine diet**, DAO supplements | https://www.nccih.nih.gov/health |
| **Sulfite Sensitivity** | Unknown (may involve sulfite oxidase deficiency) | **Asthma exacerbation**, Wheezing | **Avoid sulfites** (wine, dried fruit, processed foods) | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-additives-petitions/sulfites |

## Celiac Disease
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Autoimmune enteropathy** triggered by **gluten** (wheat, barley, rye) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases/celiac-disease |
| **Diagnosis** | **tTG-IgA** + **Total IgA** ? **Duodenal biopsy** (Marsh classification) | https://gi.org/guideline/acg-clinical-guidelines-diagnosis-and-management-of-celiac-disease/ |
| **Treatment** | **Strict lifelong gluten-free diet** | https://celiac.org/gluten-free-diet/ |
| **Gluten-Free Grains** | Rice, Corn, Quinoa, Millet, Buckwheat, Amaranth, Teff, **Certified gluten-free oats** | https://celiac.org/gluten-free-diet/ |
| **Hidden Gluten** | Soy sauce, Malt vinegar, Beer (unless gluten-free), Processed meats, Medications/supplements | https://celiac.org/gluten-free-diet/ |
