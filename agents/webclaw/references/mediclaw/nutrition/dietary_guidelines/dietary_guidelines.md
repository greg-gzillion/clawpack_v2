# Nutrition - Dietary Guidelines

## Dietary Guidelines for Americans (2020-2025)
| Recommendation | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Follow a Healthy Dietary Pattern** | At every life stage | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Customize** | Enjoy nutrient-dense food and beverage choices reflecting personal preferences, cultural traditions, and budgetary considerations | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Limit Added Sugars** | **<10% of calories** (age =2) | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Limit Saturated Fat** | **<10% of calories** (age =2) | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Limit Sodium** | **<2,300 mg/day** (age =14) | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Limit Alcoholic Beverages** | Men =2 drinks/day; Women =1 drink/day | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |

## MyPlate Recommendations
| Food Group | Daily Recommendation (2,000 calorie diet) | URL |
|------------|------------------------------------------|-----|
| **Vegetables** | **2.5 cups** (dark green, red/orange, beans/peas, starchy, other) | https://www.myplate.gov |
| **Fruits** | **2 cups** (whole fruits preferred) | https://www.myplate.gov |
| **Grains** | **6 ounce-equivalents** (=50% whole grains) | https://www.myplate.gov |
| **Protein Foods** | **5.5 ounce-equivalents** (variety: seafood, lean meat, poultry, eggs, beans, nuts, seeds) | https://www.myplate.gov |
| **Dairy** | **3 cups** (low-fat or fat-free milk, yogurt, cheese, fortified soy alternatives) | https://www.myplate.gov |
| **Oils** | **27 g** (5 teaspoons) | https://www.myplate.gov |

## Evidence-Based Dietary Patterns
| Diet | Description | Evidence | URL |
|------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Mediterranean Diet** | High in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, olive oil, fish, nuts; Low in red meat | **Strong**: CVD prevention, Diabetes, Cognitive health, ? Mortality | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/heart-healthy-eating-plans/mediterranean-diet |
| **DASH Diet** | Low sodium, High potassium/magnesium/calcium (fruits, vegetables, low-fat dairy) | **Strong**: Hypertension, ? CVD risk | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/education/dash-eating-plan |
| **Healthy Vegetarian/Vegan** | Emphasis on plant foods; Excludes animal products (vegan) | **Moderate-Strong**: ? CVD, ? Diabetes, ? Cancer | https://www.eatright.org/health/wellness/vegetarian-and-plant-based |
| **MIND Diet** | Mediterranean + DASH; Emphasis on berries, Leafy greens | **Moderate**: ? Alzheimer risk, Slower cognitive decline | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/what-do-we-know-about-diet-and-prevention-alzheimers-disease |
| **Portfolio Diet** | Plant sterols, Soluble fiber, Soy protein, Nuts | **Moderate**: ? LDL cholesterol | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/eat-smart/nutrition-basics/the-portfolio-diet |

## Food Labeling
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Nutrition Facts Label** | Required on most packaged foods | https://www.fda.gov/food/nutrition-facts-label |
| **Serving Size** | Standardized to reflect typical consumption | https://www.fda.gov/food/nutrition-facts-label |
| **% Daily Value (%DV)** | Based on 2,000 calorie diet | https://www.fda.gov/food/nutrition-facts-label |
| **Added Sugars** | Now required on label | https://www.fda.gov/food/nutrition-facts-label |
| **Ingredient List** | Listed in **descending order by weight** | https://www.fda.gov/food/nutrition-facts-label |
| **Health Claims** | Regulated by FDA (e.g., "may reduce risk of heart disease") | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/qualified-health-claims |
