## Sarcopenia
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Age-related loss of muscle mass, strength, and function** | https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/sarcopenia |
| **Diagnosis** | **Low muscle mass** (DXA, BIA) + **Low muscle strength** (grip strength) OR **Low physical performance** (gait speed) | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Prevention/Treatment** | **Protein** (1.0-1.5 g/kg/day, including **leucine-rich sources**), **Vitamin D**, **Resistance exercise** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
