## GLIM Criteria for Malnutrition Diagnosis
| Criterion | Threshold | URL |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Phenotypic Criteria** | | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Non-volitional weight loss** | **>5% in 6 months** or **>10% beyond 6 months** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Low BMI** | **<20** (age <70) or **<22** (age =70) | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Reduced muscle mass** | Validated assessment method | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Etiologic Criteria** | | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Reduced food intake** | **=50% of requirements for >1 week** or **any reduction for >2 weeks** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Inflammation** | Acute disease/injury or chronic disease | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Diagnosis** | **=1 Phenotypic + =1 Etiologic criterion** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

## Estimating Energy Requirements
| Method | Formula | URL |
|--------|---------|-----|
| **Mifflin-St Jeor** | Men: **(10 ﾗ kg) + (6.25 ﾗ cm) - (5 ﾗ age) + 5**; Women: **(10 ﾗ kg) + (6.25 ﾗ cm) - (5 ﾗ age) - 161** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/10436/mifflin-st-jeor-equation |
| **Harris-Benedict** | Men: **66.5 + (13.75 ﾗ kg) + (5.003 ﾗ cm) - (6.775 ﾗ age)**; Women: **655.1 + (9.563 ﾗ kg) + (1.850 ﾗ cm) - (4.676 ﾗ age)** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/10437/harris-benedict-equation |
