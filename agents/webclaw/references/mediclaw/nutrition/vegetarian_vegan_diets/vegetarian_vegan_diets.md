# Nutrition - Vegetarian and Vegan Diets

| Type | Includes | Excludes | URL |
|------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Lacto-Ovo-Vegetarian** | Dairy, Eggs, Plant foods | Meat, Poultry, Fish | https://www.eatright.org/health/wellness/vegetarian-and-plant-based |
| **Lacto-Vegetarian** | Dairy, Plant foods | Eggs, Meat, Poultry, Fish | https://www.eatright.org/health/wellness/vegetarian-and-plant-based |
| **Ovo-Vegetarian** | Eggs, Plant foods | Dairy, Meat, Poultry, Fish | https://www.eatright.org/health/wellness/vegetarian-and-plant-based |

## Health Benefits of Plant-Based Diets
| Benefit | Evidence | URL |
|---------|----------|-----|
| **? Cardiovascular Disease** | **Strong** (lower BMI, BP, LDL, Inflammation) | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/eat-smart/nutrition-basics/vegetarian-vegan-and-meals-without-meat |
| **? Type 2 Diabetes** | **Strong** (improved insulin sensitivity, Lower BMI) | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **? Cancer Risk** | **Moderate** (especially colorectal cancer) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/diet |
| **? All-Cause Mortality** | **Moderate** | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |

## Nutrients of Concern in Vegan Diets
| Nutrient | Risk | Strategies | URL |
|----------|------|------------|-----|
| **Vitamin B12** | **High** (no plant sources) | **Supplement** (25-100 mcg/day or 1,000 mcg 2-3x/week) OR **Fortified foods** | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/VitaminB12-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Iron** | **Moderate** (non-heme iron less bioavailable) | **Pair with vitamin C**, **Soak/sprout legumes**, **Cook in cast iron** | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Iron-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Zinc** | **Moderate** (phytates inhibit absorption) | **Soak/sprout legumes**, **Leaven bread**, **Nuts/seeds** | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Zinc-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Calcium** | **Moderate** (if no dairy) | **Fortified plant milks**, **Calcium-set tofu**, **Leafy greens** (low oxalate: kale, bok choy) | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Calcium-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Vitamin D** | **Moderate** (few plant sources) | **Sunlight**, **Fortified foods**, **Supplement** (especially winter/latitudes) | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/VitaminD-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Iodine** | **Moderate** (variable in plant foods) | **Iodized salt**, **Seaweed** (kelp - caution with excess) | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Iodine-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Omega-3 (DHA/EPA)** | **Moderate** (no direct source) | **ALA-rich foods** (flax, chia, walnuts), **Algae-based DHA/EPA supplement** | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Omega3FattyAcids-HealthProfessional/ |
| **Protein** | **Low** (if varied diet) | **Variety of plant proteins** (legumes, soy, nuts, seeds, whole grains) | https://www.eatright.org/health/wellness/vegetarian-and-plant-based |
