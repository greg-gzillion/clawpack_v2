## Type 2 Diabetes
| Intervention | Evidence | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Weight Loss** | **Strong** | **5-15% weight loss** improves glycemia, May induce remission | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Mediterranean Diet** | **Strong** | Improves A1c, ? CVD risk | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Low-Carbohydrate Diet** | **Moderate** | May improve glycemia and reduce medication needs | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Carbohydrate Quality** | **Moderate** | Emphasize **whole grains**, **legumes**, **vegetables**; Limit added sugars, Refined grains | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Meal Timing** | **Limited** | Individualize; Avoid skipping meals if on insulin/secretagogues | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |

## Chronic Kidney Disease
| Intervention | Evidence | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Sodium Restriction** | **Strong** | <2,300 mg/day (lower with HTN/edema) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/nutrikidfail_stage1-4 |
| **Protein Restriction (Non-Dialysis)** | **Moderate** | **0.55-0.60 g/kg/day** (slows progression) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/nutrikidfail_stage1-4 |
| **Protein (Dialysis)** | **Strong** | **1.2 g/kg/day** (hemodialysis), **1.2-1.5 g/kg/day** (peritoneal) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/nutrihemo |
| **Potassium Restriction** | **Variable** | Individualize based on labs (typically <2,000-3,000 mg/day) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/potassium |
| **Phosphorus Restriction** | **Variable** | Individualize; Avoid **phosphate additives** in processed foods | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/phosphorus |

## Cancer
| Intervention | Evidence | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Weight Management** | **Strong** | Avoid weight gain; Maintain healthy weight | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/diet |
| **Plant-Based Diet** | **Moderate** | Emphasize fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/diet |
| **Limit Red/Processed Meat** | **Moderate** | **<18 oz/week** red meat; **Avoid processed meat** | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/diet |
| **Limit Alcohol** | **Strong** | =1 drink/day women, =2 drinks/day men (less is better) | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/alcohol |
| **Cachexia Management** | **Moderate** | **High-protein, high-calorie**, **Omega-3 fatty acids**, **Consider appetite stimulants** | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

## Osteoporosis
| Intervention | Evidence | Recommendation | URL |
|--------------|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Calcium** | **Strong** | **1,000-1,200 mg/day** (preferably dietary) | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |
| **Vitamin D** | **Strong** | **800-1,000 IU/day** (maintain level =30 ng/mL) | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |
| **Protein** | **Moderate** | Adequate protein (1.0-1.2 g/kg/day) supports bone health | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |
| **Sodium** | **Moderate** | High sodium ? urinary calcium excretion | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |
| **Caffeine** | **Limited** | Moderate intake (<400 mg/day) unlikely harmful if adequate calcium | https://www.bonehealthandosteoporosis.org |
