# Nutrition - Culinary Medicine

## What is Culinary Medicine?
Culinary medicine is an evidence-based field that blends the art of food and cooking with the science of medicine. It aims to help people make personal decisions about accessing and eating high-quality meals that help prevent and treat disease and restore well-being.

| Core Principle | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Food as Medicine** | Using whole, minimally processed foods to prevent and treat disease | https://culinarymedicine.org |
| **Practical Skills** | Teaching patients **how to shop, cook, and store healthy food** | https://culinarymedicine.org |
| **Cultural Relevance** | Adapting recommendations to patients' **cultural preferences and traditions** | https://culinarymedicine.org |
| **Access and Affordability** | Addressing **food insecurity** and **budget constraints** | https://culinarymedicine.org |

## Healthy Cooking Techniques
| Technique | Description | Benefits | URL |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Steaming** | Cook food over boiling water | Preserves nutrients, No added fat | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/cooking-skills |
| **Roasting/Baking** | Cook in oven with dry heat | Concentrates flavor, Minimal added fat | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/cooking-skills |
| **Sautéing/Stir-Frying** | Cook quickly in small amount of oil | Quick, Retains texture/nutrients | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/cooking-skills |
| **Poaching** | Simmer gently in liquid | No added fat, Tender result | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/cooking-skills |
| **Grilling/Broiling** | Cook with direct high heat | Adds flavor, Fat drips away | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/cooking-skills |
| **Braising/Stewing** | Slow cook in liquid | Tenderizes tough cuts, Develops flavor | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/cooking-skills |
