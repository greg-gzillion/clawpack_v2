# Nutrition - Therapeutic Diets

## Cardiovascular Diets
| Diet | Indications | Key Features | URL |
|------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **DASH Diet** | **Hypertension**, **Heart failure** | **Low sodium** (<2,300 mg), High potassium/magnesium/calcium | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/education/dash-eating-plan |
| **Mediterranean Diet** | **CVD prevention**, **Diabetes**, **Cognitive health** | High fruits/vegetables, Olive oil, Fish, Nuts; Low red meat | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/heart-healthy-eating-plans/mediterranean-diet |
| **Portfolio Diet** | **Hyperlipidemia** | Plant sterols, Soluble fiber, Soy protein, Nuts | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating/eat-smart/nutrition-basics/the-portfolio-diet |
| **Heart-Healthy Diet** | General CVD prevention | Limit saturated/trans fat, Added sugars, Sodium; Emphasize whole grains, Lean protein | https://www.heart.org/en/healthy-living/healthy-eating |

## Diabetes-Specific Diets
| Approach | Description | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Carbohydrate Counting** | Track grams of carbohydrate per meal/snack | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Glycemic Index/Load** | Choose foods with lower glycemic impact | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Mediterranean Diet** | **Evidence for glycemic control and CVD risk reduction** | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Low-Carbohydrate Diet** | **<26% calories from carbs**; May improve glycemia and reduce medication needs | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Very Low-Carbohydrate/Ketogenic** | **<10% calories from carbs**; Requires medical supervision | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Plant-Based Diet** | Emphasis on whole plant foods; May improve insulin sensitivity | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |

## Renal Diets
| Stage | Diet | Key Features | URL |
|-------|------|--------------|-----|
| **CKD Stages 1-3** | General healthy diet | **Limit sodium** (<2,300 mg), **Avoid processed foods** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/nutrikidfail_stage1-4 |
| **CKD Stages 4-5 (Non-Dialysis)** | **Low protein**, Low sodium, Low potassium, Low phosphorus | **0.55-0.60 g/kg/day protein**, **<2,000 mg sodium**, Monitor K/PO4 | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/nutrikidfail_stage1-4 |
| **Hemodialysis** | **High protein**, Low sodium, Low potassium, Low phosphorus, **Fluid restriction** | **1.2 g/kg/day protein**, **<2,000 mg sodium**, **K <2,000-3,000 mg**, **PO4 <800-1,000 mg** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/nutrihemo |
| **Peritoneal Dialysis** | **High protein**, Low sodium, Low potassium, Low phosphorus, **Less fluid restriction** | **1.2-1.5 g/kg/day protein** (absorbs glucose from dialysate) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/nutripd |
| **Nephrolithiasis (Calcium Oxalate)** | **High fluid**, **Low sodium**, **Normal calcium**, **Low oxalate** | **>2.5 L/day fluid**, **<2,300 mg sodium**, **1,000-1,200 mg calcium**, Avoid high-oxalate foods | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/kidneystones_prevent |

## Gastrointestinal Diets
| Diet | Indications | Key Features | URL |
|------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **Low FODMAP** | **IBS** | Eliminate fermentable oligo-, di-, mono-saccharides and polyols ? Reintroduce | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases/irritable-bowel-syndrome/treatment |
| **Gluten-Free** | **Celiac disease**, **Non-celiac gluten sensitivity** | Eliminate wheat, barley, rye; Oats if certified gluten-free | https://celiac.org/gluten-free-diet/ |
| **Lactose-Free/Low-Lactose** | **Lactose intolerance** | Eliminate/reduce dairy; Lactase enzyme supplements | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases/lactose-intolerance |
| **Low-Residue/Low-Fiber** | **Bowel obstruction**, **Active IBD**, **Pre-colonoscopy** | Limit fiber to <10-15 g/day; Avoid seeds, nuts, raw vegetables | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |
| **Elemental/Semi-Elemental** | **Severe malabsorption**, **Crohn disease** | Predigested nutrients requiring minimal digestion | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

## Hepatic Diets
| Condition | Diet | Key Features | URL |
|-----------|------|--------------|-----|
| **Cirrhosis** | **High protein**, **High calorie** | **1.2-1.5 g/kg/day protein**, **30-35 kcal/kg/day**, **Late evening snack** | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines |
| **Hepatic Encephalopathy** | **Normal-high protein** (not low protein) | **Avoid protein restriction** (worsens sarcopenia), **Consider branched-chain amino acids** | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/hepatic-encephalopathy |
| **Ascites** | **Sodium restriction** | **<2,000 mg/day sodium** | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/ascites |
| **NAFLD/NASH** | **Weight loss** | **7-10% weight loss**; Mediterranean diet | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/nafld |

## Other Therapeutic Diets
| Diet | Indications | Key Features | URL |
|------|-------------|--------------|-----|

| **Full Liquid** | Transition from clear liquid | All liquids including milk, cream soups, ice cream | https://www.nutritioncare.org/Guidelines_and_Clinical_Resources/ |

| **Thickened Liquids** | **Aspiration risk** | Nectar-thick, Honey-thick, Pudding-thick | https://www.asha.org/public/speech/swallowing/dysphagia-diets/ |
