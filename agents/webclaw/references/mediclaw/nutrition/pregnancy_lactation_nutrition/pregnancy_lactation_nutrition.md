# Nutrition - Pregnancy and Lactation Nutrition

## Prenatal Nutrition
| Nutrient | RDA (Pregnancy) | Key Functions | Food Sources | URL |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|--------------|-----|
| **Calories** | **+340 kcal/day** (2nd trimester), **+452 kcal/day** (3rd trimester) | Support fetal growth | Nutrient-dense foods | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Protein** | **71 g/day** (1.1 g/kg) | Fetal tissue development | Lean meat, Poultry, Fish, Eggs, Beans | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Folate/Folic Acid** | **600 mcg DFE/day** | **Neural tube defect prevention** | Leafy greens, Fortified grains, **Supplement** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/folicacid/recommendations.html |
| **Iron** | **27 mg/day** | ? Blood volume, Fetal iron stores | Red meat, Poultry, Beans, Fortified cereals | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Calcium** | **1,000 mg/day** | Fetal bone development | Dairy, Leafy greens, Fortified foods | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Vitamin D** | **600 IU/day** | Calcium absorption, Immune function | Fatty fish, Fortified milk, Sunlight, Supplement | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **DHA** | **200-300 mg/day** | Fetal brain/eye development | Fatty fish (low mercury), Algae supplements | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Iodine** | **220 mcg/day** | Fetal thyroid function | Iodized salt, Seafood, Dairy, Supplement | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Choline** | **450 mg/day** | Fetal brain development | Eggs, Liver, Soybeans, Supplement | https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Choline-HealthProfessional/ |

## Prenatal Supplements
| Supplement | Recommendation | URL |
|------------|----------------|-----|
| **Prenatal Vitamin** | **Folic acid 400-800 mcg**, **Iron 27 mg**, **Iodine 150 mcg** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Vitamin D** | **600 IU/day** (many prenatal vitamins contain 400 IU; additional may be needed) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **DHA** | Consider if low fish intake | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Calcium** | Supplement if dietary intake inadequate | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |

## Foods to Avoid/Limit in Pregnancy
| Food | Rationale | URL |
|------|-----------|-----|
| **High-Mercury Fish** | **Neurotoxicity** (shark, swordfish, king mackerel, tilefish) | https://www.fda.gov/food/consumers/advice-about-eating-fish |
| **Raw/Undercooked Meat, Fish, Eggs** | **Foodborne illness** (Listeria, Salmonella, Toxoplasma) | https://www.cdc.gov/listeria/prevention.html |
| **Unpasteurized Dairy** | **Listeria** | https://www.cdc.gov/listeria/prevention.html |
| **Deli Meats/Hot Dogs** | **Listeria** (heat until steaming) | https://www.cdc.gov/listeria/prevention.html |
| **Raw Sprouts** | **Foodborne illness** | https://www.fda.gov/food/consumers/raw-sprouts |
| **Alcohol** | **Fetal alcohol spectrum disorders** (avoid completely) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/fasd/alcohol-use.html |
| **Excess Caffeine** | **Limit to <200 mg/day** (~12 oz coffee) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |

## Lactation Nutrition
| Nutrient | RDA (Lactation) | Key Functions | URL |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|-----|
| **Calories** | **+330-400 kcal/day** (first 6 months) | Support milk production | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/diet-and-micronutrients/maternal-diet.html |
| **Protein** | **71 g/day** | Milk protein synthesis | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/diet-and-micronutrients/maternal-diet.html |
| **Fluid** | **Adequate hydration** (drink to thirst) | Milk volume | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/diet-and-micronutrients/maternal-diet.html |
| **DHA** | **200-300 mg/day** | Infant brain development | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/diet-and-micronutrients/maternal-diet.html |
| **Calcium** | **1,000 mg/day** | Milk calcium (mobilized from bone regardless of intake) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/diet-and-micronutrients/maternal-diet.html |
| **Vitamin D** | **600 IU/day** | Milk vitamin D content depends on maternal status | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/diet-and-micronutrients/maternal-diet.html |

## Gestational Diabetes Nutrition
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Carbohydrate Distribution** | **Spread throughout day** (3 meals + 2-4 snacks) | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Carbohydrate Intake** | **Individualized** (typically 40-50% calories) | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Glycemic Control Goals** | **Fasting <95 mg/dL**, **1h postprandial <140 mg/dL**, **2h postprandial <120 mg/dL** | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Avoid Ketosis** | **=175 g carbohydrate/day** | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
