# Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery - Cleft and Craniofacial Surgery

## Cleft Lip and Palate
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Epidemiology** | **1 in 700** live births; **Most common congenital craniofacial anomaly** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/birthdefects/cleftlip.html |
| **Classification** | **Cleft lip** (unilateral/bilateral, Complete/incomplete), **Cleft palate** (primary/secondary), **Submucous cleft** | https://acpacares.org |
| **Multidisciplinary Team** | **Surgeon**, **Orthodontist**, **Speech pathologist**, **Otolaryngologist**, **Audiologist**, **Geneticist**, **Psychologist** | https://acpacares.org |

## Surgical Timeline for Cleft Care
| Procedure | Age | URL |
|-----------|-----|-----|
| **Cleft Lip Repair** | **3-6 months** ("Rule of 10s": 10 lbs, 10 weeks, Hgb 10) | https://acpacares.org |
| **Cleft Palate Repair** | **9-18 months** (before speech development) | https://acpacares.org |
| **Alveolar Bone Grafting** | **6-11 years** (mixed dentition, before canine eruption) | https://acpacares.org |
| **Orthognathic Surgery** | **Teenage years** (after skeletal maturity) | https://acpacares.org |
| **Rhinoplasty** | **Teenage years** (after nasal growth complete) | https://acpacares.org |

## Cleft Lip Repair Techniques
| Technique | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Millard Rotation-Advancement** | **Rotate medial lip segment**, **Advance lateral segment** | https://acpacares.org |
| **Tennison-Randall Triangular Flap** | **Triangular flap** from lateral to medial | https://acpacares.org |
| **Mulliken Repair (Bilateral)** | **Simultaneous bilateral repair** with **nasal correction** | https://acpacares.org |

## Cleft Palate Repair
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Goals** | **Separate oral and nasal cavities**, **Restore velopharyngeal competence** (speech), **Preserve maxillary growth** | https://acpacares.org |
| **Techniques** | **Von Langenbeck**, **Veau-Wardill-Kilner (V-Y pushback)** , **Furlow double-opposing Z-plasty** | https://acpacares.org |
| **Intravelar Veloplasty** | **Reconstruction of levator veli palatini sling** | https://acpacares.org |

## Velopharyngeal Insufficiency (VPI)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Inability to close velopharyngeal port** during speech ? **Hypernasality**, **Nasal emission** | https://acpacares.org |
| **Evaluation** | **Speech evaluation**, **Nasopharyngoscopy**, **Videofluoroscopy** | https://acpacares.org |
| **Treatment** | **Pharyngeal flap**, **Sphincter pharyngoplasty**, **Posterior pharyngeal wall augmentation** | https://acpacares.org |

## Craniosynostosis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Premature fusion of cranial sutures** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/craniosynostosis |
| **Types** | **Scaphocephaly** (sagittal), **Brachycephaly** (coronal), **Trigonocephaly** (metopic), **Plagiocephaly** (unilateral coronal/lambdoid) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/craniosynostosis |
| **Syndromic** | **Apert**, **Crouzon**, **Pfeiffer**, **Muenke**, **Saethre-Chotzen** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/craniosynostosis |
| **Treatment** | **Endoscopic strip craniectomy** (<3-6 months) or **Open cranial vault remodeling** (>6 months) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/craniosynostosis |
