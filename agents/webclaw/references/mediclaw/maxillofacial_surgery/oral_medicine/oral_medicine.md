## Oral Lichen Planus
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Clinical Forms** | **Reticular** (Wickham striae - most common), **Erosive** (painful), **Atrophic**, **Plaque-like**, **Bullous** | https://www.aaom.com/oral-lichen-planus |
| **Pathology** | **T-cell mediated autoimmune** against basal keratinocytes | https://www.aaom.com/oral-lichen-planus |
| **Malignant Potential** | **Erosive and atrophic forms** have **~1% risk** of transformation to SCC | https://www.aaom.com/oral-lichen-planus |
| **Treatment** | **Topical corticosteroids** (first-line), **Topical calcineurin inhibitors** (tacrolimus), **Systemic immunosuppression** (severe) | https://www.aaom.com/oral-lichen-planus |

## Oral Candidiasis
| Type | Description | Treatment | URL |
|------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Pseudomembranous (Thrush)** | **White plaques** that wipe off, **Erythematous base** | **Topical antifungals** (nystatin, clotrimazole), **Oral fluconazole** | https://www.aaom.com/oral-candidiasis |
| **Erythematous** | **Red patches**, **Burning** (denture stomatitis, Antibiotic-associated) | **Topical antifungals**, **Denture hygiene** | https://www.aaom.com/oral-candidiasis |
| **Hyperplastic** | **White plaques** that **do NOT wipe off** | **Biopsy** (rule out dysplasia/SCC), **Oral fluconazole** | https://www.aaom.com/oral-candidiasis |
| **Angular Cheilitis** | **Erythema/fissuring** at commissures | **Topical antifungal + steroid** | https://www.aaom.com/oral-candidiasis |
