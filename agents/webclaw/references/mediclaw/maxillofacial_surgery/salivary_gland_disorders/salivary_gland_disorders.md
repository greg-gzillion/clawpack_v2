## Obstructive Salivary Gland Disorders
| Disorder | Gland | Clinical Features | Diagnosis | Treatment | URL |
|----------|-------|------------------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Sialolithiasis** | **Submandibular** (80-90%), **Parotid** | **Pain/swelling with meals** (mealtime syndrome) | **CT**, **Ultrasound**, **Sialography** | **Sialendoscopy**, **Transoral removal**, **Gland excision** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-disorders |
| **Mucocele** | **Minor salivary glands** (lower lip) | **Fluctuant bluish swelling**, **Ruptures and recurs** | **Clinical** | **Excision** (including feeding minor glands) | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-disorders |
| **Ranula** | **Sublingual gland** | **Plunging** (extends through mylohyoid) or **Simple** | **CT**, **MRI** | **Marsupialization**, **Sublingual gland excision** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-disorders |

## Inflammatory Salivary Gland Disorders
| Disorder | Gland | Clinical Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|-------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Acute Bacterial Sialadenitis** | **Parotid** (most common) | **Pain**, **Swelling**, **Erythema**, **Purulent duct discharge**, **Dehydration** | **Hydration**, **Antibiotics** (anti-staphylococcal), **Sialogogues**, **I&D** if abscess | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-disorders |
| **Chronic Sialadenitis** | **Any** | **Recurrent pain/swelling** | **Sialendoscopy**, **Gland excision** (refractory) | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-disorders |
| **Sjögren Syndrome** | **All** | **Xerostomia**, **Xerophthalmia**, **Bilateral parotid enlargement** | **Symptomatic**, **Pilocarpine/Cevimeline**, **Immunosuppression** (systemic) | https://www.sjogrens.org |
| **Juvenile Recurrent Parotitis** | **Parotid** | **Children**, **Recurrent episodes** | **Conservative**, **Sialendoscopy** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-disorders |

## Salivary Gland Neoplasms
| Tumor | Frequency | Gland | Behavior | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Pleomorphic Adenoma** | **Most common** (50-60%) | **Parotid** (80%) | **Benign** (risk of malignant transformation) | **Superficial or Total parotidectomy** (with facial nerve preservation) | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-neoplasms |
| **Warthin Tumor** | **Second most common** | **Parotid** (tail) | **Benign**, **Smoking-associated**, **Bilateral/Multifocal** | **Superficial parotidectomy** or **Observation** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-neoplasms |
| **Mucoepidermoid Carcinoma** | **Most common malignant** | **Parotid** (most), **Minor glands** | **Low to high grade** | **Surgical resection** ± **Neck dissection** ± **Radiation** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-neoplasms |
| **Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma** | **Second most common malignant** | **Minor glands** | **Perineural invasion**, **Late metastases** | **Surgical resection** + **Radiation** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-neoplasms |
| **Acinic Cell Carcinoma** | **Low-grade malignant** | **Parotid** | **Indolent** | **Surgical resection** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/salivary-gland-neoplasms |
