# Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery - Orthognathic Surgery

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Surgical correction of jaw deformities** to improve **function** (occlusion, airway) and **esthetics** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Indications** | **Malocclusion** not correctable with orthodontics alone, **Obstructive sleep apnea**, **Facial asymmetry**, **TMJ disorders** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Multidisciplinary** | **Orthodontist + Surgeon** (pre-surgical orthodontics ? surgery ? post-surgical orthodontics) | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |

## Common Orthognathic Procedures
| Procedure | Indication | Description | URL |
|-----------|------------|-------------|-----|
| **Le Fort I Osteotomy** | **Maxillary deficiency/excess** | **Horizontal osteotomy** above maxillary teeth; **Mobilize and reposition** maxilla | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Le Fort I Segmental** | **Transverse maxillary deficiency** | **Le Fort I with segmentation** (2-3 pieces) to **widen maxilla** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Surgically Assisted Rapid Palatal Expansion (SARPE)** | **Severe transverse maxillary deficiency** | **Le Fort I with midpalatal split** + **Distractor** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Bilateral Sagittal Split Osteotomy (BSSO)** | **Mandibular deficiency/excess** | **Sagittal split of mandibular ramus**; **Advance** (Class II) or **Setback** (Class III) | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Intraoral Vertical Ramus Osteotomy (IVRO)** | **Mandibular setback** | **Vertical osteotomy of ramus**; **Overlaps proximally** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Genioplasty** | **Chin deficiency/excess** | **Horizontal osteotomy of chin**; **Advance**, **Setback**, **Lengthen**, **Shorten** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |

## Orthognathic Surgery for Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Maxillomandibular Advancement (MMA)** | **Le Fort I + BSSO** to **advance both jaws** 10-12 mm | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Mechanism** | **Enlarges posterior airway space** (PAS), **Tensions pharyngeal musculature** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Success Rate** | **>85-90%** (AHI reduction =50% and <20) | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Indications** | **Moderate-severe OSA** intolerant/refractory to CPAP | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |

## Complications of Orthognathic Surgery
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Hemorrhage** | **Local measures**, **Ligation of vessels**, **Angiography/embolization** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Nerve Injury** (IAN, infraorbital) | **Observation**, **Corticosteroids**, **Sensory re-education** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Malunion/Nonunion** | **Rigid fixation**, **Bone grafting**, **Revision surgery** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **Relapse** | **Orthodontic retention**, **Revision surgery** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
| **TMJ Dysfunction** | **Conservative management**, **Arthroscopy**, **Arthroplasty** | https://www.aaoms.org/procedures/corrective-jaw-surgery |
