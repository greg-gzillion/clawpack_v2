# Hospital Medicine - Inpatient Nephrology

## Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **KDIGO Criteria** | **? Cr =0.3 mg/dL in 48h** OR **? Cr =1.5x baseline** OR **UOP <0.5 mL/kg/h x6h** | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |
| **Prerenal** | **Hypovolemia**, **CHF**, **Cirrhosis** (FeNa <1%, UNa <20) | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |
| **Intrinsic (ATN)** | **Ischemia**, **Nephrotoxins**, **Sepsis** (FeNa >2%, UNa >40, Muddy brown casts) | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |
| **Postrenal** | **Obstruction** (BPH, Stones, Malignancy) ? **Bladder scan**, **Renal ultrasound** | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |
| **Management** | **Treat underlying cause**, **Maintain euvolemia**, **Avoid nephrotoxins**, **Renal replacement therapy** (if indicated) | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/ |

## Hyperkalemia
| Severity | ECG Findings | Management | URL |
|----------|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Mild** (K 5.5-6.0) | **Peaked T waves** | **Kayexalate**, **Loop diuretics**, **Treat cause** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/what-hyperkalemia |
| **Moderate** (K 6.0-7.0) | **PR prolongation**, **QRS widening** | **Calcium gluconate**, **Insulin/Dextrose**, **Albuterol**, **Sodium bicarbonate** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/what-hyperkalemia |
| **Severe** (K >7.0) | **Sine wave**, **Ventricular fibrillation** | **Emergent dialysis** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/what-hyperkalemia |

## Hyponatremia
| Classification | Cause | Management | URL |
|----------------|-------|------------|-----|
| **Hypovolemic** | **GI losses**, **Diuretics**, **Adrenal insufficiency** | **IV fluids** (NS) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/hyponatremia |
| **Euvolemic** | **SIADH**, **Hypothyroidism**, **Psychogenic polydipsia** | **Fluid restriction**, **Treat cause** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/hyponatremia |
| **Hypervolemic** | **CHF**, **Cirrhosis**, **CKD** | **Fluid restriction**, **Diuretics** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/hyponatremia |
| **Severe Symptomatic** | **Seizures**, **Altered mental status** | **Hypertonic saline** (3% NaCl) | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/hyponatremia |
