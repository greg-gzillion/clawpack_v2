# Hospital Medicine - Venous Thromboembolism (VTE)

## VTE Prophylaxis
| Risk Level | Caprini Score | Prophylaxis | URL |
|------------|---------------|-------------|-----|
| **Very Low** | **0** | **Early ambulation** | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Low** | **1-2** | **Mechanical** (SCDs) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Moderate** | **3-4** | **LMWH** or **UFH** ± Mechanical | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **High** | **=5** | **LMWH** or **UFH** + Mechanical | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |

## Acute DVT/PE Treatment
| Phase | Duration | Anticoagulant | URL |
|-------|----------|---------------|-----|
| **Initial** | **3-6 months** | **DOAC** (apixaban, rivaroxaban) preferred | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Provoked VTE** | **3 months** | Discontinue after 3 months | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Unprovoked VTE** | **Indefinite** | Continue (reassess annually) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Cancer-Associated** | **Indefinite** | **LMWH** or **DOAC** (apixaban, rivaroxaban, edoxaban) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |

## DOAC Dosing for VTE
| Drug | Acute Treatment | Extended Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Apixaban** | 10 mg BID x7d ? 5 mg BID | 2.5 mg BID (after 6 months) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Rivaroxaban** | 15 mg BID x21d ? 20 mg daily | 10 mg daily (after 6 months) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Edoxaban** | **LMWH x5-10d ? 60 mg daily** | 60 mg daily | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
