## Symptom Management at End of Life
| Symptom | Management | URL |
|---------|------------|-----|
| **Dyspnea** | **Opioids** (morphine 2.5-5 mg PO q4h PRN), **Fan**, **Oxygen** (if hypoxic) | https://www.capc.org |
| **Nausea/Vomiting** | **Haloperidol**, **Metoclopramide**, **Ondansetron**, **Dexamethasone** | https://www.capc.org |
| **Constipation** | **Stimulant laxative + Stool softener** (senna + docusate) | https://www.capc.org |
| **Delirium** | **Haloperidol** (0.5-2 mg PO/SC q4-6h), **Treat underlying cause** | https://www.capc.org |
| **Secretions** | **Glycopyrrolate**, **Scopolamine**, **Atropine** | https://www.capc.org |

## Hospice Eligibility (General Criteria)
| Condition | Criteria | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Cancer** | **Metastatic or progressive despite treatment** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **Dementia** | **FAST stage 7C** + **Complication** (aspiration, UTI, sepsis) | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **Heart Failure** | **NYHA Class IV**, **Optimal therapy**, **EF =20%** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **COPD** | **Disabling dyspnea at rest**, **FEV1 <30%**, **Recurrent hospitalizations** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **General** | **Prognosis =6 months** if disease follows normal course | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
