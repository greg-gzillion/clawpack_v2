## Discharge Planning
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Medication Reconciliation** | **Review and reconcile** admission, transfer, and discharge medications | https://www.hospitalmedicine.org/clinical-topics/medication-reconciliation/ |
| **Discharge Summary** | **Completed within 48 hours**; Includes **hospital course**, **Discharge medications**, **Follow-up plan** | https://www.hospitalmedicine.org/clinical-topics/transitions-of-care/ |
| **Follow-Up Appointment** | **Scheduled before discharge**, **Within 7-14 days** (sooner for high-risk) | https://www.hospitalmedicine.org/clinical-topics/transitions-of-care/ |
| **Teach-Back** | **Patient demonstrates understanding** of discharge instructions | https://www.ahrq.gov/patient-safety/patients-families/index.html |

## Observation vs Inpatient Status
| Feature | Observation | Inpatient | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Duration** | **<48 hours** (typically) | **=48 hours** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Purpose** | **Diagnostic evaluation**, **Short-term treatment** | **Definitive treatment**, **Complex medical needs** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Billing** | **Part B** (outpatient) | **Part A** (inpatient) | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **SNF Eligibility** | **Does NOT count** toward 3-day stay | **Counts** toward 3-day stay | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
