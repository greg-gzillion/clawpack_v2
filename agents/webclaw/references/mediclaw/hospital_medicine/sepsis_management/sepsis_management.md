# Hospital Medicine - Sepsis Management

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Sepsis** | **Life-threatening organ dysfunction** due to **dysregulated host response to infection** (SOFA =2) | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **Septic Shock** | **Sepsis + Vasopressor requirement + Lactate >2 mmol/L** despite adequate fluids | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |

## Surviving Sepsis Campaign Hour-1 Bundle
| Component | Action | URL |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **1** | **Measure lactate** | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **2** | **Obtain blood cultures** (before antibiotics) | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **3** | **Administer broad-spectrum antibiotics** | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **4** | **Administer IV fluids** (30 mL/kg crystalloid for hypotension or lactate =4) | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **5** | **Initiate vasopressors** (if hypotensive during/after fluids) | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |

## Sepsis Management After Initial Resuscitation
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Reassess Volume Status** | **Dynamic measures** (passive leg raise, IVC collapsibility, SVV) preferred over static (CVP) | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **Source Control** | **Identify and control source** within **6-12 hours** | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **De-escalate Antibiotics** | **Narrow spectrum** based on cultures/sensitivities | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
| **Corticosteroids** | **Consider for septic shock** with ongoing vasopressor requirement (hydrocortisone 200 mg/day) | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |
