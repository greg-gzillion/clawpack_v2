## Hospital-Acquired Conditions (HACs)
| Condition | Prevention | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Central Line-Associated Bloodstream Infection (CLABSI)** | **Aseptic insertion**, **Chlorhexidine skin prep**, **Daily review of necessity** | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/bsi/index.html |
| **Catheter-Associated UTI (CAUTI)** | **Avoid unnecessary catheters**, **Remove as soon as possible**, **Aseptic insertion** | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/cauti/ |
| **Surgical Site Infection (SSI)** | **Antibiotic prophylaxis**, **Normothermia**, **Glycemic control** | https://www.cdc.gov/infection-control/hcp/ssi/index.html |
| **Ventilator-Associated Events (VAE)** | **Head of bed =30ｰ**, **Daily sedation vacation**, **Oral care** | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/guidelines/vap/index.html |
| **Pressure Injury** | **Risk assessment**, **Repositioning q2h**, **Pressure-reducing surfaces** | https://www.npiap.com |
| **Falls with Injury** | **Fall risk assessment**, **Universal precautions**, **Targeted interventions** | https://www.ahrq.gov/patient-safety/settings/hospital/fall-prevention/index.html |
| **VTE** | **Risk assessment**, **Prophylaxis** | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
