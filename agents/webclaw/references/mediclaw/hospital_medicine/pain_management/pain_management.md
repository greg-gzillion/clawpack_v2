# Hospital Medicine - Inpatient Pain Management

## Pain Assessment
| Tool | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **Numeric Rating Scale (NRS)** | **0-10 scale** (0 = no pain, 10 = worst pain) | https://www.iasp-pain.org |
| **PQRST** | **P**rovoking, **Q**uality, **R**adiation, **S**everity, **T**iming | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK555962/ |
| **Behavioral Pain Scale (BPS)** | **For intubated patients** (facial expression, Upper limb movement, Ventilator compliance) | https://www.sccm.org/SurvivingSepsisCampaign/Guidelines |

## Opioid Adverse Effects
| Adverse Effect | Management | URL |
|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Constipation** | **Stimulant laxative + Stool softener** (senna + docusate) | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/healthcare-professionals/prescribing/index.html |
| **Nausea/Vomiting** | **Ondansetron**, **Metoclopramide** | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/healthcare-professionals/prescribing/index.html |
| **Sedation** | **Reduce dose**, **Avoid other CNS depressants** | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/healthcare-professionals/prescribing/index.html |
| **Respiratory Depression** | **Naloxone** (dilute 0.04 mg IV q2-3min) | https://www.cdc.gov/stop-overdose/caring/naloxone.html |
