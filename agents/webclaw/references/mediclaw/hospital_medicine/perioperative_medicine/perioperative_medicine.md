## Perioperative Medication Management
| Medication | Management | URL |
|------------|------------|-----|
| **Beta-Blockers** | **Continue** perioperatively | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/perioperative-cardiovascular-evaluation |
| **Statins** | **Continue** perioperatively | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/perioperative-cardiovascular-evaluation |
| **ACEi/ARB** | **Hold morning of surgery** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/perioperative-cardiovascular-evaluation |
| **Diuretics** | **Hold morning of surgery** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/perioperative-cardiovascular-evaluation |
| **Anticoagulants** | **Hold per bleeding/thrombosis risk** | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Diabetes Medications** | **Hold SGLT2i 3-4 days before**; **Hold other oral agents morning of surgery** | https://professional.diabetes.org/standards-of-care |
| **Aspirin (Secondary Prevention)** | **Continue** (unless high bleeding risk) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |

## Perioperative Anticoagulation Bridging
| Risk Category | Management | URL |
|---------------|------------|-----|
| **High VTE Risk** | **Bridge with LMWH** (mechanical mitral valve, Recent VTE, Afib with CHADS =7, AT deficiency) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Moderate VTE Risk** | **Consider bridging** (Afib with CHADS 5-6, Mechanical aortic valve with risk factors) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
| **Low VTE Risk** | **No bridging** (Afib with CHADS =4, Bileaflet aortic valve) | https://journal.chestnet.org/guidelines |
