# Medications - Antiinfectives (Antivirals, Antifungals, Antiparasitics)

## Antivirals
| Drug | Indication | Mechanism | Key Points | URL |
|------|------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Acyclovir** | HSV, VZV | Inhibits viral DNA polymerase | **Crystalluria** (hydrate), **Renal dosing** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/acyclovir |
| **Valacyclovir** | HSV, VZV | Prodrug of acyclovir (higher bioavailability) | Same as acyclovir | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/valacyclovir |
| **Famciclovir** | HSV, VZV | Prodrug of penciclovir | Same as acyclovir | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/famciclovir |
| **Oseltamivir** | Influenza A/B | Neuraminidase inhibitor | **Start within 48h**, **Nausea/Vomiting** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/oseltamivir |
| **Remdesivir** | COVID-19 | RNA polymerase inhibitor | **IV only**, **LFT elevation**, **Renal impairment** (consider risk/benefit) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/remdesivir |
| **Paxlovid** (Nirmatrelvir/Ritonavir) | COVID-19 | Protease inhibitor | **Start within 5 days**, **CYP3A4 interactions** (ritonavir), **Dysgeusia** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/paxlovid |
| **Molnupiravir** | COVID-19 | Viral mutagenesis | **Start within 5 days**, **Not recommended in pregnancy**, **Bone/cartilage toxicity** (animal studies) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/molnupiravir |

## Antiretrovirals (ART)
| Class | Examples | Key Points | URL |
|-------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Integrase Strand Transfer Inhibitors (INSTI)** | Bictegravir, Dolutegravir, Raltegravir | **Preferred first-line**, Well tolerated, **Weight gain** (dolutegravir/bictegravir) | https://clinicalinfo.hiv.gov/en/guidelines |
| **Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTI)** | Tenofovir (TAF/TDF), Emtricitabine, Abacavir | **Backbone of ART**, **TDF**: Renal toxicity, Bone loss; **TAF**: Less renal/bone toxicity; **Abacavir**: **HLA-B*57:01 testing required** | https://clinicalinfo.hiv.gov/en/guidelines |
| **Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTI)** | Efavirenz, Rilpivirine, Doravirine | **Efavirenz**: CNS effects, **Teratogenic**; **Rilpivirine**: Take with meal, **Contraindicated with PPIs** | https://clinicalinfo.hiv.gov/en/guidelines |
| **Protease Inhibitors (PI)** | Darunavir, Atazanavir | **Boosted with ritonavir or cobicistat**, **CYP3A4 interactions**, **Hyperlipidemia** | https://clinicalinfo.hiv.gov/en/guidelines |

## Antifungals
| Drug | Spectrum | Key Points | URL |
|------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Fluconazole** | Candida, Cryptococcus | **Renal dosing**, **QTc prolongation**, **Hepatotoxicity** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/fluconazole |
| **Voriconazole** | Aspergillus, Candida, Fusarium | **Visual disturbances**, **Photosensitivity**, **Periostitis**, **CYP2C19 interactions**, **Therapeutic drug monitoring** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/voriconazole |
| **Posaconazole** | Aspergillus, Candida, Mucorales | **QTc prolongation**, **Hepatotoxicity**, **Take with fatty meal** (suspension) or **with food** (tablet) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/posaconazole |
| **Isavuconazole** | Aspergillus, Mucorales | **Shorter QTc** than other azoles, **Well tolerated** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/isavuconazole |
| **Echinocandins** | Caspofungin, Micafungin, Anidulafungin | **IV only**, **Well tolerated**, **No activity against Cryptococcus** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/echinocandins |
| **Amphotericin B** | Broad spectrum (azole-resistant Candida, Mucorales) | **Nephrotoxicity**, **Hypokalemia**, **Hypomagnesemia**, **Infusion reactions** (fever, rigors); **Liposomal formulation preferred** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/amphotericin-b |
