## Antipsychotics
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **First-Generation (Typical)** | Haloperidol, Chlorpromazine | **D2 antagonism** | **EPS** (dystonia, Akathisia, Parkinsonism, **Tardive dyskinesia**), **NMS** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/antipsychotics |
| **Second-Generation (Atypical)** | Risperidone, Olanzapine, Quetiapine, Aripiprazole, Clozapine | D2 + 5-HT2A antagonism | **Metabolic syndrome** (weight gain, Hyperglycemia, Dyslipidemia), **QTc prolongation** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/antipsychotics |
| **Clozapine** | Unique | D2 + 5-HT2A + multiple | **Agranulocytosis** (requires ANC monitoring), **Myocarditis**, **Seizures**, Sialorrhea | https://www.clozapinerems.com |
