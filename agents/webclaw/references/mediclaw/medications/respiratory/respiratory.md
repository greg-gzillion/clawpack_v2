# Medications - Respiratory

## Asthma and COPD Inhalers
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **SABA** | Albuterol, Levalbuterol | ß2 agonist (bronchodilation) | **Tachycardia**, **Tremor**, Hypokalemia | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/beta2-agonists |
| **LABA** | Salmeterol, Formoterol, Vilanterol | ß2 agonist (long-acting) | Same as SABA; **NOT for monotherapy in asthma** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/beta2-agonists |
| **SAMA** | Ipratropium | Muscarinic antagonist | Dry mouth, Urinary retention | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/anticholinergic-bronchodilators |
| **LAMA** | Tiotropium, Umeclidinium, Glycopyrrolate | Muscarinic antagonist (long-acting) | Dry mouth, Urinary retention | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/anticholinergic-bronchodilators |
| **ICS** | Fluticasone, Budesonide, Mometasone, Beclomethasone | Anti-inflammatory | **Oral thrush**, **Dysphonia**, Adrenal suppression (high dose) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/inhaled-corticosteroids |
| **ICS/LABA Combinations** | Fluticasone-Salmeterol, Budesonide-Formoterol, Mometasone-Formoterol | Anti-inflammatory + Bronchodilation | Same as components | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/inhaled-corticosteroids |
| **ICS/LABA/LAMA Combinations** | Fluticasone-Umeclidinium-Vilanterol, Budesonide-Glycopyrrolate-Formoterol | Triple therapy | Same as components | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/inhaled-corticosteroids |
| **Leukotriene Receptor Antagonists** | **Montelukast** | Blocks cysteinyl leukotriene receptors | **Neuropsychiatric events** (boxed warning), Churg-Strauss | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/montelukast |
| **PDE4 Inhibitor** | **Roflumilast** | ? Inflammation in COPD | **GI upset**, **Weight loss**, **Psychiatric effects** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/roflumilast |
| **Biologics** | Omalizumab (anti-IgE), Mepolizumab (anti-IL5), Benralizumab (anti-IL5R), Dupilumab (anti-IL4R), Tezepelumab (anti-TSLP) | Targeted immunomodulation | Injection site reactions, Anaphylaxis (omalizumab) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/asthma-biologics |

## Inhaler Device Types
| Device | Examples | Key Points | URL |
|--------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Metered Dose Inhaler (MDI)** | Albuterol HFA, Fluticasone HFA | **Requires coordination**, **Use spacer** for better delivery | https://www.lung.org/lung-health-diseases/lung-disease-lookup/copd/treating/devices |
| **Dry Powder Inhaler (DPI)** | Diskus, Ellipta, HandiHaler | **Rapid deep inhalation required**, **Do not exhale into device** | https://www.lung.org/lung-health-diseases/lung-disease-lookup/copd/treating/devices |
| **Soft Mist Inhaler (SMI)** | Respimat | Slow-moving mist, **Less coordination required** | https://www.lung.org/lung-health-diseases/lung-disease-lookup/copd/treating/devices |
| **Nebulizer** | Albuterol, Ipratropium, Budesonide | **No coordination required**, Time-consuming | https://www.lung.org/lung-health-diseases/lung-disease-lookup/copd/treating/devices |
