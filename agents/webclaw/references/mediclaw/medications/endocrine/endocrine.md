## Thyroid Medications
| Drug | Indication | Mechanism | Key Points | URL |
|------|------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Levothyroxine (T4)** | Hypothyroidism | Synthetic T4 | **Take on empty stomach**, **Separate from Ca/Fe by 4h**, Target TSH 0.5-2.5 | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/levothyroxine |
| **Liothyronine (T3)** | Hypothyroidism, Myxedema | Synthetic T3 | Rarely used alone; Short half-life | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/liothyronine |
| **Methimazole** | Hyperthyroidism | Inhibits thyroid peroxidase | **Agranulocytosis**, **Hepatotoxicity**, **Preferred in non-pregnant** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/methimazole |
| **PTU** | Hyperthyroidism (first trimester) | Inhibits thyroid peroxidase + T4?T3 conversion | **Hepatotoxicity** (severe), **Agranulocytosis**, **Preferred in first trimester** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ptu |

## Osteoporosis Medications
| Drug | Mechanism | Dosing | Key Points | URL |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Alendronate** | Bisphosphonate (inhibits osteoclasts) | 70 mg PO weekly | **Take on empty stomach**, **Remain upright x30 min**, **Esophagitis**, **ONJ**, **Atypical femur fracture** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/bisphosphonates |
| **Risedronate** | Bisphosphonate | 35 mg PO weekly or 150 mg monthly | Same as alendronate | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/bisphosphonates |
| **Zoledronic Acid** | Bisphosphonate | 5 mg IV yearly | **Acute phase reaction** (flu-like symptoms), **Hypocalcemia**, **Renal monitoring** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/bisphosphonates |
| **Denosumab** | RANKL inhibitor | 60 mg SC q6 months | **Hypocalcemia**, **ONJ**, **Atypical femur fracture**, **Rebound fractures** if stopped | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/denosumab |
| **Teriparatide** | PTH analog (anabolic) | 20 mcg SC daily x2 years | **Osteosarcoma risk** (black box), **Hypercalcemia** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/teriparatide |
| **Romosozumab** | Sclerostin inhibitor (anabolic) | 210 mg SC monthly x12 months | **? CV risk** (avoid in recent MI/stroke) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/romosozumab |
