# Medications - Adverse Effects

## Classification of Adverse Drug Reactions (ADRs)
| Type | Description | Mechanism | Example | URL |
|------|-------------|-----------|---------|-----|
| **Type A (Augmented)** | **Dose-dependent**, Predictable | Exaggerated pharmacology | Hypoglycemia from sulfonylureas | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-consumers-and-patients-drugs/finding-and-learning-about-side-effects-adverse-reactions |
| **Type B (Bizarre)** | **Dose-independent**, Unpredictable | Idiosyncratic, Immunologic | Anaphylaxis, SJS/TEN | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-consumers-and-patients-drugs/finding-and-learning-about-side-effects-adverse-reactions |
| **Type C (Chronic)** | Long-term use | Cumulative toxicity | Osteoporosis from corticosteroids | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-consumers-and-patients-drugs/finding-and-learning-about-side-effects-adverse-reactions |
| **Type D (Delayed)** | Delayed onset | Teratogenicity, Carcinogenicity | Vaginal adenocarcinoma (DES) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-consumers-and-patients-drugs/finding-and-learning-about-side-effects-adverse-reactions |
| **Type E (End of use)** | Withdrawal | Rebound physiology | Opioid withdrawal | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-consumers-and-patients-drugs/finding-and-learning-about-side-effects-adverse-reactions |

## Severe Cutaneous Adverse Reactions (SCARs)
| Syndrome | Features | Latency | Culprit Drugs | URL |
|----------|----------|---------|---------------|-----|
| **DRESS** | Fever, Eosinophilia, Organ involvement | 2-8 weeks | Anticonvulsants, Allopurinol, Sulfonamides | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK430878/ |
| **SJS/TEN** | Skin detachment (SJS <10%, TEN >30%) | 1-4 weeks | Anticonvulsants, Allopurinol, Sulfonamides, NSAIDs | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK430878/ |
| **AGEP** | Pustular eruption, Fever | 1-5 days | Aminopenicillins, Diltiazem, Hydroxychloroquine | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK430878/ |

## Drug-Induced Liver Injury (DILI)
| Pattern | Lab Findings | Common Drugs | URL |
|---------|--------------|--------------|-----|
| **Hepatocellular** | **ALT >3x ULN**, ALT/ALP ratio =5 | Acetaminophen, Isoniazid, Valproate, Statins | https://livertox.nih.gov |
| **Cholestatic** | **ALP >2x ULN**, ALT/ALP ratio =2 | Amoxicillin-clavulanate, Estrogens, Anabolic steroids | https://livertox.nih.gov |
| **Mixed** | ALT >3x ULN, ALP >2x ULN, Ratio 2-5 | Phenytoin, Carbamazepine, Sulfonamides | https://livertox.nih.gov |
