# Medications - Pediatric Dosing

## Weight-Based Dosing Principles
| Principle | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **mg/kg** | Standard pediatric dosing method | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/medication-safety/ |
| **BSA (m˛)** | Used for chemotherapy, Some antimicrobials | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/medication-safety/ |
| **Maximum Dose** | **Never exceed adult maximum dose** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/medication-safety/ |
| **Mosteller BSA Formula** | v[(Height(cm) × Weight(kg)) / 3600] | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/medication-safety/ |

## Common Pediatric Medications
| Drug | Indication | Dose | URL |
|------|------------|------|-----|
| **Acetaminophen** | Pain/Fever | 10-15 mg/kg q4-6h (max 75 mg/kg/day or 4g/day) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/acetaminophen |
| **Ibuprofen** | Pain/Fever | 5-10 mg/kg q6-8h (max 40 mg/kg/day) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ibuprofen |
| **Amoxicillin** | AOM, Sinusitis, Pneumonia | 80-90 mg/kg/day divided BID | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/amoxicillin |
| **Amoxicillin-Clavulanate** | AOM (treatment failure), Sinusitis | 80-90 mg/kg/day (amoxicillin component) divided BID | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/amoxicillin-clavulanate |
| **Ceftriaxone** | Serious infections | 50-100 mg/kg/day divided q12-24h | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ceftriaxone |
| **Azithromycin** | CAP, Pertussis | 10 mg/kg day 1, then 5 mg/kg days 2-5 | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/azithromycin |
| **Ondansetron** | Nausea/Vomiting | 0.15 mg/kg q8h (max 8 mg/dose) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ondansetron |
| **Albuterol** | Bronchospasm | 2.5-5 mg nebulized q4-6h | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/albuterol |
| **Prednisone/Prednisolone** | Asthma exacerbation | 1-2 mg/kg/day x3-5 days | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/prednisone |

## Pediatric Drug Safety Concerns
| Drug | Concern | Age Group | URL |
|------|---------|-----------|-----|
| **Aspirin** | **Reye syndrome** | Children with viral illness | https://www.cdc.gov/reyes-syndrome/index.html |
| **Codeine/Tramadol** | **Respiratory depression** in CYP2D6 ultrarapid metabolizers | **Contraindicated <12 years** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/codeine |
| **Tetracyclines** | Tooth discoloration, Bone growth inhibition | **<8 years** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/tetracyclines |
| **Ceftriaxone** | **Biliary sludging** | Neonates (especially with calcium) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ceftriaxone |
| **Promethazine** | **Respiratory depression** | **Contraindicated <2 years** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/promethazine |
| **OTC Cough/Cold** | Ineffectiveness, Adverse effects | **<4 years** | https://www.fda.gov/consumers/consumer-updates/when-give-kids-medicine-coughs-and-colds |
| **Benzocaine** | **Methemoglobinemia** | <2 years | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/benzocaine |
