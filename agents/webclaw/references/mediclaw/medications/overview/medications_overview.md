# Medications - Overview

## What is Clinical Pharmacology?
Clinical pharmacology is the scientific discipline that involves all aspects of the relationship between drugs and humans. It encompasses pharmacokinetics, pharmacodynamics, therapeutics, and toxicology.

| Core Concept | Definition | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Pharmacokinetics (PK)** | What the body does to the drug (Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, Excretion) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/pharmacokinetics-drug-development |
| **Pharmacodynamics (PD)** | What the drug does to the body (Mechanism of action, Receptor interactions) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/pharmacodynamics-drug-development |
| **Bioavailability (F)** | Fraction of administered drug reaching systemic circulation | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/pharmacokinetics-drug-development |
| **Volume of Distribution (Vd)** | Theoretical volume drug occupies in body | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/pharmacokinetics-drug-development |
| **Clearance (CL)** | Volume of plasma cleared of drug per unit time | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/pharmacokinetics-drug-development |
| **Half-Life (t˝)** | Time for drug concentration to decrease by 50% | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/pharmacokinetics-drug-development |
| **Steady State** | Rate of administration = Rate of elimination (achieved after 4-5 half-lives) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/pharmacokinetics-drug-development |
| **Therapeutic Index** | Ratio of toxic dose to effective dose (TD50/ED50) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-drug-class/narrow-therapeutic-index-drugs |
