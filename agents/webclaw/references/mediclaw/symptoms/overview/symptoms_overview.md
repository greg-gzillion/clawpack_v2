# Symptoms - Overview

## What is a Symptom?
A symptom is a subjective experience reported by the patient that may indicate an underlying disease or condition. Unlike signs (objective findings observed by the clinician), symptoms are what the patient feels and describes.

| Core Concept | Description | URL |
|--------------|-------------|-----|

| **Sign** | **Objective** finding (fever, rash, edema) | https://medlineplus.gov/symptoms.html |
