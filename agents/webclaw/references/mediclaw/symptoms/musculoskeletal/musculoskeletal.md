|-----------|-------------|-----|

| **Oligoarthritis (2-4 joints)** | **Reactive arthritis**, **Psoriatic arthritis**, **Ankylosing spondylitis**, **Gout** | https://www.merckmanuals.com/professional/musculoskeletal-and-connective-tissue-disorders/symptoms-of-musculoskeletal-disorders/joint-pain-multiple-joints |

## Back Pain
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Pain in lumbar, thoracic, or cervical spine** | https://www.mayoclinic.org/symptoms/back-pain/basics/definition/sym-20050878 |
| **Acute (<6 weeks)** | **Muscle strain** (most common), **Disc herniation**, **Compression fracture**, **Osteoarthritis** | https://www.merckmanuals.com/professional/musculoskeletal-and-connective-tissue-disorders/neck-and-back-pain/low-back-pain |
| **Chronic (=12 weeks)** | **Degenerative disc disease**, **Spinal stenosis**, **Spondylolisthesis**, **Facet arthropathy**, **SI joint dysfunction** | https://www.merckmanuals.com/professional/musculoskeletal-and-connective-tissue-disorders/neck-and-back-pain/low-back-pain |
| **Red Flags** | **Trauma**, **Fever**, **Unexplained weight loss**, **Neurologic deficits**, **Saddle anesthesia**, **Bowel/bladder dysfunction**, **History of cancer**, **IV drug use**, **Steroid use**, **Age >50** | https://www.mayoclinic.org/symptoms/back-pain/basics/when-to-see-doctor/sym-20050878 |
