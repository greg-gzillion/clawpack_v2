## PSA Interpretation
| PSA Level | Interpretation | Next Steps | URL |
|-----------|----------------|------------|-----|
| **<2.5 ng/mL** | Low risk | Routine screening interval | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/prostate-cancer |
| **2.5-4.0 ng/mL** | Intermediate risk | Consider repeat, PSA velocity, Risk calculators | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/prostate-cancer |

| **PSA Velocity** | >0.35 ng/mL/year | Increased risk | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/prostate-cancer |

## BPH Treatment
| Treatment | Examples | Indications | Adverse Effects | URL |
|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|-----|
| **Watchful Waiting** | Lifestyle modifications | Mild symptoms | None | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bph |
| **Alpha-Blockers** | **Tamsulosin**, Alfuzosin | Moderate-severe symptoms | **Retrograde ejaculation**, Orthostasis | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bph |
| **5-ARI** | **Finasteride**, Dutasteride | **Prostate >30g**, ? PSA by 50% | ? Libido, ED, Gynecomastia | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bph |
| **PDE5 Inhibitor** | **Tadalafil 5 mg daily** | BPH + ED | Headache, Dyspepsia | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bph |
| **Combination** | Alpha-blocker + 5-ARI | Large prostate, Severe symptoms | Combined adverse effects | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/bph |
