# Family Medicine - Substance Use Disorders

## Alcohol Use Disorder (AUD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Screening** | **Single question**: "How many times in the past year have you had 5+ (men) or 4+ (women) drinks in a day?"; **AUDIT-C** | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |
| **Unhealthy Use** | Men: >14 drinks/week or >4/day; Women: >7 drinks/week or >3/day | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |
| **DSM-5 AUD** | =2 criteria within 12 months | https://www.psychiatry.org/psychiatrists/practice/dsm |

## Medications for Alcohol Use Disorder
| Medication | Dose | Mechanism | Considerations | URL |
|------------|------|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Naltrexone** | 50 mg daily (PO) or 380 mg monthly (IM) | Opioid antagonist, ? Craving | **First-line**; Requires abstinence from opioids | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Acamprosate** | 666 mg TID | Glutamate modulator | **First-line**; Safe in liver disease; Requires renal dosing | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Disulfiram** | 250-500 mg daily | Inhibits ALDH ? Aversive reaction | Requires commitment; **Contraindicated in severe CAD** | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |

## Medications for Opioid Use Disorder (MOUD)
| Medication | Setting | Dose | Considerations | URL |
|------------|---------|------|----------------|-----|
| **Buprenorphine** | **Office-based** (waivered) | 8-24 mg SL daily | **First-line**; Precipitates withdrawal if given too soon | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Methadone** | **OTP only** | 80-120 mg daily | Most effective for retention; QTc monitoring | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
| **Naltrexone** | Office-based | 380 mg IM monthly | Requires **7-14 days opioid-free**; Not first-line | https://www.samhsa.gov/medications-substance-use-disorders |
