|-----------|--------------|-----|

| **Physical Exam** | **Cardiac** (murmurs, Marfan stigmata), **Musculoskeletal**, Neurologic | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2021/0501/p539.html |
| **Clearance** | Cleared, Cleared with limitations, Not cleared | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2021/0501/p539.html |

## Concussion Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Acute Assessment** | **SCAT5** (Sport Concussion Assessment Tool) | https://www.cdc.gov/headsup/basics/concussion_recovery.html |
| **Red Flags** | Loss of consciousness, Seizure, Focal neurologic deficit, Worsening symptoms | https://www.cdc.gov/headsup/basics/concussion_recovery.html |
| **Return to Learn** | Gradual return to cognitive activities before physical | https://www.cdc.gov/headsup/basics/concussion_recovery.html |
| **Return to Play** | **Stepwise progression**: Rest ? Light aerobic ? Sport-specific ? Non-contact drills ? Full contact ? Game play | https://www.cdc.gov/headsup/basics/concussion_recovery.html |
| **Timeline** | Most resolve in 7-10 days (adults), Up to 4 weeks (adolescents) | https://www.cdc.gov/headsup/basics/concussion_recovery.html |

## Common Sports Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|--------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Ankle Sprain** | Inversion injury | **RICE**, Functional rehab, **Ottawa Ankle Rules** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/sprained-ankle/ |
| **ACL Tear** | Non-contact pivoting, "Pop" | MRI, Surgical reconstruction (young/active) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/anterior-cruciate-ligament-acl-injuries/ |
| **Meniscal Tear** | Twisting on planted foot | MRI, Conservative vs Arthroscopy | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/meniscus-tears/ |

| **Medial Tibial Stress Syndrome (Shin Splints)** | Overuse, Running on hard surfaces | **Rest**, Activity modification, PT | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shin-splints/ |

## Ottawa Ankle Rules
| Rule | Indication for X-Ray | URL |
|------|---------------------|-----|
| **Pain in malleolar zone** AND **Bone tenderness at posterior edge of distal 6 cm of lateral malleolus** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in malleolar zone** AND **Bone tenderness at posterior edge of distal 6 cm of medial malleolus** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in malleolar zone** AND **Inability to bear weight immediately and in ED (4 steps)** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in midfoot zone** AND **Bone tenderness at base of 5th metatarsal** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
| **Pain in midfoot zone** AND **Bone tenderness at navicular** | **X-ray indicated** | https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/1055/ottawa-ankle-rule |
