# Family Medicine - Overview

## What is Family Medicine?
Family medicine is the medical specialty that provides continuing, comprehensive health care for the individual and family across all ages, genders, diseases, and parts of the body.

| Core Principle | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Continuity of Care** | Long-term patient-physician relationship | https://www.aafp.org/about/policies/all/family-medicine-definition.html |
| **Comprehensive Care** | Care for all ages, genders, organ systems | https://www.aafp.org/about/policies/all/family-medicine-definition.html |
| **Coordinated Care** | Integration of specialty care and community resources | https://www.aafp.org/about/policies/all/family-medicine-definition.html |
| **Community Context** | Understanding patient in context of family and community | https://www.aafp.org/about/policies/all/family-medicine-definition.html |
