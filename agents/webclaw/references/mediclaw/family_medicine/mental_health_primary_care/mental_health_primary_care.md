# Family Medicine - Mental Health in Primary Care

## Depression Screening and Diagnosis
| Tool | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **PHQ-2** | **2-question screen**: "Little interest/pleasure" + "Feeling down/hopeless" | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/depression-in-adults-screening |
| **PHQ-9** | **9-question** diagnostic and severity tool | https://www.phqscreeners.com |
| **PHQ-9 Scoring** | 0-4: None-Minimal; 5-9: Mild; 10-14: Moderate; 15-19: Moderately Severe; 20-27: Severe | https://www.phqscreeners.com |
