## Common Symptoms at End of Life
| Symptom | Management | URL |
|---------|------------|-----|
| **Dyspnea** | **Opioids** (morphine 2.5-5 mg PO q4h PRN), Fan, Oxygen (if hypoxic) | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2020/0401/p427.html |
| **Nausea/Vomiting** | Haloperidol, Metoclopramide, Ondansetron, Dexamethasone | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2020/0401/p427.html |
| **Constipation** | **Stimulant laxative + Stool softener** (senna + docusate) | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2020/0401/p427.html |
| **Delirium** | **Haloperidol** (0.5-2 mg PO/SC q4-6h), Treat underlying cause | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2020/0401/p427.html |
| **Secretions (Death Rattle)** | **Glycopyrrolate**, Scopolamine, Atropine | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2020/0401/p427.html |
| **Anxiety** | **Lorazepam** 0.5-1 mg SL/PO q4-6h PRN | https://www.aafp.org/pubs/afp/issues/2020/0401/p427.html |

## Hospice Eligibility
| Condition | General Criteria | URL |
|-----------|------------------|-----|
| **Cancer** | Metastatic or progressive despite treatment | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **Dementia** | FAST stage 7C (unable to ambulate, bathe, dress) + complication (aspiration, UTI, sepsis) | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **Heart Failure** | NYHA Class IV, Optimal therapy, EF =20% | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **COPD** | Disabling dyspnea at rest, FEV1 <30%, Recurrent hospitalizations | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **General** | **Prognosis =6 months** if disease follows normal course | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
