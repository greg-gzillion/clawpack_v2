## Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) Management
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **BP Goal** | **<130/80** (if albuminuria) | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/ckd-evaluation-and-management/ |
| **RAAS Blockade** | **ACEi or ARB** if albuminuria (ACR =30 mg/g) | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/ckd-evaluation-and-management/ |
| **SGLT2 Inhibitor** | **Recommended** for CKD with eGFR =20 and ACR =200 mg/g | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/ckd-evaluation-and-management/ |
| **Statin** | Recommended for age =50; Consider for age 18-49 | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/ckd-evaluation-and-management/ |
| **Anemia** | Target Hb 10-11.5 g/dL (avoid >13) | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/anemia-in-ckd/ |
| **Nephrology Referral** | eGFR <30, Rapid decline, Persistent albuminuria, Uncontrolled HTN | https://kdigo.org/guidelines/ckd-evaluation-and-management/ |
