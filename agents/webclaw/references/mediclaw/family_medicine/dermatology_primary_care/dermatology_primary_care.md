# Family Medicine - Dermatology in Primary Care

## Common Rashes
| Condition | Key Features | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Atopic Dermatitis** | **Pruritic**, **Flexural**, Xerosis, Lichenification | Emollients, **Topical corticosteroids**, Topical calcineurin inhibitors | https://nationaleczema.org/eczema/treatment/ |
| **Psoriasis** | **Well-demarcated plaques with silvery scale**, Extensor surfaces | Topical corticosteroids, Vitamin D analogs, Phototherapy | https://www.psoriasis.org |
| **Seborrheic Dermatitis** | **Greasy scale**, Nasolabial folds, Scalp, Eyebrows | **Ketoconazole** cream/shampoo, Low-potency topical CS | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/seborrheic-dermatitis-overview |
| **Contact Dermatitis** | **Geometric pattern**, Pruritus, Vesicles | Avoid trigger, **Topical corticosteroids** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/contact-dermatitis |
| **Pityriasis Rosea** | **Herald patch** ? Secondary eruption in "Christmas tree" pattern | Supportive (self-limited) | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/pityriasis-rosea-overview |
| **Tinea** | **Annular plaque with central clearing**, Scale | **Topical antifungals** (limited), **Oral terbinafine** (extensive) | https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/index.html |
| **Scabies** | **Intense pruritus**, Burrows (interdigital, wrists), Nocturnal | **Permethrin 5% cream** | https://www.cdc.gov/parasites/scabies/index.html |

## Acne Vulgaris
| Severity | Lesions | Treatment | URL |
|----------|---------|-----------|-----|
| **Mild** | Comedones, Few papules | **Topical retinoid** + **Benzoyl peroxide** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/acne |
| **Moderate** | Papules, Pustules | Topical retinoid + Benzoyl peroxide + **Topical antibiotic** OR **Oral antibiotic** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/acne |
| **Severe** | Nodules, Cysts, Scarring | **Isotretinoin** (refer to dermatology) | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/acne |

## Skin Cancer Screening
| Lesion | Characteristics | Management | URL |
|--------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Basal Cell Carcinoma** | **Pearly papule**, Telangiectasias, Rolled borders | **Excision** or Mohs | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/skin-cancer/types/common/basal-cell-carcinoma |
| **Squamous Cell Carcinoma** | **Hyperkeratotic**, Crusted, Ulcerated | **Excision** or Mohs | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/skin-cancer/types/common/squamous-cell-carcinoma |
| **Melanoma** | **ABCDE**: Asymmetry, Border, Color, Diameter >6mm, Evolving | **Urgent dermatology referral** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/skin-cancer/types/common/melanoma |
| **Actinic Keratosis** | Rough, Scaly papule on sun-exposed skin | **Cryotherapy**, Topical 5-FU, Imiquimod | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/skin-cancer/actinic-keratosis |
