# Family Medicine - Preventive Care

## USPSTF Grade Definitions
| Grade | Definition | Implication for Practice | URL |
|-------|------------|-------------------------|-----|
| **A** | High certainty of substantial net benefit | **Offer or provide this service** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **B** | High certainty of moderate net benefit OR moderate certainty of substantial net benefit | **Offer or provide this service** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **C** | Moderate certainty of small net benefit | **Offer selectively** based on professional judgment and patient preferences | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **D** | Moderate or high certainty of no net benefit or harms outweigh benefits | **Discourage use** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **I** | Insufficient evidence | **Clinical judgment**; Read clinical considerations | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |

| Cancer | Test | Population | Frequency | Grade | URL |
|--------|------|------------|-----------|-------|-----|

| **Prostate** | PSA | Men 55-69 | Individualized | C | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/prostate-cancer-screening |
