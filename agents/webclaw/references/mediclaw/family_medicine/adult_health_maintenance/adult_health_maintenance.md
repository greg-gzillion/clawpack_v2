## Lifestyle Counseling - 5 A's Framework
| Step | Description | Example | URL |
|------|-------------|---------|-----|
| **Ask** | Identify risk behaviors | "Do you smoke?" | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Advise** | Give clear, personalized advice | "Quitting is the best thing for your health" | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Assess** | Evaluate readiness to change | "Are you ready to quit in the next 30 days?" | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Assist** | Provide resources and support | Prescribe pharmacotherapy, Refer to counseling | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Arrange** | Schedule follow-up | Follow-up call or visit in 1-2 weeks | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |

## Tobacco Cessation
| Pharmacotherapy | Dose | Notes | URL |
|-----------------|------|-------|-----|
| **Nicotine Replacement (Patch)** | 21 mg/day (if >10 cig/day) | Start on quit date | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Nicotine Gum/Lozenge** | 2-4 mg q1-2h PRN | Chew and park | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Varenicline (Chantix)** | 0.5 mg daily x3d, then 0.5 mg BID x4d, then 1 mg BID | Start 1 week before quit date | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Bupropion SR** | 150 mg daily x3d, then 150 mg BID | Start 1-2 weeks before quit date | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Combination NRT** | Patch + PRN gum/lozenge | More effective than single NRT | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
