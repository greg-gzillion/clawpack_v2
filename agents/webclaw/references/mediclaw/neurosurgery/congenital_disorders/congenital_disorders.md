# Neurosurgery - Congenital Disorders

## Neural Tube Defects
| Defect | Definition | Treatment | URL |
|--------|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Anencephaly** | **Absence of forebrain and skull** | **Lethal** (supportive care) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/birthdefects/anencephaly.html |
| **Encephalocele** | **Herniation of brain/meninges** through skull defect | **Surgical closure** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/birthdefects/encephalocele.html |
| **Myelomeningocele** | **Exposed neural placode** through spinal defect | **Surgical closure** (prenatal or postnatal) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/spinabifida/index.html |
| **Meningocele** | **Herniation of meninges only** (no neural tissue) | **Surgical closure** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/spinabifida/index.html |
| **Spina Bifida Occulta** | **Bony defect only** (skin covered) | **None** (usually asymptomatic) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/spinabifida/index.html |

|-----------------|------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Sagittal** | **Scaphocephaly** (long, narrow) | Usually isolated | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Craniosynostosis |
| **Coronal (Bilateral)** | **Brachycephaly** (short, wide) | **Apert**, **Crouzon**, **Pfeiffer**, **Muenke** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Craniosynostosis |
| **Coronal (Unilateral)** | **Anterior plagiocephaly** (flat forehead) | Usually isolated | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Craniosynostosis |

| **Lambdoid** | **Posterior plagiocephaly** (flat occiput) | Usually isolated; **Differentiate from positional plagiocephaly** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Craniosynostosis |
| **Multiple** | **Turricephaly** (tower skull), **Cloverleaf skull** | **Severe syndromic** (Apert, Crouzon) | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Craniosynostosis |

## Dandy-Walker Malformation
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Triad** | **Cystic dilation of fourth ventricle**, **Cerebellar vermis hypoplasia**, **Elevated torcula** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/dandy-walker-syndrome |
| **Associated Conditions** | **Hydrocephalus** (70-90%), **Corpus callosum agenesis**, **Cardiac anomalies** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/dandy-walker-syndrome |
| **Treatment** | **VP shunt** (cystoperitoneal or ventriculoperitoneal) if hydrocephalus; **Endoscopic third ventriculostomy** (controversial) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/dandy-walker-syndrome |

## Vein of Galen Malformation
