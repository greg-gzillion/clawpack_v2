## Intracranial Hemorrhage Types
| Type | Location | Typical Source | CT Appearance | URL |
|------|----------|----------------|---------------|-----|
| **Epidural Hematoma** | Between skull and dura | **Middle meningeal artery** (often temporal fracture) | **Biconvex (lentiform)** , Does not cross suture lines | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Subdural Hematoma** | Between dura and arachnoid | **Bridging veins** | **Crescentic**, Crosses suture lines | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |

| **Intraventricular Hemorrhage** | Ventricles | Extension from parenchyma, Trauma | Hyperdensity in ventricles | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |

## Epidural vs Subdural Hematoma
| Feature | Epidural | Subdural | URL |
|---------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Shape** | **Biconvex (lentiform)** | **Crescentic** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Crosses Sutures** | **No** | **Yes** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Source** | **Arterial** (middle meningeal) | **Venous** (bridging veins) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Lucid Interval** | **Classic** (hours) | Less common | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Prognosis** | **Good** if evacuated promptly | Worse (often underlying brain injury) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **CT Density Over Time** | Acute: Hyperdense; Chronic: Hypodense | Acute: Hyperdense; Subacute: Isodense; Chronic: Hypodense | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
