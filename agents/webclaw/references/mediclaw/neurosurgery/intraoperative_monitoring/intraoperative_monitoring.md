## IONM Alert Criteria
| Modality | Alert Criteria | Action | URL |
|----------|----------------|--------|-----|
| **SSEP** | **>50% amplitude reduction** OR **>10% latency increase** | **Stop manipulation**, **? MAP**, **Check anesthetic**, **Wake-up test** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Intraoperative-Neurophysiological-Monitoring |
| **MEP** | **>50-80% amplitude reduction** OR **Acute loss** | **Stop manipulation**, **? MAP**, **Check anesthetic**, **Wake-up test** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Intraoperative-Neurophysiological-Monitoring |
| **EMG** | **Sustained neurotonic discharges** or **Train activity** | **Stop manipulation**, **Irrigate**, **Reposition retractors** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Intraoperative-Neurophysiological-Monitoring |
| **BAER** | **>1 msec Wave V latency** OR **>50% amplitude reduction** | **Stop manipulation**, **Reposition retractors** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Intraoperative-Neurophysiological-Monitoring |
