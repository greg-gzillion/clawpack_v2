|-----------|-------|---------------------|-------------------|-----|

| **Tarsal Tunnel Syndrome** | **Posterior tibial** | **Tarsal tunnel** (medial ankle) | **Burning pain** in sole, Worse with activity | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Tarsal-Tunnel-Syndrome |

|-------|-----------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Schwannoma** | **Encapsulated**, **Antoni A/B**, **Verocay bodies** | **NF2** (bilateral vestibular), Sporadic | **Gross total resection** (nerve-sparing) | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Peripheral-Nerve-Tumors |
| **Neurofibroma** | **Non-encapsulated**, **Infiltrates nerve fascicles** | **NF1** (multiple) | Observation, Resection if symptomatic (may require nerve graft) | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Peripheral-Nerve-Tumors |

## Peripheral Nerve Injury Classification (Seddon and Sunderland)
| Seddon | Sunderland | Pathology | Recovery | URL |
|--------|------------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Neuropraxia** | **Grade I** | **Segmental demyelination**; Axon and connective tissue intact | **Complete** (days-weeks) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Axonotmesis** | **Grade II** | **Axon disruption**; Endoneurium intact | **Good** (months, 1mm/day) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| | **Grade III** | Axon + **Endoneurium disruption**; Perineurium intact | **Partial** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| | **Grade IV** | Axon + Endoneurium + **Perineurium disruption**; Epineurium intact | **Poor** (requires surgery) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Neurotmesis** | **Grade V** | **Complete nerve transection** | **None** (requires surgery) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
