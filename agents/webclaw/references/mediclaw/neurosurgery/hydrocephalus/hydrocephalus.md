## Normal Pressure Hydrocephalus (NPH)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Classic Triad (Hakim-Adams)** | **Gait disturbance** (magnetic, shuffling), **Cognitive impairment**, **Urinary incontinence** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Normal-Pressure-Hydrocephalus |
| **Imaging** | **Ventriculomegaly** (Evans index >0.3), **DESH** (disproportionately enlarged subarachnoid space hydrocephalus), **Tight high convexity** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Normal-Pressure-Hydrocephalus |
| **Diagnostic Test** | **High-volume lumbar puncture** (30-50 mL CSF removal) ? Assess gait improvement | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Normal-Pressure-Hydrocephalus |
| **Treatment** | **Ventriculoperitoneal shunt** (programmable valve preferred) | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Normal-Pressure-Hydrocephalus |

## Shunt Complications
| Complication | Presentation | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Obstruction** | **Headache**, Nausea/Vomiting, **Papilledema**, **Enlarged ventricles** | **Shunt revision** (proximal > distal > valve) | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Hydrocephalus-Shunt-Complications |
| **Infection** | **Fever**, **Erythema/tenderness** along shunt tract, **Meningismus** | **Shunt externalization or removal + EVD + Antibiotics** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Hydrocephalus-Shunt-Complications |
| **Overdrainage** | **Orthostatic headache**, **Subdural hematoma**, **Slit ventricles** | **Increase valve setting** (if programmable), **Anti-siphon device** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Hydrocephalus-Shunt-Complications |
| **Underdrainage** | Persistent hydrocephalus symptoms | **Decrease valve setting** or **Revise shunt** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Hydrocephalus-Shunt-Complications |
| **Abdominal Complications** | Pseudocyst, Ascites, Perforation, Inguinal hernia | **Reposition distal catheter** | https://www.aans.org/Patients/Neurosurgical-Conditions-and-Treatments/Hydrocephalus-Shunt-Complications |
