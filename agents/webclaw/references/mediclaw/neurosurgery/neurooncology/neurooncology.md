|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Meningioma
| Grade | Histology | Recurrence Risk | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Grade 1** | Meningothelial, Fibrous, Transitional, Psammomatous | **7-25%** at 5 years | **Gross total resection** (Simpson Grade I-II), Observation or SRS for small/asymptomatic | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1426 |
| **Grade 2 (Atypical)** | Mitoses 4-19/10 HPF OR Brain invasion OR =3 atypical features | **29-52%** at 5 years | **Gross total resection + Adjuvant radiation** (controversial for Simpson I) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1426 |
| **Grade 3 (Anaplastic)** | Mitoses =20/10 HPF OR Frank anaplasia | **50-94%** at 5 years | **Gross total resection + Adjuvant radiation** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1426 |

## Simpson Grading of Meningioma Resection
| Grade | Definition | Recurrence Rate (10-year) | URL |
|-------|------------|--------------------------|-----|
| **I** | **Macroscopic complete removal** of tumor, Dural attachment, and abnormal bone | **9%** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **II** | Macroscopic complete removal + **Coagulation of dural attachment** | **19%** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **III** | Macroscopic complete removal **without resection/coagulation of dural attachment** or extradural extensions | **29%** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
