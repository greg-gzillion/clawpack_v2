## ICP Monitoring
| Method | Location | Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|--------|----------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **External Ventricular Drain (EVD)** | Lateral ventricle | **Gold standard**, Therapeutic CSF drainage | Infection risk (5-10%), Hemorrhage | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Intraparenchymal Monitor** | Brain parenchyma | Lower infection risk, Easier placement | **No CSF drainage**, Drift over time | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Subdural/Subarachnoid Bolt** | Subdural/subarachnoid space | Less invasive | Less accurate, No CSF drainage | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |

## ICP Waveforms
| Wave | Description | Significance | URL |
|------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **P1 (Percussion)** | Arterial pulsation | Taller than P2 normally | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **P2 (Tidal)** | Intracranial compliance | **P2 > P1 = Decreased compliance** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **P3 (Dicrotic)** | Aortic valve closure | Variable | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Lundberg A Waves (Plateau)** | Sustained ICP 50-100 mmHg for 5-20 min | **Impending herniation** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Lundberg B Waves** | ICP oscillations 10-20 mmHg q30s-2min | Decreased compliance, May predict ICP crisis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |
| **Lundberg C Waves** | ICP oscillations 4-8/min | Related to respiratory/cardiac cycles | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537247/ |

## ICP Management Tiered Approach (Brain Trauma Foundation)
| Tier | Interventions | URL |
|------|---------------|-----|
| **Tier Zero** | **Head of bed 30ｰ**, Neck midline, Avoid jugular compression, Maintain euvolemia, Normothermia, Normocapnia (PaCO2 35-38), Sedation/Analgesia | https://www.braintrauma.org/guidelines |
| **Tier One** | **CSF drainage** (if EVD), **Hyperosmolar therapy** (Mannitol 0.25-1 g/kg or Hypertonic saline), **Neuromuscular blockade** | https://www.braintrauma.org/guidelines |
| **Tier Two** | **Barbiturate coma** (Pentobarbital), **Decompressive craniectomy**, **Hypothermia** (32-35ｰC), **Hyperventilation** (PaCO2 30-35, with monitoring) | https://www.braintrauma.org/guidelines |
