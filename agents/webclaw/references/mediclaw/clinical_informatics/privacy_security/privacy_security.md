## HIPAA Breach Notification
| Component | Requirement | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Impermissible use or disclosure** compromising security/privacy of PHI | https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/breach-notification/index.html |
| **Breach Exceptions** | **Unintentional access by workforce**, **Inadvertent disclosure**, **Good faith belief unauthorized person would not retain** | https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/breach-notification/index.html |
| **Individual Notification** | **Within 60 days** of discovery | https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/breach-notification/index.html |
| **HHS Notification** | **<500 individuals**: Annually; **=500 individuals**: Within 60 days + Media | https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/breach-notification/index.html |
