# Clinical Informatics - Overview

## What is Clinical Informatics?
Clinical informatics is the scientific discipline concerned with the acquisition, storage, retrieval, and use of information in healthcare. It applies information science and technology to improve patient care, enhance population health, and advance medical knowledge.
