## Alert Fatigue
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Desensitization to alerts** due to excessive or irrelevant notifications | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/safety/clinical-decision-support |
| **Causes** | **High volume**, **Low specificity**, **Poor timing**, **Interruptive design** | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/safety/clinical-decision-support |
| **Mitigation** | **Tiered alerts** (severity levels), **Non-interruptive**, **Reduce low-value alerts**, **Audit and feedback** | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/safety/clinical-decision-support |
