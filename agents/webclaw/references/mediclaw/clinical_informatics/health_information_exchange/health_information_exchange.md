# Clinical Informatics - Health Information Exchange (HIE)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Electronic movement of health information** among organizations according to national standards | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/health-it-and-health-information-exchange-basics/what-hie |
| **Benefits** | **Improved care coordination**, **Reduced duplicate testing**, **Enhanced patient safety** | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/health-it-and-health-information-exchange-basics/benefits-hie |

## HIE Architectures
| Architecture | Description | Pros | Cons | URL |
|-------------|-------------|------|------|-----|
| **Centralized** | **All data stored in central repository** | **Single source of truth**, **Simpler query** | **Single point of failure**, **Privacy concerns** | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/health-it-and-health-information-exchange-basics/hie-architectures |
| **Federated (Distributed)** | **Data remains at source**, **Queried when needed** | **Data control remains local**, **Scalable** | **Complex query**, **Performance variability** | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/health-it-and-health-information-exchange-basics/hie-architectures |
| **Hybrid** | **Combination of centralized and federated** | **Flexible** | **Complex governance** | https://www.healthit.gov/topic/health-it-and-health-information-exchange-basics/hie-architectures |
