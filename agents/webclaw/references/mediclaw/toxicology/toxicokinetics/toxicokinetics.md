# Toxicology - Toxicokinetics

## Overview
Toxicokinetics is the study of **what the body does to a toxicant** (absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion).

| Process | Definition | Clinical Significance | URL |
|---------|------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Absorption** | **Entry into systemic circulation** | **Determines onset and peak toxicity** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Distribution** | **Dispersion throughout body** | **Volume of distribution (Vd)** - high Vd = extensive tissue binding | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Metabolism** | **Biotransformation** (Phase I and II) | **Activation** (prodrug ? active) or **Detoxification** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Excretion** | **Elimination** from body | **Renal**, **Hepatic/biliary**, **Pulmonary** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |

## Key Toxicokinetic Parameters
| Parameter | Definition | Clinical Significance | URL |
|-----------|------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Bioavailability (F)** | **Fraction reaching systemic circulation** | **IV = 100%**; Oral variable | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Volume of Distribution (Vd)** | **Vd = Dose / C0** | **High Vd** (>1-2 L/kg) = **Extensive tissue binding**, **Not easily removed by dialysis** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Clearance (CL)** | **Volume cleared per unit time** | **Determines elimination rate** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Half-Life (t˝)** | **Time for concentration to decrease by 50%** | **4-5 half-lives to reach steady state or elimination** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |

## Zero-Order vs First-Order Kinetics
| Kinetics | Characteristics | Examples | Clinical Significance | URL |
|----------|-----------------|----------|----------------------|-----|
| **First-Order** | **Constant fraction** eliminated per time | **Most drugs** | **Predictable** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Zero-Order** | **Constant amount** eliminated per time (saturable) | **Ethanol**, **Phenytoin**, **Aspirin** (high dose) | **Small dose changes ? Large concentration changes** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
