# Toxicology - Antidotes

## Essential Antidotes
| Antidote | Indication | Mechanism | Dose | URL |
|----------|------------|-----------|------|-----|
| **Naloxone** | **Opioid overdose** | **ｵ-opioid receptor antagonist** | **0.04-2 mg IV/IM/IN** (repeat q2-3min) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/naloxone |
| **Flumazenil** | **Benzodiazepine overdose** | **GABA-A receptor antagonist** | **0.2 mg IV** over 30s ? 0.3 mg ? 0.5 mg q1min (max 3 mg) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/flumazenil |
| **N-Acetylcysteine (NAC)** | **Acetaminophen overdose** | **Replenishes glutathione** | **IV**: 150 mg/kg over 1h ? 50 mg/kg over 4h ? 100 mg/kg over 16h; **PO**: 140 mg/kg load ? 70 mg/kg q4h x17 doses | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/acetylcysteine |
| **Fomepizole** | **Methanol**, **Ethylene glycol** | **Inhibits alcohol dehydrogenase** | **15 mg/kg IV** load ? 10 mg/kg q12h x4 doses ? 15 mg/kg q12h thereafter | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/fomepizole |
| **Ethanol** | **Methanol**, **Ethylene glycol** (alternative to fomepizole) | **Competes for alcohol dehydrogenase** | **10 mL/kg of 10% ethanol IV** load ? 1-2 mL/kg/h (target serum ethanol 100-150 mg/dL) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Sodium Bicarbonate** | **TCA overdose** (wide QRS), **Salicylate poisoning** (urine alkalinization), **Na channel blockers** | **? Serum pH**, **? Urine pH** | **1-2 mEq/kg IV bolus** (TCA); **Infusion** (urine alkalinization, target urine pH >7.5) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Atropine** | **Organophosphate/carbamate poisoning** | **Muscarinic antagonist** | **1-5 mg IV** q5-15min (titrate to dried secretions) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/atropine |
| **Pralidoxime (2-PAM)** | **Organophosphate poisoning** | **Reactivates acetylcholinesterase** | **1-2 g IV** over 15-30 min ? 500 mg/h infusion | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/pralidoxime |
| **Digoxin Immune Fab (Digibind)** | **Digoxin toxicity** | **Binds digoxin** | **Dose based on serum level or amount ingested** | https://www.fda.gov/vaccines-blood-biologics/approved-blood-products/digibind |
| **Hydroxocobalamin (Cyanokit)** | **Cyanide poisoning** | **Binds cyanide ? Cyanocobalamin** (vitamin B12) | **5 g IV** over 15 min (repeat once if needed) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/hydroxocobalamin |
| **Sodium Thiosulfate** | **Cyanide poisoning** | **Enhances rhodanese-mediated detoxification** | **12.5 g IV** over 10 min | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Glucagon** | **Beta-blocker overdose**, **Calcium channel blocker overdose** | **? cAMP** (bypasses beta-receptor) | **3-10 mg IV** bolus ? **1-5 mg/h infusion** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/glucagon |
| **Calcium** | **Calcium channel blocker overdose**, **Hydrofluoric acid**, **Hyperkalemia** | **? Serum calcium** | **Calcium chloride 1-2 g IV** or **Calcium gluconate 3-6 g IV** | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **High-Dose Insulin Euglycemia (HIE)** | **Calcium channel blocker overdose**, **Beta-blocker overdose** | **? Myocardial glucose utilization** | **1 unit/kg IV bolus** ? **0.5-1 unit/kg/h infusion** (with dextrose) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Intralipid** | **Local anesthetic toxicity**, **Lipophilic drug overdose** | **"Lipid sink"** | **1.5 mL/kg of 20% lipid emulsion** bolus ? **0.25 mL/kg/min** infusion | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Physostigmine** | **Anticholinergic delirium** | **Reversible cholinesterase inhibitor** (crosses BBB) | **0.5-2 mg IV** slow push | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Pyridoxine (Vitamin B6)** | **Isoniazid overdose**, **Gyromitra mushroom** | **Cofactor for GABA synthesis** | **1 g per gram of INH ingested** (max 5 g) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Methylene Blue** | **Methemoglobinemia** | **Reduces methemoglobin to hemoglobin** | **1-2 mg/kg IV** over 5 min | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/methylene-blue |
