# Toxicology - Household Toxins

## Common Household Toxins
| Product | Toxic Ingredient | Clinical Effects | Management | URL |
|---------|------------------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Antifreeze** | **Ethylene glycol** | **Metabolic acidosis**, **Renal failure**, **CNS depression** | **Fomepizole**, **Hemodialysis** | https://www.poison.org/articles/antifreeze |
| **Windshield Washer Fluid** | **Methanol** | **Metabolic acidosis**, **Optic neuropathy** | **Fomepizole**, **Hemodialysis** | https://www.poison.org/articles/methanol |
| **Rubbing Alcohol** | **Isopropanol** | **CNS depression**, **Ketosis without acidosis** | **Supportive care** | https://www.poison.org/articles/isopropyl-alcohol |
| **Bleach** | **Sodium hypochlorite** | **Mild GI irritation** (household), **Caustic injury** (industrial) | **Dilution** | https://www.poison.org/articles/bleach |
| **Bleach + Ammonia** | **Chloramine gas** | **Respiratory irritation**, **Pneumonitis** | **Fresh air**, **Supportive care** | https://www.poison.org/articles/cleaning-products |
| **Bleach + Acid** | **Chlorine gas** | **Respiratory irritation**, **Pneumonitis** | **Fresh air**, **Supportive care** | https://www.poison.org/articles/cleaning-products |
| **Laundry Pods** | **Surfactants** | **Vomiting**, **Coughing**, **CNS depression**, **Corneal injury** | **Supportive care** | https://www.poison.org/articles/laundry-detergent-pods |
| **Silica Gel** | **Silica** | **Inert** (choking hazard, not toxic) | **Observation** | https://www.poison.org/articles/silica-gel |
| **Glow Sticks** | **Dibutyl phthalate** | **Oral/mucous membrane irritation**, **Transient burning** | **Rinse with water** | https://www.poison.org/articles/glow-products |
| **Button Batteries** | **Alkali** | **Esophageal necrosis** (within 2h) | **Emergent endoscopic removal** | https://www.poison.org/battery |
| **Magnets** | **Rare-earth magnets** | **Bowel ischemia/perforation** (if multiple ingested) | **Emergent removal** if multiple | https://www.poison.org/articles/magnets |
| **Essential Oils** | **Various** | **CNS depression** (eucalyptus, camphor), **Seizures** (eucalyptus, sage, wormwood), **Aspiration pneumonitis** | **Supportive care**, **Benzodiazepines** | https://www.poison.org/articles/essential-oils |

|-------------|---------|------------------|-----|
