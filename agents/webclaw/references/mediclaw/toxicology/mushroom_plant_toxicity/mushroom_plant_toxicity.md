# Toxicology - Mushroom and Plant Toxicity

## Mushroom Toxicity
| Mushroom | Toxin | Clinical Effects | Onset | Management | URL |
|----------|-------|------------------|-------|------------|-----|
| **Amanita phalloides** (Death Cap) | **Amatoxins** | **Stage 1** (6-12h): Severe GI; **Stage 2** (24-48h): Apparent recovery; **Stage 3** (48-96h): **Hepatic failure**, **Renal failure** | **Delayed** (>6h) | **Activated charcoal**, **N-Acetylcysteine**, **Silymarin** (milk thistle), **Penicillin G**, **Liver transplant** | https://www.poison.org/articles/mushroom-poisoning |
| **Gyromitra** (False Morel) | **Gyromitrin** (monomethylhydrazine) | **Seizures**, **Hepatotoxicity**, **Hemolysis** | **Delayed** (6-12h) | **Pyridoxine** (Vitamin B6), **Benzodiazepines** | https://www.poison.org/articles/mushroom-poisoning |
| **Cortinarius** | **Orellanine** | **Renal failure** (interstitial nephritis) | **Very delayed** (days-weeks) | **Supportive care**, **Hemodialysis** | https://www.poison.org/articles/mushroom-poisoning |
| **Psilocybe** (Magic Mushrooms) | **Psilocybin** | **Hallucinations**, **Agitation**, **Mydriasis**, **Tachycardia** | **Early** (<6h) | **Benzodiazepines**, **Supportive care** | https://www.poison.org/articles/mushroom-poisoning |
| **Amanita muscaria** | **Ibotenic acid**, **Muscimol** | **CNS depression** or **Excitation**, **Hallucinations**, **Seizures** | **Early** (<6h) | **Supportive care**, **Benzodiazepines** | https://www.poison.org/articles/mushroom-poisoning |
| **Coprinus** (Inky Cap) | **Coprine** | **Disulfiram-like reaction** with alcohol | **Early** (<6h) | **Avoid alcohol** | https://www.poison.org/articles/mushroom-poisoning |
| **Clitocybe/Inocybe** | **Muscarine** | **Cholinergic toxidrome** (SLUDGE) | **Early** (<6h) | **Atropine** | https://www.poison.org/articles/mushroom-poisoning |

## Plant Toxicity
| Plant | Toxin | Clinical Effects | Management | URL |
|-------|-------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Foxglove (Digitalis)** | **Cardiac glycosides** | **Similar to digoxin toxicity** (nausea, Arrhythmias, Hyperkalemia) | **Digoxin Immune Fab** | https://www.poison.org/articles/plant-poisoning |
| **Oleander** | **Cardiac glycosides** | **Similar to digoxin toxicity** | **Digoxin Immune Fab** | https://www.poison.org/articles/plant-poisoning |
| **Jimson Weed (Datura)** | **Anticholinergic alkaloids** (atropine, scopolamine) | **Anticholinergic toxidrome** | **Physostigmine** (severe) | https://www.poison.org/articles/plant-poisoning |
| **Poison Hemlock** | **Coniine** | **Nicotinic effects** (ascending paralysis, Respiratory failure) | **Supportive care**, **Mechanical ventilation** | https://www.poison.org/articles/plant-poisoning |
| **Water Hemlock** | **Cicutoxin** | **Seizures**, **Status epilepticus** | **Benzodiazepines**, **Barbiturates** | https://www.poison.org/articles/plant-poisoning |
| **Castor Bean** | **Ricin** | **Severe GI**, **Multi-organ failure** (inhalation: respiratory failure) | **Supportive care** | https://emergency.cdc.gov/agent/ricin/index.asp |
| **Poison Ivy/Oak/Sumac** | **Urushiol** | **Contact dermatitis** | **Topical corticosteroids**, **Oral corticosteroids** (severe) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/plants/default.html |
