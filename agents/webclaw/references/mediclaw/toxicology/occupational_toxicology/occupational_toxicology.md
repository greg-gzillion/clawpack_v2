# Toxicology - Occupational Toxicology

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Study of adverse health effects** from **workplace exposures** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/occupational.html |
| **OSHA** | **Occupational Safety and Health Administration** (sets/enforces workplace safety standards) | https://www.osha.gov |
| **NIOSH** | **National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health** (research, Recommendations) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh |
| **ACGIH** | **American Conference of Governmental Industrial Hygienists** (TLVs, BEIs) | https://www.acgih.org |

## Occupational Exposure Limits
| Limit | Definition | URL |
|-------|------------|-----|
| **PEL** (OSHA) | **Permissible Exposure Limit** (enforceable) | https://www.osha.gov/chemicaldata |
| **TLV** (ACGIH) | **Threshold Limit Value** (health-based) | https://www.acgih.org/science/tlv-bei-guidelines/ |
| **TWA** | **Time-Weighted Average** (8-hour workday) | https://www.osha.gov/chemicaldata |
| **STEL** | **Short-Term Exposure Limit** (15-minute) | https://www.osha.gov/chemicaldata |
| **Ceiling** | **Never exceed** concentration | https://www.osha.gov/chemicaldata |
| **IDLH** (NIOSH) | **Immediately Dangerous to Life or Health** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/idlh/default.html |
| **BEI** (ACGIH) | **Biological Exposure Index** (biomonitoring) | https://www.acgih.org/science/tlv-bei-guidelines/ |

## Common Occupational Exposures
| Exposure | Occupations | Health Effects | URL |
|----------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| **Asbestos** | **Construction**, **Shipyard**, **Insulation** | **Asbestosis**, **Mesothelioma**, **Lung cancer** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/asbestos/default.html |
| **Silica** | **Construction**, **Mining**, **Sandblasting** | **Silicosis**, **Lung cancer**, **COPD**, **Autoimmune disease** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/silica/default.html |
| **Coal Dust** | **Coal mining** | **Coal workers' pneumoconiosis** (black lung) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coaldust/default.html |
| **Cotton Dust** | **Textile industry** | **Byssinosis** (brown lung) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/cottondust/default.html |
| **Lead** | **Battery manufacturing**, **Construction**, **Shooting ranges** | **Neurotoxicity**, **Hypertension**, **Anemia**, **Nephropathy** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/lead/default.html |
| **Mercury** | **Dental**, **Mining**, **Fluorescent bulb recycling** | **Neurotoxicity**, **Tremor**, **Erethism** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/mercury/default.html |
| **Cadmium** | **Battery manufacturing**, **Welding**, **Pigments** | **Nephropathy**, **Osteoporosis**, **Lung cancer** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/cadmium/default.html |
| **Beryllium** | **Aerospace**, **Electronics**, **Dental labs** | **Berylliosis** (granulomatous lung disease) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/beryllium/default.html |
| **Solvents** | **Painting**, **Degreasing**, **Dry cleaning** | **Neurotoxicity**, **Hepatotoxicity**, **Nephrotoxicity** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/organsolvents/default.html |
| **Welding Fumes** | **Welding** | **Metal fume fever**, **COPD**, **Parkinsonism** (manganese) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/welding/default.html |
