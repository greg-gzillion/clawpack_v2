## Novel Psychoactive Substances (NPS)
| Class | Examples | Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|---------|-----|
| **Synthetic Cannabinoids** | **K2**, **Spice** | **Agitation**, **Hallucinations**, **Tachycardia**, **Seizures**, **Renal failure** | https://www.dea.gov/factsheets/synthetic-cannabinoids |
| **Synthetic Cathinones** | **"Bath Salts"** (MDPV, methylone) | **Sympathomimetic**, **Excited delirium**, **Hyperthermia**, **Rhabdomyolysis** | https://www.dea.gov/factsheets/synthetic-cathinones |
| **Fentanyl and Analogs** | **Fentanyl**, **Carfentanil**, **Acetylfentanyl** | **Opioid toxidrome**, **Rapid onset**, **High potency**, **Chest wall rigidity** | https://www.dea.gov/factsheets/fentanyl |
| **Xylazine** | **"Tranq"** | **CNS depression**, **Bradycardia**, **Hypotension**, **Necrotic skin ulcers** | https://www.dea.gov/factsheets/xylazine |
| **Phencyclidine (PCP)** | **PCP**, **Ketamine** | **Dissociative**, **Agitation**, **Analgesia**, **Nystagmus**, **Hypertension** | https://www.dea.gov/factsheets/pcp-phencyclidine |
| **Kratom** | **Mitragynine** | **Opioid-like effects** (low dose: stimulant; high dose: sedative), **Seizures**, **Hepatotoxicity** | https://www.dea.gov/factsheets/kratom |
