## Routes of Exposure
| Route | Characteristics | Clinical Significance | URL |
|-------|-----------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Ingestion** | **Most common** poisoning route | **Absorption variable** (pH, food, GI motility) | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Inhalation** | **Rapid absorption** (large surface area) | **Bypasses first-pass metabolism** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Dermal** | **Slow absorption** (barrier function) | **Enhanced by broken skin**, **Lipophilic substances** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
| **Injection** | **Immediate systemic availability** | **Highest risk of severe toxicity** | https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/training/toxmanual/modules/2/lecturenotes.html |
