# Toxicology - Overview

## What is Toxicology?
Toxicology is the scientific study of the adverse effects of chemical, physical, or biological agents on living organisms and the ecosystem, including the prevention and amelioration of such adverse effects. Clinical toxicology focuses on the diagnosis, management, and prevention of poisoning in humans.
