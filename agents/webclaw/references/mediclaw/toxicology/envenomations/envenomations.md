# Toxicology - Envenomations

## Snake Bites (Crotalinae - Pit Vipers)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Species** | **Rattlesnakes**, **Copperheads**, **Cottonmouths** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/snakes/default.html |
| **Envenomation Signs** | **Local**: Pain, Swelling, Ecchymosis, Bullae; **Systemic**: Coagulopathy, Thrombocytopenia, Hypotension, Neurotoxicity (rare) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Management** | **Supportive care**, **Mark and monitor swelling q15-30min**, **Elevate extremity**, **Crotalidae Polyvalent Immune Fab (CroFab)** or **Crotalidae Immune F(ab')2 (Anavip)** | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **CroFab Indications** | **Progressive swelling**, **Coagulopathy**, **Thrombocytopenia**, **Systemic signs** | https://www.fda.gov/vaccines-blood-biologics/approved-blood-products/crofab |
| **AVOID** | **Tourniquets**, **Incision/suction**, **Ice**, **NSAIDs** (bleeding risk) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |

## Snake Bites (Elapidae - Coral Snakes)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Species** | **Eastern coral snake**, **Texas coral snake**, **Arizona coral snake** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/snakes/default.html |
| **Identification** | **"Red touches yellow, kill a fellow; red touches black, venom lack"** (US only) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Envenomation Signs** | **Delayed onset** (up to 12h), **Neurotoxicity** (ptosis, Diplopia, Dysphagia, Respiratory failure) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Management** | **Antivenin (Micrurus fulvius)** for confirmed/suspected envenomation | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |

## Spider Bites
| Spider | Clinical Effects | Management | URL |
|--------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Black Widow (Latrodectus)** | **Latrodectism**: **Severe muscle pain/cramps**, **Abdominal rigidity**, **Hypertension**, **Tachycardia**, **Diaphoresis** | **Benzodiazepines**, **Opioids**, **Antivenom** (severe) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/spiders/default.html |
| **Brown Recluse (Loxosceles)** | **Loxoscelism**: **Necrotic skin lesion** (red, white, blue sign), **Systemic** (hemolysis, DIC, Renal failure - rare) | **Wound care**, **Dapsone** (controversial), **Avoid early excision** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/spiders/default.html |

## Marine Envenomations
| Organism | Clinical Effects | Management | URL |
|----------|-----------------|------------|-----|
| **Stingray** | **Intense pain**, **Edema**, **Laceration**, **Retained barb** | **Hot water immersion** (45蚓 x30-90 min), **Wound care**, **Antibiotics** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/marine-stings/default.html |
| **Lionfish/Scorpionfish/Stonefish** | **Intense pain**, **Edema**, **Systemic** (hypotension, Arrhythmias - stonefish) | **Hot water immersion**, **Stonefish antivenom** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/marine-stings/default.html |
| **Jellyfish (Portuguese Man-of-War)** | **Pain**, **Linear urticarial lesions** | **Vinegar** (inactivates nematocysts), **Hot water immersion**, **Remove tentacles** (with gloves/tweezers) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/marine-stings/default.html |
| **Box Jellyfish (Chironex fleckeri)** | **Severe pain**, **Cardiovascular collapse**, **Death within minutes** | **Vinegar**, **Box jellyfish antivenom**, **CPR** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/marine-stings/default.html |
