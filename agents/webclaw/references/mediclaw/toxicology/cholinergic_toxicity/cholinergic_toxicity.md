## Organophosphates vs Carbamates
| Feature | Organophosphates | Carbamates | URL |
|---------|------------------|------------|-----|
| **AChE Binding** | **Irreversible** (covalent) | **Reversible** | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Duration** | **Days-weeks** (requires new enzyme synthesis) | **24-48 hours** | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **CNS Penetration** | **Yes** (lipophilic) | **No** (hydrophilic) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Pralidoxime (2-PAM)** | **Effective** | **Not indicated** (may worsen) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |

## Management of Organophosphate Poisoning
| Intervention | Dose | URL |
|--------------|------|-----|
| **Decontamination** | **Remove clothing**, **Wash skin with soap and water** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/pesticides/default.html |
| **Atropine** | **1-5 mg IV** q5-15min (titrate to dried secretions, Clear chest) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Pralidoxime (2-PAM)** | **1-2 g IV** over 15-30 min ? **500 mg/h infusion** (continue =24h) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Benzodiazepines** | **Seizures**, **Agitation** | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
| **Avoid Succinylcholine** | **Prolonged paralysis** (AChE inhibition) | https://www.acmt.net/Resources/Clinical-Resources.aspx |
