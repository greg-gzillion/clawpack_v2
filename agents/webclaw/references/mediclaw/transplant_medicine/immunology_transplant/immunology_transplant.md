| **MHC Class II** | **HLA-DR, HLA-DQ, HLA-DP** | **APCs** (dendritic cells, macrophages, B cells) | **Present exogenous antigens to CD4+ T cells** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |

## Types of Rejection
| Type | Timing | Mechanism | Histology | URL |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Hyperacute** | **Minutes-hours** | **Preformed DSA** ? **Complement activation** | **Thrombosis**, **Hemorrhage**, **Necrosis** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Acute Cellular** | **Days-weeks** | **T cell-mediated** (CD8+ cytotoxicity, CD4+ help) | **Lymphocytic infiltrate**, **Tubulitis**, **Endothelitis** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Acute Antibody-Mediated (AMR)** | **Days-weeks** | **De novo DSA** ? **Complement activation**, **Endothelial injury** | **C4d deposition**, **Microvascular inflammation**, **Thrombosis** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Chronic** | **Months-years** | **Multifactorial** (immune and non-immune) | **Interstitial fibrosis**, **Tubular atrophy**, **Vascular obliteration** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
