# Transplant Medicine - Pancreas Transplantation

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indications** | **Type 1 diabetes** with **ESRD** (SPK) or **severe metabolic complications** (PTA, PAK) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |
| **Types** | **SPK** (Simultaneous Pancreas-Kidney), **PAK** (Pancreas After Kidney), **PTA** (Pancreas Transplant Alone) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |
| **SPK** | **Most common** (80-90%) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |

## Surgical Considerations
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Exocrine Drainage** | **Enteric** (preferred) or **Bladder** (allows urine amylase monitoring) | https://asts.org/patient-information/pancreas-transplant |
| **Venous Drainage** | **Systemic** (iliac vein) or **Portal** (more physiologic) | https://asts.org/patient-information/pancreas-transplant |

## Post-Transplant Complications
| Complication | Timing | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Graft Thrombosis** | **Early** | **Emergent thrombectomy**, **Re-transplantation** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |
| **Pancreatitis** | **Early** | **Supportive care**, **Octreotide** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |
| **Anastomotic Leak** | **Early** | **Percutaneous drainage**, **Surgical revision** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |
| **Acute Rejection** | **Weeks-months** | **Pulse steroids**, **Thymoglobulin** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |
| **Recurrent Autoimmune Diabetes** | **Months-years** | **Immunosuppression optimization** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/pancreas/ |
