# Transplant Medicine - Overview

## What is Transplant Medicine?
Transplant medicine is the medical specialty concerned with the evaluation, selection, and management of patients undergoing solid organ transplantation. It encompasses pre-transplant evaluation, perioperative care, immunosuppression management, and long-term follow-up of transplant recipients.
