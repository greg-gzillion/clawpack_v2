## Post-Transplant Complications
| Complication | Timing | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Primary Graft Dysfunction (PGD)** | **<72h** | **Supportive care**, **Low tidal volume ventilation**, **ECMO** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Acute Rejection** | **Weeks-months** | **Pulse steroids**, **Augment immunosuppression** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Chronic Lung Allograft Dysfunction (CLAD)** | **Months-years** | **Azithromycin**, **Augment immunosuppression**, **Retransplantation** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Infection** | **Any** | **Antimicrobials**, **Prophylaxis** (PJP, CMV, Fungal) | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **Airway Complications** | **Weeks-months** | **Bronchoscopic dilation**, **Stenting**, **Surgery** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
