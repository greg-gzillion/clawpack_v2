# Transplant Medicine - Immunosuppression

## Phases of Immunosuppression
| Phase | Goal | Duration | URL |
|-------|------|----------|-----|
| **Induction** | **Intense immunosuppression** to prevent early rejection | **Peri-transplant** (days) | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **Maintenance** | **Long-term immunosuppression** to prevent rejection | **Lifelong** | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **Anti-Rejection** | **Treat acute rejection episodes** | **As needed** | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |

## Induction Agents
| Agent | Mechanism | Dose | Considerations | URL |
|-------|-----------|------|----------------|-----|
| **Basiliximab** | **Anti-CD25** (IL-2R antagonist) | 20 mg day 0 and 4 | **First-line** for low-risk kidney transplant | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **Thymoglobulin (rATG)** | **Polyclonal anti-T cell** | 1.5 mg/kg/day x3-7 days | **Higher risk**, **Cytokine release syndrome**, **Leukopenia**, **Thrombocytopenia** | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **Alemtuzumab** | **Anti-CD52** (lymphocyte depletion) | 30 mg day 0-1 | **Prolonged lymphopenia**, **Higher infection risk** | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |

## Maintenance Immunosuppression
| Class | Agents | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|--------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Calcineurin Inhibitors (CNI)** | **Tacrolimus**, **Cyclosporine** | **Inhibit NFAT** ? **? IL-2** | **Nephrotoxicity**, **Hypertension**, **Tremor**, **Hyperglycemia**, **Neurotoxicity**, **Gingival hyperplasia** (cyclosporine) | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **Antimetabolites** | **Mycophenolate mofetil (MMF)** , **Mycophenolic acid (MPA)** , **Azathioprine** | **Inhibit IMPDH** ? **? Purine synthesis** | **GI upset** (diarrhea), **Myelosuppression**, **Teratogenicity** (MMF) | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **mTOR Inhibitors** | **Sirolimus**, **Everolimus** | **Inhibit mTOR** ? **? Cell cycle** | **Hyperlipidemia**, **Thrombocytopenia**, **Delayed wound healing**, **Mouth ulcers**, **Proteinuria** | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **Corticosteroids** | **Prednisone** | **Multiple** (anti-inflammatory) | **Hyperglycemia**, **Weight gain**, **Osteoporosis**, **Cataracts**, **Avascular necrosis** | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
| **Costimulation Blockers** | **Belatacept** | **CTLA4-Ig** (blocks CD28-B7) | **Monthly IV**, **? PTLD risk** (EBV-naive), **Lower nephrotoxicity** | https://www.myast.org/education/immunosuppression |
