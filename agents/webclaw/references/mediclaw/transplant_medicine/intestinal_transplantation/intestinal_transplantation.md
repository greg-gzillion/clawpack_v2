# Transplant Medicine - Intestinal Transplantation

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indications** | **Irreversible intestinal failure** with **complications of parenteral nutrition** (liver disease, Loss of venous access, Recurrent sepsis) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/intestine/ |
| **Types** | **Isolated intestine**, **Liver-intestine**, **Multivisceral** (stomach, duodenum, pancreas, intestine ± liver) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/intestine/ |

## Post-Transplant Complications
| Complication | Timing | Management | URL |
|--------------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Acute Rejection** | **Common** (high immunogenicity) | **Pulse steroids**, **Thymoglobulin**, **Increased CNI** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/intestine/ |
| **Infection** | **Common** (bacterial translocation) | **Antibiotics**, **Antifungals**, **Antivirals** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/intestine/ |
| **PTLD** | **Higher risk** (high lymphoid tissue) | **Reduce immunosuppression**, **Rituximab**, **Chemotherapy** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/intestine/ |
| **GVHD** | **Higher risk** | **Corticosteroids**, **Optimize immunosuppression** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/intestine/ |
