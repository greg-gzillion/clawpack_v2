# Transplant Medicine - Rejection

## Kidney Allograft Rejection (Banff Classification)
| Category | Histology | Management | URL |
|----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Borderline** | **Mild tubulitis** (t1), **No intimal arteritis** | **Optimize CNI** or **Pulse steroids** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Acute T Cell-Mediated (Type IA)** | **Significant tubulitis** (t2), **No intimal arteritis** | **Pulse steroids** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Acute T Cell-Mediated (Type IB)** | **Severe tubulitis** (t3), **No intimal arteritis** | **Pulse steroids**, **Thymoglobulin** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Acute T Cell-Mediated (Type II)** | **Intimal arteritis** (v1-v2) | **Thymoglobulin** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Acute T Cell-Mediated (Type III)** | **Transmural arteritis/Fibrinoid necrosis** (v3) | **Thymoglobulin**, **Plasmapheresis/IVIG** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Antibody-Mediated Rejection (AMR)** | **C4d+**, **Microvascular inflammation**, **DSA+** | **Plasmapheresis + IVIG + Rituximab** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Chronic Active TCMR** | **Chronic tubulointerstitial changes** + **Tubulitis** | **Optimize immunosuppression** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |
| **Chronic Active AMR** | **C4d+**, **Transplant glomerulopathy**, **DSA+** | **Plasmapheresis/IVIG**, **Rituximab**, **Limited efficacy** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-immunology |

## Liver Allograft Rejection (Banff Classification)
| Category | Histology | Management | URL |
|----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Acute Cellular Rejection** | **Portal inflammation**, **Bile duct damage**, **Endothelitis** (Rejection Activity Index 1-9) | **Pulse steroids**, **Optimize CNI** | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/liver-transplantation |
| **Acute AMR** | **Portal microvascular endothelial hypertrophy**, **C4d+**, **DSA+** | **Plasmapheresis + IVIG + Rituximab** | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/liver-transplantation |
| **Chronic Rejection** | **Ductopenia**, **Obliterative arteriopathy** | **Optimize immunosuppression**, **Re-transplantation** | https://www.aasld.org/practice-guidelines/liver-transplantation |

## Heart Allograft Rejection (ISHLT Classification)
| Grade | Histology | Management | URL |
|-------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **0R** | **No rejection** | **Continue maintenance** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **1R** | **Mild** (interstitial/perivascular infiltrate, No myocyte damage) | **Optimize immunosuppression** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **2R** | **Moderate** (=2 foci with myocyte damage) | **Pulse steroids** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **3R** | **Severe** (diffuse infiltrate, Multifocal damage) | **Thymoglobulin**, **Plasmapheresis/IVIG** (if AMR) | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **AMR** | **Intravascular macrophages**, **C4d+**, **DSA+** | **Plasmapheresis + IVIG + Rituximab** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |

## Lung Allograft Rejection (ISHLT Classification)
| Grade | Histology | Management | URL |
|-------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **A0** | **No rejection** | **Continue maintenance** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
| **A1** | **Minimal** (perivascular infiltrate) | **Optimize immunosuppression** | https://ishlt.org/ishlt-documents/guidelines |
