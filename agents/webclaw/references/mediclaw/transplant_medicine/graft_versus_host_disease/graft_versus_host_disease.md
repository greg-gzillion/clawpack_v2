# Transplant Medicine - Graft-Versus-Host Disease (GVHD)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Donor immune cells attack recipient tissues** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **Most Common** | **Intestinal/Multivisceral transplant** (high lymphoid tissue), **Liver transplant** (uncommon but severe) | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **Timing** | **Acute** (<100 days) or **Chronic** (>100 days) | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |

## Acute GVHD
| Organ | Manifestations | URL |
|-------|----------------|-----|
| **Skin** | **Maculopapular rash**, **Erythroderma**, **Bullae**, **Desquamation** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **GI** | **Diarrhea**, **Nausea/Vomiting**, **Abdominal pain**, **Ileus** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **Liver** | **Cholestatic hepatitis** (? ALP, ? Bilirubin) | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |

## Acute GVHD Grading (Glucksberg)
| Grade | Skin | Liver (Bilirubin) | GI (Diarrhea) | URL |
|-------|------|------------------|---------------|-----|
| **I** | **Rash <25% BSA** | **2-3 mg/dL** | **500-1000 mL/day** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **II** | **Rash 25-50% BSA** | **3-6 mg/dL** | **1000-1500 mL/day** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **III** | **Rash >50% BSA** | **6-15 mg/dL** | **>1500 mL/day** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **IV** | **Erythroderma with bullae** | **>15 mg/dL** | **Severe pain/ileus** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |

## GVHD Management
| Severity | Treatment | URL |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Grade I** | **Topical corticosteroids** (skin), **Optimize immunosuppression** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **Grade II-IV** | **High-dose systemic corticosteroids**, **Augment immunosuppression** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
| **Steroid-Refractory** | **Ruxolitinib** (JAK inhibitor), **Extracorporeal photopheresis**, **Anti-TNF**, **Anti-IL-2R** | https://www.myast.org/education/gvhd |
