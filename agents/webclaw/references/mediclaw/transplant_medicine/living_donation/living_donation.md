# Transplant Medicine - Living Donation

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Donation of organ or portion of organ** from living donor | https://www.livingdonorassistance.org |
| **Organs** | **Kidney** (most common), **Liver** (right lobe or left lateral segment) | https://www.livingdonorassistance.org |
| **Benefits** | **Shorter wait time**, **Better graft survival**, **Elective timing** | https://www.livingdonorassistance.org |

## Living Kidney Donor Evaluation (OPTN Guidelines)
| Component | Assessment | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Medical** | **ABO compatibility**, **Crossmatch negative**, **GFR =80 mL/min/1.73m˛** (or =90 for age <50) | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/kidney-living-donation/ |
| **Infectious Disease** | **HIV**, **HBV**, **HCV**, **CMV**, **EBV**, **TB**, **Syphilis** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/kidney-living-donation/ |
| **Malignancy** | **Age-appropriate screening**, **No active malignancy** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/kidney-living-donation/ |
| **Psychosocial** | **Informed consent**, **No coercion**, **Understanding of risks** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/kidney-living-donation/ |

## Living Donor Risks
| Risk | Kidney | Liver | URL |
|------|--------|-------|-----|
| **Mortality** | **0.03%** (1 in 3,000) | **0.2-0.5%** (1 in 200-500) | https://www.livingdonorassistance.org |
| **ESRD** | **<1%** lifetime (slightly higher than general population) | N/A | https://www.livingdonorassistance.org |
| **Hypertension** | **Slightly increased** | N/A | https://www.livingdonorassistance.org |
| **Surgical Complications** | **10-20%** (minor), **1-3%** (major) | **20-40%** (biliary leak, Infection) | https://www.livingdonorassistance.org |
