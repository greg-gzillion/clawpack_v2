# Transplant Medicine - Ethical and Legal Issues

## Organ Donation
| Principle | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Opt-In (USA)** | **Donor registration** required (driver's license, Donor registry) | https://www.organdonor.gov |
| **Opt-Out** | **Presumed consent** unless opted out (many European countries) | https://www.organdonor.gov |
| **Dead Donor Rule** | **Donor must be declared dead before organ procurement** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/resources/ethics/ |

## Determination of Death
| Type | Criteria | URL |
|------|----------|-----|
| **Neurologic Death (Brain Death)** | **Irreversible cessation of all brain function**, **Including brainstem** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/resources/ethics/brain-death/ |
| **Circulatory Death (DCD)** | **Irreversible cessation of circulatory and respiratory function** | https://optn.transplant.hrsa.gov/professionals/by-organ/dcd/ |

## Allocation Ethics
| Principle | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
