# Transplant Medicine - Transplant Complications

## Cardiovascular Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Hypertension** | **Treat aggressively** (CCB preferred for CNI-induced) | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
| **Hyperlipidemia** | **Statin** (caution with CNI/mTORi interactions) | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
| **Post-Transplant Diabetes Mellitus (PTDM)** | **Screen**, **Treat**, **Consider steroid minimization**, **Avoid CNI if possible** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |

## Metabolic Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Osteoporosis** | **Calcium + Vitamin D**, **Bisphosphonates** (if indicated) | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
| **Hyperuricemia/Gout** | **Allopurinol** (caution with azathioprine), **Febuxostat**, **Colchicine** (caution with CNI) | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |

## Hematologic Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Anemia** | **Evaluate cause** (MMF, CNI, Iron deficiency, B12/folate, Parvovirus) | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
| **Leukopenia** | **Reduce MMF or valganciclovir**, **G-CSF** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
| **Thrombocytopenia** | **Evaluate cause** (MMF, mTORi, Valganciclovir, TTP/HUS) | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |

## Neurologic Complications
| Complication | Management | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Tremor** | **Reduce CNI**, **Consider switch to cyclosporine or belatacept** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
| **Posterior Reversible Encephalopathy Syndrome (PRES)** | **Reduce CNI**, **Control BP**, **Supportive care** | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
| **Peripheral Neuropathy** | **Evaluate cause** (CNI, Diabetes, Uremia) | https://www.myast.org/education/transplant-complications |
