## Serotonin Syndrome
| Severity | Symptoms | Management | URL |
|----------|----------|------------|-----|
| **Mild** | Tremor, Hyperreflexia, Diaphoresis | Discontinue serotonergic agents, Benzodiazepines | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Moderate** | Clonus, Agitation, Hyperthermia, Rigidity | Discontinue agents, Benzodiazepines, Cyproheptadine | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Severe** | Severe hyperthermia, Autonomic instability, Delirium | ICU, Cyproheptadine, Cooling, Paralysis | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
