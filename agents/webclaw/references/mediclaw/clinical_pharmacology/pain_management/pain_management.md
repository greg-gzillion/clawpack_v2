## Opioid Adverse Effects and Management
| Adverse Effect | Management | URL |
|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Constipation** | **Stimulant laxative + Stool softener** (senna + docusate) | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/healthcare-professionals/prescribing/index.html |
| **Nausea/Vomiting** | Antiemetics (ondansetron, metoclopramide) | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/healthcare-professionals/prescribing/index.html |
| **Sedation** | Reduce dose, Avoid other CNS depressants | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/healthcare-professionals/prescribing/index.html |
| **Pruritus** | Antihistamines, Opioid rotation, Nalbuphine | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/healthcare-professionals/prescribing/index.html |
| **Respiratory Depression** | **Naloxone** (dilute 0.04 mg IV q2-3min) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/naloxone |
