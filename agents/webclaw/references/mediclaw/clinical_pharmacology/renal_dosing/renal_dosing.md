## Drugs Requiring Renal Dose Adjustment
| Drug | CrCl Threshold | Adjustment | URL |
|------|---------------|------------|-----|
| **Vancomycin** | <60 mL/min | Reduce dose or extend interval | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
| **Aminoglycosides** | <60 mL/min | Extend interval or reduce dose | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/aminoglycosides/ |
| **Enoxaparin** | <30 mL/min | Reduce to 1 mg/kg daily | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Dabigatran** | <30 mL/min | **Avoid** (CrCl 15-30: 75 mg BID) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Rivaroxaban** | <30 mL/min | **Avoid** (except Afib: 15 mg daily if CrCl 15-50) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Metformin** | 30-45 mL/min | Reduce dose (max 1000 mg/day) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Metformin** | <30 mL/min | **Contraindicated** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **SGLT2 Inhibitors** | <45 mL/min | Reduced efficacy; Avoid initiation | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Nitrofurantoin** | <60 mL/min | **Avoid** (ineffective, toxicity risk) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Bisphosphonates** | <35 mL/min | **Avoid** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Gabapentin/Pregabalin** | <60 mL/min | Reduce dose | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Cephalosporins** | <50 mL/min | Reduce dose or extend interval | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |

## Nephrotoxic Drugs
| Drug Class | Mechanism | Prevention | URL |
|------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Aminoglycosides** | Tubular toxicity | **Extended-interval dosing**, Monitor levels | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/aminoglycosides/ |
| **Vancomycin** | Tubular toxicity | **AUC-guided dosing**, Avoid trough >20 | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
| **NSAIDs** | Afferent arteriole constriction | **Avoid in CKD, HF, Volume depletion** | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/pain-medicines-analgesics |
| **Contrast Media** | Tubular toxicity, Ischemia | Hydration, Hold nephrotoxins | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Contrast-Manual |
| **Amphotericin B** | Tubular toxicity | **Liposomal formulation**, Hydration | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Cisplatin** | Tubular toxicity | Hydration, Amifostine | https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/cisplatin |
| **Calcineurin Inhibitors** | Vasoconstriction, Tubular toxicity | Monitor levels, Lowest effective dose | https://www.kidney.org/atoz/content/immunosuppression |
