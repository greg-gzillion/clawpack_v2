## Teratogenic Drugs (Absolute Contraindications)
| Drug | Effect | Timing | URL |
|------|--------|--------|-----|
| **Isotretinoin** | **Severe craniofacial, Cardiac, CNS defects** | First trimester | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/isotretinoin |
| **Thalidomide** | **Phocomelia** (limb reduction) | Days 20-36 | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/thalidomide |
| **Valproate** | **Neural tube defects (10%)** , Cognitive impairment | First trimester | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/valproate |
| **Methotrexate** | **Aminopterin syndrome** (craniofacial, Limb defects) | First trimester | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/methotrexate |
| **Warfarin** | **Warfarin embryopathy** (nasal hypoplasia, Stippled epiphyses) | Weeks 6-12 | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/warfarin |
| **ACE Inhibitors/ARBs** | **Renal dysgenesis, Oligohydramnios, Skull hypoplasia** | Second/Third trimester | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/ace-inhibitors |
| **Lithium** | **Ebstein anomaly** (tricuspid valve) | First trimester | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/lithium |
| **Tetracyclines** | **Tooth discoloration, Bone growth inhibition** | Second/Third trimester | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |

## Relatively Safe Drugs in Pregnancy
| Drug Class | Examples | Comments | URL |
|------------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Analgesics** | Acetaminophen | **First-line** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Antibiotics** | Penicillins, Cephalosporins, Macrolides (not estolate) | Generally safe | https://www.cdc.gov/antibiotic-use/ |
| **Antiemetics** | Ondansetron, Doxylamine-pyridoxine | First-line for N/V | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Antihypertensives** | Labetalol, Nifedipine, Methyldopa | Avoid ACEi/ARBs | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Anticoagulants** | Heparin, LMWH | **Do not cross placenta** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Antidepressants** | Sertraline | Preferred SSRI | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
