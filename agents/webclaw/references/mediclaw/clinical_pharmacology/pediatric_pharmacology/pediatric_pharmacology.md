## Pediatric Drug Safety Concerns
| Drug | Concern | Age Group | URL |
|------|---------|-----------|-----|
| **Aspirin** | **Reye syndrome** | Children with viral illness | https://www.cdc.gov/reyes-syndrome/index.html |
| **Codeine/Tramadol** | **Respiratory depression** in CYP2D6 ultrarapid metabolizers | **Contraindicated <12 years** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/codeine |
| **Tetracyclines** | Tooth discoloration, Bone growth inhibition | **<8 years** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Fluoroquinolones** | Tendon injury | All children (use only if no alternative) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Ceftriaxone** | **Biliary sludging** | Neonates (especially with calcium) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Promethazine** | **Respiratory depression** | **Contraindicated <2 years** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **OTC Cough/Cold** | Ineffectiveness, Adverse effects | **<4 years** | https://www.fda.gov/consumers/consumer-updates/when-give-kids-medicine-coughs-and-colds |
| **Benzocaine** | **Methemoglobinemia** | <2 years | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |

## Common Pediatric Dosing Formulas
| Formula | Use | URL |
|---------|-----|-----|
| **Young's Rule** | Age / (Age + 12) × Adult dose | https://www.msdmanuals.com/professional/pediatrics/principles-of-drug-treatment-in-children/drug-treatment-in-children |
