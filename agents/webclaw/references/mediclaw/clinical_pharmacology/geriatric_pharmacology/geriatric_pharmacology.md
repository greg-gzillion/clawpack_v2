## Beers Criteria - Potentially Inappropriate Medications in Older Adults
| Drug Class | Rationale | Recommendation | Alternative | URL |
|------------|-----------|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Anticholinergics** (diphenhydramine, oxybutynin) | **Confusion, Constipation, Falls** | **Avoid** | Non-anticholinergic alternatives | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Benzodiazepines** | **Falls, Fractures, Cognitive impairment** | **Avoid** | Non-pharmacologic, SSRIs | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Nonbenzodiazepine hypnotics** (zolpidem, eszopiclone) | Falls, Delirium | **Avoid** | CBT-I, Melatonin | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **NSAIDs** | GI bleeding, AKI, ? BP | **Avoid long-term** | Acetaminophen, Topical NSAIDs | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **PPIs >8 weeks** | C. diff, Bone loss, B12 deficiency | Deprescribe | H2 blockers, Lifestyle | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Sliding scale insulin** | Hypoglycemia, No benefit | Avoid | Basal insulin ± prandial | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Sulfonylureas (long-acting)** | **Severe prolonged hypoglycemia** | Avoid glyburide | Glipizide, Metformin | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Peripheral alpha-blockers** | Orthostasis | Avoid for HTN | Other antihypertensives | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **TCAs** | Anticholinergic, Orthostasis, QT prolongation | **Avoid** | SSRIs, SNRIs | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Antipsychotics** | Stroke, Mortality in dementia | Avoid unless psychosis/agitation refractory | Non-pharmacologic | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
