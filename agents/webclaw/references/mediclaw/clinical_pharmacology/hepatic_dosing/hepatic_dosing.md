## Drugs Requiring Hepatic Dose Adjustment
| Drug | Child-Pugh A | Child-Pugh B | Child-Pugh C | URL |
|------|--------------|--------------|--------------|-----|
| **Acetaminophen** | Full dose | Max 2-3 g/day | Max 2 g/day | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Metronidazole** | Full dose | Full dose | Reduce by 50% | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Morphine** | Reduce dose | Reduce dose/extend interval | Avoid | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Benzodiazepines** | Reduce dose | Reduce dose | Avoid (except lorazepam) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **PPIs** | Full dose | Full dose | Max 20 mg omeprazole | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Statins** | Full dose | **Avoid** high-dose | **Avoid** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **NSAIDs** | Full dose | **Avoid** | **Avoid** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Warfarin** | Monitor INR | Monitor INR closely | Avoid | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **DOACs** | Full dose (most) | Avoid (most) | Avoid | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
