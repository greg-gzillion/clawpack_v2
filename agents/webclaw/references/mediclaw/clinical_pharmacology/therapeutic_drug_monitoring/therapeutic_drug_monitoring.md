## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Measurement of drug concentrations to optimize dosing | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/therapeutic-drug-monitoring |
| **Indications** | Narrow therapeutic index, PK variability, Toxicity assessment, Adherence monitoring | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-approvals-and-databases/therapeutic-drug-monitoring |

| Drug | Therapeutic Range | Toxic Level | Timing | Critical Factors | URL |
|------|------------------|-------------|--------|------------------|-----|
| **Digoxin** | 0.5-0.9 ng/mL (HF), 0.8-2.0 ng/mL (AF) | >2.0 ng/mL | Trough (=6h post-dose) | **Renal function**, Hypokalemia ? toxicity | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/digoxin |
| **Lithium** | 0.6-1.2 mEq/L (maintenance), 1.0-1.5 mEq/L (acute) | >1.5 mEq/L | Trough (12h post-dose) | **Renal function**, Sodium intake, NSAIDs | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Phenytoin** | 10-20 mcg/mL (total) | >20 mcg/mL | Trough | **Albumin** (corrected = total / [0.2 ﾗ alb + 0.1]) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/phenytoin |
| **Valproic Acid** | 50-100 mcg/mL | >120 mcg/mL | Trough | Hepatic function | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/valproic-acid |
| **Vancomycin** | Trough 10-20 mg/L (severe MRSA) | >20 mg/L (nephrotoxicity) | Trough (pre-4th dose) | **Renal function**, AUC-guided preferred | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
| **Aminoglycosides** | Peak 5-10 mg/L (gent/tobra), Trough <2 mg/L | Trough >2 mg/L | Peak (30 min post), Trough (pre-dose) | **Renal function**, Extended-interval dosing preferred | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/aminoglycosides/ |

| **Tacrolimus** | 5-20 ng/mL (transplant type specific) | >20 ng/mL | Trough (12h post-dose) | CYP3A5 genotype, Drug interactions | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/tacrolimus |

## Vancomycin Monitoring (2020 IDSA/ASHP Guidelines)
| Parameter | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Preferred Method** | **AUC/MIC-guided** (target 400-600) | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
| **Alternative** | Trough-guided (15-20 mg/L) for non-severe infections | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
| **Loading Dose** | 20-35 mg/kg (actual body weight) | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
| **Timing** | Trough before 4th dose, then weekly if stable | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
| **Nephrotoxicity Risk** | AUC >600 or Trough >20 mg/L | https://www.idsociety.org/practice-guideline/vancomycin/ |
