# Preventive Medicine - Mental Health Screening

## Depression Screening
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **USPSTF Grade** | **B** (Adults, including pregnant and postpartum) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/depression-in-adults-screening |
| **Screening Tools** | **PHQ-2** (initial), **PHQ-9** (confirmatory) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/depression-in-adults-screening |
| **Frequency** | **At least once**; More often if risk factors or symptoms | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/depression-in-adults-screening |
| **PHQ-2** | "Over the past 2 weeks, have you felt: 1) Little interest or pleasure; 2) Down, depressed, or hopeless?" (Score =3 ? PHQ-9) | https://www.phqscreeners.com |
| **PHQ-9 Scoring** | 0-4: None-minimal; 5-9: Mild; 10-14: Moderate; 15-19: Moderately severe; 20-27: Severe | https://www.phqscreeners.com |

## Suicide Risk Screening
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **USPSTF Grade** | **I** (insufficient evidence for routine screening) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/suicide-risk-screening |
| **When to Screen** | **Depression**, **Anxiety**, **Substance use**, **Chronic pain**, **Recent loss/trauma**, **Prior attempts** | https://www.samhsa.gov/suicide |
| **Screening Tool** | **C-SSRS** (Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale), **PHQ-9 item 9** | https://cssrs.columbia.edu |
| **Ask Directly** | "Have you had thoughts of harming yourself?" | https://www.samhsa.gov/suicide |
