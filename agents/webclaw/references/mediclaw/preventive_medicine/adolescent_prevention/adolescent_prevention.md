## CRAFFT Screening for Adolescent Substance Use
| Question | URL |
|----------|-----|
| **C** - Have you ever ridden in a **C**ar driven by someone who was high or had been using alcohol or drugs? | https://crafft.org |
| **R** - Do you ever use alcohol or drugs to **R**elax, feel better about yourself, or fit in? | https://crafft.org |
| **A** - Do you ever use alcohol or drugs while you are by yourself, **A**lone? | https://crafft.org |
| **F** - Do you ever **F**orget things you did while using alcohol or drugs? | https://crafft.org |
| **F** - Do your family or **F**riends ever tell you that you should cut down on your drinking or drug use? | https://crafft.org |
| **T** - Have you ever gotten into **T**rouble while you were using alcohol or drugs? | https://crafft.org |
| **Scoring** | **=2 "Yes"** = High risk for substance use disorder | https://crafft.org |
