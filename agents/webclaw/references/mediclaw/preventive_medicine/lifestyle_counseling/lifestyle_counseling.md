# Preventive Medicine - Lifestyle Counseling

## The 5 A's Framework for Behavior Change Counseling
| Step | Description | Example | URL |
|------|-------------|---------|-----|
| **Ask** | **Identify** risk behaviors | "Do you smoke?" | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Advise** | Give **clear, personalized advice** to change | "Quitting is the best thing you can do for your health" | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Assess** | Evaluate **readiness to change** | "Are you ready to quit in the next 30 days?" | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Assist** | Provide **resources and support** | Prescribe pharmacotherapy, Refer to counseling | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |
| **Arrange** | **Schedule follow-up** | Follow-up call or visit in 1-2 weeks | https://www.ahrq.gov/prevention/guidelines/tobacco/5steps.html |

## Stages of Change (Transtheoretical Model)
| Stage | Description | Intervention | URL |
|-------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| **Precontemplation** | **Not considering change** (next 6 months) | **Raise awareness**, Express concern | https://www.cancer.gov/publications/dictionaries/cancer-terms/def/stages-of-change-model |
| **Contemplation** | **Considering change** (next 6 months) | **Explore ambivalence**, Discuss pros/cons | https://www.cancer.gov/publications/dictionaries/cancer-terms/def/stages-of-change-model |
| **Preparation** | **Planning change** (next 30 days) | **Develop action plan**, Set specific goals | https://www.cancer.gov/publications/dictionaries/cancer-terms/def/stages-of-change-model |
| **Action** | **Actively changing** (past 6 months) | **Provide support**, Reinforce success | https://www.cancer.gov/publications/dictionaries/cancer-terms/def/stages-of-change-model |
| **Maintenance** | **Sustained change** (>6 months) | **Relapse prevention**, Address triggers | https://www.cancer.gov/publications/dictionaries/cancer-terms/def/stages-of-change-model |
| **Relapse** | Return to previous behavior | **Normalize**, Learn from experience, Re-engage | https://www.cancer.gov/publications/dictionaries/cancer-terms/def/stages-of-change-model |

## Motivational Interviewing (MI) Principles
| Principle | Description | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Express Empathy** | **Reflective listening**, Understand patient's perspective | https://motivationalinterviewing.org |
| **Develop Discrepancy** | Help patient see **gap between current behavior and goals/values** | https://motivationalinterviewing.org |
| **Roll with Resistance** | **Avoid arguing**, Invite new perspectives | https://motivationalinterviewing.org |
| **Support Self-Efficacy** | **Belief in patient's ability to change** | https://motivationalinterviewing.org |
| **OARS Skills** | **O**pen-ended questions, **A**ffirmations, **R**eflections, **S**ummaries | https://motivationalinterviewing.org |
