# Preventive Medicine - Geriatric Prevention

## Medicare Annual Wellness Visit (AWV)
| Component | Requirement | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Health Risk Assessment** | Medical history, Medications, Functional status, Psychosocial risks | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Cognitive Assessment** | Direct observation + Informant report | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Functional Assessment** | **ADLs** (bathing, dressing, toileting), **IADLs** (shopping, finances) | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Fall Risk Assessment** | **STEADI**, **Timed Up and Go (TUG)** | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **Depression Screening** | **PHQ-2/PHQ-9** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Advance Care Planning** | Discuss goals of care, Healthcare proxy, Living will | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |
| **Personalized Prevention Plan** | Based on assessment findings | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |

## Falls Prevention
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **USPSTF Grade** | **B** (Exercise interventions to prevent falls in adults =65 at increased risk) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/falls-prevention-community-dwelling-older-adults-interventions |
| **Screening Questions** | "Have you fallen in the past year?" "Do you feel unsteady?" "Do you worry about falling?" | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **Timed Up and Go (TUG)** | **=12 seconds** = Increased fall risk | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **STEADI Algorithm** | CDC's **S**topping **E**lderly **A**ccidents, **D**eaths & **I**njuries | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **Interventions** | **Exercise** (balance, strength), **Medication review**, **Vitamin D** (800-1000 IU), **Home safety**, **Vision correction** | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
