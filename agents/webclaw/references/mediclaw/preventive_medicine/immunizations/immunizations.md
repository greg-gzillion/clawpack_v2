# Preventive Medicine - Immunizations

## Adult Immunization Schedule (CDC/ACIP 2024)
| Vaccine | Recommendation | URL |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Influenza** | **Annually** for all =6 months | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Tdap/Td** | **Tdap once**, then **Td booster q10y**; **Tdap in each pregnancy** (27-36 weeks) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **COVID-19** | **Per current CDC guidelines** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/covid-19/clinical-considerations/index.html |
| **RSV** | **Adults =60** (shared decision); **Pregnancy 32-36 weeks** (seasonal) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/rsv/index.html |
| **Zoster (Shingrix)** | **Adults =50** (2 doses, 2-6 months apart) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/shingles/hcp/shingrix/recommendations.html |
| **Pneumococcal** | **Adults =65** (PCV15 or PCV20) OR high-risk (immunocompromised, CSF leak, Cochlear implant) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/pneumo/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **HPV** | **Routine at age 11-12**; Catch-up through 26; Shared decision 27-45 | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hpv/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **Hepatitis B** | **Adults 19-59** (universal); =60 with risk factors | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Hepatitis A** | **High-risk** (travel, MSM, IVDU, Chronic liver disease, Homeless) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Meningococcal (MenACWY)** | **High-risk** (asplenia, Complement deficiency, HIV, Travel, Military, College students in dorms) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Meningococcal B (MenB)** | **High-risk** (asplenia, Complement deficiency, Lab workers, Outbreaks); **Shared decision** for adolescents/young adults 16-23 | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **MMR** | **Born =1957**: =1 dose (unless evidence of immunity); **Born <1957**: Consider if no evidence | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Varicella** | **Born =1980**: 2 doses (unless evidence of immunity) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Mpox** | **High-risk** (MSM with multiple partners, HIV, STIs) | https://www.cdc.gov/poxvirus/mpox/vaccines/index.html |

## Pneumococcal Vaccine Recommendations (CDC 2024)
| Scenario | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Adults =65 (no prior PCV)** | **PCV20 alone** OR **PCV15 followed by PPSV23 =1 year later** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/pneumo/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **Adults =65 (prior PPSV23 only)** | **PCV15 or PCV20 =1 year after PPSV23** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/pneumo/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **Adults 19-64 with risk factors** | **PCV20 alone** OR **PCV15 followed by PPSV23 =8 weeks later** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/pneumo/hcp/recommendations.html |

## HPV Vaccine Recommendations
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Routine Age** | **11-12 years** (can start at age 9) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hpv/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **Catch-Up** | **Through age 26** (if not adequately vaccinated) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hpv/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **Shared Decision 27-45** | Discuss with provider (most benefit if not previously exposed) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hpv/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **Dosing** | **2 doses** if started before age 15 (0, 6-12 months); **3 doses** if started at =15 or immunocompromised (0, 1-2, 6 months) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hpv/hcp/recommendations.html |

## Vaccine Contraindications and Precautions
| Condition | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Severe allergic reaction to prior dose or vaccine component** | **Contraindication** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/hcp/acip-recs/general-recs/contraindications.html |
| **Pregnancy** | **Avoid live vaccines** (MMR, Varicella, LAIV) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/hcp/acip-recs/general-recs/contraindications.html |
| **Immunocompromise** | **Avoid live vaccines**; May need **additional doses** or **higher titers** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/hcp/acip-recs/general-recs/contraindications.html |
| **Moderate-severe acute illness** | **Precaution** (defer until improved) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/hcp/acip-recs/general-recs/contraindications.html |
| **Recent antibody-containing product** | **Defer live vaccines** (interval varies by product) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/hcp/acip-recs/general-recs/contraindications.html |
