## Testosterone Deficiency Screening
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Routine Screening** | **Not recommended** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/testosterone-deficiency |
| **When to Test** | **Symptoms of hypogonadism** (? libido, ED, Fatigue, Depression, ? Muscle mass, ? Body fat) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/testosterone-deficiency |
| **Testing Protocol** | **Morning total testosterone** (8-10 AM) on **2 separate occasions** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/testosterone-deficiency |
| **Diagnostic Threshold** | **<300 ng/dL** (with symptoms) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/testosterone-deficiency |

## Erectile Dysfunction as Sentinel Marker
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Significance** | **ED is a marker for underlying CVD** (often precedes CAD by 2-5 years) | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/erectile-dysfunction |
| **Evaluation** | **Cardiovascular risk assessment**, **Fasting glucose/HbA1c**, **Lipid panel**, **Morning testosterone** | https://www.auanet.org/guidelines/erectile-dysfunction |
