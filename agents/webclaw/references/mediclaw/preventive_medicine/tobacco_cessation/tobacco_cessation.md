## Pharmacotherapy for Tobacco Cessation
| Medication | Dose | Duration | Key Considerations | URL |
|------------|------|----------|-------------------|-----|
| **Nicotine Patch** | 21 mg/day (if >10 cig/day) | 8-12 weeks (taper) | **Start on quit date**; Rotate sites | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Nicotine Gum** | 2-4 mg q1-2h PRN | Up to 12 weeks | **Chew and park**; Avoid acidic drinks | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Nicotine Lozenge** | 2-4 mg q1-2h PRN | Up to 12 weeks | **Dissolve slowly**; Avoid acidic drinks | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Nicotine Inhaler** | 6-16 cartridges/day | Up to 6 months | **Prescription only** | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Nicotine Nasal Spray** | 1-2 doses/h | 3-6 months | **Prescription only**; Rapid absorption | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |
| **Varenicline** | 0.5 mg daily x3d ? 0.5 mg BID x4d ? 1 mg BID | 12-24 weeks | **Start 1 week before quit date**; Nausea, Vivid dreams | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/varenicline |
| **Bupropion SR** | 150 mg daily x3d ? 150 mg BID | 12-24 weeks | **Start 1-2 weeks before quit date**; Seizure risk, Contraindicated in eating disorders | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/bupropion |
| **Combination NRT** | Patch + PRN gum/lozenge | As above | **More effective than single NRT** | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/campaign/tips/quit-smoking/index.html |

## Varenicline Safety
| Concern | Recommendation | URL |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Neuropsychiatric Events** | **No clear causal association**; Monitor for mood changes | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/varenicline |
| **Cardiovascular Events** | **Small increased risk**; Weigh against benefits of quitting | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/varenicline |
| **Alcohol Interaction** | **Reduce alcohol**; May ? tolerance | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/varenicline |
| **Seizures** | **Caution** in patients with seizure history | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/varenicline |

## E-Cigarettes and Vaping
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **For Cessation** | **Not FDA-approved**; May help some adults quit but evidence limited | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/index.htm |
| **Dual Use** | **Avoid** (continuing both cigarettes and e-cigarettes) | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/index.htm |
| **Youth** | **Strongly discourage** (nicotine harms developing brain) | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/index.htm |
| **EVALI** | **E-cigarette or Vaping Associated Lung Injury** (associated with THC-containing products, Vitamin E acetate) | https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/basic_information/e-cigarettes/severe-lung-disease.html |
