## Preconception Care
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Folic Acid** | **400-800 mcg/day** (prevent neural tube defects) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/folicacid/recommendations.html |
| **Immunizations** | Ensure **MMR**, **Varicella**, **Tdap**, **Hepatitis B**, **Influenza**, **COVID-19** up to date | https://www.cdc.gov/preconception/index.html |
| **Chronic Disease Management** | Optimize control of **diabetes**, **hypertension**, **thyroid disease**, **seizures**, **mental health** | https://www.cdc.gov/preconception/index.html |
| **Medication Review** | Avoid **teratogens** (ACEi, ARBs, Valproate, Warfarin, Isotretinoin) | https://www.cdc.gov/preconception/index.html |
| **Substance Use** | **Stop tobacco**, **Alcohol**, **Illicit drugs** | https://www.cdc.gov/preconception/index.html |
| **Genetic Carrier Screening** | Offer **CF**, **Hemoglobinopathies**, **Tay-Sachs**, **Spinal muscular atrophy** based on ancestry/family history | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
