# Preventive Medicine - Alcohol and Substance Screening

## Alcohol Screening and Brief Intervention
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **USPSTF Grade** | **B** (Adults =18, including pregnant women) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/unhealthy-alcohol-use-in-adolescents-and-adults-screening-and-behavioral-counseling-interventions |
| **Screening Frequency** | **Annually** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/unhealthy-alcohol-use-in-adolescents-and-adults-screening-and-behavioral-counseling-interventions |
| **Single-Question Screen** | **"How many times in the past year have you had 5+ (men) or 4+ (women) drinks in a day?"** (=1 = positive) | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/alcohol-health/screening-and-assessment-tools |
| **AUDIT-C** | 3-item validated screen | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/alcohol-health/screening-and-assessment-tools |
| **Low-Risk Drinking Limits** | Men: =14 drinks/week AND =4 drinks/day; Women: =7 drinks/week AND =3 drinks/day | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov |

## AUDIT-C Scoring
| Score | Interpretation | Action | URL |
|-------|----------------|--------|-----|
| **Women: 0-2; Men: 0-3** | **Low risk** | Positive reinforcement | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/alcohol-health/screening-and-assessment-tools |
| **Women: 3-7; Men: 4-9** | **At-risk drinking** | **Brief intervention** | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/alcohol-health/screening-and-assessment-tools |
| **Women: =8; Men: =10** | **High probability of AUD** | **Full assessment** (AUDIT), Consider treatment | https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/alcohol-health/screening-and-assessment-tools |

## Brief Intervention for Unhealthy Alcohol Use
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Feedback** | Provide **personalized feedback** about drinking compared to norms | https://www.samhsa.gov/sbirt |
| **Advice** | Give **clear advice to cut down or abstain** | https://www.samhsa.gov/sbirt |
| **Goal Setting** | Help patient set **specific drinking goal** | https://www.samhsa.gov/sbirt |
| **Follow-Up** | Schedule follow-up to assess progress | https://www.samhsa.gov/sbirt |

## Drug Screening
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **USPSTF Grade** | **I** (insufficient evidence for routine screening) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/drug-use-illicit-screening |
| **When to Screen** | **Clinical suspicion**, **Risk factors** (mental health disorders, Chronic pain, History of SUD), **Pregnancy**, **Adolescents** | https://www.samhsa.gov/sbirt |
| **Screening Tools** | **DAST-10** (Drug Abuse Screening Test), **ASSIST**, **Single question** ("How many times in the past year have you used an illegal drug or prescription medication for non-medical reasons?") | https://www.drugabuse.gov |
| **Urine Drug Testing** | **Not for routine screening**; Use for monitoring treatment, Clinical suspicion | https://www.samhsa.gov/workplace/drug-testing-resources |

## Naloxone Co-Prescribing
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Indications** | **High-dose opioids** (=50 MME/day), **Concurrent benzodiazepines**, **History of overdose**, **History of SUD**, **Medical conditions increasing overdose risk** (COPD, Sleep apnea) | https://www.cdc.gov/stop-overdose/caring/naloxone.html |
| **Formulations** | **Narcan Nasal Spray 4 mg**, **IM/IV naloxone** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/naloxone |
| **Patient Education** | **Signs of overdose** (unresponsiveness, Slow/irregular breathing, Pinpoint pupils), **Call 911**, **Administer naloxone**, **Stay until help arrives** | https://www.cdc.gov/stop-overdose/caring/naloxone.html |
