## ASCVD Risk Assessment
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **ASCVD Risk Estimator Plus** | Estimates **10-year ASCVD risk** for adults 40-79 | https://tools.acc.org/ASCVD-Risk-Estimator-Plus |
| **Risk Categories** | **Low** (<5%), **Borderline** (5-7.4%), **Intermediate** (7.5-19.9%), **High** (=20%) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/cholesterol |
| **Risk-Enhancing Factors** | Family history of premature ASCVD, Chronic inflammatory conditions, South Asian ancestry, CKD, Metabolic syndrome, Preeclampsia, Premature menopause, High-risk biomarkers (hs-CRP =2, Lp(a) =50, apoB =130) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/cholesterol |
| **Coronary Artery Calcium (CAC)** | **CAC = 0**: Low risk, May defer statin; **CAC =100**: High risk, Consider statin | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/cholesterol |
