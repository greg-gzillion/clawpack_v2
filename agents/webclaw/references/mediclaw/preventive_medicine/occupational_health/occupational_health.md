## Occupational Health Screenings
| Screening | Population | Frequency | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Tuberculosis** | **Healthcare workers**, Correctional workers, Homeless shelter workers | Baseline, Then per risk | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/testing/healthcareworkers.htm |
| **Hepatitis B** | Healthcare workers, First responders | **Vaccination + Titer** 1-2 months post-series | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hepb/index.html |
| **Lead** | Construction, Battery manufacturing, Painting | Per OSHA standard | https://www.osha.gov/lead |
| **Asbestos** | Construction, Shipyard, Insulation workers | Per OSHA standard | https://www.osha.gov/asbestos |
| **Silica** | Construction, Mining, Foundry workers | Per OSHA standard | https://www.osha.gov/silica |
| **Hearing Conservation** | Noise exposure =85 dBA | **Annual audiometry** | https://www.osha.gov/noise |
| **Respirator Clearance** | Workers required to wear respirators | **Initial and annual** medical evaluation | https://www.osha.gov/respiratory-protection |

## Bloodborne Pathogens Standard
| Component | Requirement | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Exposure Control Plan** | Written plan to eliminate/minimize exposure | https://www.osha.gov/bloodborne-pathogens |
| **Universal Precautions** | Treat all blood/body fluids as infectious | https://www.osha.gov/bloodborne-pathogens |
| **Hepatitis B Vaccine** | **Offer at no cost** to all employees with occupational exposure | https://www.osha.gov/bloodborne-pathogens |
| **Post-Exposure Prophylaxis** | **Immediate evaluation** and treatment | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/bbp/pep.html |

## Work-Related Injuries and Illnesses
| Condition | Risk Factors | Prevention | URL |
|-----------|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Musculoskeletal Disorders** | Repetitive motion, Awkward posture, Forceful exertion | **Ergonomics**, **Job rotation**, **Stretching** | https://www.osha.gov/ergonomics |
| **Carpal Tunnel Syndrome** | Repetitive hand/wrist motion, Vibration | Ergonomic tools, Wrist splints, Breaks | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/ergonomics |
| **Occupational Asthma** | Isocyanates, Flour dust, Latex, Animal dander | **Engineering controls**, **PPE**, **Medical surveillance** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/asthma |
| **Contact Dermatitis** | Irritants (solvents, detergents), Allergens (latex, nickel) | **Gloves**, **Barrier creams**, **Substitution** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/skin |
| **Noise-Induced Hearing Loss** | Noise =85 dBA | **Hearing protection**, **Engineering controls**, **Audiometry** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/noise |
