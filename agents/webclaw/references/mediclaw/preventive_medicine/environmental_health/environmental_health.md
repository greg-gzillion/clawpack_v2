# Preventive Medicine - Environmental Health

## Common Environmental Exposures
| Exposure | Sources | Health Effects | Prevention | URL |
|----------|---------|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Lead** | Old paint, Contaminated soil, Water pipes | **Neurodevelopmental delay** (children), **Hypertension**, **Anemia**, **Nephropathy** | **Abatement**, **Water filters**, **Nutrition** (calcium, iron) | https://www.cdc.gov/nceh/lead |
| **Radon** | Soil gas (basements) | **Lung cancer** (second leading cause) | **Testing**, **Mitigation** | https://www.epa.gov/radon |
| **Carbon Monoxide** | Incomplete combustion (furnaces, Generators, Cars) | **Headache**, **Dizziness**, **Confusion**, **Death** | **CO detectors**, **Proper ventilation** | https://www.cdc.gov/co |
| **Mold** | Damp indoor environments | **Allergic symptoms**, **Asthma exacerbation** | **Moisture control**, **Remediation** | https://www.cdc.gov/mold |
| **Asbestos** | Insulation, Floor tiles, Roofing | **Mesothelioma**, **Lung cancer**, **Asbestosis** | **Professional abatement** | https://www.epa.gov/asbestos |
| **Pesticides** | Agricultural, Residential use | **Neurotoxicity**, **Endocrine disruption**, **Cancer** | **Integrated pest management**, **PPE** | https://www.epa.gov/pesticides |
| **Air Pollution** | Particulate matter, Ozone, NO2 | **Respiratory disease**, **Cardiovascular disease**, **Mortality** | **Air quality alerts**, **Reduce outdoor activity** | https://www.airnow.gov |
