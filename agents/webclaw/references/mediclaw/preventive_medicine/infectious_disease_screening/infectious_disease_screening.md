# Preventive Medicine - Infectious Disease Screening

## USPSTF Infectious Disease Screening Recommendations
| Disease | Population | Test | Grade | URL |
|---------|------------|------|-------|-----|
| **HIV** | **Adults 15-65** (and all pregnant, and older/younger at risk) | Antigen/antibody immunoassay | **A** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/human-immunodeficiency-virus-hiv-infection-screening |
| **Hepatitis C** | **Adults 18-79** (once) | **Anti-HCV** with reflex PCR | **B** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/hepatitis-c-screening |
| **Hepatitis B** | **High-risk** (endemic regions, HIV, IVDU, MSM, Household contacts), **All pregnant** | **HBsAg** | **B** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/hepatitis-b-virus-infection-screening |
| **Chlamydia/Gonorrhea** | **Sexually active women =24** (and older at risk) | **NAAT** (urine, cervical, vaginal) | **B** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/chlamydia-and-gonorrhea-screening |
| **Syphilis** | **High-risk** (MSM, HIV, Prior syphilis, Incarcerated, Endemic areas), **All pregnant** | **RPR/VDRL** with confirmatory treponemal test | **A** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/syphilis-infection-in-nonpregnant-adults-and-adolescents-screening |
| **Latent Tuberculosis** | **High-risk** (birth in high-prevalence country, Homeless, Incarcerated, HIV, Immunocompromised) | **IGRA** (QuantiFERON, T-SPOT) or **TST** | **B** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/latent-tuberculosis-infection-screening |
| **Cervical HPV** | **Women 30-65** (as part of cervical cancer screening) | **HPV DNA test** (alone or with Pap) | **A** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/cervical-cancer-screening |
| **Genital Herpes** | **Asymptomatic adults** | Serology | **D** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/genital-herpes-screening |
| **Bacterial Vaginosis** | **Asymptomatic pregnant women** (not at high risk for preterm delivery) | Gram stain, Amsel criteria | **D** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/bacterial-vaginosis-in-pregnancy-screening |

## HIV Screening Details
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Screening Interval** | **Once** for most; **Annual or more frequent** for high-risk | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/testing/index.html |
| **High-Risk Groups** | MSM, IVDU, Multiple partners, Sex workers, Partners of HIV+ | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/testing/index.html |
| **Opt-Out Testing** | **Recommended** in healthcare settings | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/testing/index.html |
| **PrEP** | **Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis** for high-risk HIV-negative individuals | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/risk/prep/index.html |

## Hepatitis C Screening Details
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Screening Frequency** | **Once** for all adults 18-79; **Periodic** for ongoing risk | https://www.cdc.gov/hepatitis/hcv/guidelinesc.htm |
| **Ongoing Risk Groups** | IVDU (ever or current), Hemodialysis, HIV+, Unregulated tattoos | https://www.cdc.gov/hepatitis/hcv/guidelinesc.htm |
| **Confirmatory Testing** | **HCV RNA PCR** for positive anti-HCV | https://www.cdc.gov/hepatitis/hcv/guidelinesc.htm |
| **Treatment** | **Direct-acting antivirals (DAAs)** cure >95% | https://www.hcvguidelines.org |

## Tuberculosis Screening Details
| Test | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **IGRA (QuantiFERON-TB Gold, T-SPOT.TB)** | **Preferred** for most (no cross-reactivity with BCG) | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/testing/igra.htm |
| **TST (Tuberculin Skin Test)** | Requires **return visit** 48-72h; **Cross-reactivity with BCG** | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/testing/tbtesttypes.htm |
| **Positive TST Threshold** | **=5 mm**: HIV, Immunocompromised, Recent contact; **=10 mm**: High-risk (immigrants, IVDU, Healthcare); **=15 mm**: Low-risk | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/testing/tbtesttypes.htm |
| **LTBI Treatment** | **Rifampin x4 months** (preferred), **Isoniazid x9 months**, **Rifapentine + Isoniazid x3 months** (weekly) | https://www.cdc.gov/tb/topic/treatment/ltbi.htm |
