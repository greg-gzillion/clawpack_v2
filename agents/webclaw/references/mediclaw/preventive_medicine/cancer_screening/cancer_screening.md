## Colorectal Cancer Screening Options
| Test | Frequency | Sensitivity for CRC | URL |
|------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Colonoscopy** | **Every 10 years** | **>95%** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/colorectal-cancer-screening |
| **FIT (Fecal Immunochemical Test)** | **Annually** | 74-80% | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/colorectal-cancer-screening |
| **FIT-DNA (Cologuard)** | **Every 1-3 years** | 92% | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/colorectal-cancer-screening |
| **Flexible Sigmoidoscopy** | **Every 5 years** (± FIT annually) | 95% (for reachable area) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/colorectal-cancer-screening |
| **CT Colonography** | **Every 5 years** | 88-92% | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/colorectal-cancer-screening |
| **gFOBT** | **Annually** | 50-70% | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/colorectal-cancer-screening |

## Lung Cancer Screening Eligibility (USPSTF 2021)
| Criterion | Requirement | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Age** | **50-80 years** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Smoking History** | **=20 pack-years** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Current Smoker** | Eligible | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Former Smoker** | **Quit within past 15 years** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |
| **Discontinue Screening** | **Quit >15 years ago**, **Develops health problem limiting life expectancy** or ability for curative surgery | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/lung-cancer-screening |

## Lung-RADS (ACR)
| Category | Description | Management | URL |
|----------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **0** | Incomplete | Additional imaging needed | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/Lung-Rads |
| **1** | **Negative** | Continue annual screening | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/Lung-Rads |
| **2** | **Benign appearance** | Continue annual screening | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/Lung-Rads |
| **3** | **Probably benign** | **6-month follow-up CT** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/Lung-Rads |
| **4A** | **Suspicious** | **3-month follow-up CT** or PET/CT | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/Lung-Rads |
| **4B** | **Very suspicious** | **Tissue sampling** or PET/CT | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/Lung-Rads |
| **4X** | **Very suspicious with additional features** | **Tissue sampling** | https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/Lung-Rads |
