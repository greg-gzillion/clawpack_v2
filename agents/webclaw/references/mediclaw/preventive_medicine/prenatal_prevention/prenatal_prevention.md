# Preventive Medicine - Prenatal Prevention

## Prenatal Care Schedule (ACOG)
| Trimester | Key Components | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **First Trimester** | **Confirm pregnancy**, **Establish due date**, **Prenatal labs** (CBC, Blood type/Rh, Antibody screen, Rubella, Syphilis, Hepatitis B, HIV, Chlamydia/Gonorrhea, Urinalysis/Urine culture), **Genetic screening** (NIPT, Sequential screen), **Ultrasound** (dating/viability) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Second Trimester** | **Anatomy ultrasound** (18-22 weeks), **Diabetes screening** (24-28 weeks), **Tdap** (27-36 weeks), **Rhogam** (28 weeks if Rh-negative) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Third Trimester** | **GBS screening** (36-37 weeks), **RSV vaccine** (32-36 weeks, seasonal), **COVID-19**, **Influenza**, **Discuss labor/delivery plan** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |

## Prenatal Screening Tests
| Test | Timing | Purpose | URL |
|------|--------|---------|-----|
| **NIPT (cfDNA)** | =10 weeks | **Aneuploidy screening** (T21, T18, T13, Sex chromosomes) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Sequential/Integrated Screen** | First and second trimester | **Aneuploidy and neural tube defect screening** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Carrier Screening** | Preconception or early pregnancy | **CF**, **Hemoglobinopathies**, **Tay-Sachs**, **SMA**, **Fragile X** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Anatomy Ultrasound** | 18-22 weeks | **Fetal anatomy survey** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Diabetes Screening** | 24-28 weeks | **1-hour glucose challenge test** (=130-140 ? 3-hour OGTT) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **GBS Screening** | 36-37 weeks | **Vaginal-rectal culture** for Group B Streptococcus | https://www.cdc.gov/groupbstrep/index.html |

## Prenatal Immunizations
| Vaccine | Recommendation | URL |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Tdap** | **Every pregnancy** (27-36 weeks) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/pregnancy/hcp-toolkit/tdap-vaccine-pregnancy.html |
| **Influenza** | **Every pregnancy** (any trimester) | https://www.cdc.gov/flu/highrisk/pregnant.htm |
| **COVID-19** | **Every pregnancy** (any trimester) | https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/vaccines/recommendations/pregnancy.html |
| **RSV** | **32-36 weeks** (seasonal, September-January) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/rsv/public/pregnancy.html |
| **Hepatitis B** | If at risk or not previously vaccinated | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/pregnancy/hcp-toolkit/hepatitis-b-vaccine-pregnancy.html |

## Postpartum Care
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Depression Screening** | **PHQ-9** at comprehensive postpartum visit (4-12 weeks) | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Contraception** | Discuss and provide before discharge or at postpartum visit | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Breastfeeding Support** | Lactation consultant referral if needed | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
| **Chronic Disease Follow-Up** | Monitor for resolution of **gestational diabetes**, **Hypertensive disorders** | https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance |
