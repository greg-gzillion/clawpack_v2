# Preventive Medicine - Overview

## What is Preventive Medicine?
Preventive medicine is the medical specialty focused on protecting, promoting, and maintaining health and well-being and preventing disease, disability, and premature death. It encompasses primary, secondary, and tertiary prevention.

| Level | Definition | Examples | URL |
|-------|------------|----------|-----|
| **Primary Prevention** | **Prevent disease before it occurs** | Immunizations, Lifestyle counseling, Environmental interventions | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/prevention-taskforce |
| **Secondary Prevention** | **Detect disease early** (before symptoms) | **Screening tests** (mammography, colonoscopy, BP checks) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/prevention-taskforce |
| **Tertiary Prevention** | **Reduce complications** of established disease | Rehabilitation, Disease management programs, Palliative care | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/prevention-taskforce |

## USPSTF Grade Definitions
| Grade | Definition | Implication for Practice | URL |
|-------|------------|-------------------------|-----|
| **A** | High certainty of **substantial net benefit** | **Offer or provide this service** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **B** | High certainty of **moderate net benefit** OR moderate certainty of substantial net benefit | **Offer or provide this service** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **C** | Moderate certainty of **small net benefit** | **Offer selectively** based on professional judgment and patient preferences | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **D** | Moderate or high certainty of **no net benefit** or **harms outweigh benefits** | **Discourage use** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **I** | **Insufficient evidence** to assess balance of benefits and harms | **Clinical judgment**; Read clinical considerations | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
