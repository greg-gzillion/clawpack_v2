# Preventive Medicine - Nutrition and Obesity

## Obesity Screening and Management
| Component | Recommendation | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **USPSTF Grade** | **B** (screen all adults for obesity, Offer/refer intensive behavioral interventions) | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/obesity-in-adults-interventions |
| **Screening** | **BMI** at least annually | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/obesity-in-adults-interventions |
| **BMI Categories** | **Overweight**: 25-29.9; **Class 1 Obesity**: 30-34.9; **Class 2**: 35-39.9; **Class 3**: =40 | https://www.cdc.gov/obesity/basics/adult-defining.html |
| **Intensive Behavioral Therapy** | **=12-26 sessions/year** (Medicare covers 22 sessions) | https://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database |

## Dietary Guidelines for Americans (2020-2025)
| Recommendation | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Limit Added Sugars** | **<10% of calories** | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Limit Saturated Fat** | **<10% of calories** | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Limit Sodium** | **<2,300 mg/day** | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Limit Alcohol** | Men =2 drinks/day; Women =1 drink/day | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |
| **Encourage** | Vegetables, Fruits, Whole grains, Lean protein, Low-fat dairy, Healthy oils | https://www.dietaryguidelines.gov |

## Evidence-Based Dietary Patterns
| Diet | Description | Evidence | URL |
|------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Mediterranean Diet** | High in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, olive oil, fish, nuts; Low in red meat | **Strong**: CVD prevention, Diabetes, Cognitive health | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/heart-healthy-eating-plans/mediterranean-diet |
| **DASH Diet** | Low sodium, High potassium/magnesium/calcium | **Strong**: Hypertension | https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/education/dash-eating-plan |
| **Plant-Based Diet** | Emphasis on plant foods; May include limited animal products | **Moderate-Strong**: CVD, Diabetes, Weight | https://www.eatright.org/health/wellness/vegetarian-and-plant-based |
