|------------|-------------|-----|

## Amputation - Complications
| Complication | Description | Management | URL |
|--------------|-------------|------------|-----|
| **Phantom Limb Pain** | **Pain in amputated limb** | **Mirror therapy**, **Gabapentinoids**, **TCAs**, **TENS** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/phantom-limb-pain |
| **Neuroma** | **Painful nerve ending** | **Surgical revision**, **Targeted muscle reinnervation (TMR)** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/treatment/amputation-and-prosthetics/ |
| **Contracture** | **Joint stiffness** | **Physical therapy**, **Splinting** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/treatment/amputation-and-prosthetics/ |
| **Skin Breakdown** | **Pressure sores** from prosthesis | **Socket adjustment**, **Skin care** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/treatment/amputation-and-prosthetics/ |
