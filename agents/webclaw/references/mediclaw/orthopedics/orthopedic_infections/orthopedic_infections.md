# Orthopedics - Orthopedic Infections

## Osteomyelitis
| Type | Pathogenesis | Common Pathogens | Treatment | URL |
|------|--------------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Hematogenous** | **Bacteremia** ? Seeding of bone (children - metaphysis, Adults - vertebrae) | **S. aureus** (most common), **CoNS**, Gram-negatives, **Salmonella** (SCD) | **IV antibiotics** x 4-6 weeks ｱ **Surgical debridement** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteomyelitis |
| **Contiguous** | Spread from adjacent infection (diabetic foot, Pressure ulcer, Trauma) | **Polymicrobial** (S. aureus, Gram-negatives, Anaerobes) | **Surgical debridement** + **IV antibiotics** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteomyelitis |

## Septic Arthritis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pathogens** | **S. aureus** (most common), **Streptococci**, **N. gonorrhoeae** (young adults), Gram-negatives | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/infectious-arthritis |
| **Risk Factors** | **Rheumatoid arthritis**, **Prosthetic joint**, **IVDU**, **Diabetes**, **Immunosuppression**, **Recent joint injection/surgery** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/infectious-arthritis |
| **Diagnosis** | **Arthrocentesis**: WBC >50,000, PMN >90%, Gram stain, Culture; **Blood cultures** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/infectious-arthritis |
| **Treatment** | **Empiric IV antibiotics** (Vancomycin + Ceftriaxone), **Drainage** (serial aspiration, Arthroscopy, Arthrotomy) | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/infectious-arthritis |

## Prosthetic Joint Infection (PJI)
| Classification | Timing | Pathogens | Treatment | URL |
|----------------|--------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Early** | <3 months | S. aureus, Gram-negatives | **DAIR** (Debridement, Antibiotics, Implant Retention) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/joint-replacement-infection/ |
| **Delayed** | 3-12 months | CoNS, S. aureus | **One-stage** or **Two-stage exchange** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/joint-replacement-infection/ |
| **Late** | >12 months | Hematogenous seeding (S. aureus, Streptococci) | **DAIR** (if acute), **Two-stage exchange** (if chronic) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/joint-replacement-infection/ |

## Two-Stage Exchange for PJI
| Stage | Procedure | Duration | URL |
|-------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Stage 1** | **Removal of prosthesis** + **Aggressive debridement** + **Placement of antibiotic spacer** | **6-8 weeks** of IV antibiotics | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/joint-replacement-infection/ |
| **Antibiotic Holiday** | **Hold antibiotics** for 2-4 weeks | **2-4 weeks** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/joint-replacement-infection/ |
| **Stage 2** | **Reimplantation of prosthesis** (if CRP/ESR normalized, Negative cultures) | - | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/joint-replacement-infection/ |
