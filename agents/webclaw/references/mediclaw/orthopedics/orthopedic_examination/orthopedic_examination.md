## Shoulder Examination
| Test | Technique | Positive Finding | Indicates | URL |
|------|-----------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Neer Impingement** | Passive forward flexion with scapula stabilized | **Pain** | **Subacromial impingement** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Hawkins-Kennedy** | Forward flexion to 90ｰ, Internal rotation | **Pain** | **Subacromial impingement** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Empty Can (Jobe)** | Arm elevated 90ｰ in scapular plane, Thumbs down, Resist downward force | **Pain or weakness** | **Supraspinatus tear/tendinopathy** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Drop Arm** | Passively abduct to 90ｰ, Patient lowers slowly | **Unable to lower smoothly (arm drops)** | **Rotator cuff tear** (supraspinatus) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Lift-Off (Gerber)** | Hand behind back, Push against resistance | **Weakness** | **Subscapularis tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Belly Press** | Press palm against abdomen, Keep elbow forward | **Elbow drops back** | **Subscapularis tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Apprehension** | Abduction + External rotation | **Apprehension or pain** | **Anterior shoulder instability** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Sulcus Sign** | Downward traction on arm | **Sulcus below acromion** | **Inferior shoulder instability** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Speed Test** | Forward flexion against resistance, Palm up | **Pain in bicipital groove** | **Biceps tendinopathy** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **Yergason Test** | Elbow flexed 90ｰ, Supination against resistance | **Pain in bicipital groove** | **Biceps tendinopathy** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |
| **O'Brien Test** | Arm flexed 90ｰ, Adducted 15ｰ, Thumb down, Resist downward force | **Pain relieved with palm up** | **SLAP tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/shoulder-pain-and-common-shoulder-problems/ |

## Knee Examination
| Test | Technique | Positive Finding | Indicates | URL |
|------|-----------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **Lachman** | Knee flexed 20-30ｰ, Anterior translation of tibia | **Increased translation**, **Soft endpoint** | **ACL tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **Anterior Drawer** | Knee flexed 90ｰ, Anterior translation of tibia | **Increased translation** | **ACL tear** (less sensitive than Lachman) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **Pivot Shift** | Valgus + Internal rotation + Extension ? Flexion | **"Clunk"** at 20-30ｰ flexion | **ACL tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **Posterior Drawer** | Knee flexed 90ｰ, Posterior translation of tibia | **Increased posterior translation** | **PCL tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **Valgus Stress** | Valgus force at 0ｰ and 30ｰ flexion | **Opening at 0ｰ** (MCL + cruciates), **Opening at 30ｰ only** (MCL) | **MCL tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **Varus Stress** | Varus force at 0ｰ and 30ｰ flexion | **Opening** | **LCL tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **McMurray** | Flexion + External rotation + Varus ? Extension (medial); Flexion + Internal rotation + Valgus ? Extension (lateral) | **Click or pain** | **Meniscal tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **Apley Grind** | Prone, Knee flexed 90ｰ, Compression + Rotation | **Pain** | **Meniscal tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |
| **Patellar Apprehension** | Lateral pressure on patella with knee flexed | **Apprehension or quadriceps contraction** | **Patellar instability** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/knee-pain/ |

| **FADIR** | Flexion, ADduction, Internal Rotation | **Anterior groin pain** | **Femoroacetabular impingement (FAI)** , **Labral tear** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hip-pain/ |

| **Trendelenburg** | Stand on one leg | **Pelvis drops on contralateral side** | **Gluteus medius weakness** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hip-pain/ |
