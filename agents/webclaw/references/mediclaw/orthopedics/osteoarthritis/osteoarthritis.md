# Orthopedics - Osteoarthritis (OA)

## Osteoarthritis - Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Degenerative joint disease** characterized by **cartilage loss**, **Subchondral sclerosis**, **Osteophyte formation** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |
| **Epidemiology** | **Most common form of arthritis**; Affects 30 million US adults | https://www.cdc.gov/arthritis/basics/osteoarthritis.htm |
| **Risk Factors** | **Age**, **Obesity**, **Genetics**, **Female**, **Joint injury**, **Occupation**, **Malalignment** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |

## OA - Common Sites and Management
| Joint | Non-Operative | Operative | URL |
|-------|---------------|-----------|-----|
| **Knee** | Weight loss, NSAIDs, Physical therapy, Bracing, Intra-articular corticosteroids, Viscosupplementation | **Total Knee Arthroplasty (TKA)** , **Unicompartmental Knee Arthroplasty (UKA)** , **High Tibial Osteotomy (HTO)** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/osteoarthritis-of-the-knee/ |
| **Hip** | Weight loss, NSAIDs, Physical therapy, Intra-articular corticosteroids | **Total Hip Arthroplasty (THA)** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/osteoarthritis-of-the-hip/ |
| **Hand** | NSAIDs, Splinting, Intra-articular corticosteroids | **Arthroplasty** or **Arthrodesis** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/osteoarthritis-of-the-hand/ |
| **Spine** | NSAIDs, Physical therapy, Epidural steroid injections | **Decompression + Fusion** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/osteoarthritis-of-the-spine/ |

## OA - Pharmacologic Management
| Drug Class | Examples | Indications | URL |
|------------|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Acetaminophen** | Tylenol | Mild pain | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |
| **Topical NSAIDs** | Diclofenac gel | **First-line** for knee/hand OA | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |
| **Oral NSAIDs** | Ibuprofen, Naproxen, Celecoxib | Moderate-severe pain; **Lowest effective dose** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |
| **Intra-articular Corticosteroids** | Triamcinolone, Methylprednisolone | **Short-term relief** of flares | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |
| **Viscosupplementation** | Hyaluronic acid injections | Controversial; **Not routinely recommended** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |
| **Duloxetine** | Cymbalta | **Chronic OA pain** (especially with central sensitization) | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoarthritis |
