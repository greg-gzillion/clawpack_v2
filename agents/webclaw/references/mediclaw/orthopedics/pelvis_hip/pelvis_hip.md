# Orthopedics - Pelvis and Hip

## Pelvic Ring Fractures
| Type | Description | Stability | Treatment | URL |
|------|-------------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Lateral Compression (LC)** | **Internal rotation** of hemipelvis | Usually stable | **Protected weight-bearing** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Anteroposterior Compression (APC)** | **External rotation** ? **Open book** | **Unstable** | **Pelvic binder** ? **ORIF** or **Sacroiliac screw** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Vertical Shear (VS)** | **Vertical displacement** | **Unstable** | **ORIF** (anterior and posterior) | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Combined Mechanism (CM)** | Combination | **Unstable** | **ORIF** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |

## Acetabular Fracture
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Mechanism** | **High-energy trauma** (MVC, Fall from height) | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Classification (Letournel)** | **Elementary** (posterior wall, Posterior column, Anterior wall, Anterior column, Transverse) or **Associated** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Treatment** | **ORIF** for displaced (>2 mm), Unstable, Incongruent | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Complications** | **Post-traumatic arthritis**, **AVN of femoral head**, **Sciatic nerve injury** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |

## Hip Fractures
| Type | Location | Blood Supply | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Femoral Neck** | **Intracapsular** | **Tenous** (medial circumflex femoral artery) ? **High AVN risk** | **Non-Displaced**: **Cannulated screws**; **Displaced** (elderly): **Hemiarthroplasty** or **THA**; **Displaced** (young): **ORIF** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hip-fractures/ |
| **Intertrochanteric** | **Extracapsular** | **Good** (low AVN risk) | **Cephalomedullary nail** or **Sliding hip screw** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hip-fractures/ |
| **Subtrochanteric** | **Extracapsular** | Good | **Cephalomedullary nail** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/hip-fractures/ |

## Hip Dislocation
| Type | Frequency | Mechanism | Associated Injuries | URL |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Posterior** | **90%** | **Dashboard injury** (flexed, Adducted knee) | **Posterior wall acetabular fracture**, **Sciatic nerve injury** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-hip/ |
| **Anterior** | 10% | **Abduction + External rotation** | **Femoral head impaction** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-hip/ |
| **Management** | **Emergent closed reduction** (<6 hours to prevent AVN), **Post-reduction CT** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-hip/ |
