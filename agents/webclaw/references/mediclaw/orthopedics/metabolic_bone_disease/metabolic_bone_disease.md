# Orthopedics - Metabolic Bone Disease

## Osteoporosis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Decreased bone mass and microarchitectural deterioration** ? **Increased fracture risk** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoporosis |
| **Diagnosis** | **DEXA**: **T-score = -2.5** = Osteoporosis; **-1.0 to -2.5** = Osteopenia | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoporosis |
| **Screening** | **Women =65**, **Men =70**, Younger if risk factors | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/recommendation/osteoporosis-screening |
| **Treatment** | **Bisphosphonates** (alendronate, Risedronate, Zoledronic acid), **Denosumab**, **Teriparatide**, **Abaloparatide**, **Romosozumab** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/osteoporosis/treatment |

## Paget Disease of Bone
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Focal disorder of bone remodeling** ? **Enlarged**, **Deformed**, **Weak bones** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/pagets-disease-bone |
| **Symptoms** | **Bone pain**, **Deformity** (bowing of tibia, Skull enlargement), **Hearing loss**, **Fracture**, **High-output heart failure** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/pagets-disease-bone |
| **Diagnosis** | **X-ray** (coarsened trabeculae, Cortical thickening, Deformity), **Elevated alkaline phosphatase** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/pagets-disease-bone |
| **Treatment** | **Bisphosphonates** (zoledronic acid preferred), **Calcitonin** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/pagets-disease-bone |
