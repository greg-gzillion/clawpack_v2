## Post-Operative Rehabilitation Protocols
| Procedure | Weight-Bearing | ROM Progression | Return to Sport | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|-----|
| **ACL Reconstruction** | **WBAT** with brace | Early full extension, Progressive flexion | **9-12 months** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/recovery/acl-reconstruction-rehab/ |
| **Rotator Cuff Repair** | - | **Passive only** x 4-6 weeks, **Active** after 6 weeks | **6-9 months** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/recovery/rotator-cuff-repair-rehab/ |
| **Total Hip Arthroplasty** | **WBAT** (posterior precautions) | No hip flexion >90°, No internal rotation/adduction | **3-6 months** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/recovery/total-hip-replacement-rehab/ |
| **Total Knee Arthroplasty** | **WBAT** | Early motion, Emphasis on extension | **3-6 months** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/recovery/total-knee-replacement-rehab/ |
