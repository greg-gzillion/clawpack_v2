# Orthopedics - Orthopedic Imaging

## Imaging Modalities
| Modality | Principle | Best For | URL |
|----------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **X-Ray (Radiography)** | Differential absorption of X-rays | **Fractures**, **Arthritis**, **Alignment**, **Bone tumors** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/x-rays |
| **CT** | Cross-sectional X-ray imaging | **Complex fractures**, **Pre-operative planning**, **Spinal pathology** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/computed-tomography-ct |
| **MRI** | Magnetic field and radio waves | **Soft tissue** (ligaments, Tendons, Menisci, Labrum, Discs, Cartilage, Bone marrow) | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/magnetic-resonance-imaging-mri |
| **Ultrasound** | High-frequency sound waves | **Tendons**, **Ligaments**, **Fluid collections**, **Foreign bodies**, **Dynamic assessment** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/ultrasound |
| **Bone Scan** | Radiotracer (Tc-99m MDP) | **Metastases**, **Infection**, **Stress fractures**, **Paget disease** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/nuclear-medicine |
| **DEXA** | Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry | **Bone mineral density** (Osteoporosis) | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/bone-mineral-density-tests |

## Fracture Healing - Stages
| Stage | Timeframe | Process | URL |
|-------|-----------|---------|-----|
| **Hematoma Formation** | Hours-days | Blood clot, Inflammatory response | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/bone-health |
| **Soft Callus Formation** | Days-weeks | Fibrocartilage, Collagen | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/bone-health |

## Salter-Harris Classification (Pediatric Physeal Fractures)
| Type | Description | Prognosis | URL |
|------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Type I** | **Straight across physis** | **Good** | https://posna.org/Physician-Education/Study-Guide/Salter-Harris-Fractures |
| **Type II** | **Above** (metaphysis + physis) - **Most common** | **Good** | https://posna.org/Physician-Education/Study-Guide/Salter-Harris-Fractures |
| **Type III** | **Lower** (epiphysis + physis) - **Intra-articular** | **Fair** (risk of growth arrest) | https://posna.org/Physician-Education/Study-Guide/Salter-Harris-Fractures |
| **Type IV** | **Through** (metaphysis + physis + epiphysis) - **Intra-articular** | **Poor** (high risk of growth arrest) | https://posna.org/Physician-Education/Study-Guide/Salter-Harris-Fractures |
| **Type V** | **Crush** injury to physis | **Poor** (growth arrest) | https://posna.org/Physician-Education/Study-Guide/Salter-Harris-Fractures |

## Gustilo-Anderson Classification (Open Fractures)
| Type | Wound Size | Soft Tissue | Treatment | URL |
|------|------------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Type I** | <1 cm | Minimal contamination | **Cefazolin** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Type II** | 1-10 cm | Moderate soft tissue injury | **Cefazolin** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Type IIIA** | >10 cm | Adequate soft tissue coverage | **Cefazolin + Gentamicin** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Type IIIB** | >10 cm | Inadequate soft tissue coverage (requires flap) | **Cefazolin + Gentamicin** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Type IIIC** | Any | **Vascular injury requiring repair** | **Cefazolin + Gentamicin + Penicillin** | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
