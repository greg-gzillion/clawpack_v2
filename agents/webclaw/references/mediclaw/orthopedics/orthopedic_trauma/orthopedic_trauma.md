## Damage Control Orthopedics (DCO)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indications** | **Hemodynamically unstable**, **Hypothermic** (<35°C), **Coagulopathic** (INR >1.5), **Acidotic** (pH <7.2) | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Initial Management** | **External fixation** of long bone fractures, **Pelvic binder/ex-fix** for unstable pelvis | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |
| **Definitive Fixation** | **Delayed** until physiologically stable (24-72 hours or longer) | https://ota.org/for-patients/fracture-information |

## Fat Embolism Syndrome
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Systemic inflammatory response** to fat globules in circulation | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/fat-embolism-syndrome/ |
| **Risk Factors** | **Long bone fractures** (femur, Tibia), **Pelvic fractures**, **Multiple fractures** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/fat-embolism-syndrome/ |
| **Clinical Triad** | **Respiratory distress**, **Neurologic symptoms** (confusion, Coma), **Petechial rash** (axillae, Conjunctiva, Neck) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/fat-embolism-syndrome/ |
| **Treatment** | **Supportive** (oxygen, Mechanical ventilation, Fluid management) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/fat-embolism-syndrome/ |
