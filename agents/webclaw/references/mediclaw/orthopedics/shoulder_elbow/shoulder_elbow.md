|------|-----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|

## Anterior Shoulder Dislocation - Management
| Phase | Intervention | URL |
|-------|--------------|-----|
| **Pre-Reduction** | **Neurovascular exam** (axillary nerve - deltoid, Lateral shoulder sensation), **X-ray** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-shoulder/ |
| **Reduction Techniques** | **Stimson**, **Cunningham**, **Scapular manipulation**, **Milch**, **Hennepin** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-shoulder/ |
| **Post-Reduction** | **Neurovascular exam**, **Post-reduction X-ray**, **Sling** | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-shoulder/ |
| **Young (<25)** | **Surgery** (Bankart repair) - reduces recurrence | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-shoulder/ |
| **Older (>40)** | **Non-operative** - lower recurrence; Assess rotator cuff | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/dislocated-shoulder/ |

|-----------|-------------|-----|
