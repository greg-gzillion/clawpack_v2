# Orthopedics - Overview

## What is Orthopedics?
Orthopedics is the medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation, and prevention of disorders of the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, muscles, and nerves.
