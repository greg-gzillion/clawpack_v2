# Orthopedics - Bone Tumors

## Benign Bone Tumors
| Tumor | Age | Location | Characteristics | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----|----------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Osteochondroma** | 10-30 | **Metaphysis** of long bones (distal femur, Proximal tibia, Proximal humerus) | **Most common benign tumor**; **Cartilage-capped bony projection** | **Observation**; **Excision** if symptomatic | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/bone-fact-sheet |
| **Enchondroma** | 20-50 | **Phalanges**, Metacarpals, Metatarsals | **Lytic**, **Chondroid matrix** (rings and arcs) | **Observation**; **Curettage + Bone graft** if symptomatic | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/bone-fact-sheet |
| **Osteoid Osteoma** | 5-25 | **Femur**, **Tibia**, Posterior spine | **Nocturnal pain relieved by NSAIDs**, **Radiolucent nidus** with surrounding sclerosis | **NSAIDs**, **Radiofrequency ablation** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/bone-fact-sheet |
| **Giant Cell Tumor** | 20-40 | **Epiphysis** of long bones (distal femur, Proximal tibia, Distal radius) | **Lytic**, **Soap-bubble appearance**, **Locally aggressive** | **Extended curettage + Adjuvant** (phenol, Cryotherapy) + **Cement** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/hp/giant-cell-treatment-pdq |
| **Chondroblastoma** | 10-25 | **Epiphysis** | **Pain**, **Effusion** | **Curettage + Bone graft** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/bone-fact-sheet |
| **Aneurysmal Bone Cyst** | 10-30 | Metaphysis | **Expansile**, **Fluid-fluid levels** on MRI | **Curettage + Bone graft** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/bone-fact-sheet |
| **Fibrous Dysplasia** | 10-30 | Femur, Tibia, Ribs, Skull | **Ground-glass matrix**, **Shepherd crook deformity** (femur) | **Observation**, **Bisphosphonates**, **Surgery** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/fibrous-dysplasia |

## Malignant Bone Tumors
| Tumor | Age | Location | Characteristics | Treatment | URL |
|-------|-----|----------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Osteosarcoma** | 10-25 (primary), >60 (secondary) | **Metaphysis** of long bones (distal femur, Proximal tibia, Proximal humerus) | **Most common primary malignant bone tumor**, **Codman triangle**, **Sunburst pattern** | **Neoadjuvant chemotherapy** ? **Limb salvage surgery** ? Adjuvant chemotherapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/hp/osteosarcoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Ewing Sarcoma** | 5-20 | **Diaphysis** of long bones, Flat bones | **Onion-skin periosteal reaction**, **t(11;22) translocation** | **Neoadjuvant chemotherapy** ? **Surgery/Radiation** ? Adjuvant chemotherapy | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/hp/ewing-treatment-pdq |
| **Chondrosarcoma** | 40-70 | Pelvis, Femur, Humerus, Ribs | **Chondroid matrix**, **Endosteal scalloping** | **Surgical resection** (chemotherapy/radiation resistant) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/hp/chondrosarcoma-treatment-pdq |
| **Chordoma** | 40-70 | **Sacrum**, Clivus, Mobile spine | **Lobulated**, **Physaliphorous cells** | **En bloc resection** + **Radiation** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/bone/hp/chordoma-treatment-pdq |

## Metastatic Bone Disease
| Primary Cancer | Characteristics | Treatment | URL |
|----------------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Breast** | **Most common**; **Osteolytic** or **Osteoblastic** | **Systemic therapy**, **Bisphosphonates/Denosumab**, **Radiation**, **Surgery** (prophylactic fixation for impending fracture) | https://www.cancer.gov/types/metastatic-cancer |
| **Prostate** | **Osteoblastic** | **Androgen deprivation therapy**, **Bisphosphonates/Denosumab**, **Radiation**, **Radium-223** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/metastatic-cancer |
| **Lung** | **Osteolytic** | **Systemic therapy**, **Bisphosphonates/Denosumab**, **Radiation**, **Surgery** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/metastatic-cancer |
| **Renal** | **Osteolytic**, **Hypervascular** | **Systemic therapy** (immunotherapy, TKI), **Pre-operative embolization**, **Surgery** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/metastatic-cancer |
| **Thyroid** | **Osteolytic**, **Hypervascular** | **Radioactive iodine**, **Systemic therapy**, **Surgery** | https://www.cancer.gov/types/metastatic-cancer |
