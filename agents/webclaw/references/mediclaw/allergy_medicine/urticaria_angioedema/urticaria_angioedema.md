# Allergy Medicine - Urticaria and Angioedema

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Urticaria** | Pruritic, erythematous, raised wheals due to dermal edema | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/hives-urticaria |
| **Angioedema** | Deeper swelling of subcutaneous/submucosal tissue | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/angioedema |
| **Prevalence** | 15-25% lifetime prevalence of urticaria | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/hives-overview |

## Classification of Urticaria
| Type | Duration | Characteristics | URL |
|------|----------|-----------------|-----|
| **Acute Urticaria** | <6 weeks | Often trigger identifiable (infection, food, drug) | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/hives-urticaria |
| **Chronic Spontaneous Urticaria (CSU)** | >6 weeks | No identifiable trigger, Autoimmune in 40-50% | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/hives-urticaria |
| **Chronic Inducible Urticaria** | >6 weeks | Specific physical trigger | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/hives-urticaria |

## Angioedema Classification
| Type | Mechanism | Features | URL |
|------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Histaminergic** | Mast cell degranulation | Responds to antihistamines, Often with urticaria | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/angioedema |
| **Bradykinin-Mediated** | Excess bradykinin | **NO urticaria**, **NO response to antihistamines/epinephrine**, GI symptoms common | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/angioedema |

## Chronic Spontaneous Urticaria Treatment
| Step | Treatment | Notes | URL |
|------|-----------|-------|-----|
| **Step 1** | Second-generation H1 antihistamine | Up to 4x daily | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/hives-urticaria |
| **Step 2** | Add H2 antagonist or LTRA | Limited evidence | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/hives-urticaria |
| **Step 3** | **Omalizumab** (anti-IgE) | **FDA-approved** for CSU | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Step 4** | Cyclosporine | Off-label, Monitor renal function | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/hives-urticaria |
