## Major Primary Immunodeficiencies
| Disorder | Defect | Clinical Features | Diagnosis | Treatment | URL |
|----------|--------|-------------------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **X-Linked Agammaglobulinemia (XLA)** | BTK mutation, No B cells | Recurrent bacterial infections after 6 mo | ? All immunoglobulins, ? B cells | **IVIG/SCIG**, Antibiotics | https://primaryimmune.org/scid-compass |
| **Common Variable Immunodeficiency (CVID)** | Defective B cell differentiation | Recurrent sinopulmonary infections, Autoimmunity | ? IgG + ? IgA and/or IgM, Poor vaccine response | **IVIG/SCIG**, Monitor for complications | https://primaryimmune.org/scid-compass |
| **Selective IgA Deficiency** | Defective IgA production | Often asymptomatic, Sinopulmonary infections, Autoimmunity | IgA <7 mg/dL, Normal IgG/IgM | Antibiotics, **NO IVIG** (anaphylaxis risk) | https://primaryimmune.org/scid-compass |
| **Severe Combined Immunodeficiency (SCID)** | Multiple genetic defects, No T cells | **Early severe infections**, Failure to thrive, **Newborn screen** | Lymphopenia, ? T cells, ? B/NK cells | **HSCT**, Gene therapy | https://primaryimmune.org/scid-compass |
| **Chronic Granulomatous Disease (CGD)** | NADPH oxidase defect | Catalase-positive infections (S. aureus, Serratia, Burkholderia, Aspergillus) | **DHR flow cytometry** | Antibiotics, Antifungals, IFN-?, HSCT | https://primaryimmune.org/scid-compass |
| **Hyper-IgE Syndrome** | STAT3 mutation | **Eczema**, **Boils**, **Pneumatoceles**, Retained primary teeth | ? IgE, Eosinophilia | Antibiotics, Antifungals | https://primaryimmune.org/scid-compass |

## Secondary Immunodeficiency
| Cause | Mechanism | Management | URL |
|-------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **HIV/AIDS** | CD4+ T cell depletion | Antiretroviral therapy | https://www.cdc.gov/hiv |
| **Malignancy** | CLL, Multiple myeloma, Chemotherapy | Treat underlying, IVIG if indicated | https://www.cancer.gov |
| **Medications** | Corticosteroids, Immunosuppressants, Biologics, Anticonvulsants | Reduce/stop if possible, Monitor | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
| **Protein-Losing States** | Nephrotic syndrome, PLE, Severe burns | Treat underlying, IVIG | https://www.niddk.nih.gov |
| **Splenectomy** | ? Antibody response to encapsulated organisms | Vaccination, Antibiotic prophylaxis | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines |

## Immunoglobulin Replacement Therapy
| Route | Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|-------|------------|---------------|-----|
| **IVIG** | Higher doses possible, 100% compliance | Systemic reactions, Vascular access, Monthly infusions | https://primaryimmune.org/treatment-information/immunoglobulin-replacement-therapy |
| **SCIG** | **Home administration**, **Stable levels**, **Fewer systemic reactions** | Weekly infusions, Multiple needle sticks, Lower doses | https://primaryimmune.org/treatment-information/immunoglobulin-replacement-therapy |
| **Facilitated SCIG (fSCIG)** | SCIG + hyaluronidase, Monthly infusions | Larger volume per site | https://primaryimmune.org/treatment-information/immunoglobulin-replacement-therapy |
