# Allergy Medicine - Atopic Dermatitis (Eczema)

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Chronic, pruritic inflammatory skin disease | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/atopic-dermatitis |
| **Prevalence** | 15-20% of children, 1-3% of adults | https://www.cdc.gov/eczema/data/index.html |
| **Pathophysiology** | Epidermal barrier dysfunction + Immune dysregulation (Th2-skewed) | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/diseases-conditions/eczema-atopic-dermatitis |

## Treatment - Stepwise Approach
| Step | Severity | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **1. Basic Care** | All patients | **Emollients**, Avoid triggers, Gentle skin care | https://nationaleczema.org/eczema/treatment |
| **2. Mild** | Occasional flares | **Low-mid potency topical CS**, Topical calcineurin inhibitors | https://nationaleczema.org/eczema/treatment |
| **3. Moderate** | Recurrent flares | **Mid-high potency topical CS**, **Crisaborole**, Phototherapy | https://nationaleczema.org/eczema/treatment |
| **4. Severe** | Persistent, widespread | **Dupilumab**, **Tralokinumab**, **JAK inhibitors** (upadacitinib, abrocitinib) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
