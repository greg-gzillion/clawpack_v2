## Biologic Selection for Severe Asthma
| Phenotype | Biomarkers | Preferred Biologic | URL |
|-----------|------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Allergic Asthma** | IgE >30, Positive SPT/sIgE | **Omalizumab** | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/asthma |
| **Eosinophilic Asthma** | Blood eos =150-300, FeNO =25 | **Mepolizumab, Reslizumab, Benralizumab, Dupilumab** | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/asthma |
| **Allergic + Eosinophilic** | IgE elevated + Eos elevated | **Dupilumab** | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/asthma |
| **Phenotype Independent** | Any phenotype | **Tezepelumab** | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/asthma |
