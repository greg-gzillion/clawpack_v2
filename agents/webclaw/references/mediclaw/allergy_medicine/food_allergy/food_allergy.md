# Allergy Medicine - Food Allergy

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Adverse immune response to food proteins | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/diseases-conditions/food-allergy |
| **Prevalence** | 8% of children, 10% of adults in US | https://www.foodallergy.org/resources/facts-and-statistics |
| **Major Allergens** | Milk, Egg, Peanut, Tree nuts, Soy, Wheat, Fish, Shellfish, Sesame | https://www.fda.gov/food/food-labeling-nutrition/food-allergies |

## Clinical Presentations
| Syndrome | Timing | Symptoms | URL |
|----------|--------|----------|-----|
| **IgE-Mediated** | Minutes-2 hours | Urticaria, Angioedema, Anaphylaxis, OAS | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/food-allergy |
| **FPIES** | 1-4 hours | Profuse vomiting, Diarrhea, Lethargy, Dehydration | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/food-allergy |
| **FPIAP** | Days-weeks | Blood-streaked stools in well infant | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/food-allergy |
| **EoE** | Chronic | Dysphagia, Food impaction, Reflux | https://apfed.org/about-eoe/ |
| **Celiac Disease** | Chronic | Diarrhea, Weight loss, Malabsorption | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/digestive-diseases/celiac-disease |

## Diagnosis
| Test | Use | Interpretation | URL |
|------|-----|----------------|-----|
| **Skin Prick Test** | IgE sensitization | Wheal =3 mm > negative control | https://www.aaaai.org/tools-for-the-public/conditions-library/allergies/food-allergy-testing |
| **Serum-Specific IgE** | IgE sensitization | =0.35 kU/L = positive | https://www.aaaai.org/tools-for-the-public/conditions-library/allergies/food-allergy-testing |
| **Component Testing** | Prognostication | Ara h 2, Cor a 9, etc. | https://www.aaaai.org/tools-for-the-public/conditions-library/allergies/food-allergy-testing |
| **Oral Food Challenge** | **Gold standard** | Diagnostic confirmation | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/food-allergy |
| **Elimination Diet** | Non-IgE mediated | Symptom resolution | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/food-allergy |

## Management
| Strategy | Description | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Strict Avoidance** | Eliminate trigger food completely | https://www.foodallergy.org/living-food-allergies/food-allergy-essentials/avoiding-cross-contact |
| **Emergency Plan** | Epinephrine autoinjector prescription, Anaphylaxis action plan | https://www.foodallergy.org/living-food-allergies/food-allergy-essentials/anaphylaxis-action-plan |
| **Oral Immunotherapy** | **Palforzia** (peanut OIT approved) | https://www.fda.gov/vaccines-blood-biologics/allergenics/palforzia |
| **Epicutaneous Immunotherapy** | **Viaskin** (peanut patch - investigational) | https://www.fda.gov/vaccines-blood-biologics/allergenics |
