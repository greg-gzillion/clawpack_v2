## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Irritant vs Allergic Contact Dermatitis
| Feature | Irritant | Allergic | URL |
|---------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Mechanism** | Direct cytotoxicity | T-cell mediated | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/contact-dermatitis |
| **Onset** | Minutes-hours | 24-72 hours | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/contact-dermatitis |
| **Distribution** | Sharply demarcated | May spread beyond contact | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/contact-dermatitis |
| **Symptoms** | Burning, Stinging | **Pruritus** (predominant) | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/contact-dermatitis |
| **Common Causes** | Soaps, Solvents, Acids | Nickel, Fragrances, Preservatives | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/contact-dermatitis |

## Management
| Strategy | Description | URL |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Avoidance** | Identify and eliminate causative agent | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/contact-dermatitis |
| **Topical Corticosteroids** | Mid-high potency for body, Low potency for face | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/contact-dermatitis |
| **Topical Calcineurin Inhibitors** | Tacrolimus, Pimecrolimus (steroid-sparing) | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/contact-dermatitis |

| **Systemic Therapy** | Prednisone (severe acute), Dupilumab (refractory) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |
