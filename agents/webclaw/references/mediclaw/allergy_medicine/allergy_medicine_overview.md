# Allergy Medicine - Overview

## What is Allergy/Immunology?
Allergy and Immunology is the medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of allergic and immunologic disorders.

| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Allergy** | Hypersensitivity reaction to typically harmless environmental antigens | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies |
| **Immunology** | Study of the immune system and its disorders | https://www.niaid.nih.gov/research/immune-system-overview |

## Hypersensitivity Reactions (Gell-Coombs Classification)
| Type | Mechanism | Examples | URL |
|------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Type I** | IgE-mediated, mast cell degranulation | Anaphylaxis, Allergic rhinitis, Asthma, Urticaria | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560631/ |
| **Type II** | IgG/IgM-mediated cytotoxicity | Hemolytic anemia, ITP, Goodpasture syndrome | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560631/ |
| **Type III** | Immune complex deposition | Serum sickness, SLE, Hypersensitivity pneumonitis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560631/ |
| **Type IV** | T-cell mediated (delayed) | Contact dermatitis, PPD reaction, Drug rash | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560631/ |
