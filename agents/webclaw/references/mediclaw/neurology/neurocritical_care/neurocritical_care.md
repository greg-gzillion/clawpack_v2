# Neurology - Neurocritical Care

## Neuromonitoring in ICU
| Modality | Measures | Indications | URL |
|----------|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Intracranial Pressure (ICP)** | **ICP** (normal <15 mmHg) | Severe TBI, SAH, ICH with hydrocephalus, Fulminant hepatic failure | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/traumatic-brain-injury |
| **Cerebral Perfusion Pressure (CPP)** | **CPP = MAP - ICP** | Goal 60-70 mmHg | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/traumatic-brain-injury |
| **Brain Tissue Oxygen (PbtO2)** | **PbtO2** (normal >20 mmHg) | Severe TBI, SAH | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/traumatic-brain-injury |
| **Jugular Venous O2 Saturation (SjvO2)** | **SjvO2** (normal 55-75%) | TBI, SAH; Low = ischemia, High = hyperemia | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/traumatic-brain-injury |
| **Cerebral Microdialysis** | **Lactate/Pyruvate ratio**, Glucose, Glutamate | TBI, SAH; Detect metabolic crisis | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/traumatic-brain-injury |
| **Continuous EEG (cEEG)** | **Seizures**, **Burst suppression**, **Background activity** | Status epilepticus, SAH, TBI, Post-cardiac arrest | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |

## Status Epilepticus - ICU Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Continuous EEG** | **Confirm seizure cessation**, **Detect non-convulsive seizures** (50% after convulsive SE) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Burst Suppression Goal** | **Burst suppression ratio 50-80%** or **Complete suppression** for 24-48 hours | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Anesthetic Agents** | **Midazolam**, **Propofol**, **Pentobarbital** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Hemodynamic Support** | **Vasopressors** (anesthetic-induced hypotension) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Complication Monitoring** | **Propofol infusion syndrome** (metabolic acidosis, Rhabdomyolysis, Cardiac failure), **Ileus**, **Infection**, **DVT** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |

## Subarachnoid Hemorrhage - ICU Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Blood Pressure** | **SBP <160 mmHg** before aneurysm securement; **Permissive hypertension** after securement | https://www.stroke.org/en/professionals/stroke-resource-library/guidelines-and-quality |
| **Nimodipine** | **60 mg q4h x 21 days** (reduces vasospasm and poor outcome) | https://www.stroke.org/en/professionals/stroke-resource-library/guidelines-and-quality |
| **Euvolemia** | **Avoid hypovolemia**; Goal euvolemia (not prophylactic hypervolemia) | https://www.stroke.org/en/professionals/stroke-resource-library/guidelines-and-quality |
| **Vasospasm Monitoring** | **Transcranial Doppler (TCD)** , **CTA/CTP**, **DSA** | https://www.stroke.org/en/professionals/stroke-resource-library/guidelines-and-quality |
| **Vasospasm Treatment** | **Hypertension** (pressors), **Intra-arterial vasodilators** (verapamil, Milrinone), **Angioplasty** | https://www.stroke.org/en/professionals/stroke-resource-library/guidelines-and-quality |
| **Hydrocephalus** | **EVD placement**; **Ventriculoperitoneal shunt** if persistent | https://www.stroke.org/en/professionals/stroke-resource-library/guidelines-and-quality |
| **Hyponatremia** | **Cerebral salt wasting** (hypovolemic) vs **SIADH** (euvolemic) | https://www.stroke.org/en/professionals/stroke-resource-library/guidelines-and-quality |

## Guillain-Barré Syndrome - ICU Management
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Respiratory Monitoring** | **Serial FVC and NIF**; **Intubate if FVC <20 mL/kg** or **NIF <30 cm H2O** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/guillain-barre-syndrome |
| **Autonomic Dysfunction** | **Labile BP**, **Arrhythmias**, **Bradycardia**; May require **temporary pacing** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/guillain-barre-syndrome |
| **DVT Prophylaxis** | **LMWH** or **UFH** + **IPC** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/guillain-barre-syndrome |
| **Pain Management** | **Gabapentinoids**, **Carbamazepine**, **Opioids** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/guillain-barre-syndrome |
| **Early Rehabilitation** | **PT/OT** once stabilized | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/guillain-barre-syndrome |

## Brain Death Determination
| Component | Criteria | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Prerequisites** | **Known irreversible cause**, **Normothermia** (>36°C), **Normotension** (SBP =100), **No sedating/paralytic drugs**, **No severe metabolic derangements** | https://www.aan.com/practice/guidelines |
| **Clinical Examination** | **Coma** (GCS 3), **Absent brainstem reflexes** (pupillary, Corneal, Oculocephalic, Oculovestibular, Gag, Cough), **Apnea test** (PaCO2 =60 or =20 mmHg above baseline) | https://www.aan.com/practice/guidelines |
| **Ancillary Testing** | **EEG** (electrocerebral silence), **Cerebral angiography** (no flow), **Transcranial Doppler** (reverberating flow), **Nuclear medicine** (no uptake) | https://www.aan.com/practice/guidelines |
| **Declaration** | **Two physicians** (one attending) document findings | https://www.aan.com/practice/guidelines |
