# Neurology - Neuroimaging

## Neuroimaging Modalities
| Modality | Principle | Best For | URL |
|----------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **CT (Non-Contrast)** | X-ray attenuation | **Acute hemorrhage**, **Trauma**, **Hydrocephalus**, **Bone** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/computed-tomography-ct |
| **CT Angiography (CTA)** | Contrast-enhanced CT | **Vascular occlusion** (stroke), **Aneurysm**, **Dissection** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/computed-tomography-ct |
| **CT Perfusion** | Dynamic contrast flow | **Ischemic penumbra** (core vs. salvageable tissue) | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/computed-tomography-ct |
| **MRI (T1)** | Spin-lattice relaxation | **Anatomy**, **Fat**, **Contrast enhancement** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/magnetic-resonance-imaging-mri |
| **MRI (T2/FLAIR)** | Spin-spin relaxation | **Edema**, **Demyelination**, **Ischemia** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/magnetic-resonance-imaging-mri |
| **DWI/ADC** | Water diffusion | **Acute ischemia** (restricted diffusion), **Abscess**, **Cytotoxic edema** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/magnetic-resonance-imaging-mri |
| **SWI/GRE** | Susceptibility | **Hemorrhage**, **Microbleeds**, **Calcium**, **Iron deposition** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/magnetic-resonance-imaging-mri |
| **MRA** | Flow-related enhancement | **Vascular anatomy**, **Stenosis**, **Aneurysm** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/magnetic-resonance-imaging-mri |
| **MR Perfusion** | Dynamic contrast flow | **Ischemic penumbra**, **Tumor vascularity** | https://www.nibib.nih.gov/science-education/science-topics/magnetic-resonance-imaging-mri |

## Stroke Imaging - Key Findings
| Finding | CT | MRI | URL |
|---------|----|----|-----|
| **Hyperacute (<6 hours)** | **Hyperdense MCA sign**, Loss of gray-white differentiation | **DWI hyperintense**, **ADC hypointense** (restricted diffusion) | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/types-of-stroke/ischemic-stroke |
| **Acute (6-24 hours)** | **Hypodensity**, **Sulcal effacement** | **DWI hyperintense**, **FLAIR hyperintense** (after 4-6h) | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/types-of-stroke/ischemic-stroke |
| **Subacute (days-weeks)** | **Well-defined hypodensity**, **Mass effect** | **T2/FLAIR hyperintense**, **Gyral enhancement** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/types-of-stroke/ischemic-stroke |
| **Chronic (>weeks)** | **Encephalomalacia**, **Volume loss** | **T2 hyperintense**, **Volume loss** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/types-of-stroke/ischemic-stroke |
| **Hemorrhagic Transformation** | **Hyperdensity within infarct** | **T2*/SWI hypointense** | https://www.stroke.org/en/about-stroke/types-of-stroke/ischemic-stroke |

## Intracranial Hemorrhage - CT Findings
| Type | CT Appearance | Common Locations | URL |
|------|---------------|------------------|-----|
| **Epidural Hematoma** | **Lentiform (biconvex)** hyperdensity | **Temporal** (middle meningeal artery) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/traumatic-brain-injury |
| **Subdural Hematoma** | **Crescentic** hyperdensity (acute) or hypodensity (chronic) | **Convexity** (bridging veins) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/traumatic-brain-injury |
| **Subarachnoid Hemorrhage** | **Hyperdensity in sulci/basal cisterns** | **Circle of Willis** (aneurysm) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/cerebral-aneurysms |
| **Intraparenchymal Hemorrhage** | **Hyperdense** round/oval | **Basal ganglia**, **Thalamus**, **Pons**, **Cerebellum**, **Lobar** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/cerebral-hemorrhage |
| **Intraventricular Hemorrhage** | **Hyperdensity in ventricles** | **Ventricles** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/cerebral-hemorrhage |
