# Neurology - Neurorehabilitation

## Neurorehabilitation - Principles
| Principle | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Neuroplasticity** | Brain's ability to reorganize and form new connections after injury | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/rehabilitation |
| **Interdisciplinary Team** | **Physiatrist**, **Neurologist**, **PT**, **OT**, **SLP**, **Neuropsychologist**, **Social worker**, **Rehabilitation nurse** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/rehabilitation |
| **Goal-Oriented** | Individualized, Measurable goals focused on **function and quality of life** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/rehabilitation |
| **Task-Specific Training** | Repetitive, Intensive practice of functional tasks | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/rehabilitation |
| **Constraint-Induced Movement Therapy (CIMT)** | Restraint of unaffected limb + Intensive training of affected limb (stroke) | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/rehabilitation |

## Stroke Rehabilitation
| Domain | Interventions | URL |
|--------|---------------|-----|
| **Motor Recovery** | **PT** (gait training, Balance, Strengthening), **OT** (ADLs, Fine motor), **CIMT**, **Robotic therapy**, **Functional electrical stimulation (FES)** , **Mirror therapy** | https://www.stroke.org/en/life-after-stroke/recovery |
| **Spasticity** | **PT/OT** (stretching, Splinting), **Oral medications** (baclofen, Tizanidine), **Botulinum toxin**, **Intrathecal baclofen** | https://www.stroke.org/en/life-after-stroke/recovery |
| **Aphasia** | **SLP** (speech-language therapy), **Melodic intonation therapy**, **Augmentative and alternative communication (AAC)** | https://www.stroke.org/en/life-after-stroke/recovery |
| **Dysphagia** | **SLP** (swallowing therapy, Diet modification), **PEG tube** if severe | https://www.stroke.org/en/life-after-stroke/recovery |
| **Cognitive Rehabilitation** | **Cognitive therapy** (attention, Memory, Executive function), **Compensatory strategies** | https://www.stroke.org/en/life-after-stroke/recovery |
| **Mood** | **Screen and treat depression/anxiety** (SSRIs, CBT), **Support groups** | https://www.stroke.org/en/life-after-stroke/recovery |

## Spinal Cord Injury Rehabilitation
| Level | Functional Goals | Equipment | URL |
|-------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **C1-C4** | **Ventilator-dependent** (C1-C3), **Power wheelchair with head/chin control**, **Environmental control unit**, **Full assistance for ADLs** | Power wheelchair, Ventilator, ECU | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spinal-cord-injury |
| **C5** | **Elbow flexion**, **Power wheelchair with hand control**, **Assist with feeding/grooming** (with adaptive equipment) | Power wheelchair, Mobile arm support | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spinal-cord-injury |
| **C6** | **Wrist extension**, **Tenodesis grasp**, **Independent feeding/grooming**, **Manual wheelchair** (short distances) | Manual wheelchair, Tenodesis splint | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spinal-cord-injury |
| **C7-C8** | **Elbow extension**, **Hand function**, **Independent transfers**, **Manual wheelchair** | Manual wheelchair | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spinal-cord-injury |
| **T1-T12** | **Independent with wheelchair**, **Standing frame**, **Therapeutic ambulation** (with braces) | Manual wheelchair, Standing frame, KAFO | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spinal-cord-injury |
| **L1-L5** | **Community ambulation** (with braces/crutches), **Bowel/bladder management** | AFO, Crutches, Walker | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/spinal-cord-injury |

## Traumatic Brain Injury Rehabilitation
| Phase | Setting | Goals | URL |
|-------|---------|-------|-----|
| **Acute** | ICU, Acute care | **Prevent secondary injury**, **Medical stabilization** | https://www.braintrauma.org/guidelines |
| **Inpatient Rehabilitation** | IRF, SNF | **Improve function**, **Maximize independence**, **Family training** | https://www.braintrauma.org/guidelines |
| **Outpatient Rehabilitation** | Outpatient clinic, Day program | **Community reintegration**, **Return to work/school**, **Long-term management** | https://www.braintrauma.org/guidelines |
