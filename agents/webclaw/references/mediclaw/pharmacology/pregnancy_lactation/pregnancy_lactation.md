# Pharmacology - Pregnancy and Lactation

## FDA Pregnancy Categories (Historical)
| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **A** | Controlled studies show **no risk** | Levothyroxine, Folic acid |
| **B** | Animal studies show no risk; No human studies | Penicillins, Cephalosporins, Acetaminophen |
| **C** | Animal studies show risk; No human studies | Fluoroquinolones, SSRIs, Beta-blockers |
| **D** | Evidence of human risk; **Benefits may outweigh risks** | ACE-I/ARB, Valproate, Lithium, Phenytoin |
| **X** | **Contraindicated in pregnancy** | Isotretinoin, Warfarin, Methotrexate, Statins |

## Drugs Safe in Pregnancy
| Condition | Safe Options | URL |
|-----------|--------------|-----|

| **Infection** | **Penicillins**, **Cephalosporins**, **Macrolides** (erythromycin, Azithromycin) | https://www.acog.org/womens-health/faqs/medicine-and-pregnancy |
| **Hypertension** | **Labetalol**, **Nifedipine**, **Methyldopa**, **Hydralazine** | https://www.acog.org/womens-health/faqs/preeclampsia-and-high-blood-pressure-during-pregnancy |

| **Depression/Anxiety** | **Sertraline**, **Fluoxetine**, **Citalopram** | https://www.acog.org/womens-health/faqs/depression-during-pregnancy |

## Lactation Safety
| Generally Safe | Use with Caution | Avoid | URL |
|----------------|------------------|-------|-----|
| Penicillins, Cephalosporins | SSRIs (sertraline, Paroxetine preferred) | **Codeine** (CYP2D6 ultra-rapid metabolizers) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| Acetaminophen, Ibuprofen | Benzodiazepines (lorazepam preferred) | **Lithium** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| Loratadine, Cetirizine | Opioids (short-term) | **Amiodarone** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| Levothyroxine | Methadone, Buprenorphine | **Chemotherapy** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
| Warfarin, LMWH | - | **Radioactive iodine** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/contraindications-to-breastfeeding.html |
