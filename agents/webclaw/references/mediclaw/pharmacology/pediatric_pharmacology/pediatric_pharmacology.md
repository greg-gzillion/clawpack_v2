|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Common Pediatric Dosing Pearls
| Drug | Pediatric Consideration | URL |
|------|------------------------|-----|
| **Acetaminophen** | 10-15 mg/kg q4-6h (max 75 mg/kg/day) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
| **Ibuprofen** | 5-10 mg/kg q6-8h (max 40 mg/kg/day) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
| **Amoxicillin** | 80-90 mg/kg/day divided BID-TID for AOM | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
| **Ceftriaxone** | 50-100 mg/kg/day (max 2g) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
| **Dexamethasone (Croup)** | 0.6 mg/kg PO/IM (max 10 mg) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
| **Epinephrine (Anaphylaxis)** | 0.01 mg/kg IM (max 0.3-0.5 mg) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |

## Drugs to Avoid in Children
| Drug | Concern | URL |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Aspirin** | **Reye syndrome** (in viral illness) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
| **Tetracyclines** | **Teeth discoloration** (<8 years) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
| **Fluoroquinolones** | **Tendinopathy**, **Arthropathy** (use with caution) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-drug-class/fluoroquinolone-antimicrobial-drugs |
| **Codeine** | **Respiratory depression** (CYP2D6 ultra-rapid metabolizers) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/codeine-information |
| **Loperamide** | **Ileus**, **CNS depression** (<2 years) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/pediatric-medication-safety/ |
