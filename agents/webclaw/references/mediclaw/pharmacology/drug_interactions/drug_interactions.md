## Common Drug Interactions
| Drug Combination | Mechanism | Effect | Management | URL |
|------------------|-----------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Warfarin + NSAIDs** | Additive antiplatelet + GI irritation | **Increased bleeding risk** | Avoid NSAIDs; Use acetaminophen | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **Warfarin + Amiodarone** | CYP2C9 inhibition | **Increased INR**, **Bleeding** | Reduce warfarin dose 30-50%, Monitor INR | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **Warfarin + TMP-SMX** | CYP2C9 inhibition + Displacement | **Increased INR**, **Bleeding** | Avoid combination or monitor INR closely | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **ACE-I/ARB + NSAIDs** | ? Prostaglandin-mediated renal perfusion | **AKI**, **Hyperkalemia** | Avoid NSAIDs in CKD, CHF | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **ACE-I/ARB + K+-sparing diuretics** | Additive K+ retention | **Hyperkalemia** | Monitor K+, Avoid in CKD | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **Digoxin + Amiodarone** | ? Digoxin clearance | **Digoxin toxicity** | Reduce digoxin dose 50%, Monitor levels | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **Statins + Fibrates** | Additive myotoxicity | **Rhabdomyolysis** | Avoid gemfibrozil; Use fenofibrate with caution | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **Statins + Grapefruit** | CYP3A4 inhibition | **Increased statin levels**, **Myopathy** | Avoid grapefruit with simvastatin, Lovastatin, Atorvastatin | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **SSRIs + NSAIDs** | Additive antiplatelet | **Increased GI bleeding** | Use PPI, Consider alternative antidepressant | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **SSRIs + Triptans** | Additive serotonergic | **Serotonin syndrome** | Monitor for symptoms, Avoid combination if possible | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
| **Lithium + NSAIDs/Thiazides** | ? Lithium clearance | **Lithium toxicity** | Avoid NSAIDs/Thiazides; Monitor lithium levels | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/drug-interactions-labeling |
