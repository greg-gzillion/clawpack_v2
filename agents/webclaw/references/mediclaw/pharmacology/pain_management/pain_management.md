# Pharmacology - Pain Management

## Opioid Analgesics
| Drug | Potency (vs Morphine) | Duration | Key Features | URL |
|------|----------------------|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Morphine** | 1 | 3-4 hours | **Histamine release**, Active metabolites (M6G) | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/patients/index.html |
| **Hydromorphone** | 5-7 | 3-4 hours | Less histamine, No active metabolites | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/patients/index.html |
| **Oxycodone** | 1.5-2 | 3-4 hours | Oral bioavailability high | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/patients/index.html |
| **Fentanyl** | 100 | 0.5-1 hour (IV) | **Rapid onset**, **Chest wall rigidity** | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/patients/index.html |
| **Methadone** | 1-2 (analgesia), Variable (maintenance) | 24-36 hours (half-life) | **NMDA antagonism**, **QTc prolongation** | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/patients/index.html |
| **Tramadol** | 0.1-0.2 | 4-6 hours | **SNRI**, **Seizure risk**, **Serotonin syndrome** | https://www.cdc.gov/opioids/patients/index.html |
| **Naloxone** | Antagonist | 30-60 min | **Opioid reversal** | https://www.cdc.gov/stop-overdose/index.html |

## Non-Opioid Analgesics
| Drug Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Acetaminophen** | Tylenol | Central COX inhibition | **Hepatotoxicity** (overdose) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-drug-class/acetaminophen |
| **NSAIDs** | Ibuprofen, Naproxen, Celecoxib | Inhibit COX-1/COX-2 | **GI bleeding**, **AKI**, **Hypertension**, **CV events** (COX-2) | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/pain-management |
| **Gabapentinoids** | Gabapentin, Pregabalin | a2d calcium channel ligands | **Dizziness**, **Somnolence**, **Edema**, **Weight gain** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |
| **SNRIs** | Duloxetine, Venlafaxine | Inhibit serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake | Nausea, Hypertension, Sexual dysfunction | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |
| **TCAs** | Amitriptyline, Nortriptyline | Inhibit serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake | **Anticholinergic**, **Orthostasis**, **QTc prolongation** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/peripheral-neuropathy |
