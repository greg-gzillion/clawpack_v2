# Pharmacology - Antimicrobials

## Beta-Lactam Antibiotics
| Class | Examples | Spectrum | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Penicillins** | Penicillin G, Amoxicillin, Ampicillin | Streptococci, Oral anaerobes, Listeria | Inhibit cell wall synthesis (bind PBPs) | **Hypersensitivity** (rash, Anaphylaxis), GI upset | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK548127/ |
| **Anti-Staphylococcal Penicillins** | Nafcillin, Oxacillin, Dicloxacillin | **MSSA** | Same | Same; Interstitial nephritis | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557829/ |
| **Beta-Lactamase Inhibitor Combinations** | Amoxicillin-clavulanate, Ampicillin-sulbactam, Piperacillin-tazobactam | MSSA, Anaerobes, Gram-negatives | Same + Inhibit beta-lactamases | Same as parent drug | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557829/ |
| **Cephalosporins (1st Gen)** | Cefazolin, Cephalexin | MSSA, Streptococci, E. coli, Klebsiella | Inhibit cell wall synthesis | Hypersensitivity, GI | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK551517/ |
| **Cephalosporins (3rd Gen)** | Ceftriaxone, Cefotaxime, Ceftazidime | Broad Gram-negative, Streptococci | Same | **Biliary sludging** (ceftriaxone) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK459163/ |
| **Carbapenems** | Meropenem, Imipenem-cilastatin | Broadest spectrum (Gram-positive, Gram-negative, Anaerobes) | Same | **Seizures** (imipenem) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK545281/ |

## Non-Beta-Lactam Antibiotics
| Class | Examples | Spectrum | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Glycopeptides** | Vancomycin | Gram-positive only (MRSA, MRSE, Enterococci) | Inhibit cell wall synthesis | **Red Man Syndrome**, **Nephrotoxicity**, **Ototoxicity** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK459263/ |
| **Macrolides** | Azithromycin, Clarithromycin | Atypicals, Streptococci | Inhibit protein synthesis (50S) | **QTc prolongation**, **GI intolerance** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK551495/ |
| **Aminoglycosides** | Gentamicin, Tobramycin, Amikacin | Aerobic Gram-negatives | Inhibit protein synthesis (30S) | **Nephrotoxicity**, **Ototoxicity** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK541105/ |
| **Tetracyclines** | Doxycycline, Minocycline | Atypicals, Rickettsia, Borrelia | Inhibit protein synthesis (30S) | **Photosensitivity**, **Teeth discoloration** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK555905/ |
| **Fluoroquinolones** | Ciprofloxacin, Levofloxacin, Moxifloxacin | Gram-negatives (cipro), Respiratory (levo, Moxi) | Inhibit DNA gyrase/topoisomerase | **Tendinopathy**, **QTc prolongation**, **Peripheral neuropathy** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-drug-class/fluoroquinolone-antimicrobial-drugs |
| **Sulfonamides** | TMP-SMX | Gram-negatives, Pneumocystis, Nocardia | Inhibit folate synthesis | **Hypersensitivity** (SJS/TEN), **Hyperkalemia** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557758/ |
| **Metronidazole** | Metronidazole | Anaerobes, Protozoa | DNA damage | **Disulfiram-like reaction**, **Metallic taste** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539728/ |
