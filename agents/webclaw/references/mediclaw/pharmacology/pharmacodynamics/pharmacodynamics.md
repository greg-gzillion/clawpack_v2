## Therapeutic Index (TI)
| TI Value | Interpretation | Examples | URL |
|----------|----------------|----------|-----|
| **TI = TD50 / ED50** | Higher TI = **Safer drug** | Penicillin (high), Warfarin (low) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557829/ |
| **Narrow TI Drugs** | Require **therapeutic drug monitoring** | **Warfarin**, **Digoxin**, **Lithium**, **Phenytoin**, **Theophylline**, **Cyclosporine** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/information-consumers-and-patients-drugs/narrow-therapeutic-index-drugs |
