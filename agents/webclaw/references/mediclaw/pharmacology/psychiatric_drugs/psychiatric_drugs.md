# Pharmacology - Psychiatric Drugs

## Antidepressants
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **SSRIs** | Sertraline, Escitalopram, Fluoxetine, Paroxetine, Citalopram | Inhibit serotonin reuptake | **GI upset**, **Sexual dysfunction**, **Insomnia/Activation**, **Hyponatremia** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **SNRIs** | Venlafaxine, Duloxetine, Desvenlafaxine | Inhibit serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake | **Hypertension** (venlafaxine), Nausea, Sexual dysfunction | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Bupropion** | Bupropion | Inhibits norepinephrine and dopamine reuptake | **Seizures**, **Insomnia**, **Anxiety**, **No sexual dysfunction** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Mirtazapine** | Mirtazapine | a2 antagonist | **Sedation**, **Weight gain**, **No sexual dysfunction** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **TCAs** | Amitriptyline, Nortriptyline | Inhibit serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake | **Anticholinergic**, **Orthostasis**, **QTc prolongation**, **Lethal in overdose** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **MAOIs** | Phenelzine, Tranylcypromine | Inhibit monoamine oxidase | **Hypertensive crisis** with tyramine, **Serotonin syndrome** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |

## Antipsychotics
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **First-Generation (FGA)** | Haloperidol, Fluphenazine, Chlorpromazine | **D2 antagonism** | **EPS**, **Tardive dyskinesia**, **Hyperprolactinemia**, **NMS** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |

## Mood Stabilizers
| Drug | Mechanism | Indications | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Lithium** | Multiple (inositol, GSK-3) | **Bipolar** (mania, Maintenance), **Depression augmentation** | **Tremor**, **Polyuria/Polydipsia**, **Hypothyroidism**, **Teratogenicity** (Ebstein anomaly) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Valproate** | Multiple (Na+ channels, GABA) | **Bipolar** (mania, Maintenance), **Epilepsy**, **Migraine** | **Weight gain**, **Tremor**, **Thrombocytopenia**, **Hepatotoxicity**, **Teratogenicity** (neural tube defects) | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Lamotrigine** | Blocks Na+ channels | **Bipolar** (depression, Maintenance) | **Rash (SJS/TEN)** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
| **Carbamazepine** | Blocks Na+ channels | **Bipolar** (mania), **Epilepsy**, **Trigeminal neuralgia** | **Hyponatremia**, **Rash (SJS/TEN in HLA-B*1502)** , **Drug interactions** | https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/mental-health-medications |
