# Pharmacology - Antiparasitics

## Antimalarials
| Drug | Mechanism | Indications | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Artemether/Lumefantrine** | Artemisinin: Free radical damage; Lumefantrine: Inhibits heme polymerization | **Uncomplicated P. falciparum** | **QTc prolongation**, Headache | https://www.cdc.gov/malaria/resources/treatment.html |
| **Atovaquone/Proguanil** | Atovaquone: Inhibits mitochondrial electron transport; Proguanil: Inhibits dihydrofolate reductase | **Prophylaxis**, **Treatment** | GI upset, Headache | https://www.cdc.gov/malaria/resources/treatment.html |
| **Chloroquine** | Inhibits heme polymerization | **P. vivax**, **P. ovale**, **P. malariae**, **P. knowlesi** (chloroquine-sensitive) | **Retinopathy**, **Pruritus**, **QTc prolongation** | https://www.cdc.gov/malaria/resources/treatment.html |
| **Primaquine** | Unknown | **Radical cure** of P. vivax/ovale (hypnozoites) | **Hemolysis in G6PD deficiency** | https://www.cdc.gov/malaria/resources/treatment.html |
