# Pharmacology - CNS Drugs

## Antiepileptic Drugs (AEDs)
| Drug | Mechanism | Seizure Types | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|------|-----------|---------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Phenytoin** | Blocks voltage-gated Na+ channels | **Focal**, **GTC** | **Gingival hyperplasia**, **Osteoporosis**, **Cerebellar atrophy**, **Rash (SJS/TEN)** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Valproate** | Multiple (Na+ channels, GABA, Ca2+ channels) | **Broad-spectrum** (GTC, Absence, Myoclonic) | **Weight gain**, **Tremor**, **Thrombocytopenia**, **Hepatotoxicity**, **Teratogenicity** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Lamotrigine** | Blocks voltage-gated Na+ channels | **Focal**, **GTC**, **Absence** | **Rash (SJS/TEN)** , **Insomnia** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Levetiracetam** | Binds SV2A | **Focal**, **GTC**, **Myoclonic** | **Irritability**, **Aggression**, **Depression** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Topiramate** | Multiple (Na+ channels, GABA, Glutamate, CAI) | **Focal**, **GTC**, **Migraine** | **Cognitive slowing**, **Word-finding difficulty**, **Weight loss**, **Metabolic acidosis**, **Kidney stones** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |
| **Ethosuximide** | Blocks T-type Ca2+ channels | **Absence** | **GI upset**, **Hiccups** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/epilepsy |

## Antiparkinsonian Drugs
| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Key Adverse Effects | URL |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Levodopa/Carbidopa** | Sinemet | **Dopamine precursor** | **Motor fluctuations**, **Dyskinesia**, **Nausea**, **Orthostasis**, **Hallucinations** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/parkinsons-disease |
| **Dopamine Agonists** | Pramipexole, Ropinirole, Rotigotine | Stimulate dopamine receptors | **Impulse control disorders**, **Nausea**, **Orthostasis**, **Edema** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/parkinsons-disease |
| **MAO-B Inhibitors** | Rasagiline, Selegiline, Safinamide | Inhibit dopamine breakdown | Insomnia, Orthostasis | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/parkinsons-disease |
| **COMT Inhibitors** | Entacapone, Opicapone | Inhibit levodopa breakdown | **Diarrhea**, **Orange urine** | https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/parkinsons-disease |
