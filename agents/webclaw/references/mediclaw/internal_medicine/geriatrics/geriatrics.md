# Internal Medicine - Geriatrics

## Comprehensive Geriatric Assessment
| Domain | Assessment Tools | URL |
|--------|-----------------|-----|
| **Functional Status** | **ADLs** (bathing, dressing, toileting), **IADLs** (shopping, finances) | https://www.americangeriatrics.org |
| **Cognition** | **Mini-Cog**, **MMSE**, **MoCA** | https://www.alz.org |
| **Mood** | **PHQ-9**, **Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS)** | https://www.americangeriatrics.org |
| **Falls Risk** | **Timed Up and Go (TUG)** , **STEADI** | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **Polypharmacy** | **Beers Criteria**, **STOPP/START** | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |

## Beers Criteria - Key Inappropriate Medications
| Drug Class | Rationale | Alternative | URL |
|------------|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Anticholinergics** | Confusion, Constipation, Falls | Non-anticholinergic options | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Benzodiazepines** | Falls, Cognitive impairment | Non-pharmacologic, SSRIs | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **NSAIDs (long-term)** | GI bleeding, AKI, ? BP | Acetaminophen, Topical NSAIDs | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **PPIs >8 weeks** | C. diff, Bone loss, B12 deficiency | H2 blocker, Deprescribe | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |
| **Sliding Scale Insulin** | Hypoglycemia | Basal insulin | https://dcri.org/beers-criteria/ |

## Falls Prevention
| Component | Intervention | URL |
|-----------|--------------|-----|
| **STEADI Algorithm** | CDC's Stopping Elderly Accidents, Deaths & Injuries | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **Screening Questions** | "Have you fallen in the past year?" "Do you feel unsteady?" | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **Timed Up and Go (TUG)** | **=12 seconds = Increased fall risk** | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
| **Interventions** | Exercise, Medication review, Vitamin D, Home safety | https://www.cdc.gov/steadi |
