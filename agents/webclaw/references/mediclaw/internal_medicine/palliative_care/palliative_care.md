## Symptom Management at End of Life
| Symptom | Management | URL |
|---------|------------|-----|
| **Dyspnea** | **Opioids** (morphine), Fan, Oxygen (if hypoxic) | https://www.capc.org |
| **Nausea/Vomiting** | Haloperidol, Metoclopramide, Ondansetron | https://www.capc.org |
| **Constipation** | **Stimulant laxative + Stool softener** (senna + docusate) | https://www.capc.org |
| **Delirium** | **Haloperidol**, Treat underlying cause | https://www.capc.org |
| **Secretions** | **Glycopyrrolate**, Scopolamine | https://www.capc.org |

## Hospice Eligibility
| Condition | General Criteria | URL |
|-----------|------------------|-----|
| **Cancer** | Metastatic or progressive despite treatment | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **Dementia** | FAST stage 7C + complication | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **Heart Failure** | NYHA Class IV, Optimal therapy | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **COPD** | Disabling dyspnea at rest, FEV1 <30% | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
| **General** | **Prognosis =6 months** | https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/hospice |
