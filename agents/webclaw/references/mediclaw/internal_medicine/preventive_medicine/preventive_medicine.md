## USPSTF Grade Definitions
| Grade | Definition | Implication | URL |
|-------|------------|-------------|-----|
| **A** | High certainty of substantial net benefit | **Offer or provide** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **B** | High certainty of moderate benefit | **Offer or provide** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **C** | Moderate certainty of small benefit | **Offer selectively** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
| **D** | No net benefit or harms outweigh | **Discourage use** | https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/about-uspstf/methods-and-processes/grade-definitions |
