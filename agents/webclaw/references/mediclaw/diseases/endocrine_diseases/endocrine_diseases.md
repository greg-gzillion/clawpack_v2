|------|-----------------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Type 1 DM** | Autoimmune beta-cell destruction | **Insulin deficiency**, Ketoacidosis prone | **Insulin** (multiple daily injections or pump) | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/diabetes/overview/what-is-diabetes/type-1-diabetes |
| **Type 2 DM** | Insulin resistance + relative deficiency | Gradual onset, Associated with obesity | Lifestyle, Metformin, GLP-1 agonists, SGLT2i, Insulin | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/diabetes/overview/what-is-diabetes/type-2-diabetes |

|---------|-----|-----|-----|

| **pH** | **<7.3** | >7.3 | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/diabetes/overview/managing-diabetes |
| **Bicarbonate** | **<18 mEq/L** | >18 mEq/L | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/diabetes/overview/managing-diabetes |
| **Anion Gap** | **>12** | Normal or mildly elevated | https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/diabetes/overview/managing-diabetes |
