## Sinusitis
| Type | Duration | Pathogens | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|-----|

## Hearing Loss
| Type | Location | Causes | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Conductive** | External or middle ear | **Cerumen impaction**, **Otitis media**, **Tympanic membrane perforation**, Otosclerosis | Treat underlying cause, Hearing aids, Surgery | https://www.nidcd.nih.gov/health/hearing-loss |
| **Sensorineural** | Cochlea or CN VIII | **Presbycusis** (age-related), Noise exposure, **Ototoxic drugs**, Meniere disease | **Hearing aids**, Cochlear implant | https://www.nidcd.nih.gov/health/hearing-loss |
| **Mixed** | Both conductive and sensorineural | Chronic otitis media, Trauma | Combination of above | https://www.nidcd.nih.gov/health/hearing-loss |
