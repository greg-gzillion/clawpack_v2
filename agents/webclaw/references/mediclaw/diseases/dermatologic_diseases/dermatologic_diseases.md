## Psoriasis
| Type | Features | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Plaque Psoriasis** | **Well-demarcated erythematous plaques with silvery scale** (extensor surfaces) | Topical corticosteroids, Vitamin D analogs, Phototherapy, Systemics | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/psoriasis |

## Herpes Zoster (Shingles)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Reactivation of latent VZV in dorsal root ganglia | https://www.cdc.gov/shingles/index.html |
| **Clinical Features** | **Painful vesicular rash in dermatomal distribution**, Does not cross midline | https://www.cdc.gov/shingles/hcp/clinical-overview.html |

| **Prevention** | **Shingrix** (recombinant zoster vaccine) recommended for adults =50 | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/shingles/public/shingrix/index.html |

## Skin Cancer
| Type | Frequency | Appearance | Risk | Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------|-------------|------|-----------|-----|
| **Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)** | **Most common** | **Pearly papule**, Telangiectasias, Rolled borders | Locally destructive, **Rarely metastasizes** | Excision, Mohs surgery | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/skin-cancer/types/common/basal-cell-carcinoma |
| **Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC)** | Second most common | **Hyperkeratotic papule/plaque**, Ulceration, Crusting | **Metastasizes** (especially lip, ear, scar) | Excision, Mohs surgery | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/skin-cancer/types/common/squamous-cell-carcinoma |
| **Melanoma** | **Most deadly** | **ABCDE**: Asymmetry, Border irregularity, Color variation, Diameter >6mm, Evolution | **High metastatic potential** | **Wide local excision**, Sentinel lymph node biopsy, Immunotherapy | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/skin-cancer/types/common/melanoma |
