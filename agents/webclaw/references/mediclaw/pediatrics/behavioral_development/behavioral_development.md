# Pediatrics - Behavioral and Developmental Pediatrics

## Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Persistent deficits in social communication** + **Restricted, Repetitive behaviors** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/autism/index.html |
| **Red Flags** | **No babbling by 12 mo**, **No words by 16 mo**, **No 2-word phrases by 24 mo**, **Loss of language or social skills** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/autism/index.html |
| **Screening** | **M-CHAT-R/F** at 18 and 24 months | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/autism/screening.html |
| **Treatment** | **Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)** , **Speech therapy**, **Occupational therapy**, **Social skills training** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/autism/treatment.html |

## Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Persistent pattern of inattention and/or hyperactivity-impulsivity**; Onset before **age 12**; Present in **=2 settings** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/adhd/index.html |
| **Treatment** | **Stimulants** (methylphenidate, Amphetamine), **Non-stimulants** (atomoxetine, Guanfacine), **Behavioral therapy**, **Parent training** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/adhd/treatment.html |

## Specific Learning Disorder
| Type | Features | Treatment | URL |
|------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Dyslexia** | **Difficulty with word recognition, Decoding, Spelling** | **Special education**, **Multisensory structured language education** | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/learningdisabilities |
| **Dyscalculia** | **Difficulty with number sense, Calculation, Math reasoning** | **Special education**, **Manipulatives**, **Extra time** | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/learningdisabilities |
| **Dysgraphia** | **Difficulty with written expression, Handwriting, Spelling** | **Occupational therapy**, **Keyboarding**, **Speech-to-text** | https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/learningdisabilities |

## Global Developmental Delay / Intellectual Disability
| Severity | IQ Range | Adaptive Functioning | URL |
|----------|----------|---------------------|-----|
| **Mild** | 50-70 | **Delayed but independent** in many areas | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/intellectual-disability/ |
| **Moderate** | 35-50 | **Can learn self-care and simple tasks** with support | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/intellectual-disability/ |
| **Severe** | 20-35 | **Limited self-care**, **Requires significant support** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/intellectual-disability/ |
| **Profound** | <20 | **Total dependence** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/intellectual-disability/ |
