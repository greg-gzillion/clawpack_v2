# Pediatrics - Child Abuse and Neglect

## Physical Abuse - Red Flags
| Finding | Concern | URL |
|---------|---------|-----|
| **Bruises** | **Patterned** (hand, Belt, Loop cord), **Multiple stages**, **Non-mobile infant**, **Unusual locations** (ears, Neck, Genitalia, Buttocks) | https://www.cdc.gov/violenceprevention/childabuseandneglect/index.html |
| **Burns** | **Stocking-glove pattern** (forced immersion), **Symmetric**, **Cigarette burns**, **Distinct margins** | https://www.cdc.gov/violenceprevention/childabuseandneglect/index.html |
| **Fractures** | **Metaphyseal corner** (pathognomonic), **Posterior rib**, **Multiple stages**, **Femur in non-ambulatory** | https://www.cdc.gov/violenceprevention/childabuseandneglect/index.html |
| **Head Trauma** | **Subdural hematoma**, **Retinal hemorrhages**, **Diffuse axonal injury** | https://www.cdc.gov/violenceprevention/childabuseandneglect/index.html |
| **Abdominal Trauma** | **Duodenal hematoma**, **Pancreatic injury**, **Liver/splenic laceration** | https://www.cdc.gov/violenceprevention/childabuseandneglect/index.html |

## Physical Abuse - Evaluation
| Age | Imaging | Labs | URL |
|-----|---------|------|-----|
| **<2 years** | **Skeletal survey** (repeat in 2 weeks), **Head CT** (if <6 mo or signs of trauma) | **CBC**, **CMP**, **Lipase**, **UA**, **Tox screen** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/child-abuse/ |
| **2-5 years** | **Skeletal survey** (if high suspicion) | Same | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/child-abuse/ |
| **>5 years** | **Targeted imaging** | Same | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/child-abuse/ |

## Sexual Abuse
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Presentation** | **Behavioral changes**, **Sexualized behavior**, **Genital/anal trauma**, **STIs**, **Pregnancy** | https://www.cdc.gov/violenceprevention/childsexualabuse/index.html |
| **Evaluation** | **Forensic interview** (trained professional), **Anogenital exam** (colposcope), **STI testing**, **Pregnancy test** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/sexual-abuse/ |
| **Mandated Reporting** | **Immediate report** to Child Protective Services or law enforcement | https://www.childwelfare.gov/topics/systemwide/laws-policies/state/ |

## Medical Child Abuse (Munchausen by Proxy)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Caregiver fabricates or induces illness** in child | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/medical-child-abuse/ |
| **Red Flags** | **Persistent unexplained symptoms**, **Discrepancy between history and findings**, **Symptoms only in caregiver's presence**, **Multiple providers/hospitals** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/medical-child-abuse/ |
| **Management** | **Covert video monitoring** (if legal), **Multidisciplinary team**, **Separation from caregiver** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/medical-child-abuse/ |
