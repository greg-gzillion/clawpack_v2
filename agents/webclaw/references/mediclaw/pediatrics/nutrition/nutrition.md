# Pediatrics - Nutrition

## Infant Feeding
| Age | Feeding Recommendations | URL |
|-----|------------------------|-----|
| **0-6 months** | **Exclusive breastfeeding** (preferred) or **Iron-fortified formula** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-feeding/ |
| **6-12 months** | Continued breastfeeding/formula + **Introduction of solid foods** (single-ingredient, Iron-rich) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-feeding/ |
| **12-24 months** | **Whole cow's milk** (limit 16-24 oz/day), Family diet | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-feeding/ |

## Breastfeeding
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Benefits** | Reduced infections (otitis media, Gastroenteritis, Pneumonia), Reduced SIDS, Improved neurodevelopment, Maternal benefits (reduced breast/ovarian cancer) | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/about-breastfeeding/index.html |
| **Contraindications** | **HIV** (in US), **HTLV-1/2**, **Active untreated TB**, **Herpes simplex on breast**, **Certain medications**, **Classic galactosemia** | https://www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-special-circumstances/index.html |
| **Vitamin D Supplementation** | **400 IU/day** for all breastfed infants | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/vitamin-d-supplementation/ |

## Formula Feeding
| Type | Indications | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **Cow's Milk-Based** | Most infants | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-formula/ |
| **Soy-Based** | Vegan families, Galactosemia, Congenital lactase deficiency | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-formula/ |
| **Extensively Hydrolyzed** | **Cow's milk protein allergy** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-formula/ |
| **Amino Acid-Based** | Severe cow's milk protein allergy, Eosinophilic esophagitis | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-formula/ |
| **Premature** | Preterm infants (<34 weeks, <1800g) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/infant-formula/ |

## Introduction of Solid Foods
| Age | Foods to Introduce | Foods to Avoid | URL |
|-----|-------------------|----------------|-----|
| **4-6 months** | **Iron-fortified single-grain cereal**, Pureed vegetables, Pureed fruits | **Honey** (botulism risk), Cow's milk, Choking hazards | https://www.cdc.gov/nutrition/infantandtoddlernutrition/foods-and-drinks/index.html |
| **6-8 months** | Pureed meats, Beans, Yogurt, Mashed vegetables | Honey, Cow's milk, Choking hazards | https://www.cdc.gov/nutrition/infantandtoddlernutrition/foods-and-drinks/index.html |
| **8-12 months** | Soft finger foods, Small pieces of soft fruits/vegetables, Cheese, Eggs | Honey, Whole nuts, Grapes, Hot dogs, Popcorn | https://www.cdc.gov/nutrition/infantandtoddlernutrition/foods-and-drinks/index.html |
| **12+ months** | **Whole cow's milk**, Family diet | **Limit juice** (<4 oz/day) | https://www.cdc.gov/nutrition/infantandtoddlernutrition/foods-and-drinks/index.html |

## Failure to Thrive (FTT)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | Weight <3rd percentile OR Weight for length <3rd percentile OR Crossing =2 major percentile lines | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/failure-to-thrive/ |
| **Etiology** | **Inadequate intake** (most common - feeding difficulties, Poverty, Neglect), **Malabsorption** (celiac, CF), **Increased metabolic demand** (CHD, Hyperthyroidism), **Genetic/Syndromic** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/failure-to-thrive/ |
| **Evaluation** | **Dietary history**, **Growth charts**, **Observe feeding**, **CBC**, **CMP**, **UA**, **Celiac screen**, **CF testing**, **Stool studies** | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/failure-to-thrive/ |
| **Management** | **Increase caloric density** (formula, Foods), **Treat underlying cause**, **Multidisciplinary team** (nutrition, OT/SLP, Social work) | https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/failure-to-thrive/ |

## Obesity in Children
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **BMI =95th percentile** for age/sex | https://www.cdc.gov/obesity/childhood/index.html |
| **Complications** | **Hypertension**, **Dyslipidemia**, **Type 2 diabetes**, **NAFLD**, **OSA**, **Psychosocial** | https://www.cdc.gov/obesity/childhood/index.html |
| **Management** | **Lifestyle modification** (diet, Exercise, Screen time), **Family-based approach**, **Motivational interviewing** | https://www.cdc.gov/obesity/childhood/index.html |
