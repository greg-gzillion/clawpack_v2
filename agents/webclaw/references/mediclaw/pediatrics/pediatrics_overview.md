# Pediatrics - Overview

## What is Pediatrics?
Pediatrics is the medical specialty concerned with the physical, emotional, and social health of infants, children, adolescents, and young adults from birth to age 21. It encompasses preventive health care, diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic diseases, and guidance for optimal growth and development.
