## Menstrual Disorders
| Disorder | Features | Treatment | URL |
|----------|----------|-----------|-----|

| **Abnormal Uterine Bleeding** | **Heavy**, **Irregular**, **Prolonged** bleeding | **OCPs**, **Tranexamic acid**, **Iron** | https://www.acog.org/womens-health/faqs/abnormal-uterine-bleeding |
