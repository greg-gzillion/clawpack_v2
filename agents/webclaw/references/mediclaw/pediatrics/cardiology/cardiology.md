## Cyanotic Congenital Heart Disease
| Lesion | Key Features | Management | URL |
|--------|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Tetralogy of Fallot** | **VSD**, **RVOT obstruction**, **Overriding aorta**, **RVH**; **"Tet spells"** (hypercyanotic episodes) | **Knee-chest position**, **Oxygen**, **Morphine**, **Propranolol**, **Phenylephrine**, **Surgical repair** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/heartdefects/tetralogyoffallot.html |
| **Transposition of Great Arteries** | **Aorta from RV**, **Pulmonary artery from LV**; **Cyanosis at birth** | **Prostaglandin E1** (maintain PDA), **Balloon atrial septostomy**, **Arterial switch operation** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/heartdefects/transpositiongreatarteries.html |
| **Tricuspid Atresia** | **Absent tricuspid valve**, **Hypoplastic RV**, **ASD**, **VSD** | **Prostaglandin E1**, **Staged palliation** (Blalock-Taussig shunt, Glenn, Fontan) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/heartdefects/tricuspidatresia.html |

## Acyanotic Congenital Heart Disease
| Lesion | Key Features | Management | URL |
|--------|--------------|------------|-----|
| **VSD** | **Most common CHD**; **Harsh holosystolic murmur** at LLSB; **Small**: usually closes spontaneously; **Large**: CHF, FTT | **Observation** (small), **Diuretics**, **Surgical closure** (large) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/heartdefects/ventricularseptaldefect.html |
| **ASD** | **Wide fixed split S2**, **Systolic ejection murmur** at LUSB; **Right heart enlargement** | **Observation** (small), **Device closure** or **Surgical repair** (moderate-large) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/heartdefects/atrialseptaldefect.html |
| **PDA** | **Continuous machine-like murmur** at left infraclavicular area; **Bounding pulses** | **Indomethacin/Ibuprofen** (premature), **Device closure** (term) | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/heartdefects/patentductusarteriosus.html |
| **Coarctation of Aorta** | **Upper extremity hypertension**, **Lower extremity hypotension**, **Radio-femoral delay**, **Rib notching** on CXR | **Prostaglandin E1** (neonate), **Balloon angioplasty**, **Surgical repair** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/heartdefects/coarctationofaorta.html |
