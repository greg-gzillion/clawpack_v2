## Anaphylaxis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Diagnostic Criteria** | **Acute onset** with **skin/mucosal** + **respiratory compromise** OR **hypotension** OR **=2 organ systems** after allergen exposure | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/anaphylaxis |
| **Treatment** | **IM epinephrine** 0.01 mg/kg (max 0.3-0.5 mg) into anterolateral thigh; **Repeat q5-15min**; **Position supine**; **O2**, **IVF**, **Adjunctive** (H1/H2, Steroids) | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/anaphylaxis |
| **Biphasic Reaction** | 1-20% recur within 72h; **Observe 4-24h** | https://www.aaaai.org/conditions-treatments/allergies/anaphylaxis |

## Atopic Dermatitis (Eczema)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pathophysiology** | **Skin barrier dysfunction** (filaggrin), **Immune dysregulation** | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/atopic-dermatitis |
| **Clinical Features** | **Pruritic**, **Erythematous**, **Scaly plaques**; **Infantile** (face, Extensors), **Childhood** (flexural) | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/atopic-dermatitis |
| **Management** | **Emollients**, **Topical corticosteroids**, **Topical calcineurin inhibitors**, **Dupilumab** (severe) | https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/atopic-dermatitis |
