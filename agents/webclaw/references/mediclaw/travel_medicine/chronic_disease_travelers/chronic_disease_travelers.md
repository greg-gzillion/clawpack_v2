# Travel Medicine - Travelers with Chronic Diseases

## General Recommendations
| Condition | Considerations | URL |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Diabetes** | **Carry supplies in carry-on**, **Letter for insulin/pumps/syringes**, **Adjust insulin for time zone changes**, **Monitor glucose frequently** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/diabetes |
| **Cardiovascular Disease** | **Carry copy of recent ECG**, **Medication list**, **Avoid strenuous activity at altitude** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/cardiovascular-disease |
| **Chronic Lung Disease** | **Assess need for in-flight oxygen**, **Carry rescue inhalers**, **Consider altitude effects** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/lung-disease |
| **Chronic Kidney Disease** | **Arrange dialysis in advance**, **Medication adjustments**, **Avoid nephrotoxins** (NSAIDs) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/kidney-disease |
| **Inflammatory Bowel Disease** | **Carry medications**, **Consider prophylactic antibiotics** (rifaximin), **Know location of medical facilities** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/ibd |

## Medications and Travel
| Recommendation | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Carry-On** | **All medications in carry-on** (not checked luggage) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/medicine-abroad |
| **Original Containers** | **Keep in original labeled containers** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/medicine-abroad |
| **Letter from Provider** | **List of medications, diagnoses, allergies** (on letterhead) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/medicine-abroad |
| **Controlled Substances** | **Check regulations** of destination country | https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/travelers-with-special-considerations/traveling-with-medications.html |
| **Time Zone Changes** | **Plan for medication timing** (insulin, Anticonvulsants) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/medicine-abroad |

## Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) Prevention
| Risk Factors | Prevention | URL |
|--------------|------------|-----|
| **Flights >4 hours**, **Obesity**, **Pregnancy**, **OCPs**, **Prior VTE**, **Thrombophilia**, **Cancer**, **Recent surgery** | **Compression stockings** (15-30 mmHg), **Ambulate q2h**, **Calf exercises**, **Aisle seat**, **Hydration**, **Avoid alcohol/sedatives** | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/dvt/travel.html |
| **Pharmacologic Prophylaxis** | **Consider LMWH** for high-risk travelers | https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/dvt/travel.html |
