# Travel Medicine - Environmental Hazards

## Heat-Related Illness
| Condition | Symptoms | Core Temp | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Heat Cramps** | **Painful muscle spasms** | **Normal** | **Rest**, **Oral hydration with electrolytes** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |
| **Heat Exhaustion** | **Fatigue**, **Headache**, **Nausea**, **Profuse sweating** | **<40°C (104°F)** | **Move to cool area**, **Oral/IV hydration** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |
| **Heat Stroke** | **CNS dysfunction** (confusion, Seizure), **Hot/dry skin** | **>40°C (104°F)** | **Emergent cooling** (cold water immersion), **IV fluids** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/heatstress/heatrelillness.html |

## Cold-Related Illness
| Condition | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Frostbite** | **Hard, pale, cold skin**, **Numbness** | **Rapid rewarming** in warm water (37-39°C) | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |
| **Hypothermia** | **Shivering**, **Confusion**, **Drowsiness** | **Remove wet clothing**, **Insulate**, **Warm fluids** | https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/coldstress/coldrelatedillnesses.html |

## Sun Exposure
| Condition | Prevention | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|------------|-----------|-----|
| **Sunburn** | **Sunscreen** (SPF =30, Broad spectrum), **Protective clothing**, **Avoid peak hours** (10am-4pm) | **Cool compresses**, **Aloe vera**, **NSAIDs** | https://www.cdc.gov/skin-cancer/sun-safety/index.html |
| **Photosensitivity** | **Doxycycline**, **Voriconazole**, **NSAIDs**, **Thiazides** can cause photosensitivity | **Avoid sun**, **Protective clothing** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs |

## Water-Related Hazards
| Hazard | Prevention | URL |
|--------|------------|-----|
| **Drowning** | **Supervise children**, **Avoid alcohol**, **Wear life jackets**, **Know water conditions** | https://www.cdc.gov/drowning/prevention/index.html |
| **Marine Envenomation** | **Shuffle feet** (stingrays), **Avoid touching marine life** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/marine-envenomation |
| **Schistosomiasis** | **Avoid freshwater swimming/bathing** in endemic areas | https://www.cdc.gov/schistosomiasis/index.html |
| **Leptospirosis** | **Avoid freshwater exposure** if skin abrasions | https://www.cdc.gov/leptospirosis/index.html |

## Animal Bites and Rabies
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP)** | **2 doses** (0, 7 days) for high-risk travelers | https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/specific_groups/travelers/pre-exposure_vaccinations.html |
| **Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (PEP)** | **Thorough wound washing** + **Rabies immune globulin (RIG)** + **Vaccine series** (days 0, 3, 7, 14) | https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/specific_groups/travelers/post-exposure_prophylaxis.html |
| **If Received PrEP** | **2 vaccine doses** (days 0, 3), **No RIG needed** | https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/specific_groups/travelers/post-exposure_prophylaxis.html |
| **Availability** | **RIG often unavailable** in developing countries | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/rabies |
