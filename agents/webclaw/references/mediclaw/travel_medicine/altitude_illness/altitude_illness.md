## Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Onset** | **6-12 hours** after ascent | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Lake Louise Criteria** | **Headache** + **=1 of**: **Nausea/Vomiting**, **Fatigue/Weakness**, **Dizziness/Lightheadedness**, **Insomnia** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Treatment** | **Stop ascent**, **Acetazolamide** (125-250 mg BID), **Dexamethasone** (4 mg q6h for severe), **Descend if worsening** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |

## High-Altitude Cerebral Edema (HACE)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Life-threatening** cerebral edema | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Symptoms** | **Ataxia** (cardinal sign), **Confusion**, **Altered mental status**, **Severe headache**, **Seizures**, **Coma** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Treatment** | **Immediate descent**, **Dexamethasone** (8 mg IV/IM/PO ? 4 mg q6h), **Oxygen**, **Hyperbaric bag** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |

## High-Altitude Pulmonary Edema (HAPE)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Non-cardiogenic pulmonary edema** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Symptoms** | **Dyspnea at rest**, **Dry cough** ? **Pink frothy sputum**, **Crackles**, **Cyanosis** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Treatment** | **Immediate descent**, **Nifedipine** (30 mg ER q12h or 20 mg ER q8h), **Oxygen**, **Hyperbaric bag**, **Avoid diuretics** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |

## Prevention of Altitude Illness
| Strategy | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Gradual Ascent** | **Above 2,500m**: Increase sleeping altitude by **=500m/day**, **Rest day q3-4 days** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **"Climb High, Sleep Low"** | **Day hikes to higher altitude**, **Sleep at lower altitude** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Acetazolamide Prophylaxis** | **125 mg BID** starting **day before ascent**, Continue **2-3 days** at maximum altitude | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Dexamethasone Prophylaxis** | **2 mg q6h** or **4 mg q12h** (for those intolerant of acetazolamide, Very rapid ascent) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
| **Avoid** | **Alcohol**, **Sedatives**, **Strenuous exertion** first 1-2 days | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/altitude-illness |
