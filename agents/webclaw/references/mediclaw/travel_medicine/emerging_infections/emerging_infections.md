## CDC Travel Health Notice Levels
| Level | Description | Recommendation | URL |
|-------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| **Level 1 (Watch)** | **Practice usual precautions** | **Be aware**, **Follow standard prevention** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/notices |
| **Level 2 (Alert)** | **Practice enhanced precautions** | **Consider avoiding non-essential travel** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/notices |
| **Level 3 (Warning)** | **Avoid non-essential travel** | **Avoid travel** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/notices |
