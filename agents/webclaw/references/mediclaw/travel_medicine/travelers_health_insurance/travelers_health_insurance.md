# Travel Medicine - Travel Health Insurance and Medical Evacuation

## Travel Health Insurance
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Importance** | **Most domestic health insurance does NOT cover international care** (or covers only emergency care with high out-of-pocket costs) | https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/your-health-abroad.html |
| **Coverage to Look For** | **Medical expenses**, **Emergency medical evacuation**, **Trip cancellation/interruption**, **24/7 assistance** | https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/your-health-abroad.html |
| **Medicare** | **Does NOT cover care outside US** (except limited situations) | https://www.medicare.gov/coverage/travel-outside-the-u-s |

## Medical Evacuation
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Transport to appropriate medical facility** (often to home country) | https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/your-health-abroad.html |
| **Cost** | **$50,000 - $250,000+** without insurance | https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/your-health-abroad.html |
| **Recommended Coverage** | **=$100,000** medical evacuation coverage | https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/your-health-abroad.html |
