# Travel Medicine - Travel Vaccines

## Routine Vaccines (Update Before Travel)
| Vaccine | Recommendation | URL |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Influenza** | **Annually** (most common vaccine-preventable disease in travelers) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Tdap/Td** | **Tdap once**, then **Td booster q10 years** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **MMR** | **=1 dose** if born =1957 (unless evidence of immunity) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **Varicella** | **2 doses** if born =1980 (unless evidence of immunity) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |
| **COVID-19** | **Up to date** per current CDC guidelines | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/covid-19/clinical-considerations/index.html |
| **Pneumococcal** | **Adults =65** or **high-risk** | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/pneumo/hcp/recommendations.html |
| **Hepatitis B** | **Universal** for adults 19-59 | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/adult.html |

## Required Vaccines
| Vaccine | Required For | Schedule | URL |
|---------|--------------|----------|-----|
| **Yellow Fever** | **Sub-Saharan Africa**, **Tropical South America** (and some countries require proof of vaccination for entry) | **Single dose** (lifelong protection for most) | https://www.cdc.gov/yellowfever/index.html |
| **Meningococcal (MenACWY)** | **Hajj/Umrah** (Saudi Arabia), **Meningitis belt** (sub-Saharan Africa) | **Single dose** (booster q5 years if ongoing risk) | https://www.cdc.gov/meningococcal/index.html |
| **Polio** | **Endemic countries** (Afghanistan, Pakistan) may require proof of vaccination | **Single booster** for adults | https://www.cdc.gov/polio/index.html |

## Recommended Vaccines (Based on Risk)
| Vaccine | Indications | Schedule | URL |
|---------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Hepatitis A** | **Most travelers** to developing countries | **2 doses** (0, 6-12 months) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/vpd/hepa/index.html |
| **Typhoid** | **South Asia**, **Africa**, **Latin America** | **IM** (single dose, Booster q2y) OR **Oral** (4 doses, Booster q5y) | https://www.cdc.gov/typhoid-fever/index.html |
| **Japanese Encephalitis** | **Rural Asia**, **=1 month stay** (or shorter if extensive outdoor exposure) | **2 doses** (0, 28 days) | https://www.cdc.gov/japaneseencephalitis/index.html |
| **Rabies** | **High-risk activities** (animal handling, Caving), **Remote areas** with limited medical access | **Pre-exposure**: 2 doses (0, 7 days) | https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/index.html |
| **Cholera** | **Humanitarian workers**, **Travel to active outbreak areas** | **Single dose oral** (CVD 103-HgR) | https://www.cdc.gov/cholera/index.html |
| **Tick-Borne Encephalitis** | **Forest/grassland exposure** in endemic areas (Europe, Asia) | **3 doses** (0, 1-3 months, 5-12 months) | https://www.cdc.gov/tick-borne-encephalitis/index.html |

## Yellow Fever Vaccine
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Vaccine** | **Live attenuated** (17D strain) | https://www.cdc.gov/yellowfever/vaccine/index.html |
| **Efficacy** | **>99%** (lifelong protection) | https://www.cdc.gov/yellowfever/vaccine/index.html |
| **Contraindications** | **Age <6 months**, **Anaphylaxis to vaccine components**, **Immunocompromise**, **Thymus disorder** | https://www.cdc.gov/yellowfever/vaccine/contraindications.html |
| **Precautions** | **Age =60** (first-time vaccinees), **Age 6-8 months**, **Pregnancy**, **Breastfeeding** | https://www.cdc.gov/yellowfever/vaccine/contraindications.html |
| **International Certificate** | **"Yellow Card"** (ICVP) valid **10 days after vaccination**, **Lifetime validity** | https://www.cdc.gov/yellowfever/vaccine/vaccine-certificate.html |
| **YF-VAX Shortage** | **Fractional dosing** (0.1 mL) may be used under IND protocol at designated clinics | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/yellow-fever-vaccine-shortage |

## Vaccine Administration Timing
| Timing | Vaccines | URL |
|--------|----------|-----|
| **=4 weeks before** | **Yellow Fever**, **MMR**, **Varicella** (live vaccines - space by 28 days if not given simultaneously) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/hcp/acip-recs/general-recs/timing.html |
| **=2 weeks before** | **Inactivated vaccines** (Hepatitis A, Typhoid, JE, Rabies) | https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/hcp/acip-recs/general-recs/timing.html |
| **Last-minute** | **Some protection** even if given close to departure; **Accelerated schedules** for Hepatitis A/B, Rabies | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/last-minute-travelers |
