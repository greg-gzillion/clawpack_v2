# Travel Medicine - Overview

## What is Travel Medicine?
Travel medicine is the medical specialty concerned with the prevention and management of health problems associated with international travel. It encompasses pre-travel risk assessment, vaccinations, malaria prophylaxis, traveler's diarrhea prevention and treatment, and post-travel evaluation of ill travelers.
