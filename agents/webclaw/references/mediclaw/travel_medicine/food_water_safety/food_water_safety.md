# Travel Medicine - Food and Water Safety

## Water Safety
| Recommendation | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **Safe Water Sources** | **Bottled water** (sealed), **Boiled water** (1 minute rolling boil), **Filtered water** (=0.2 micron pore size), **Chemically treated** (iodine, chlorine dioxide) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/food-water-safety |
| **Unsafe Water Sources** | **Tap water**, **Ice**, **Fountain drinks**, **Freshly squeezed juice** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/food-water-safety |
| **Brushing Teeth** | **Use bottled/treated water** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/food-water-safety |
| **Swimming** | **Avoid swallowing water** (pools, lakes, oceans) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/food-water-safety |

## Food Safety
| Recommendation | Description | URL |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| **"Boil it, cook it, peel it, or forget it"** | **Eat only thoroughly cooked food**, **Peel fruits/vegetables yourself** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/food-water-safety |
| **Avoid** | **Raw/undercooked meat/seafood**, **Unpasteurized dairy**, **Street food** (unless steaming hot), **Buffet food** (held at unsafe temperatures), **Raw leafy vegetables** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/food-water-safety |
| **Safe Foods** | **Piping hot foods**, **Bread/tortillas**, **Fruits you peel yourself** (bananas, oranges), **Canned foods** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/food-water-safety |

## Water Treatment Methods
| Method | Efficacy | Notes | URL |
|--------|----------|-------|-----|
| **Boiling** | **>99.9%** (bacteria, viruses, parasites) | **1 minute rolling boil** (3 minutes at altitude >2,000m) | https://www.cdc.gov/healthywater/drinking/travel/backcountry_water_treatment.html |
| **Filtration** | **>99.9%** (bacteria, parasites), **Variable for viruses** | **Pore size =0.2 microns**; **Add chemical treatment for viruses** | https://www.cdc.gov/healthywater/drinking/travel/backcountry_water_treatment.html |
| **Chlorine Dioxide** | **>99.9%** (bacteria, viruses), **Moderate for parasites** | **More effective than iodine**, **Better taste** | https://www.cdc.gov/healthywater/drinking/travel/backcountry_water_treatment.html |
| **Iodine** | **>99.9%** (bacteria, viruses), **Poor for Cryptosporidium** | **Contraindicated in pregnancy**, **Thyroid disease**, **Avoid long-term use** | https://www.cdc.gov/healthywater/drinking/travel/backcountry_water_treatment.html |
| **UV Light** | **>99.9%** (bacteria, viruses, parasites) | **Requires clear water**, **Batteries** | https://www.cdc.gov/healthywater/drinking/travel/backcountry_water_treatment.html |
