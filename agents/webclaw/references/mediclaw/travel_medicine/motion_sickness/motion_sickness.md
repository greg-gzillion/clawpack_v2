# Travel Medicine - Motion Sickness

## Overview
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Nausea, vomiting, dizziness** due to **vestibular-visual mismatch** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Pathophysiology** | **Conflict between visual, vestibular, and proprioceptive inputs** ? **Autonomic symptoms** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Risk Factors** | **Age 2-12** (peak), **Female sex**, **Migraine**, **Pregnancy**, **Anxiety**, **Genetics** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |

## Prevention of Motion Sickness
| Strategy | Recommendation | URL |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| **Behavioral** | **Fix gaze on horizon**, **Minimize head movement**, **Sit in front** (car), **Over wing** (plane), **Midship** (boat) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Avoid** | **Reading**, **Screens**, **Alcohol**, **Heavy meals** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Ginger** | **1-2 g** 30-60 min before travel (moderate evidence) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Acupressure** | **P6 (Neiguan)** point (limited evidence) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |

## Pharmacotherapy for Motion Sickness
| Medication | Dose | Onset | Duration | Side Effects | URL |
|------------|------|-------|----------|--------------|-----|
| **Scopolamine Patch** | **1 patch behind ear** | **4-6 hours** | **72 hours** | **Dry mouth**, **Blurred vision**, **Drowsiness**, **Confusion** (elderly) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine)** | **50-100 mg** q4-6h | **30-60 min** | **4-6 hours** | **Drowsiness**, **Dry mouth** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Meclizine (Antivert)** | **25-50 mg** daily | **1 hour** | **24 hours** | **Drowsiness**, **Dry mouth** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Promethazine** | **25 mg** q6-8h | **30-60 min** | **4-6 hours** | **Significant sedation**, **Contraindicated <2 years** | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
| **Ondansetron** | **4-8 mg** q8h | **30-60 min** | **8 hours** | **Not effective for motion sickness** (only for nausea after it occurs) | https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/page/motion-sickness |
