## Acne Treatment
| Severity | First-Line | Second-Line | URL |
|----------|------------|-------------|-----|
| Comedonal | Topical retinoids (Tretinoin, Adapalene, Tazarotene, Trifarotene) | Azelaic acid; Salicylic acid | https://www.aad.org/guidelines |
| Mild-Moderate Inflammatory | Topical retinoid + Benzoyl peroxide ± Topical antibiotic | Add oral antibiotic; Hormonal therapy | https://www.aad.org/guidelines |
| Moderate-Severe | Oral antibiotic + Topical retinoid + Benzoyl peroxide | Oral isotretinoin; Hormonal therapy | https://www.aad.org/guidelines |
| Severe Nodulocystic | Oral isotretinoin | Oral antibiotic + Topical therapy (bridge) | https://www.aad.org/guidelines |

## Oral Antibiotics for Acne

## Rosacea Subtypes
| Subtype | Clinical Features | URL |
|---------|-------------------|-----|
| Erythematotelangiectatic (ETR) | Flushing, persistent centrofacial erythema, telangiectasias | https://www.aad.org |
| Papulopustular (PPR) | Erythema + inflammatory papules/pustules | https://www.aad.org |
| Phymatous | Rhinophyma (bulbous nose), skin thickening | https://www.aad.org |
| Ocular | Blepharitis, conjunctivitis, keratitis | https://www.aad.org |
| Granulomatous | Firm, yellow-brown papules | https://www.aad.org |

## Rosacea Treatment
| Subtype | Treatment | URL |
|---------|-----------|-----|

## Hidradenitis Suppurativa
