# Dermatology - Vasculitis and Purpura

## Cutaneous Small-Vessel Vasculitis
| Type | Key Features | URL |
|------|--------------|-----|
| IgA Vasculitis (Henoch-Schönlein Purpura) | Palpable purpura (buttocks, lower extremities); Arthritis; Abdominal pain; IgA nephropathy; Children > adults | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482336/ |
| Urticarial Vasculitis | Urticarial lesions lasting >24 hours; Burning rather than itching; Residual purpura/hyperpigmentation; Hypocomplementemic vs normocomplementemic | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482336/ |
| Erythema Elevatum Diutinum | Persistent red-violaceous papules/plaques on extensor surfaces; Associated with IgA gammopathy, HIV, autoimmune disease | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482336/ |
| Granulomatosis with Polyangiitis (GPA) | Necrotizing granulomatous vasculitis; c-ANCA/PR3+; Upper/lower respiratory tract; Kidneys; Cutaneous: palpable purpura, nodules, ulcers | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557827/ |
| Eosinophilic Granulomatosis with Polyangiitis (EGPA, Churg-Strauss) | Asthma; Eosinophilia; Neuropathy; p-ANCA/MPO+ (40%); Cutaneous: palpable purpura, nodules | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557827/ |
| Microscopic Polyangiitis | Necrotizing vasculitis; p-ANCA/MPO+; Glomerulonephritis; Pulmonary hemorrhage; Cutaneous: palpable purpura | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK557827/ |

## Medium-Vessel Vasculitis
| Type | Key Features | URL |
|------|--------------|-----|
| Polyarteritis Nodosa | Necrotizing arteritis; Skin: livedo reticularis, nodules, ulcers, digital ischemia; Systemic: renal, GI, neurologic | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482157/ |
| Cutaneous Polyarteritis Nodosa | Limited to skin; No systemic involvement; Livedo reticularis, nodules, ulcers on lower extremities | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK482157/ |
| Kawasaki Disease | Children <5 years; Fever >5 days; Conjunctival injection; Oral changes; Cervical lymphadenopathy; Polymorphous rash; Extremity changes | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537163/ |

## Purpura Classification
| Type | Mechanism | Examples | URL |
|------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Palpable Purpura | Inflammation/vascular damage | Vasculitis, emboli | https://www.aad.org |
| Non-Palpable Purpura | Hemorrhage without inflammation | Thrombocytopenia, coagulation disorders, senile purpura, steroid purpura | https://www.aad.org |
| Retiform Purpura | Occlusion of dermal vessels | Calciphylaxis, warfarin necrosis, heparin necrosis, catastrophic APS, purpura fulminans, cholesterol emboli | https://www.aad.org |
| Pigmented Purpuric Dermatoses | Capillaritis | Schamberg disease, Majocchi disease, Gougerot-Blum, Lichen aureus | https://www.aad.org |
