## Pharmacologic Stress Agents
| Agent | Mechanism | Duration | Contraindications | URL |
|-------|-----------|----------|-------------------|-----|
| **Regadenoson (Lexiscan)** | **A2A adenosine receptor agonist** | 2-3 min | **High-grade AV block**, **Hypotension**, **Bronchospasm** (caution) | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/regadenoson |
| **Adenosine** | **Non-selective adenosine receptor agonist** | <10 sec | **High-grade AV block**, **Hypotension**, **Bronchospasm** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/adenosine |
| **Dipyridamole** | **Inhibits adenosine reuptake** | 20-30 min | **Bronchospasm**, **Hypotension** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/dipyridamole |
| **Dobutamine** | **Ô1 agonist** | 2-10 min | **Arrhythmias**, **Hypertension**, **Recent MI** | https://www.fda.gov/drugs/postmarket-drug-safety-information-patients-and-providers/dobutamine |

## MPI Interpretation
| Pattern | Description | Clinical Significance | URL |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|-----|
| **Normal** | Homogeneous tracer uptake at stress and rest | **Low risk** (<1% annual event rate) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/stable-ischemic-heart-disease |
| **Reversible Defect** | ? Uptake on stress, Normalizes on rest | **Ischemia** (viable myocardium) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/stable-ischemic-heart-disease |
| **Fixed Defect** | ? Uptake on stress and rest | **Infarction** or **Hibernating myocardium** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/stable-ischemic-heart-disease |
| **Reverse Redistribution** | Normal stress, ? Rest | **Artifact**, **Prior MI**, **Non-obstructive CAD** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/stable-ischemic-heart-disease |
| **Transient Ischemic Dilation (TID)** | **LV cavity larger on stress than rest** | **Severe multivessel CAD** (high-risk finding) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/stable-ischemic-heart-disease |
| **Increased Lung Uptake** | ? Tracer in lungs on stress | **LV dysfunction**, **Severe CAD** | https://www.acc.org/guidelines/hubs/stable-ischemic-heart-disease |
