## Biliary Atresia vs Neonatal Hepatitis
| Feature | Biliary Atresia | Neonatal Hepatitis | URL |
|---------|-----------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Hepatic Uptake** | **Good** | **Poor** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Bowel Activity** | **Absent** at 24h | **Present** (may be delayed) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Phenobarbital Pre-treatment** | **Essential** (5 mg/kg/day x5 days) to enhance excretion | Essential | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Clinical Significance** | **Surgical emergency** (Kasai procedure before 60 days) | Medical management | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
