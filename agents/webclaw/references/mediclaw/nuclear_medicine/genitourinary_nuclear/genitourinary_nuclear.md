## Captopril Renogram
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Indication** | **Diagnose renovascular hypertension** (renal artery stenosis) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Protocol** | **Baseline study**, then **Captopril challenge** (ACE inhibitor) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Positive Study** | **Worsening of function/clearance** after captopril | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Mechanism** | ACEi **reduces GFR** in post-stenotic kidney by blocking angiotensin II-mediated efferent arteriolar constriction | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Sensitivity/Specificity** | **90% / 95%** (in appropriate patients) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
