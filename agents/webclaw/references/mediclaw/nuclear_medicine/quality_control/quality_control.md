# Nuclear Medicine - Quality Control

## Gamma Camera Quality Control
| Test | Frequency | Acceptable Range | URL |
|------|-----------|------------------|-----|
| **Uniformity (Flood)** | **Daily** | **Integral uniformity <5%** | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Spatial Resolution** | **Weekly** | **FWHM = manufacturer spec** | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Center of Rotation** | **Weekly** | **<1 pixel variation** | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Energy Resolution** | **Monthly** | **FWHM <12% for Tc-99m** | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Sensitivity** | **Annually** | **Within 10% of baseline** | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Linearity** | **Annually** | **Response linear within 10%** | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
