# Nuclear Medicine - Therapy and Radioimmunotherapy

## I-131 Therapy for Thyroid Disease
| Indication | Dose | Goal | URL |
|------------|------|------|-----|
| **Graves Disease** | 10-15 mCi (or calculated dose) | **Ablate thyroid** ? Hypothyroidism | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Toxic Multinodular Goiter** | 15-30 mCi | **Ablate hyperfunctioning nodules** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Thyroid Cancer Remnant Ablation** | 30-100 mCi | **Ablate residual normal thyroid tissue** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1448 |
| **Thyroid Cancer Metastases** | 100-200 mCi | **Treat metastatic disease** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1448 |

## I-131 Therapy Precautions
| Precaution | Duration | URL |
|------------|----------|-----|
| **Pregnancy Test** | **Required** within 72h before therapy | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Breastfeeding** | **Complete cessation** (for this child) | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Radiation Safety** | **Sleep alone**, **Avoid close contact** (especially children/pregnant women), **Separate bathroom**, **Flush twice** | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
| **Duration of Precautions** | **Varies by dose** (typically 3-7 days for Graves, Longer for cancer therapy) | https://www.nrc.gov/materials/miau/med-use-toolkit.html |
