# Nuclear Medicine - Musculoskeletal Imaging

## Bone Scintigraphy (Bone Scan)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Radiopharmaceutical** | **Tc-99m MDP** (Methylene Diphosphonate) or **Tc-99m HDP** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Mechanism** | **Chemisorption** onto **hydroxyapatite crystals** in bone matrix | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Phases** | **Flow** (0-60s), **Blood pool** (5-10 min), **Delayed** (2-4 hours) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Indications** | **Metastases**, **Osteomyelitis**, **Fracture**, **Prosthetic loosening**, **CRPS**, **Paget disease** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |

## Three-Phase Bone Scan Interpretation
| Phase | Increased Uptake | Decreased Uptake | URL |
|-------|-----------------|------------------|-----|
| **Flow** | **Hyperemia** (infection, inflammation, Tumor) | **Ischemia**, **Infarction** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Blood Pool** | **Hyperemia** | **Ischemia** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Delayed** | **Osteoblastic activity** (infection, Tumor, Fracture, Arthritis) | **Osteolytic lesions** (multiple myeloma, Aggressive metastases), **Avascular necrosis** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |

## Common Bone Scan Patterns
| Pattern | Description | Common Causes | URL |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-----|
| **Superscan** | **Diffuse intense skeletal uptake**, **Absent renal activity** | **Widespread metastases** (prostate, breast), **Hyperparathyroidism**, **Paget disease** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **"Flare Phenomenon"** | **Increased uptake** after initiation of therapy | **Healing response** to chemotherapy/hormonal therapy | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Linear Uptake** | Linear pattern | **Fracture**, **Shin splints** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Focal Uptake** | Discrete focus | **Metastasis**, **Osteomyelitis**, **Paget disease** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **"Tram-Track" Sign** | Parallel linear uptake | **Paget disease** (long bones) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **"Tie" Sign** | Sternum uptake | **Paget disease**, **Metastasis** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **"Mickey Mouse" Sign** | Vertebral body + posterior elements | **Paget disease** of vertebra | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |

## Osteomyelitis Imaging
| Modality | Sensitivity | Specificity | Comments | URL |
|----------|------------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Three-Phase Bone Scan** | **>90%** | **50-70%** | **High NPV**; Can be false-positive in fracture, Neuropathic joint | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **WBC Scan (In-111 or Tc-99m HMPAO)** | **80-90%** | **80-90%** | **Improved specificity**; False-negative in chronic infection | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Bone Marrow Scan (Tc-99m Sulfur Colloid)** | N/A | N/A | **Combined with WBC scan** (WBC uptake without marrow uptake = Infection) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **FDG PET/CT** | **>90%** | **>90%** | **Excellent accuracy**, Especially in **diabetic foot**, **Prosthetic joint** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |

## Prosthetic Joint Evaluation
| Finding | Interpretation | URL |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Periprosthetic uptake on delayed bone scan** | **Normal** up to **1 year post-op** (especially around stem) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **WBC/Marrow mismatch** | **Infection** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **WBC/Marrow match** | **Aseptic loosening** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **FDG uptake at bone-prosthesis interface** | **Infection** > Loosening | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
