## Thyroid Scan Patterns
| Pattern | Uptake | Common Causes | URL |
|---------|--------|---------------|-----|
| **Diffuse Increased** | **High** (>35% at 24h) | **Graves disease** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Focal Increased** | High in nodule, Suppressed background | **Autonomous "hot" nodule** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Diffuse Decreased** | **Low** (<5%) | **Subacute thyroiditis**, **Painless thyroiditis**, **Amiodarone-induced** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Focal Decreased** | Low in nodule | **"Cold" nodule** (5-10% malignancy risk) | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |
| **Heterogeneous** | Patchy uptake | **Multinodular goiter**, **Hashimoto thyroiditis** | https://www.thyroid.org/professionals/ata-professional-guidelines/ |

## Parathyroid Imaging
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Radiopharmaceutical** | **Tc-99m Sestamibi** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Technique** | **Dual-phase** (early and delayed) or **Subtraction** (Tc-99m Sestamibi - I-123 or Tc-99m Pertechnetate) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Interpretation** | **Focal persistent uptake** on delayed images = **Parathyroid adenoma** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **SPECT/CT** | **Improves localization** for surgical planning | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Sensitivity** | **80-90%** for single adenoma, **Lower** for multigland disease | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |

## Adrenal Cortical Imaging

## Adrenal Medullary Imaging (MIBG)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Radiopharmaceutical** | **I-123 MIBG** or **I-131 MIBG** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Indication** | **Pheochromocytoma**, **Paraganglioma**, **Neuroblastoma** | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Preparation** | **Thyroid blockade** (SSKI or Lugol solution), **Avoid interfering medications** (labetalol, TCAs, sympathomimetics) | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
| **Sensitivity** | **85-90%** for pheochromocytoma | https://www.snmmi.org/Clinical-Practice/Guidelines |
