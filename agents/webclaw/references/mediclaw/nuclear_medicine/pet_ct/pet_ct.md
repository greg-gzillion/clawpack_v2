## Deauville Criteria (FDG PET for Lymphoma)
| Score | Definition | Interpretation | URL |
|-------|------------|----------------|-----|
| **1** | **No uptake** (or = background) | **Complete metabolic response (CMR)** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **2** | Uptake **= mediastinal blood pool** | **CMR** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **3** | Uptake **> mediastinum but = liver** | **CMR** (likely) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **4** | Uptake **moderately > liver** | **Partial metabolic response (PMR)** or **Progressive disease** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **5** | Uptake **markedly > liver** and/or **new lesions** | **Progressive metabolic disease (PMD)** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **X** | New uptake **unlikely related to lymphoma** | **Inflammation**, **Infection** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |

## Lugano Classification (Lymphoma Response)
| Response | Definition | URL |
|----------|------------|-----|
| **Complete Metabolic Response (CMR)** | **Deauville 1, 2, or 3** (with or without residual mass) | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **Partial Metabolic Response (PMR)** | **Deauville 4 or 5** with **reduced uptake** compared to baseline | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **No Metabolic Response (NMR)** | **No change** in uptake | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |
| **Progressive Metabolic Disease (PMD)** | **Deauville 4 or 5** with **increased uptake** and/or **new lesions** | https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1480 |

## PERCIST Criteria (Solid Tumors)
| Response Category | Definition | URL |
|------------------|------------|-----|
| **Complete Metabolic Response (CMR)** | **Complete resolution** of FDG uptake (= background) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2743118/ |
| **Partial Metabolic Response (PMR)** | **=30% decrease in SULpeak** (minimum 0.8 SUL units) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2743118/ |
| **Stable Metabolic Disease (SMD)** | **<30% decrease** and **<30% increase** in SULpeak | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2743118/ |
| **Progressive Metabolic Disease (PMD)** | **=30% increase in SULpeak** (minimum 0.8 SUL units) OR **new lesions** | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2743118/ |
