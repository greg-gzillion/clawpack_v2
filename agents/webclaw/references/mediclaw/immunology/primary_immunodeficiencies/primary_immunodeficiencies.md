## Antibody Deficiencies (B Cell)
| Disease | Gene | Immunologic Defect | Clinical Features | URL |
|---------|------|--------------------|-------------------|-----|
| **X-Linked Agammaglobulinemia (XLA)** | **BTK** | **Absent B cells**, **Very low all immunoglobulins** | Recurrent sinopulmonary infections after 6 months (maternal IgG wanes), **Absent tonsils** | https://primaryimmune.org |
| **Common Variable Immunodeficiency (CVID)** | Multiple (TACI, ICOS, BAFFR) | **Low IgG + Low IgA and/or IgM**, Impaired antibody response | Recurrent sinopulmonary infections, **Autoimmunity** (ITP, AIHA), **Lymphoproliferation**, **Enteropathy**, **Malignancy** | |
| **Selective IgA Deficiency** | Unknown | **IgA <7 mg/dL**, Normal IgG, IgM | **Most common PID** (1:300-500); Often asymptomatic; Sinopulmonary infections, Allergies, Autoimmunity | |
| **IgG Subclass Deficiency** | Unknown | Low one or more IgG subclasses | Recurrent sinopulmonary infections (especially IgG2, IgG3) | |
| **Specific Antibody Deficiency (SAD)** | Unknown | **Impaired response to polysaccharide vaccines** | Recurrent sinopulmonary infections | |
| **Transient Hypogammaglobulinemia of Infancy** | Unknown | Low IgG, IgA, IgM (prolonged physiologic nadir) | Recurrent infections; **Resolves by age 2-4 years** | |
