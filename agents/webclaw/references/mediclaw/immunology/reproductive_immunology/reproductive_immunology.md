## Immunologic Causes of Recurrent Pregnancy Loss (RPL)
| Cause | Mechanism | Diagnosis | Management | URL |
|-------|-----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Antiphospholipid Syndrome (APS)** | **Anti-ﾟ2GPI, Anticardiolipin, Lupus anticoagulant** ? **Thrombosis**, **Complement activation**, **Impaired trophoblast invasion** | **Laboratory criteria** (positive x2, =12 weeks apart) + **Clinical criteria** (=3 losses <10 weeks, =1 loss =10 weeks, Preterm birth <34 weeks for severe preeclampsia/placental insufficiency) | **Lovenox + Low-dose aspirin** | https://www.rheumatology.org/APS |
| **Alloimmune Rejection** | Maternal immune response against **paternal HLA antigens** | **No validated diagnostic test**; Research use: HLA compatibility, KIR/HLA-C mismatch, NK cell testing | **No proven therapy** (IVIG, LIT, TNF inhibitors controversial) | |
| **Autoimmune Thyroid Disease** | **Anti-TPO antibodies** associated with increased miscarriage risk | **TSH, Anti-TPO** | **Levothyroxine** (goal TSH <2.5) | https://www.thyroid.org |
| **Celiac Disease** | **Anti-tTG IgA** ? Malabsorption, Inflammation | **Anti-tTG IgA, Total IgA, EGD with biopsy** | **Gluten-free diet** | https://celiac.org |

## Neonatal Alloimmune Disorders
| Disorder | Mechanism | Diagnosis | Management | URL |
|----------|-----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| **Hemolytic Disease of the Fetus/Newborn (HDFN)** | **Maternal IgG antibodies against fetal RBC antigens** (Rh(D), Kell, ABO) cross placenta ? **Hemolysis**, **Anemia**, **Hydrops** | **Maternal antibody screen**, **Fetal MCA Doppler** (anemia), **Cord blood DAT** | **Rh(D)**: **RhoGAM prophylaxis** (28 weeks, Postpartum, Sensitizing events); **Intrauterine transfusion**; **Phototherapy**, **Exchange transfusion**, **IVIG** postnatally | https://www.acog.org |
| **Neonatal Alloimmune Thrombocytopenia (NAIT)** | **Maternal antibodies against fetal platelet antigens** (HPA-1a, HPA-5b) ? **Thrombocytopenia**, **ICH** (10-20%) | **Neonatal thrombocytopenia**, **Maternal anti-platelet antibodies**, **Parental HPA typing** | **IVIG 1 g/kg/day x 1-2 days**, **HPA-compatible platelets**, **Cranial ultrasound**; **Future pregnancies**: IVIG ｱ Prednisone | https://www.ash.org/NAIT |
| **Neonatal Alloimmune Neutropenia** | Maternal antibodies against fetal neutrophil antigens (HNA-1a, HNA-1b) ? **Neutropenia** | **Neonatal neutropenia**, **Maternal anti-neutrophil antibodies** | **Self-limited** (weeks-months); **G-CSF** if severe infections | |

## Autoimmune Diseases in Pregnancy
| Disease | Effect of Pregnancy on Disease | Effect of Disease on Pregnancy | Management | URL |
|---------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|------------|-----|
| **SLE** | **Variable**; 30-50% flare (especially if active at conception) | **Increased risk**: Preeclampsia, Preterm birth, IUGR, **Neonatal lupus** (anti-Ro/La ? Congenital heart block) | **Hydroxychloroquine** (continue), **Azathioprine**, **Tacrolimus**, **Corticosteroids**; **AVOID** Mycophenolate, Methotrexate, Cyclophosphamide | https://www.lupus.org/pregnancy |
| **Antiphospholipid Syndrome** | Increased thrombotic risk | **Increased risk**: Miscarriage, Fetal death, IUGR, Preeclampsia, Preterm birth | **Lovenox + Low-dose aspirin** | |
| **Rheumatoid Arthritis** | **Improves in 50-75%** (3rd trimester); **Flares postpartum** | Minimal | **Hydroxychloroquine**, **Sulfasalazine**, **Corticosteroids**, **TNF inhibitors** (certolizumab preferred - no placental transfer) | https://www.rheumatology.org/pregnancy |
| **Myasthenia Gravis** | **Variable**; 1/3 improve, 1/3 stable, 1/3 worsen | **Transient neonatal myasthenia** (10-20%) due to maternal AChR antibodies | **Pyridostigmine**, **Corticosteroids**, **IVIG/PLEX**; **Avoid magnesium sulfate** | https://myasthenia.org |
| **ITP** | May worsen | **Neonatal thrombocytopenia** (10-20%) | **IVIG**, **Corticosteroids**; **Avoid splenectomy**; **Neonatal platelet monitoring** | https://www.ash.org/ITP-pregnancy |
