# Immunology - Autoimmune Diseases

## Mechanisms of Autoimmunity
| Mechanism | Description | Examples | URL |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **Molecular Mimicry** | Pathogen antigen resembles self-antigen ? Cross-reactive immune response | **Rheumatic fever** (S. pyogenes M protein ? Cardiac myosin), **Guillain-BarrÕ** (C. jejuni ? Gangliosides) | https://www.niaid.nih.gov |
| **Bystander Activation** | Inflammation ? Release of self-antigens ? Activation of autoreactive cells | **Autoimmune thyroiditis** (post-viral) | |
| **Epitope Spreading** | Initial immune response to one epitope ? Spread to other epitopes on same or different antigens | **SLE**, **Multiple sclerosis** | |
| **Loss of Central Tolerance** | Defective negative selection in thymus/bone marrow | **AIRE mutations** ? **APS-1** | |
| **Loss of Peripheral Tolerance** | Defective Treg function, Impaired anergy | **IPEX** (FOXP3 mutation) | |
| **Release of Sequestered Antigens** | Trauma/inflammation exposes previously hidden antigens | **Sympathetic ophthalmia** | |

## Organ-Specific Autoimmune Diseases
| Disease | Autoantigen | Pathogenic Mechanism | Key Features | URL |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|--------------|-----|
| **Graves Disease** | **TSH Receptor** | **Stimulating autoantibodies** (Type II) | Hyperthyroidism, Diffuse goiter, Ophthalmopathy | https://www.thyroid.org |
| **Hashimoto Thyroiditis** | **Thyroglobulin, TPO** | T cell-mediated (Type IV) + Autoantibodies | Hypothyroidism, Firm goiter, Lymphocytic infiltrate | |
| **Type 1 Diabetes** | **Insulin, GAD65, IA-2, ZnT8** | T cell-mediated (Type IV) | Hyperglycemia, Ketoacidosis, Autoantibodies | https://www.diabetes.org |
| **Myasthenia Gravis** | **Acetylcholine Receptor (AChR)** (85%), **MuSK** (5-10%) | **Blocking antibodies** (Type II) | Fluctuating weakness, Fatigability, Ptosis, Diplopia | https://myasthenia.org |
| **Goodpasture Syndrome** | **Type IV Collagen (a3 chain)** | Anti-GBM antibodies (Type II) | **Rapidly progressive GN + Diffuse alveolar hemorrhage** | https://www.kidney.org |
| **Pemphigus Vulgaris** | **Desmoglein 3** (mucosal), **Desmoglein 1** (skin) | IgG antibodies ? Acantholysis (Type II) | Flaccid bullae, Mucosal erosions, **Positive Nikolsky** | https://www.aad.org |
| **Bullous Pemphigoid** | **BP180, BP230** (hemidesmosome proteins) | IgG antibodies ? Subepidermal blister (Type II) | **Tense bullae**, Urticarial plaques, **Negative Nikolsky** | |
| **Multiple Sclerosis** | **Myelin proteins** (MBP, PLP, MOG) | T cell-mediated (Type IV) | **White matter lesions** (separated in time and space), Optic neuritis, Myelitis | https://www.nationalmssociety.org |
| **Autoimmune Hepatitis** | Unknown (hepatocyte antigens) | T cell-mediated (Type IV) | Elevated transaminases, Hypergammaglobulinemia, **Interface hepatitis** | https://www.aasld.org |
| **Primary Biliary Cholangitis** | **PDC-E2** (mitochondrial antigen) | T cell-mediated + **AMA** | **AMA positive**, Elevated Alk Phos, **Florid duct lesion** | |
| **Celiac Disease** | **Tissue Transglutaminase (tTG)** | T cell-mediated (Type IV) ? Villous atrophy | **HLA-DQ2/DQ8**, Anti-tTG IgA, Gluten-dependent enteropathy | https://celiac.org |

## Systemic Autoimmune Diseases

|---------|-------------|----------------------|--------------|-----|
| **Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE)** | **dsDNA**, **Sm**, **RNP**, **Ro/SSA**, **La/SSB**, **Histones** | **Immune complex (Type III)** + Type II | Malar rash, Arthritis, Serositis, Nephritis, **ANA positive** (98%) | https://www.lupus.org |
| **Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)** | **Citrullinated proteins** (anti-CCP), **IgG Fc** (RF) | T cell-mediated (Type IV) + Immune complex | **Symmetric polyarthritis**, Erosions, **Rheumatoid nodules** | https://www.rheumatology.org |
| **Sj—gren's Syndrome** | **Ro/SSA**, **La/SSB** | T cell-mediated (Type IV) | **Sicca syndrome** (dry eyes, Dry mouth), **Anti-Ro/La positive** | https://www.sjogrens.org |
| **Systemic Sclerosis (Scleroderma)** | **Topoisomerase I (Scl-70)** (diffuse), **Centromere** (limited) | Fibrosis, Vasculopathy | Skin thickening, **Raynaud's**, **ILD**, **PAH**, **Scleroderma renal crisis** | https://www.scleroderma.org |
| **Mixed Connective Tissue Disease (MCTD)** | **U1-RNP** | Overlap features | **High-titer anti-U1-RNP**, Overlap of SLE, SSc, PM | |
| **Antiphospholipid Syndrome (APS)** | **Ô2-Glycoprotein I**, **Cardiolipin** | Thrombosis, Pregnancy morbidity | **Lupus anticoagulant**, **Anticardiolipin**, **Anti-Ô2GPI** | https://www.rheumatology.org/APS |

| **IgG4-Related Disease** | Unknown | Lymphoplasmacytic infiltrate, **Storiform fibrosis** | **Elevated IgG4**, **Tumefactive lesions** (pancreas, Salivary, Retroperitoneum) | https://www.igG4relateddisease.org |
