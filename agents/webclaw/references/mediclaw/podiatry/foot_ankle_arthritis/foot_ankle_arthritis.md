# Podiatry - Foot and Ankle Arthritis

## Osteoarthritis
| Joint | Presentation | Treatment | URL |
|-------|--------------|-----------|-----|
| **Ankle OA** | **Post-traumatic** (most common), Pain with weight-bearing, Stiffness | **NSAIDs**, **Bracing**, **Intraarticular CS**, **Ankle fusion** or **Total ankle arthroplasty** | https://www.arthritisfoundation.org/arthritis/osteoarthritis |
| **Subtalar OA** | **Pain with uneven ground**, **Stiffness** | **NSAIDs**, **Orthotics**, **Subtalar fusion** | https://www.arthritisfoundation.org/arthritis/osteoarthritis |
| **Hallux Rigidus** | **Pain/stiffness** of 1st MTP, **Dorsal osteophyte** | **Stiff-soled shoe**, **Rocker bottom**, **Cheilectomy**, **Fusion** (MTP arthrodesis) | https://www.arthritisfoundation.org/arthritis/osteoarthritis |
| **Midfoot OA** | **Dorsal midfoot pain**, **Osteophytes** | **NSAIDs**, **Rigid orthotic**, **Midfoot fusion** | https://www.arthritisfoundation.org/arthritis/osteoarthritis |

## Rheumatoid Arthritis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Foot Involvement** | **>90%** of RA patients have foot involvement | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Rheumatoid-Arthritis |
| **Forefoot** | **Hallux valgus**, **MTP subluxation/dislocation**, **Hammertoes**, **Splayed foot** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Rheumatoid-Arthritis |
| **Hindfoot** | **Pes planovalgus** (posterior tibial tendon dysfunction), **Ankle arthritis** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Rheumatoid-Arthritis |
| **Treatment** | **DMARDs**, **Orthotics**, **Custom footwear**, **Surgical reconstruction** (forefoot arthroplasty, Fusion) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Rheumatoid-Arthritis |

## Gout
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Podagra** | **Acute inflammation of 1st MTP** (most common presentation) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Gout |
| **Diagnosis** | **Joint aspiration** (negatively birefringent needle-shaped crystals) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Gout |
| **Acute Treatment** | **NSAIDs**, **Colchicine**, **Corticosteroids** | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Gout |
| **Chronic Treatment** | **Allopurinol**, **Febuxostat** (target uric acid <6 mg/dL) | https://www.rheumatology.org/I-Am-A/Patient-Caregiver/Diseases-Conditions/Gout |
