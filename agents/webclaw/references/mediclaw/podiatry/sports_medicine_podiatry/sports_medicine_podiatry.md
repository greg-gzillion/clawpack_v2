# Podiatry - Sports Medicine in Podiatry

## Common Sports-Related Foot and Ankle Injuries
| Injury | Mechanism | Sport | Treatment | URL |
|--------|-----------|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Ankle Sprain** | **Inversion** | Basketball, Soccer, Volleyball | **Functional rehabilitation**, **Bracing**, **Proprioceptive training** | https://www.aapsm.org/ankle-sprain.html |
| **High Ankle Sprain (Syndesmosis)** | **External rotation**, **Dorsiflexion** | Football, Hockey, Skiing | **Non-weight bearing**, **Longer recovery**, **Surgery** (unstable) | https://www.aapsm.org/high-ankle-sprain.html |
| **Achilles Tendinopathy** | **Overuse**, **? Training** | Running, Basketball | **Eccentric strengthening**, **Activity modification** | https://www.aapsm.org/achilles-tendinopathy.html |
| **Plantar Fasciitis** | **Overuse**, **Tight Achilles** | Running, Dancing | **Stretching**, **Night splint**, **Orthotics** | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Stress Fracture** | **Repetitive loading**, **? Intensity** | Running, Basketball, Gymnastics | **Rest**, **Address underlying factors** | https://www.aapsm.org/stress-fractures.html |
| **Jones Fracture** | **Inversion**, **Repetitive stress** | Soccer, Basketball | **Surgical fixation** (athletes) | https://www.aapsm.org/jones-fracture.html |
| **Turf Toe** | **Hyperextension** of 1st MTP | Football (artificial turf) | **Stiff-soled shoe**, **Taping**, **Surgery** (grade III) | https://www.aapsm.org/turf-toe.html |
| **Lisfranc Injury** | **Axial load** on plantarflexed foot | Football, Equestrian | **Surgical fixation** (unstable) | https://www.aapsm.org/lisfranc-injury.html |

## Return to Running Guidelines
| Phase | Criteria | URL |
|-------|----------|-----|
| **Pain-Free Walking** | **No pain** with normal gait | https://www.aapsm.org/return-to-running.html |
| **Strength** | **=90% of contralateral strength** | https://www.aapsm.org/return-to-running.html |
| **Balance** | **Single-leg stance =30 sec** | https://www.aapsm.org/return-to-running.html |
| **Progression** | **Walk-jog**, **Jog-run**, **Full running** (10% increase per week) | https://www.aapsm.org/return-to-running.html |
| **Sport-Specific Drills** | **Agility**, **Cutting**, **Jumping** | https://www.aapsm.org/return-to-running.html |
