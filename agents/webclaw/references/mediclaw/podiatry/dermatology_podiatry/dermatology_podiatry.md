# Podiatry - Dermatology in Podiatry

## Common Skin Conditions of the Foot
| Condition | Description | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Tinea Pedis (Athlete's Foot)** | **Maceration, scaling, itching** between toes | **Topical antifungals** (clotrimazole, terbinafine), **Oral terbinafine** (severe) | https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/index.html |
| **Plantar Warts (Verruca Plantaris)** | **Hyperkeratotic papules** with **interrupted skin lines**, **Black dots** (thrombosed capillaries) | **Salicylic acid**, **Cryotherapy**, **Curettage**, **Cantharidin**, **Bleomycin** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/warts-overview |
| **Hyperkeratosis (Callus/Corn)** | **Thickened skin** from **pressure/friction** | **Debridement**, **Padding**, **Footwear modification**, **Orthotics** | https://www.apma.org/Patients/content.cfm?ItemNumber=992 |
| **Psoriasis** | **Well-demarcated plaques** with **silvery scale** | **Topical corticosteroids**, **Vitamin D analogs**, **Systemic therapy** | https://www.psoriasis.org/about-psoriasis/ |
| **Contact Dermatitis** | **Erythema**, **Vesicles**, **Pruritus** (allergic or irritant) | **Avoid trigger**, **Topical corticosteroids** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/contact-dermatitis |
| **Dyshidrotic Eczema** | **Pruritic vesicles** on soles | **Topical corticosteroids**, **Avoid irritants** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/dyshidrotic-eczema |
| **Hyperhidrosis** | **Excessive sweating** | **Aluminum chloride**, **Iontophoresis**, **Botox**, **Oral anticholinergics** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/hyperhidrosis-treatment |

## Differentiating Callus vs Wart
| Feature | Callus | Wart | URL |
|---------|--------|------|-----|
| **Skin Lines** | **Preserved** (continue through lesion) | **Interrupted** | https://www.apma.org/Patients/content.cfm?ItemNumber=992 |
| **Black Dots** | **Absent** | **Present** (thrombosed capillaries) | https://www.apma.org/Patients/content.cfm?ItemNumber=992 |
| **Pinch Test** | **No pain** | **Painful** with lateral compression | https://www.apma.org/Patients/content.cfm?ItemNumber=992 |
| **Paring** | **No bleeding** | **Pinpoint bleeding** | https://www.apma.org/Patients/content.cfm?ItemNumber=992 |
