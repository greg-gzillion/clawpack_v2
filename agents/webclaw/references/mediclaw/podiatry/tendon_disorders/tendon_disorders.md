## Achilles Tendon Disorders
| Condition | Mechanism | Symptoms | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Achilles Tendinopathy** | **Overuse**, **? Training intensity**, **Tight gastroc/soleus** | **Posterior heel pain**, **Morning stiffness**, **Thickened tendon** | **Eccentric strengthening**, **Activity modification**, **Heel lift**, **Shockwave**, **PRP** | https://www.aapsm.org/achilles-tendinopathy.html |
| **Insertional Achilles Tendinopathy** | **Overuse**, **Haglund deformity** | **Pain at calcaneal insertion**, **Worse with dorsiflexion** | **Eccentric strengthening** (below horizontal), **Heel lift**, **Shockwave**, **Surgical debridement** | https://www.aapsm.org/achilles-tendinopathy.html |
| **Achilles Tendon Rupture** | **Eccentric overload**, **Sudden push-off** | **"Pop"**, **Palpable gap**, **Weak plantarflexion**, **+ Thompson test** | **Surgical repair** (young/active) or **Functional rehabilitation** (older) | https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/achilles-tendon-rupture/ |

## Posterior Tibial Tendon Dysfunction (PTTD)
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Progressive tendinopathy** leading to **acquired adult flatfoot deformity** | https://www.aapsm.org/posterior-tibial-tendon-dysfunction.html |
| **Stages (Johnson and Strom)** | **Stage I**: Tenosynovitis, No deformity; **Stage II**: Flexible flatfoot; **Stage III**: Rigid flatfoot; **Stage IV**: Ankle valgus | https://www.orthobullets.com/foot-and-ankle/7005/foot-anatomy |
| **Symptoms** | **Medial ankle pain**, **Swelling**, **Difficulty with single-leg heel raise**, **"Too many toes" sign** | https://www.aapsm.org/posterior-tibial-tendon-dysfunction.html |
| **Treatment** | **Stage I**: Immobilization, PT, Orthotics; **Stage II**: Orthotics, Bracing (Arizona), FDL transfer, Medializing calcaneal osteotomy; **Stage III-IV**: Triple arthrodesis | https://www.aapsm.org/posterior-tibial-tendon-dysfunction.html |
