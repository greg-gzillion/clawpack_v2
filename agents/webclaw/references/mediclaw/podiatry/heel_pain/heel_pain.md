# Podiatry - Heel Pain

## Plantar Fasciitis
| Component | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Definition** | **Degenerative condition** of plantar fascia (not primarily inflammatory) | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Symptoms** | **First-step pain** in morning, **Tenderness at medial calcaneal tubercle**, **Worse after prolonged sitting** | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Risk Factors** | **Obesity**, **Prolonged standing**, **Tight Achilles**, **Pes cavus/planus**, **Running** | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Imaging** | **Ultrasound** (thickened fascia >4 mm), **MRI** (if atypical or refractory) | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |

## Treatment of Plantar Fasciitis
| Intervention | Evidence | URL |
|--------------|----------|-----|
| **Stretching** | **Plantar fascia-specific stretching**, **Achilles stretching** (strong evidence) | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Night Splint** | **Maintains dorsiflexion** (moderate evidence) | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Orthotics** | **Arch support**, **Heel cushion** | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **NSAIDs** | Short-term pain relief | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Corticosteroid Injection** | **Short-term relief** (=4-6 weeks), **Risk of fat pad atrophy/fascial rupture** | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Extracorporeal Shockwave Therapy (ESWT)** | **Strong evidence** for chronic cases | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **PRP Injection** | **Moderate evidence** | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |
| **Surgery** | **Plantar fasciotomy** (refractory cases, =6-12 months conservative) | https://www.aapsm.org/plantar-fasciitis.html |

## Other Causes of Heel Pain
| Condition | Key Features | Differentiation from Plantar Fasciitis | URL |
|-----------|--------------|---------------------------------------|-----|
| **Fat Pad Atrophy** | **Central heel pain**, **Thinning of fat pad**, **Older adults** | **Central** (vs medial) tenderness, **No first-step pain** | https://www.aapsm.org/heel-pain.html |
| **Calcaneal Stress Fracture** | **Insidious onset**, **Pain with squeeze test** | **+ Squeeze test**, **MRI diagnostic** | https://www.aapsm.org/heel-pain.html |
| **Tarsal Tunnel Syndrome** | **Burning pain**, **Paresthesias**, **+ Tinel** | **Neuropathic** quality, **Radiates** | https://www.aapsm.org/heel-pain.html |
| **Baxter Nerve Entrapment** | **Medial heel pain**, **Burning** | **Neuropathic** quality | https://www.aapsm.org/heel-pain.html |
| **Sever Disease** | **Pediatric** (8-14 years), **Calcaneal apophysitis** | **Squeeze test**, **Age** | https://www.aapsm.org/heel-pain.html |
