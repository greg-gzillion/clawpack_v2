# Podiatry - Pediatric Podiatry

## Common Pediatric Foot Conditions
| Condition | Age | Description | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|-----|-------------|-----------|-----|
| **Metatarsus Adductus** | **Birth-1 year** | **Medial deviation of forefoot** | **Stretching**, **Reverse-last shoes**, **Serial casting** (rigid), **Surgery** (refractory) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Calcaneovalgus Foot** | **Birth** | **Dorsiflexion and eversion** (foot against anterior tibia) | **Observation** (resolves spontaneously), **Stretching** | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Congenital Vertical Talus** | **Birth** | **Rigid rocker-bottom foot** | **Serial casting**, **Surgical correction** | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Clubfoot (Talipes Equinovarus)** | **Birth** | **CAVE**: **C**avus, **A**dductus, **V**arus, **E**quinus | **Ponseti method** (serial casting + Achilles tenotomy), **Bracing** | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Flexible Flatfoot** | **Toddler/Child** | **Arch present when non-weight-bearing**, **Collapses with weight-bearing** | **Observation** (asymptomatic), **Orthotics** (symptomatic) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Rigid Flatfoot** | **Child/Adolescent** | **Arch absent** even when non-weight-bearing | **Evaluate for tarsal coalition** (CT), **Orthotics**, **Surgery** | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Sever Disease** | **8-14 years** | **Calcaneal apophysitis** | **Activity modification**, **Stretching**, **Heel lifts**, **Self-limited** | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Freiberg Infraction** | **Adolescent females** | **Osteonecrosis of 2nd metatarsal head** | **Conservative** (stiff-soled shoe, Activity modification), **Surgery** (refractory) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Kohler Disease** | **3-7 years** | **Osteonecrosis of navicular** | **Observation** (self-limited), **Immobilization** (painful) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |

## Clubfoot (Ponseti Method)
| Phase | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| **Serial Casting** | **Weekly cast changes** to correct CAVE (cavus, adductus, varus, equinus) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Achilles Tenotomy** | **Percutaneous release** of Achilles tendon (corrects equinus) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Final Cast** | **3 weeks** post-tenotomy in corrected position | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Bracing** | **Denis Browne bar** (23h/day x3 months ? nights until age 4) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
| **Pirani Score** | **Assesses severity** (midfoot contracture, hindfoot contracture) | https://www.orthobullets.com/pediatrics/7005/pediatric-foot |
