# Podiatry - Overview

## What is Podiatry?
Podiatry is the medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis, medical, and surgical treatment of disorders of the foot, ankle, and lower extremity. Podiatrists are Doctors of Podiatric Medicine (DPM) who provide comprehensive care for conditions affecting the lower extremity.
