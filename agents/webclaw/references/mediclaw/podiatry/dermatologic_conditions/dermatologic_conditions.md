# Podiatry - Dermatologic Conditions of the Foot

## Hyperkeratotic Lesions
| Condition | Location | Characteristics | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Corns (Heloma)** | **Dorsal joints** (heloma durum), **Interdigital** (heloma molle) | **Focal hyperkeratosis** with **central core** | **Debridement**, **Padding**, **Footwear modification**, **Surgical correction** of underlying deformity | https://www.apma.org/content.cfm?ItemNumber=989 |
| **Calluses (Tyloma)** | **Plantar weight-bearing areas** | **Diffuse hyperkeratosis** without central core | **Debridement**, **Padding**, **Footwear modification**, **Orthotics** | https://www.apma.org/content.cfm?ItemNumber=989 |
| **Porokeratosis** | **Plantar** | **Annular hyperkeratotic papules** with **central atrophy** | **Keratolytics** (urea, salicylic acid), **Cryotherapy**, **Topical retinoids** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/porokeratosis-overview |
| **IPK (Intractable Plantar Keratosis)** | **Metatarsal heads**, **Heel** | **Painful focal callus** resistant to debridement | **Debridement**, **Orthotics**, **Metatarsal osteotomy** | https://www.apma.org/content.cfm?ItemNumber=989 |

## Warts (Verrucae)
| Type | Characteristics | Treatment | URL |
|------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Verruca Plantaris** | **Hyperkeratotic papules** with **interrupted skin lines**, **Black dots** (thrombosed capillaries), **Pain with lateral compression** | **Salicylic acid**, **Cryotherapy**, **Cantharidin**, **Laser**, **Immunotherapy** (candida antigen), **Surgical excision** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/warts-overview |
| **Mosaic Warts** | **Coalescing plantar warts** | Same as above (often more resistant) | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/warts-overview |
| **Periungual Warts** | **Around nails** | Same as above (caution with nail matrix) | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/a-z/warts-overview |

## Eczematous Conditions
| Condition | Characteristics | Treatment | URL |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|-----|
| **Dyshidrotic Eczema** | **Vesicles/bullae** on **soles**, **Pruritus** | **Topical corticosteroids**, **Avoid irritants**, **Moisturizers** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/dyshidrotic-eczema |
| **Contact Dermatitis** | **Erythema**, **Vesicles**, **Pruritus** in **geometric pattern** | **Avoid allergen/irritant**, **Topical corticosteroids** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/contact-dermatitis |
| **Atopic Dermatitis** | **Pruritic**, **Flexural**, **Xerosis** (may involve feet) | **Emollients**, **Topical corticosteroids**, **Topical calcineurin inhibitors** | https://www.aad.org/public/diseases/eczema/types/atopic-dermatitis |

| **Blisters (Friction)** | **Fluid-filled vesicles** from **friction** | **Drainage** (if large/painful), **Leave roof intact**, **Padding**, **Proper footwear** | https://www.aad.org/public/everyday-care/injured-skin/burns/prevent-treat-blisters |
