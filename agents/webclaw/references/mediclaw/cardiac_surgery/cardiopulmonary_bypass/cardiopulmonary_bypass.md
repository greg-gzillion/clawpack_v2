## Alpha-Stat vs. pH-Stat
| Strategy | Principle | Indications | URL |
|----------|-----------|-------------|-----|
| Alpha-Stat | Maintain pH 7.4 at 37°C (uncorrected for temperature) | Adults; moderate hypothermia | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| pH-Stat | Maintain pH 7.4 at patient temperature (temperature-corrected) | Pediatric; deep hypothermia | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
