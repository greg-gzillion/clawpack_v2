# Cardiac Surgery - Overview

## What is Cardiac Surgery?
Cardiac surgery is the surgical specialty concerned with operative procedures on the heart and great vessels. It encompasses coronary artery bypass grafting, valve repair and replacement, aortic surgery, congenital heart defect repair, heart failure surgery, and heart transplantation.

## Major Cardiac Surgical Procedures
| Procedure | Indications | Approach |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) | Multivessel CAD; left main disease | Sternotomy; minimally invasive |
| Aortic Valve Replacement (AVR) | Severe aortic stenosis; regurgitation | Sternotomy; TAVR |
| Mitral Valve Repair/Replacement | Severe mitral regurgitation; stenosis | Sternotomy; minimally invasive |
| Tricuspid Valve Repair | Tricuspid regurgitation | Sternotomy; transcatheter |
| Ascending Aortic Replacement | Aneurysm; dissection | Sternotomy |
| Ventricular Assist Device (VAD) | End-stage heart failure | Sternotomy; thoracotomy |
| Heart Transplantation | End-stage heart failure | Sternotomy |
| Congenital Defect Repair | Various congenital anomalies | Sternotomy; thoracotomy |
| Maze Procedure | Atrial fibrillation | Sternotomy; minimally invasive |
