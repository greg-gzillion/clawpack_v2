## TAVR Complications
| Complication | Incidence | Management | URL |
|--------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| Pacemaker Requirement | 10-25% (self-expanding); 5-10% (balloon-expandable) | Permanent pacemaker | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Paravalvular Leak | 5-10% (=mild) | Post-dilation; valve-in-valve | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Stroke | 2-3% | Cerebral embolic protection | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Vascular Complications | 5-10% | Vascular surgery; covered stent | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Annular Rupture | <1% | Emergency surgery; covered stent | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Coronary Obstruction | <1% | PCI; emergency CABG | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Valve Embolization | <1% | Snare retrieval; second valve | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
