# Cardiac Surgery - Heart Transplantation

## Indications for Heart Transplantation
| Indication | Criteria | URL |
|------------|----------|-----|
| Refractory Cardiogenic Shock | Requiring IV inotropes or MCS | https://ishlt.org |
| NYHA Class IV Heart Failure | Persistent symptoms despite GDMT | https://ishlt.org |
| Recurrent Ventricular Arrhythmias | Refractory to medical/device therapy | https://ishlt.org |
| VO2 max <14 mL/kg/min | In patients on GDMT | https://ishlt.org |
| VO2 max <12 mL/kg/min | In patients on beta-blockers | https://ishlt.org |
| HFSS (Heart Failure Survival Score) | High-risk | https://ishlt.org |

|-----------|----------------|-----|

## Heart Transplantation Procedure
| Step | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 1. Donor Cardiectomy | Preservation with cardioplegia; cold storage | https://ishlt.org |
| 2. Recipient Sternotomy | Median sternotomy | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| 3. CPB Initiation | Bicaval or bicaval cannulation | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| 4. Recipient Cardiectomy | Excision of ventricles; preservation of atrial cuffs | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| 5. Implantation | Bicaval technique (standard); biatrial technique | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| 6. Reperfusion | Gradual rewarming; de-airing | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| 7. Weaning CPB | Inotropic support (isoproterenol; epinephrine) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |

## Bicaval vs. Biatrial Technique
| Technique | Advantages | Disadvantages | URL |
|-----------|------------|---------------|-----|
| Bicaval | Better atrial function; less TR; fewer arrhythmias | Longer ischemic time | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Biatrial (Lower-Shumway) | Shorter ischemic time; technically simpler | Atrial dysfunction; TR; arrhythmias | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |

## Post-Transplant Immunosuppression
| Phase | Agents | URL |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Induction | Basiliximab (IL-2 antagonist); Thymoglobulin (ATG) for high-risk | https://ishlt.org |
| Maintenance | Tacrolimus + Mycophenolate mofetil (MMF) + Prednisone | https://ishlt.org |
| Steroid Wean | Prednisone tapered over 6-12 months (selected patients) | https://ishlt.org |
| Proliferation Signal Inhibitors | Everolimus/Sirolimus (CNI-sparing; reduced CAV) | https://ishlt.org |

## Post-Transplant Surveillance
| Test | Frequency | Purpose | URL |
|------|-----------|---------|-----|

| Echocardiography | Serial | LV function; valves; pericardial effusion | https://ishlt.org |
