## LVAD Complications
| Complication | Incidence | Management | URL |
|--------------|-----------|------------|-----|
| Bleeding | 20-40% (early) | Transfusion; surgical re-exploration | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Infection (Driveline) | 20-30% | Antibiotics; surgical debridement | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Pump Thrombosis | 2-10% | Anticoagulation; thrombolytics; pump exchange | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
