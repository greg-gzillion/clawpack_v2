## Mechanical vs. Bioprosthetic Aortic Valve
| Factor | Mechanical | Bioprosthetic | URL |
|--------|------------|---------------|-----|
| Age | <50 years (Class IIa); 50-65 (individualized) | >65 years | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Durability | Lifetime | 10-20 years | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Anticoagulation | Lifelong warfarin (INR 2.5-3.5) | None (ASA only) | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Thromboembolism Risk | 0.5-1% per year | 0.5-1% per year | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Bleeding Risk | 1-2% per year | Lower | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |
| Pregnancy | Contraindicated (warfarin) | Preferred | https://www.acc.org/guidelines |

## Prosthetic Valve Complications
| Complication | Incidence | Presentation | Management | URL |
|--------------|-----------|--------------|------------|-----|
| Thromboembolism | 0.5-1%/year | TIA; stroke; peripheral emboli | Anticoagulation optimization | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Valve Thrombosis | 0.1-0.5%/year | Dyspnea; elevated gradients | Thrombolysis; surgery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Structural Valve Deterioration | 5-10% at 10 years | Heart failure; murmur | Reoperation; Valve-in-Valve | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Endocarditis | 0.3-0.6%/year | Fever; bacteremia; new murmur | Antibiotics; surgery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Paravalvular Leak | 5-15% | Hemolysis; heart failure | Medical; percutaneous closure; surgery | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Patient-Prosthesis Mismatch | 20-50% (moderate); 2-10% (severe) | High gradients; heart failure | Prevention (adequate size); reoperation | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
