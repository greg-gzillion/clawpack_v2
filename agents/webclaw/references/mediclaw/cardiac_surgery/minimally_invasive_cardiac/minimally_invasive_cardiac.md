## Advantages of Minimally Invasive Cardiac Surgery
| Outcome | Evidence | URL |
|---------|----------|-----|
| Blood Transfusion | Reduced (20-30% reduction) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Hospital Length of Stay | 1-2 days shorter | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Postoperative Pain | Reduced | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Wound Infection | Reduced (especially in high-risk) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Return to Activity | Faster | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Mortality | Similar to sternotomy | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
| Long-Term Outcomes | Similar (mitral repair durability may be slightly lower) | https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK470357/ |
