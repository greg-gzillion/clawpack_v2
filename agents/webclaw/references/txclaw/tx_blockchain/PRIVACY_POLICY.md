## Introduction

PhoenixPME ("we," "us," "our," "Platform") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our precious metals auction platform.

**By using PhoenixPME, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- Email address
- Username/display name
- Wallet addresses
- Shipping addresses (for physical delivery)
- Communications and support tickets

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and time spent
- Transaction data on blockchain

### 1.3 Blockchain Data
- Wallet addresses and transaction hashes are public on TX blockchain (formerly Coreum)
- Smart contract interactions are permanently recorded

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Process transactions and escrow services
- Facilitate communication between users
- Manage user accounts
- Provide customer support
- Resolve disputes
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Improve platform functionality

⚠️ **Note:** Until March 6, 2026, the platform is in mock mode on testnet. Real transactions are not yet processed.

---

## 3. Information Sharing

### 3.1 We DO Share With:
- **Transaction Counterparties:** Information needed to complete transactions
- **Blockchain Networks:** Transaction data is public by design
- **Community Reserve Fund:** Fee data is recorded on-chain
- **Service Providers:** Hosting, analytics, and security services
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law

### 3.2 We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data for third-party marketing
- Provide email addresses to advertisers

---

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments

### 4.2 Your Responsibilities
- Protect your account credentials
- Secure your wallet private keys (we never have access)
- Report suspicious activity

### 4.3 No Guarantee
Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
