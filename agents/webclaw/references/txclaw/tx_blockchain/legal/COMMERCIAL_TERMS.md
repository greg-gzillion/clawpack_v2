## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE

All commercial license fees and royalties are paid **directly to the Community Reserve Fund (CRF)** , NOT to the developer personally.

## Part 1: Financial Terms

### License Fees
- **Minimum Annual:** $1,000 USD
- **Due:** Upon signing, then annually
- **Destination:** Community Reserve Fund smart contract

### Royalty Structure

| Phase | Period | Royalty Rate | Destination |
|-------|--------|--------------|-------------|
| Phase 1 | 0-3 months | 1.1% of transaction volume | Community Reserve Fund |
| Phase 2 | 4-12 months | 1.5% of transaction volume | Community Reserve Fund |
| Phase 3 | Year 2+ | 2.0% of transaction volume | Community Reserve Fund |

### Minimum Royalties
- **Quarterly minimum:** $250 USD (to CRF)
- **Annual minimum:** $1,000 USD (to CRF)

### No Personal Payments
- Developer receives **zero direct payments** from commercial licenses
- All funds flow to CRF smart contract
- Developer's 10% allocation is **voting weight only**, not withdrawal rights
- Funds locked until DAO governance active
