If you believe your copyrighted work has been copied in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please provide our Copyright Agent with the following information:

### Required Information:
1. **Your physical or electronic signature** (typing your full name counts)
2. **Identification of the copyrighted work** you claim has been infringed
3. **Identification of the material** that is claimed to be infringing and where it is located (URL or specific file path)
4. **Your contact information**: address, telephone number, and email address
5. **A statement** that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
6. **A statement**, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
