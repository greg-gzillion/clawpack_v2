# State Case Law - Overview

## What is State Case Law?
State case law consists of judicial decisions from state courts interpreting state constitutions, statutes, regulations, and common law. State courts handle approximately 95% of all judicial proceedings in the United States and are the primary arbiters of most legal disputes affecting individuals and businesses.
