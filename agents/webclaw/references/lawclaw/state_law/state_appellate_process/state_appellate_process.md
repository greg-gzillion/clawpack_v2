## Standards of Review
| Standard | Description | Application | URL |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| De Novo | No deference to trial court | Questions of law | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/de_novo |
| Clear Error | Highly deferential | Findings of fact | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/clearly_erroneous |
| Abuse of Discretion | Substantial deference | Discretionary rulings; evidentiary rulings; sanctions | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/abuse_of_discretion |
| Substantial Evidence | Deference to agency findings | Administrative agency factual determinations | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/substantial_evidence |
| Arbitrary and Capricious | Highly deferential | Agency decision-making | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/arbitrary_and_capricious |
