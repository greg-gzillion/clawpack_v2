# Civil Procedure - Discovery

## Overview of Discovery
Discovery is the pre-trial phase where parties obtain evidence from each other.

- **FRCP Rule 26 (Duty to Disclose)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/rules/frcp/rule_26
- **Cornell LII - Discovery**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/discovery
