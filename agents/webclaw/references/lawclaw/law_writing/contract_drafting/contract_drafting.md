## Common Drafting Issues
| Issue | Poor Drafting | Better Drafting |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| Ambiguity | "Reasonable time" | "Within 30 days" |
| Inconsistency | "Seller" and "Vendor" used interchangeably | Use consistent defined term |
| Redundancy | "Null and void" | "Void" |
| Vagueness | "Material breach" | Define specific breaches |
| Passive Voice | "Payment shall be made" | "Buyer shall pay" |
| Archaic Language | "Witnesseth," "whereas" | Plain English |
| Ambiguous Modifiers | "Bi-annual" | "Every six months" or "Every two years" |
