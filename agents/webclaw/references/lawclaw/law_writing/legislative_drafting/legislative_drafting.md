## Drafting Conventions
| Convention | Example | URL |
|------------|---------|-----|
| Use "shall" for duty | "The Secretary shall promulgate regulations" | Varies |
| Use "may" for discretion | "The Secretary may grant waivers" | Varies |
| Use "must" for condition precedent | "The applicant must submit a completed form" | Varies |
| Use present tense | "The Commission issues licenses" (not "shall issue") | Varies |
| Use active voice | "The Administrator shall determine" | Varies |
| Avoid provisos | Use separate subsections | Varies |
| Avoid "and/or" | Use "or" or specify both | Varies |
