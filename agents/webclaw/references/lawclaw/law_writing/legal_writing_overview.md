# Legal Writing - Overview

## What is Legal Writing?
Legal writing encompasses the specialized forms of written communication used in the legal profession. It includes objective analysis (memos, briefs), persuasive writing (appellate briefs, motions), transactional drafting (contracts, wills), and legislative drafting (statutes, regulations).
