# Supreme Court - History and Structure

## History of the Supreme Court
| Period | Event | Description | URL |
|--------|-------|-------------|-----|
| 1789 | Judiciary Act of 1789 | Established Supreme Court with 6 Justices | https://www.archives.gov/milestone-documents/judiciary-act |
| 1801 | Midnight Judges Act | Reduced Court to 5 Justices (repealed 1802) | https://www.fjc.gov/history/legislation/landmark-legislation-judiciary-act-1801 |
| 1803 | Marbury v. Madison | Established judicial review | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/5/137/ |
| 1869 | Judiciary Act of 1869 | Set Court at 9 Justices | https://www.fjc.gov/history/legislation/landmark-legislation-judiciary-act-1869 |
| 1891 | Evarts Act | Created Circuit Courts of Appeals | https://www.fjc.gov/history/legislation/landmark-legislation-evarts-act |
| 1925 | Judges' Bill | Gave Court discretion over certiorari | https://www.fjc.gov/history/legislation/landmark-legislation-judges-bill-1925 |
| 1935 | Supreme Court Building | Court moved to current building | https://www.supremecourt.gov/about/building.aspx |
| 1937 | Court Packing Plan | FDR's unsuccessful proposal to expand Court | https://www.archives.gov/milestone-documents/franklin-roosevelts-court-packing-plan |
