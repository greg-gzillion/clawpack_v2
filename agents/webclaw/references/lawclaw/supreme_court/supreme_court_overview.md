# Supreme Court of the United States - Overview

## What is the Supreme Court?
The Supreme Court of the United States is the highest federal court and the ultimate interpreter of the U.S. Constitution. Established by Article III of the Constitution, the Court has final appellate jurisdiction over all federal cases and state cases involving federal law. Its decisions establish binding precedent for all lower courts.
