## Shadow Docket
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Definition | Orders issued without full briefing and argument | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/shadow_docket |
| Types | Emergency applications; stays; injunctions | https://www.supremecourt.gov/orders/orders.aspx |
| Procedure | Single Justice may act; often referred to full Court | https://www.supremecourt.gov/ctrules/2019RulesoftheCourt.pdf |
| Criticism | Lack of transparency; limited reasoning | https://www.scotusblog.com/category/emergency-appeals/ |
