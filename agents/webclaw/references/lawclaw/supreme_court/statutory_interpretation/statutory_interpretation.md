## Methods of Statutory Interpretation
| Method | Description | Key Justices | URL |
|--------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| Textualism | Focus on plain meaning of statutory text | Scalia, Thomas, Gorsuch, Barrett | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/textualism |

## Canons of Construction
| Canon | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Plain Meaning Rule | Words given ordinary meaning | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/plain_meaning_rule |
| Whole Act Rule | Interpret in context of entire statute | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/whole_act_rule |
| Ejusdem Generis | General terms limited by specific terms | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/ejusdem_generis |
| Expressio Unius | Mention of one thing excludes others | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/expressio_unius_est_exclusio_alterius |
| Noscitur a Sociis | Word known by its associates | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/noscitur_a_sociis |
| Rule of Lenity | Ambiguity in criminal statutes resolved for defendant | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/rule_of_lenity |
| Constitutional Avoidance | Avoid constitutional questions if possible | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/constitutional_avoidance |
