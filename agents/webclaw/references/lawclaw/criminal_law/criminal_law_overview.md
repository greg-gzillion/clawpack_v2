# Criminal Law - Overview

## What is Criminal Law?
Criminal law defines conduct prohibited by the government and prescribes punishment for violations. Unlike civil law, criminal law involves the state prosecuting individuals or entities for offenses against society.

## Classification of Crimes
| Classification | Severity | Example |
|----------------|----------|---------|
| Felony | Most serious (1+ year imprisonment) | Murder, rape, robbery |
| Misdemeanor | Less serious (<1 year) | Petty theft, simple assault |
