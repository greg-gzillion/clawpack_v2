# Landlord-Tenant Law - Overview

## What is Landlord-Tenant Law?
Landlord-tenant law governs the rental of residential and commercial property. It establishes the rights and responsibilities of both landlords and tenants, covering lease agreements, security deposits, habitability standards, eviction procedures, and fair housing requirements.
