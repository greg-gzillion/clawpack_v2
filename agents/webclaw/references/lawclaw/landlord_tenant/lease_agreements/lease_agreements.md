## Commercial vs. Residential Leases
| Feature | Residential | Commercial |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| Habitability | Implied warranty | Generally negotiable |
| Consumer Protection | Extensive | Limited |
| Rent Control | May apply | Rarely applies |
| Security Deposit | Statutory limits | Negotiable |
| Eviction Protection | Greater | Less |
| ADA Compliance | Title III applies | Title III applies |
