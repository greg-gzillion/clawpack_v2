# Military Law - Nonjudicial Punishment (Article 15)

## Nonjudicial Punishment Overview
| Topic | Article 15 | Description | URL |
|-------|-----------|-------------|-----|
| Authority | Art. 15(a) | Commander may impose NJP for minor offenses | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/815 |
| Purpose | Disciplinary tool | Correct misconduct without court-martial | https://jsc.defense.gov/Military-Law/Manual-for-Courts-Martial/ |
| Service Terminology | Army/Air Force: Article 15; Navy/Marines: Captain's Mast; Coast Guard: Admiral's Mast | Varies by branch |

## NJP Procedure
| Step | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Notification | Service member notified of alleged offense | https://jsc.defense.gov/Military-Law/Manual-for-Courts-Martial/ |
| Right to Demand Trial | Member may refuse NJP and demand court-martial (except aboard ship) | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/815 |
| Personal Appearance | Member appears before commander | https://jsc.defense.gov/Military-Law/Manual-for-Courts-Martial/ |
| Evidence Considered | Commander considers evidence of offense | https://jsc.defense.gov/Military-Law/Manual-for-Courts-Martial/ |
| Findings | Commander determines if offense committed | https://jsc.defense.gov/Military-Law/Manual-for-Courts-Martial/ |
| Punishment Imposed | Commander imposes appropriate punishment | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/815 |
| Appeals | Member may appeal to next superior authority | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/815 |

## Maximum Punishments by Rank of Imposing Commander
| Commander Rank | Maximum Punishment | URL |
|----------------|-------------------|-----|
| Company Grade (O-3 and below) | 7 days extra duty; 7 days restriction; 7 days correctional custody (E-3 and below); forfeiture of 7 days pay; reduction of one grade (E-4 and below) | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/815 |
| Field Grade (O-4 to O-6) | 45 days extra duty; 60 days restriction; 30 days correctional custody; forfeiture of 1/2 month pay for 2 months; reduction to lowest enlisted grade | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/815 |
| General Officer (O-7+) | Same as field grade plus: forfeiture of 1/2 month pay for 2 months; arrest in quarters up to 30 days | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/815 |
