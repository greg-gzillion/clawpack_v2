## Key Differences from Civilian Law
| Difference | Military Law | Civilian Law | URL |
|------------|--------------|--------------|-----|
| Constitutional Rights | Modified under military necessity | Full constitutional protections | https://www.law.cornell.edu/constitution |
| Jury Composition | Panel members appointed by convening authority | Randomly selected impartial jury | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/jury |
| Unanimous Verdict | Not required for most offenses (3/4 majority) | Unanimous generally required | https://www.law.cornell.edu/constitution/amendmentvi |
| Double Jeopardy | Limited protection; separate sovereigns | Full protection | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/double_jeopardy |
| Commander's Authority | Convening authority has significant powers | Prosecutor controls charging | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/prosecutorial_discretion |
| Appeal Rights | Automatic appeal for certain sentences | Discretionary appeal | https://www.armfor.uscourts.gov |
