## Retirement Eligibility
| Type | Service Requirement | URL |
|------|---------------------|-----|
| Regular Retirement | 20 years active service | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/7311 |
| Reserve Retirement | 20 qualifying years (age 60) | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12731 |
| Temporary Early Retirement Authority (TERA) | 15-19 years (force reduction) | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1293 |
| Medical Retirement | Unfitting condition with 30%+ rating | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1201 |
| Disability Severance | Unfitting condition with <30% rating | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1203 |
