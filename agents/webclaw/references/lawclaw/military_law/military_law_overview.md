# Military Law - Overview

## What is Military Law?
Military law governs the armed forces of the United States, encompassing criminal justice under the Uniform Code of Military Justice (UCMJ), administrative law affecting military personnel, and specialized statutes protecting service members' rights. It operates as a separate legal system distinct from civilian criminal and civil law.
