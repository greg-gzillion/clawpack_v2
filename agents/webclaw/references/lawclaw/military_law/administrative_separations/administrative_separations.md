## Types of Administrative Discharges
| Character of Service | Description | URL |
|----------------------|-------------|-----|
| Honorable | Fully met standards of conduct and performance | https://www.law.cornell.edu/cfr/text/32/part-41 |
| General (Under Honorable Conditions) | Satisfactory but significant departure from conduct expected | https://www.law.cornell.edu/cfr/text/32/part-41 |
