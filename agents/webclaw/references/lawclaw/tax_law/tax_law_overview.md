# Tax Law - Overview

## What is Tax Law?
Tax law governs the assessment and collection of taxes by federal, state, and local governments. It encompasses the Internal Revenue Code (IRC), Treasury Regulations, IRS guidance, and judicial decisions interpreting tax provisions. Tax law affects individuals, businesses, estates, trusts, and tax-exempt organizations.
