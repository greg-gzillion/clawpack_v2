## What is State Legislative History?
State legislative history consists of the documents and records produced during the legislative process, including bill drafts, committee reports, floor debates, and amendments. It can be used to interpret ambiguous statutes and determine legislative intent.

## Legislative History Documents by Type
| Document Type | Description | Availability | URL |
|---------------|-------------|--------------|-----|
| Introduced Bill | Original version of bill | Most states online | Varies by state |
| Amended Bill | Bill as amended during process | Most states online | Varies by state |
| Committee Reports | Committee analysis and recommendations | Limited availability | Varies by state |
| Floor Debate Transcripts | Record of legislative debates | Some states; varies | Varies by state |
| Fiscal Notes | Fiscal impact analysis | Most states | Varies by state |
| Governor's Messages | Signing or veto messages | Most states | Varies by state |
