# State Statutory Law - Uniform Laws

## What are Uniform Laws?
Uniform laws are model statutes drafted by the Uniform Law Commission (ULC) for adoption by state legislatures. They promote consistency across states in areas where uniformity is desirable, such as commercial transactions, family law, and trust and estate law.
