# State Statutory Law - Interstate Compacts

## What are Interstate Compacts?
Interstate compacts are agreements between two or more states, approved by Congress, that create binding obligations and often establish multi-state agencies. They are authorized by the Compact Clause of the U.S. Constitution (Art. I, ｧ 10, cl. 3).
