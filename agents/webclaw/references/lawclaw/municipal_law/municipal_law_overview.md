# Municipal Law - Overview

## What is Municipal Law?
Municipal law governs the powers, duties, and operations of local governments, including cities, counties, towns, villages, and special districts. It encompasses the legal framework for local legislation, land use regulation, public finance, municipal liability, and the relationship between local governments and state/federal authorities.
