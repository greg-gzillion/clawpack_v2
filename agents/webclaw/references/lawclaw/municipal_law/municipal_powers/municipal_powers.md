## Dillon's Rule vs. Home Rule
| Doctrine | Description | States | URL |
|----------|-------------|--------|-----|
| Dillon's Rule | Local governments possess only powers expressly granted, necessarily implied, or essential to declared objects | Majority of states | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/dillons_rule |
| Home Rule | Local governments may exercise any power not prohibited by state law | 40+ states in varying degrees | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/home_rule |
