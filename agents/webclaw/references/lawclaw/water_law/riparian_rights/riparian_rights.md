# Water Law - Riparian Rights

## Riparian Rights Overview
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Definition | Water rights based on ownership of land bordering watercourse | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/riparian_rights |
| Geographic Application | Eastern United States; states with abundant water | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/riparian_rights |
| Riparian Land | Land adjacent to or crossed by watercourse | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/riparian_land |
| Watercourse | Natural stream or river; generally not diffuse surface water | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/watercourse |

## Reasonable Use Theory
| Principle | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

## Riparian Rights Limitations
| Limitation | Description | URL |
