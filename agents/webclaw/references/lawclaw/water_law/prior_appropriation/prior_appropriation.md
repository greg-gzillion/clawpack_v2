## Elements of Prior Appropriation
| Element | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Intent to Appropriate | Manifest intent to take water for beneficial use | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/prior_appropriation |
| Diversion | Physical diversion of water (some states allow instream appropriations) | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/diversion |
| Beneficial Use | Water applied to beneficial use | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/beneficial_use |
| Priority Date | Date appropriation initiated | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/priority_date |
| Perfection | Completion of appropriation | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/perfection_of_water_right |

## Priority System
| Rule | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Senior/Junior Rights | Earlier appropriations have priority over later ones | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/water_priority |
| "First in Time, First in Right" | Fundamental principle | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/prior_appropriation |
| Priority Calls | Senior may "call" the river; juniors must curtail | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/priority_call |
| During Shortage | Juniors receive no water until seniors satisfied | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/water_shortage |
