# Water Law - Overview

## What is Water Law?
Water law governs the allocation, use, and protection of water resources. In the United States, water law is primarily state law, with two dominant systems: riparian rights (eastern states) and prior appropriation (western states). Federal law plays a significant role in water quality, endangered species protection, federal water projects, and tribal water rights.
