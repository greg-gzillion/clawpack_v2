# Transportation Law - Overview

## What is Transportation Law?
Transportation law governs the movement of people and goods across all modes of transport揺ighway, rail, air, water, and pipeline. It encompasses federal and state regulation of carriers, safety standards, infrastructure development, environmental compliance, and labor relations within the transportation industry.
