## FOIA Exemptions (9 Categories)
- **Exemption 1**: Classified national defense/foreign policy
- **Exemption 2**: Internal personnel rules
- **Exemption 3**: Exempted by other statutes
- **Exemption 4**: Trade secrets/confidential business
- **Exemption 5**: Privileged communications
- **Exemption 6**: Personal privacy
- **Exemption 7**: Law enforcement records
- **Exemption 8**: Financial institution reports
- **Exemption 9**: Geological information

## Key Cases
- **DOJ v. Reporters Committee** (1989) - Privacy interests
- **Milner v. Dept of Navy** (2011) - FOIA exemptions narrowly construed
- **Food Marketing Inst. v. Argus Leader** (2019) - Confidential business information
- **ACLU v. DOJ** (various) - Government surveillance records
