# Administrative Procedure Act (APA) - 5 U.S.C. §§ 551-559, 701-706

## Overview
The APA governs federal administrative agencies' rulemaking and adjudication procedures.

## Key Provisions
- **§ 551**: Definitions
- **§ 552**: Public information (FOIA)
- **§ 553**: Rulemaking procedures
- **§ 554**: Adjudication requirements
- **§ 555**: Ancillary matters
- **§ 556-557**: Hearings and decisions
- **§ 701-706**: Judicial review

## Rulemaking (§ 553)
- Notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM)
- Public comment period
- Final rule publication
- Exceptions: military/foreign affairs, agency management

## Adjudication (§§ 554, 556-557)
- Formal adjudication requirements
- Separation of functions
- ALJ presiding
- Burden of proof

## Judicial Review (§§ 701-706)
- Scope of review
- Arbitrary and capricious standard
- Substantial evidence standard
