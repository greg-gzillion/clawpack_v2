# Agency Appeals Process - Administrative Exhaustion

## Overview
Before seeking judicial review, parties must typically exhaust administrative remedies by appealing within the agency.

## Official Resources
- **APA ｧ 557 (Initial Decisions & Appeals)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/557
- **ACUS Exhaustion Guide**: https://www.acus.gov/report/exhaustion-administrative-remedies

## Exhaustion Doctrine
- **Purpose**: Allow agency to correct errors, develop record, apply expertise
- **Key Case**: McKart v. United States (1969) - Exhaustion required unless futile or inadequate
- **Key Case**: McCarthy v. Madigan (1992) - Prisoner exhaustion requirements

## Exceptions to Exhaustion
1. **Futility**: Agency has predetermined issue
2. **Inadequate Remedy**: Agency cannot provide relief sought
3. **Irreparable Injury**: Delay would cause harm
4. **Constitutional Claims**: Agency cannot resolve constitutional issues
5. **Jurisdictional**: Agency exceeded authority

## Typical Appeal Structure
1. **ALJ Initial Decision**
2. **Appeal to Agency Appeals Board**
3. **Agency Head Final Decision**
4. **Motion for Reconsideration**
5. **Judicial Review** (Article III court)

## Agency-Specific Appeal Processes

### Social Security Administration
- **ALJ Decision ? Appeals Council**: 20 CFR ｧ 404.967
- **Appeals Council Review**: Discretionary
- **60-Day Deadline** for judicial review after final decision
- **SSA Appeals Portal**: https://www.ssa.gov/appeals/

### Environmental Protection Agency
- **ALJ Initial Decision**
- **Environmental Appeals Board (EAB)**: 40 CFR Part 22
- **EAB Final Decision** is agency final action
- **EAB Website**: https://www.epa.gov/eab

### Department of Labor
- **Benefits Review Board (BRB)**: Black lung, longshore claims
- **Administrative Review Board (ARB)**: Immigration, whistleblower
- **DOL OALJ**: https://www.oalj.dol.gov/

### Merit Systems Protection Board (MSPB)
- **Federal Employee Appeals**: https://www.mspb.gov/
- **ALJ Initial Decision**
- **Board Review**: Petition for review required
- **Judicial Review**: Federal Circuit (except discrimination claims)

### Federal Trade Commission
- **ALJ Initial Decision**
- **Commission Review**: Appeal to full Commission
- **Cease and Desist Orders**: 15 U.S.C. ｧ 45

### NLRB
- **ALJ Decision**
- **Board Review**: Exceptions filed by parties
- **Enforcement in Court of Appeals**: 29 U.S.C. ｧ 160(e)

## Interlocutory Appeals
- Generally not available in administrative proceedings
- **Collateral Order Doctrine**: Limited exceptions
- **Key Case**: FTC v. Standard Oil of California (1980)

## Judicial Review Timing
- **APA ｧ 704**: Final agency action required
- **Finality**: Action must mark "consummation of agency decision-making"
- **Key Case**: Bennett v. Spear (1997) - Two-part test for finality

- **Agency Appeal Deadlines**: Check specific agency regulations
- **Tolling Agreements**: May extend judicial review deadlines
