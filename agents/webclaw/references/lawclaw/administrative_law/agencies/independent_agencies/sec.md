## Key Administrative Law Issues
- **Appointments Clause**: Lucia v. SEC (2018) - SEC ALJs are "Officers of the United States"
- **Seventh Amendment**: SEC v. Jarkesy (2024) - SEC fraud actions require jury trials in federal court
- **Chevron Deference**: Overturned in Loper Bright (2024)

## Key Cases
- **Lucia v. SEC (2018)** - ALJs must be appointed under Appointments Clause
- **SEC v. Jarkesy (2024)** - SEC administrative enforcement unconstitutional (jury trial required)
- **Free Enterprise Fund v. PCAOB (2010)** - Removal protections
- **Kokesh v. SEC (2017)** - Disgorgement as penalty subject to 5-year statute of limitations
- **Liu v. SEC (2020)** - Disgorgement limited to net profits

## Post-Jarkesy Impact
- SEC fraud actions must now be brought in federal district court
- ALJ proceedings limited to non-fraud matters
- Significant impact on SEC enforcement strategy
