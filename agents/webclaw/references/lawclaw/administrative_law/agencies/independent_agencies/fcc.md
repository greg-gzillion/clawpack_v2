# Federal Communications Commission (FCC) - Administrative Law Reference

## Official Resources
- **FCC Home**: https://www.fcc.gov/
- **FCC Regulations (47 CFR)**: https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-47
- **Office of Administrative Law Judges**: https://www.fcc.gov/office-administrative-law-judges

## Key Statutes
- **Communications Act of 1934**: 47 U.S.C. § 151 et seq.
- **Telecommunications Act of 1996**: Major overhaul
- **Spectrum Act of 2012**: Incentive auctions
- **RAY BAUM'S Act of 2018**: 911 location accuracy

## FCC Structure
- **Commission (5 Commissioners)**: No more than 3 from one party
- **Bureaus**: Wireless, Wireline, Media, Enforcement, International, Public Safety, Consumer
- **Office of ALJs**: Independent adjudicators

## FCC Adjudication
- **ALJ Proceedings**: Formal hearings under APA
- **Commission Review**: Appeal of ALJ decisions
- **Enforcement Bureau**: Investigations and citations
- **Forfeiture Proceedings**: Monetary penalties

## Licensing and Spectrum
- **Wireless Licenses**: 47 CFR Parts 1, 22, 24, 27
- **Broadcast Licenses**: 47 CFR Parts 73, 74
- **License Renewal**: Public interest standard
- **Transfer of Control**: FCC approval required

## Enforcement Tools
- **Notice of Apparent Liability (NAL)**: Proposed forfeiture
- **Forfeiture Order**: Final monetary penalty
- **Consent Decree**: Settlement mechanism
- **License Revocation**: Most severe sanction

## Key Administrative Law Cases
- **FCC v. Fox Television Stations (2009)** - Agency policy changes require reasoned explanation
- **FCC v. Prometheus Radio Project (2021)** - Media ownership rules
- **Mozilla Corp. v. FCC (D.C. Cir. 2019)** - Net neutrality repeal
- **ACA International v. FCC (D.C. Cir. 2018)** - TCPA autodialer definition
- **Loper Bright v. Raimondo (2024)** - Chevron deference overturned

## Major Policy Areas
- **Net Neutrality**: Restored 2024 (Open Internet Order)
- **Spectrum Auctions**: 47 U.S.C. § 309(j)
- **Universal Service Fund (USF)**: 47 U.S.C. § 254
- **Broadband Mapping**: Broadband DATA Act
- **Robocall Enforcement**: STIR/SHAKEN, TRACED Act
