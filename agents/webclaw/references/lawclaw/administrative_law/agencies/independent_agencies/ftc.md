- **Part 3 Administrative Litigation**: https://www.ftc.gov/enforcement/administrative-litigation

- **Commission Opinion**: Final agency action
- **Cease and Desist Orders**: 15 U.S.C. ｧ 45(b)
- **Civil Penalties**: Up to $50,120 per violation (adjusted annually)

## FTC Enforcement Priorities
- **Antitrust**: Mergers, monopolization
- **Consumer Protection**: Deceptive advertising, privacy, data security
- **Technology**: AI claims, dark patterns, subscriptions

## Key Cases
- **FTC v. Standard Oil of California (1980)** - Interlocutory review limitations
- **AMG Capital Management v. FTC (2021)** - FTC cannot seek equitable monetary relief under ｧ 13(b)
- **Axon Enterprise v. FTC (2023)** - District court jurisdiction over constitutional challenges
- **FTC v. Microsoft/Activision (2023)** - Merger challenge

## Rulemaking Authority
- **Magnuson-Moss Rulemaking**: 15 U.S.C. ｧ 57a (Consumer product warranties)
- **Section 18 Rulemaking**: Unfair/deceptive acts or practices
- **Recent Rules**: Non-compete ban, "click to cancel" subscription rule
