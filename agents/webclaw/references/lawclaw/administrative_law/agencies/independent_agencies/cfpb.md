## CFPB Structure & Constitutionality
- **Director**: Single Director, removable only for cause
- **Seila Law v. CFPB (2020)**: For-cause removal unconstitutional, Director removable at will
- **CFSA v. CFPB (2024)**: Funding mechanism constitutional

## CFPB Adjudication
- **Rules of Practice**: 12 CFR Part 1081
- **Administrative Law Judge**: Independent adjudicator
- **Enforcement Counsel**: Prosecutes cases
- **Commissioner-like Authority**: Director reviews ALJ decisions

## Enforcement Powers (12 U.S.C. ｧ 5564-5565)
- **Civil Investigative Demands (CIDs)**: Pre-lawsuit discovery
- **Administrative Proceedings**: ALJ adjudication
- **Civil Penalties**: Tiered structure
  - Tier 1: $6,813 per day (knowing violation)
  - Tier 2: $34,065 per day (reckless)
  - Tier 3: $1,362,600 per day (knowing)

## Key Administrative Law Issues
- **Constitutionality**: Seila Law (2020), CFSA (2024)
- **ALJ Appointments**: Lucia v. SEC applies
- **Administrative Exhaustion**: Not required for all claims
- **Preemption**: State laws not preempted unless inconsistent

## Key Cases
- **Seila Law LLC v. CFPB (2020)** - Director removal protection unconstitutional
- **CFSA v. CFPB (2024)** - Funding through Federal Reserve constitutional
