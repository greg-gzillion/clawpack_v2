- **NLRB Home**: https://www.nlrb.gov/

## Key Statutes
- **National Labor Relations Act (NLRA)**: 29 U.S.C. ｧ 151 et seq.
- **Section 7 Rights**: Organize, bargain collectively, engage in concerted activities
- **Section 8 Unfair Labor Practices**: Employer and union violations

## NLRB Structure
- **Board (5 Members)**: Adjudicates contested cases, issues rules
- **General Counsel**: Independent prosecutor, investigates and prosecutes ULP charges
- **Regional Offices**: 26 regions, conduct elections, investigate charges
- **Division of Judges**: ALJs preside over ULP hearings

## NLRB Adjudication Process
1. **Charge Filed**: With Regional Director
2. **Investigation**: Regional Director determines merit
3. **Complaint Issued**: If merit found
4. **ALJ Hearing**: Trial-type proceeding under APA
5. **ALJ Decision**: Recommended order
6. **Board Review**: Exceptions filed by parties
7. **Board Decision**: Final agency order
8. **Judicial Review**: D.C. Circuit or circuit where ULP occurred

## Unfair Labor Practice (ULP) Procedures
- **Section 10**: 29 U.S.C. ｧ 160
- **ALJ Authority**: 29 CFR Part 102, Subpart B
- **Burden of Proof**: General Counsel must prove ULP by preponderance
- **Remedies**: Cease and desist, reinstatement, backpay, posting notices

## Representation Elections (R Cases)
- **Section 9**: 29 U.S.C. ｧ 159
- **RC Petition**: Union seeking certification
- **RM Petition**: Employer seeking election
- **RD Petition**: Decertification
- **Election Procedures**: 29 CFR Part 102, Subpart D
- **Representation Case Rules (2024)**: Expedited election timelines

## Key Administrative Law Cases
- **Universal Camera Corp. v. NLRB (1951)** - Substantial evidence review standard
- **NLRB v. Gissel Packing Co. (1969)** - Bargaining orders
- **NLRB v. Yeshiva University (1980)** - Managerial employee exclusion
- **Epic Systems Corp. v. Lewis (2018)** - Class action waivers enforceable
- **NLRB v. SW General, Inc. (2017)** - Vacancies Act limitations
- **Loper Bright v. Raimondo (2024)** - Chevron deference overturned
