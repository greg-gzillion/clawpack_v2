- **Immigration Adjudications**: USCIS processes benefits, waivers
- **Removal Proceedings**: ICE prosecutes, EOIR adjudicates
- **Border Enforcement**: CBP inspections, expedited removal
- **Transportation Security**: TSA screening, redress program
- **Disaster Assistance**: FEMA Individual Assistance appeals
