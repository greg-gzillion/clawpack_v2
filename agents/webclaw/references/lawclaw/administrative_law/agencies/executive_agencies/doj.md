## Official Resources
- **DOJ Home**: https://www.justice.gov/

- **Office of Information Policy (OIP)**: https://www.justice.gov/oip - FOIA guidance
- **Civil Division**: https://www.justice.gov/civil - Defends agency actions
- **Environment and Natural Resources Division**: https://www.justice.gov/enrd
- **Antitrust Division**: https://www.justice.gov/atr
- **Civil Rights Division**: https://www.justice.gov/crt
- **Office of the Solicitor General**: https://www.justice.gov/osg - Supreme Court representation

## Key Administrative Functions
- **Legal Advice to Executive Branch**: OLC opinions binding on agencies
- **Defense of Agency Actions**: Civil Division represents agencies in court
- **FOIA Oversight**: OIP sets government-wide FOIA policy
- **Immigration Adjudication**: EOIR immigration courts
