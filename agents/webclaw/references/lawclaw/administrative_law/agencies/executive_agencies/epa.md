## Key Administrative Law Cases
- **Chevron v. NRDC** (1984) - EPA's bubble policy upheld
- **Massachusetts v. EPA** (2007) - Greenhouse gases as pollutants
- **Utility Air Regulatory Group v. EPA** (2014) - GHG permitting
- **Michigan v. EPA** (2015) - Cost consideration in regulations
- **West Virginia v. EPA** (2022) - Major Questions Doctrine
