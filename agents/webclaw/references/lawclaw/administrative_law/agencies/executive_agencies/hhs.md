## CMS/Medicare Administrative Appeals
- **Medicare Appeals Process**: 42 CFR Part 405
- **Five Levels of Medicare Appeals**:
  1. Redetermination (MAC)
  2. Reconsideration (QIC)
  3. ALJ Hearing (OMHA)
  4. Medicare Appeals Council
  5. Judicial Review (Federal District Court)
- **Office of Medicare Hearings and Appeals (OMHA)**: https://www.hhs.gov/about/agencies/omha/index.html
- **Medicare Appeals Council**: https://www.hhs.gov/about/agencies/omha/current-council-members/index.html
- **Key Case**: Mathews v. Eldridge (1976) - Disability benefits due process

## Provider Enrollment and Sanctions
- **Medicare Provider Enrollment**: 42 CFR Part 424

- **OIG Exclusions**: https://oig.hhs.gov/exclusions/

## Public Health Emergency Powers
- **Public Health Service Act**: 42 U.S.C. § 201 et seq.

- **Key Case**: National Federation of Independent Business v. OSHA (2022) - Vaccine mandate limits
