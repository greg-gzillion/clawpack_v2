# State Administrative Law Variations

## Major State Differences

### California
- **APA**: California Administrative Procedure Act (Gov. Code ｧ 11340 et seq.)
- **ALJ System**: Centralized Office of Administrative Hearings (OAH)
- **Rulemaking**: Office of Administrative Law (OAL) review required
- **Judicial Review**: Mandamus under CCP ｧ 1094.5
- **Resources**: https://www.dgs.ca.gov/oah | https://oal.ca.gov

### New York
- **APA**: State Administrative Procedure Act (SAPA)
- **ALJ System**: Agency-specific (no central panel)
- **Rulemaking**: Department of State review
- **Judicial Review**: Article 78 proceeding
- **Resources**: https://dos.ny.gov/state-administrative-procedure-act-sapa

### Texas
- **APA**: Texas Administrative Procedure Act (Gov. Code Ch. 2001)
- **ALJ System**: State Office of Administrative Hearings (SOAH)
- **Rulemaking**: Texas Register publication
- **Judicial Review**: Substantial evidence rule
- **Resources**: https://www.soah.texas.gov | https://www.sos.texas.gov/texreg

### Florida
- **APA**: Florida Administrative Procedure Act (Ch. 120, Fla. Stat.)
- **ALJ System**: Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH)
- **Rulemaking**: Joint Administrative Procedures Committee (JAPC) review
- **Judicial Review**: District Court of Appeal
- **Resources**: https://www.doah.state.fl.us

### Illinois
- **APA**: Illinois Administrative Procedure Act (5 ILCS 100)
- **ALJ System**: Agency-specific
- **Rulemaking**: Joint Committee on Administrative Rules (JCAR) review
- **Judicial Review**: Administrative Review Law
- **Resources**: https://www.ilga.gov/commission/jcar

### Massachusetts
- **APA**: Massachusetts Administrative Procedure Act (M.G.L. Ch. 30A)
- **ALJ System**: Division of Administrative Law Appeals (DALA)
- **Rulemaking**: Secretary of State publication
- **Judicial Review**: Superior Court
- **Resources**: https://www.mass.gov/orgs/division-of-administrative-law-appeals

### New Jersey
- **APA**: Administrative Procedure Act (N.J.S.A. 52:14B-1 et seq.)
- **ALJ System**: Office of Administrative Law (OAL)
- **Rulemaking**: Office of Administrative Law review
- **Judicial Review**: Appellate Division
- **Resources**: https://www.nj.gov/oal

### Pennsylvania
- **APA**: Commonwealth Documents Law (45 P.S. ｧ 1102 et seq.)
- **ALJ System**: Agency-specific
- **Rulemaking**: Pennsylvania Bulletin publication
- **Judicial Review**: Commonwealth Court
- **Resources**: https://www.pacodeandbulletin.gov

### Ohio
- **APA**: Ohio Administrative Procedure Act (R.C. 119)
- **ALJ System**: Agency-specific
- **Rulemaking**: Joint Committee on Agency Rule Review (JCARR)
- **Judicial Review**: Court of Common Pleas
- **Resources**: https://www.jcarr.state.oh.us

### Michigan
- **APA**: Administrative Procedures Act (MCL 24.201 et seq.)
- **ALJ System**: Agency-specific; some centralized hearing officers
- **Rulemaking**: Joint Committee on Administrative Rules (JCAR)
- **Judicial Review**: Circuit Court
- **Resources**: https://www.michigan.gov/jcar

### Washington
- **APA**: Administrative Procedure Act (RCW 34.05)
- **ALJ System**: Office of Administrative Hearings (OAH)
- **Rulemaking**: Washington State Register
- **Judicial Review**: Superior Court
- **Resources**: https://www.oah.wa.gov

## Common State Variations

### Rulemaking
| Feature | Federal | Common State Variations |
|---------|---------|------------------------|
| Publication | Federal Register | State Register/Code |
| Comment Period | 30-60 days | 21-45 days typical |
| Legislative Review | CRA | JCAR/JCAP review |
| Economic Analysis | EO 12866 | Some states require; others don't |

### Adjudication
| Feature | Federal | Common State Variations |
|---------|---------|------------------------|
| Central ALJ Panel | No | Yes in ~30 states |
| Separation of Functions | Required | Varies by state |
| Ex Parte Rules | Strict | Similar to federal |

### Judicial Review
| Feature | Federal | Common State Variations |
|---------|---------|------------------------|
| Exhaustion | Required | Required with exceptions |
| Standard | APA ｧ 706 | Similar "arbitrary/capricious" |
| Venue | D.C. Circuit/circuit of residence | County/state courts |
