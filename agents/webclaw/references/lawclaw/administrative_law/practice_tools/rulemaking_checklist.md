# Agency Rulemaking Checklist - APA Compliance

## Pre-Rulemaking Phase
- [ ] **Statutory Authority Identified**: Enabling statute authorizes rule
- [ ] **Regulatory Agenda**: Included in Unified Agenda (EO 12866)
- [ ] **Regulatory Impact Analysis**: For significant rules
- [ ] **Small Business Analysis**: Regulatory Flexibility Act compliance
- [ ] **Paperwork Reduction Act**: OMB clearance for information collection

## Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM)
- [ ] **Federal Register Publication**: 30+ days before effective date
- [ ] **Summary of Proposed Rule**: Clear statement of basis and purpose
- [ ] **Legal Authority Cited**: Specific statutory provisions
- [ ] **Cost-Benefit Analysis**: For economically significant rules
- [ ] **Alternatives Considered**: Discussed in preamble
- [ ] **Comment Period**: Minimum 30 days (60 recommended for major rules)

## Public Comment Period
- [ ] **Regulations.gov Posting**: Docket established
- [ ] **Comments Received**: Tracked and organized
- [ ] **Significant Comments Addressed**: Respond in final rule
- [ ] **Late Comments**: Considered if feasible
- [ ] **Ex Parte Communications**: Disclosed if formal rulemaking

## Final Rule Preparation
- [ ] **Response to Comments**: Preamble addresses significant issues
- [ ] **Changes Explained**: Differences from proposal justified
- [ ] **Logical Outgrowth Test**: Final rule within scope of proposal
- [ ] **Effective Date**: Minimum 30 days after publication (60 for major rules)
- [ ] **Congressional Review Act**: Submission to Congress and GAO

## OIRA Review (EO 12866)
- [ ] **Significance Determination**: Not significant, significant, economically significant
- [ ] **OIRA Submission**: 90-day review period
- [ ] **Return Letter Response**: If applicable
- [ ] **Interagency Review**: Circulated to affected agencies

## Post-Rule Actions
- [ ] **Federal Register Publication**: Final rule published
- [ ] **Compliance Guides**: Small entity compliance guides (SBREFA)
- [ ] **Effective Date Monitoring**: No early enforcement
- [ ] **Judicial Review Preparation**: Administrative record compiled
- [ ] **Retrospective Review**: Scheduled per EO 13563

## Common Pitfalls
- ?? **Inadequate Response to Comments**: Most common remand ground
- ?? **Logical Outgrowth Violation**: Final rule too different from proposal
- ?? **Insufficient Notice**: Comment period too short
- ?? **Missing Regulatory Impact Analysis**: Required for major rules
- ?? **CRA Non-Compliance**: Major rules must be submitted to Congress
