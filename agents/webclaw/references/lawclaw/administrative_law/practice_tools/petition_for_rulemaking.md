# Petition for Rulemaking - APA § 553(e)

## Overview
APA § 553(e) requires agencies to "give an interested person the right to petition for the issuance, amendment, or repeal of a rule."

## Petition Template
BEFORE THE [AGENCY NAME]

PETITION FOR RULEMAKING
Submitted Pursuant to 5 U.S.C. § 553(e)

[DATE]

Submitted by:
[Name]
[Organization]
[Address]
[Phone]
[Email]

I. Requested Action
Petitioner requests that the [Agency] initiate rulemaking to:
[ ] Issue a new rule regarding [subject]
[ ] Amend existing [CFR citation] to [proposed change]
[ ] Repeal [CFR citation] because [reason]

II. Statement of Interest
[Describe who you are and why this rulemaking affects you.]

III. Legal Authority
The [Agency] has authority to promulgate this rule under [statutory citation(s)].

IV. Proposed Rule Text
[Provide specific proposed regulatory language or describe in detail.]

V. Need for Rulemaking
This rulemaking is necessary because:

[Reason 1 - market failure, public health, safety, etc.]

[Reason 2 - statutory mandate, court order, etc.]

[Reason 3 - changed circumstances, new technology, etc.]

VI. Supporting Evidence
[Attach studies, data, expert reports, economic analysis.]

VII. Alternatives Considered
[Discuss alternatives and why proposed approach is superior.]

VIII. Conclusion
For the foregoing reasons, Petitioner respectfully requests that the [Agency] initiate rulemaking as described above.

Respectfully submitted,

[Signature]

## Agency Petition Procedures
| Agency | Petition Process | Response Time |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| EPA | 40 CFR Part 21 | Reasonable time |
| FDA | 21 CFR § 10.30 | 180 days |
| FTC | 16 CFR § 1.7 | No statutory deadline |
| SEC | 17 CFR § 201.192 | No statutory deadline |
| NHTSA | 49 CFR Part 552 | 120 days |

## Key Cases
- **WWHT, Inc. v. FCC (D.C. Cir. 1981)** - Agency must respond to petitions
- **American Horse Protection Ass'n v. Lyng (D.C. Cir. 1987)** - Judicial review of petition denials
- **Massachusetts v. EPA (2007)** - Agency must provide reasoned explanation for denial

## Agency Denial Standards
- Agency denial reviewed under "arbitrary and capricious" standard
- Must provide reasoned explanation for denial
- Cannot ignore petition indefinitely
