## Tips for Effective Comments
- **Be Specific**: Reference exact page numbers, paragraph numbers, or regulatory text
- **Provide Data**: Cite studies, surveys, or economic analysis
- **Suggest Alternatives**: Don't just criticize炥ropose solutions
- **Personalize**: Explain how the rule affects you/your members specifically
- **Be Professional**: Respectful tone is more persuasive
- **Timely Submission**: Submit before deadline (11:59 PM ET on closing date)
