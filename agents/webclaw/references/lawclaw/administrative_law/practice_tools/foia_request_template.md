# FOIA Request Template - 5 U.S.C. ｧ 552

## Simple FOIA Request Letter
[DATE]

VIA [EMAIL/CERTIFIED MAIL]

[FOIA Officer Name]
[Agency Name]
[Agency Address]
[City, State ZIP]

Re: Freedom of Information Act Request

Dear FOIA Officer:

Pursuant to the Freedom of Information Act, 5 U.S.C. ｧ 552, I request access to and copies of the following records:

[Describe records as specifically as possible. Include:

Date ranges

File names or types

Subject matter

Names of individuals involved

Any identifying numbers (case #, contract #, etc.)]

Examples:

All records relating to [subject matter] from [date] to [date]

Emails between [person] and [person] regarding [topic]

The [document name] dated [date] filed in [matter name]

All agency guidance documents concerning [topic]

If any of these records are maintained in electronic format, I request electronic copies.

Fee Category and Waiver Request
I request that I be placed in the [commercial use / educational institution / noncommercial scientific institution / news media / other] fee category.

[Optional fee waiver request:]
I request a waiver of all fees pursuant to 5 U.S.C. ｧ 552(a)(4)(A)(iii). Disclosure of the requested information is in the public interest because it will contribute significantly to public understanding of government operations and activities. [Explain how]. I am [not] seeking this information for commercial purposes.

Expedited Processing Request (if applicable)
I request expedited processing pursuant to 5 U.S.C. ｧ 552(a)(6)(E) and [agency regulation citation] because:
[ ] Circumstances threaten the life or physical safety of an individual
[ ] Urgently needed by media to inform public of actual or alleged government activity
[ ] Substantial loss of due process rights
[ ] Widespread and exceptional media interest with questions about government integrity

Agreement to Pay Fees
I agree to pay fees up to $[AMOUNT]. Please notify me if estimated fees exceed this amount before processing.

Contact Information
If you have any questions regarding this request, I can be reached at:

[Name]
[Address]
[Phone]
[Email]

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely,

[Signature]
[Printed Name]
