# Judicial Review Checklist - APA Claims

## Pre-Filing Requirements
- [ ] **Final Agency Action**: Is action final under Bennett v. Spear (1997)?
- [ ] **Exhaustion**: Have all administrative remedies been exhausted?
- [ ] **Ripeness**: Is claim ripe under Abbott Laboratories (1967)?
- [ ] **Standing**: Does client have Article III standing under Lujan?
- [ ] **Statute of Limitations**: Within 6 years under 28 U.S.C. ｧ 2401(a)? (Corner Post - accrual upon injury)

## APA Claims Checklist

### ｧ 706(2)(A) - Arbitrary and Capricious
- [ ] Agency failed to consider relevant factors (State Farm)
- [ ] Agency relied on factors Congress did not intend
- [ ] Agency offered no reasoned explanation for policy change (State Farm, Fox)
- [ ] Agency failed to respond to significant comments
- [ ] Agency failed to consider obvious alternatives
- [ ] Agency decision not supported by record evidence

### ｧ 706(2)(C) - Exceeded Statutory Authority
- [ ] Agency interpretation contrary to plain text (Loper Bright)
- [ ] Agency action exceeds delegated authority
- [ ] Major Questions Doctrine applies (West Virginia v. EPA)
- [ ] Agency action violates nondelegation doctrine

### ｧ 706(2)(D) - Procedural Violations
- [ ] Agency failed to provide required notice (APA ｧ 553)
- [ ] Agency failed to allow meaningful comment opportunity
- [ ] Agency failed to follow its own regulations
- [ ] Agency violated ex parte communication rules
- [ ] Agency violated FOIA or Sunshine Act

### ｧ 706(2)(E) - Substantial Evidence
- [ ] Formal rulemaking or adjudication (hearing required)
- [ ] Record lacks "such relevant evidence as a reasonable mind might accept"

### ｧ 706(2)(B) - Constitutional Claims
- [ ] Due Process violation (Mathews v. Eldridge factors)
- [ ] Equal Protection violation
- [ ] First Amendment violation
- [ ] Appointments Clause violation (Lucia)
- [ ] Seventh Amendment violation (Jarkesy)

## Remedy Selection
- [ ] **Vacatur**: Request complete vacatur of unlawful rule/action
- [ ] **Remand Without Vacatur**: Request remand for correction without disruption
- [ ] **Injunction**: Request preliminary/permanent injunction
- [ ] **Declaratory Judgment**: Request declaration of rights
- [ ] **Mandamus**: Compel agency action unlawfully withheld (APA ｧ 706(1))

## Administrative Record
- [ ] Request certified administrative record from agency
- [ ] Review record for completeness
- [ ] Identify extra-record evidence exceptions (if needed)
- [ ] Prepare privilege log for any withheld materials

## Complaint Drafting Checklist
- [ ] Identify proper defendant(s) (agency, officials in official capacity)
- [ ] Establish subject matter jurisdiction (28 U.S.C. ｧ 1331 - federal question)
- [ ] Establish venue (28 U.S.C. ｧ 1391(e) - where plaintiff resides or action arose)
- [ ] Cite APA ｧ 702 (waiver of sovereign immunity)
- [ ] Allege final agency action
- [ ] Identify specific APA ｧ 706 grounds
- [ ] Request specific relief

## Key Deadlines
| Action | Deadline | Authority |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| File APA complaint | 6 years from injury | 28 U.S.C. ｧ 2401(a) |
| Motion for preliminary injunction | ASAP with complaint | FRCP 65 |
| Administrative record filed | 60 days after service | Local rules |
| Motion for summary judgment | Per scheduling order | FRCP 56 |

## Post-Loper Bright Considerations (2024)
- [ ] Chevron deference no longer applies
- [ ] Court exercises independent judgment on statutory interpretation
- [ ] Agency interpretations receive Skidmore weight only
- [ ] Major Questions Doctrine remains viable
- [ ] Corner Post expands statute of limitations for new plaintiffs
