| International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights (ICESCR) | 1966 | Economic, social, cultural rights; U.S. signed but not ratified | https://www.ohchr.org/en/instruments-mechanisms/instruments/international-covenant-economic-social-and-cultural-rights |
