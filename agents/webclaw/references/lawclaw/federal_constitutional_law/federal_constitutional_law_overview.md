# Federal Constitutional Law - Overview

## What is Federal Constitutional Law?
Federal constitutional law is the body of law interpreting and applying the United States Constitution, the supreme law of the land. It establishes the framework of the federal government, allocates power among the three branches, defines the relationship between federal and state governments, and protects fundamental individual rights against government intrusion.
