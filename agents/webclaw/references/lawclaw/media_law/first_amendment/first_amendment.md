## Unprotected Categories of Speech
| Category | Definition | Test | URL |
|----------|------------|------|-----|
| Incitement | Speech directed to inciting imminent lawless action and likely to produce it | Brandenburg v. Ohio | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/395/444/ |
| Obscenity | Prurient interest; patently offensive; lacks serious value (SLAPS test) | Miller v. California | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/413/15/ |
| Fighting Words | Personally abusive epithets inherently likely to provoke violent reaction | Chaplinsky v. New Hampshire | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/315/568/ |
| True Threats | Serious expression of intent to commit unlawful violence | Virginia v. Black | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/538/343/ |
| Child Pornography | Visual depiction of minors in sexual conduct | New York v. Ferber | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/458/747/ |
| Defamation | False statements of fact harming reputation | Varies |
| Fraud | Knowing misrepresentation for gain | Varies |
