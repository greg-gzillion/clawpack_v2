# Media Law - Overview

## What is Media Law?
Media law governs the dissemination of information through various channels including print, broadcast, cable, and digital platforms. It encompasses constitutional protections under the First Amendment, tort liability for defamation and invasion of privacy, regulatory frameworks for broadcast and cable, and emerging issues in digital media.
