# Sports Law - Title IX and Gender Equity

## Title IX Overview
| Topic | Statute | Description | URL |
|-------|---------|-------------|-----|
| Title IX Text | 20 U.S.C. § 1681(a) | "No person...shall, on the basis of sex, be excluded from participation in, be denied the benefits of, or be subjected to discrimination under any education program or activity receiving Federal financial assistance" | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1681 |
| Athletics Application | 34 CFR Part 106 | Regulations implementing Title IX in athletics | https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-34/subtitle-B/chapter-I/part-106 |
| Grove City v. Bell | 465 U.S. 555 (1984) | Narrowed Title IX application to specific program | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/465/555/ |
| Civil Rights Restoration Act of 1987 | Pub. L. 100-259 | Overturned Grove City; institution-wide application | https://www.congress.gov/bill/100th-congress/senate-bill/557 |
