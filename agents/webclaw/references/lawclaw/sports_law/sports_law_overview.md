# Sports Law - Overview

## What is Sports Law?
Sports law encompasses the legal issues affecting professional and amateur athletics, including contract negotiation, antitrust and labor law, intellectual property, NCAA compliance, athlete representation, Title IX gender equity, tort liability, media rights, and sports betting regulation.
