# Government Contracts Law - Overview

## What is Government Contracts Law?
Government contracts law governs the formation, administration, and resolution of disputes arising from contracts between private entities and federal, state, or local government agencies. It is a specialized field combining contract law, administrative law, and procurement regulations.
