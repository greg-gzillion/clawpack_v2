# Vermont Constitution of 1793

## Overview
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Current Constitution | Constitution of Vermont (1793) |
| Previous Constitutions | 1777, 1786 |
| Amendments | 50+ amendments |
| Length | ~8,700 words |

## Key Provisions
| Chapter | Subject |
|---------|---------|
| Chapter I | A Declaration of the Rights of the Inhabitants of the State of Vermont |
| Chapter II | Plan or Frame of Government |

## Unique Features
- **First constitution to abolish slavery (1777)**
- **First constitution to establish universal manhood suffrage**
- Right to bear arms for common defense
- Strong individual rights protections
