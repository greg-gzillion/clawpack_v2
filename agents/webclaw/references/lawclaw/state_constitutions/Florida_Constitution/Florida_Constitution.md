## Overview
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Current Constitution | Constitution of Florida (1968) |
| Previous Constitutions | 1838, 1861, 1865, 1868, 1885 |
| Amendments | 140+ amendments |
| Length | ~45,000 words |

## Unique Features
- Explicit right to privacy (Art. I, § 23)
- Sunshine Law (Art. I, § 24)
- Save Our Homes (Art. VII, § 4) - Homestead tax cap
- Constitution Revision Commission (every 20 years)
