## Bankruptcy Certifications
| Certification | Issuing Body | Requirements |
|--------------|--------------|--------------|
| **Consumer Bankruptcy Specialist** | American Board of Certification (ABC) | 5+ years practice, 60+ cases, exam, references |
| **Business Bankruptcy Specialist** | American Board of Certification (ABC) | 5+ years practice, substantial involvement, exam |
| **Certified Bankruptcy Specialist** | State Bar (varies by state) | Varies; check state bar requirements |
