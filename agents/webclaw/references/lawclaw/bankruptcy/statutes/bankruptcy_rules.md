# Federal Rules of Bankruptcy Procedure

## Overview
The Federal Rules of Bankruptcy Procedure govern the procedure in bankruptcy cases and proceedings. They are promulgated by the Supreme Court under the Rules Enabling Act.

## Official Resources
- **Full Rules**: https://www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/current-rules-practice-procedure/federal-rules-bankruptcy-procedure
- **Rule 1001**: Scope of Rules and Forms

## Key Parts of the Bankruptcy Rules
| Part | Rules | Subject |
|------|-------|---------|
| **I** | 1001-1021 | Commencement of Case; Proceedings Relating to Petition and Order for Relief |
| **II** | 2001-2020 | Officers and Administration; Notices; Meetings; Examinations; Elections; Attorneys and Accountants |
| **III** | 3001-3022 | Claims and Distribution to Creditors and Equity Interest Holders; Plans |
| **IV** | 4001-4008 | The Debtor: Duties and Benefits |
| **V** | 5001-5012 | Courts and Clerks |
| **VI** | 6001-6011 | Collection and Liquidation of the Estate |
| **VII** | 7001-7087 | Adversary Proceedings |
| **VIII** | 8001-8028 | Appeals to District Court or Bankruptcy Appellate Panel |
| **IX** | 9001-9037 | General Provisions |
| **X** | 1001-1007 | United States Trustees |

## Adversary Proceeding Rules (Part VII)
Adversary proceedings are governed by Part VII rules, which largely incorporate the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure:

| Bankruptcy Rule | FRCP Counterpart |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Rule 7004 | Rule 4 (Service) |
| Rule 7008 | Rule 8 (General Rules of Pleading) |
| Rule 7012 | Rule 12 (Defenses and Objections) |
| Rule 7015 | Rule 15 (Amended and Supplemental Pleadings) |
| Rule 7026 | Rule 26 (Duty to Disclose; Discovery) |
| Rule 7056 | Rule 56 (Summary Judgment) |

## Official Bankruptcy Forms
Forms are mandatory and can be found at: https://www.uscourts.gov/forms/bankruptcy-forms

| Form Series | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| **100 Series** | Voluntary Petition and Schedules |
| **200 Series** | Statement of Financial Affairs, Attorney Disclosure |
| **300 Series** | Claims and Noticing |
| **400 Series** | Chapter 11, Chapter 12, Chapter 13 Plans |

## Local Bankruptcy Rules
Each bankruptcy court has local rules that supplement the Federal Rules. Check individual court websites for:
- **Local filing requirements**
- **Judge-specific procedures**
- **Electronic filing rules (CM/ECF)**
