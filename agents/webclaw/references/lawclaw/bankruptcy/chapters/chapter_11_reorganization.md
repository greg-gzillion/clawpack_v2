# Chapter 11 Bankruptcy - Reorganization

## Overview
Chapter 11 allows businesses (and some individuals) to reorganize their debts while continuing operations. The debtor typically remains in possession as "debtor-in-possession" (DIP).

## Eligibility
- **Businesses**: Corporations, LLCs, partnerships (no debt limits)
- **Individuals**: Those exceeding Chapter 13 debt limits or with business debts
- **Excluded**: Stockbrokers, commodity brokers

## The Chapter 11 Process
1. **File Petition**: Voluntary or involuntary
2. **Automatic Stay**: Immediate protection
3. **Debtor-in-Possession**: Management remains in control (unless trustee appointed)
4. **Creditors' Committee**: Appointed to represent unsecured creditors
5. **Exclusivity Period**: Debtor has exclusive right to file plan (120 days, extendable)
6. **Disclosure Statement**: Approved before soliciting votes
7. **Plan Confirmation**: Court approves plan (ž 1129)
8. **Consummation**: Debtor implements plan

## Plan Requirements (ž 1129)
- **Feasibility**: Plan must be feasible
- **Best Interests Test**: Creditors receive at least Chapter 7 liquidation value
- **Acceptance**: At least one impaired class accepts (excluding insiders)
- **Absolute Priority Rule**: Senior classes paid in full before junior classes receive anything

## Small Business Debtor (Subchapter V)
- **Eligibility**: Aggregate debts = $3,024,725 (adjusted periodically)
- **Benefits**: No creditors' committee, no disclosure statement, streamlined plan confirmation
- **Trustee**: Standing trustee appointed to facilitate consensual plan

## DIP Financing (ž 364)
- **Super-priority**: Lenders receive priority over pre-petition creditors
- **Priming Liens**: May prime existing secured creditors (with adequate protection)
- **Roll-ups**: Pre-petition debt rolled into DIP facility

## Key Sections
| Section | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| **ž 363** | Use/Sale of assets (including ž 363 sales "free and clear") |
| **ž 365** | Assumption/rejection of executory contracts and leases |
| **ž 503(b)(9)** | Administrative priority for goods delivered within 20 days pre-petition |
| **ž 547** | Preference avoidance (ordinary course defense) |
