# Chapter 13 Bankruptcy Checklist - Debtor's Attorney

## Pre-Filing Assessment
- [ ] **Eligibility Check**: Individual with regular income (§ 109(e))
- [ ] **Debt Limits** (as of 2024):
  - [ ] Unsecured debts < $465,275
  - [ ] Secured debts < $1,395,875
- [ ] **Prior Bankruptcy**: No Ch. 13 within 2 years; Ch. 7 within 4 years (§ 1328(f))
- [ ] **Credit Counseling**: Completed within 180 days pre-filing
- [ ] **Calculate Disposable Income** (Form 122C):
  - [ ] Current Monthly Income (CMI)
  - [ ] Applicable Commitment Period (36 or 60 months)
  - [ ] Projected disposable income

## Plan Preparation
- [ ] **Classify Claims**: Secured, priority unsecured, general unsecured
- [ ] **Determine Plan Length**: 36 months (below median) or 60 months (above median)
- [ ] **Secured Claims Treatment**:
  - [ ] Mortgage arrears cured over plan
  - [ ] Vehicle cramdowns (910-day rule)
  - [ ] Strip wholly unsecured junior liens
- [ ] **Priority Claims**: Paid in full (taxes, domestic support)
- [ ] **Unsecured Claims**: Pay projected disposable income
- [ ] **Trustee Fee**: 10% of plan payments (varies by district)
- [ ] **Adequate Protection Payments**: Pre-confirmation payments to secured creditors

## Filing Day
- [ ] **Voluntary Petition** (Form 101)
- [ ] **Schedules A/B through J** (Forms 106-107)
- [ ] **Statement of Financial Affairs** (Form 107)
- [ ] **Chapter 13 Plan** (Local form)
- [ ] **Rights and Responsibilities** (Form 101)
- [ ] **Creditor Matrix**
- [ ] **Filing Fee**: $313 (as of 2024)
- [ ] **First Plan Payment**: Due within 30 days (§ 1326(a))

## Post-Filing (First 30 Days)
- [ ] **Notice to Creditors**: 28 days notice of confirmation hearing
- [ ] **File Pay Advices**: 60 days pre-filing
- [ ] **File Tax Returns**: Most recent 4 years (to trustee, any creditor upon request)
- [ ] **Begin Plan Payments to Trustee**: Within 30 days
- [ ] **Objections to Claims**: Deadline set by court

## § 341 Meeting of Creditors
- [ ] **Debtor MUST Attend**: Spouse if joint case
- [ ] **Bring Documents**: ID, Social Security card, bank statements, tax returns
- [ ] **Trustee Review**: Plan feasibility, disposable income calculation
- [ ] **Address Trustee Objections**: Amend plan if necessary

## Confirmation Process
- [ ] **Confirmation Hearing**: Usually 45-60 days post-filing
- [ ] **Requirements for Confirmation** (§ 1325):
  - [ ] Plan proposed in good faith
  - [ ] Plan feasible
  - [ ] Best interests test satisfied
  - [ ] Disposable income test satisfied
  - [ ] Secured claims properly treated
  - [ ] Priority claims paid in full
- [ ] **Trustee Recommendation**: Address any objections
- [ ] **Confirmation Order**: Entered if requirements met

## Plan Administration (3-5 Years)
- [ ] **Monthly Payments**: Debtor pays trustee
- [ ] **Trustee Distributes**: Per confirmed plan
- [ ] **Annual Reporting**: Debtor provides tax returns to trustee
- [ ] **Post-Petition Debt**: Court approval required for new credit
- [ ] **Employment Changes**: Notify trustee
- [ ] **Mortgage Payments**: Must remain current post-petition

## Plan Modification
- [ ] **Change in Circumstances**: Job loss, medical emergency
- [ ] **Motion to Modify**: File with court
- [ ] **Modified Plan**: Must meet confirmation requirements
- [ ] **Suspension of Payments**: Possible for temporary hardship

## Plan Completion
- [ ] **All Plan Payments Made**: Trustee certifies completion
- [ ] **Domestic Support Current**: Certification required (§ 1328(a))
- [ ] **Financial Management Course**: Complete and file certificate
- [ ] **Discharge Order**: Broader than Chapter 7
- [ ] **Case Closed**

## Hardship Discharge
- [ ] **Motion for Hardship Discharge** (§ 1328(b))
- [ ] **Requirements**:
  - [ ] Failure due to circumstances beyond debtor's control
  - [ ] Creditors received = Chapter 7 liquidation value
  - [ ] Modification not practicable

## Common Pitfalls
- ?? **Missing Plan Payments**: Trustee moves to dismiss
- ?? **Post-Petition Debt**: Without court approval, may jeopardize plan
- ?? **Tax Returns Not Provided**: Confirmation denied or case dismissed
- ?? **Mortgage Payments**: Must stay current or seek modification
