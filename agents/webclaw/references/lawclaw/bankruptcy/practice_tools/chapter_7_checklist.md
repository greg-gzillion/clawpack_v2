# Chapter 7 Bankruptcy Checklist - Debtor's Attorney

## Pre-Filing (30+ Days Before)
- [ ] **Initial Client Interview**: Complete questionnaire, identify all debts/assets
- [ ] **Credit Counseling**: Client must complete approved course within 180 days pre-filing
- [ ] **Means Test Calculation** (Form 122A-1/2): Determine eligibility
- [ ] **Gather Documents**:
  - [ ] Tax returns (last 2 years)
  - [ ] Pay stubs (last 6 months)
  - [ ] Bank statements (last 3-6 months)
  - [ ] Real estate deeds, mortgage statements
  - [ ] Vehicle titles, loan statements
  - [ ] Credit reports from all 3 bureaus
  - [ ] Lawsuits, judgments, garnishments
- [ ] **Identify Exemptions**: State vs. federal (ｧ 522)
- [ ] **Identify Non-Exempt Assets**: Determine if client can protect
- [ ] **Check Prior Bankruptcy History**: 8-year rule for Ch. 7 (ｧ 727(a)(8))
- [ ] **Collect Retainer/Fee**: Flat fee typically; payment plan must be pre-petition

## Filing Day
- [ ] **Prepare Petition** (Official Form 101)
- [ ] **Schedules A/B through J** (Forms 106-107)
- [ ] **Statement of Financial Affairs** (Form 107)
- [ ] **Statement of Intent** (Form 108) - Surrender/Redeem/Reaffirm
- [ ] **Creditor Matrix**: Complete list with accurate addresses
- [ ] **File Electronically** (or in person)
- [ ] **Pay Filing Fee**: $338 (as of 2024) or application to pay in installments/waiver
- [ ] **Automatic Stay**: Effective immediately upon filing

## Post-Filing (First 30 Days)
- [ ] **Notice to Creditors**: Clerk sends within days
- [ ] **Schedule ｧ 341 Meeting**: Usually 21-40 days post-filing
- [ ] **File Pay Advices**: 60 days pre-filing (if not filed with petition)
- [ ] **File Tax Returns**: Most recent year to trustee (at least 7 days before ｧ 341)
- [ ] **Complete Financial Management Course**: Required for discharge
- [ ] **File Certification of Course Completion** (Form 423)

## ｧ 341 Meeting of Creditors
- [ ] **Prepare Client**: Review questions trustee will ask
- [ ] **Bring Documents**: ID, Social Security card, bank statements
- [ ] **Attend Meeting**: Client MUST attend
- [ ] **Address Trustee Concerns**: Provide additional documents if requested

## Post-ｧ 341 Meeting
- [ ] **Respond to Trustee Inquiries**: Timely production of requested documents
- [ ] **Reaffirmation Agreements** (if any): File within 60 days of ｧ 341
- [ ] **Redemption/Surrender**: Complete per Statement of Intent
- [ ] **Objections to Exemptions**: Due 30 days after ｧ 341 concludes
- [ ] **Objections to Discharge**: Due 60 days after first date set for ｧ 341

## Discharge (60-90 Days After ｧ 341)
- [ ] **Discharge Order**: Usually entered automatically
- [ ] **Verify No Objections Filed**: ｧ 727 or ｧ 523 complaints
- [ ] **Case Closed**: Trustee files final report
- [ ] **Client Counseling**: Rebuilding credit, fresh start

## Common Pitfalls
- ?? **Pre-Filing Credit Counseling**: Cannot waive; case dismissed if not completed
- ?? **Inaccurate Schedules**: Amendment required; may raise red flags
- ?? **Hidden Assets**: Denial of discharge, potential criminal charges
- ?? **Post-Filing Inheritance**: Must report within 180 days (ｧ 541(a)(5))
- ?? **Missed ｧ 341 Meeting**: Case may be dismissed

## Key Deadlines
| Deadline | Action | Citation |
|----------|--------|----------|
| 180 days pre-filing | Credit counseling | ｧ 109(h) |
| 14 days post-filing | Pay advices (if not filed with petition) | Rule 1007(b) |
| 7 days before ｧ 341 | Tax return to trustee | Rule 4002(b) |
| 30 days after ｧ 341 | Objections to exemptions | Rule 4003(b) |
| 60 days after ｧ 341 | Objections to discharge (ｧ 727) | Rule 4004(a) |
| 60 days after ｧ 341 | Objections to dischargeability (ｧ 523(a)(2),(4),(6)) | Rule 4007(c) |
| 60 days after ｧ 341 | Reaffirmation agreement filing | Rule 4008(a) |
| 60 days after ｧ 341 | Financial management course | ｧ 727(a)(11) |
