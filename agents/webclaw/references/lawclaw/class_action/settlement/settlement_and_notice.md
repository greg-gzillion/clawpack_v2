## Settlement Approval Factors
| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Strength of plaintiff's case | Likelihood of success at trial |
| Complexity, expense, and duration of litigation | Resources required to litigate |
| Stage of proceedings | Amount of discovery completed |
| Judgment to be rendered | Defendant's ability to pay |
| Range of possible recovery | Potential damages at trial |
| Reaction of class members | Objections and opt-outs |

## Objections to Settlement
- Class members may object to proposed settlement
- Must file objection and state grounds
- May appear at fairness hearing
- **Rule 23(e)(5)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/rules/frcp/rule_23#e_5

## Cy Pres Distributions
- **Cornell LII - Cy Pres**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/cy_pres_doctrine
- Distribution of unclaimed funds to charitable organizations
- Must have nexus to class claims
