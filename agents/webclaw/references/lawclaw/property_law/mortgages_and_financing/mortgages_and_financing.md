## Transfer of Mortgaged Property
| Scenario | Rule | Description | URL |
|----------|------|-------------|-----|
| Assumption | Grantee assumes mortgage | Both grantor and grantee personally liable | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/assumption_of_mortgage |
