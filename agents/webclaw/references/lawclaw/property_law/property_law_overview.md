# Property Law - Overview

## What is Property Law?
Property law governs the ownership, use, and transfer of real and personal property. It establishes the legal framework for estates in land, concurrent ownership, landlord-tenant relationships, real estate transactions, mortgages, easements, covenants, and land use regulation.
