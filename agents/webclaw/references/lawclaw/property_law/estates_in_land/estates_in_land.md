# Property Law - Estates in Land

## Freehold Estates
| Estate | Description | Duration | URL |
|--------|-------------|----------|-----|
| Fee Simple Absolute | Full ownership; unlimited duration; freely alienable | Perpetual | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fee_simple |
| Fee Simple Determinable | Automatically terminates upon occurrence of specified event | Potentially infinite | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fee_simple_determinable |
| Fee Simple Subject to Condition Subsequent | May be terminated by grantor upon occurrence of event | Potentially infinite | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fee_simple_subject_to_condition_subsequent |
| Fee Simple Subject to Executory Limitation | Terminates in favor of third party upon event | Potentially infinite | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fee_simple_subject_to_executory_limitation |
| Fee Tail | Inheritable only by lineal descendants (abolished in most states) | Potentially infinite | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fee_tail |
| Life Estate | Ownership for duration of measuring life | Life of grantee or autre vie | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/life_estate |

## Words of Limitation Creating Fee Simple Defeasible Estates
| Estate | Words of Limitation | URL |
|--------|---------------------|-----|

| Fee Simple Subject to Condition Subsequent | "To A, but if...then grantor may re-enter" | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fee_simple_subject_to_condition_subsequent |
| Fee Simple Subject to Executory Limitation | "To A, but if...then to B" | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fee_simple_subject_to_executory_limitation |

## Non-Freehold Estates (Leaseholds)
| Estate | Description | Duration | URL |
|--------|-------------|----------|-----|
| Term of Years | Fixed period; automatically terminates | Specified term | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/term_of_years |
| Periodic Tenancy | Renewable period; notice required to terminate | Month-to-month; year-to-year | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/periodic_tenancy |
