# Property Law - Concurrent Ownership

## Forms of Concurrent Ownership
| Form | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Tenancy in Common | Separate but undivided interests; no right of survivorship; freely alienable | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/tenancy_in_common |
| Joint Tenancy | Right of survivorship; requires four unities | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/joint_tenancy |
| Tenancy by the Entirety | Married couples only; right of survivorship; neither can unilaterally sever | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/tenancy_by_the_entirety |
| Community Property | Marital property in nine states; equal ownership of property acquired during marriage | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/community_property |

## Four Unities of Joint Tenancy
| Unity | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Unity of Time | Interests acquired at same time | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/joint_tenancy |
| Unity of Title | Interests acquired by same instrument | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/joint_tenancy |
| Unity of Interest | Equal undivided interests | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/joint_tenancy |
| Unity of Possession | Equal right to possess whole | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/joint_tenancy |

## Rights and Obligations of Co-Tenants
| Right/Obligation | Description | URL |
|------------------|-------------|-----|
| Right to Possess | Each co-tenant has equal right to possess whole | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/concurrent_estate |
| Ouster | Excluding co-tenant from possession; liable for rental value | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/ouster |
| Accounting for Rents | Co-tenant collecting rents from third party must account to others | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/accounting |
| Operating Expenses | Co-tenant paying necessary expenses may seek contribution | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/contribution |
| Repairs | Generally no right to contribution for voluntary repairs | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/repairs |
| Improvements | No contribution; credit in partition for value added | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/improvements |
| Waste | Co-tenant liable for waste to other co-tenants | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/waste |
