## Types of Deeds
| Type | Description | Covenant of Title | URL |
|------|-------------|------------------|-----|
| General Warranty Deed | Warrants title against all defects | Full covenants | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/general_warranty_deed |
| Special Warranty Deed | Warrants title only against grantor's acts | Limited covenants | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/special_warranty_deed |
| Quitclaim Deed | Releases any interest grantor may have; no warranties | No covenants | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/quitclaim_deed |
| Bargain and Sale Deed | Implies ownership but no express warranties | Implied covenant of title | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/bargain_and_sale_deed |
