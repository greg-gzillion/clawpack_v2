## Occurrence vs. Claims-Made Policies
| Feature | Occurrence | Claims-Made |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| Trigger | Injury/damage during policy period | Claim made during policy period |
| Retroactive Date | Not applicable | Limits coverage for prior acts |
| Extended Reporting Period (ERP) | Not applicable | Tail coverage option |
| Common Uses | CGL, auto | E&O, D&O, malpractice |
