# Product Liability Law - Overview

## What is Product Liability Law?
Product liability law governs the liability of manufacturers, distributors, suppliers, and retailers for injuries caused by defective products. It encompasses claims based on strict liability, negligence, and breach of warranty, and is primarily governed by state law, subject to federal preemption in certain regulated industries.
