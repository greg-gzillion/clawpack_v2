## Tests for Design Defect
| Test | Description | Jurisdictions | URL |
|------|-------------|---------------|-----|

| Factor | Description | URL |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| 1. Usefulness and Desirability | Product's utility to user and public | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/risk_utility_test |
| 2. Safety Aspects | Likelihood and seriousness of injury | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/risk_utility_test |
| 3. Availability of Substitute | Existence of safer alternative product | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/risk_utility_test |
| 4. Ability to Eliminate Unsafe Character | Manufacturer's ability to eliminate danger without impairing usefulness | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/risk_utility_test |
