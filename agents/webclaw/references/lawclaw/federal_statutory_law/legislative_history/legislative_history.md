# Federal Statutory Law - Legislative History Research

## What is Legislative History?
Legislative history consists of the documents and records produced during the legislative process. Courts use legislative history to interpret ambiguous statutory provisions and determine congressional intent.

## Hierarchy of Legislative History Materials
| Weight | Document | Description | URL |
|--------|----------|-------------|-----|
| Highest | Committee Reports | Official committee analysis and recommendations | https://www.congress.gov/congressional-reports |
| High | Conference Reports | Report reconciling House and Senate versions | https://www.congress.gov/conference-reports |
| High | Bill Text (as enacted) | Final version of legislation | https://www.congress.gov/bill |
| Medium | Floor Debate Statements | Sponsor statements; manager's amendment explanations | https://www.congress.gov/congressional-record |
| Medium | Earlier Bill Versions | Show evolution of statutory language | https://www.congress.gov/bill |
| Lower | Hearing Testimony | Witness statements (limited weight) | https://www.congress.gov/committee-hearings |
| Lowest | Post-Enactment Statements | Statements after passage (minimal weight) | https://www.congress.gov/congressional-record |

## Legislative History Research Process
| Step | Action | Resource | URL |
|------|--------|----------|-----|
| 1 | Find U.S. Code citation | U.S. Code | https://uscode.house.gov |
| 2 | Identify Public Law number | U.S. Code notes | https://uscode.house.gov |
| 3 | Locate Public Law | Congress.gov | https://www.congress.gov/public-laws/ |
| 4 | Find bill number | Public Law preamble | https://www.congress.gov |
| 5 | Track bill through Congress | Congress.gov bill summary | https://www.congress.gov/bill |
| 6 | Locate committee reports | Congress.gov | https://www.congress.gov/congressional-reports |
| 7 | Review Congressional Record | Congress.gov | https://www.congress.gov/congressional-record |
| 8 | Review hearing records | Committee websites; Congress.gov | https://www.congress.gov/committee-hearings |
