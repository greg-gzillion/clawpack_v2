# Federal Statutory Law - Positive Law Codification

## What is Positive Law Codification?
Positive law codification is the process by which Congress enacts a title of the United States Code as positive law, making that title legal evidence of the law. Non-positive law titles are only prima facie evidence of the law; the Statutes at Large remain the legal evidence for those titles.

## Positive Law vs. Non-Positive Law
| Feature | Positive Law Title | Non-Positive Law Title | URL |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|-----|
| Legal Status | Enacted by Congress as law | Compiled by Law Revision Counsel | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| Evidence | Constitutes legal evidence of the law | Prima facie evidence of the law | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| Amendments | Amended directly by Congress | Congress amends underlying statutes | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| Repeal of Prior Law | Repeals prior statutes on same subject | Prior statutes remain in effect | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| Citation | Cite directly to U.S. Code | Statutes at Large remain authoritative | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |

## Positive Law Codification Process
| Step | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 1. Preparation | Law Revision Counsel prepares title for enactment | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| 2. Review | Congress reviews proposed title | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| 3. Enactment | Congress enacts title as positive law | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| 4. Repeal | Prior laws on subject repealed | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
| 5. Future Amendments | Congress amends title directly | https://uscode.house.gov/about/positive_law_codification.xhtml |
