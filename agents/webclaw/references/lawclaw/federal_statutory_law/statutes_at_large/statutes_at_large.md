# Federal Statutory Law - Statutes at Large

## What are the Statutes at Large?
The United States Statutes at Large is the official chronological compilation of all laws and resolutions enacted by Congress. It is published by the Government Publishing Office and serves as the permanent evidence of federal statutory law.
