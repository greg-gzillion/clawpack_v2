# Health Law - Overview

## What is Health Law?
Health law encompasses federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and policies governing the healthcare industry, including providers, payers, patients, and the relationships among them. It covers regulatory compliance, reimbursement, fraud and abuse, privacy, and bioethics.
