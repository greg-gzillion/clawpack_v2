# Labor Law - Overview

## What is Labor Law?
Labor law governs the relationship between employers, employees, and labor unions. It encompasses union organizing and collective bargaining (traditional labor law), as well as employment law covering wages, hours, workplace safety, discrimination, and employee benefits.
