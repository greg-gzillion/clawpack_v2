# International Trade Law - Overview

## What is International Trade Law?
International trade law governs the exchange of goods, services, and intellectual property across national borders. It encompasses the rules and agreements of the World Trade Organization (WTO), free trade agreements (FTAs), customs regulations, trade remedies, export controls, and economic sanctions.
