| Criterion | Description | URL |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| Annual Effect | $100 million or more in any year | https://www.archives.gov/federal-register/executive-orders/1993.html |
| Material Adverse Effect | Economy; productivity; competition; jobs; environment; public health | https://www.archives.gov/federal-register/executive-orders/1993.html |
| Inconsistency | Creates serious inconsistency or interferes with other agency action | https://www.archives.gov/federal-register/executive-orders/1993.html |
