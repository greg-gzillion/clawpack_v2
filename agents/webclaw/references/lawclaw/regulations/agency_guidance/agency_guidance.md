## Binding Effect of Guidance
| Doctrine | Case | Description | URL |
|----------|------|-------------|-----|
| Non-Binding Nature | Appalachian Power v. EPA | Guidance does not have force of law | https://law.justia.com/cases/federal/appellate-courts/cadc/97-1323/ |
| Practical Binding Effect | Appalachian Power | Guidance that effectively binds regulated parties may be reviewable | https://law.justia.com/cases/federal/appellate-courts/cadc/97-1323/ |
| Final Agency Action | Bennett v. Spear | Guidance may be final action if determines rights/obligations and legal consequences flow | https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/520/154/ |
| Judicial Review | APA Ï 704 | Final agency action subject to review | https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/704 |
