# Regulations and Administrative Law - Overview

## What is Administrative Law?
Administrative law governs the creation, operation, and review of federal and state administrative agencies. It encompasses the procedures agencies must follow when promulgating regulations (rulemaking) and when adjudicating individual cases (adjudication), as well as the standards for judicial review of agency actions.
