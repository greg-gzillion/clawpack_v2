# Criminal Procedure - Sixth Amendment

## Overview
The Sixth Amendment guarantees the rights of criminal defendants, including the right to a speedy and public trial, an impartial jury, confrontation of witnesses, compulsory process, and assistance of counsel.

- **Full Text**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/constitution/sixth_amendment
