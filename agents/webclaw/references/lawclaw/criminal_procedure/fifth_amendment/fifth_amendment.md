# Criminal Procedure - Fifth Amendment

## Overview
The Fifth Amendment protects against self-incrimination and double jeopardy, requires grand jury indictment for federal felonies, and guarantees due process.

- **Full Text**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/constitution/fifth_amendment
