## Methods of Distribution
| Method | Description | Example | URL |
|--------|-------------|---------|-----|
| Per Stirpes | Each branch of family takes share of deceased ancestor | Children of deceased child share parent's portion | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/per_stirpes |
| Per Capita at Each Generation | Equal shares at each generational level | All living descendants at nearest generation take equally | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/per_capita_at_each_generation |
| Per Capita | Equal shares to all named individuals | Each named person takes equal share | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/per_capita |
