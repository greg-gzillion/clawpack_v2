# Trusts and Estates Law - Overview

## What is Trusts and Estates Law?
Trusts and estates law governs the disposition of property at death and the management of property for beneficiaries. It encompasses intestate succession, wills, trusts, estate administration, fiduciary duties, and estate and gift taxation. This area is primarily state law, with significant federal tax overlay.
