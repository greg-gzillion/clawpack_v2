# Professional Liability Law - Overview

## What is Professional Liability Law?
Professional liability law governs the legal responsibility of professionals𡟙ncluding lawyers, doctors, accountants, architects, and others㻖or harm caused by negligence, errors, omissions, or breaches of fiduciary duty in the performance of professional services. It encompasses both tort and contract claims and is often governed by specialized statutes and common law doctrines.
