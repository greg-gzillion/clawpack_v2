## Case-Within-a-Case Requirement
| Element | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Definition | Plaintiff must prove underlying case would have succeeded | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/case_within_a_case |
| Transactional Malpractice | Must show better outcome with competent representation | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/legal_malpractice |
| Burden of Proof | Plaintiff bears burden to prove causation | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/burden_of_proof |
| Settlement Value | Lost settlement opportunity may be compensable | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/legal_malpractice |

## Defenses to Legal Malpractice
| Defense | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| No Attorney-Client Relationship | No duty owed | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/attorney_client_relationship |
| Statute of Limitations | Typically 1-6 years depending on state and discovery rule | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/statute_of_limitations |
| Judgment Immunity | Attorney not liable for exercise of professional judgment | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/judgmental_immunity |
| Comparative Negligence | Client's own fault reduces or bars recovery | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/comparative_negligence |
