# Maritime Law (Admiralty) - Overview

## What is Maritime Law?
Maritime law (admiralty law) governs activities on navigable waters, including shipping, navigation, commerce, and marine casualties. It encompasses both domestic statutes and international conventions, with unique jurisdictional rules and remedies.
