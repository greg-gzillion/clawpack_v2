# Legal Ethics - State Variations

## California
| Topic | Variation from Model Rules | URL |
|-------|---------------------------|-----|
| Rules of Professional Conduct | California Rules of Professional Conduct (CRPC) | https://www.calbar.ca.gov/Attorneys/Conduct-Discipline/Rules/Rules-of-Professional-Conduct |
| State Bar | Mandatory membership in State Bar of California | https://www.calbar.ca.gov |
| Confidentiality | Different exceptions; duty of confidentiality | CRPC 1.6 |
| Conflicts | CRPC 1.7-1.10 | Varies |
| Advertising | Strict rules; filing requirements | CRPC 7.1-7.5 |
| Client Trust Account Protection Program | Mandatory registration and compliance | https://www.calbar.ca.gov/Attorneys/Client-Trust-Account-Protection-Program |

## New York
| Topic | Variation from Model Rules | URL |
|-------|---------------------------|-----|
| New York Rules of Professional Conduct | Adopted 2009; closely follows Model Rules | https://www.nysba.org/rules-of-professional-conduct/ |
| State Bar | Voluntary NYSBA; mandatory registration with OCA | https://www.nycourts.gov/attorneys/ |
| Pro Bono Requirement | 50-hour pro bono requirement for bar admission | https://www.nycourts.gov/attorneys/probono/ |
| Cybersecurity | SHIELD Act data security requirements | Varies |
| CLE | 24 hours every 2 years | https://www.nycourts.gov/attorneys/cle/ |

## Texas
| Topic | Variation from Model Rules | URL |

## District of Columbia
| Topic | Variation from Model Rules | URL |
