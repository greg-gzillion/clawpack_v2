# Legal Ethics and Professional Responsibility - Overview

## What is Legal Ethics?
Legal ethics governs the professional conduct of lawyers. It encompasses the duties lawyers owe to clients, courts, the profession, and the public. The rules are primarily codified in the ABA Model Rules of Professional Conduct, which have been adopted with variations by all 50 states.
