## Rule 1.6: Confidentiality of Information
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Basic Duty | Shall not reveal information relating to representation | https://www.americanbar.org/groups/professional_responsibility/publications/model_rules_of_professional_conduct/rule_1_6_confidentiality_of_information/ |
| Scope | Broader than attorney-client privilege; covers all information relating to representation | Varies |
| Exceptions - Consent | May reveal with informed consent | Varies |
| Exceptions - Implied Authority | May reveal to carry out representation | Varies |
| Exceptions - Prevent Harm | May reveal to prevent reasonably certain death or substantial bodily harm | Varies |
| Exceptions - Prevent Crime/Fraud | May reveal to prevent client from committing crime/fraud reasonably certain to cause substantial financial injury | Varies |
| Exceptions - Legal Claims | May reveal to establish claim/defense in lawyer-client controversy | Varies |
| Exceptions - Compliance | May reveal to comply with law or court order | Varies |

## Rule 1.7: Conflict of Interest - Current Clients
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Basic Prohibition | No representation if concurrent conflict exists | https://www.americanbar.org/groups/professional_responsibility/publications/model_rules_of_professional_conduct/rule_1_7_conflict_of_interest_current_clients/ |
| Direct Adversity | Representation directly adverse to another current client | Varies |
| Material Limitation | Significant risk that representation will be materially limited by responsibilities to another client, former client, or personal interest | Varies |
| Consentability | Conflict waivable only if lawyer reasonably believes competent and diligent representation possible, not prohibited by law, and not involving assertion of claim by one client against another in same litigation | Varies |
| Informed Consent | Must obtain informed consent, confirmed in writing | Varies |

| Related Lawyers | Conflict extends to lawyer related as parent, child, sibling, or spouse | Varies |
