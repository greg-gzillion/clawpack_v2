## Rule 3.4: Fairness to Opposing Party and Counsel
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Evidence Obstruction | Shall not unlawfully obstruct access to evidence; alter/destroy evidence; conceal evidence | https://www.americanbar.org/groups/professional_responsibility/publications/model_rules_of_professional_conduct/rule_3_4_fairness_to_opposing_party_and_counsel/ |
| Falsifying Evidence | Shall not falsify evidence or counsel/assist witness to testify falsely | Varies |
| Discovery Obligations | Shall not make frivolous discovery request or fail to make diligent effort to comply | Varies |

| Voir Dire | Shall not allude to irrelevant or inadmissible matters; cannot assert personal knowledge | Varies |

## Rule 3.6: Trial Publicity
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Prejudicial Statements | Shall not make extrajudicial statement that lawyer knows will have substantial likelihood of materially prejudicing proceeding | https://www.americanbar.org/groups/professional_responsibility/publications/model_rules_of_professional_conduct/rule_3_6_trial_publicity/ |
| Permitted Statements | May state: claim/defense; public information; investigation in progress; scheduling; request for assistance; warning of danger; identity of persons (except when prohibited) | Varies |
| Right of Reply | May make statement to protect client from substantial undue prejudicial effect of recent publicity not initiated by lawyer | Varies |

## Rule 3.7: Lawyer as Witness
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| General Prohibition | Lawyer shall not act as advocate at trial where lawyer is likely to be necessary witness | https://www.americanbar.org/groups/professional_responsibility/publications/model_rules_of_professional_conduct/rule_3_7_lawyer_as_witness/ |
| Exceptions | (1) Uncontested issues; (2) Legal services rendered; (3) Substantial hardship on client | Varies |
| Firm Imputation | Generally imputed to entire firm unless precluded by Rule 1.7 or 1.9 | Varies |
