# Legal Ethics - Fees and Billing

## Rule 1.5: Fees
| Topic | Description | URL |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Reasonableness Requirement | Fees must be reasonable | https://www.americanbar.org/groups/professional_responsibility/publications/model_rules_of_professional_conduct/rule_1_5_fees/ |
| Communication | Basis or rate of fee communicated before or within reasonable time | Varies |
| Writing | Preferable but not required (except contingent fees) | Varies |

## Types of Fee Arrangements
| Arrangement | Description | Ethical Considerations | URL |
|-------------|-------------|------------------------|-----|
| Hourly Billing | Fee based on time spent | Must be reasonable; avoid overbilling; bill accurately | Varies |
| Flat Fee | Fixed fee for matter | Refund if not earned; may be non-refundable with clear disclosure | Varies |
| Contingent Fee | Percentage of recovery | Must be in writing; prohibited in criminal and certain domestic cases | Varies |
| Retainer (True) | Fee to secure availability | Earned when paid; non-refundable if so agreed | Varies |
| Advance Fee/Deposit | Payment for future services | Unearned until services performed; must be in trust account | Varies |
| Hybrid Fees | Combination of arrangements | Must clearly explain and document | Varies |
| Value Billing | Fee based on value, not time | Permitted if reasonable | Varies |
| Subscription/Flat Monthly | Ongoing flat fee for services | Permitted if reasonable and scope clear | Varies |
