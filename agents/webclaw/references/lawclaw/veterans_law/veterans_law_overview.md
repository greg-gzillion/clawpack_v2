# Veterans Law - Overview

## What is Veterans Law?
Veterans law governs the rights, benefits, and protections afforded to veterans of the U.S. Armed Forces. It encompasses disability compensation, pensions, health care, education benefits, home loan guarantees, burial benefits, and legal protections under laws such as the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA) and the Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act (USERRA).
