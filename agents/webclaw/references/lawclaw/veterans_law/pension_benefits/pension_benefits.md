## Pension vs. Disability Compensation
| Feature | Pension | Disability Compensation | URL |
|---------|---------|------------------------|-----|
| Basis | Financial need | Service-connected disability | https://www.va.gov |
| Service Requirement | Wartime service | Any active duty | https://www.va.gov |
| Income Test | Yes | No | https://www.va.gov |
| Disability Rating | Not required | Required (except presumptives) | https://www.va.gov |
| Higher of Two | Veteran receives higher benefit | Cannot receive both | https://www.va.gov |
