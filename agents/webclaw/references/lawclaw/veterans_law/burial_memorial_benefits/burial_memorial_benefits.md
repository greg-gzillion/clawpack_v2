## Burial Allowances
| Allowance | Amount (2024) | Eligibility | URL |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|-----|
| Service-Connected Death | Up to $2,000 | Death from service-connected disability | https://www.va.gov/burials-memorials/veterans-burial-allowance/ |
| Non-Service-Connected Death | $948 (plot); $948 (burial) | Veteran hospitalized by VA at time of death | https://www.va.gov/burials-memorials/veterans-burial-allowance/ |
| Non-Service-Connected Death | $300 (burial) | Veteran not hospitalized by VA | https://www.va.gov/burials-memorials/veterans-burial-allowance/ |
| Transportation Reimbursement | Varies | Transporting remains to national cemetery | https://www.va.gov/burials-memorials/veterans-burial-allowance/ |
