## AMA Review Lanes
| Lane | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Supplemental Claim | Submit new and relevant evidence | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/supplemental-claim/ |
| Higher-Level Review | De novo review by senior adjudicator; no new evidence | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/higher-level-review/ |
| Board Appeal | Appeal directly to Board of Veterans' Appeals | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/board-appeal/ |

## Supplemental Claim
| Feature | Description | URL |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| New Evidence | Must submit new and relevant evidence | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/supplemental-claim/ |
| Time Limit | Generally 1 year from decision | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/supplemental-claim/ |
| Duty to Assist | VA provides assistance in gathering evidence | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/supplemental-claim/ |
| Effective Date | May preserve earlier effective date | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/supplemental-claim/ |

## Higher-Level Review
| Feature | Description | URL |

| Time Limit | 1 year from decision | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/higher-level-review/ |

## Board Appeal (Board of Veterans' Appeals)
| Type | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Direct Review | Board reviews existing record; no new evidence; no hearing | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/board-appeal/ |
| Evidence Submission | Submit additional evidence within 90 days; no hearing | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/board-appeal/ |
| Hearing | Hearing with Veterans Law Judge; may submit evidence | https://www.va.gov/decision-reviews/board-appeal/ |
