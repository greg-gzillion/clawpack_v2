# Agency Litigation Checklist - APA Challenges

## Pre-Filing Requirements
- [ ] **Final Agency Action**: Is action final under Bennett v. Spear (1997)?
- [ ] **Exhaustion**: Have all agency appeals been exhausted?
- [ ] **Ripeness**: Is claim ripe under Abbott Labs (1967)?
- [ ] **Standing**: Does client have standing under Lujan?
- [ ] **Statute of Limitations**: Within 6 years of injury? (Corner Post v. FRB, 2024)

## APA ｧ 706 Claims Checklist

### ｧ 706(2)(A) - Arbitrary and Capricious
- [ ] Agency failed to consider relevant factors (State Farm)
- [ ] No reasoned explanation for policy change
- [ ] Failed to respond to significant comments
- [ ] Failed to consider obvious alternatives
- [ ] Inadequate cost-benefit analysis
- [ ] Decision not supported by record evidence

### ｧ 706(2)(C) - Exceeded Statutory Authority
- [ ] Post-Loper Bright: Court exercises independent judgment
- [ ] Argue "best reading" of statute, not reasonableness
- [ ] Major Questions Doctrine applies? (West Virginia v. EPA)
- [ ] Agency action exceeds delegated authority
- [ ] Cite agency's organic statute limits

### ｧ 706(2)(D) - Procedural Violations
- [ ] Inadequate notice (APA ｧ 553(b))
- [ ] Insufficient comment period
- [ ] Failure to follow agency regulations
- [ ] Ex parte communication violations
- [ ] FOIA or Sunshine Act violations

### ｧ 706(2)(E) - Substantial Evidence
- [ ] Formal rulemaking/adjudication required
- [ ] Record lacks "relevant evidence a reasonable mind might accept"

### ｧ 706(2)(B) - Constitutional Claims
- [ ] Appointments Clause (Lucia - ALJ appointments)
- [ ] Seventh Amendment (Jarkesy - jury trial for fraud penalties)
- [ ] Due Process (Mathews v. Eldridge)
- [ ] Nondelegation doctrine
- [ ] Removal power (Seila Law, Free Enterprise Fund)

## Administrative Record Checklist
- [ ] Request certified administrative record
- [ ] Review for completeness
- [ ] Identify extra-record evidence exceptions
- [ ] Prepare privilege log challenges
- [ ] Consider motion to complete/supplement record

## Remedy Selection
| Remedy | Standard | Strategic Use |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| **Vacatur** | Default remedy | Complete rule set aside |
| **Remand Without Vacatur** | Allied-Signal factors | Rule stays, agency corrects |
| **Preliminary Injunction** | Irreparable harm, likelihood of success | Pre-enforcement relief |
| **Declaratory Judgment** | Actual controversy | Clarify rights |
| **Mandamus** | Clear duty, no discretion | Compel delayed action |

## Post-Loper Bright Strategy
- [ ] Do not cite Chevron deference (overturned)
- [ ] Argue "best reading" of statute
- [ ] Use Skidmore for agency persuasiveness
- [ ] Raise Major Questions Doctrine for expansive claims
- [ ] Emphasize State Farm for policy reversals

## Key Deadlines
| Action | Deadline | Authority |
|--------|----------|-----------|
