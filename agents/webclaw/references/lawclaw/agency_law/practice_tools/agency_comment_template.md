## Tips for Agency-Specific Comments
- **SEC**: Include economic data and empirical support
- **FTC**: Address consumer protection and competition effects
- **EPA**: Cite scientific studies and technical data
- **FCC**: Discuss public interest and market impacts
- **CFPB**: Focus on consumer harm and market failures
- **All Agencies**: Preserve constitutional/Appointments Clause arguments

## Post-Loper Bright Considerations
- Do not rely on Chevron deference (overturned)
- Argue "best reading" of statute, not "reasonableness"
- Cite Skidmore factors for agency persuasiveness
- Raise Major Questions Doctrine for expansive claims
