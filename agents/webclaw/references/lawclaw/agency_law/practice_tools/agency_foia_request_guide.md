## Agency FOIA Request Template
[DATE]

VIA [EMAIL/CERTIFIED MAIL]

FOIA Officer
[Agency Name]
[Agency Address]

Re: Freedom of Information Act Request - [Agency Name]

Dear FOIA Officer:

Pursuant to the Freedom of Information Act, 5 U.S.C. § 552, I request access to and copies of the following [Agency] records:

[BE SPECIFIC - Include:]

Date ranges (MM/DD/YYYY to MM/DD/YYYY)

File names or types (e.g., emails, reports, guidance documents)

Subject matter (e.g., "[Topic] rulemaking")

Names of individuals involved

Docket numbers, case numbers, or other identifiers

Examples for [Agency]:

All emails between [Official A] and [Official B] regarding [Topic] from [Date] to [Date]

The [Document Name] dated [Date] filed in [Matter Name]

All agency guidance documents concerning [Topic]

Complete administrative record for rulemaking [Docket No.]

Fee Category and Waiver
I request placement in the [appropriate fee category] category.

[If requesting fee waiver]: I request a fee waiver under 5 U.S.C. § 552(a)(4)(A)(iii). Disclosure is in the public interest because [explain]. This information is not sought for commercial purposes.

Agreement to Pay Fees
I agree to pay fees up to $[AMOUNT]. Please notify me if estimated fees exceed this amount.

Contact Information
[Name] | [Address] | [Phone] | [Email]

Sincerely,
[Signature]

## Agency-Specific FOIA Tips
| Agency | Tip |
|--------|-----|
| **DOJ** | OIP provides excellent guidance; use their templates |
| **FBI** | Extremely specific descriptions required; expect redactions |
| **EPA** | Large document productions; narrow requests |
| **SEC** | Enforcement records may be exempt (Exemption 7) |
| **State Department** | Foreign policy exemptions common |
| **DHS** | Immigration records have special procedures |

## FOIA Exemptions Quick Reference
| Exemption | Subject | Common Agency Use |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **1** | Classified info | DOD, CIA, FBI, State |
| **4** | Trade secrets | SEC, FTC, EPA, FDA |
| **5** | Privileged communications | All agencies |
| **6** | Personal privacy | SSA, HHS, VA |
| **7** | Law enforcement | FBI, DEA, ATF, SEC Enforcement |
