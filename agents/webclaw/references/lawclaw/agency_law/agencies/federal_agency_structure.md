## Agency Independence Factors
| Factor | Executive Agencies | Independent Agencies |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Head Removal** | At will (President) | For cause only |
| **Structure** | Single Secretary | Multi-member Commission |
| **Terms** | Serves at pleasure | Fixed terms (5-7 years) |
| **Partisan Balance** | Not required | No more than simple majority from one party |
| **Budget** | OMB review | Direct to Congress (some) |

## Key Cases on Agency Independence
| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Humphrey's Executor v. US** | 295 U.S. 602 (1935) | FTC commissioners protected from at-will removal |
| **Wiener v. US** | 357 U.S. 349 (1958) | War Claims Commission independence |
| **Morrison v. Olson** | 487 U.S. 654 (1988) | Independent counsel constitutional |
| **Free Enterprise Fund v. PCAOB** | 561 U.S. 477 (2010) | Dual for-cause removal unconstitutional |
| **Seila Law v. CFPB** | 140 S. Ct. 2183 (2020) | Single-director independent agency unconstitutional |
| **Collins v. Yellen** | 141 S. Ct. 1761 (2021) | FHFA director removal protection unconstitutional |
