# Agency Rulemaking Authority - Agency Law

## Sources of Rulemaking Authority
1. **Enabling Statute**: Organic act delegating specific rulemaking power
2. **APA § 553**: General framework for rulemaking procedures
3. **Executive Orders**: Presidential directives (EO 12866, 13563, 14094)
4. **Statutory Mandates**: Congress directs agency to issue specific rules

## Delegation of Rulemaking Authority
- **Intelligible Principle Test**: Congress must provide "intelligible principle" to guide agency
- **Key Cases**: 
  - **J.W. Hampton, Jr. & Co. v. US (1928)**: Intelligible principle required
  - **Whitman v. American Trucking (2001)**: Broad delegations upheld
  - **Gundy v. US (2019)**: Nondelegation doctrine debated
  - **West Virginia v. EPA (2022)**: Major Questions Doctrine

## Types of Agency Rules
| Type | APA Section | Notice & Comment | Binding Effect |
|------|-------------|------------------|----------------|
| **Legislative Rules** | § 553 | Required | Binding law |
| **Interpretive Rules** | § 553(b)(A) | Exempt | Persuasive only |
| **Policy Statements** | § 553(b)(A) | Exempt | Not binding |
| **Procedural Rules** | § 553(b)(A) | Exempt | Binding internal procedures |
| **Interim Final Rules** | § 553(b)(B) | Post-promulgation | Binding, emergency |

## Rulemaking Procedure Requirements
| Requirement | Source | Details |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| **Notice** | APA § 553(b) | Federal Register publication |
| **Comment Period** | APA § 553(c) | Minimum 30 days |
| **Statement of Basis and Purpose** | APA § 553(c) | Concise general statement |
| **Logical Outgrowth** | Case law | Final rule within scope of proposal |
| **OIRA Review** | EO 12866 | Significant regulatory actions |
| **RFA Analysis** | 5 U.S.C. §§ 601-612 | Small entity impact |
| **PRA Clearance** | 44 U.S.C. §§ 3501-3521 | Information collections |
| **CRA Submission** | 5 U.S.C. § 801 | Congress and GAO |

## Limits on Rulemaking Authority
| Limit | Source | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Statutory Authority** | Organic act | Cannot exceed delegated power |
| **Major Questions Doctrine** | West Virginia v. EPA | Clear statement required |
| **Arbitrary and Capricious** | APA § 706(2)(A) | Judicial review |
| **Congressional Review Act** | 5 U.S.C. §§ 801-808 | Congressional disapproval |
| **Appropriations Riders** | Spending bills | Defund specific rules |
| **Anti-Lobbying Restrictions** | 18 U.S.C. § 1913 | Grassroots lobbying ban |

## Key Cases on Agency Rulemaking
| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Vermont Yankee v. NRDC** | 435 U.S. 519 (1978) | Courts cannot add procedures |
| **Motor Vehicle Mfrs. v. State Farm** | 463 U.S. 29 (1983) | Rescission requires reasoned explanation |
| **Chevron v. NRDC** | 467 U.S. 837 (1984) | Deference to interpretations (OVERTURNED 2024) |
| **Perez v. Mortgage Bankers** | 575 U.S. 92 (2015) | Interpretive rules exempt from notice |
| **Loper Bright v. Raimondo** | 144 S. Ct. 2244 (2024) | Chevron overruled |
