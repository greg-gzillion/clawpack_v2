# Notice-and-Comment Rulemaking - Agency Law

## APA Ï 553 Requirements
| Requirement | Standard | Agency Variations |
|-------------|----------|-------------------|
| **Notice** | Federal Register publication | Agency-specific portals (SEC, FCC) |
| **Comment Period** | Minimum 30 days | FTC: 60 days typical; EPA: 30-90 days |
| **Response to Comments** | Concise general statement | SEC: detailed economic responses required |
| **Logical Outgrowth** | Final rule within scope of proposal | Strictly enforced by D.C. Circuit |

## Agency-Specific Notice Requirements
| Agency | Additional Notice Requirements | Citation |
|--------|------------------------------|----------|
| **SEC** | Economic analysis must accompany NPRM | 15 U.S.C. Ï 77b(b) |
| **FTC** | Advance notice for Magnuson-Moss rules | 15 U.S.C. Ï 57a(b) |
| **EPA** | Scientific data must be publicly available | 40 CFR Part 22 |
| **FCC** | Must demonstrate public interest benefit | 47 U.S.C. Ï 151 |
| **CFPB** | Small business review panel (SBREFA) | 5 U.S.C. Ï 609 |

| Agency | Standard Period | Extension Policy |
|--------|-----------------|------------------|

| **EPA** | 30-90 days | Varies by rule significance |
| **FCC** | 30-60 days | Limited extensions |
| **CFPB** | 30-60 days | Extensions available |

## Response to Comments - Agency Practices
| Agency | Practice | Key Case |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **SEC** | Detailed economic analysis of comments | Business Roundtable (2011) |
| **FTC** | Statement of Basis and Purpose | 15 U.S.C. Ï 57a(c) |
| **EPA** | Response to significant comments document | 40 CFR Part 22 |
| **FCC** | Report and Order with comment analysis | 47 CFR Part 1 |

## Logical Outgrowth Doctrine
- **Test**: Would interested parties have anticipated final rule from proposal?
- **Agency Variation**: D.C. Circuit strictly enforces against all agencies
- **Key Case**: CSX Transportation v. STB (D.C. Cir. 2009)
- **Remedy**: Agency must re-propose rule or provide supplemental notice

## Exceptions to Notice-and-Comment
| Exception | APA Ï 553(b) | Agency Application |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Interpretive Rules** | Exempt | SEC staff interpretations; FTC guidance |
| **Policy Statements** | Exempt | Agency enforcement priorities |
| **Procedural Rules** | Exempt | Agency internal procedures |
| **Good Cause** | Exempt | Emergency rules (rarely upheld) |

## Good Cause Exception - Agency Use
| Agency | Good Cause Usage | Judicial Scrutiny |
|--------|------------------|-------------------|
| **EPA** | Emergency environmental threats | Strict review |
| **FCC** | Spectrum auctions | Mixed success |
| **FDA** | Public health emergencies | Deferential (COVID) |
| **DHS** | Border security | Strict review |

## Post-Loper Bright Considerations
- Agency interpretations in rulemaking no longer receive Chevron deference
- Courts independently interpret statutes authorizing rulemaking
- Agency must demonstrate statutory authority under "best reading" standard
- Major Questions Doctrine requires clear congressional authorization
