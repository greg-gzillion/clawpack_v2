# Agency Hearing Procedures - Agency Law

## SEC Administrative Proceedings
- **Rules of Practice**: 17 CFR Part 201
- **ALJ Presides**: Initial decision appealable to Commission
- **Enforcement Actions**: Post-Jarkesy (2024) - fraud actions require federal court
- **Cease-and-Desist Proceedings**: Still in SEC ALJs
- **Key Case**: Lucia v. SEC (2018) - ALJs are Officers of the United States

## FTC Administrative Litigation
- **Part 3 Proceedings**: 16 CFR Part 3
- **ALJ Initial Decision**: Appeal to full Commission
- **Consent Orders**: Most common resolution
- **Key Case**: FTC v. Standard Oil (1980) - Interlocutory review limited

## NLRB Unfair Labor Practice Hearings
- **Section 10 Proceedings**: 29 U.S.C. § 160
- **ALJ Presides**: Recommended order to Board
- **General Counsel Prosecutes**: Independent from Board
- **Key Case**: Universal Camera v. NLRB (1951) - Substantial evidence review

## EPA Administrative Hearings
- **Environmental Appeals Board**: 40 CFR Part 22
- **ALJ Initial Decision**: Appeal to EAB
- **Permit Appeals**: PSD, NPDES, RCRA permits
- **Enforcement Proceedings**: Civil penalties

## Agency-Specific Variations
| Agency | Adjudication Body | Appeal Route | Key Distinction |
|--------|-------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| SEC | ALJs | Commission ? D.C. Circuit | Post-Jarkesy: fraud to district court |
| FTC | ALJs | Commission ? Circuit Court | Part 3 administrative litigation |
| NLRB | ALJs | Board ? D.C. Circuit | General Counsel prosecutes |
| EPA | ALJs | EAB ? Circuit Court | Environmental Appeals Board |
| SSA | ALJs | Appeals Council ? District Court | 1,600+ ALJs, disability focus |
| DOL | ALJs | BRB/ARB ? Circuit Court | Benefits Review Board |
| CFPB | ALJs | Director ? Circuit Court | Seila Law removal issue |
