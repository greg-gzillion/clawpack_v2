## Contribution Limits (2023-2024 Cycle)
| Recipient | Individual Limit | PAC Limit |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Candidate Committee | $3,300 per election | $5,000 per election |
| National Party Committee | $41,300 per year | $15,000 per year |
| State/District/Local Party | $10,000 per year | $5,000 per year |
| PAC (SSF/Non-Connected) | $5,000 per year | $5,000 per year |
