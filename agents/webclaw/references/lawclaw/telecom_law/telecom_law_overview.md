# Telecommunications Law - Overview

## What is Telecommunications Law?
Telecommunications law governs the transmission of information by wire, radio, optical cable, electromagnetic systems, and other means. It encompasses federal and state regulation of broadcast, cable, wireline, wireless, satellite, and broadband communications, primarily under the Federal Communications Commission (FCC).
