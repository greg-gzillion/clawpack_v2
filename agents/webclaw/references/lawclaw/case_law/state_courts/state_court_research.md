## Unpublished/Non-Precedential Opinions
Many states restrict citation to unpublished opinions:
| State | Rule |
|-------|------|
| **California** | Unpublished opinions not citable (CRC 8.1115) |
| **Federal Courts** | FRAP 32.1 permits citation; court rules vary |
| **Texas** | Unpublished opinions may be cited as persuasive |

## State Court Structure by Region
### Northeast
- **New York**: Court of Appeals ? Appellate Division ? Supreme Court (trial)
- **Massachusetts**: Supreme Judicial Court ? Appeals Court ? Superior/District Courts

### South
- **Texas**: Supreme Court (civil) / Court of Criminal Appeals ? Courts of Appeals ? District/County Courts
- **Florida**: Supreme Court ? District Courts of Appeal ? Circuit/County Courts

### Midwest
- **Illinois**: Supreme Court ? Appellate Court ? Circuit Courts
- **Ohio**: Supreme Court ? Courts of Appeals ? Courts of Common Pleas

### West
- **California**: Supreme Court ? Courts of Appeal ? Superior Courts
- **Washington**: Supreme Court ? Courts of Appeals ? Superior/District Courts
