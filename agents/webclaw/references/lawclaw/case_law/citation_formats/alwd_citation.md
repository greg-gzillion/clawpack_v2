# ALWD Citation Format - Case Law

## Overview
The ALWD Guide to Legal Citation is an alternative to the Bluebook, designed to be more accessible and consistent. It is widely used in law schools and some courts.

## Official Resources
- **ALWD Guide**: https://www.alwd.org
- **ALWD Companion**: https://www.alwd.org/resources

## Case Citation Format
### General Format
`[Case Name], [Volume] [Reporter] [First Page], [Pinpoint Page] ([Court] [Year])`

*Note: Format is nearly identical to Bluebook but with some differences in abbreviations*

## Key Differences from Bluebook
| Element | Bluebook | ALWD |
|---------|----------|------|
| **Case name italics** | Italicized | Italicized or underlined |
| **Court designation** | (2d Cir. 2020) | (2d Cir. 2020) - same |
| **Pinpoint cite** | 410 U.S. at 115 | 410 U.S. 113, 115 |
| **Parallel cites** | Required in some courts | Optional, discouraged |
| **Unpublished opinions** | F. App'x | WL or LEXIS cite preferred |

## Examples by Court
| Court | ALWD Citation |
|-------|---------------|
| **U.S. Supreme Court** | *Roe v. Wade*, 410 U.S. 113, 153-54 (1973) |
| **Federal Courts of Appeals** | *United States v. Jones*, 565 U.S. 400, 404 (2d Cir. 2012) |
| **Federal District Courts** | *Smith v. Jones*, 123 F. Supp. 2d 456, 460 (S.D.N.Y. 2020) |
| **State Supreme Court** | *Palsgraf v. Long Island R.R. Co.*, 162 N.E. 99, 100 (N.Y. 1928) |

## ALWD-Specific Abbreviations
| Term | Bluebook | ALWD |
|------|----------|------|
| Association | Ass'n | Assn. |
| Company | Co. | Co. |
| Corporation | Corp. | Corp. |
| Incorporated | Inc. | Inc. |
| Limited | Ltd. | Ltd. |
| Number | No. | No. |
| And | & | & |

## Short Form Citations (ALWD Rule 11)
| Full Citation | Short Form |
|---------------|------------|
| *Roe v. Wade*, 410 U.S. 113, 115 (1973) | *Roe*, 410 U.S. at 115 |
| Immediately preceding | *Id.* at 115 |

## Neutral Citations (ALWD Rule 12.15)
When available, neutral citations are preferred:
`*Smith v. Jones*, 2021 UT 12, ¶ 15, 485 P.3d 1234`
