# Bluebook Citation Format - Case Law

## Overview
The Bluebook: A Uniform System of Citation is the standard legal citation guide used in law reviews, court documents, and legal memoranda.

## Official Resources
- **The Bluebook Online**: https://www.legalbluebook.com
- **Bluebook Quick Style Guide**: https://www.legalbluebook.com/bluebook-v21/quick-style-guide
- **Georgetown Bluebook Guide**: https://guides.ll.georgetown.edu/bluebook

## Case Citation Format
### General Format
`[Case Name], [Volume] [Reporter] [First Page], [Pinpoint Page] ([Court] [Year])`

### Examples by Court
| Court | Example Citation |
|-------|-----------------|
| **U.S. Supreme Court** | *Roe v. Wade*, 410 U.S. 113, 153-54 (1973) |
| **Federal Courts of Appeals** | *United States v. Jones*, 565 U.S. 400, 404 (2d Cir. 2012) |
| **Federal District Courts** | *Smith v. Jones*, 123 F. Supp. 2d 456, 460 (S.D.N.Y. 2020) |
| **State Supreme Court** | *Palsgraf v. Long Island R.R. Co.*, 162 N.E. 99, 100 (N.Y. 1928) |
| **State Appellate Court** | *People v. Smith*, 45 Cal. App. 4th 123, 130 (1996) |

## Case Name Rules (Rule 10.2)
| Rule | Example |
|------|---------|
| Only last names | *Roe v. Wade* (not *Jane Roe v. Henry Wade*) |
| Italicize or underline | *Brown v. Board of Education* |
| Abbreviate common terms | Ass'n, Co., Corp., Inc., Ltd. |
| Omit "The" | *New York Times* (not *The New York Times*) |
| Only first party on each side | *Jones v. Smith* (not *Jones & Brown v. Smith & Green*) |

## Pinpoint Citations (Rule 3.2)
| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Single page | 410 U.S. 113, 115 |
| Multiple pages | 410 U.S. 113, 115-17 |
| Footnote | 410 U.S. 113, 115 n.3 |
| Multiple footnotes | 410 U.S. 113, 115 nn.3-5 |
| Section | 410 U.S. 113, § II |

## Short Form Citations (Rule 10.9)
| Full Citation | Short Form |
|---------------|------------|
| *Roe v. Wade*, 410 U.S. 113, 115 (1973) | *Roe*, 410 U.S. at 115 |
| *Brown v. Board of Education*, 347 U.S. 483 (1954) | *Brown*, 347 U.S. at 490 |
| *Id.* (immediately preceding) | *Id.* at 115 |

## Parallel Citations (Rule 10.3.1)
When citing to multiple reporters:
`*Roe v. Wade*, 410 U.S. 113, 153-54, 93 S. Ct. 705, 727, 35 L. Ed. 2d 147, 177 (1973)`

## Unpublished Opinions (Rule 10.8.1)
| Type | Format |
|------|--------|
| Federal (post-2007) | *Smith v. Jones*, 123 F. App'x 456, 458 (9th Cir. 2005) |
| Westlaw/Lexis citation | *Smith v. Jones*, No. 20-1234, 2021 WL 123456, at *3 (2d Cir. Jan. 15, 2021) |
