# Landmark Supreme Court Cases

## Overview
This guide catalogs the most significant Supreme Court decisions across major areas of law, providing essential citations and holdings for legal research.

| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Marbury v. Madison** | 5 U.S. 137 (1803) | Established judicial review |
| **McCulloch v. Maryland** | 17 U.S. 316 (1819) | Federal supremacy; implied powers |
| **Gibbons v. Ogden** | 22 U.S. 1 (1824) | Commerce Clause authority |
| **Youngstown Sheet & Tube v. Sawyer** | 343 U.S. 579 (1952) | Presidential power limits |
| **United States v. Nixon** | 418 U.S. 683 (1974) | Executive privilege not absolute |
