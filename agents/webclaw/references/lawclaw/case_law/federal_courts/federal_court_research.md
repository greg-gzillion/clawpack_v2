## Circuit Splits
When circuits disagree on a legal issue, it creates a "circuit split" that often leads to Supreme Court review.

### Resources for Tracking Circuit Splits
- **SCOTUSblog Circuit Split Tracker**: https://www.scotusblog.com/category/circuit-splits/
- **Bloomberg Law Circuit Splits**: https://news.bloomberglaw.com/circuit-splits
