# Consumer Protection Law - Overview

## What is Consumer Protection Law?
Consumer protection law safeguards consumers against unfair, deceptive, and fraudulent business practices. It encompasses federal and state statutes, regulations, and common law doctrines designed to protect consumers in transactions involving goods, services, and credit.
