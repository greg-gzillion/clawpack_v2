# Tort Law - Intentional Torts

## Battery
Battery is the intentional infliction of harmful or offensive bodily contact.

- **Cornell LII - Battery**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/battery
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 13**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/13
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 18**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/18

## Assault
Assault is the intentional causing of reasonable apprehension of imminent harmful or offensive contact.

- **Cornell LII - Assault**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/assault
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 21**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/21

## False Imprisonment
False imprisonment is the intentional confinement of a person within fixed boundaries.

- **Cornell LII - False Imprisonment**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/false_imprisonment
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 35**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/35
- **Shopkeeper's Privilege**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/shopkeepers_privilege

## Trespass to Land
Trespass to land is the intentional entry onto land in possession of another.

- **Cornell LII - Trespass to Land**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/trespass_to_land
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 158**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/158

## Trespass to Chattels
Trespass to chattels is the intentional interference with personal property of another.

- **Cornell LII - Trespass to Chattels**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/trespass_to_chattels
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 217**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/217

## Conversion
Conversion is the substantial interference with personal property justifying forced sale.

- **Cornell LII - Conversion**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/conversion
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 222A**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/222A

## Intentional Infliction of Emotional Distress (IIED)
IIED is extreme and outrageous conduct intentionally or recklessly causing severe emotional distress.

- **Cornell LII - IIED**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/intentional_infliction_of_emotional_distress
- **Restatement (Second) of Torts ｧ 46**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/torts/46
