# Remedies - Equitable Relief

## Specific Performance
Specific performance compels a party to perform their contractual obligations when legal remedies are inadequate.

- **Cornell LII - Specific Performance**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/specific_performance
- **UCC Ï 2-716**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-716
- **Restatement (Second) of Contracts Ï 357**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/357
- **Inadequacy of Legal Remedy**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/inadequate_remedy_at_law

## Injunction
An injunction is a court order requiring a party to do or refrain from doing specific acts.

- **Cornell LII - Injunction**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/injunction
- **FRCP Rule 65 (Injunctions)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/rules/frcp/rule_65

### Types of Injunctions
| Type | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Preliminary Injunction | Temporary relief pending trial | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/preliminary_injunction |
| Temporary Restraining Order (TRO) | Emergency relief | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/temporary_restraining_order |
| Permanent Injunction | Final relief after trial | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/permanent_injunction |

## Declaratory Judgment
A declaratory judgment declares the rights of parties without ordering specific performance or awarding damages.

- **Cornell LII - Declaratory Judgment**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/declaratory_judgment
- **Declaratory Judgment Act (28 U.S.C. Ï 2201)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/2201
- **FRCP Rule 57**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/rules/frcp/rule_57

## Rescission
Rescission cancels a contract and returns parties to their pre-contract positions.

- **Cornell LII - Rescission**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/rescission
- **Restatement (Second) of Contracts Ï 376**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/376

## Reformation
Reformation corrects a written instrument to reflect the parties' true agreement.

- **Cornell LII - Reformation**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/reformation
- **Restatement (Second) of Contracts Ï 155**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/155
