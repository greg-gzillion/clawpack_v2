# Civil Procedure - Preclusion Doctrines

## Claim Preclusion (Res Judicata)
Claim preclusion bars relitigation of claims that were or could have been raised in a prior action.

- **Cornell LII - Res Judicata**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/res_judicata
- **Claim Preclusion Requirements**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/claim_preclusion
- **Restatement (Second) of Judgments § 18**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/judgments/18

## Issue Preclusion (Collateral Estoppel)
Issue preclusion bars relitigation of issues actually litigated and determined in a prior action.

- **Cornell LII - Collateral Estoppel**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/collateral_estoppel
- **Restatement (Second) of Judgments § 27**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/judgments/27
- **Non-Mutual Collateral Estoppel**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/nonmutual_collateral_estoppel
