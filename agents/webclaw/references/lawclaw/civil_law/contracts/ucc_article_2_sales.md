# UCC Article 2 - Sale of Goods

## Scope of Article 2
UCC Article 2 applies to transactions in goods - all things movable at the time of identification to the contract.

- **UCC ž 2-102 (Scope)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-102
- **UCC ž 2-105 (Definition of Goods)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-105
- **Cornell LII - UCC Overview**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/uniform_commercial_code

## Merchant Definition and Standards
Merchants are held to higher standards under Article 2.

- **UCC ž 2-104 (Merchant Definition)**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-104
- **Cornell LII - Merchant**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/merchant

## Statute of Frauds (UCC)
Contracts for sale of goods priced at $500 or more must be in writing.

- **UCC ž 2-201**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-201
- **Merchant's Confirmation Exception**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-201#2

## Firm Offers
A merchant's signed written offer to buy or sell goods is irrevocable for the stated time.

- **UCC ž 2-205**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-205

## Battle of the Forms
Acceptance with additional or different terms may still form a contract.

- **UCC ž 2-207**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-207
- **Cornell LII - Battle of the Forms**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/battle_of_the_forms

## Perfect Tender Rule
Buyer may reject goods that fail in any respect to conform.

- **UCC ž 2-601**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-601

## Cure
Seller may cure a non-conforming tender within the time for performance.

- **UCC ž 2-508**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-508
