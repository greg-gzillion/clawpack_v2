# Contract Defenses - Formation and Enforcement

## Mistake
A mistake is a belief not in accord with the facts. Mutual mistake may make a contract voidable.

- **Cornell LII - Mistake**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/mistake
- **Restatement (Second) § 151-154**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/151
- **Mutual Mistake Overview**: https://www.justia.com/contracts/mutual-mistake/

## Misrepresentation
A false statement of fact that induces another to enter into a contract.

- **Cornell LII - Misrepresentation**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/misrepresentation
- **Restatement (Second) § 159-164**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/159
- **Fraudulent Misrepresentation**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/fraudulent_misrepresentation

## Duress
Duress is a threat of harm made to compel a person to do something against their will.

- **Cornell LII - Duress**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/duress
- **Restatement (Second) § 174-176**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/174
- **Economic Duress**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/economic_duress

## Undue Influence
Undue influence is excessive pressure on a party to enter into an agreement.

- **Cornell LII - Undue Influence**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/undue_influence
- **Restatement (Second) § 177**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/177

## Unconscionability
Unconscionability is an absence of meaningful choice together with contract terms unreasonably favorable to one party.

- **Cornell LII - Unconscionability**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/unconscionability
- **UCC § 2-302**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-302
- **Restatement (Second) § 208**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/208

## Statute of Frauds
Certain contracts must be in writing to be enforceable.

- **Cornell LII - Statute of Frauds**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/statute_of_frauds
- **UCC § 2-201**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-201
- **Restatement (Second) § 110**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/110
