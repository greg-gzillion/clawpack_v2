# Contract Formation - Offer, Acceptance, Consideration

## Offer
An offer is a manifestation of willingness to enter into a bargain, made so that another person understands assent is invited and will conclude the bargain.

- **Cornell LII - Offer**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/offer
- **Restatement (Second) § 24**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/24
- **FindLaw - Contract Offer**: https://www.findlaw.com/smallbusiness/business-contracts-and-forms/what-is-an-offer-in-contract-law.html

## Acceptance
Acceptance is a voluntary act by the offeree showing assent to the terms of the offer.

- **Cornell LII - Acceptance**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/acceptance
- **Restatement (Second) § 50**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/50
- **Mailbox Rule Explanation**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/mailbox_rule

## Consideration
Consideration is a bargained-for exchange of value. It may be a benefit to the promisor or a detriment to the promisee.

- **Cornell LII - Consideration**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/consideration
- **Restatement (Second) § 71**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/71
- **Consideration Overview**: https://www.justia.com/contracts/consideration/

## Mutual Assent
Mutual assent is the meeting of the minds between parties regarding the essential terms of a contract.

- **Cornell LII - Mutual Assent**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/mutual_assent
- **Restatement (Second) § 17**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/17
- **Meeting of the Minds**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/meeting_of_the_minds

## Promissory Estoppel
Promissory estoppel allows a promise to be enforced even without consideration when reliance is reasonable and foreseeable.

- **Cornell LII - Promissory Estoppel**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/promissory_estoppel
- **Restatement (Second) § 90**: https://www.law.cornell.edu/restatement/second/contracts/90
- **Nolo - Promissory Estoppel**: https://www.nolo.com/legal-encyclopedia/promissory-estoppel.html
