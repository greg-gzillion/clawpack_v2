# Arbitration Procedure - ADR

## Overview
Arbitration is a private adjudication process where parties present their case to a neutral arbitrator who renders a binding decision.

## The Arbitration Process
1. **Arbitration Agreement**: Contractual provision or separate agreement
2. **Demand for Arbitration**: Claimant files with arbitral institution
3. **Arbitrator Selection**: Parties select from panel or by agreement
4. **Preliminary Hearing**: Schedule, discovery, procedures established
5. **Discovery**: Limited compared to litigation
6. **Hearing**: Presentation of evidence and arguments
7. **Award**: Arbitrator issues written decision (usually 30 days)
8. **Confirmation**: Court confirms award as judgment

## Institutional Rules Comparison
| Feature | AAA Commercial | JAMS Comprehensive | FINRA |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|-------|
| **Filing Fee** | Varies by claim | $2,000+ | Varies |
| **Arbitrator Selection** | Strike-and-rank | Party agreement | FINRA list |
| **Discovery** | Limited | Moderately broad | Limited |
| **Hearing Location** | Convenient location | AAA/JAMS office | FINRA hearing location |
| **Award Timeline** | 30 days after close | 30 days | 30 business days |

## Discovery in Arbitration
- Generally more limited than litigation
- Arbitrator has discretion under AAA Rule 22, JAMS Rule 17
- E-discovery increasingly common but controlled
- Depositions limited (AAA: usually 1-2 per side)

## The Arbitration Hearing
- Less formal than court trial
- Rules of evidence relaxed
- Arbitrator controls procedure
- Witnesses testify under oath
- Exhibits admitted by agreement or ruling

## The Award
- Written decision required (AAA Rule 47, JAMS Rule 24)
- Findings of fact and conclusions of law unless parties waive
- No precedential value (no stare decisis)
- Limited grounds for appeal/vacatur

## Post-Award Procedures
| Action | Grounds | Deadline |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **Confirmation** | Party seeks judgment | 1 year |
| **Vacatur** | FAA § 10 grounds | 3 months |
| **Modification** | FAA § 11 grounds | 3 months |
| **Appeal** | Limited grounds | Varies |
