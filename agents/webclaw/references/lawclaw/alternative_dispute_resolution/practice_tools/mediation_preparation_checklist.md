# Mediation Preparation Checklist

## Pre-Mediation (Weeks 1-4 Before)
- [ ] **Select Mediator**: Review profiles, check conflicts, confirm availability
- [ ] **Execute Mediation Agreement**: Confidentiality and ground rules
- [ ] **Prepare Mediation Statement**: Confidential submission to mediator
- [ ] **Gather Key Documents**: Contract, correspondence, evidence of damages
- [ ] **Identify Decision-Makers**: Ensure client representatives with authority attend
- [ ] **Analyze BATNA/WATNA**: Best/Worst Alternative to Negotiated Agreement
- [ ] **Prepare Opening Statement**: Concise, persuasive, non-inflammatory
- [ ] **Consider Settlement Authority**: Minimum/maximum, non-monetary terms

## Mediation Day
- [ ] **Opening Session**: Present case, listen to other side
- [ ] **Private Caucus**: Confidential discussions with mediator
- [ ] **Evaluate Information**: Adjust positions based on new information
- [ ] **Consider Mediator's Proposals**: Reality testing of positions
- [ ] **Document Any Agreement**: Written and signed before leaving
- [ ] **If No Settlement**: Schedule follow-up, agree on next steps

## Post-Mediation
- [ ] **Follow-Up with Mediator**: Continued negotiations if beneficial
- [ ] **Draft Formal Settlement Agreement**: Within 7-14 days
- [ ] **File Notice of Settlement**: If court case pending
- [ ] **Implement Settlement Terms**: Payment, performance, dismissal
