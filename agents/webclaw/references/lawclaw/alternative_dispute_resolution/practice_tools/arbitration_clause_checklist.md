# Arbitration Clause Drafting Checklist

## Essential Elements
- [ ] **Scope**: What disputes are covered? ("any dispute arising out of or relating to")
- [ ] **Arbitral Institution**: AAA, JAMS, FINRA, or ad hoc
- [ ] **Rules**: Reference specific institutional rules (e.g., AAA Commercial Rules)
- [ ] **Number of Arbitrators**: One or three
- [ ] **Arbitrator Qualifications**: Industry expertise, legal background
- [ ] **Location/Venue**: Where hearings will occur
- [ ] **Governing Law**: State or federal substantive law
- [ ] **Discovery Limitations**: Scope and timing
- [ ] **Award Form**: Reasoned award or bare award
- [ ] **Confidentiality**: Explicit confidentiality provisions
- [ ] **Class Action Waiver**: Individual arbitration only
- [ ] **Delegation Clause**: Arbitrator decides arbitrability

## Sample AAA Commercial Clause
Any controversy or claim arising out of or relating to this contract, or the breach thereof, shall be settled by arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association under its Commercial Arbitration Rules, and judgment on the award rendered by the arbitrator(s) may be entered in any court having jurisdiction thereof.

## Sample JAMS Clause
Any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to this Agreement or the breach, termination, enforcement, interpretation, or validity thereof, including the determination of the scope or applicability of this agreement to arbitrate, shall be determined by arbitration in [City, State] before [one/three] arbitrator(s). The arbitration shall be administered by JAMS pursuant to its Comprehensive Arbitration Rules and Procedures. Judgment on the Award may be entered in any court having jurisdiction.

## Sample Mediation Clause
The parties agree to submit any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement to mediation before initiating arbitration or litigation. The mediation shall be conducted by a mutually agreeable mediator. If the dispute is not resolved within 60 days of the written request for mediation, either party may pursue arbitration or litigation as provided herein.

## Sample Multi-Tier Clause (Mediation then Arbitration)
The parties shall first attempt to resolve any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement through good-faith negotiations. If the dispute is not resolved within 30 days, the parties shall submit the dispute to mediation administered by [AAA/JAMS]. If the dispute is not resolved within 60 days of the mediation request, the dispute shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered by [AAA/JAMS] pursuant to its [Commercial] Arbitration Rules.

## Red Flags to Avoid
- ?? **Unconscionable terms**: Excessive costs, biased selection procedures
- ?? **Unavailable forum**: Specified institution no longer exists
- ?? **Inconsistent provisions**: Contradictions with other contract clauses
- ?? **Unclear delegation**: Ambiguity on who decides arbitrability
- ?? **Inadequate notice**: Hidden or inconspicuous arbitration clause
