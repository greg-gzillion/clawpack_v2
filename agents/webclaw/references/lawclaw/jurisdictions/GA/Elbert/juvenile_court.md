# Elbert County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Elbert
- **Court Name**: Elbert County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (706) 213-2610
- **Address**: 40 Davis Street, Elberton, GA 30635
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
