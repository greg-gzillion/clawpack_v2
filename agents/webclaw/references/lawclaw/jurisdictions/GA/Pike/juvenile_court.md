# Pike County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Pike
- **Court Name**: Pike County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (770) 567-8700
- **Address**: 16021 Barnesville Street, Zebulon, GA 30295
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
