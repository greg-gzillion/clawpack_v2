# Chickasaw County Chancery Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Chickasaw
- **Court Name**: Chickasaw County Chancery Court
- **Phone**: (662) 456-2511
- **Address**: 1 Courthouse Square, Houston, MS 38851
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Domestic relations (divorce, custody, support)
- Probate and estates
- Guardianships and conservatorships
- Land title disputes
- Contract disputes
- Injunctions and equity matters
