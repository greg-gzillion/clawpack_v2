# Supreme Court of Puerto Rico

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of Puerto Rico (Tribunal Supremo de Puerto Rico)
- **Website**: https://www.poderjudicial.pr/ts/
- **Phone**: (787) 723-6033
- **Fax**: (787) 725-1985
- **Address**: 250 Fortaleza Street, San Juan, PR 00901
- **Chief Justice**: Maite Oronoz Rodríguez
- **Number of Justices**: 9
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM AST

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction for Commonwealth matters
- Constitutional questions under Puerto Rico Constitution
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys

## Language
- Proceedings conducted in Spanish
- Opinions published in Spanish (some with English translations)
