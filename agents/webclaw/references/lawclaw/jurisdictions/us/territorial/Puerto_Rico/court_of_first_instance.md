# Puerto Rico Court of First Instance (Tribunal de Primera Instancia)

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Puerto Rico Court of First Instance
- **Website**: https://www.poderjudicial.pr/tpi/
- **Phone**: (787) 641-6600
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM AST

## Divisions
- **Superior Division**: Felonies, civil over $10,000, equity
- **Municipal Division**: Misdemeanors, small claims (up to $10,000)
- **Juvenile Division**: Delinquency, child protection
- **Family Division**: Divorce, custody, support
