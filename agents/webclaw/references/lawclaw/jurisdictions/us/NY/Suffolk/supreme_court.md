# Suffolk County Supreme Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Suffolk
- **County Seat**: Riverhead
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of the State of New York, Suffolk County
- **Website**: https://www.nycourts.gov/supreme
- **Phone**: (631) 852-2000
- **Address**: 1 Court Street, Riverhead, NY 11901
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Felonies
- Civil cases (unlimited)
- Divorce and matrimonial
- Unlimited jurisdiction
- NOTE: In NY, Supreme Court is a trial-level court, NOT the highest court
