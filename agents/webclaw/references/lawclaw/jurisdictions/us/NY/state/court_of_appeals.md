# New York Court of Appeals

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: New York Court of Appeals
- **Website**: https://www.nycourts.gov/ctapps
- **Phone**: (518) 455-7700
- **Fax**: (518) 455-7828
- **Address**: 20 Eagle Street, Albany, NY 12207
- **Clerk Email**: clerk@nycourts.gov
- **Chief Judge**: Rowan D. Wilson
- **Number of Judges**: 7
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction (highest court in NY)
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Appeals from Appellate Division

## Note: In New York, the "Court of Appeals" is the highest court, not the Supreme Court
