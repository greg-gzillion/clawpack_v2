# Anne Arundel County Orphans' Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Anne Arundel
- **Court Name**: Orphans' Court for Anne Arundel
- **Phone**: (410) 222-1400
- **Address**: 8 Church Circle, Annapolis, MD 21401
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Wills and Estates
- Trusts
- Guardianships
- Conservatorships
- Inheritance disputes

## Note: Called "Orphans' Court" - unique to Maryland
- Judges are elected (not required to be lawyers)
- Also known as Probate Court
