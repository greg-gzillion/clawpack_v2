# Newport County Probate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Newport
- **Court Name**: Newport County Probate Courts
- **Phone**: (401) 846-4200
- **Address**: 45 Washington Square, Newport, RI 02840
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

- Wills and Estates
- Trusts
- Guardianships
