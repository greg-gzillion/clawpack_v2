# 3rd Judicial District — Local Law Resources

## District Overview
The 3rd Judicial District covers Southcentral Alaska including Anchorage, Matanuska-Susitna Valley, Kenai Peninsula, Kodiak Island, Bristol Bay, Dillingham, the Aleutians, and Pribilof Islands.
