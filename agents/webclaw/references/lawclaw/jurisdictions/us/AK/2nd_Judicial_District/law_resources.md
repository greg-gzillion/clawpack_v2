# 2nd Judicial District — Local Law Resources

## District Overview
The 2nd Judicial District covers Northern and Northwest Alaska: Nome, Kotzebue, Utqiagvik (Barrow), and surrounding communities including the North Slope, Northwest Arctic, Nome Census Area, and Bering Strait region.
