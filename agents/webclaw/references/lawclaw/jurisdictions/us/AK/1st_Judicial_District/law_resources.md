# 1st Judicial District — Local Law Resources

## District Overview
The 1st Judicial District covers Southeast Alaska: Juneau, Ketchikan, Sitka, Petersburg, Wrangell, Skagway, Haines, Hoonah, Angoon, Yakutat, Prince of Wales, and surrounding communities.
