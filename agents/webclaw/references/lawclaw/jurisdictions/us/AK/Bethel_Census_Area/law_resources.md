## Fish & Game
- ADF&G Bethel Office — (907) 543-2433 — https://www.adfg.alaska.gov/
- Yukon-Kuskokwim Delta — major subsistence region
- Salmon: king, chum, coho — subsistence and commercial (Kuskokwim and Yukon rivers)
- Moose, caribou (Mulchatna, Western Arctic herds) — subsistence hunting
- Waterfowl, seabird egg gathering — spring/summer subsistence
- Marine mammal subsistence — seal, beluga (coastal villages)
- Herring, sheefish, whitefish, pike, blackfish
- Yukon Delta National Wildlife Refuge — massive USFWS refuge — federal enforcement
