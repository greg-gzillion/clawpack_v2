## Fish & Game
- ADF&G — served by King Salmon Office — (907) 246-3340

- Bristol Bay sockeye run passes through borough waters
- Katmai National Park — NPS — https://www.nps.gov/katm/
- Lake Clark National Park — NPS — https://www.nps.gov/lacl/
- Aniakchak National Monument — NPS
- Brown bear, moose, caribou hunting — abundant
- Commercial fishing — permit holders in Chignik, other coastal communities
