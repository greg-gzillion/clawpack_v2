# 4th Judicial District — Local Law Resources

## District Overview
The 4th Judicial District covers Interior Alaska including Fairbanks, North Pole, Delta Junction, Tok, Fort Yukon, Galena, and rural communities along the Yukon, Tanana, and Kuskokwim rivers.
