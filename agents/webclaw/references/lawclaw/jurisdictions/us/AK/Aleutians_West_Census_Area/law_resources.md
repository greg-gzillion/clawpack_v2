- Commercial fisheries: pollock (largest in world), cod, halibut, crab (Bering Sea king and snow crab), black cod, rockfish
- NOAA Office of Law Enforcement — Unalaska — https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/
- North Pacific Fishery Management Council — https://www.npfmc.org/

- Pribilof Islands — fur seal management — NOAA enforcement
- Sea otter, Steller sea lion, walrus, seal — marine mammal enforcement
