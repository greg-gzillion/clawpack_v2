# Jefferson County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Jefferson
- **Court Name**: Jefferson County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (360) 385-9125
- **Address**: 1820 Jefferson Street, Port Townsend, WA 98368
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 8-17)
- At-risk youth (ARY)
- Child in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Dependency (abuse/neglect)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
