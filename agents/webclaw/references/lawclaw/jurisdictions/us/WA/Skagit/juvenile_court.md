# Skagit County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Skagit
- **Court Name**: Skagit County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (360) 416-1700
- **Address**: 205 W Kincaid Street, Mount Vernon, WA 98273
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 8-17)
- At-risk youth (ARY)
- Child in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Dependency (abuse/neglect)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
