## Court Information
- **County**: Island

- **Phone**: (360) 679-7360

## Jurisdiction

- At-risk youth (ARY)
- Child in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Dependency (abuse/neglect)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
