# Chittenden County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Chittenden
- **Court Name**: Juvenile Court of Chittenden County (within Family Division)
- **Phone**: (802) 863-3460
- **Address**: 175 Church Street, Burlington, VT 05401
