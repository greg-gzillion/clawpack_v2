# Richmond County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Richmond
- **Court Name**: Richmond County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (706) 821-2900
- **Address**: 735 James Brown Boulevard, Augusta, GA 30901
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)

- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
