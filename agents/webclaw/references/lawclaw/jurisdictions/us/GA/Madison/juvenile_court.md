# Madison County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Madison
- **Court Name**: Madison County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (706) 795-6323
- **Address**: 91 Albany Avenue, Danielsville, GA 30633
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
