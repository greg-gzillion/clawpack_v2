# Butts County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Butts
- **Court Name**: Butts County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (770) 775-8200
- **Address**: 25 3rd Street, Jackson, GA 30233
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
