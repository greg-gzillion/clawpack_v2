# Towns County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Towns
- **Court Name**: Towns County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (706) 896-2110
- **Address**: 48 Courthouse Square, Hiawassee, GA 30546
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
