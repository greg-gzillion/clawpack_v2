# Wayne County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Wayne
- **Court Name**: Wayne County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (912) 427-5900
- **Address**: 174 N Brunswick Street, Jesup, GA 31545
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
