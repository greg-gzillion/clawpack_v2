# Chattooga County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Chattooga
- **Court Name**: Chattooga County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (706) 857-0711
- **Address**: 10004 Commerce Street, Summerville, GA 30747
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
