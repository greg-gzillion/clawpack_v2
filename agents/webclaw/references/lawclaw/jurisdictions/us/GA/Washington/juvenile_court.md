# Washington County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Washington
- **Court Name**: Washington County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (478) 552-2311
- **Address**: 119 S Harris Street, Sandersville, GA 31082
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)

- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
