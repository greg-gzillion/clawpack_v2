# Murray County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Murray
- **Court Name**: Murray County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (706) 695-2932
- **Address**: 121 N 3rd Avenue, Chatsworth, GA 30705
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
