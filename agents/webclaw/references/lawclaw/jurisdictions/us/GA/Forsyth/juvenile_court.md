# Forsyth County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Forsyth
- **Court Name**: Forsyth County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (770) 781-2100
- **Address**: 100 W Courthouse Square, Cumming, GA 30040
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
