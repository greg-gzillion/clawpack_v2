# Seminole County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Seminole
- **Court Name**: Seminole County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (229) 524-2411

- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)

- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
