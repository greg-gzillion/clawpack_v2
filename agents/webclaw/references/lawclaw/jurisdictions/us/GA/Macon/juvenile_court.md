# Macon County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Macon
- **Court Name**: Macon County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (478) 472-7661
- **Address**: 121 S Sumter Street, Oglethorpe, GA 31068
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
