# Houston County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Houston
- **Court Name**: Houston County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (478) 218-4800
- **Address**: 201 N Perry Parkway, Perry, GA 31069
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
