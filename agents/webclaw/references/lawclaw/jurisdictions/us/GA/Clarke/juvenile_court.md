# Clarke County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Clarke
- **Court Name**: Clarke County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (706) 613-3340
- **Address**: 325 E Washington Street, Athens, GA 30601
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
