# Gwinnett County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Gwinnett
- **Court Name**: Gwinnett County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (770) 822-8000
- **Address**: 75 Langley Drive, Lawrenceville, GA 30046
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
