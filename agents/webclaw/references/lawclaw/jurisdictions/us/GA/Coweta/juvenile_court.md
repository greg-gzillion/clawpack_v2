# Coweta County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Coweta
- **Court Name**: Coweta County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (770) 254-2690
- **Address**: 200 Court Square, Newnan, GA 30263
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
