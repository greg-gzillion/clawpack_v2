# Walton County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Walton
- **Court Name**: Walton County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (770) 267-1306
- **Address**: 303 S Hammond Drive, Monroe, GA 30655
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
