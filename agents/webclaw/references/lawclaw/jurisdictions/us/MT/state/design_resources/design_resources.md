# Montana Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- Montana Arts Council — https://www.art.mt.gov/
- AIA Montana — https://www.aia-mt.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Extreme climate design (-40F to 100F)
- Heavy mountain snow loads (60-100 psf in some areas)
- Western and rustic design aesthetic
- Strong craft and maker tradition
- Fire-resistant design in wildland-urban interface
