# Wayne County Magistrate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Wayne
- **Court Name**: Wayne County Magistrate Court
- **Phone**: (304) 272-6360
- **Address**: 700 Hendricks St, Wayne, WV 25570
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM ET
- **Website**: http://www.courtswv.gov

## Jurisdiction
- Misdemeanors
- Traffic violations
- Small claims (up to $10,000)
- Civil cases (up to $10,000)
- Preliminary hearings
- Issue warrants and emergency orders
