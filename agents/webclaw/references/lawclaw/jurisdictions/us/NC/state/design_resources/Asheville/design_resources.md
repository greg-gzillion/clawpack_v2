## Notes
Strong craft and maker community. Biltmore is America's largest home and a Gilded Age masterpiece. Black Mountain College legacy (1933-1957) was one of the most influential experimental art schools in US history — faculty included Josef and Anni Albers, Buckminster Fuller, John Cage.
