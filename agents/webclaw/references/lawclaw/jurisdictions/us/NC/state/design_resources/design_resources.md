# North Carolina Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Raleigh — https://raleigh.aiga.org/
- AIGA Charlotte — https://charlotte.aiga.org/
- North Carolina Arts Council — https://www.ncarts.org/
- AIA North Carolina — https://www.aianc.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Coastal hurricane zones (130-140 mph wind)
- Mountain snow loads in western NC
- Strong furniture and textile design heritage
- Research Triangle drives innovation design
