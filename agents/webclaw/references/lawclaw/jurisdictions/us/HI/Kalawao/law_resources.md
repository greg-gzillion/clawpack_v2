# Kalawao County — Local Law Resources
## Note
- Kalawao County is the smallest county in the U.S., located on the Kalaupapa Peninsula on Molokai
- Administered by the Hawaii Department of Health as Kalaupapa National Historical Park
- No county government — law enforcement by Maui County and State of Hawaii
## Law Enforcement
- Maui Police (serves Kalawao) — https://www.mauipolice.com/
- National Park Service Rangers — https://www.nps.gov/kala/
## Courts
- Served by Maui Circuit Court — https://www.courts.state.hi.us/courts/circuit/
## County Attorney / Defender
- Hawaii Public Defender — https://dps.hawaii.gov/administration/public-defender/
## Hunting Regulations
- HI Hunting Regulations — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/recreation/hunting/
- Kalaupapa National Historical Park — no hunting — https://www.nps.gov/kala/
## Fishing Regulations
- HI Fishing Regulations — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dar/fishing/fishing-regulations/
- Molokai shoreline fishing — limited to park boundaries
## Legal Aid
- Legal Aid Society of Hawaii — https://www.legalaidhawaii.org/
## Corrections
- Nearest: Maui Community Correctional Center — https://dps.hawaii.gov/mccc/
