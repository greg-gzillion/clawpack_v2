## Hunting Regulations
- HI Hunting Regulations — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/recreation/hunting/
- Big Island public hunting areas — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/recreation/hunting/hawaii/
- Feral pig, feral goat, feral sheep, mouflon sheep — year-round in many areas
- Axis deer (introduced) — expanding population
- Game birds: ring-necked pheasant, kalij pheasant, francolin, Japanese quail, wild turkey, spotted dove, barred dove — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/recreation/hunting/hawaii/hawaii-game-bird-hunting/
- Mauna Kea Forest Reserve — pig, sheep, goat, mouflon — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/forestry/
- Puu Anahulu, Puu Waawaa, Mauna Loa — hunting units
- Hakalau Forest NWR — limited hunting — https://www.fws.gov/refuge/hakalau-forest/
- Hawaii Volcanoes National Park — no hunting — https://www.nps.gov/havo/
## Inland/Freshwater Fishing Regulations
- HI Freshwater Fishing — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dar/fishing/freshwater-fishing/
- Wailoa River, Wailuku River — bass, tilapia, catfish
- Lake Waiau (Mauna Kea) — very small alpine lake, no fishing
- Kona reservoirs — bass, tilapia
## Saltwater/Ocean Fishing Regulations
- HI Saltwater Fishing Regulations — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dar/fishing/fishing-regulations/
- Shoreline fishing: ulua, papio, oio, kaku
- Kona — world-class blue marlin, striped marlin, spearfish, mahi, ahi, ono — https://konasportfishing.com/
- Hilo Bay — shoreline net fishing, throw net
- Spearfishing — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dar/fishing/spearfishing/
- Bottomfish: opakapaka, onaga, uku, hapuupuu
- West Hawaii Regional Fishery Management Area — aquarium fish restrictions — https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dar/fishing/west-hawaii/
