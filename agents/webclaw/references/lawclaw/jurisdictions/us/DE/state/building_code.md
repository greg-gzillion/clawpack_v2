## Notes
- Delaware has only 3 counties: New Castle, Kent, Sussex
- No county-level building departments; permits at municipal level
- State Fire Marshal performs plan review in areas without local AHJ
- Coastal areas in Sussex County require wind-borne debris protection
- Flood zones prevalent in all counties (Delaware Bay, Atlantic Ocean)
