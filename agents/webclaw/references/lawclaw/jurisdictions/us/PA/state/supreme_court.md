- **Number of Justices**: 7
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Administrative supervision over all Pennsylvania courts
- Oldest appellate court in the United States (established 1684)
