# South Dakota Supreme Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: South Dakota Supreme Court
- **Website**: https://ujs.sd.gov/Supreme_Court
- **Phone**: (605) 773-3791
- **Fax**: (605) 773-6137
- **Address**: 500 E Capitol Avenue, Pierre, SD 57501
- **Clerk Email**: supremecourtclerk@ujs.state.sd.us
- **Chief Justice**: Steven R. Jensen
- **Number of Justices**: 5
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT/MT

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Administrative supervision over all South Dakota courts
- Only appellate court in SD (no intermediate appeals court)
