# Washington DC Design Resources

## Overview
The nation's capital is a design powerhouse. Federal architecture, monuments, memorials, and museums set a global standard for civic design. Strong graphic design, UX, and policy communication design communities. World-class museums drive exhibition and interpretive design.

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA DC — https://dc.aiga.org/
- National Building Museum — https://www.nbm.org/
- DC Commission on the Arts and Humanities — https://dcarts.dc.gov/
- American Institute of Architects DC — https://www.aiadc.com/

## Key Design Considerations
- Height Act limits buildings to 130 ft (commercial) and 90 ft (residential)
- Federal design standards (GSA, NPS, Smithsonian)
- Historic preservation (L'Enfant Plan, landmarks)
- Monumental civic design tradition
- Security design post 9/11
