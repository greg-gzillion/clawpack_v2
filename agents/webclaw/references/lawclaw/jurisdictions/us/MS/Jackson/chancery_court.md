# Jackson County Chancery Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Jackson
- **Court Name**: Jackson County Chancery Court
- **Phone**: (228) 769-3000
- **Address**: 3109 Magnolia Street, Pascagoula, MS 39567
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Domestic relations (divorce, custody, support)
- Probate and estates
- Guardianships and conservatorships
- Land title disputes
- Contract disputes
- Injunctions and equity matters
