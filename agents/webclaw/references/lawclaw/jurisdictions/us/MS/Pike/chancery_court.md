# Pike County Chancery Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Pike
- **Court Name**: Pike County Chancery Court
- **Phone**: (601) 783-2273
- **Address**: 200 E Bay Street, Magnolia, MS 39652
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Domestic relations (divorce, custody, support)
- Probate and estates
- Guardianships and conservatorships
- Land title disputes
- Contract disputes
- Injunctions and equity matters
