# Pontotoc County Chancery Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Pontotoc
- **Court Name**: Pontotoc County Chancery Court
- **Phone**: (662) 489-3900
- **Address**: 15 W Washington Street, Pontotoc, MS 38863
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Domestic relations (divorce, custody, support)
- Probate and estates
- Guardianships and conservatorships
- Land title disputes
- Contract disputes
- Injunctions and equity matters
