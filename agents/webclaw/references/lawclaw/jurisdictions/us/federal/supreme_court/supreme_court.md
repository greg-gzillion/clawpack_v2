# Supreme Court of the United States (SCOTUS)

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of the United States
- **Website**: https://www.supremecourt.gov
- **Phone**: (202) 479-3000
- **Fax**: (202) 479-3030
- **Address**: 1 First Street NE, Washington, DC 20543
- **Clerk Email**: supremecourtclerk@supremecourt.gov
- **Chief Justice**: John G. Roberts, Jr.
- **Associate Justices**: 8
- **Total Justices**: 9
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction over all federal and state courts
- Original jurisdiction in cases involving:
  - Ambassadors and public ministers
  - States as parties
- Constitutional interpretation
- Judicial review of federal and state laws
- Habeas corpus appeals

## Term
- **First Monday in October** through late June/early July
- Oral arguments typically Monday-Wednesday
