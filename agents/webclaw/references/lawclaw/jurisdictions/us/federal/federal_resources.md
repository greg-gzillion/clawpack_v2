## 💰 PACER (Fee-Based) & ALTERNATIVES

### Official PACER
- **Website**: https://pacer.uscourts.gov
- **Cost**: $0.10 per page ($3.00 maximum per document)
- **Free Threshold**: No fees for < $30 per quarter
- **Registration**: Required for access

### FREE Alternatives
- **RECAP**: Reverse PACER - free access to documents others have purchased
- **CourtListener**: Free searchable database with RECAP integration
- **Harvard Free Law Project**: Bulk case law access (https://free.law)
- **Internet Archive (PACER)**: Archived federal court documents
