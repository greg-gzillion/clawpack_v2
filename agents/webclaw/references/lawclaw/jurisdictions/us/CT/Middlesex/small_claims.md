# Middlesex County Small Claims Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Middlesex
- **Court Name**: Middlesex County Small Claims Court
- **Part of**: Superior Court
- **Phone**: (860) 343-6400
- **Address**: 1 Court Street, Middletown, CT 06457
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Small Claims Information
- **Claim Limit**: ,000
- **Filing Fee**: 
- **Attorney Representation**: Not required (but allowed)
- **Time to Trial**: 30-60 days
- **Appeals**: Either party may appeal

## Filing Process
1. Obtain forms from court or online at https://www.jud.ct.gov
2. Complete Plaintiff's Claim form
3. File with clerk and pay fee
4. Court serves defendant via sheriff or marshal
5. Attend hearing (bring all evidence)
6. Judgment issued within 30 days

## What CAN be filed:
- Unpaid debts
- Property damage
- Breach of contract
- Return of security deposit

## What CANNOT be filed:
- Personal injury claims
- Claims against government
- Defamation or libel
