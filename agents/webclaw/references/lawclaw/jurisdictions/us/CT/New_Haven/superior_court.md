## Jurisdiction
- **Felonies**: All felony cases
- **Civil Cases**: Unlimited jurisdiction
- **Family Matters**: Divorce, custody, support
- **Probate Appeals**: Appeals from probate court
- **Housing Matters**: Landlord/tenant, evictions
- **Small Claims**: Claims up to ,000
