# California Courts of Appeal

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: California Courts of Appeal
- **Website**: https://www.cacourts.gov/appeals
- **Phone**: (415) 865-7000
- **Address**: 350 McAllister Street, San Francisco, CA 94102
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Districts/Divisions
6 Districts: 1st (San Francisco), 2nd (Los Angeles), 3rd (Sacramento), 4th (San Diego), 5th (Fresno), 6th (San Jose)

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals from trial courts
- Review of administrative agency decisions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
