# California Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Los Angeles — https://losangeles.aiga.org/
- AIGA San Francisco — https://sanfrancisco.aiga.org/
- AIGA San Diego — https://sandiego.aiga.org/
- California Arts Council — https://arts.ca.gov/

## Key Design Considerations
- Seismic design (SDC D-E) influences architecture statewide
- Strong indoor/outdoor living tradition
- Sustainability and net-zero design leadership
- Entertainment industry drives set, prop, and experiential design
- Tech industry drives UX/UI and product design
