## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Administrative supervision over all Utah courts
- Mandatory jurisdiction over: first-degree felony appeals, election contests, judicial discipline
