# Salt Lake County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Salt Lake
- **Court Name**: Salt Lake County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (801) 468-3400
- **Address**: 350 S Main Street, Salt Lake City, UT 84111
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM MT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 12-17)
- Abuse, neglect, and dependency
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
- Children in Need of Services
- Traffic violations for minors
