# Harris County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Harris
- **Court Name**: Harris County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (713) 755-5000
- **Address**: 201 Caroline Street, Houston, TX 77002
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction

- Children in Need of Supervision

- Emancipation
