# Dallas County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Dallas
- **Court Name**: Dallas County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (214) 653-6000
- **Address**: 600 Commerce Street, Dallas, TX 75202
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction

- Children in Need of Supervision

- Emancipation
