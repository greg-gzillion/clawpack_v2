# Jasper County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Jasper
- **Court Name**: Jasper County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (843) 726-7793
- **Address**: 3818 E Main Street, Ridgeland, SC 29936
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 18)
- Status offenses (truancy, running away)
- Children in Need of Supervision
- Abuse and neglect
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
