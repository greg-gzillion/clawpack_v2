# Hampton County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Hampton
- **Court Name**: Hampton County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (803) 914-2200
- **Address**: 201 Jackson Avenue E, Hampton, SC 29924
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 18)
- Status offenses (truancy, running away)
- Children in Need of Supervision
- Abuse and neglect
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
