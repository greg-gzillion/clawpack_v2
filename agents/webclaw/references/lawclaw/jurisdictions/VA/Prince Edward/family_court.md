# Prince Edward County Family Court

## Court Information
- **County/City**: Prince Edward
- **Court Name**: Family Court Services of Prince Edward
- **Phone**: (434) 392-5145
- **Address**: 111 S Main Street, Farmville, VA 23901
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:15 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Services
- Divorce and Dissolution (handled by Circuit Court)
- Child Custody and Visitation
- Child Support
- Domestic Violence Protection Orders
- Paternity
- Guardianships of minors

## Note: Family matters are handled by both Juvenile & Domestic Relations District Court and Circuit Court depending on the issue.
