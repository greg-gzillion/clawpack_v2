# Albany County Supreme Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Albany
- **County Seat**: Albany
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of the State of New York, Albany County
- **Website**: https://www.nycourts.gov/supreme
- **Phone**: (518) 285-8500

## Jurisdiction
- Felonies
- Civil cases (unlimited)
- Divorce and matrimonial
- Unlimited jurisdiction
- NOTE: In NY, Supreme Court is a trial-level court, NOT the highest court
