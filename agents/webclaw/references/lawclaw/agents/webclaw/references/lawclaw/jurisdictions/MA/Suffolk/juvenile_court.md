# Suffolk County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Suffolk
- **Court Name**: Suffolk County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (617) 788-8100
- **Address**: 3 Pemberton Square, Boston, MA 02108
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency
- Care and protection (abuse/neglect)
- Children Requiring Assistance (CRA)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
