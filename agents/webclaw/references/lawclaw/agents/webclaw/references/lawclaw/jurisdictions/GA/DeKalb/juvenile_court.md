# DeKalb County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: DeKalb
- **Court Name**: DeKalb County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (404) 371-2000
- **Address**: 556 N McDonough Street, Decatur, GA 30030
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
