# Abbeville County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Abbeville
- **Court Name**: Abbeville County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (864) 366-5312
- **Address**: 102 Court Square, Abbeville, SC 29620
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 18)
- Status offenses (truancy, running away)
- Children in Need of Supervision
- Abuse and neglect
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
