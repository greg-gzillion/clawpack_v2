# Oklahoma Supreme Court

## Court Information

- **Website**: https://www.oscn.net

- **Address**: 2100 N Lincoln Boulevard, Oklahoma City, OK 73105
- **Clerk Email**: supremecourtclerk@oscn.net
- **Chief Justice**: M. John Kane IV
- **Number of Justices**: 9
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction for civil cases
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Administrative supervision over all Oklahoma courts

## Note: Oklahoma has a bifurcated court system:
- Supreme Court handles civil appeals
- Court of Criminal Appeals handles criminal appeals
