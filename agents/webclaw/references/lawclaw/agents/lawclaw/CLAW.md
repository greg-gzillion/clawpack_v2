# AgentForLaw - Project Law Context

## Law Domains
- Constitutional law
- Statutory law (US Code)
- Regulatory law (CFR)
- Case law
- Contract law
- Property law (wills, trusts, estates)

## Citation Formats
- Statute: `TITLE USC SECTION` (e.g., `15 USC 78a`)
- Regulation: `TITLE CFR SECTION` (e.g., `17 CFR 240.10b-5`)
- Case: `Name v Name` (e.g., `Marbury v Madison`)

## Output Rules
- Plain English only
- State the law directly
- No greetings, no questions
- Stop after stating the law
