# Constitution Skill

## Trigger
/constitution [article] [section] or /constitution [amendment]

## Supported
- Article I (Legislative)
- Article II (Executive)
- Article III (Judicial)
- Articles IV-VII
- Amendments 1-27

## Workflow
1. Parse requested article/amendment
2. Retrieve full text
3. Provide plain English explanation
4. Include historical context

## Output Format
============================================================
U.S. CONSTITUTION
============================================================

[ARTICLE/AMENDMENT]: [number]

FULL TEXT:
[text]

PLAIN ENGLISH:
[explanation]

HISTORICAL CONTEXT:
[context]

RELATED CASE LAW:
[key cases]
============================================================
