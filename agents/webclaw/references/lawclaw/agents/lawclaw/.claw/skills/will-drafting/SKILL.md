# Will Drafting Skill

## Trigger
/draft will

## Components
- Testator information
- Beneficiaries
- Executor appointment
- Asset distribution
- Guardian for minors (if applicable)
- Witness requirements

## Workflow
1. Collect testator info
2. List beneficiaries and assets
3. Define distribution percentages
4. Appoint executor
5. Apply state-specific requirements

## Template
============================================================
LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT
============================================================

I, [testator_name], being of sound mind, declare this to be my Last Will and Testament.

1. EXECUTOR: I appoint [executor_name] as Executor.

2. BENEFICIARIES:
   - [name]: [percentage]% of estate

3. RESIDUARY CLAUSE: All remaining assets to [residuary_beneficiary].

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have signed this Will on [date].

________________________________
Testator Signature

Witness 1: ______________________
Witness 2: ______________________
============================================================
