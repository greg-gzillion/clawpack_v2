# Nonprofit Law - Dissolution and Winding Up

## Voluntary Dissolution
| Step | Description | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Board Resolution | Board approves dissolution plan | https://www.councilofnonprofits.org/running-nonprofit/administration-and-financial-management/dissolving-nonprofit |
| Member Approval | If organization has voting members | https://www.councilofnonprofits.org/running-nonprofit/governance-leadership/member-approval-for-dissolution |
| Attorney General Notice | Required in most states | https://www.naag.org/issues/charities/ |
| Articles of Dissolution | File with Secretary of State | https://www.sos.ca.gov/business-programs/business-entities/dissolution |
| Wind Up Affairs | Pay debts; distribute remaining assets | https://www.councilofnonprofits.org/running-nonprofit/administration-and-financial-management/winding-up |

## Distribution of Assets
| Requirement | Description | URL |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Charitable Asset Distribution | Assets must transfer to another 501(c)(3) or government for public purpose | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/dissolution-of-exempt-organizations |
| Dissolution Clause | Articles must require charitable distribution | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/charitable-organizations/dissolution-clauses |
| Cy Pres Doctrine | Court may modify restrictions on charitable gifts | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/cy_pres_doctrine |
| No Private Inurement | No assets to insiders or private individuals | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/inurement-and-private-benefit |

## Involuntary Dissolution
| Ground | Description | URL |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| Attorney General Action | Breach of charitable trust; fraud; insolvency | https://www.naag.org/issues/charities/ |
| Judicial Dissolution | Court-ordered dissolution | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/judicial_dissolution |
