## 501(h) Expenditure Limits
| Exempt Purpose Expenditures | Direct Lobbying Limit | Grassroots Lobbying Limit | URL |
|----------------------------|----------------------|--------------------------|-----|
| Up to $500,000 | 20% | 5% | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/measuring-lobbying-activity-expenditure-test |
| $500,001 - $1,000,000 | $100,000 + 15% of excess | $25,000 + 3.75% of excess | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/measuring-lobbying-activity-expenditure-test |
| $1,000,001 - $1,500,000 | $175,000 + 10% of excess | $43,750 + 2.5% of excess | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/measuring-lobbying-activity-expenditure-test |
| Over $1,500,000 | $225,000 + 5% of excess | $56,250 + 1.25% of excess | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/measuring-lobbying-activity-expenditure-test |
| Overall Cap | $1,000,000 | $250,000 | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/measuring-lobbying-activity-expenditure-test |
