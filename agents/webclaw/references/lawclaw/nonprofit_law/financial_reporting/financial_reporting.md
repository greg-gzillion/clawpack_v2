# Nonprofit Law - Financial Reporting and Form 990

## Form 990 Series Overview
| Form | Filing Threshold | Description | URL |
|------|-----------------|-------------|-----|
| Form 990 | Gross receipts =$200,000 or assets =$500,000 | Full annual return | https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-990 |
| Form 990-EZ | Gross receipts <$200,000 and assets <$500,000 | Short form return | https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-990-ez |
| Form 990-N (e-Postcard) | Gross receipts =$50,000 | Electronic notice | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/annual-electronic-filing-requirement-for-small-exempt-organizations-form-990-n-e-postcard |
| Form 990-PF | All private foundations | Private foundation return | https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-990-pf |
| Form 990-T | $1,000+ UBIT | Unrelated business income tax return | https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-990-t |
