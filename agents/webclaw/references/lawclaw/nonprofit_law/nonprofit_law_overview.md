# Nonprofit Law - Overview

## What is Nonprofit Law?
Nonprofit law governs organizations formed for purposes other than generating profit for owners or shareholders. It encompasses federal and state laws regarding formation, tax exemption, governance, fundraising, and compliance for charitable, educational, religious, and other tax-exempt organizations.
