## IRS Substantiation Requirements (IRC § 170(f)(8))
| Contribution | Documentation Required | URL |
|--------------|----------------------|-----|
| $250 or more | Contemporaneous written acknowledgment | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/substantiating-charitable-contributions |
| Less than $250 | Bank record or written communication from charity | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/substantiating-charitable-contributions |
| Quid Pro Quo >$75 | Written disclosure of fair market value of goods/services provided | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/quid-pro-quo-contributions |
| Vehicle Donations >$500 | Form 1098-C or acknowledgment within 30 days | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/charitable-organizations/vehicle-donations |
| Non-Cash Contributions >$5,000 | Qualified appraisal required | https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/charitable-organizations/substantiating-noncash-contributions |
