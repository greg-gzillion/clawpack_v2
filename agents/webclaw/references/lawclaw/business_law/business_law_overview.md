# Business Law - Overview

## What is Business Law?
Business law encompasses the legal rules governing commercial entities and transactions. It covers business formation and governance, commercial transactions, secured transactions, negotiable instruments, bankruptcy, antitrust, securities regulation, employment relationships, and intellectual property protection.
