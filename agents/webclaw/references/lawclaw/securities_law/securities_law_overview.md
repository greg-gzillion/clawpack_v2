# Securities Law - Overview

## What is Securities Law?
Securities law governs the issuance, trading, and regulation of securities庸inancial instruments representing ownership (stocks), debt (bonds), or rights to future payment. It encompasses federal and state statutes designed to protect investors, ensure market transparency, and maintain fair and efficient capital markets.
