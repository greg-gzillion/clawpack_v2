## Comparison of Structures
| Factor | Merger | Stock Purchase | Asset Purchase |
|--------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| Survivor Liability | All liabilities assumed | Liabilities remain with target | Only assumed liabilities |
| Shareholder Approval | Required | Target shareholder approval | Buyer shareholders may vote |
| Dissenting Shareholders | Appraisal rights | Generally none | Generally none |
| Third-Party Consents | Not required | Not required | May be required |
| Tax Treatment | Can be tax-free | Taxable | Taxable (buyer basis step-up) |
