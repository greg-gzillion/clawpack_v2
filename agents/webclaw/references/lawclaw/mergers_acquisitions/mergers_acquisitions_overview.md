# Mergers & Acquisitions Law - Overview

## What is M&A Law?
Mergers and acquisitions law governs the consolidation of companies through various transaction structures, including mergers, stock purchases, asset purchases, and tender offers. It encompasses corporate law, securities regulation, antitrust review, tax considerations, and contractual negotiation.
