# Civil Rights Law - Overview

## What are Civil Rights?
Civil rights are the rights of individuals to receive equal treatment and be free from discrimination and unfair treatment based on protected characteristics. Civil rights laws protect against discrimination in employment, housing, education, public accommodations, and government programs.
