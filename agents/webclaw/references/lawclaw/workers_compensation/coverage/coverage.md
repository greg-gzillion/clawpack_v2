## Employment Relationship
| Factor | Description | URL |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| Right to Control | Primary test for employee vs. independent contractor | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/right_to_control |
| Economic Realities Test | Dependence on employer for livelihood | https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd/flsa/misclassification |
| ABC Test | Strict test for employee status (CA, MA, NJ) | Varies by state |
| Borrowed Servant Doctrine | Employee of one employer loaned to another | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/borrowed_servant |
| Joint Employment | Two employers share control | https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd/flsa/misclassification |
