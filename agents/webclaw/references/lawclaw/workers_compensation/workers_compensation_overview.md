# Workers' Compensation Law - Overview

## What is Workers' Compensation?
Workers' compensation is a no-fault insurance system that provides benefits to employees who suffer work-related injuries or illnesses. In exchange for guaranteed benefits, employees give up the right to sue their employers for negligence. The system is primarily state-based, with each state having its own workers' compensation statute and administrative framework.
