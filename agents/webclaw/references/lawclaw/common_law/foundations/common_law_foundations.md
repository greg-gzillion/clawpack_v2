## Judicial Hierarchy in Federal System
| Court | Binding On | Bound By |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| U.S. Supreme Court | All courts | Own prior decisions |
| Circuit Courts of Appeals | District courts within circuit | Supreme Court; own prior decisions (en banc) |
| District Courts | None (trial courts) | Supreme Court; Circuit Court of Appeals |

## Law vs. Equity
| Feature | Law | Equity |
|---------|-----|--------|
| Remedy | Damages | Injunction, specific performance |
| Jury Trial | Available | Not available |
| Defenses | Legal defenses | Equitable defenses (laches, unclean hands) |
| Historical Courts | King's Bench, Common Pleas | Chancery |
