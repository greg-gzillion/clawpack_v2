# Torts Law - Overview

## What is Torts Law?
Torts law governs civil wrongs—other than breaches of contract—for which courts provide remedies in the form of damages. It encompasses intentional torts, negligence, strict liability, products liability, defamation, privacy torts, and economic torts. Torts law is primarily state common law, supplemented by statutes and the Restatements of Torts.
