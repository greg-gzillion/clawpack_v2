## Wrongful Death vs. Survival Actions
| Feature | Wrongful Death | Survival Action | URL |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|-----|
| Beneficiaries | Statutory beneficiaries | Decedent's estate | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/wrongful_death |
| Damages | Survivors' losses | Decedent's losses | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/wrongful_death |
| Accrual | At death | Before death (continues after) | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/wrongful_death |
| Both Available | Yes | Yes | https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/wrongful_death |
