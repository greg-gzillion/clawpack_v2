# Entertainment Law - Overview

## What is Entertainment Law?
Entertainment law governs the legal aspects of the entertainment industry, including film, television, music, theater, publishing, digital media, and sports. It encompasses intellectual property, contracts, labor relations, and First Amendment issues.
