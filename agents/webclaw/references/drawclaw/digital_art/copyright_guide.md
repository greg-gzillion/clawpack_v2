## Common Terms
- **Public Domain** - Works with no intellectual property restrictions
- **Creative Commons** - Free to share with specified restrictions
- **Creative Commons Zero (CC0)** - Public domain dedication by creator
- **Copyleft** - Free to modify and redistribute with same terms
- **Open Access** - Scholarly research freely available online
- **Open Source** - Free to share and modify per specific principles
