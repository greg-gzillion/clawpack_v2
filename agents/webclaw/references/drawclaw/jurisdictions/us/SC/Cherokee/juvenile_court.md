# Cherokee County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Cherokee
- **Court Name**: Cherokee County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (864) 487-2570
- **Address**: 110 Railroad Avenue, Gaffney, SC 29340
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 18)
- Status offenses (truancy, running away)
- Children in Need of Supervision
- Abuse and neglect
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
