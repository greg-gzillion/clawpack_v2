# Baker County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Baker
- **Court Name**: Baker County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (229) 734-3005
- **Address**: 100 Main Street, Newton, GA 39870
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
