# Connecticut Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Connecticut — https://connecticut.aiga.org/
- Connecticut Office of the Arts — https://portal.ct.gov/culture-and-tourism

## Key Design Considerations
- Strong graphic design and branding heritage (Mad Men era legacy)
- Proximity to NYC drives design talent and firms
- Historic New England architecture and preservation
- Coastal resilience design for Long Island Sound communities
