# Texas Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Austin — https://austin.aiga.org/
- AIGA Houston — https://houston.aiga.org/
- AIGA Dallas/Fort Worth — https://dallasfortworth.aiga.org/
- Texas Commission on the Arts — https://www.arts.texas.gov/
- AIA Texas — https://www.texasarchitects.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Explosive population growth driving design demand
- Hurricane codes on Gulf Coast (150 mph wind zones)
- Hot/humid and hot/dry climate zones
- Strong healthcare, sports, and energy design sectors
