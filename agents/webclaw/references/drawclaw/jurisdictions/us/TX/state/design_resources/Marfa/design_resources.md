## Cultural Design
- Chinati Foundation — https://www.chinati.org/ — Donald Judd art and design
- Judd Foundation — https://www.juddfoundation.org/

## Notes
Marfa is a globally significant art and design destination founded by Donald Judd. The Chinati Foundation transformed a military base into a permanent large-scale art installation. Minimalist design and architecture define the town.
