# Maine Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- Maine Arts Commission — https://www.mainearts.com/
- AIA Maine — https://www.aiamaine.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Cold climate and heavy snow loads (55-80 psf)
- Coastal resilience and sea-level rise design
- Strong craft and maker tradition
- Historic New England architecture preservation
- Boat and maritime design heritage
