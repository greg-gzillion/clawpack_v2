- AIGA Miami — https://miami.aiga.org/
- AIGA Orlando — https://orlando.aiga.org/
- AIA Florida — https://www.aiafla.org/
- Florida Division of Arts and Culture — https://dos.myflorida.com/cultural/

## Key Design Considerations
- Hurricane codes (150-175 mph wind zones) heavily influence structural and envelope design
- Tropical modernism and climate-responsive architecture
- Strong Latin American and Caribbean design influence
- Theme park and entertainment design (Orlando)
- Coastal resilience and sea-level rise design
