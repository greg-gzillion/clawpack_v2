# Arkansas Court of Appeals

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Arkansas Court of Appeals
- **Website**: https://www.arcourts.gov/courts/court-of-appeals
- **Phone**: (501) 682-6840
- **Address**: 625 Marshall Street, Little Rock, AR 72201
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CST

## Districts/Divisions
Single statewide court with 12 judges sitting in panels of 3

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals from trial courts
- Review of administrative agency decisions
- Interlocutory appeals
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
