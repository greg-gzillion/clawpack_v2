# Arkansas Supreme Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Arkansas Supreme Court
- **Website**: https://www.arcourts.gov/courts/supreme-court
- **Phone**: (501) 682-6849
- **Address**: 625 Marshall Street, Little Rock, AR 72201
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CST
- **Chief Justice**: John Dan Kemp
- **Number of Justices**: 7

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Administrative control over lower courts
- Bar admissions and discipline
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Rule-making authority for state courts
