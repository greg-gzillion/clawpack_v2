# Supreme Court of the United States

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of the United States
- **Website**: https://www.supremecourt.gov
- **Phone**: (202) 479-3000
- **Address**: 1 First Street NE, Washington, DC 20543
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Highest court in the United States
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Original jurisdiction in limited cases
- Constitutional interpretation
- Judicial review

## Justices
- 1 Chief Justice and 8 Associate Justices
- Appointed by President, confirmed by Senate
- Lifetime appointments
