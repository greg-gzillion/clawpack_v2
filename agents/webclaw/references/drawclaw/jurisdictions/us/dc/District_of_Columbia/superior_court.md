# Superior Court of the District of Columbia

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Superior Court of the District of Columbia
- **Website**: https://www.dccourts.gov/superior-court
- **Phone**: (202) 879-1010
- **Address**: 500 Indiana Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- General trial court for DC
- Civil cases (all amounts)
- Criminal misdemeanors and felonies
- Family law (divorce, custody)
- Probate and estates
- Juvenile matters
- Small claims (up to $10,000)
- Landlord and tenant

## Divisions
- Civil Division
- Criminal Division
- Family Court
- Domestic Violence Division
- Probate Division
- Tax Division
