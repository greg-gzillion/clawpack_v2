# Somerset County Orphans' Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Somerset
- **Court Name**: Orphans' Court for Somerset
- **Phone**: (410) 651-1500
- **Address**: 120 N Somerset Avenue, Princess Anne, MD 21853
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Wills and Estates
- Trusts
- Guardianships
- Conservatorships
- Inheritance disputes

## Note: Called "Orphans' Court" - unique to Maryland
- Judges are elected (not required to be lawyers)
- Also known as Probate Court
