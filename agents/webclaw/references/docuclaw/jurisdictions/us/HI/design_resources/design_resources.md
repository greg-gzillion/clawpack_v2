# Hawaii Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Honolulu — https://honolulu.aiga.org/
- Hawaii State Foundation on Culture and the Arts — https://sfca.hawaii.gov/
- AIA Hawaii — https://www.aiahawaii.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Tropical climate and passive cooling design
- Hurricane and tsunami resilience
- Indigenous Hawaiian design and cultural sensitivity
- Remote island logistics and material constraints
- Indoor/outdoor living tradition
