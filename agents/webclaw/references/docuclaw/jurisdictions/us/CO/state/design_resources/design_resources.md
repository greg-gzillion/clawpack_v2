# Colorado Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Colorado — https://colorado.aiga.org/
- Colorado Creative Industries — https://oedit.colorado.gov/colorado-creative-industries
- Denver Design Week — https://www.denverdesignweek.com/

## Key Design Considerations
- Mountain and high-plains context
- Strong sustainability and outdoor industry design
- Growing aerospace and tech design sectors
- Snow load (40-100 psf) and altitude influence structural design
