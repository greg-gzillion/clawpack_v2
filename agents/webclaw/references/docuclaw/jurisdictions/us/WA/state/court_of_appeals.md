# Washington Court of Appeals

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Washington Court of Appeals
- **Website**: https://www.courts.wa.gov/court_of_appeals
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Three Divisions
- **Division I**: Seattle (King, Snohomish, Whatcom, Skagit, Island, San Juan, Clallam, Jefferson, Kitsap counties) - (206) 464-7750
- **Division II**: Tacoma (Pierce, Thurston, Mason, Grays Harbor, Pacific, Lewis, Clark, Cowlitz, Wahkiakum counties) - (253) 593-2970
- **Division III**: Spokane (Eastern Washington - 20 counties) - (509) 456-3082

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals from Superior Courts
- Review of administrative agency decisions
- Most appeals go here before Supreme Court
