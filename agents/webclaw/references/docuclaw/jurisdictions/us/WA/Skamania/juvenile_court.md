# Skamania County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Skamania
- **Court Name**: Skamania County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (509) 427-9490
- **Address**: 240 NW Vancouver Avenue, Stevenson, WA 98648
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 8-17)
- At-risk youth (ARY)
- Child in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Dependency (abuse/neglect)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
