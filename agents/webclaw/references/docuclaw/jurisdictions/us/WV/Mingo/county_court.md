# Mingo County Magistrate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Mingo
- **Court Name**: Mingo County Magistrate Court
- **Phone**: (304) 235-3580
- **Address**: 75 E 2nd Ave, Williamson, WV 25661
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM ET
- **Website**: http://www.courtswv.gov

## Jurisdiction
- Misdemeanors
- Traffic violations
- Small claims (up to $10,000)
- Civil cases (up to $10,000)
- Preliminary hearings
- Issue warrants and emergency orders
