# Roane County Magistrate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Roane
- **Court Name**: Roane County Magistrate Court
- **Phone**: (304) 927-2860
- **Address**: 200 Main St, Spencer, WV 25276
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM ET
- **Website**: http://www.courtswv.gov

## Jurisdiction
- Misdemeanors
- Traffic violations
- Small claims (up to $10,000)
- Civil cases (up to $10,000)
- Preliminary hearings
- Issue warrants and emergency orders
