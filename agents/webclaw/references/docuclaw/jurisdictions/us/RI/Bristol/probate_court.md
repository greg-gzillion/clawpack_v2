# Bristol County Probate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Bristol
- **Court Name**: Bristol County Probate Courts
- **Phone**: (401) 253-7000
- **Address**: 240 High Street, Bristol, RI 02809
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Wills and Estates
- Trusts
- Guardianships
- Conservatorships
- Name changes

## Note: Each city and town in Rhode Island has its own Probate Court. Contact the county for specific municipal probate court locations.
