# Rhode Island Probate Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Rhode Island Probate Court
- **Website**: https://www.courts.ri.gov/probate
- **Phone**: (401) 222-2550
- **Fax**: (401) 222-3633
- **Address**: 250 Benefit Street, Providence, RI 02903
- **Clerk Email**: probatecourtclerk@courts.ri.gov
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Wills and Estates
- Trusts
- Guardianships
- Conservatorships
- Name changes

## Note: Each city/town has its own Probate Court. The statewide administrative office coordinates them.
