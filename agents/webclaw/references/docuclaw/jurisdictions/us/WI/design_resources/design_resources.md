# Wisconsin Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Wisconsin — https://wisconsin.aiga.org/
- Wisconsin Arts Board — https://www.artsboard.wisconsin.gov/
- AIA Wisconsin — https://www.aiaw.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Cold climate design (-25F to 95F)
- Heavy snow loads in northern counties
- Strong manufacturing and industrial design heritage
- Frank Lloyd Wright architectural legacy
