# Winnebago County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Winnebago
- **Court Name**: Winnebago County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (920) 236-4800
- **Address**: 415 Jackson Street, Oshkosh, WI 54901
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 10-16)
- Children in Need of Protection or Services (CHIPS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
- Truancy
