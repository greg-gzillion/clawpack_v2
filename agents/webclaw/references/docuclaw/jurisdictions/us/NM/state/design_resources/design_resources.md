## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA New Mexico — https://newmexico.aiga.org/
- New Mexico Arts — https://www.nmarts.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Adobe and Pueblo architecture heritage (oldest continuously inhabited buildings in US)
- High desert climate design
- Strong Native American and Hispanic design traditions
- Santa Fe Style architectural code
