# Pennsylvania Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Philadelphia — https://philadelphia.aiga.org/
- AIGA Pittsburgh — https://pittsburgh.aiga.org/
- Pennsylvania Council on the Arts — https://www.arts.pa.gov/
- AIA Pennsylvania — https://www.aiapa.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Strong industrial and manufacturing design heritage
- Historic preservation (colonial to industrial)
- Two major design hubs: Philadelphia and Pittsburgh
- Affordable cities attracting creative professionals
