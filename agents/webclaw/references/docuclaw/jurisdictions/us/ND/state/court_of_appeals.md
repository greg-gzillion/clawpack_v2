# North Dakota Court of Appeals

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: North Dakota Court of Appeals
- **Website**: https://www.ndcourts.gov/court-of-appeals
- **Phone**: (701) 328-2221
- **Fax**: (701) 328-4480
- **Address**: 600 E Boulevard Avenue, Bismarck, ND 58505
- **Clerk Email**: clerk@ndcourts.gov
- **Chief Judge**: Jerod E. Tufte
- **Number of Judges**: 3 (part-time, appointed from district court judges)
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals assigned by Supreme Court
- Cases that do not involve constitutional questions
- Cases not involving felonies
- Created as a part-time, voluntary court to help with caseload
