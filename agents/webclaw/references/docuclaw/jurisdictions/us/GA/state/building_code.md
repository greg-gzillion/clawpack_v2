## Key Georgia Notes
- 159 counties — each has its own building permit authority
- State sets minimum codes; local jurisdictions may amend (more stringent only)
- Coastal counties in 100-mph wind zone per IRC (wind-borne debris region)
- North GA mountain counties have frost depth requirements
- Contact county Building Official / Planning Department for permits
- Some small counties may reference state-level plan review
