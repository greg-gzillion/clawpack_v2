# Mitchell County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Mitchell
- **Court Name**: Mitchell County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (229) 336-2000
- **Address**: 11 E Broad Street, Camilla, GA 31730
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
