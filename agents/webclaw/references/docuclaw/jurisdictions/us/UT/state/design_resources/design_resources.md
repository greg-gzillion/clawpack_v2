# Utah Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Salt Lake City — https://saltlakecity.aiga.org/
- Utah Division of Arts & Museums — https://www.artsandmuseums.utah.gov/
- AIA Utah — https://www.aiautah.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Mountain and desert climate zones
- Snow loads (30-80 psf) vary dramatically
- Strong outdoor recreation and resort design
- Growing tech sector (Silicon Slopes)
- LDS Church architecture influence
