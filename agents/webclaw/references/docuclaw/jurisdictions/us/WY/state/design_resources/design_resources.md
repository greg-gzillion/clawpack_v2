# Wyoming Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- Wyoming Arts Council — https://www.wyomingartscouncil.org/
- AIA Wyoming — https://www.aia-wyoming.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Extreme climate design (-40F to 100F)
- Heavy mountain snow loads (60-100 psf)
- Western and rustic design aesthetic
- Strong craft and ranch design tradition
- Wind-resistant design (high wind zones)
