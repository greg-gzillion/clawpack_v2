# High Court of American Samoa

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: High Court of American Samoa
- **Website**: https://www.asbar.org/courts/
- **Phone**: (684) 633-1125
- **Fax**: (684) 633-1125
- **Address**: HC-02, Utulei, AS 96799
- **Chief Justice**: Michael Kruse
- **Number of Justices**: 1 (Chief Justice) + Associate Justices
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM SST

## Divisions
- **Appellate Division**: Final appellate jurisdiction
- **Trial Division**: Felonies, civil cases over $20,000
- **Probate Division**: Wills, estates, guardianships
- **Land and Titles Division**: Matai title disputes, communal land

## Note: American Samoa is the only US territory with no federal district court
