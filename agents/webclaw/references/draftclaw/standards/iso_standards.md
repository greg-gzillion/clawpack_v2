# Technical Drawing Standards - ISO
## ISO 128 - Technical Drawings General Principles
- ISO 128-1:2020 Introduction and index
- ISO 128-2:2020 Basic conventions for lines
- ISO 128-3:2020 Views, sections and cuts
- https://www.iso.org/standard/71599.html

## ISO 129 - Dimensioning
- ISO 129-1:2018 General principles
- https://www.iso.org/standard/69009.html

## Paper Sizes (ISO 216)
- A0: 841 x 1189 mm (33.1 x 46.8 in)
- A1: 594 x 841 mm (23.4 x 33.1 in)
- A2: 420 x 594 mm (16.5 x 23.4 in)
- A3: 297 x 420 mm (11.7 x 16.5 in)
- A4: 210 x 297 mm (8.3 x 11.7 in)

## Line Types
- Continuous thick: Visible outlines (0.5-0.7mm)
- Continuous thin: Dimension lines, leader lines, hatching (0.25-0.35mm)
- Dashed: Hidden edges (0.35-0.5mm)
- Chain thin: Center lines, symmetry lines (0.25-0.35mm)
- Chain thick: Cutting planes (0.5-0.7mm)
