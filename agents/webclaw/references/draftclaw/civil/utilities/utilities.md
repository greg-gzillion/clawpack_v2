# Utility Systems - Reference
## All URLs verified May 2026

## Sanitary Sewer
| Element | Standard | Source |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Main line | PVC SDR-35 or ductile iron; 8" minimum diameter for public mains; 2% minimum slope (1/4" per foot) | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sanitary_sewer |
| Service lateral | 4" minimum from building to main; 2% minimum slope; cleanout at building exterior and at property line | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sanitary_sewer |
| Manhole | At every change in direction, slope, or pipe size; maximum 400' spacing for 8"-18" pipes | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manhole |
| Force main | Pressurized sewer line from pump/lift station; ductile iron or HDPE; restrained joints at all bends | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sanitary_sewer |

## Storm Drainage
| Element | Standard | Source |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Storm main | Reinforced concrete pipe (RCP) or HDPE; 12" minimum diameter for public systems | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Storm_drain |
| Catch basin | Inlet structure with sump (2' minimum below invert); hooded outlet to trap floatables | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Storm_drain |
| Junction box | Connect multiple storm lines; provide access for cleaning | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Storm_drain |
| Headwall | Concrete collar at pipe outlet into channel or basin; energy dissipation apron | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Storm_drain |

## Water Supply
| Element | Standard | Source |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Water main | Ductile iron Class 52 or C900 PVC; 8" minimum for public mains with fire hydrants | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Water_supply_network |
| Service line | 3/4" or 1" Type K copper or HDPE from main to meter; minimum 42" cover (below frost) | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Water_supply_network |
| Fire hydrant | Maximum 400' spacing in commercial, 500' in residential; 6" minimum lead from main | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fire_hydrant |
| Backflow preventer | Required on all commercial services and irrigation connections per local code | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Water_supply_network |
| Water meter vault | Precast concrete or HDPE; locate at ROW/property line; provide bypass for fire services | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Water_supply_network |

## Natural Gas
| Element | Standard | Source |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Gas main | Coated steel or medium-density PE (MDPE); typically 2"-6" for distribution | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gas_main |
| Service line | 3/4" or 1" MDPE from main to meter; minimum 18" cover | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gas_main |
| Meter location | At building exterior; 3' clearance from electrical, 10' from air intakes | Per local gas utility and NFPA 54 |
