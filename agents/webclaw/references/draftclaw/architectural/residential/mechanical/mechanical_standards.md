|--------|-----|-----|

## Ductwork Standards (IRC Chapter 16 / ACCA Manual D)
| Element | Standard | URL |
|---------|----------|-----|
| Supply duct (main trunk) | Sheet metal 26 gauge minimum, insulated R-8 in unconditioned space | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Return duct | Same as supply, R-4 minimum in unconditioned space | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Flexible duct | Max 14' length, no more than 4" sag per 2', no kinks | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Duct sealing | Mastic or UL 181 tape, no duct tape | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Register placement | Perimeter (under windows) for heating, high wall/ceiling for cooling | https://www.acca.org/standards |
| Return air path | 1 sq in per CFM (minimum), transfer grilles for bedrooms | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
