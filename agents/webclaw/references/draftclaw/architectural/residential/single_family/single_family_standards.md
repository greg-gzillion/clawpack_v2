# Single-Family Residential Standards - Reference

| Room | Floor Area | Minimum Dimension | URL |
|------|-----------|------------------|-----|

| Bathroom | No minimum sq ft | Per fixture clearance requirements | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |

## Ceiling Heights (IRC R305)
| Space | Minimum Height | URL |
|-------|---------------|-----|
| Habitable rooms | 7'-0" for >50% of required floor area | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Hallways, bathrooms, laundry | 6'-8" | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Basement (habitable rooms) | 7'-0" | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Basement (non-habitable) | 6'-8" (6'-4" under beams/ducts) | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
| Sloped ceiling | 5'-0" minimum over 50% of room, no portion <4'-0" | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IRC2021P1 |
