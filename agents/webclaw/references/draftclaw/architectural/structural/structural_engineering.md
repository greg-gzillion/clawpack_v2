## Jurisdictional Notes
- **Seismic zones**: ASCE 7 seismic maps determine Seismic Design Category. California (D-L), Alaska (D-E), New Madrid region (D) require special detailing
- **Wind zones**: ASCE 7 wind maps. Florida coast (150-180 mph), Gulf Coast (140-160 mph), Midwest (115-130 mph typical)
- **Snow loads**: Ground snow load maps in ASCE 7 Chapter 7. Northeast US (40-70 psf), Mountain West (50-300+ psf), Southern US (5-20 psf)
- **California**: California Building Code (CBC) Chapter 16A includes additional seismic requirements; DSA for schools, OSHPD for hospitals
- **Florida**: High-Velocity Hurricane Zones (HVHZ) - Miami-Dade and Broward counties have missile impact requirements per TAS 201/202/203
