## Primary Reference
The 2010 ADA Standards for Accessible Design are the enforceable federal standard: https://www.ada.gov/law-and-regs/design-standards/2010-stds/
The ICC A117.1-2017 Accessible and Usable Buildings and Facilities is the technical standard adopted by the IBC: https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/ICCA117-12017P1
