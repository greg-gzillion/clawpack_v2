## Jurisdictional Notes
- **Federal OSHA**: 29 CFR 1910 applies to all industrial workplaces in the US
- **State OSHA plans**: 22 states have OSHA-approved state plans (California/Cal-OSHA, Michigan/MIOSHA, etc.)
- **Local fire codes**: IFC (International Fire Code) adopted with local amendments; check with local fire marshal
- **Environmental**: EPA NESHAP (National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants) for specific industries
- **Zoning**: Industrial zones (I-1 light, I-2 medium, I-3 heavy) per local zoning ordinance; determine permitted uses before design
