# Healthcare Facility Standards - Reference

## Room Types and Sizes (FGI Guidelines)
| Room | Minimum Size | URL |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Patient room (private) | 120 sq ft clear floor area | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Patient room (semi-private) | 100 sq ft per patient | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| ICU room | 200 sq ft clear floor area | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Exam room | 80 sq ft minimum, 8' minimum width | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Operating room (general) | 400 sq ft minimum | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Operating room (cardiac/neuro) | 600 sq ft minimum | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Nurse station | 80-120 sq ft per nurse, open design | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Waiting area | 20 sq ft per seat, 2-3 seats per exam room | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |

## Corridor Standards
| Area | Minimum Width | URL |
|------|--------------|-----|
| Patient corridors (general) | 96" (8'-0") minimum | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Patient corridors (surgery/ICU) | 96" minimum | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Staff-only corridors | 60" minimum | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Stretcher clearance | 60" for two passing stretchers | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |
| Ceiling height | 7'-10" minimum in patient areas | https://www.fgiguidelines.org/ |

## Infection Control
| Element | Standard | URL |
|---------|----------|-----|
| Hand washing sink | Every patient room, exam room, procedure room | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/ |
| Air changes per hour (OR) | 20 minimum, 4 outdoor air | https://www.ashrae.org/technical-resources/bookstore/standards-170 |
| Air changes per hour (patient room) | 6 minimum, 2 outdoor air | https://www.ashrae.org/technical-resources/bookstore/standards-170 |
| HEPA filtration | Required for protective environment rooms | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/ |
| Negative pressure room (airborne isolation) | 12 air changes/hour, exhausted to outside | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/ |
| Positive pressure room (protective) | 12 air changes/hour, HEPA supply | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/ |
| Surface materials | Non-porous, cleanable, no gaps >1/32" | https://www.cdc.gov/infectioncontrol/ |
