## Jurisdictional Notes
- **Federal**: CMS Conditions of Participation (42 CFR Part 482) require compliance with NFPA 101 Life Safety Code - https://www.cms.gov/Medicare/Provider-Enrollment-and-Certification/CertificationandComplianc
- **Joint Commission**: Accredits hospitals under Environment of Care (EC) standards citing NFPA 101, NFPA 99, and ASHRAE 170
- **California**: OSHPD (Office of Statewide Health Planning and Development) enforces California Building Code with hospital-specific amendments - https://oshpd.ca.gov/
- **Florida**: AHCA (Agency for Health Care Administration) reviews hospital construction - https://ahca.myflorida.com/
- **41 states + DC**: FGI Guidelines adopted by reference for hospital licensing
