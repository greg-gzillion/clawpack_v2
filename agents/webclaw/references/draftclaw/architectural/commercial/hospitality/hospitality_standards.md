# Hospitality Standards - Hotels & Restaurants

## Hotel Room Standards
| Element | Minimum | Recommended | URL |
|---------|---------|-------------|-----|
| Standard guest room | 250-300 sq ft | 325-400 sq ft | https://www.ahla.com/ |
| Suite (junior) | 400-500 sq ft | 500-600 sq ft | https://www.ahla.com/ |
| Bathroom (hotel) | 40-60 sq ft | 60-80 sq ft | https://www.ahla.com/ |
| Corridor width (hotel) | 44" minimum (48" with luggage) | 60" typical | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IBC2021P1 |
| Accessible rooms | 5-7% of total rooms minimum (ADA) | Per ADA 224 | https://www.ada.gov/law-and-regs/design-standards/2010-stds/ |
| Ceiling height | 7'-6" minimum sleeping rooms | 8'-0" to 9'-0" | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IBC2021P1 |
| Sound transmission | STC 50 minimum between rooms | STC 55 recommended | https://www.ahla.com/ |

|---------|----------|-----|

| Dining area (casual) | 12-15 sq ft per seat | https://www.restaurant.org/ |
| Dining area (fast food) | 8-12 sq ft per seat | https://www.restaurant.org/ |
| Kitchen-to-dining ratio | 30-50% of total seating area | https://www.restaurant.org/ |
| Bar/counter seating | 24" width per stool, 18" depth counter | https://www.restaurant.org/ |
| Aisle width (between tables) | 36" minimum, 42" for service aisles | https://www.restaurant.org/ |
| Ceiling height (dining) | 8'-0" minimum | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IBC2021P1 |
| Occupancy (restaurant, A-2) | 15 sq ft net per occupant | https://codes.iccsafe.org/content/IBC2021P1 |
