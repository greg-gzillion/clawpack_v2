# Jones County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Jones
- **Court Name**: Jones County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (478) 986-6671
- **Address**: 110 Industrial Boulevard, Gray, GA 31032
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
