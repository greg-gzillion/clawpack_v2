# Rhode Island Supreme Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Rhode Island Supreme Court
- **Website**: https://www.courts.ri.gov/supreme
- **Phone**: (401) 222-3272
- **Fax**: (401) 222-3853
- **Address**: 250 Benefit Street, Providence, RI 02903
- **Clerk Email**: supremecourtclerk@courts.ri.gov
- **Chief Justice**: Paul A. Suttell
- **Number of Justices**: 5
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Administrative supervision over all Rhode Island courts
- Oldest continuously operating court in the US (established 1747)
