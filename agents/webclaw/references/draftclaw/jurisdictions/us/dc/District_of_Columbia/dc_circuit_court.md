# United States Court of Appeals for the DC Circuit

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: U.S. Court of Appeals for the DC Circuit
- **Website**: https://www.cadc.uscourts.gov
- **Phone**: (202) 216-7000
- **Address**: 333 Constitution Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals from U.S. District Court for DC
- Federal administrative agency appeals
- Regulatory and constitutional challenges

## Notable
- Second most important court in US (after Supreme Court)
- Chief Judge and 12 active judges
