# Florida District Courts of Appeal

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Florida District Courts of Appeal
- **Website**: https://www.flcourts.gov/appeals
- **Phone**: (850) 488-0125
- **Address**: 500 S Duval Street, Tallahassee, FL 32399
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Districts/Divisions
6 Districts: 1st (Tallahassee), 2nd (Lakeland), 3rd (Miami), 4th (West Palm Beach), 5th (Daytona Beach), 6th (Lakeland)

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals from trial courts
- Review of administrative agency decisions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
