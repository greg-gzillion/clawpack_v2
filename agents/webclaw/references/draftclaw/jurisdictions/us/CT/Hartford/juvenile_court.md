# Hartford County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Hartford
- **Court Name**: Hartford County Juvenile Court
- **Part of**: Superior Court
- **Phone**: (860) 566-8030
- **Address**: 95 Washington Street, Hartford, CT 06106
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- **Juvenile Delinquency**: Criminal acts by minors
- **Child Protection**: Abuse and neglect cases
- **Family with Service Needs**: Truancy, runaways
- **Termination of Parental Rights**: Involuntary termination
- **Emancipation**: Minor seeking adult status
- **Adoption**: Juvenile court adoptions

## Age Jurisdiction
- Delinquency: Ages 7-17
- Family with Service Needs: Ages 7-17
- Child Protection: Ages 0-17
