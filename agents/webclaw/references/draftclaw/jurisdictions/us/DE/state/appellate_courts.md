# Delaware Court of Chancery

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Delaware Court of Chancery
- **Website**: https://www.decourts.gov/appeals
- **Phone**: (302) 255-0100
- **Address**: 500 N King Street, Wilmington, DE 19801
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Districts/Divisions
Corporate law, trusts, equity matters

## Jurisdiction
- Appeals from trial courts
- Review of administrative agency decisions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
