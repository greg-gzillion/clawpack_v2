# Vermont Superior Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Vermont Superior Court
- **Website**: https://www.vermontjudiciary.org/superior-court
- **Phone**: (802) 828-3274
- **Fax**: (802) 828-3457
- **Address**: 111 State Street, Montpelier, VT 05609
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Divisions
- **Criminal Division**: Felonies and misdemeanors
- **Civil Division**: Civil cases over \$25,000
- **Family Division**: Domestic relations, juvenile matters
- **Probate Division**: Wills, estates, guardianships
- **Environmental Division**: Environmental and land use cases

## Jurisdiction
- General trial court for all matters
- Unified court system - one court with multiple divisions
- Appeals go to Vermont Supreme Court
