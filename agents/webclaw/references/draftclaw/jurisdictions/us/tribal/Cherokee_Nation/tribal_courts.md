# Cherokee Nation District Court

## Court Information
- **Nation**: Cherokee Nation
- **Court Name**: Cherokee Nation District Court
- **Phone**: (918) 458-5040
- **Address**: 17675 S Muskogee Ave, Tahlequah, OK 74464
- **Website**: https://www.cherokee.org/our-government/judicial-branch/

## Description
Largest tribal court system in the US. District Court and Supreme Court.

## Jurisdiction
- Tribal civil and criminal matters
- Family law and child welfare
- Tribal code violations
- Civil disputes
- Probate and estates
