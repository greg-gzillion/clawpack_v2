# Navajo Nation District Court

## Court Information
- **Nation**: Navajo Nation
- **Court Name**: Navajo Nation District Court
- **Phone**: (928) 871-6771
- **Address**: Navajo Nation Judicial Branch, Window Rock, AZ 86515
- **Website**: https://www.navajocourts.org/

## Description
Largest tribal court system in the US. 11 district courts, Supreme Court.

## Jurisdiction
- Tribal civil and criminal matters
- Family law and child welfare
- Tribal code violations
- Civil disputes
- Probate and estates
