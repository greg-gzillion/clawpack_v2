# Marshall County Magistrate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Marshall
- **Court Name**: Marshall County Magistrate Court
- **Phone**: (304) 845-2130
- **Address**: 600 7th St, Moundsville, WV 26041
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM ET
- **Website**: http://www.courtswv.gov

## Jurisdiction
- Misdemeanors
- Traffic violations
- Small claims (up to $10,000)
- Civil cases (up to $10,000)
- Preliminary hearings
- Issue warrants and emergency orders
