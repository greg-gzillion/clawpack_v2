# Commercial Translation APIs for uk

## Major Providers
- [Google Cloud Translation](https://cloud.google.com/translate)
  - \ per million characters
  - 500,000 free characters/month
  
- [DeepL API](https://www.deepl.com/pro-api)
  - \ per million characters
  - Free tier: 500,000 characters/month
  
- [Azure Translator](https://azure.microsoft.com/services/cognitive-services/translator/)
  - \ per million characters
  - 2 million free characters/month
  
- [AWS Translate](https://aws.amazon.com/translate/)
  - \ per million characters
  - 2 million free characters/month (first 12 months)
