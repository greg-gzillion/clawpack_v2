- AIGA Detroit — https://detroit.aiga.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Automotive and industrial design capital of North America
- Great Lakes coastal and waterfront design
- Strong manufacturing and fabrication heritage
- Cold climate design (Upper Peninsula heavy snow loads 70-80 psf)
