# Milwaukee County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Milwaukee
- **Court Name**: Milwaukee County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (414) 278-4500
- **Address**: 901 N 9th Street, Milwaukee, WI 53233
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 10-16)
- Children in Need of Protection or Services (CHIPS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
- Truancy
