# New Canaan Design Resources

## Architecture
- Philip Johnson Glass House — https://www.theglasshouse.org/
- Harvard Five architectural heritage — multiple mid-century modern landmarks

## Notes
The Glass House is a National Trust Historic Site and icon of American modernism. New Canaan has an extraordinary concentration of mid-century modern architecture from the Harvard Five architects (Philip Johnson, Marcel Breuer, Landis Gores, John Johansen, Eliot Noyes).
