# New Haven Probate District - Connecticut
## Probate District (Court Jurisdiction Only)
## Area: New Haven area - New Haven County
## Building Permits: Contact New Haven County
## URL: https://www.newhavenct.gov/

## NOTE
This is a probate court district, NOT a building permit jurisdiction.
All building permits in Connecticut are issued at city/town level.
Contact your local town/city hall for permit applications.
