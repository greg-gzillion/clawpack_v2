## California Code of Regulations, Title 24 (California Building Code)

## Adopted Codes (Title 24)
| Code | Edition | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Building | CBC 2022 (Part 2) | IBC-based + CA amendments |
| Residential | CRC 2022 (Part 2.5) | IRC-based |
| Electrical | CEC 2022 (Part 3) | NEC-based + CA amendments |
| Plumbing | CPC 2022 (Part 5) | UPC-based |
| Mechanical | CMC 2022 (Part 4) | UMC-based |
| Fire | CFC 2022 (Part 9) | IFC-based |
| Energy | CEnC 2022 (Part 6) | CalGreen mandatory |
| Accessibility | CBC Ch 11A/11B | Stricter than ADA |
| Green | CALGreen (Part 11) | Mandatory statewide |

## Key CA Requirements
- Title 24 energy compliance mandatory (solar PV for new residential)
- CALGreen mandatory measures statewide
- Seismic design critical statewide
- Local amendments via city/county ordinance
- SF in high fire severity zones require WUI compliance
