# Lauderdale County Chancery Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Lauderdale
- **Court Name**: Lauderdale County Chancery Court
- **Phone**: (601) 482-9801
- **Address**: 500 Constitution Avenue, Meridian, MS 39301
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Domestic relations (divorce, custody, support)
- Probate and estates
- Guardianships and conservatorships
- Land title disputes
- Contract disputes
- Injunctions and equity matters
