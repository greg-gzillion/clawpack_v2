## Key Hawaii Notes
- 5 counties: Hawaii, Honolulu, Kauai, Maui, Kalawao
- Honolulu: Consolidated city-county (entire island of Oahu)
- Hawaii County: Big Island — active volcanoes, lava zones
- High seismic hazard on Big Island (Kilauea/Mauna Loa)
- Hurricane wind zone — wind-borne debris in coastal areas
- Salt air corrosion considerations for materials
- County building departments handle all permits
