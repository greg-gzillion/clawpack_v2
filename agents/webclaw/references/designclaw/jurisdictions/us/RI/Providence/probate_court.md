# Providence County Probate Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Providence
- **Court Name**: Providence County Probate Courts
- **Phone**: (401) 458-3300
- **Address**: 250 Benefit Street, Providence, RI 02903
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Wills and Estates
- Trusts
- Guardianships
- Conservatorships
