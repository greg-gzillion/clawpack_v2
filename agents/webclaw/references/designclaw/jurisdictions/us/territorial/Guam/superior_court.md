## Divisions
- **Criminal Division**: Felonies, misdemeanors
- **Civil Division**: Civil cases over $15,000
- **Family Division**: Divorce, custody, support, juvenile
- **Probate Division**: Wills, estates, guardianships
- **Small Claims Division**: Claims up to $15,000
