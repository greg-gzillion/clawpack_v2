# Fallingwater Area Design Resources

## Architecture
- Fallingwater — https://www.fallingwater.org/ — Frank Lloyd Wright masterpiece (1935)
- Kentuck Knob — https://www.kentuckknob.com/ — Frank Lloyd Wright design (1954)
- Polymath Park — https://www.polymathpark.com/ — FLW and apprentice houses

## Notes
Fallingwater is arguably the most famous house in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage site. The Laurel Highlands have an extraordinary concentration of Frank Lloyd Wright architecture. A pilgrimage destination for architects and designers globally.
