# New Jersey Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA New Jersey — https://newjersey.aiga.org/
- New Jersey State Council on the Arts — https://www.nj.gov/state/njsca/

## Key Design Considerations
- Coastal resilience design (Hurricane Sandy legacy)
- Strong pharmaceutical and life sciences design
- Proximity to NYC and Philadelphia design markets
- Historic industrial adaptive reuse
