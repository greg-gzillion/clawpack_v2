# West Virginia Supreme Court of Appeals

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of Appeals of West Virginia
- **Website**: http://www.courtswv.gov/supreme-court
- **Phone**: (304) 558-2601
- **Address**: 1900 Kanawha Blvd E, Charleston, WV 25305
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate court for West Virginia
- Appeals from Circuit Courts
- Original jurisdiction in limited matters (habeas corpus, mandamus, prohibition)
- Rule-making authority for state court system
- Attorney discipline and admissions

## Justices
- 5 Justices elected to 12-year terms
- Chief Justice selected by rotation
