# Anchorage Design Resources

## Overview
Alaska's largest city and primary design center. Most of the state's architecture, engineering, and design firms are based here. Strong military, oil/gas, and healthcare design sectors.

## Design Schools
- University of Alaska Anchorage - Art and Design - https://www.uaa.alaska.edu/academics/college-of-arts-and-sciences/departments/art/
- Alaska Pacific University - Sustainability and Design - https://www.alaskapacific.edu/
- Charter College - Graphic Design - https://www.chartercollege.edu/
