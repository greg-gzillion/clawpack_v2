# Juneau Design Resources

## Overview
Alaska's capital city. Government, tourism, and Native Alaskan cultural design dominate. Southeast Alaska's temperate rainforest context.

## Architecture and Design Firms
- Northwind Architects - https://www.northwindarchitects.com/
- Jensen Yorba Lott (JYL Architects) - https://www.jylarchitects.com/
- MRV Architects - https://www.mrvarchitects.com/

## Native Design and Cultural Institutions
- Sealaska Heritage Institute - https://www.sealaskaheritage.org/ - Tlingit, Haida, Tsimshian art and design
- Walter Soboleff Center - Native arts and culture

## Notes
Government buildings drive institutional design. Strong Tlingit/Haida/Tsimshian design heritage. Rainforest climate requires unique moisture management.
