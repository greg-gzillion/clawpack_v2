# Alaska Design Resources

## Overview
Small but dedicated design community. Extreme climate, remote logistics, and indigenous cultural heritage shape Alaska's unique design identity. Strong focus on cold-climate architecture, subsistence-based community planning, and Native Alaskan art integration.

## Major Design Hubs
- Anchorage - Primary design center. Most architecture firms based here.
- Fairbanks - Interior Alaska. Strong university influence (UAF).
- Juneau - Southeast Alaska. Government and Native design focus.
- Nome, Bethel, Utqiagvik - Rural/hub communities with unique Arctic design challenges.

## Statewide Organizations
- Alaska Design Forum - statewide design lectures and events
- Alaska State Council on the Arts - https://arts.alaska.gov/
- Alaska Native Arts Foundation - https://www.alaskanativearts.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Extreme cold engineering requirements
- Permafrost and ice-resistant foundations
- Remote construction logistics (barge, air freight)
- Indigenous cultural heritage integration
- Short building season (May-September)
