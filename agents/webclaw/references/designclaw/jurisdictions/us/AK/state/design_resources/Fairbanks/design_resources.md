# Fairbanks Design Resources

## Overview
Interior Alaska's design center. Strong university influence from UAF. Cold-climate engineering and Arctic research drive unique design challenges.

## Design Schools
- University of Alaska Fairbanks - Art and Native Art - https://www.uaf.edu/art/
- UAF - Engineering and Arctic Design - https://www.uaf.edu/cee/

## Architecture and Design Firms
- Bettisworth North (Fairbanks office) - https://www.bettisworthnorth.com/
- Design Alaska - https://www.designalaska.com/
- Arctic Fire Design - graphic and web design

## Design Communities
- Alaska Design Forum (Fairbanks events)
- Fairbanks Arts Association

## Notes
Extreme cold (-50F) drives unique building envelope requirements. Permafrost research at UAF influences foundation design statewide.
