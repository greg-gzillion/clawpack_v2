# Box Elder County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Box Elder
- **Court Name**: Box Elder County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (435) 734-3300
- **Address**: 1 S Main Street, Brigham City, UT 84302
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM MT

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (ages 12-17)
- Abuse, neglect, and dependency
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
- Children in Need of Services
- Traffic violations for minors
