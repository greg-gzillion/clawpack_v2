# Alabama Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Birmingham — https://birmingham.aiga.org/
- Alabama State Council on the Arts — https://arts.alabama.gov/
- Alabama Center for Architecture — https://www.alcfa.org/
- Create Birmingham — https://www.createbirmingham.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Hot/humid climate drives HVAC and envelope design
- Tornado-resistant construction in northern counties
- Hurricane codes in Mobile/Baldwin counties (150 mph wind zone)
- Strong iron/steel fabrication heritage
