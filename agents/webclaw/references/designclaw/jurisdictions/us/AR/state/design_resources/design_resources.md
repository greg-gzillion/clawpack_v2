# Arkansas Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- Arkansas Arts Council — https://www.arkansasheritage.com/arkansas-arts-council
- University of Arkansas — Fay Jones School of Architecture — https://fayjones.uark.edu/
- AIGA Arkansas (informal chapter)

## Key Design Considerations
- Mild winters, hot humid summers
- Tornado-resistant design
- Strong retail/supply chain design (Walmart HQ influence)
- Ozark and Delta regional vernacular architecture
