# Muscogee (Creek) Nation District Court

## Court Information
- **Nation**: Muscogee Creek Nation
- **Court Name**: Muscogee (Creek) Nation District Court
- **Phone**: (918) 758-1400
- **Address**: 1000 E Airport Rd, Okmulgee, OK 74447
- **Website**: https://www.muscogeenation.com/services/judicial/

## Description
District Court and Supreme Court. One of the largest tribal courts.

## Jurisdiction
- Tribal civil and criminal matters
- Family law and child welfare
- Tribal code violations
- Civil disputes
- Probate and estates
