# Columbus Design Resources

## Architecture
- First Christian Church — Eliel Saarinen design (1942)
- Miller House — Eero Saarinen design — https://www.imamuseum.org/visit/miller-house/
- North Christian Church — Eero Saarinen design (1964)
- Cleo Rogers Memorial Library — I.M. Pei design
- Irwin Union Bank — Eero Saarinen design
- Columbus City Hall — Skidmore, Owings & Merrill

## Notes
Columbus has one of the greatest concentrations of modern architecture in the US. J. Irwin Miller's Cummins Engine Foundation funded world-class architects to design public buildings. 7 National Historic Landmarks. A must-visit for architecture and design.
