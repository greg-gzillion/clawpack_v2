# Vermont Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- Vermont Arts Council — https://www.vermontartscouncil.org/
- AIA Vermont — https://www.aiavt.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Cold climate and heavy snow loads (60-80 psf)
- Historic New England architecture preservation
- Strong craft and woodworking tradition
- Rural and small-town design context
- Environmental stewardship leadership
