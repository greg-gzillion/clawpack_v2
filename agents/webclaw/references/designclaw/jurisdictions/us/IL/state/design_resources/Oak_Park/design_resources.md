# Oak Park Design Resources

## Architecture
- Frank Lloyd Wright Home and Studio — https://www.flwright.org/
- Unity Temple — https://www.utrf.org/ — UNESCO World Heritage
- Arthur Heurtley House — Wright design (1902)
- Ernest Hemingway Birthplace — https://www.hemingwaybirthplace.com/

## Notes
Oak Park has the largest concentration of Frank Lloyd Wright-designed buildings in the world. Unity Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage site and revolutionary in its use of reinforced concrete.
