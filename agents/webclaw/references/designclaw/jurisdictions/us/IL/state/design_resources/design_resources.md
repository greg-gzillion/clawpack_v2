# Illinois Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Chicago — https://chicago.aiga.org/
- Illinois Arts Council — https://arts.illinois.gov/
- AIA Illinois — https://www.aiail.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Chicago is a global architecture capital
- Frank Lloyd Wright and Prairie School heritage
- Strong industrial design tradition
- Extreme temperature swings (-20F to 100F) drive envelope design
