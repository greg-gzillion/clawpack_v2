## Notes
Chicago is the birthplace of the skyscraper and a global architecture capital. IIT Institute of Design founded by Laszlo Moholy-Nagy as the New Bauhaus. SOM, the firm that defined the modern skyscraper, is headquartered here. NeoCon is the largest commercial interiors trade show in North America.
