# Thomas County Juvenile Court

## Court Information
- **County**: Thomas
- **Court Name**: Thomas County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (229) 225-4100
- **Address**: 100 N Broad Street, Thomasville, GA 31792
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)
- Children in Need of Services (CHINS)
- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
