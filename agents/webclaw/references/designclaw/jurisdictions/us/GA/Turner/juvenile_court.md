## Court Information
- **County**: Turner
- **Court Name**: Turner County Juvenile Court
- **Phone**: (229) 567-2361

- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ET

## Jurisdiction
- Juvenile delinquency (under 17)
- Deprivation (abuse/neglect)

- Termination of parental rights
- Emancipation
