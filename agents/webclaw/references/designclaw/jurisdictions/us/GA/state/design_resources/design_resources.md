# Georgia Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- AIGA Atlanta — https://atlanta.aiga.org/
- Georgia Council for the Arts — https://gaarts.org/
- Museum of Design Atlanta (MODA) — https://www.museumofdesign.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Hot/humid climate drives HVAC and envelope design
- Strong civil rights and memorial design heritage
- Film and entertainment design (Georgia is a major production center)
- Rapid urban growth and transit-oriented design
