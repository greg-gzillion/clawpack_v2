# Fort Worth Design Resources

## Cultural Design
- Kimbell Art Museum — https://www.kimbellart.org/ — Louis Kahn and Renzo Piano designs
- Modern Art Museum of Fort Worth — https://www.themodern.org/ — Tadao Ando design
- Amon Carter Museum — https://www.cartermuseum.org/ — Philip Johnson design

## Notes
Fort Worth has an extraordinary concentration of museum architecture by Pritzker Prize winners. The Kimbell's Louis Kahn building is considered one of the greatest museum designs of the 20th century.
