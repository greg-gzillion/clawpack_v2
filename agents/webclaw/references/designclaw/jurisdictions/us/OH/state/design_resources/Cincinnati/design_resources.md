# Cincinnati Design Resources

## Design Schools
- University of Cincinnati — College of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning (DAAP) — https://www.daap.uc.edu/
- Art Academy of Cincinnati — https://www.artacademy.edu/

## Cultural Design
- Cincinnati Art Museum — https://www.cincinnatiartmuseum.org/
- Contemporary Arts Center — https://www.contemporaryartscenter.org/ — Zaha Hadid design
- Union Terminal — https://www.cincymuseum.org/ — Art Deco masterpiece

## Design Communities
- AIGA Cincinnati — https://cincinnati.aiga.org/

## Notes
DAAP is one of the top design schools in the country and invented the co-op education model. Zaha Hadid's first US museum commission was the Contemporary Arts Center. Union Terminal is one of the greatest Art Deco buildings in the US.
