# Louisiana Design Resources

## Statewide Organizations
- Louisiana Division of the Arts — https://www.crt.state.la.us/cultural-development/arts/
- AIA Louisiana — https://www.aialouisiana.org/

## Key Design Considerations
- Hurricane codes (150 mph wind zones) heavily influence design
- French, Spanish, and Caribbean architectural heritage
- Strong preservation and adaptive reuse
- Coastal restoration and resilient design
