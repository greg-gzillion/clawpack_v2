**MEMORANDUM**

**Subject: Implementation of New Company Policy**

**Date:** April 11, 2026

**To:** All Employees

**From:** [Management/HR Representative]

**Purpose:** As part of our ongoing efforts to promote a safe, productive, and respectful work environment, we are introducing a new company policy effective immediately.

**Policy Overview:**

The new policy, "Electronic Device Use and Personal Social Media," aims to clarify guidelines for employee use of company-provided electronic devices and personal social media platforms during work hours. The policy is designed to promote responsible use, minimize distractions, and protect company data and reputation.

**Key Provisions:**

1. **Personal Social Media Use:** Employees are prohibited from accessing and posting on personal social media platforms during work hours, except in cases where the usage is directly related to company business.
2. **Company-Provided Electronic Devices:** Employees are expected to use company-provided electronic devices only for work-related purposes. Any personal use of these devices must be approved in advance by management.
3. **Data Protection:** Employees must ensure that all company data and confidential information are handled and stored in accordance with company policies and industry standards.
4. **Reporting Misconduct:** Employees are encouraged to report any instances of misconduct or policy breaches to management or HR representatives.

**Implementation:**

The new policy will be enforced immediately. All employees are expected to comply with the provisions outlined above. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

**Training and Support:**

A comprehensive training program will be provided to all employees to ensure a smooth transition to the new policy. HR representatives will be available to address any questions or concerns.

**Acknowledgement:**

Please acknowledge receipt of this policy and confirm your understanding of its provisions by signing and returning the attached acknowledgement form to HR by April 18, 2026.

**Contact Information:**

If you have any questions or concerns regarding the new policy, please do not hesitate to contact your supervisor or HR representative.

Sincerely,

[Management/HR Representative]
