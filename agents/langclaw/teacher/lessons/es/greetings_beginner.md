# Spanish Greetings - Beginner Level

## Vocabulary
hola = hello
buenos días = good morning
buenas tardes = good afternoon
buenas noches = good night
adiós = goodbye
hasta luego = see you later

## Lesson
In Spanish, greetings change based on the time of day:
- Morning (until 12pm): "Buenos días"
- Afternoon (12pm-8pm): "Buenas tardes"  
- Night (after 8pm): "Buenas noches"

"Adiós" is formal, "Hasta luego" is casual.

## Practice
1. How do you say "Good morning"?
2. What greeting would you use at 3pm?
3. Which is more casual - "Adiós" or "Hasta luego"?
